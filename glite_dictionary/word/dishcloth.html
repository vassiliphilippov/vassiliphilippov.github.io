dishcloth

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪʃˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃˌklɒθ / (BrE)
A cloth, usually made of absorbent material, used for washing or cleaning dishes.
cloth for washing dishes

Examples

She used a clean dishcloth to wipe the plates after dinner.
After rinsing the pans, he hung the wet dishcloth by the sink.
The dishcloth needs to be replaced because it smells musty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rag - a piece of old cloth used for cleaning
  • Synonym: sponge - a porous material used to clean surfaces, often in washing dishes
