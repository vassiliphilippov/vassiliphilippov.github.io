P.E.

1. NOUN

/ piː ˈiː / (AmE), / piː ˈiː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for potential energy; the mechanical energy stored in an object by virtue of its position or arrangement.
abbreviation for potential energy (physics)

Examples

In physics class, the teacher explained that P.E. increases as an object is lifted higher.
By converting P.E. to kinetic energy, the roller coaster gained speed going downhill.
The equation for gravitational P.E. involves mass, gravity, and height.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potential energy - energy possessed by a body due to its position or condition
  • Antonym: kinetic energy - energy of motion

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'potential energy.'
