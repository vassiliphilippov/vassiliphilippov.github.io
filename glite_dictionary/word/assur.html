Assur

1. PROPN

/ ˈæs.ər / (AmE), / ˈas.ʊə / (BrE)
an ancient Assyrian city situated on the Tigris River, south of modern Mosul in Iraq, and the traditional capital of Assyria.
ancient Assyrian city on the Tigris River

Examples

Archaeologists have discovered many artifacts in Assur that shed light on the Assyrian Empire.
The ruins of Assur are a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
In ancient times, Assur served as the religious center of Assyria.
