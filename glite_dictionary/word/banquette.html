banquette

1. NOUN

/ bæŋˈkɛt / (AmE), / bæŋˈkɛt / (BrE)
a long upholstered bench, typically one placed against a wall, commonly found in restaurants or dining areas
upholstered bench, often in restaurants

Examples

The cozy café was furnished with a banquette along the windowed wall.
They requested a table by the banquette for added comfort and privacy.
A green velvet banquette ran the length of the dining room, offering plenty of seating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bench - a long seat for several people, typically made of wood or stone

Etymology

From French 'banquette' meaning a small bench.
