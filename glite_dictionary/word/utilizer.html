utilizer

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːtəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈjuːtɪˌlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes use of something for a particular purpose
person or thing that uses something

Examples

The organization is seeking feedback from each utilizer of its services.
All eligible utilizers must complete the registration form before the deadline.
In the context of energy efficiency, a utilizer may refer to a device that consumes fuel or power.

Synset Examples

not all organisms are utilizers of oxygen
the social agencies and their utilizers both objected to the budget cut

Related Terms

  • Synonym: user - a person who uses or operates something
  • Synonym: to utilize - to make practical and effective use of something

Etymology

From the verb 'utilize' + '-er' suffix indicating an agent noun.
