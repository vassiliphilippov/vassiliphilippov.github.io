gardening

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdənɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of cultivating and caring for a garden, especially as a hobby
cultivation and care of a garden

Examples

She spends her weekends gardening in her backyard, growing vegetables and flowers.
Many people find gardening to be a relaxing way to connect with nature.
The local community center offers classes on organic gardening for beginners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horticulture - the art or practice of garden cultivation and management

Etymology

From garden + -ing, denoting the practice or action.
