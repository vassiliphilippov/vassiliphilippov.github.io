eyeless

1. ADJ

/ ˈaɪləs / (AmE), / ˈaɪləs / (BrE)
lacking eyes or the power of sight
lacking eyes or sight

Examples

The deep sea fish was completely eyeless due to living in total darkness.
An eyeless statue stood silently in the ancient temple's shadows.
After the accident, he became eyeless and had to adjust to an entirely new way of life.

Synset Examples

blind as an eyeless beggar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blind - unable to see; lacking sight

Etymology

Formed from "eye" + "-less" (without).

2. ADJ

/ ˈaɪləs / (AmE), / ˈaɪləs / (BrE)
Having no eyes or lacking openings that resemble eyes.
having no eyes; lacking eye openings

Examples

Some cave-dwelling animals have evolved to become completely eyeless.
The artist sculpted an eyeless face to evoke a sense of mystery.
She threaded the string through an eyeless needle with surprising ease.

Synset Examples

eyeless fish that evolved in dark caves
an eyeless needle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sightless - unable to see; blind
  • Synonym: blind - unable to see; lacking the sense of sight

Etymology

From 'eye' + '-less' (Old English 'lēas', meaning 'without').
