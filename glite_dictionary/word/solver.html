solver

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːlvər / (AmE), / ˈsɒlvə / (BrE)
a person who finds answers to problems, especially by analyzing information logically
person who solves problems

Examples

The company hired her because she is an excellent solver of complex technical problems.
As a puzzle solver, he enjoys challenging crosswords every morning.
In mathematics, a digital solver can process thousands of equations in seconds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: problem solver - a person skilled at finding solutions to difficult issues
  • Synonym: thinker - a person who thinks deeply or intellectually

Etymology

From the verb 'solve,' from Latin 'solvere' (to loosen, resolve).
