Johore

1. PROPN

/ dʒoʊˈhɔr / (AmE), / dʒəʊˈhɔː / (BrE)
A sultanate and one of the thirteen states of Malaysia, located on the southern tip of the Malay Peninsula.
state in southern Malaysia

Examples

The causeway between Singapore and Johore is a vital link for both countries.
During our trip to Malaysia, we spent two days exploring Johore and its capital, Johor Bahru.
Johore is known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant cultural festivals.

Etymology

From Malay 'Johor', meaning 'jewel', originally from Arabic 'jawhar' (jewel).

2. PROPN

/ dʒoʊˈhɔr / (AmE), / dʒəˈhɔː / (BrE)
a sultanate and one of the 13 states in the Federation of Malaysia, located in the southern part of Peninsular Malaysia
a state in southern Peninsular Malaysia

Examples

Many tourists visit Johore for its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture.
The Sultan of Johore played a significant role in the state's development.
Trade between Johore and Singapore has historically been important for both regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malaysia - country in Southeast Asia of which Johore is a state
  • Synonym: Peninsular Malaysia - the part of Malaysia located on the Malay Peninsula, where Johore is situated

Etymology

From Malay 'Johor', possibly derived from the Arabic 'jauhar' meaning 'gem' or 'jewel'.
