Piperales

1. NOUN

/ paɪpɚˈeɪliːz / (AmE), / pɪpəˈreɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of flowering plants that includes families such as Piperaceae, Saururaceae, and Chloranthaceae
an order of flowering plants

Examples

The black pepper plant belongs to the order Piperales, known for aromatic herbs and shrubs.
Scientists discussed the evolutionary relationships among the families of Piperales at the conference.
Many members of Piperales have been used in traditional medicine for centuries.

Etymology

From Latinized modern botanical Latin, formed from Piper (the pepper genus) + -ales (order suffix).
