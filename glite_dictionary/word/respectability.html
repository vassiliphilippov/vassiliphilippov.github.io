respectability

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌspɛktəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / rɪˌspɛktəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
The quality of being considered socially acceptable, proper, or having a good reputation.
the quality of being respectable

Examples

His actions threatened the respectability of the organization.
She worked hard to maintain an image of respectability in her community.
The family's long history of respectability was well known throughout the town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respectable - worthy of respect; regarded as proper or decent

Etymology

From Latin 'respectabilis' (worthy of respect) via French 'respectabilité'.
