mujahid

1. NOUN

/ muːˈdʒɑːhɪd / (AmE), / muːˈdʒɑːhɪd / (BrE)
A Muslim who is engaged in or advocates jihad, especially in an armed struggle.
a Muslim engaged in jihad (often armed)

Examples

The mujahid fought fiercely to defend his beliefs.
After returning from battle, the mujahid was welcomed as a hero in his village.
Many young men aspired to become a mujahid in the hopes of achieving martyrdom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jihad - a struggle or fight against the enemies of Islam

Etymology

From Arabic مُجَاهِد‎ (mujāhid), meaning 'one who struggles or fights for Islam'.
