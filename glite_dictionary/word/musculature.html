musculature

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌs.kjə.lə.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.kjʊ.lə.tjʊə / (BrE)
the system or arrangement of muscles in a body or part of a body
system of muscles in a body

Examples

The musculature of elite athletes is often highly developed due to intense training.
She studied the musculature of the human face for her anatomy class.
Fish have a different musculature compared to mammals, adapted for swimming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muscle - a fibrous tissue that contracts to produce movement

Etymology

From Late Latin musculatura (system of muscles), from Latin musculus (muscle).
