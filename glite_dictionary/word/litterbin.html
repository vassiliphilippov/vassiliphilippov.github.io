litterbin

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬ər bɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɪtə bɪn / (BrE)
a container, usually placed in public spaces, for people to deposit rubbish
public rubbish container
british

Examples

Please put your empty bottle in the litterbin before you leave the park.
The city installed a new litterbin on every street corner to help reduce pollution.
Overflowing litterbins can make public areas look untidy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trash can - a container for rubbish (American English)
  • Synonym: wastebasket - small container for holding rubbish, especially indoors
