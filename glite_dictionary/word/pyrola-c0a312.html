pyrola

1. NOUN

/ paɪˈroʊlə / (AmE), / pɪˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
any of several small evergreen perennial plants of the genus Pyrola, commonly known as wintergreens, native to temperate and boreal regions.
a genus of evergreen perennials (wintergreens)

Examples

The botanist carefully examined the pyrola growing at the edge of the forest.
Many species of pyrola are found in cool, shady woodlands across the Northern Hemisphere.
Hikers often overlook the tiny flowers of the pyrola amidst the forest floor.

Etymology

From New Latin Pyrola, diminutive of Latin pyrus (pear), referring to the leaf shape.
