messmate

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛsˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛs.meɪt / (BrE)
a person with whom one regularly shares meals in the same mess, especially aboard a ship or in military service
a person sharing meals in the same mess

Examples

During their deployment, John and his messmate developed a strong friendship.
The captain insisted that each sailor respect his messmate at the dining table.
She cherished the lively stories her messmate would share during meals on the ship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: companion - a person who accompanies or associates with another

Etymology

Derived from 'mess' (a place where people eat, especially in the military) + 'mate' (companion).
