Loyang

1. PROPN

/ loʊˈjɑːŋ / (AmE), / ləʊˈjɑːŋ / (BrE)
an ancient city in central China, former capital of several Chinese dynasties; also known as Luoyang
ancient city in central China

Examples

The city of Loyang served as the capital of several ancient Chinese dynasties.
Archaeologists have discovered many ancient artifacts in Loyang.
Tourists visit Loyang to see its historic temples and gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Luoyang - alternate Romanization of Loyang, ancient city in China

Etymology

From Chinese 洛阳 (Luòyáng), the name of a historical Chinese city.
