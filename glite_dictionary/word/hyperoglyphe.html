Hyperoglyphe

1. NOUN

/ haɪ.pəˈrɒɡ.lɪ.fi / (AmE), / haɪ.pəˈrɒɡ.lɪ.fi / (BrE)
A genus of marine fish in the family Centrolophidae, commonly known as medusafishes.
genus of medusafishes

Examples

The Hyperoglyphe species are often found in temperate oceanic waters.
Researchers discovered a new member of the Hyperoglyphe genus off the southern coast.
Juveniles of Hyperoglyphe are sometimes associated with floating seaweed and jellyfish.
