sticking out

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɪkɪŋ ˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈstɪkɪŋ ˌaʊt / (BrE)
extending outward above or beyond a surface or boundary
extending outward or beyond a surface

Examples

The cat's tail was sticking out from under the couch.
Several wires were sticking out from the back of the computer.
His shirt was sticking out from under his sweater.
