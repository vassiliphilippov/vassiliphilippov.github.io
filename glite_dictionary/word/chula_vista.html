Chula Vista

1. PROPN

/ ˌtʃuːlə ˈvɪstə / (AmE), / ˌtʃuːlə ˈvɪstə / (BrE)
A city in southern California, located south of San Diego and near the Mexican border; known for its industry and residential communities.
City in southern California, USA

Examples

The population of Chula Vista has grown rapidly over the last decade.
After moving to Chula Vista, she enrolled her children in local schools.
Many people commute from Chula Vista to jobs in San Diego.

Etymology

From Spanish 'chula vista' meaning 'pretty view.'
