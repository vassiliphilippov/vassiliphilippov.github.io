rhinostenosis

1. NOUN

/ ˌraɪnoʊstəˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌraɪnəʊstəˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
abnormal narrowing of the nasal cavity passages
narrowing of the nasal passages
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with rhinostenosis after experiencing chronic nasal obstruction.
Surgery was recommended to treat her severe rhinostenosis and improve breathing.
Long-standing allergies can sometimes lead to rhinostenosis, making nasal airflow difficult.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stenosis - abnormal narrowing of a passage in the body

Etymology

From Greek 'rhino-' (nose) + 'stenosis' (narrowing).
