double-bedded

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʌbəl ˈbɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʌbl ˈbɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of a room) having or furnished with a double bed.
room with a double bed

Examples

We requested a double-bedded room for our stay at the hotel.
The hotel offers both single and double-bedded accommodations to suit different needs.
They confirmed that the double-bedded suite was available for the weekend.

Synset Examples

a double-bedded room

Related Terms

  • Antonym: single-bedded - (of a room) having or furnished with a single bed
