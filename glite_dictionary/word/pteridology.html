pteridology

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛrɪˈdɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌtɛrɪˈdɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of botany that deals with the scientific study of ferns
the study of ferns in botany

Examples

She decided to specialize in pteridology after discovering her fascination with ferns.
The university offers advanced courses in pteridology for students interested in plant sciences.
His extensive research in pteridology has led to the discovery of several new fern species.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: botany - the scientific study of plants

Etymology

From Greek 'pteris' (fern) + '-logy' (study).
