ignited

1. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈnaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnaɪtɪd / (BrE)
set on fire; caused to start burning.
set on fire; burning

Examples

The firefighters quickly extinguished the ignited debris on the rooftop.
An ignited candle filled the room with a soft, flickering light.
Check to ensure no ignited materials remain before leaving the campground.

Synset Examples

the ignited paper
a kindled fire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burning - in the process of being on fire

Etymology

From the verb 'ignite', from Latin 'ignītus' (set on fire), from 'ignis' (fire).
