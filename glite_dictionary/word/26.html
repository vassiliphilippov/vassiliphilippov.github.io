26

1. NUM

/ twɛnti sɪks / (AmE), / twenti sɪks / (BrE)
the number that is equal to twenty-five plus one; a cardinal number.
the number twenty-six

Examples

There are 26 letters in the English alphabet.
She turned 26 years old last month.
Only 26 people attended the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-five - the number twenty-five; cardinal numeral
  • Degree Higher: twenty-seven - the number twenty-seven; cardinal numeral
