monorail

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːnəˌreɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəˌreɪl / (BrE)
a railway system in which trains travel on a single rail, typically elevated above the ground
railway with a single track

Examples

The city introduced a new monorail to make public transportation more efficient.
We took the monorail from the airport to the downtown area.
Construction of the monorail was completed ahead of schedule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: railway - a track made of steel rails along which trains run

Etymology

From mono- (single) + rail (track).
