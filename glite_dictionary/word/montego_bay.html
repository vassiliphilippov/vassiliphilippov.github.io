Montego Bay

1. PROPN

/ mɒnˈtiːɡoʊ beɪ / (AmE), / mɒnˈtiːɡəʊ beɪ / (BrE)
a port and resort city in northwestern Jamaica, known for its beaches, tourism, and vibrant culture.
resort city in Jamaica

Examples

Many tourists visit Montego Bay each year to enjoy its beautiful beaches and lively nightlife.
The international airport in Montego Bay receives flights from around the world.
She booked her honeymoon at a luxury resort in Montego Bay.
