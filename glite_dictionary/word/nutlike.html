nutlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the flavor or taste similar to that of nuts
tasting like nuts

Examples

The cheese had a subtle, nutlike flavor that complemented the wine perfectly.
Many gourmets appreciate the nutlike taste of roasted pumpkin seeds.
This particular coffee is known for its smooth, nutlike aftertaste.

Synset Examples

a nutty sherry

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutty - having a flavor or quality reminiscent of nuts

2. ADJ

/ ˈnʌt.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈnʌt.laɪk / (BrE)
Resembling a nut in shape or size.
resembling a nut in shape/size

Examples

The seeds have a nutlike appearance and are often mistaken for actual nuts.
She found a small, nutlike object lying on the forest floor.
The chef used a spice with a nutlike flavor to enhance the dish.

  • Synonym: nutty - having a flavor or aroma like that of nuts
