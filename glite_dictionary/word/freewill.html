freewill

1. ADJ

/ ˈfriˌwɪl / (AmE), / ˈfriːwɪl / (BrE)
(of an action) done voluntarily, without being forced or compelled; of one's own accord.
voluntary; done of one's own accord

Examples

The donation was a freewill gesture, not requested by the organizers.
The temple accepted freewill offerings from its visitors.
She made a freewill confession, without any pressure from the authorities.

Synset Examples

a freewill offering

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voluntary - done by choice, not forced
