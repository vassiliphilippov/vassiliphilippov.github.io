cephalitis

1. NOUN

/ sɛfəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / sɛfəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A medical condition involving inflammation of the brain, usually caused by a viral infection.
inflammation of the brain
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with cephalitis after experiencing severe headaches and confusion.
Early treatment is critical for viral cephalitis to prevent serious complications.
Symptoms of cephalitis can develop rapidly and require immediate medical attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encephalitis - inflammation of the brain, usually due to infection

Etymology

From Greek 'kephalē' (head) and '-itis' (inflammation).
