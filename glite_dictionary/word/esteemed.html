esteemed

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈstimd / (AmE), / ɪˈstiːmd / (BrE)
regarded with great respect or admiration
respected; admired

Examples

The esteemed professor received an award for his groundbreaking research.
She was invited as an esteemed guest to the international conference.
Our company is proud to have such esteemed clients in its portfolio.

Synset Examples

our esteemed leader
a prestigious author

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respected - admired for abilities, qualities, or achievements
  • Synonym: regarded - considered or thought of in a particular way
  • Antonym: disparaged - regarded as being of little worth

Etymology

Past participle of 'esteem', from Latin aestimare (to estimate, value).
