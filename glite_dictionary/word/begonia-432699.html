Begonia

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / bɪˈɡəʊniə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical and subtropical succulent plants, widely cultivated for their decorative leaves and flowers.
tropical flower plant genus

Examples

Many gardeners choose Begonia for its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
She keeps several potted Begonias on her balcony for their colorful blooms.
The Begonia is a popular houseplant due to its ability to thrive indoors.

Etymology

Named after Michel Bégon, French administrator and plant collector.
