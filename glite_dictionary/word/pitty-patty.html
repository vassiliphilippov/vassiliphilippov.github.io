pitty-patty

1. ADV

/ ˌpɪtiˈpæti / (AmE), / ˌpɪtiˈpæti / (BrE)
with a light, rapid, and repeated sound, as of footsteps or a heartbeat.
with a light, rapid repeated sound

Examples

She heard the pitty-patty of raindrops against her window as she was falling asleep.
He tiptoed across the wooden floor, his footsteps going pitty-patty in the quiet house.
The child's heart went pitty-patty at the thought of performing on stage.

Synset Examples

he came running pit-a-pat down the hall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pit-a-pat - with a light, repeated tapping sound, like footsteps or a heartbeat

Etymology

Imitative; formed from reduplication of 'pit' to convey a repeated sound.
