Verona

1. PROPN

/ vəˈroʊnə / (AmE), / vəˈrəʊnə / (BrE)
a city in northern Italy, in the region of Veneto, situated on the River Adige; noted for its rich history and as the setting of Shakespeare's 'Romeo and Juliet'
city in northern Italy, setting of 'Romeo and Juliet'

Examples

Many tourists visit Verona to see Juliet's famous balcony.
Verona is known for its well-preserved Roman amphitheater.
The opera festival in Verona attracts thousands of music lovers every summer.
