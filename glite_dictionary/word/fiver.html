fiver

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪvər / (AmE), / ˈfaɪvə / (BrE)
an informal term for a five-dollar bill in the United States
slang for five-dollar bill (US)
informal american

Examples

He took a fiver out of his wallet to pay for lunch.
Do you have a fiver I can borrow until tomorrow?
I found a fiver on the sidewalk this morning.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: tenner - informal term for a ten-dollar bill
  • Degree Lower: buck - slang term for one dollar bill

Etymology

From 'five' + suffix '-er', forming a noun meaning 'five-dollar bill'.
