Brosme brosme

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊzmi ˈbroʊzmi / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊzmi ˈbrəʊzmi / (BrE)
a large edible marine fish of the North Atlantic, related to cod, known as the tusk or cusk
a large edible North Atlantic fish (tusk or cusk)

Examples

Fishermen in Norway often catch Brosme brosme for both local markets and export.
The flesh of Brosme brosme is prized for its firm texture and mild flavor.
A single large Brosme brosme can provide several meals for a family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tusk - common name for Brosme brosme, the marine fish
  • Synonym: cod - a related species of edible marine fish

Etymology

From scientific Latin; the genus and species name used in zoological taxonomy.
