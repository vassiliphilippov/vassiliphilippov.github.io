kinsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪnzmən / (AmE), / ˈkɪnzmən / (BrE)
a male relative, especially a blood relation
male relative

Examples

He invited every kinsman he could find to the family reunion.
After his father's death, his closest kinsman took responsibility for him.
The old traditions required that a kinsman avenge any wrongdoing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relative - a person connected by blood or marriage
  • Antonym: kinswoman - a female relative

Etymology

From Old English cynnes man (man of kin); 'kin' meaning family + 'man'.
