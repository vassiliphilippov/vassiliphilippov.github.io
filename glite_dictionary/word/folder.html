folder

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈfəʊldə / (BrE)
a folded cover or holder, typically made of cardboard or plastic, used for storing and protecting papers or documents
cover for storing papers or documents

Examples

She tucked the papers inside a blue folder to keep them safe.
Each student received a welcome folder with important information.
The report was missing from the folder on his desk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: file - a container or folder for holding papers

Etymology

From fold + -er, indicating something that folds or holds by folding.
