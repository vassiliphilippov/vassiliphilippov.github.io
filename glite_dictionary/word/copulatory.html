copulatory

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːpjəˌleɪˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈkɒpjʊlətəri / (BrE)
Relating to copulation or sexual intercourse.
relating to copulation

Examples

The frog's copulatory behavior was observed during the breeding season.
Certain insects have specialized copulatory organs for mating.
Researchers studied the copulatory patterns of the species in captivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexual - relating to sex or the sexes
  • Antonym: asexual - not involving sexual activity

Etymology

From Latin 'copulatus', past participle of 'copulare' (to join, connect).
