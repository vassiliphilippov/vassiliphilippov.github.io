uproariously

1. ADV

/ ʌpˈrɔːriəsli / (AmE), / ʌpˈrɔːrɪəsli / (BrE)
In a way that causes loud laughter or great amusement; extremely funny or noisy.
In a hilariously loud or funny way

Examples

The comedian's jokes had the entire audience laughing uproariously throughout the show.
They celebrated their victory uproariously, cheering and shouting in excitement.
The children played uproariously in the backyard, their laughter echoing down the street.

Synset Examples

hilariously funny

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hilariously - in a very funny or amusing manner
  • Antonym: quietly - in a quiet manner, without noise

Etymology

From 'uproarious' (loud and noisy), itself from 'uproar' (a loud disturbance) + '-ious' + '-ly'.
