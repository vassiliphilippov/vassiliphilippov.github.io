Southeast

1. PROPN

/ ˌsaʊθˈist / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈiːst / (BrE)
the southeastern region of the United States, typically including states such as Georgia, Florida, Alabama, Mississippi, South Carolina, North Carolina, and Tennessee
the southeastern region of the US

Examples

Many people retire to the Southeast because of its warm climate and friendly communities.
The Southeast has a rich tradition of music, especially blues and country genres.
This summer, we're planning a road trip across the Southeast to visit several national parks.
