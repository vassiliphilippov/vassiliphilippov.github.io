unthawed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈθɔd / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɔːd / (BrE)
(of food or other substances) still frozen; not thawed.
still frozen; not thawed

Examples

The chicken was still unthawed when I took it from the freezer.
We couldn't make dinner because all the vegetables were unthawed.
Leave the fish out overnight if it remains unthawed.

Synset Examples

there wasn't time to cook the unthawed turkey, so they had to settle for hotdogs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frozen - turned into ice or a solid state by cold
  • Antonym: thawed - no longer frozen; unfrozen
