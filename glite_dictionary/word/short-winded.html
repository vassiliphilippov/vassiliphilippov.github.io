short-winded

1. ADJ

/ ʃɔrt ˈwɪndɪd / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ˈwɪndɪd / (BrE)
breathing in a labored or convulsive manner; having difficulty in breathing normally, especially after exertion.
breathing with difficulty

Examples

After climbing the steep hill, he was noticeably short-winded and needed to rest.
His short-winded condition worried the doctor, who recommended further tests.
The old dog grew short-winded after running in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: short of breath - having difficulty breathing; unable to inhale enough air
