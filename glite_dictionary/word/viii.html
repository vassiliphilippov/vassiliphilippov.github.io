VIII

1. NUM

/ viː ˈeɪt / (AmE), / viː ˈeɪt / (BrE)
The Roman numeral representing the number eight.
Roman numeral for eight

Examples

The clock on the wall showed the time as VIII in Roman numerals.
Chapter VIII of the book is about ancient history.
She proudly wrote the number VIII on her jersey for the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eight - the cardinal number equal to 8

Etymology

From Latin 'octo' (eight), eight represented with 'VIII' in Roman numerals ('V' = 5, 'III' = 3).
