viii

1. NUM

/ viː ˈaɪ ˈaɪ ˈaɪ / (AmE), / viː ˈaɪ ˈaɪ ˈaɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number eight
Roman numeral for eight

Examples

The king was known as Henry VIII of England.
Please turn to chapter viii in your textbooks.
The inscription on the building reads 'Anno Domini MDCCLXXVIII', where viii indicates the year ending with eight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eight - the cardinal number equivalent to 8

Etymology

From Latin 'VIII', from 'octo' (eight), representing 5 (V) plus 3 (III).
