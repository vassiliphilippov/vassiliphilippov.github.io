fistulate

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪs.tʃə.leɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪs.tjʊ.leɪt / (BrE)
(of a structure) hollow and tube-shaped, resembling a reed.
hollow and tube-shaped

Examples

The plant's stem is fistulate, allowing fluids to pass through its center.
Certain worms have a fistulate body adapted for burrowing in mud.
The straw is fistulate, making it ideal for sipping liquids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tubular - shaped like a tube
  • Antonym: solid - not hollow or containing empty space

Etymology

From Latin fistulatus, from fistula (pipe, tube).
