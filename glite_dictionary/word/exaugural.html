exaugural

1. ADJ

/ ɛɡˈzɔɡjərəl / (AmE), / ɛɡˈzɔːɡjʊərəl / (BrE)
Relating to or marking the end of a term of office.
marking the end of a term of office

Examples

The president delivered his exaugural address to summarize his achievements in office.
An exaugural dinner was held to honor the outgoing mayor.
Her exaugural remarks reflected on the challenges and successes of her tenure.

Synset Examples

an exaugural message

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inaugural - relating to the beginning of a term of office

Etymology

From Latin 'exaugurare', by analogy with 'inaugural'.
