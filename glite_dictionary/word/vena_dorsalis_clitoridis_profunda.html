vena dorsalis clitoridis profunda

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪnə dɔrˈsælɪs klɪtəˈraɪdɪs prəˈfʊndə / (AmE), / ˈviːnə dɔːˈsælɪs klɪtɒˈraɪdɪs prəˈfʊndə / (BrE)
the deep dorsal vein of the clitoris, which drains blood from the clitoris and is a tributary of the vesical venous plexus
deep dorsal vein of the clitoris
medical

Examples

During surgery, the vena dorsalis clitoridis profunda must be carefully identified to prevent excessive bleeding.
Imaging studies can reveal abnormalities of the vena dorsalis clitoridis profunda in some rare conditions.
The vena dorsalis clitoridis profunda is an important vessel draining blood from the clitoris.

Etymology

From Latin: vena (vein), dorsalis (dorsal), clitoridis (of the clitoris), profunda (deep).
