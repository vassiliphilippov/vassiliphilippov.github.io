snow-white

1. ADJ

/ snoʊ waɪt / (AmE), / snəʊ waɪt / (BrE)
pure white in color, like freshly fallen snow
pure white in color

Examples

Her dress was snow-white and sparkled in the sunlight.
He had snow-white hair even though he was only in his forties.
The snow-white sheets smelled fresh and clean.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: white - the color of milk or fresh snow
  • Degree Lower: milky - having a white color like milk; less pure than snow-white
