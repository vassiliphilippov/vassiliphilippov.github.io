piscatorial

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɪskəˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / ˌpɪskəˈtɔːrɪəl / (BrE)
relating to fishing or fishermen; characteristic of or connected with the activity of fishing.
relating to fishing

Examples

He spent his retirement enjoying a piscatorial lifestyle by the lake.
The museum features a collection of piscatorial artifacts from ancient times.
Their conversation soon turned to piscatorial tales of the biggest fish they had ever caught.

Synset Examples

a piscatory life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishing - the activity of catching fish

Etymology

From Latin piscatorius (of fishermen), from piscator (fisherman), from piscis (fish).
