raj

1. NOUN

/ rɑdʒ / (AmE), / rɑːdʒ / (BrE)
The period of British rule over India from 1757 to 1947.
British rule in India (1757–1947)
british

Examples

Many novels set during the raj explore the complexities of colonial relationships.
After the end of the raj, India became an independent nation in 1947.
Historians still debate the economic and social impact of the British raj.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: British Raj - The British rule in the Indian subcontinent from 1858 to 1947

Etymology

From Hindi *rāj* (rule, reign), from Sanskrit *rāja* (king).
