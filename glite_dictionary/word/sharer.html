sharer

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɛrər / (AmE), / ˈʃeərə / (BrE)
a person who gives, receives, or has a share of something with others
a person participating in sharing

Examples

As a sharer of the prize, she received an equal amount as the other winners.
He was not only a hardworking employee but also a generous sharer of his ideas.
Social media platforms rely on every active sharer to circulate content widely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shareholder - a person who owns shares in a company

Etymology

From the verb 'share' + '-er', forming agent nouns.
