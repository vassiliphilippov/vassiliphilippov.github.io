iridoncus

1. NOUN

/ ɪrɪˈdɑŋkəs / (AmE), / ɪrɪˈdɒŋkəs / (BrE)
a swelling of the iris of the eye
swelling of the iris
medical

Examples

The ophthalmologist diagnosed an iridoncus after observing the swelling in the patient's iris.
Treatment of iridoncus may involve anti-inflammatory medication.
An iridoncus is a rare but serious eye condition requiring prompt attention.

Etymology

From Greek iris (rainbow, iris of the eye) + onkos (mass, tumor).
