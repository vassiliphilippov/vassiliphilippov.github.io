hosanna

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈzænə / (AmE), / həʊˈzænə / (BrE)
an exclamation of praise or adoration, especially in a religious context
cry of praise or adoration

Examples

The congregation sang out a joyful hosanna during the service.
She raised her hands and shouted hosanna in gratitude.
Ancient hymns often include the word hosanna as a call to praise God.

Etymology

From Hebrew 'hoshiʿā-nna' meaning 'save, please'.
