aralia

1. NOUN

/ əˈreɪliə / (AmE), / əˈreɪlɪə / (BrE)
Any of various plants of the genus Aralia, often aromatic, with compound leaves and small umbel-like flowers.
a genus of aromatic plants with compound leaves

Examples

Several species of aralia are used as ornamental plants in gardens around the world.
The fragrant leaves of the aralia add texture and scent to the landscape.
Botanists discovered a rare variety of aralia in the remote mountains.

Etymology

From New Latin Aralia, genus name, probably of Canadian origin.
