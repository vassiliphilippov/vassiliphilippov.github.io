connubial

1. ADJ

/ kəˈnuːbiəl / (AmE), / kəˈnjuːbiəl / (BrE)
relating to marriage or the relationship between a husband and wife; marital.
relating to marriage or spouses

Examples

Their ten years of connubial life were marked by love and understanding.
The novel explores the connubial struggles of a newlywed couple.
They enjoyed a quiet evening of connubial bliss at home.

Synset Examples

connubial bliss
conjugal visits

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conjugal - relating to marriage or the relationship between married people

Etymology

From Latin 'connubialis', from 'conubium' (marriage, right to marry).
