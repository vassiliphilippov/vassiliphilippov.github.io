pro-rape

1. ADJ

/ proʊ ˈreɪp / (AmE), / prəʊ ˈreɪp / (BrE)
Favouring or supporting rape (used derogatorily and offensively).
supporting or favouring rape (offensive)
offensive

Examples

The activist condemned the pro-rape rhetoric found on certain online forums.
There was public outrage over the pro-rape comments made by the official.
The organization takes a strong stance against any pro-rape attitudes or behavior.

Etymology

Formed from the prefix pro- (in favor of) and rape.
