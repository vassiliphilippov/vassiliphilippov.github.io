expeditiously

1. ADV

/ ˌɛk.spəˈdɪʃ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˌɛk.spɪˈdɪʃ.əs.li / (BrE)
In an efficient and prompt manner; quickly and effectively.
efficiently and promptly

Examples

The project was completed expeditiously thanks to careful planning and teamwork.
We expect the matter to be resolved expeditiously to minimize any disruption.
All necessary documents were processed expeditiously by the agency.

Synset Examples

he functions efficiently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: efficiently - in an efficient manner
  • Synonym: promptly - immediately or without delay

Etymology

From Latin expeditus (unimpeded, swift) + English adverbial suffix -ly.
