moderate-size

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑdərət saɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɒdərət saɪz / (BrE)
Of intermediate size; neither small nor large.
intermediate in size

Examples

They bought a moderate-size apartment in the city center.
A moderate-size suitcase should be enough for a weekend trip.
The meeting room can accommodate up to twenty people in a moderate-size group.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: small - of limited or below average size
  • Degree Higher: large - of above average or big size
