forefinger

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrˌfɪŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
the finger next to the thumb; the index finger.
the finger next to the thumb

Examples

She pointed at the map with her forefinger to show the location.
He accidentally cut his forefinger while chopping vegetables.
Wearing a ring on her forefinger was her signature style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: index finger - the first finger next to the thumb; synonym for 'forefinger'

Etymology

From fore- (meaning 'front') + finger.
