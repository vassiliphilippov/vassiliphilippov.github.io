Paramaribo

1. PROPN

/ ˌpɑːrəˈmɑːrɪboʊ / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈmɑːrɪbəʊ / (BrE)
The capital city and largest port of Suriname, located on the northern coast of South America.
capital city of Suriname

Examples

She visited Paramaribo during her trip to Suriname.
The Suriname River flows through the heart of Paramaribo.
Many colonial-era buildings can still be found in Paramaribo.

Etymology

From Arawak language, possibly meaning 'place of flowering trees'.
