licensed

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪsənst / (AmE), / ˈlaɪsənst / (BrE)
officially permitted or authorized by a relevant authority to perform a specified activity or role
officially permitted or authorized

Examples

Only a licensed electrician should attempt to rewire a house.
She was relieved to know the tour guide was fully licensed and knowledgeable.
You must be licensed to sell pharmaceuticals in this country.

Synset Examples

an accredited college
commissioned broker
licensed pharmacist
authorized representative

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certified - officially recognized as having a certain status or qualification
  • Antonym: unauthorized - not having official permission or approval

Etymology

From the verb 'license', from Old French 'licencier', from Latin 'licentia' (freedom, leave).
