Equus hemionus hemionus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛkwəs hiːˈmaɪ.ən.əs hiːˈmaɪ.ən.əs / (AmE), / ˈɛkwɪs hɪˈmʌɪ.ən.əs hɪˈmʌɪ.ən.əs / (BrE)
the subspecies of wild ass native to Mongolia and northern China, also known as the Mongolian wild ass
Mongolian wild ass subspecies

Examples

The Equus hemionus hemionus population has declined due to habitat loss and hunting.
Researchers tracked several Equus hemionus hemionus individuals across the Mongolian steppe.
Conservationists are working to protect the genetic diversity of Equus hemionus hemionus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onager - another common name for Equus hemionus, a closely related species/subspecies of wild ass

Etymology

From Latin: 'Equus' (horse), 'hemionus' (half-ass/half-horse).
