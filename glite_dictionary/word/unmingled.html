unmingled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmɪŋɡəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɪŋɡəld / (BrE)
not mixed with anything else; pure or undiluted.
not mixed; pure

Examples

The story was told with unmingled joy and pride.
She prefers her tea unmingled with milk or sugar.
It was a moment of unmingled happiness in his life.

Synset Examples

plain water
sheer wine
not an unmixed blessing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pure - free from any contamination; not mixed with anything else
  • Antonym: mixed - combined with other elements; not pure

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'mingled', past participle of 'mingle'.
