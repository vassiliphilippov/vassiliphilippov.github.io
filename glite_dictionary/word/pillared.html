pillared

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪlɚd / (AmE), / ˈpɪləd / (BrE)
having pillars or columns, often as a support or decorative feature
having pillars or columns

Examples

The ancient temple featured a pillared entrance that impressed all who visited.
They held the reception under a grand, pillared pavilion in the gardens.
Her home is known for its elegant pillared balcony overlooking the river.

Synset Examples

the pillared portico

Related Terms

  • Synonym: columned - having columns; furnished with columns

Etymology

From 'pillar' + '-ed', meaning 'provided with pillars'.
