lightheartedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌlaɪtˈhɑrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌlaɪtˈhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being cheerful and unconcerned; carefree disposition.
cheerful, carefree quality

Examples

Her lightheartedness made everyone at the party feel more relaxed and welcome.
Despite the stressful situation, he maintained a sense of lightheartedness that lifted the team's spirits.
The movie's lightheartedness provided much-needed relief after a long week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerfulness - the quality of being noticeably happy and optimistic
  • Antonym: seriousness - the quality of being solemn or important rather than cheerful

Etymology

Formed from 'lighthearted' + '-ness'. 'Lighthearted' from 'light' (not heavy) + 'heart'.
