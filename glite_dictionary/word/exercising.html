exercising

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛksɚˌsaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛksəˌsaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of engaging in physical movements or routines to improve or maintain fitness and health
activity to keep fit and healthy

Examples

Daily exercising can help reduce the risk of heart disease.
She found exercising in the morning gave her more energy throughout the day.
His doctor advised regular exercising to manage his stress levels.

Synset Examples

the doctor recommended regular exercise
he did some exercising
the physical exertion required by his work kept him fit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exercise - physical activity to improve fitness
