midweek

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪdˈwik / (AmE), / ˌmɪdˈwiːk / (BrE)
the middle part of the week, typically referring to Wednesday or the period around it
the middle of the week, usually Wednesday

Examples

Many people choose to take a short break midweek to recharge before the weekend.
The football team played a midweek match on Wednesday evening.
Prices are often cheaper midweek than during the busy weekend period.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: weekend - the end of the week, typically Saturday and Sunday

Etymology

Formed from 'mid-' (middle) + 'week'.
