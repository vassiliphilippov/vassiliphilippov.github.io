Mohammad Reza Pahlavi

1. PROPN

/ moʊˌhɑːməd ˌreɪzɑː pɑːˈlɑːvi / (AmE), / məˌhæməd ˌreɪzɑː pæˈlɑːvi / (BrE)
the last Shah of Iran, who ruled from 1941 until his overthrow during the Iranian Revolution in 1979
last Shah of Iran (ruled 1941–1979)

Examples

Mohammad Reza Pahlavi was forced into exile during the revolution in 1979.
Many historians debate the policies implemented by Mohammad Reza Pahlavi during his reign.
The palace of Mohammad Reza Pahlavi is now a museum open to the public.
