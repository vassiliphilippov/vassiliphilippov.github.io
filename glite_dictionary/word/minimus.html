minimus

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnɪməs / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪməs / (BrE)
the smallest finger or toe, especially the little finger or little toe
the little finger or little toe

Examples

A fracture of the minimus can be painful despite its small size.
She wore a ring on her minimus, drawing attention to her delicate hands.
The doctor examined his minimus after the accident to check for injuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: little finger - the smallest finger of the hand
  • Synonym: little toe - the smallest toe of the foot

Etymology

From Latin minimus (smallest).
