fibrocartilaginous

1. ADJ

/ ˌfaɪbroʊˌkɑrtlˈædʒɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌfʌɪbrəʊˌkɑːtɪˈladʒɪnəs / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of fibrocartilage, a type of cartilage containing fibrous bundles of collagen
relating to fibrocartilage
medical

Examples

The intervertebral discs are composed of fibrocartilaginous tissue that provides cushioning between the vertebrae.
A torn meniscus is a common fibrocartilaginous injury in the knee.
The doctor explained the fibrocartilaginous characteristics of the tissue found in the joint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cartilaginous - relating to cartilage

Etymology

From fibro- (relating to fibers) + cartilaginous (relating to cartilage), both from Latin roots.
