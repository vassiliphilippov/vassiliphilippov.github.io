Odesa

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈdɛsə / (AmE), / əʊˈdɛsə / (BrE)
a port city in southern Ukraine on the northwestern shore of the Black Sea
Ukrainian port city on the Black Sea

Examples

Many cruise ships make a stop at Odesa during their Black Sea journeys.
The architecture of Odesa reflects a blend of different European influences.
After moving to Odesa, she fell in love with the city's vibrant cultural scene.

Etymology

Ukrainian Одеса (Odesa), possibly from the ancient Greek colony Odessos.
