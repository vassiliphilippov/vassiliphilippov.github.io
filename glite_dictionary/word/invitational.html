invitational

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.vɪˈteɪ.ʃən.əl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.vɪˈteɪ.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an invitation, especially describing an event for which participation is by invitation only.
relating to an invitation or invitational event

Examples

The school will host an invitational basketball tournament next weekend.
Entry to the invitational gala is limited to those who receive personal invitations.
She received an invitational letter to participate in the international art exhibition.

Etymology

Formed from 'invitation' + '-al' (adjectival suffix).
