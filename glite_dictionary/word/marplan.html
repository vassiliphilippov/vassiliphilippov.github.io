Marplan

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrˌplæn / (AmE), / ˈmɑːˌplæn / (BrE)
A trademarked monoamine oxidase inhibitor used as an antidepressant in the treatment of clinical depression.
antidepressant medication (brand name)
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Marplan to help manage my depression when other medications were ineffective.
Some patients experience side effects when switching from another antidepressant to Marplan.
It is important not to combine Marplan with certain foods due to the risk of dangerous interactions.
