to sudate

1. VERB

/ ˈsuː.deɪt / (AmE), / ˈsjuː.deɪt / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) to produce or excrete sweat through the pores of the skin.
to excrete sweat through the skin
medical

Examples

During intense exercise, the body will sudate more to help regulate temperature.
Some medical conditions cause people to sudate excessively, even at rest.
After running a mile in the heat, she began to sudate heavily.

Synset Examples

Exercise makes one sweat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweat - to excrete perspiration through the skin

Etymology

From Latin sudare (to sweat).
