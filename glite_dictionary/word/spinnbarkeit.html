spinnbarkeit

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪn.bɑrˌkaɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɪn.baːˌkaɪt / (BrE)
The ability of a viscous liquid, particularly cervical mucus, to be stretched into a thread or drawn into a strand.
ability of mucus to stretch into a thread
medical

Examples

The spinnbarkeit of cervical mucus increases at ovulation, making it easier for sperm to swim through.
Doctors assess the spinnbarkeit of mucus as part of fertility evaluations.
Changes in the spinnbarkeit of cervical secretions can indicate hormonal fluctuations.

Etymology

From German 'Spinnbarkeit' (the ability to be spun), from 'spinnen' (to spin) and 'Bar' (ability).
