Martin Scorsese

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑrtɪn skɔrˈsɛzi / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtɪn skɔːˈseɪzi / (BrE)
(born 1942) An American film director, producer, screenwriter, and actor, widely regarded as one of the most influential filmmakers in the history of cinema.
famous American film director

Examples

Martin Scorsese directed several classic films including 'Taxi Driver' and 'Goodfellas'.
Many aspiring filmmakers cite Martin Scorsese as a major influence on their work.
The collaboration between Martin Scorsese and Robert De Niro produced some of cinema's most memorable characters.
