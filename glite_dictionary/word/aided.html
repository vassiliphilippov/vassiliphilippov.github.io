aided

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈeɪdɪd / (BrE)
Having received help or assistance, often used in combination with other words to form compounds.
having received assistance

Examples

The hikers reached the summit aided by professional guides.
Her aided recovery was much quicker than expected.
Many students complete their work aided by modern technology.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unaided - not having assistance
  • Synonym: assisted - having received assistance; supported

Etymology

From the verb 'aid', ultimately from Old French 'aidier' and Latin 'adiutare' (to help).
