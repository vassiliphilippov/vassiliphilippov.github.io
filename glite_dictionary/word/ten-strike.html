ten-strike

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnˌstraɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɛnˌstraɪk / (BrE)
a score in tenpin bowling achieved by knocking down all ten pins with the first ball in a frame
bowling score of all ten pins down on first ball

Examples

She cheered loudly when he got a ten-strike in the final frame.
Getting a ten-strike can change the outcome of a close bowling match.
After missing earlier, he managed a ten-strike to end his game on a high note.

Synset Examples

he finished with three strikes in the tenth frame

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strike - in bowling, knocking down all pins with the first ball
