vicinal

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɪsɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈvɪsɪnəl / (BrE)
Relating to or limited to a particular area or vicinity; neighboring or nearby.
of or relating to a vicinity; neighboring

Examples

The vicinal towns share many community resources.
This species is only found in the vicinal environment of the lake.
The vicinal landscape is characterized by gentle hills and small forests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: local - relating to or occurring in a particular area, city, or town
  • Antonym: distant - far away in space; not near

Etymology

From Latin 'vicinus' meaning 'neighboring'.
