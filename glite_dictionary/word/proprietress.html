proprietress

1. NOUN

/ prəˈpraɪətrɪs / (AmE), / prəˈprʌɪətrɪs / (BrE)
a woman who owns a business, property, or establishment, especially one such as a shop, hotel, or restaurant
female owner of a business or property

Examples

The proprietress greeted every guest personally as they entered the inn.
As proprietress of the bakery, she made it her mission to know all the regular customers by name.
The proprietress prides herself on running the oldest bookstore in town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proprietor - a person who owns a business or property

Etymology

Formed by adding the feminine suffix -ess to 'proprietor', from Latin 'proprietarius' (owner).
