sunrise

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌraɪz / (BrE)
The time in the morning when the sun appears or begins to rise above the horizon, marking the beginning of daylight.
the time when the sun appears in the morning

Examples

We woke up early to watch the sunrise over the mountains.
The colors at sunrise were breathtaking and filled the sky with pink and orange hues.
Every sunrise gives us a new opportunity to start fresh.

Synset Examples

we got up before dawn
they talked until morning

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sunset - the time in the evening when the sun disappears below the horizon
  • Synonym: dawn - the time just before sunrise when the sky begins to lighten

Etymology

Formed from 'sun' + 'rise'; attested in English since the 15th century.
