booked

1. ADJ

/ bʊkt / (AmE), / bʊkt / (BrE)
(of a place, ticket, etc.) reserved in advance so that it is not available for others.
reserved in advance

Examples

The hotel was fully booked for the holidays, so we had to look elsewhere.
I tried to get a table at the restaurant, but they were already booked for the evening.
All of the seats for the concert have been booked in advance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reserved - kept or set aside for use by a particular person or group
  • Antonym: available - able to be used or obtained; not reserved
