homosexuality

1. NOUN

/ ˌhoʊməˌsɛkʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməˌsɛkʃuˈæləti / (BrE)
sexual attraction to people of the same sex, or sexual activity between people of the same sex
sexual attraction to the same sex

Examples

In many societies, homosexuality is now more openly discussed and accepted than in previous generations.
He wrote a paper exploring the history of homosexuality in literature and art.
The organization works to promote equal rights regardless of one's homosexuality or heterosexuality.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heterosexuality - sexual attraction to people of the opposite sex
  • Synonym: bisexuality - sexual attraction to both men and women

Etymology

From Greek 'homo-' (same) + Latin 'sexualis' (sexual).
