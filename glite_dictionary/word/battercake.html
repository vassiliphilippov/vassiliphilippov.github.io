battercake

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɾər.keɪk / (AmE), / ˈbæt.ə.keɪk / (BrE)
A flat cake made from a thin batter and fried on both sides on a griddle, similar to a pancake.
flat cake of thin batter, fried

Examples

She served blueberry battercakes with maple syrup for breakfast.
It's easy to make battercakes with just flour, milk, and eggs.
The children enjoyed flipping battercakes on the hot griddle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pancake - a thin, flat cake made from batter and cooked on a griddle or frying pan
  • Degree Lower: crêpe - a very thin pancake of French origin
