MAT

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛmˌeɪˈti / (AmE), / ˌɛmˌeɪˈti / (BrE)
a Master of Arts in Teaching degree; a postgraduate academic degree in teaching.
Master of Arts in Teaching degree

Examples

She earned her MAT last year and now teaches high school biology.
Applicants with an MAT are preferred for teaching positions at this school.
After completing his MAT, Tom specialized in educational leadership.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MEd - Master of Education degree
  • Degree Lower: MA - Master of Arts degree (general)

Etymology

Acronym formed from the initial letters of 'Master of Arts in Teaching'.
