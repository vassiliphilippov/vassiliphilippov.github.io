mat

1. NOUN

/ mæt / (AmE), / mat / (BrE)
a mass of something tangled or densely interwoven, such as hair, weeds, or fibers
dense tangled mass of something

Examples

The cat's fur was covered in a thick mat after playing outside.
She struggled to comb out the mat of hair at the nape of her neck.
A dense mat of seaweed washed up on the shore after the storm.

Synset Examples
