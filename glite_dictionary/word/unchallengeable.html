unchallengeable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈtʃælɪndʒəbl / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈtʃælɪndʒəbl / (BrE)
impossible to question, dispute, or challenge; certain or definite
impossible to dispute or challenge

Examples

The evidence presented in court was considered unchallengeable by the defense.
He held an unchallengeable lead throughout the marathon, finishing far ahead of the others.
Her authority as head of the department is unchallengeable after years of successful leadership.

Synset Examples

unchallengeable facts
a position of unchallengeable supremacy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indisputable - impossible to doubt or question
  • Antonym: questionable - open to doubt or challenge

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'challengeable' (able to be challenged).
