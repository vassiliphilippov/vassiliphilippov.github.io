adoring

1. ADJ

/ əˈdɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈdɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
showing deep love, admiration, or affection for someone or something.
showing deep love or affection

Examples

She gave her child an adoring smile as he took his first steps.
The puppy looked up at its owner with adoring eyes.
Fans lined up outside the theater, waving signs and shouting in adoring support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admiring - showing admiration or respect
  • Antonym: indifferent - showing no interest or concern

2. ADJ

/ əˈdɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈdɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
showing deep love and admiration, often in a way that is affectionate or indulgent
showing deep love or admiration

Examples

She looked at her child with an adoring gaze every morning.
The puppy was surrounded by his adoring owners after the performance.
He wrote an adoring letter to his favorite author, expressing his admiration.

Synset Examples

adoring grandparents
deceiving her preoccupied and doting husband with a young captain
hopelessly spoiled by a fond mother

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doting - excessively fond or indulgent
  • Synonym: fond - having an affection or liking for

Etymology

From Latin 'adorare' (to worship), via Old French 'adorer'.
