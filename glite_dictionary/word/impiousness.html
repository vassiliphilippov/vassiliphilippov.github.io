impiousness

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈpaɪəsnəs / (AmE), / ɪmˈpaɪəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of lacking respect or reverence for a god or religion
lack of reverence for religion or god

Examples

The village elders condemned his impiousness during the sacred festival.
In some societies, public impiousness could result in serious punishment.
Her blatant impiousness shocked the conservative congregation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impiety - lack of religious respect or reverence
  • Antonym: piety - devotion and reverence to a god or religion

Etymology

From Latin impius (without reverence) + English -ness.
