Francis Drake

1. PROPN

/ ˈfræn.sɪs dreɪk / (AmE), / ˈfræn.sɪs dreɪk / (BrE)
(1540–1596) English explorer and admiral, the first Englishman to circumnavigate the globe and a leader in the defeat of the Spanish Armada.
English explorer and admiral (1540–1596)

Examples

Francis Drake is renowned for leading the second successful circumnavigation of the world.
The English fleet, commanded by Francis Drake, played a crucial role in defeating the Spanish Armada.
Many historians credit Francis Drake with expanding England's global influence during the Elizabethan era.
