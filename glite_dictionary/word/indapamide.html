indapamide

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdæpəˌmaɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈdapəmaɪd / (BrE)
A diuretic medication used to treat high blood pressure and fluid retention, often sold under the brand name Lozal.
a diuretic drug for hypertension
medical

Examples

After starting indapamide, her blood pressure decreased within a few weeks.
The doctor prescribed indapamide to help reduce fluid retention.
Some patients may experience dizziness when taking indapamide for hypertension.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diuretic - a drug that increases the excretion of urine
  • Synonym: hydrochlorothiazide - another diuretic drug used for hypertension
