mispickel

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪsˌpɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪsˌpɪkəl / (BrE)
A silver-white or grey mineral, also known as arsenopyrite, that is the principal ore of arsenic.
a mineral; ore of arsenic

Examples

The geologist identified the shiny sample as mispickel during the mineral survey.
Large deposits of mispickel can be found in some sulfide ore mining regions.
Handling mispickel requires caution because it contains arsenic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arsenopyrite - a mineral, an ore of arsenic, also called mispickel

Etymology

From German 'Mißpickel', likely from Middle High German 'mise' (fault) + 'pickel' (pickaxe).
