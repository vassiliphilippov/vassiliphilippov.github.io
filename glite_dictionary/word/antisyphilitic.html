antisyphilitic

1. NOUN

/ ˌæntiˌsɪfəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌsɪfɪˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
a drug or chemical agent effective in treating syphilis
drug effective against syphilis
medical

Examples

Penicillin is considered the most effective antisyphilitic available today.
Early antisyphilitic treatments often had severe side effects.
The doctor prescribed an antisyphilitic to combat the infection.

Etymology

From anti- (against) + syphilitic 'of or relating to syphilis'.
