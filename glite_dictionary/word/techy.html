techy

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛki / (AmE), / ˈtɛki / (BrE)
(of a person) easily irritated or annoyed; touchy or irritable.
easily irritated or annoyed
archaic

Examples

After a long day at work, he became rather techy whenever someone interrupted him.
She apologized for her techy remarks earlier in the meeting.
The children grew techy as the wait dragged on.

Synset Examples

an incorrigibly fractious young man
not the least nettlesome of his countrymen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tetchy - easily annoyed or irritated
  • Synonym: irritable - quickly annoyed; easily angered
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or anger

Etymology

Probably a variant of 'tetchy,' from Middle English tecche (fault, stain).
