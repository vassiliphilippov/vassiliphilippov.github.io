unassigned

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈsaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈsaɪnd / (BrE)
not given a specific task, role, or place; not allocated or assigned.
not given a task or role

Examples

Several unassigned seats were available at the back of the auditorium.
The manager kept a list of unassigned tasks for the new interns.
During the transition, some employees remained unassigned to any department.

Synset Examples

unassigned personnel

Related Terms

  • Antonym: assigned - allocated or given a specific role or task
