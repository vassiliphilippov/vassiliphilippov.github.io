wished-for

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪʃt fɔr / (AmE), / ˈwɪʃt fɔː / (BrE)
greatly desired or longed for; wanted very much.
greatly desired or longed for

Examples

After years of effort, she finally achieved her wished-for promotion.
The wished-for rain arrived just in time to save the crops.
He gazed at her with the hope of a wished-for reconciliation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desired - strongly wished for or wanted
  • Antonym: unwanted - not desired or wished for
