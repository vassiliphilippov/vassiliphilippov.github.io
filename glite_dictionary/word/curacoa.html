curacoa

1. NOUN

/ ˌkjʊrəˈkoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkjʊərəˈkaʊ / (BrE)
a liqueur flavored with sour orange peel, originally made in Curaçao
liqueur with sour orange flavor

Examples

He enjoyed a glass of curacoa after dinner.
The bartender used curacoa to add a citrus note to the cocktail.
She bought a bottle of curacoa as a gift for her friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curaçao - a liqueur made with orange peel, originally from Curaçao

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'curaçao', from the island of Curaçao, where the liqueur originated.
