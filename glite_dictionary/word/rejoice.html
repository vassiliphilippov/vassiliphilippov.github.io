to rejoice

1. VERB

/ rɪˈdʒɔɪs / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɔɪs / (BrE)
to feel or show great joy or delight; to be extremely happy or glad
feel or express great joy

Examples

Fans rejoiced when their team finally won the championship after years of disappointment.
She rejoiced at the news of her friend's recovery.
The community rejoiced together during the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to celebrate - to show happiness about something special
  • Antonym: lament - to express sadness or grief

Etymology

From Old French 'rejoir', from Latin 'gaudere' (to be glad).
