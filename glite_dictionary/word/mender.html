mender

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛndər / (AmE), / ˈmɛndə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to mend or repair things, especially clothing or equipment
person who repairs things

Examples

The mender quickly fixed the torn seam in my jacket.
They called a shoe mender to repair the damaged heels.
In the village, the bicycle mender was known for his skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repairer - a person who fixes or restores things
  • Antonym: breaker - a person or thing that breaks something

Etymology

From the verb 'mend' (to repair), which originates from Old French 'amender'.
