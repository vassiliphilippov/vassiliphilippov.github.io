cryptanalytic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkrɪptəˈnælɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəˈnælɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the analysis of codes and ciphers, especially for the purpose of breaking them.
relating to codebreaking or cryptanalysis

Examples

The agency is renowned for its cryptanalytic expertise in breaking sophisticated codes.
She pursued a career in cryptanalytic research after graduating in mathematics.
During the war, several critical messages were intercepted and subjected to cryptanalytic examination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cryptanalysis - the study of analyzing or breaking codes and ciphers
  • Antonym: cryptographic - relating to the creation or use of codes and ciphers

Etymology

From 'cryptanalysis', formed by combining 'crypto-' (hidden) and 'analysis'.
