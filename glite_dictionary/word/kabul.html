Kabul

1. PROPN

/ kəˈbuːl / (AmE), / kəˈbʊl / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Afghanistan, situated in the eastern part of the country
capital city of Afghanistan

Examples

Many international organizations operate from Kabul despite the security challenges.
She spent two years living and working in Kabul.
The government held an important summit in Kabul last month.

Etymology

From Persian کابل (Kābol), the name of the city.
