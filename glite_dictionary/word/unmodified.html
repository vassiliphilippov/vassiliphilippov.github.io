unmodified

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmɑdɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɒdɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not changed in form, character, or detail; remaining in its original state.
not changed; in original state

Examples

The car’s design remained unmodified for over a decade.
He submitted the document unmodified, exactly as he received it.
This software was used in its unmodified version during the trial.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: modified - changed in form or character

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + modify + -ed, meaning 'not modified'.
