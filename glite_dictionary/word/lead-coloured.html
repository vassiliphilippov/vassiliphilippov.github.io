lead-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛd ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈlɛd ˌkʌləd / (BrE)

dull gray, like lead
british

Examples

A lead-coloured sky signaled the arrival of the storm.

The old statue had turned a lead-coloured shade over the years.
