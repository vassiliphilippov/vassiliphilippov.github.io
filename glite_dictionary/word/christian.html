Christian

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɪstʃən / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstʃən / (BrE)
Relating to Christianity or its teachings.
relating to Christianity

Examples

The wedding ceremony included several Christian rituals and prayers.
He attended a Christian school for most of his childhood education.
Many Christian holidays are celebrated throughout the year.

Synset Examples

Christian rites

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christian - a person who follows Christianity
  • Antonym: secular - not connected with religion or spirituality

Etymology

From Latin 'Christianus', from Greek 'Christianos', from 'Christos' (Christ).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02963612-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.tʃən / (BrE)
A person who believes in Jesus Christ and follows the religion based on his teachings.
follower of Christianity

Examples

She was raised as a Christian and goes to church every Sunday.
Early Christians often faced persecution for their faith.
He became a Christian after studying the teachings of Jesus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: believer - a person who has faith in a particular religion or idea
  • Antonym: nonbeliever - a person who does not believe in a particular religion or idea
  • Antonym: atheist - a person who does not believe in the existence of God

Etymology

From Greek 'Christianos' (follower of Christ), from 'Christos' (anointed one).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09697405-n
Synset POS: n

3. ADJ

/ ˈkrɪstʃən / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstʃən / (BrE)
Showing qualities, values, or behavior associated with the teachings or spirit of Jesus Christ.
relating to qualities or teachings of Jesus Christ

Examples

Her Christian charity toward strangers was admired by everyone.
They tried to act in a Christian manner even when it was difficult.
Forgiveness is often described as a truly Christian virtue.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unchristian - not showing qualities associated with Christ

Etymology

From Greek 'christianos' (follower of Christ), from 'Christos' (the anointed one).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00412086-a
Synset POS: a
