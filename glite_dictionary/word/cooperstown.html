Cooperstown

1. PROPN

/ ˈkuːpərzˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈkuːpəzˌtaʊn / (BrE)
A small town in central New York State, known as the site of the National Baseball Hall of Fame.
town in New York, site of Baseball Hall of Fame

Examples

Every summer, thousands of baseball fans travel to Cooperstown to visit the Hall of Fame.
She grew up in Cooperstown, surrounded by baseball legends and history.
The annual induction ceremony in Cooperstown attracts visitors from all over the world.
