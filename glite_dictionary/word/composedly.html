composedly

1. ADV

/ kəmˈpoʊzɪdli / (AmE), / kəmˈpəʊzɪdli / (BrE)
in a calm and self-possessed manner; showing control of one's emotions or actions
in a calm, self-possessed manner

Examples

She answered all the difficult questions composedly, without a hint of nervousness.
Despite the chaos in the room, he handled the situation composedly.
After hearing the disappointing news, she accepted it composedly and moved on.

Synset Examples

he announced the death of his father collectedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calmly - in a calm manner; without agitation
  • Antonym: nervously - in an anxious or agitated manner

Etymology

From 'composed' (calm, self-possessed) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
