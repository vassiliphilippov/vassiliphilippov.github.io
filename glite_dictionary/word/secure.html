secure

1. ADJ

/ sɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
protected against danger, risk, or loss; safe from threat or harm.
safe; free from danger or risk

Examples

This vault is one of the most secure places in the building.
The company wants to ensure its data is secure from cyberattacks.
She felt secure knowing the doors were locked and the alarm was set.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe - protected from harm or danger
  • Antonym: unsafe - not protected from danger or risk

Etymology

From Latin 'securus' (free from care), via Old French 'secur'.
