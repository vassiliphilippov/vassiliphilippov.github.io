overcredulous

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdʒələs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkrɛdjʊləs / (BrE)
Too willing to believe things; excessively credulous or gullible.
excessively credulous or gullible

Examples

Her overcredulous nature made her an easy target for online scams.
The overcredulous investor believed every promise of quick riches.
It is unwise to be overcredulous when evaluating extraordinary claims.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: credulous - too ready to believe things
  • Synonym: gullible - easily deceived or tricked

Etymology

From over- (excessively) + credulous, from Latin credulus (believing, trusting).
