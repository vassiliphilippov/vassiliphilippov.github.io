noninstitutionalized

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˌɪn.stɪˈtu.ʃə.nəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / nɒnˌɪn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃə.nəˌlaɪzd / (BrE)
Not committed to or living in an institution, such as a hospital, prison, or care facility.
not committed to an institution

Examples

The survey included only noninstitutionalized adults living in the community.
Programs were designed to help noninstitutionalized people with disabilities remain independent.
Most of the data refer to the noninstitutionalized population in urban areas.

Etymology

Formed from non- (not) + institutionalized (placed in an institution).
