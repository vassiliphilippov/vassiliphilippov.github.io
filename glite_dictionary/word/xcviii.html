xcviii

1. NUM

/ ɛks si vi aɪ aɪ / (AmE), / ɛks siː viː aɪ aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for 98
Roman numeral for 98

Examples

The monument was erected in the year xcviii according to Roman numerals.
Chapter xcviii of the book describes the final battle.
The inscription read simply: 'Legion xcviii'.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 98 - the cardinal number ninety-eight

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10), C (100), V (5), I (1); combined and arranged according to Roman numeral rules.
