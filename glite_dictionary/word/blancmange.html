blancmange

1. NOUN

/ bləˈmɑndʒ / (AmE), / bləˈmɒndʒ / (BrE)
a sweet, almond-flavored milk pudding thickened with gelatin or cornstarch, typically set in a mold and served as a dessert
sweet almond-flavored milk pudding dessert

Examples

She prepared a delicious blancmange for dessert at the family dinner.
The soft texture of blancmange makes it a favorite treat for children.
At the party, everyone admired the beautifully molded blancmange on the buffet table.

Etymology

From French 'blanc-manger' (white food), from 'blanc' (white) and 'manger' (to eat).
