mouser

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊsər / (AmE), / ˈmaʊsə / (BrE)
a cat (or occasionally another animal) that is skilled at catching mice
cat that catches mice

Examples

The shop owners keep a mouser in the storeroom to help with the rodent problem.
Our barn has always had an excellent mouser to keep the mice away from the grain.
Many old manor houses relied on a resident mouser to prevent infestations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouse - small rodent, often considered a pest

Etymology

From mouse + -er, indicating an animal that catches mice.
