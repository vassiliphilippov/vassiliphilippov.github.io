unfortunately

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈfɔrtʃənətli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɔːtʃənətli / (BrE)
Used to express that something has happened by bad luck or is regrettable.
by bad luck; regrettably

Examples

Unfortunately, our flight was delayed due to the storm.
Unfortunately, I left my keys at home this morning.
Unfortunately, the restaurant was already closed when we arrived.

Synset Examples

unfortunately it rained all day
alas, I cannot stay

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regrettably - used to express regret, similar in meaning to 'unfortunately'

Etymology

From 'unfortunate' + '-ly', with 'unfortunate' from Late Latin *infortunatus* (unlucky).
