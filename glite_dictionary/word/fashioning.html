fashioning

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfæʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
the action of shaping or making something, especially by hand or through a particular process
the act of shaping or making

Examples

The fashioning of the sculpture took several weeks of careful work.
Her interests include the fashioning of handmade jewelry.
The fashioning of new policies is a complex process.

Synset Examples

the devising of plans
the fashioning of pots and pans
the making of measurements
it was already in the making

Related Terms

  • Synonym: making - the act of forming or producing something
  • Synonym: creation - the act of bringing something into existence

Etymology

From the verb 'fashion' (to make, shape), ultimately from Old French 'façon' (appearance, form), from Latin 'factio' (a making, doing).
