habilimented

1. ADJ

/ həˈbɪləməntɪd / (AmE), / həˈbɪlɪmɛntɪd / (BrE)
dressed or clothed, especially in fine or distinctive attire; often used to describe someone in formal or special garments
dressed or clothed, often in fine attire
archaic

Examples

She arrived at the gala habilimented in a dazzling silver gown that caught everyone's attention.
The statues were habilimented in traditional ceremonial robes for the annual festival.
He always appeared habilimented in fine suits, perfectly tailored to his frame.

Synset Examples

crimson-robed Harvard professors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attired - dressed, especially in a particular way
  • Synonym: clothed - wearing clothes
  • Synonym: garbed - dressed in distinctive clothes

Etymology

From 'habiliment', from Old French 'habillement' (clothing), from 'habiler' (to equip) based on Latin 'habilis' (able, suitable).
