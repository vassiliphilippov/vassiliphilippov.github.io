testee

1. NOUN

/ tɛˈsti / (AmE), / tɛˈsti / (BrE)
a person who is being tested, especially in an examination or experiment
person undergoing a test or exam

Examples

During the experiment, each testee was asked to solve a series of puzzles.
The results for every testee must remain confidential according to the study guidelines.
Each testee waited nervously for their turn to be called into the examination room.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: examiner - person who conducts or administers a test

Etymology

Formed from 'test' + suffix '-ee' indicating the person to whom an action is done.

2. NOUN

/ tɛsˈti / (AmE), / tɛsˈtiː / (BrE)
a person who is taking a test or examination
person taking a test

Examples

The testee must follow all instructions given by the supervisor in the exam room.
Anxious and quiet, the testee waited for her name to be called.
Each testee receives a unique identification number for grading purposes.

Synset Examples

The testee seemed nervous before the big exam.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: examiner - person who administers a test or examination

Etymology

Formed from 'test' + '-ee', a suffix denoting someone who is the recipient or subject of an action.
