Andorra

1. PROPN

/ ænˈdɔːrə / (AmE), / ænˈdɔːrə / (BrE)
A small independent country located in the eastern Pyrenees mountains, between Spain and France.
small country between Spain and France

Examples

Andorra is known for its ski resorts and beautiful mountain scenery.
The capital of Andorra is Andorra la Vella.
We drove through Andorra on our way from Spain to France.

Etymology

Name of the country; likely from Old Basque 'andur' (shrub-covered land), but etymology is uncertain.
