Fergon

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝːɡɒn / (AmE), / ˈfɜːɡɒn / (BrE)
A trademarked preparation of ferrous gluconate, an iron supplement used to treat or prevent iron deficiency anemia.
iron supplement (trade name)
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended Fergon to increase her iron levels rapidly.
He experienced mild side effects after starting Fergon for his anemia.
Patients with chronic anemia might be prescribed Fergon as part of their treatment plan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ferrous gluconate - an iron compound used as a dietary supplement
