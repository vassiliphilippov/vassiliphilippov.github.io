Tereshkova

1. PROPN

/ təˌrɛʃˈkoʊvə / (AmE), / təˌrɛʃˈkəʊvə / (BrE)
Valentina Tereshkova (born 1937), Soviet cosmonaut and the first woman to travel into space.
first woman in space; Soviet cosmonaut

Examples

Tereshkova became a global icon after her historic spaceflight in 1963.
Many young women were inspired by Tereshkova's achievements in the Soviet space program.
The museum displayed memorabilia from Tereshkova's pioneering mission aboard Vostok 6.

Etymology

Russian surname; borne by Valentina Tereshkova, cosmonaut.
