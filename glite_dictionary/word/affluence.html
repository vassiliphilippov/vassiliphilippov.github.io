affluence

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.lu.əns / (AmE), / ˈæf.lu.əns / (BrE)
the state of having a great deal of money, wealth, or material abundance
abundant wealth or material prosperity

Examples

The city’s affluence is evident in its luxury shops and expensive restaurants.
After years of hard work, she finally enjoyed the affluence she had dreamed of.
Despite the country’s affluence, many citizens still live below the poverty line.

Synset Examples

they studied forerunners of richness or poverty
the richness all around unsettled him for he had expected to find poverty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wealth - an abundance of valuable possessions or money
  • Antonym: poverty - the state of being extremely poor

Etymology

From Latin affluentia (abundance, flow toward), from affluere (to flow to).
