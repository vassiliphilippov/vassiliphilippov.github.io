stotinka

1. NOUN

/ stoˈtɪŋkə / (AmE), / stɒˈtɪŋkə / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Bulgaria, equal to one hundredth of a lev.
Bulgarian currency unit, 1/100 lev

Examples

The price of the postcard was fifty stotinka.
She handed over a few stotinki to the cashier for the newspaper.
Coins of one stotinka are rarely used in daily transactions.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: lev - basic unit of currency in Bulgaria

Etymology

From Bulgarian 'стотинка' (stotinka), diminutive of 'сто' (sto) meaning 'hundred'.
