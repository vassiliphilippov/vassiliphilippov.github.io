Marrakech

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɛrəˈkɛʃ / (AmE), / ˌmærəˈkɛʃ / (BrE)
A city in western Morocco, known for its historical sites and as a major tourist destination.
city in western Morocco

Examples

I've always wanted to visit Marrakech during the famous arts festival.
The markets of Marrakech are vibrant and full of life.
Marrakech attracts tourists with its historic medina and lively atmosphere.

Etymology

From Arabic مراكش (Marrākush).
