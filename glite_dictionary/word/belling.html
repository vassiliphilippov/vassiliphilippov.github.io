belling

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
a noisy, mocking serenade given to a newly married couple, often by banging pans and kettles
noisy mock serenade for newlyweds

Examples

The villagers celebrated the wedding with a loud belling outside the bride and groom's home.
A traditional belling was held to welcome the newlyweds to the neighborhood.
Despite the late hour, the belling continued until the couple finally appeared at the window.
