unreached

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈritʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈriːtʃt / (BrE)
not reached; inaccessible or situated in a place that has not been attained or arrived at
not reached; inaccessible

Examples

The remote island remains unreached by modern transportation.
Many of the planet's deepest caves are still unreached by explorers.
The mountaintop stood unreached despite many attempts to climb it.

Synset Examples

an unapproachable chalet high in the mountains
an unreachable canyon
the unreachable stars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inaccessible - not able to be reached or entered
  • Antonym: reachable - able to be reached
