nudism

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːdɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈnjuːdɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice of going without clothes, especially in mixed social settings, as a lifestyle or philosophy
practice of social nudity

Examples

The resort is known for its acceptance of nudism and offers clothing-optional amenities.
He became interested in nudism as a way to promote body positivity and freedom.
Some beaches in Europe allow nudism during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naturism - the practice of social nudity, often emphasizing harmony with nature

Etymology

Formed from 'nude' + '-ism', after French nudisme.
