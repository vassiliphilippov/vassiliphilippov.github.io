mincingly

1. ADV

/ ˈmɪnsɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmɪnsɪŋli / (BrE)
in a delicate or affectedly dainty manner, especially in movement or speech
in a delicate or affected manner

Examples

She walked mincingly through the elegant ballroom, careful not to trip on her gown.
The cat stepped mincingly across the wet grass, avoiding puddles at all costs.
He spoke mincingly, his words exaggerated to amuse his friends.

Synset Examples

she stepped mincingly over the puddles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicately - in a delicate manner
  • Antonym: boldly - in a bold, confident manner

Etymology

From 'mincing' + '-ly', 'mincing' meaning affectedly dainty, from Middle English 'mincen' (to chop into small pieces).
