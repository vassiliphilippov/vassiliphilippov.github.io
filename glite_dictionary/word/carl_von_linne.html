Carl von Linne

1. PROPN

/ kɑrl vɑn lɪˈneɪ / (AmE), / kɑːl von lɪˈneɪ / (BrE)
(1707–1778) Swedish botanist known for establishing the modern system of biological nomenclature.
Swedish botanist; creator of binomial nomenclature

Examples

Carl von Linne's classification system revolutionized the naming of plants and animals worldwide.
The contributions of Carl von Linne continue to influence modern biology textbooks.
Many scientific names include abbreviations referencing Carl von Linne's work.

Etymology

Latinized as Carolus Linnaeus; from Swedish name Carl von Linné.
