Tabuk

1. PROPN

/ tɑˈbuːk / (AmE), / tɑːˈbuːk / (BrE)
a city in northwestern Saudi Arabia, known for its historical sites and as a regional administrative center
city in northwestern Saudi Arabia

Tabuk is famous for its ancient fortress and vibrant markets.
