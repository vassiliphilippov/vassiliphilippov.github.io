enlisting

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈlɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈlɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of obtaining recruits, especially for the military, or encouraging people to join a group, cause, or job
recruiting or signing people up

Examples

Enlisting of new soldiers increased dramatically after the nationwide campaign.
Her enlisting in the environmental group inspired many of her friends to join as well.
The company began an active enlisting of talented graduates from top universities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recruitment - the process of finding and enlisting new people for a job or organization
