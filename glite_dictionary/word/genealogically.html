genealogically

1. ADV

/ ˌdʒiːniəˈlɑːdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːniəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or based on family lineage or ancestry.
in a manner relating to genealogy or ancestry

Examples

The researchers traced the royal family genealogically back to the twelfth century.
The species were classified genealogically using ancient DNA.
He demonstrated his connection to the estate genealogically through records and birth certificates.

Synset Examples

he charted his family tree genealogically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genealogical - relating to family lineage or descent

Etymology

From 'genealogical' + '-ly', 'genealogical' from French généalogique, from généalogie (genealogy).
