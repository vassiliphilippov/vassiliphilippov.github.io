sociopathic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsoʊ.si.oʊˈpæθ.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌsəʊ.si.əˈpæθ.ɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of sociopathy or a sociopathic personality disorder, typically marked by a lack of regard for the rights or feelings of others.
relating to sociopathy or sociopaths

Examples

The detective believed the killer's sociopathic tendencies made him difficult to catch.
Her behavior was so sociopathic that she seemed incapable of empathy.
A sociopathic personality often manipulates others without feeling any guilt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antisocial - showing a lack of regard for the rights of others; contrary to the laws and customs of society

Etymology

From 'sociopathy', formed from Latin 'socius' (companion) + Greek 'pathos' (suffering, disease).
