Ayatollah Khomeini

1. PROPN

/ aɪəˌtoʊlə koʊˈmeɪni / (AmE), / aɪəˌtɒlə kəʊˈmeɪni / (BrE)
(1900–1989) Iranian religious leader and politician who led the 1979 Iranian Revolution and became the Supreme Leader of Iran.
Iranian leader of the 1979 Revolution

Examples

Ayatollah Khomeini became the Supreme Leader of Iran after the 1979 revolution.
Many historians study the impact of Ayatollah Khomeini on modern Iranian society.
The policies introduced by Ayatollah Khomeini drastically changed the country's political structure.

Etymology

Khomeini is from his place of origin, Khomein (Iranian city); 'Ayatollah' from Arabic 'āyat Allāh' (sign of God).
