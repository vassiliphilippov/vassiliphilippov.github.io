coattail

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtˌteɪl / (BrE)
the loose back flap of a coat, especially one of a formal or tailcoat, that hangs down below the waist
loose back flap of a coat

Examples

The butler's coattail brushed against the door as he left the room.
He stepped on his own coattail while hurrying across the stage in his formal suit.
The old photograph showed a gentleman with long coattails standing at a wedding.
