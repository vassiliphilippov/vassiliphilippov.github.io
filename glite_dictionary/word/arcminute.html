arcminute

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrkˌmɪnɪt / (AmE), / ˈɑːkˌmɪnɪt / (BrE)
A unit of angular measurement equal to one sixtieth (1⁄60) of a degree.
unit of angular measurement (1⁄60 degree)

Examples

The distance between the two stars is less than one arcminute apart.
Maps often indicate latitude and longitude in degrees, arcminutes, and arcseconds.
Each arcminute is further divided into sixty arcseconds.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: arcsecond - a unit of angular measurement equal to 1/60 of an arcminute
  • Degree Higher: degree - a unit of angular measurement equal to 1/360 of a full circle

Etymology

From Latin 'arcus' (arc) and 'minute' (a sixtieth part), analogous to 'minute' as a unit of time.
