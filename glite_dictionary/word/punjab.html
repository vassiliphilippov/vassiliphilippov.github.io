Punjab

1. PROPN

/ pʊnˈdʒɑb / (AmE), / pʊnˈdʒɑːb / (BrE)
a large historical region in northwestern India and northern Pakistan, known for its fertile land and distinct cultural heritage
historical region in South Asia

Examples

Many Sikhs trace their heritage back to Punjab, a region famous for its rich culture and history.
The Green Revolution had a major impact on agriculture in Punjab.
We studied the partition of Punjab in our modern history class.

Etymology

From Persian 'panj āb', meaning 'five waters', referring to the five rivers of the region.
