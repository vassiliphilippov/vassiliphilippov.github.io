Nevado Sajama

1. PROPN

/ neˈvɑːdoʊ səˈhɑːmə / (AmE), / neˈvɑːdəʊ səˈhɑːmə / (BrE)
the highest mountain in Bolivia, a peak in the Andes reaching 21,391 feet (6,542 meters)
the highest mountain in Bolivia

Examples

Many hikers travel to Bolivia to climb Nevado Sajama every year.
The snow-capped summit of Nevado Sajama dominates the landscape for miles around.
Scientists are studying the ancient forests found near the base of Nevado Sajama.

Etymology

From Spanish 'nevado' (snow-covered) and Aymara 'Sajama' (the local name for the mountain).
