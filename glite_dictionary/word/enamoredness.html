enamoredness

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈnæmɚrdnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈnæmədnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being deeply in love or extremely fond of someone or something
state of being in love or fond

Examples

His enamoredness with classical music was evident from his vast record collection.
She spoke of her enamoredness for art with sincere passion and admiration.
The movie captured the enamoredness of young lovers perfectly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enamored - deeply in love or fond of someone or something

Etymology

Formed from 'enamored' + '-ness', with 'enamored' from Old French 'enamourer'.
