buttonhook

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtənˌhʊk / (AmE), / ˈbʌt.ənˌhʊk / (BrE)
a small tool with a hook at one end, used to pull buttons through buttonholes
tool for fastening buttons

Examples

My grandmother used a buttonhook to fasten the tiny buttons on her dress.
The museum displayed a silver buttonhook from the Victorian era.
He carried a buttonhook in his pocket to help with his stiff shirt cuffs.
