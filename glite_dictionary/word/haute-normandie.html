Haute-Normandie

1. PROPN

/ oʊt nɔrˈmɑːndi / (AmE), / əʊt nɔːˈmӕndi / (BrE)
A former administrative region in northern France, comprising the upper part of Normandy.
former region of northern France

Examples

During our trip to France, we spent two days exploring Haute-Normandie and its scenic coastline.
The cuisine of Haute-Normandie is famous for its dairy products and apple cider.
In 2016, Haute-Normandie was merged with Basse-Normandie to form the new Normandy region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Normandy - historic region of northern France

Etymology

French, meaning 'Upper Normandy'.
