Clark Gable

1. PROPN

/ klɑrk ˈɡeɪbəl / (AmE), / klɑːk ˈɡeɪbəl / (BrE)
(1901–1960) an American film actor known for his leading roles in classic Hollywood films, especially 'Gone with the Wind'.
famous American film actor (1901–1960)

Examples

Clark Gable starred as Rhett Butler in the classic film 'Gone with the Wind'.
Many film historians regard Clark Gable as one of the greatest actors of Hollywood's golden era.
Posters of Clark Gable still decorate the walls of movie lovers around the world.
