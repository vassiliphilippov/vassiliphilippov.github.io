cafe

1. NOUN

/ kæˈfeɪ / (AmE), / kæˈfeɪ / (BrE)
A small restaurant where drinks and light meals or snacks are served, often in a casual or informal setting.
small informal restaurant or coffeehouse

Examples

We met at our favorite cafe for coffee and conversation.
The new cafe on the corner serves excellent pastries and tea.
After a long walk, they stopped by a small cafe to rest and enjoy a snack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffeehouse - an establishment where coffee and other refreshments are served
  • Degree Higher: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals

Etymology

From French 'café' (coffee, coffeehouse), ultimately from Turkish 'kahve' (coffee).
