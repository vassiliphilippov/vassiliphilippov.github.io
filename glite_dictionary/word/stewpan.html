stewpan

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈstjuːˌpæn / (BrE)
A deep saucepan with a lid, used for stewing food.
deep pan for stewing food

Examples

She placed the chopped vegetables into the stewpan to simmer slowly.
The recipe calls for a heavy stewpan with a tight-fitting lid.
He lifted the stewpan off the stove and served the rich, fragrant stew.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saucepan - a deep cooking pan, typically with a long handle and a lid

Etymology

stew + pan.
