Hamastan-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑːməsˌtænˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɑːməstænˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a region controlled by Hamas, especially in terms of political, social, or security conditions.
resembling a Hamas-controlled region

Examples

Some commentators fear parts of Gaza may become Hamastan-like if the current situation continues.
The city fell into a Hamastan-like state after the militant group seized control.
Observers described the atmosphere in the area as increasingly Hamastan-like in recent months.

Synset Examples

They joked about living in a Hamastan-like situation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Taliban-like - Resembling or characteristic of areas controlled by the Taliban

Etymology

Formed by analogy to country names ending in '-stan' (meaning 'land') with reference to Hamas, the Palestinian political and militant organization.
