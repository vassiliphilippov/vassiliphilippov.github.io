lapboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæpˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈlæp.bɔːd / (BrE)
a portable flat board used on the lap as a writing surface or makeshift desk
portable writing board used on the lap

Examples

He placed the lapboard across his knees and started to write a letter.
Many artists use a lapboard for sketching outdoors in the park.
She keeps a lapboard by the sofa to make working from home more comfortable.
