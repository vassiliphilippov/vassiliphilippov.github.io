imbiber

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈbaɪbɚ / (AmE), / ɪmˈbaɪbə / (BrE)
a person who drinks alcoholic beverages, especially in large amounts
person who drinks alcohol (often heavily)

Examples

As a known imbiber, he was always the life of the party.
The club was filled with happy imbibers enjoying the music and drinks.
The festival attracts every kind of imbiber, from wine enthusiasts to beer lovers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drinker - a person who drinks alcoholic beverages
  • Antonym: teetotaler - a person who abstains from drinking alcohol

Etymology

From Latin 'imbibere' (to drink in).
