Omdurman

1. PROPN

/ ɑmˈdʊrmɑn / (AmE), / ɒmˈdʊəmæn / (BrE)
a city in central Sudan, located on the White Nile opposite Khartoum; known for its historical and commercial significance.
city in Sudan, opposite Khartoum

Examples

The bustling markets of Omdurman attract visitors from all over Sudan.
After crossing the bridge from Khartoum, we arrived in Omdurman.
Omdurman played a key role in Sudan's nineteenth-century history.
