Dalmatia

1. PROPN

/ dælˈmeɪʃə / (AmE), / dælˈmeɪʃə / (BrE)
a historical region on the Adriatic coast of Croatia, known for its mountainous terrain and numerous islands.
historical region of Croatia on the Adriatic Sea

Examples

She spent her summer vacation exploring Dalmatia's beautiful coastline and ancient towns.
The cuisine of Dalmatia features fresh seafood and Mediterranean flavors.
Historical records reveal that Dalmatia was once a Roman province.

Etymology

From Latin Dalmatia, referring to the ancient Roman province in the area.
