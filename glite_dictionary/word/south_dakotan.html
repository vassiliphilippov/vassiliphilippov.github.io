South Dakotan

1. NOUN

/ saʊθ dəˈkoʊtən / (AmE), / saʊθ dəˈkəʊtən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of South Dakota, a state in the United States
person from South Dakota, US state

Examples

She is a proud South Dakotan who loves her home state.
Many South Dakotans enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking and fishing.
As a South Dakotan, he often attends the annual State Fair.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: North Dakotan - a native or resident of North Dakota, South Dakota's neighboring state
