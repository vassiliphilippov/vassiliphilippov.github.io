barometric

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɛrəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to atmospheric pressure or measured by a barometer
relating to atmospheric pressure

Examples

The storm was predicted due to a sudden drop in barometric pressure.
Changes in barometric readings can signal shifts in the weather.
He used a barometric sensor to monitor atmospheric conditions.

Synset Examples

barometric pressure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atmospheric - relating to the atmosphere

Etymology

From barometer + -ic (relating to), after French barométrique.
