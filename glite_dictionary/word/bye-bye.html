bye-bye

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪ ˌbaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ ˌbaɪ / (BrE)
an informal expression said when leaving or parting from someone; goodbye
informal expression of farewell
informal

Examples

It's time to go, so let's say bye-bye to everyone.
She gave her grandmother a hug and whispered bye-bye before leaving.
The toddler waved and called out bye-bye as his parents left the room.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - a conventional expression used at parting
  • Antonym: hello - a conventional greeting or expression of welcome

Etymology

Reduplication of 'bye', a shortening of 'goodbye'.
