Khachaturian

1. PROPN

/ kəˌhɑtʃəˈtʊriən / (AmE), / kəˌhatʃəˈtʊəriən / (BrE)
Aram Khachaturian (1903–1978), a Russian composer born in Armenia, known for romantic works influenced by folk music.
Russian-Armenian composer
