thin-skinned

1. ADJ

/ θɪn skɪnd / (AmE), / θɪn skɪnd / (BrE)
easily upset or offended by criticism or remarks; overly sensitive.
easily offended; overly sensitive

Examples

She became thin-skinned after years of harsh reviews from critics.
As a thin-skinned person, he finds it hard to ignore casual remarks.
If you're thin-skinned, social media can sometimes be overwhelming.

Synset Examples

He's too thin-skinned to handle criticism well.
Being thin-skinned, she often took offense easily.
A thin-skinned manager struggles with employee feedback.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: thick-skinned - not easily upset or offended; insensitive to criticism
  • Synonym: sensitive - easily affected or hurt by external factors, including criticism
