unchanged

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʃeɪndʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃeɪndʒd / (BrE)
not altered; remaining the same as before.
not changed; still the same

Examples

The basic design has remained unchanged for over a century.
Despite new management, the company's policies are largely unchanged.
Her feelings for him seemed unchanged by the years apart.

Synset Examples

the causes that produced them have remained unchanged

Related Terms

  • Antonym: changed - become different; altered

Etymology

From un- (not) + changed (past participle of change).

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʃeɪndʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃeɪndʒd / (BrE)
remaining in the same state, condition, or form; not altered
not altered; same as before

Examples

Despite the renovations, the building's exterior has remained unchanged for over a century.
His feelings for her have stayed unchanged over the years.
The document was returned unchanged after the review process.

Synset Examples

persisting unaltered through time

Related Terms

  • Antonym: changed - having become different; altered
  • Synonym: unaltered - not changed or modified

Etymology

From un- (not) + changed, past participle of 'change'.
