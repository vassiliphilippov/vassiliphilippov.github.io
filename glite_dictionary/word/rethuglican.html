Rethuglican

1. NOUN

/ ɹɪˈθʌɡ.lɪ.kən / (AmE), / ɹɪˈθʌɡ.lɪ.kən / (BrE)
(slang, derogatory) A pejorative term for a member or candidate of the Republican Party, implying thuggish behavior or attitudes.
derogatory term for a Republican
slang offensive american

Examples

Many critics accuse the Rethuglican candidate of resorting to dirty campaign tactics.
Social media was rife with memes mocking the latest Rethuglican proposal.
He refused to vote for any Rethuglican after the recent scandals.

Synset Examples

The Rethuglican candidates are campaigning hard this election season.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Republican - Member of the Republican Party

Etymology

Blend of 'Republican' and 'thug'.
