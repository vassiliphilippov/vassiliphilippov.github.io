evasively

1. ADV

/ ɪˈveɪsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪˈveɪsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that avoids giving a direct answer or admitting the truth; in an evasive manner
in an evasive manner

Examples

He answered the journalist's question evasively, never quite addressing the real issue.
When pressed for details, she responded evasively to avoid revealing too much.
The politician spoke evasively about the allegations during the televised interview.

Synset Examples

her husband seemed to know many of the people who were named, but he replied evasively when asked who they were

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evasive - tending to avoid commitment or self-revelation, especially by responding only indirectly
