partsong

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrtˌsɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːt.sɒŋ / (BrE)
A song written for two or more vocal parts, typically intended to be sung by a group without accompaniment.
song for multiple vocal parts

Examples

The choir performed a beautiful partsong during the concert.
This partsong requires each section to sing a different melody.
She enjoys arranging traditional folk tunes as a partsong for her vocal ensemble.

Etymology

From 'part' + 'song', indicating a song in several parts.
