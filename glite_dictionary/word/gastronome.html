gastronome

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæstrəˌnoʊm / (AmE), / ˈɡæstrənəʊm / (BrE)
a person who enjoys and appreciates fine food and drink; a gourmet
person keen on fine food and drink

Examples

As a true gastronome, she seeks out the finest restaurants wherever she travels.
The magazine featured an interview with a renowned gastronome from Paris.
Although he’s a gastronome, he still enjoys a simple meal from time to time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gourmet - a person with refined taste in food and drink

Etymology

From French 'gastronome', from Greek 'gastēr' (stomach) + '-nomos' (arranger).
