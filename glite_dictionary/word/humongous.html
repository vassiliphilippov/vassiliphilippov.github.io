humongous

1. ADJ

/ hjuˈmʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / hjuːˈmʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
Extremely large; enormous.
very large; enormous
informal american

Examples

The new shopping mall is absolutely humongous compared to the old one.
He ordered a humongous pizza, big enough to feed a whole family.
A humongous wave crashed onto the beach during the storm.

Synset Examples

a thumping loss

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enormous - very large in size, quantity, or extent
  • Synonym: gigantic - very large; like a giant
  • Antonym: tiny - very small

Etymology

Originating in American English, possibly a blend of 'huge' and 'monstrous'.
