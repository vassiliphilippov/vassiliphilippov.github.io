cardigan

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrdɪɡən / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdɪɡən / (BrE)
a knitted jacket that opens at the front and is fastened with buttons or a zipper
knitted jacket open at the front

Examples

She wore a light cardigan over her dress because the evening was cool.
I found my favorite cardigan in the back of my closet.
He buttoned his cardigan before leaving the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweater - a knitted garment worn on the upper body
  • Synonym: jumper - a knitted garment worn over the upper body, typically pulled on over the head

Etymology

Named after James Brudenell, 7th Earl of Cardigan (1797–1868), a British army officer.
