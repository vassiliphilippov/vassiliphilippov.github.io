to permute

1. VERB

/ pərˈmjut / (AmE), / pəˈmjuːt / (BrE)
to change the order or arrangement of a set of things
change the order of

Examples

You can permute the letters of the word to create new words.
To solve the puzzle, simply permute the tiles until they are in order.
The program will randomly permute the sequence each time it runs.

Synset Examples

Dyslexics often transpose letters in a word

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to rearrange - to change the position or order of something
  • Synonym: shuffle - to mix up or change the order of things, especially cards

Etymology

From Latin 'permutare' (to change thoroughly).
