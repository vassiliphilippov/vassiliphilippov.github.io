loamless

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊmlɪs / (AmE), / ˈləʊmlɪs / (BrE)
(of soil or ground) lacking loam; not containing any loam.
not containing loam

Examples

The plants struggled to grow in the loamless garden soil.
A loamless mixture will not retain moisture well, making it poor for most crops.
He tested several plots and found most were loamless and stony.

Synset Examples

a stony loamless yard

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loamy - containing or resembling loam
  • Synonym: loam - a rich, fertile soil composed of sand, silt, and clay

Etymology

Formed from 'loam' + '-less' (without).
