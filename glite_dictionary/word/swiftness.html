swiftness

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪfnəs / (AmE), / ˈswɪfnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of moving or happening very quickly; rapidity or speed
quickness; rapidity; speed

Examples

The swiftness of her response caught everyone by surprise.
Modern trains are valued for their swiftness and comfort.
The cheetah's swiftness makes it the fastest land animal in the world.

Synset Examples

the project advanced with gratifying speed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speed - the rate at which someone or something moves or operates
  • Antonym: slowness - the quality of moving or happening slowly

Etymology

From Old English 'swift' (quick, fast) + '-ness' (noun-forming suffix).
