to jio

1. VERB

/ dʒioʊ / (AmE), / dʒiːəʊ / (BrE)
(informal, Singaporean/Malaysian English) To invite someone to join an activity, especially gaming or meeting up socially.
invite to join (activity); esp. gaming (Singapore/Malaysia)
informal

Examples

We always jio our friends on weekends to play video games together.
She jioed everyone from the group chat for a board game night last Friday.
Are you jioing anyone else, or is it just us meeting this weekend?

Synset Examples

They always jio on weekends for their gaming sessions.

Etymology

From Hokkien 招 (jiu), meaning 'to invite'.
