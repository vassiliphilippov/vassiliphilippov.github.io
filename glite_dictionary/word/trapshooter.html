trapshooter

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræpˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈtræpˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
A person who participates in the sport of trapshooting by shooting at clay targets launched into the air.
person who shoots at clay targets

Examples

He became an accomplished trapshooter after years of practice at the local club.
The trapshooter adjusted her stance carefully before each shot.
Several trapshooters competed in the state tournament last weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trapshooting - the sport of shooting at clay targets launched from a trap

Etymology

From 'trap' (referring to the device that launches targets) + 'shooter'.
