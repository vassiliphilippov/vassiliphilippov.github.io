numismatist

1. NOUN

/ nuˈmɪzmətɪst / (AmE), / njuˈmɪzmətɪst / (BrE)
a person who collects or studies coins, paper money, and medals
coin and money collector or scholar

Examples

As a dedicated numismatist, she spent weekends cataloging rare coins.
The museum hired a numismatist to appraise its collection of ancient currency.
Being a numismatist requires patience and a keen eye for detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coin collector - a person who collects coins as a hobby or for study
  • Synonym: philatelist - a collector or scholar of postage stamps

Etymology

From French numismatiste, from Latin numisma (coin, currency).
