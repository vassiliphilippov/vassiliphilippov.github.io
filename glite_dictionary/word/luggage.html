luggage

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌɡɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈlʌɡɪdʒ / (BrE)
Suitcases, bags, or other containers used for carrying a traveler's belongings.
suitcases and bags for travel

Examples

Please keep your luggage with you at all times in the airport.
Her luggage was lost on the flight to Paris.
He packed his luggage the night before the trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baggage - suitcases and bags used when traveling

Etymology

From Old French 'luggage', from 'luguer' (to drag).
