concise

1. ADJ

/ kənˈsaɪs / (AmE), / kənˈsaɪs / (BrE)
giving a lot of information clearly and in a few words; brief but comprehensive.
brief and to the point

Examples

Her concise summary made the report easy to understand.
The instructions were concise and left no room for confusion.
Please try to keep your explanation concise during the presentation.

Synset Examples

a concise explanation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: succinct - expressed in a clear and brief manner
  • Antonym: verbose - using more words than needed; wordy

Etymology

From Latin 'concisus' (cut short), from 'concidere' (to cut up).
