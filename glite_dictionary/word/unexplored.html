unexplored

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪkˈsplɔrd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈsplɔːd / (BrE)
Not yet discovered, investigated, or examined.
not yet discovered or investigated

Examples

There are still many unexplored areas in the Amazon rainforest.
The scientist dedicated his life to studying unexplored phenomena.
The film introduced viewers to the beauty of unexplored ocean depths.

Synset Examples

undiscovered islands

Related Terms

  • Antonym: explored - already discovered or investigated
  • Synonym: untried - not yet attempted or tested

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + explored (from Latin explorare, to investigate).
