Principe

1. PROPN

/ ˈprɪn.sə.peɪ / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.sɪ.peɪ / (BrE)
an island in the Gulf of Guinea, forming part of the country of São Tomé and Príncipe.
island in the Gulf of Guinea, Africa

Examples

The tropical forests of Principe are home to many unique species.
Tourists are drawn to Principe for its beautiful beaches and biodiversity.
She spent her vacation exploring the culture and scenery of Principe.

Etymology

From Portuguese, meaning 'prince'.
