Germanism

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɜːrməˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmənɪzəm / (BrE)
A custom, practice, or characteristic that is typical of Germany or its citizens.
custom typical of Germany

Examples

Some aspects of their local government show a clear Germanism in organization.
The festival featured several Germanisms, such as traditional Bavarian dancing and food.
His punctuality and orderliness are often cited as examples of Germanism.

Etymology

From German 'Germanismus', itself from Latin 'Germania' (Germany) + '-ism'.
