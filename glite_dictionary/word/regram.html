to regram

1. VERB

/ ˌriːˈɡræm / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɡræm / (BrE)
To repost a photo, video, or other content originally posted by another user on Instagram.
repost on Instagram
informal

Examples

She decided to regram her friend's inspiring quote to share it with all her followers.
If you regram this post, please remember to credit the original photographer.
I've seen that photo several times; people keep regramming it this week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repost - to post something again, especially on social media

Etymology

Blend of 're-' (again) and 'gram' from 'Instagram'.
