pinkish-purple

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪŋkɪʃ ˈpɝːpəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋkɪʃ ˈpɜːpəl / (BrE)
having a shade of purple that is tinged with pink.
shade of purple tinged with pink

Examples

Her dress was a delicate pinkish-purple that shimmered in the sunlight.
The sky turned a soft pinkish-purple at dusk, creating a beautiful backdrop.
He chose a pinkish-purple paint for the accent wall in his bedroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magenta - a purplish-red color
  • Degree Lower: lavender - a light purple color
  • Degree Higher: violet - a bluish-purple color
