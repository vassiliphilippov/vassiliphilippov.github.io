safeness

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfnəs / (AmE), / ˈseɪfnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being safe; freedom from danger or risk
state of being safe

Examples

The safeness of the new drug was verified through extensive clinical trials.
She always checked the safeness of the building before letting the workers in.
Opinions differ regarding the safeness of this neighborhood at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safety - the condition of being protected from harm or danger
  • Antonym: danger - the possibility of harm or injury

Etymology

From safe + -ness, forming a noun meaning the state of being safe.
