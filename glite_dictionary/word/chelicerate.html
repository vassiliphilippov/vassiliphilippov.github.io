chelicerate

1. ADJ

/ kəˈlɪsəˌreɪt / (AmE), / kɪˈlɪsɪrət / (BrE)
relating to or resembling chelicerae, the mouthparts found in arachnids and related animals.
relating to chelicerae (arachnid mouthparts)

Examples

All spiders are chelicerate animals with distinct mouthparts.
Scientists studied the fossil to determine whether it belonged to a chelicerate group.
Horseshoe crabs are considered primitive chelicerate arthropods.

Etymology

From New Latin *Chelicerata*, from Greek *chele* (claw) + *keras* (horn).
