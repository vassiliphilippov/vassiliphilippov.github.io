genus Zantedeschia

1. NOUN

/ ˌziː.nəˈdɛski.ə / (AmE), / ˌzan.tɪˈdɛski.ə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa, commonly known as calla lilies, valued for their showy, trumpet-shaped flowers.
genus of calla lilies (plants)

Examples

The genus Zantedeschia encompasses several species, including the popular white calla lily.
Botanists classified the new specimen under the genus Zantedeschia after careful examination.
Gardeners often plant members of the genus Zantedeschia for their striking floral displays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calla lily - a common name for plants in the genus Zantedeschia with large, showy flowers

Etymology

From New Latin, named after Italian botanist Giovanni Zantedeschi (1773–1846).
