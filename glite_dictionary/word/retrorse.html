retrorse

1. ADJ

/ ɹɪˈtrɔrs / (AmE), / ɹɪˈtrɔːs / (BrE)
(of a part or structure) bent or curved backward or downward.
bent or curved backward or downward

Examples

The seed capsule is covered with retrorse hairs that point downward.
Some plants have retrorse spines to help them cling to animal fur.
The biologist noted the presence of retrorse barbs on the leaves.

Synset Examples

leaves with retrorse barbs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recurved - curved backward; bent backward
  • Antonym: protrorse - bent or directed forward (opposite of retrorse)

Etymology

From Latin retrorsus (turned backward).
