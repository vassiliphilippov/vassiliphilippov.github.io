spray

1. NOUN

/ spreɪ / (AmE), / spreɪ / (BrE)
a collection or burst of small objects or drops flying through the air together
a burst of small objects or drops in the air

Examples

A spray of water droplets glistened in the sunlight.
The attacker fired a spray of bullets across the courtyard.
The children ran through the spray of foam at the parade.

Synset Examples

a spray of bullets

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shower - a brief fall of small things such as rain or bullets
