measure

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
a container of a standard size used to obtain a fixed amount of a substance, such as in cooking or measuring liquids
container for a standard quantity

Examples

Please pass me the measure so I can add exactly one cup of flour.
The bartender poured whiskey into a measure before adding it to the glass.
Each recipe calls for a level measure of sugar to ensure consistency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: measuring cup - container with marked amounts used for measuring ingredients

Etymology

From Old French mesure, from Latin mensura (act of measuring, standard).
