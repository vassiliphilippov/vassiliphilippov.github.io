medium-dry

1. ADJ

/ ˈmiːdiəm ˈdraɪ / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiəm ˈdraɪ / (BrE)
(of a wine or other beverage) dry but not extremely so; moderately dry in taste.
moderately dry (wine or beverage)

Examples

She prefers a medium-dry white wine with her seafood.
The restaurant offers both sweet and medium-dry varieties of sherry.
Their best-selling champagne is a medium-dry sparkling wine with a delicate finish.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: dry - wine or beverage with little or no sweetness
  • Antonym: medium-sweet - moderately sweet (wine or beverage)
