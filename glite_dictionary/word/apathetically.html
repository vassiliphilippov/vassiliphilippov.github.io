apathetically

1. ADV

/ ˌæpəˈθɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌapəˈθɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows or expresses little or no emotion, interest, or concern.
without emotion or enthusiasm

Examples

He listened apathetically to the news, barely reacting to the announcement.
During the meeting, she responded apathetically to every suggestion that was made.
After several setbacks, the team played apathetically, showing no energy or motivation.

Synset Examples

she behaves apathetically these days

Related Terms

  • Antonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval
  • Synonym: indifferently - in a way that shows lack of interest or concern

Etymology

From "apathetic" + "-ally"; "apathetic" from Greek apatheia (freedom from feeling).
