migrational

1. ADJ

/ maɪˈɡreɪʃənl / (AmE), / maɪˈɡreɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of migration, especially of animals or people
relating to migration

Examples

The migrational patterns of birds are studied by ornithologists across the world.
Climate change is affecting the migrational routes of many marine animals.
Researchers track the migrational behavior of fish in different river systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: migration - the act of moving from one place to another, especially regularly for breeding or work

Etymology

Formed from 'migration' + '-al', patterning after Latin-derived adjective forms.
