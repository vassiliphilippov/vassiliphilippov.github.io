ineffably

1. ADV

/ ɪˈnɛfəbli / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛfəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words.
to an inexpressible degree

Examples

The mountains looked ineffably beautiful in the early morning light.
She smiled ineffably, leaving everyone around her feeling uplifted.
His generosity was ineffably touching to the entire community.

Synset Examples

she was looking very young tonight, and, as usual, indescribably beautiful, in a simple strapless dress of a green and white silky cotton

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indescribably - in a way that cannot be described

Etymology

From Latin 'ineffabilis' (not able to be expressed).
