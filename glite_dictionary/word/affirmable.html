affirmable

1. ADJ

/ əˈfɝːməbl / (AmE), / əˈfɜːməbl / (BrE)
Able to be affirmed or declared as true.
able to be affirmed or stated as true

Examples

This is an affirmable statement based on the available evidence.
The rights mentioned in the constitution are affirmable in a court of law.
Her innocence is not affirmable without further investigation.

Synset Examples

a quality affirmable of every member of the family

Related Terms

  • Antonym: deniable - able to be denied or contradicted

Etymology

From Latin 'affirmare' (to affirm) + English suffix '-able'.
