arborary

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːrbərˌɛri / (AmE), / ˈɑːbərəri / (BrE)
relating to or formed by trees
relating to trees

Examples

The park was known for its arborary beauty, filled with ancient oaks and maples.
A striking feature of the old university was its arborary archways covered in vines.
Designers praised the arborary character of the garden, with its elegant tree-lined paths.

Synset Examples

an arborous roof

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arborous - relating to or resembling trees
  • Synonym: arborescent - resembling a tree; tree-like in growth or appearance

Etymology

From Latin arborarius, from arbor (tree).
