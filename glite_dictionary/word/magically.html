magically

1. ADV

/ ˈmædʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a magical or seemingly supernatural manner; as if by magic.
in a magical manner

Examples

The old castle magically appeared out of the mist as we approached.
The wound magically healed without a trace within hours.
She magically transformed the room with a few simple decorations.

Synset Examples

it disappeared magically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magical - having the qualities of magic or enchantment
  • Synonym: supernaturally - in a manner beyond what is natural or normal

Etymology

Formed from 'magical' + '-ly'.
