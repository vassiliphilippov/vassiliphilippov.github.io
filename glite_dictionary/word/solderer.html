solderer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːdərər / (AmE), / ˈsɒl.dər.ər / (BrE)
a person whose job is to join or repair metal parts by melting solder into the joint
person who joins or repairs with solder

Examples

The solderer carefully joined the two wires on the circuit board.
As a skilled solderer, she repairs small electronics for a living.
The factory employs several solderers to assemble their products.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welder - a person who joins metal pieces by welding
  • Synonym: solder - to join or mend with a low-melting alloy (verb)

Etymology

From the verb 'solder' (to join with solder) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
