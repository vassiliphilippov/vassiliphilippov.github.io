silverrod

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvərˌrɒd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌrɒd / (BrE)
A plant of eastern North America having creamy white flowers, belonging to the genus Solidago.
North American plant with white flowers

Examples

The silverrod blooms in late summer, brightening the forest edges with its white flowers.
We identified the silverrod by its creamy white flower clusters and slender green stems.
Many pollinators are attracted to the silverrod when it flowers in the fall.
