unelaborated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪˈlæbəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈlæb(ə)reɪtɪd / (BrE)
not fully developed or detailed; lacking in elaboration or completeness.
not fully developed or detailed

Examples

The article provided only an unelaborated outline of the events.
Her explanation was unelaborated, leaving many important details unanswered.
We received an unelaborated summary of the proposal during the meeting.

Synset Examples

a sketchy account
details of the plan remain sketchy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sketchy - not thorough or detailed

Etymology

From un- (not) + elaborated, past participle of elaborate.
