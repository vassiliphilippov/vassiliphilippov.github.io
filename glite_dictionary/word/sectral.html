Sectral

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛktrəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛktrəl / (BrE)
a brand name for an oral beta blocker (acebutolol) used in the treatment of high blood pressure and certain heart conditions
beta blocker medication (brand name)
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Sectral to help control my blood pressure.
After starting Sectral, she noticed her heart rate became more regular.
You should not stop taking Sectral suddenly without consulting your physician.
