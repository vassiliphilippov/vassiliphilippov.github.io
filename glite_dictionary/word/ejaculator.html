ejaculator

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈdʒækjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒækjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a man or male organism who ejaculates semen
a man who ejaculates semen

Examples

The doctor explained the physiological processes of the ejaculator during reproduction.
In some medical studies, the age of the ejaculator is recorded for data accuracy.
The research focused on hormonal factors influencing the ejaculator's reproductive health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ejaculate - to eject semen (of a male)

Etymology

From Latin 'ejaculatus', past participle of 'ejaculari' (to throw out).

/ ɪˈd͡ʒækjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈd͡ʒækjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who suddenly utters an exclamation; one who exclaims abruptly
person who utters a sudden exclamation
archaic

Examples

The poet was known as a passionate ejaculator of profound words during his performances.
She became an unwitting ejaculator when she shouted out during the lecture.
In the old novel, the character is described as an ejaculator because of his frequent sudden remarks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exclaimer - a person who exclaims

Etymology

From Latin 'ejaculatus' (thrown out), from 'ejaculari' (to throw out).
