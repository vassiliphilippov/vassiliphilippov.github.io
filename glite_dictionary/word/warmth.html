warmth

1. NOUN

/ wɔrmpθ / (AmE), / wɔːmθ / (BrE)
A quality of being friendly, kind, or showing affection toward others.
friendliness or affection

Examples

Her warmth and generosity made everyone feel instantly at home.
You could sense the warmth in his voice when he spoke about his family.
The teacher greeted each student with genuine warmth every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly; kind regard
  • Antonym: coldness - lack of friendliness or affection

Etymology

From Old English *wearmth*, from *wearm* (warm).
