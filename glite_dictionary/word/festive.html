festive

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɛstɪv / (AmE), / ˈfɛstɪv / (BrE)
cheerful and suitable for a celebration or holiday; offering fun and gaiety
cheerful, suitable for celebration

Examples

The house was decorated in a festive style for the holiday celebration.
Everyone wore bright clothes to create a more festive atmosphere at the party.
Street musicians added a festive touch to the city’s cultural festival.

Synset Examples

a festive (or festal) occasion
gay and exciting night life
a merry evening

Related Terms

  • Synonym: merry - cheerful and lively; full of fun and laughter
  • Synonym: celebratory - expressing happiness or pride about an event or special occasion
  • Antonym: solemn - very serious or formal in manner, often used to contrast with festive

Etymology

From French *festif* and Latin *festivus* (of a feast, festival).
