stonewort

1. NOUN

/ ˈstoʊnˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˈstəʊnˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a submerged aquatic plant of the genus Chara, with nodes bearing whorled, filamentous branches, often encrusted with calcium carbonate
submerged aquatic plant (Chara genus)

Examples

The pond was filled with stonewort, giving the water a greenish tint.
Researchers studied the growth patterns of stonewort in varying water conditions.
Some fish lay their eggs among the thick branches of stonewort.
