unoxygenated

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˌɑksɪdʒəˈneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˌɒksɪdʒɪˈneɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of blood) not supplied with or carrying oxygen.
not supplied with oxygen (blood)
medical

Examples

The right side of the heart receives unoxygenated blood from the body.
After traveling through the tissues, the blood becomes unoxygenated and returns to the heart.
The pulmonary artery carries unoxygenated blood to the lungs for oxygenation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: oxygenated - (of blood) supplied with or carrying oxygen

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + oxygenated (supplied with oxygen).
