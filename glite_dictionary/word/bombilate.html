to bombilate

1. VERB

/ ˈbɑːmbɪˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɒmbɪˌleɪt / (BrE)
(of an insect or similar) to make a buzzing or droning sound.
make a buzzing or droning sound

Examples

Every summer, bees bombilate in the garden as they search for nectar.
The faint sound of insects bombilating filled the hot, drowsy afternoon.
The air was heavy and still, disturbed only by flies bombilating near the fruit bowl.

Synset Examples

bees were buzzing around the hive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buzz - make a continuous low, humming or murmuring sound

Etymology

From Latin bombilare (to buzz), from bombus (a humming, buzzing).
