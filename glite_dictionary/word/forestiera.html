Forestiera

1. NOUN

/ fɔːˈrɛstiːərə / (AmE), / fɒˈrɛstiːərə / (BrE)
A genus of often spiny shrubs and small trees of the olive family, native to the Americas.
genus of spiny American shrubs and trees

Examples

The Forestiera genus thrives in various habitats across North and Central America.
Gardeners appreciate Forestiera species for their drought resistance and attractive appearance.
Many birds are attracted to the berries produced by Forestiera shrubs.

Etymology

Named after Italian botanist Basilio Forestieri.
