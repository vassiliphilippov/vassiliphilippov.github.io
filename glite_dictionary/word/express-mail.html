to express-mail

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈsprɛs ˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛs ˌmeɪl / (BrE)
to send something using express mail or a courier service for rapid delivery
send by express mail or courier

Examples

Please express-mail the signed agreement to our office as soon as possible.
She express-mailed her application documents to meet the deadline.
We are express-mailing the replacement part to ensure it arrives by tomorrow.

Synset Examples

Express-mail the documents immediately

Related Terms

  • Synonym: courier - a service or person that delivers messages or packages rapidly

Etymology

From 'express mail' (noun), itself from 'express' meaning 'fast' and 'mail' meaning 'postal delivery'.
