talentless

1. ADJ

/ ˈtæləntləs / (AmE), / ˈtalɪntləs / (BrE)
having no talent or special ability; not gifted or skilled
having no talent or special ability

Examples

Some critics accused the singer of being talentless, but her fans passionately disagreed.
The talentless performance left the audience unimpressed and bored.
He worried that others would see him as talentless despite his hard work.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: talented - having a natural ability or aptitude

Etymology

From 'talent' + '-less', where '-less' is a suffix meaning 'without'.
