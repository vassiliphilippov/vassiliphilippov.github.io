Ninhursag

1. PROPN

/ nɪnˈhɜːrˌsɑːɡ / (AmE), / nɪnˈhɜːsɑːɡ / (BrE)
a great mother goddess in Sumerian mythology, also known as Aruru, Mama, and Nintu
Sumerian mother goddess

Examples

In ancient myths, Ninhursag played a vital role as the mother of gods and humans.
Some clay tablets describe Ninhursag creating humans from clay to help the other gods.
The temple dedicated to Ninhursag was an important center for worship in Sumer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aruru - another name of the Sumerian mother goddess
  • Synonym: Nintu - another name for the Sumerian mother goddess Ninhursag
  • Synonym: Enlil - a chief deity of Sumerian mythology

Etymology

From Sumerian, combining *nin* (lady) and *ḫursag* (mountain), meaning 'Lady of the Mountain'.
