nonresidential

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑːnˌrɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌrɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl / (BrE)
not used as a residence or for living purposes; not residential
not used for living; not residential

Examples

The city is planning to expand its nonresidential districts to support new businesses.
Most nonresidential buildings are closed on weekends.
The university offers both residential and nonresidential programs for students.

Synset Examples

the commercial or nonresidential areas of a town
community colleges are typically nonresidential

Related Terms

  • Antonym: residential - used as a residence or for living purposes

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'non-' (not) + 'residential' (from residence).
