Thibet

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɪbɛt / (AmE), / ˈtɪbɛt / (BrE)
An older or variant spelling of 'Tibet', an autonomous region of China located in the Himalayas.
variant spelling of 'Tibet', Himalayan region

Examples

Older English texts often refer to the region as Thibet rather than Tibet.
Explorers in the 19th century described their journeys through Thibet in detail.
Historical maps sometimes use the name Thibet for what is now known as Tibet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tibet - an autonomous region of China in the Himalayas

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'Tibet', from Persian 'Tibbat' via European languages.
