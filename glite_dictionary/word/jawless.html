jawless

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɔləs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːləs / (BrE)
(of animals) having no jaw.
not having a jaw

Examples

The hagfish is a jawless fish found in cold ocean waters.
Scientists study jawless vertebrates to understand the evolution of jaws.
Lampreys are an ancient group of jawless animals.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: jawed - having a jaw

Etymology

From 'jaw' + '-less' (Old English '-lēas', lacking).
