dandified

1. ADJ

/ ˈdændɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈdændɪfaɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or their dress) affectedly elegant or fashionable in appearance or manner.
affectedly elegant or showy in dress

Examples

He walked into the room in his dandified outfit, attracting curious glances.
Her brother's dandified manners annoyed their more practical family members.
The novelist often mocked dandified characters who cared too much about style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandy - a man who places particular importance upon physical appearance, refined language, and leisurely hobbies

Etymology

From 'dandy', meaning a man unduly concerned with his appearance, with the suffix '-fied' meaning 'made to appear as'.
