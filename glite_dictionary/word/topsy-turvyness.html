topsy-turvyness

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɑpsi ˈtɜrvinəs / (AmE), / ˌtɒpsi ˈtɜːvinəs / (BrE)
A state of complete confusion or disorder.
state of confusion or disorder

Examples

The sudden announcement threw the whole office into a state of topsy-turvyness.
After the storm passed, the topsy-turvyness of the neighborhood was heartbreaking to witness.
The play humorously captured the topsy-turvyness of everyday family life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disorder - a state of confusion or lack of order
  • Antonym: orderliness - the quality of being orderly; lack of confusion

Etymology

Formed from 'topsy-turvy' (meaning inverted or in disorder) + the suffix '-ness'.
