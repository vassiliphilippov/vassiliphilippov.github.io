airfare

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌfɛr / (AmE), / ˈeəˌfeə / (BrE)
the cost or price of a ticket for traveling by airplane
cost of airplane ticket

Examples

The airfare to Tokyo was much higher during the holiday season.
She compared different airlines to find the cheapest airfare for her trip.
Many travelers book early to secure a lower airfare.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fare - the money paid for a journey on public transport

Etymology

Formed from 'air' (from 'airplane') + 'fare' (payment for travel).
