basely

1. ADV

/ ˈbeɪsli / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsli / (BrE)
In a despicable or morally low manner; in an ignoble or contemptible way.
in a despicable or ignoble manner

Examples

He acted basely in betraying his closest friends for personal gain.
The villain basely lied to cover up his crime.
She was accused of basely manipulating others to achieve her ambitions.

Synset Examples

this new leader meanly threatens the deepest values of our society

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignobly - in an ignoble or dishonorable manner
  • Antonym: nobly - in a noble or honorable manner

Etymology

From the adjective 'base' (morally low) + '-ly'.
