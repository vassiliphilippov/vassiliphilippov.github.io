head-spinning

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛdˌspɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌspɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
causing confusion or overwhelming feelings; making you feel as if things are spinning around you
confusing or overwhelming

Examples

The constant changes in company policy are truly head-spinning for the employees.
After reading the complex legal documents, he found himself in a head-spinning state of confusion.
The market's volatility has made investing decisions head-spinning for many newcomers.

Synset Examples

Her rapid-fire questions were head-spinning for him.
After the lecture, my thoughts were head-spinning.
The news was so shocking, it left me head-spinning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bewildering - causing confusion or puzzlement
  • Synonym: confusing - difficult to understand; bewildering
  • Antonym: clear - easy to understand; not confusing
