Carphophis amoenus

1. NOUN

/ kɑːrˈfoʊfɪs əˈmiːnəs / (AmE), / kɑːˈfɒfɪs əˈmiːnəs / (BrE)
a small, reddish, wormlike snake found in the eastern United States
small reddish wormlike snake (U.S.)

Examples

The Carphophis amoenus is often found under logs or rocks in moist woodlands.
Due to its secretive nature, the Carphophis amoenus is rarely seen by hikers.
Herpetologists study the Carphophis amoenus to learn more about its role in the ecosystem.

Etymology

From Greek 'karphos' (dry stalk) and 'ophis' (snake); 'amoenus' is Latin for 'pleasant.'
