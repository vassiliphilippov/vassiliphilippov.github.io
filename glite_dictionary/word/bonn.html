Bonn

1. PROPN

/ bɑn / (AmE), / bɒn / (BrE)
A city in western Germany on the Rhine River; former capital of West Germany from 1949 to 1989.
city in western Germany, former capital

Examples

They visited Bonn to explore its museums and historic sites.
During the Cold War, Bonn served as the capital of West Germany.
The conference will be held in Bonn next March.
