unconsolable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kənˈsoʊ.lə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈsəʊ.lə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a person) extremely sad and unable to be comforted; not able to be consoled.
unable to be comforted; extremely sad

Examples

After losing her beloved pet, she felt utterly unconsolable for weeks.
The child became unconsolable when his favorite toy was lost.
He was unconsolable after hearing the devastating news about his friend.

Synset Examples

inconsolable when her son died

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inconsolable - unable to be comforted; extremely sad

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'consolable', modeled after 'inconsolable'.
