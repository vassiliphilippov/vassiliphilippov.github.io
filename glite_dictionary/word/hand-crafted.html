hand-crafted

1. ADJ

/ ˈhændˌkræftɪd / (AmE), / ˈhændˌkrɑːftɪd / (BrE)
made by hand rather than by machine; produced using a manual process
made by hand, not machine

Examples

She bought a hand-crafted wooden bowl from the local market.
Their store specializes in hand-crafted jewelry and ceramics.
A hand-crafted quilt can take months to complete.

Synset Examples

delicate handmade baby dresses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handmade - made by hand rather than by machine

Etymology

Formed from 'hand' + past participle of 'craft', meaning made by hand.
