to singularise

1. VERB

/ ˈsɪŋɡjələrˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡjʊləˌaɪz / (BrE)
to make or treat as singular; to distinguish something as singular
to make or treat as singular
british

Examples

In this sentence, the verb form singularises the subject.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to singularize - to make or treat as singular (American spelling)

Etymology

From Latin singularis (single, unique), by analogy with 'singular' + '-ise' (British spelling of '-ize').
