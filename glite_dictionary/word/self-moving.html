self-moving

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf ˈmuːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ˈmuːvɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a device or machine) able to move or operate by itself without direct human control.
capable of moving by itself

Examples

The new warehouse uses self-moving robots to transport goods across the floor.
He was fascinated by the self-moving mechanism inside the antique clock.
The researchers developed a self-moving platform to test autonomous navigation technology.

Synset Examples

a self-activating sprinkler system

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automatic - (of a machine or device) working by itself with little or no direct human control
  • Antonym: manual - operated or done by hand rather than automatically
