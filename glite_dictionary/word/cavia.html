Cavia

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪ.vi.ə / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.vɪ.ə / (BrE)
A genus of rodents in the family Caviidae, including guinea pigs and related animals.
genus of rodents including guinea pigs

Examples

Scientists studied Cavia in order to understand the behavior of guinea pigs.
The domesticated guinea pig is a well-known member of the genus Cavia.
Fossils of ancient Cavia species have been discovered in South America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guinea pig - a small, tailless rodent often kept as a pet (belonging to genus Cavia)

Etymology

From Latin *cavia*, used in scientific taxonomy.
