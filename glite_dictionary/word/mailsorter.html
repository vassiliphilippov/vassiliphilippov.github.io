mailsorter

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪlˌsɔrtər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlˌsɔːtə / (BrE)
a machine or device used for sorting mail items according to their addresses
machine for sorting mail by address

Examples

The new mailsorter allows the post office to process letters much faster than before.
Each envelope passes through the mailsorter before being sent to its destination.
The technician repaired the broken mailsorter so that mail delivery could resume on schedule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sorter - a machine or person that sorts things
