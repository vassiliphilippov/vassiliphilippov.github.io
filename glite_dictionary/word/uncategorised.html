uncategorised

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkætəɡəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkatɪɡəraɪzd / (BrE)
not placed into a particular category or classification; not sorted according to type.
not categorized or sorted

Examples

Most of the files in this folder remain uncategorised and need to be sorted.
The report listed several uncategorised expenses that required further explanation.
She created a section for uncategorised books that didn't fit any existing genre.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: categorised - placed into a category or sorted by type

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'categorised', from Greek 'katēgoria' (category).
