acetic

1. ADJ

/ əˈsiːtɪk / (AmE), / əˈsiːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing acetic acid, especially with a sour or vinegary quality
relating to acetic acid

Examples

The laboratory prepared an acetic solution for the experiment.
She noticed an acetic smell coming from the bottle, reminiscent of vinegar.
Certain bacteria thrive in acetic environments, where acetic acid is present.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acetous - having the characteristics of vinegar or acetic acid

Etymology

From Latin acētum (vinegar), from acer (sour, sharp).
