Argun

1. PROPN

/ ɑrˈɡun / (AmE), / ɑːˈɡuːn / (BrE)
a river in eastern Asia that rises in China and flows northeast along the border between China and Russia, joining the Amur River
river in eastern Asia, China–Russia border

Examples

The Argun River forms part of the long border between China and Russia.
In spring, the melting ice causes the Argun to swell and flood nearby areas.
Many birds migrate along the Argun during their seasonal journeys.
