invigorated

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈvɪɡəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɪɡərətɪd / (BrE)
feeling filled with renewed strength, energy, or vitality
filled with renewed energy or strength

Examples

After a brisk walk in the fresh air, she felt invigorated and ready to face the day.
The team returned from their retreat invigorated and motivated to work on the new project.
He drank a strong cup of coffee and immediately felt invigorated by the caffeine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhilarated - very happy and animated; thrilled
  • Synonym: refreshed - feeling rested and energetic again
  • Antonym: weary - feeling or showing tiredness

Etymology

From 'invigorate', itself from Latin 'in-' (into) + 'vigor' (liveliness, force).
