dark-haired

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɑrkˌhɛrd / (AmE), / ˈdɑːkˌheəd / (BrE)
Having hair that is black or brown in color.
having black or brown hair

Examples

She has always been dark-haired, just like her father.
The main character in the novel is a tall, dark-haired detective.
At the party, a dark-haired woman caught everyone's attention with her laughter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brunette - a person, especially a woman, with dark brown hair
  • Antonym: blond - having fair or yellow hair
  • Antonym: light-haired - having light-colored hair

Examples

He was known for his charming smile and dark-haired good looks.
