Regina

1. PROPN

/ rəˈdʒaɪnə / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒiːnə / (BrE)
the capital city of the province of Saskatchewan, Canada
capital of Saskatchewan, Canada

Examples

I grew up in Regina, where winters can be harsh but beautiful.
Regina has a vibrant arts scene and several annual festivals.
The Royal Saskatchewan Museum is one of the top attractions in Regina.

Etymology

From Latin 'regina' meaning 'queen', named in honor of Queen Victoria.
