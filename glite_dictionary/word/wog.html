wog

1. NOUN

/ wɑɡ / (AmE), / wɒɡ / (BrE)
(offensive, slang) A highly offensive British slang term historically used by some to refer to people of color, especially from Africa or Asia.
offensive term for person of color
offensive slang british

Examples

The use of the word wog is considered highly offensive and unacceptable in modern society.
That film was criticized for including the term wog in its dialogue.
He was shocked to see the word wog written on the wall as an insult.

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from colonial slang, sometimes suggested as an acronym, but without reliable evidence.
