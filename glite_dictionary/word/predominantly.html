predominantly

1. ADV

/ prɪˈdɑːmɪnəntli / (AmE), / prɪˈdɒmɪnəntli / (BrE)
Mainly; for the most part; to a large extent or mostly.
mainly; for the most part

Examples

The area is predominantly rural, with only a few small towns scattered throughout.
Her work is predominantly focused on environmental issues in developing countries.
The audience was predominantly made up of young professionals.

Synset Examples

the patients are predominantly indigenous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mainly - for the most part; chiefly
  • Synonym: mostly - mainly; for the most part
  • Antonym: partially - in part; not totally

Etymology

Formed from 'predominant' (from Latin 'praedominari' – to rule over) + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
