Ateles

1. NOUN

/ əˈtiːlɛz / (AmE), / əˈtiːliːz / (BrE)
A genus of New World monkeys known as spider monkeys, found in Central and South America.
genus of spider monkeys

Examples

The genus Ateles includes several species of spider monkeys native to tropical forests.
Researchers observed the behavior of Ateles in the canopy of the Amazon rainforest.
Deforestation is threatening the habitat of many Ateles species across Central and South America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spider monkey - a New World monkey of the genus Ateles, with long limbs and tail

Etymology

From Greek atelēs (incomplete), referring to the reduced or absent thumb.
