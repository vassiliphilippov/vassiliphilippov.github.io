supremo

1. NOUN

/ suˈpriːmoʊ / (AmE), / suˈpriːməʊ / (BrE)
a person who holds the highest position of authority or importance in an organization or activity
most important person in an organization

Examples

He is regarded as the supremo of the country's football association.
The company supremo announced a major restructuring plan yesterday.
She rose rapidly through the ranks to become marketing supremo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chief - the person with the highest rank or authority in an organization
  • Synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group, organization, or country

Etymology

From Italian 'supremo' (supreme), from Latin 'supremus' (highest, supreme).
