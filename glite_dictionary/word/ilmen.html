Ilmen

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪlmɛn / (AmE), / ˈɪlmən / (BrE)
a lake in northwestern Russia that drains through the Volkhov River into Lake Ladoga.
lake in northwestern Russia

Examples

Lake Ilmen is known for its scenic beauty and historical significance in the Novgorod region.
The Volkhov River begins at Lake Ilmen and flows north into Lake Ladoga.
Researchers collected water samples from Ilmen as part of their environmental studies.
