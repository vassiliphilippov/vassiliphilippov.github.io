Cuprimine

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuːprɪˌmaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkjuːprɪˌmaɪn / (BrE)
a drug used to treat heavy metal poisoning, Wilson's disease, and severe rheumatoid arthritis
drug for Wilson's disease and metal poisoning
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Cuprimine to help manage the patient's Wilson's disease.
Some cases of severe arthritis respond well to Cuprimine treatment.
Because of its chelating properties, Cuprimine is used in cases of copper poisoning.
