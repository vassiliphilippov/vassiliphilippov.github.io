kaph

1. NOUN

/ kɑf / (AmE), / kɑːf / (BrE)
the eleventh letter of the Hebrew alphabet
11th letter of Hebrew alphabet

Examples

The word begins with the letter kaph in Hebrew script.
Hebrew students learn that kaph often represents a 'k' or 'kh' sound.
The scribe carefully inscribed the kaph at the start of the word.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caph - alternative spelling for the same Hebrew letter

Etymology

From Hebrew כ (kaph), meaning 'palm' (of the hand).
