aperiodic

1. ADJ

/ ˌeɪˌpɪr.iˈɑː.dɪk / (AmE), / ˌeɪˌpɪə.riˈɒd.ɪk / (BrE)
not occurring or recurring at regular intervals; lacking periodicity.
not recurring regularly

Examples

Some astronomical phenomena are aperiodic and cannot be predicted by simple calendar calculations.
The aperiodic signal in the experiment made it difficult to find any repeating patterns.
Her study focused on aperiodic tiling, which never repeats itself across a surface.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: periodic - occurring at regular intervals

Etymology

From Greek prefix a- (not) + periodikos (periodic).
