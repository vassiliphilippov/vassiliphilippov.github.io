thermodynamic

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɜːrmoʊdaɪˈnæmɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊdaɪˈnæmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with the science of thermodynamics, which deals with heat and its relation to energy and work.
relating to thermodynamics

Examples

The thermodynamic properties of the system were analyzed in detail by the researchers.
They discussed the thermodynamic efficiency of the new engine design.
Understanding the thermodynamic behavior of materials is crucial for chemical engineering.

Synset Examples

the thermodynamic limit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermal - relating to heat
  • Synonym: thermodynamical - alternative form: relating to thermodynamics

Etymology

From thermo- (relating to heat) + dynamic (relating to force or power), after thermodynamics.
