firedog

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚˌdɔg / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌdɒg / (BrE)
a metal support, usually one of a pair, used to hold logs in a fireplace; andiron.
metal support for fireplace logs

Examples

The antique firedog in the hearth added a decorative touch to the living room.
He placed the logs carefully on the firedog before lighting the fire.
They polished the brass firedogs every winter to keep them shining.

Synset Examples

the andirons were too hot to touch

Related Terms

  • Synonym: andirons - metal supports for holding logs in a fireplace
