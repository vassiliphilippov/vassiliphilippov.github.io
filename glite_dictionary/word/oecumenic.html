oecumenic

1. ADJ

/ ˌiːˌkjuːˈmɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌiːˌkjuːˈmɛnɪk / (BrE)
concerned with promoting unity among different Christian churches or religions; relating to ecumenism.
promoting unity among churches or religions

Examples

The conference adopted an oecumenic approach to discussions between faiths.
Her oecumenic efforts have been praised by leaders of various Christian denominations.
The city's churches organized an oecumenic service to celebrate community unity.

Synset Examples

ecumenical thinking
ecumenical activities
the ecumenical movement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecumenical - concerned with promoting unity among churches or religions

Etymology

From Greek oikoumenikos, relating to the inhabited world, via Latin and later adopted in English as a variant of 'ecumenical'.
