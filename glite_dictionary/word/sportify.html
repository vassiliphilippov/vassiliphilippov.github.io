to sportify

1. VERB

/ ˈspɔːr.tə.faɪ / (AmE), / ˈspɔː.tɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to make something more engaging, fun, or active, especially by adding sports or game-like elements
make more fun or active, add sport-like elements

Examples

The organizers decided to sportify the conference to boost participation and enthusiasm.
Teachers are finding creative ways to sportify their lessons, adding competitions and physical activities.
Why don’t we sportify our work breaks to make them more energizing?

Synset Examples

Let’s sportify our weekend plans with some games.
We need to sportify this event for more fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gamify - to apply game-like elements to something to increase engagement

Etymology

Formed by adding the suffix '-ify' (to make or become) to 'sport'.
