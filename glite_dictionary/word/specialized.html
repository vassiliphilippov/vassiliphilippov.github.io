specialized

1. ADJ

/ ˈspɛʃəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃ.əl.aɪzd / (BrE)
designed, developed, or trained for a particular purpose, activity, or area of knowledge
designed for a specific purpose

Examples

The doctor recommended a specialized treatment plan tailored to the patient's condition.
This museum has a specialized collection focused exclusively on medieval artifacts.
She uses a specialized software program designed for scientific research.

Synset Examples

a specialized tool

Related Terms

  • Antonym: general - not specialized; wider or broader in use or scope

Etymology

From the verb 'specialize', which comes from Late Latin *specializare* (to make special).
