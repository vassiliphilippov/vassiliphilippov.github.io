Tuskegee

1. PROPN

/ tʌsˈkiːɡi / (AmE), / tʌsˈkiːɡi / (BrE)
A town in eastern Alabama, known for its historically significant university and as the home of the Tuskegee Airmen.
town in eastern Alabama, USA
american

Examples

She decided to attend Tuskegee University, which is located in Tuskegee, Alabama.
The history of Tuskegee includes its important role in African American education.
Many visitors travel to Tuskegee each year to learn about its rich heritage.

Etymology

From the name of a Creek Native American tribe formerly in the area.
