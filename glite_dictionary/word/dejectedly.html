dejectedly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈdʒɛktɪdli / (AmE), / dɪˈdʒɛktɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows sadness or disappointment; in a dejected manner
in a sad or disappointed way

Examples

He walked dejectedly out of the office after hearing the news.
The players sat dejectedly on the bench after losing the game.
She sighed dejectedly when her plans fell through.

Synset Examples

when she came back Sophie and Esther were sitting dejectedly in the kitchen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sadly - in a sad manner
  • Antonym: cheerfully - in a cheerful manner

Etymology

From 'dejected' + '-ly'; 'dejected' from Latin 'deicere' (to throw down).
