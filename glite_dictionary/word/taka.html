taka

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑː.kə / (AmE), / ˈtɑː.kə / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Bangladesh, equal to 100 paisa
currency of Bangladesh

Examples

I exchanged my dollars for Bangladeshi taka at the airport.
The price of the book is 300 taka.
She sent 5,000 taka to her relatives in Dhaka.

Etymology

From Bengali টাকা (ṭākā), meaning 'coin' or 'money'.
