Egeria densa

1. NOUN

/ iˈdʒɪriə ˈdɛnsə / (AmE), / ɛˈdʒɪəriə ˈdɛnsə / (BrE)
an aquatic plant with deep green foliage, commonly used to oxygenate aquariums; sometimes classified in the genus Egeria.
aquatic plant used in aquariums

Examples

Many aquarists add Egeria densa to their tanks to improve water quality.
The fast growth of Egeria densa makes it ideal for beginners in the aquarium hobby.
Invasive populations of Egeria densa can sometimes disrupt local waterways.

Etymology

From scientific Latin: 'Egeria' (a genus named after a Roman nymph) and Latin 'densa' (dense).
