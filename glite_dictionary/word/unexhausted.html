unexhausted

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪɡˈzɔ.stɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪɡˈzɔː.stɪd / (BrE)
not used up completely; remaining available or not depleted.
not used up; still remaining

Examples

Despite years of mining, significant ore remained unexhausted in the area.
The committee allocated funds from the unexhausted balance of last year’s budget.
The well was still unexhausted after the summer drought.

Synset Examples

an unexhausted well

Related Terms

  • Antonym: exhausted - completely used up; depleted

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'exhausted' (depleted, used up).
