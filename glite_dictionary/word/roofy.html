roofy

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːfi / (AmE), / ˈruːfi / (BrE)
A slang term for flunitrazepam (Rohypnol), a powerful sedative sometimes used illicitly.
slang for flunitrazepam (Rohypnol)
slang

Examples

There are warnings about people slipping roofies into drinks at parties.
He was arrested for selling roofies at the nightclub.
The doctor explained the dangers of taking roofy or similar sedatives without prescription.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rohypnol - trademarked name for flunitrazepam, a powerful sedative

Etymology

Alteration of Rohypnol (brand name).
