picaninny

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪkəˈnɪni / (AmE), / ˌpɪkəˈnɪni / (BrE)
An extremely offensive term for a Black child, considered highly derogatory and racist.
offensive term for a Black child
offensive

Examples

The word picaninny is now recognized as an offensive racial slur and should not be used.
He came across the term picaninny in an old book and was shocked by its use.
Educational materials warn against the historical use of picaninny to describe children.

Etymology

From Portuguese *pequenino* (very small, tiny), diminutive of *pequeno* (small). The term spread via colonial contexts and came to be used as an offensive racial slur in English.
