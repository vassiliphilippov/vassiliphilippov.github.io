hindering

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɪndɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɪndərɪŋ / (BrE)
causing delay or obstruction; preventing movement, action, or progress
preventing movement or progress

Examples

The fallen tree became a hindering obstacle in the middle of the road.
She found his constant interruptions extremely hindering to her concentration.
Heavy snow can be hindering for travelers trying to reach their destinations.

Synset Examples

the clogging crowds of revelers overflowing into the street

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstructive - causing or constituting an obstruction; hindering
  • Antonym: helpful - giving or ready to give help (opposite of hindering)

Etymology

From hinder (to delay, impede) + -ing.
