squeaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈskwikɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskwiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
making or characterized by a high-pitched, often short or shrill, sound like that of a mouse or a rusty hinge.
making a high-pitched sound

Examples

The old wooden floor was squeaking with every step he took.
We could hear squeaking noises coming from the mice in the attic.
Her new shoes were squeaking loudly as she walked down the hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squeaky - making a high-pitched sound
