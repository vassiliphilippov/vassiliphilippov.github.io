citrous

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪtɹəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪtɹəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of plants of the genus Citrus
of or relating to citrus plants

Examples

The orchard specializes in citrous fruits like oranges and lemons.
A citrous scent filled the greenhouse from the blossoming trees.
This region is prone to several citrous diseases that affect orange crops.

Synset Examples

a citrous disease

Related Terms

  • Synonym: citrus - a genus of trees and shrubs including oranges, lemons, limes, etc.

Etymology

Formed from 'citrus' with the -ous adjectival suffix.
