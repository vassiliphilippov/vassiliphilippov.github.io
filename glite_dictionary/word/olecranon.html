olecranon

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈlɛkrəˌnɑn / (AmE), / əʊˈlɛkrənɒn / (BrE)
the bony projection of the ulna at the elbow, forming the outer bump and fitting into the humerus when the arm is extended
bony elbow projection (ulna)
medical

Examples

After falling from his bike, he fractured his olecranon and needed surgery.
The doctor examined the swelling over the patient's olecranon at the back of the elbow.
Pain near the olecranon may indicate bursitis or other injuries of the elbow joint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elbow - joint connecting the upper arm and forearm

Etymology

From Greek 'ōlekranon' (elbow, elbow tip).
