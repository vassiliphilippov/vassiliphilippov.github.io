beachball

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːtʃˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a large, lightweight ball, typically made of plastic, used for recreation at the beach or in pools
large lightweight ball for beach play

Examples

Children tossed the beachball back and forth along the shoreline.
A gust of wind sent the colorful beachball rolling down the sand.
We packed a large beachball for our trip to the seaside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ball - a spherical object used in games and sports
