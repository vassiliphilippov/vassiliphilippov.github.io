meaningful

1. ADJ

/ ˈmiː.nɪŋ.fəl / (AmE), / ˈmiː.nɪŋ.fəl / (BrE)
having a clear meaning or purpose; significant or important
having meaning or significance

Examples

Her volunteer work made her life feel truly meaningful.
They shared a meaningful conversation about their future together.
The gift had a meaningful message engraved on it.

Synset Examples

a meaningful explanation
a meaningful discussion
a meaningful pause

Related Terms

  • Synonym: significant - having importance or meaning
  • Antonym: meaningless - lacking meaning or significance

Etymology

From 'meaning' + '-ful' (suffix meaning 'full of').
