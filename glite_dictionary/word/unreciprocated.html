unreciprocated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnrɪˈsɪprəˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈsɪprəkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a feeling, action, or emotion) not returned or shared by the other person or party.
not returned or shared

Examples

She found it painful that her unreciprocated love was obvious to everyone but him.
His efforts to make friends remained unreciprocated despite his persistence.
Many songs have been written about the sorrow of unreciprocated feelings.

Synset Examples

unrequited (unanswered) love

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reciprocated - (of a feeling or action) given or felt in return
  • Synonym: unrequited - (especially of love) not returned by the other person

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'reciprocated', from Latin reciprocatus (moved back and forth, alternated).
