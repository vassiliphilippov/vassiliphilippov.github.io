hunt

1. NOUN

/ hʌnt / (AmE), / hʌnt / (BrE)
the activity of pursuing and killing or capturing wild animals, especially as a sport
sport of pursuing wild animals

Examples

The annual hunt attracts participants from all over the country.
She opposed the idea of a fox hunt in the neighboring woods.
During the hunt, they rode horses and followed the scent hounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chase - the act of pursuing something or someone
  • Synonym: pursuit - the action of following or chasing someone or something

Etymology

From Old English *hontan* (to hunt, chase).
