built

1. ADJ

/ bɪlt / (AmE), / bɪlt / (BrE)
(of soaps or cleaning agents) having an added substance, such as an abrasive or filler, to increase cleaning effectiveness.
containing additives for greater cleaning

Examples

This detergent is a built product, making it more effective on greasy stains.
Many built soaps contain phosphates to soften the water and enhance cleaning.
The label indicates that this is a built liquid detergent for heavy-duty washing.
