sourish

1. ADJ

/ ˈsaʊrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsaʊərɪʃ / (BrE)
somewhat sour in taste; slightly sour
slightly sour in taste

Examples

The yogurt had a sourish flavor that reminded me of fresh lemon.
She noticed a sourish tang in the sauce, making it more refreshing.
This apple is still sweet, but it also tastes a bit sourish.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sour - having a sharp, acidic taste
  • Antonym: sweet - having the pleasant taste characteristic of sugar or honey

Etymology

Formed with the suffix -ish (meaning 'somewhat' or 'like') added to 'sour'.
