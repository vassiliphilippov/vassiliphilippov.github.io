Melia

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːliə / (AmE), / ˈmiːliə / (BrE)
A genus of deciduous trees in the Meliaceae family, native to East India and Australia, with leaves resembling those of the ash tree.
genus of East Indian and Australian trees

Examples

The Melia species are known for their ornamental value in parks and gardens.
Botanists studied the leaves of Melia to compare them with those of ash trees.
Several Melia trees were planted along the avenue to provide shade.

Etymology

From Greek 'melia', meaning 'ash tree'.
