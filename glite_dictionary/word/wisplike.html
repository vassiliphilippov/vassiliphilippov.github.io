wisplike

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪsplaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɪsplaɪk / (BrE)
Thin, delicate, or weak in appearance or form, resembling a wisp.
thin and delicate, like a wisp

Examples

A wisplike figure appeared on the misty path at dawn.
Her wisplike hair fluttered in the breeze, barely touching her shoulders.
The cat's wisplike meow was barely audible from across the room.

Synset Examples

a wispy little fellow with small hands and feet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wispy - resembling wisps; thin, light, or delicate

Etymology

From 'wisp' (a thin or delicate piece or strand) + 'like'.
