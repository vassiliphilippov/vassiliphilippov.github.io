unpompous

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpɑːmpəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɒmpəs / (BrE)
Not affectedly grand, solemn, or self-important; lacking pomp or pretentiousness.
not pompous or pretentious

Examples

Despite his achievements, he remained remarkably unpompous and approachable to everyone.
Her unpompous style of leadership earned her the genuine respect of her team.
I appreciated the unpompous tone of the ceremony, which made everyone feel comfortable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pompous - affectedly and irritatingly grand, solemn, or self-important
