Munich

1. PROPN

/ ˈmjunɪk / (AmE), / ˈmjuːnɪk / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Bavaria, located in southern Germany
major city in southern Germany

Examples

I visited Munich last summer and enjoyed its famous beer gardens.
The annual Oktoberfest celebration in Munich attracts millions of visitors from around the world.
Munich is renowned for its art museums and historic architecture.

Etymology

From German München, meaning 'Home of the Monks' (from Old High German Munichen).
