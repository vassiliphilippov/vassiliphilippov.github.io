schnoz

1. NOUN

/ ʃnɑz / (AmE), / ʃnɒz / (BrE)
an informal term for the nose, especially a large or prominent one
informal term for nose
informal

Examples

He broke his schnoz playing basketball last week.
That's quite a schnoz you've got there!
She bumped her schnoz on the door and it turned bright red.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nose - the part of the face for smelling and breathing
  • Synonym: snout - the projecting nose and mouth of an animal

Etymology

From Yiddish 'shnoyts' (snout, nose), probably influenced by German 'Schnauze' (snout).
