dampener

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæmpənər / (AmE), / ˈdampənə / (BrE)
a device or tool used to make something damp or moist, especially before ironing or processing
device for making something damp

Examples

He used a dampener to moisten the shirts before ironing them.
The tennis player applied a dampener to reduce string vibration on her racket.
A small dampener is often used in the kitchen to add moisture to baked goods.

Synset Examples

he used a dampener to moisten the shirts before he ironed them

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sprayer - device used to spray liquid

Etymology

From 'dampen' (to make moist or wet) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
