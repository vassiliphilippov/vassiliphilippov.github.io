Dneprodzerzhinsk

1. PROPN

/ dnɛˌprodʒɛrˈʒɪnsk / (AmE), / dnɛˌprɒdʒɛˈʒɪnsk / (BrE)
A port city and industrial center in east-central Ukraine, located on the Dnieper River.
Ukrainian port city on the Dnieper

Examples

The steel industry has long been central to Dneprodzerzhinsk's economy.
After the war, Dneprodzerzhinsk became a major hub for industrial development.
Riverside parks in Dneprodzerzhinsk offer scenic views of the Dnieper.

Etymology

Named after Felix Dzerzhinsky, a Soviet statesman; from Russian Днепродзержинск, combining Dnepr (the river) and Dzerzhinsky.
