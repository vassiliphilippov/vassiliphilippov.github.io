Robert Browning

1. PROPN

/ ˈroʊbərt ˈbraʊnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɒbət ˈbraʊnɪŋ / (BrE)
(1812–1889) An English poet noted for his dramatic monologues and as the husband of Elizabeth Barrett Browning.
English poet (1812–1889)

Examples

Many consider Robert Browning to be one of the foremost Victorian poets.
The dramatic monologue became a signature style in the works of Robert Browning.
His marriage to Elizabeth Barrett inspired much of Robert Browning's poetry.
