semiformal

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛmiˈfɔrməl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈfɔːməl / (BrE)
moderately formal; suitable for events or attire that are less formal than black tie but more formal than casual.
moderately formal; between casual and formal

Examples

The invitation specified semiformal attire for the evening event.
He bought a new blazer and trousers to wear to the semiformal dance.
Guests are expected to dress in semiformal clothing for the awards ceremony.

Synset Examples

he wore semiformal attire
a black-tie dinner

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: formal - very proper or official in style or dress
  • Degree Lower: casual - relaxed or informal in style or attire

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'semi-' (meaning half or partly) and 'formal'.
