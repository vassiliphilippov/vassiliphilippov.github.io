Meticorten

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛtɪˈkɔːrtən / (AmE), / ˌmɛtɪˈkɔːtən / (BrE)
A trademarked name for prednisone, a synthetic corticosteroid drug used as an anti-inflammatory and immunosuppressant, especially in the treatment of arthritis.

The doctor prescribed Meticorten to help reduce the patient's inflammation.

Meticorten is often used to manage symptoms of autoimmune diseases.
