watermeal

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtərˌmil / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌmiːl / (BrE)
any of various tiny, free-floating aquatic plants of the genus Wolffia, found in warm regions worldwide
tiny aquatic plant of the genus Wolffia

Examples

The pond surface was covered with a layer of watermeal, giving it a bright green appearance.
Some fish feed on watermeal due to its high protein content and abundance.
Researchers studied the rapid growth of watermeal in tropical wetlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: duckweed - small free-floating aquatic plant of the family Lemnaceae
