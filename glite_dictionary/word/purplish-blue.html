purplish-blue

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝː.plɪʃ bluː / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.plɪʃ bluː / (BrE)
Blue tinged with purple; a color between blue and purple.
Blue with a purple tint

Examples

The evening sky took on a purplish-blue hue as the sun set.
She wore a purplish-blue dress that shimmered in the light.
Artists often mix paints to achieve the perfect purplish-blue shade for their landscapes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: violet - A bluish-purple color on the visible spectrum
  • Degree Lower: blue - The color of the clear sky; a primary color
