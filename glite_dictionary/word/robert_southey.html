Robert Southey

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɑbərt ˈsaʊði / (AmE), / ˈrɒbət ˈsaʊði / (BrE)
(1774–1843) English Romantic poet, noted for his literary works and association with Wordsworth and Coleridge.
English Romantic poet (1774–1843)

Examples

Robert Southey was Poet Laureate of the United Kingdom from 1813 until his death.
The works of Robert Southey are important contributions to English Romantic poetry.
Many students study the correspondence between Robert Southey and William Wordsworth.
