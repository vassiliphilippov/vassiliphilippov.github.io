newswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌwʊm.ən / (BrE)
a woman who investigates, reports, or edits news stories as a journalist
female journalist or news reporter

Examples

The newswoman interviewed witnesses at the scene of the accident for the evening broadcast.
As a respected newswoman, she received several awards for investigative reporting.
A veteran newswoman covered the presidential election results live from the campaign headquarters.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: newsman - a male journalist or news reporter
  • Synonym: journalist - a person who writes or reports news stories

Etymology

Formed from 'news' + 'woman', patterned after 'newsman'.
