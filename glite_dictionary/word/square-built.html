square-built

1. ADJ

/ skwɛr ˈbɪlt / (AmE), / skweə ˈbɪlt / (BrE)
broad and solidly built, often with a strong and sturdy appearance
broad and solidly built

Examples

The security guard was a square-built man with broad shoulders.
She admired the square-built horses used on the farm for their strength and sturdiness.
His square-built frame made him look strong even in an ordinary suit.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: slender - gracefully thin and narrow in build
