Budapest

1. PROPN

/ ˈbjuːdəˌpɛst / (AmE), / ˈbjuːdəpɛst / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Hungary, situated on the Danube River in the north-central part of the country
capital city of Hungary

Examples

We spent a week exploring the historic architecture and vibrant nightlife of Budapest.
The Danube River divides Budapest into two main parts: Buda and Pest.
Many tourists visit Budapest each year to relax in its famous thermal baths.

Etymology

From Hungarian 'Budapest', formed from the names Buda and Pest, two historical cities on opposite sides of the Danube.
