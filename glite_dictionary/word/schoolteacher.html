schoolteacher

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌtitʃər / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌtiːtʃə / (BrE)
A person who teaches students in a school below college or university level.
teacher in a school below college level

Examples

The schoolteacher explained the lesson with patience and clarity.
As a dedicated schoolteacher, she inspired many children to love reading.
The town held a ceremony to honor its retiring schoolteacher.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school
