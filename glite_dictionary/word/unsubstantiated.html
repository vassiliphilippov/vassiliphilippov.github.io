unsubstantiated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnsəbˈstænʃieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəbˈstænʃieɪtɪd / (BrE)
Not supported or proven by evidence.
not supported by evidence

Examples

The journalist was criticized for publishing unsubstantiated claims about the election.
Many rumors circulating online are completely unsubstantiated.
The report was dismissed because the allegations remained unsubstantiated.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: substantiated - supported by evidence

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'substantiated', from 'substantiate' (to support with proof).
