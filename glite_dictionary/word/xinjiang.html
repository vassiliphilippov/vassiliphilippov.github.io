Xinjiang

1. PROPN

/ ʃɪnˈdʒjɑŋ / (AmE), / ʃɪnˈdʒiːaŋ / (BrE)
an autonomous region in northwestern China, bordering Mongolia and Kazakhstan, known for being the homeland of the Uighur people
autonomous region of China in northwest

Examples

She spent a year studying language and culture in Xinjiang.
The region of Xinjiang is known for its diverse landscapes and ethnic groups.
Tensions in Xinjiang have drawn international attention in recent years.

Etymology

From Mandarin Chinese 新疆 (Xīnjiāng), meaning 'new frontier' or 'new territory'.
