skincare

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪnˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈskɪnˌkeə / (BrE)
the use of products or routines for cleaning, protecting, and improving the appearance of the skin
care and maintenance of the skin

Examples

Daily skincare routines can help prevent acne and dryness.
She invested in new skincare products to keep her complexion healthy.
Good skincare starts with regular cleansing and moisturizing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skin care - care for the skin; synonymous with 'skincare'
  • Antonym: haircare - care and maintenance of the hair
