Monegasque

1. NOUN

/ ˌmoʊnɪˈgæsk / (AmE), / ˌmɒnɪˈgæsk / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Monaco
a native or resident of Monaco

Examples

The famous Grand Prix attracts many Monegasques and international visitors each year.
She became friends with a Monegasque during her travels in Europe.
Many Monegasques are multilingual, speaking French, Italian, and English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Monacan - a native or inhabitant of Monaco (alternative form)

Etymology

From French 'Monégasque', relating to Monaco.
