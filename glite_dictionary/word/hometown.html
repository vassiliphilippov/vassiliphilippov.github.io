hometown

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌtaʊn / (BrE)
the town or city where someone was born, grew up, or has lived for a long time; one’s place of origin.
one's town or city of origin

Examples

After years abroad, she finally returned to her hometown to visit her family.
The festival attracted people back to their hometown from all over the country.
He often speaks fondly of his small hometown by the river.

Synset Examples

he never went back to his hometown again

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birthplace - the place where someone was born

Etymology

From 'home' + 'town', first used in the United States in the mid-19th century.
