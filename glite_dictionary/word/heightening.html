heightening

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪtənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪtənɪŋ / (BrE)
an increase in intensity, degree, or effect of something
an increase in intensity or effect

Examples

There was a heightening of tensions between the two countries before the negotiations began.
A heightening awareness of environmental issues is shaping policy decisions worldwide.
The film successfully achieves a heightening of suspense as the story unfolds.

Synset Examples

their heightening fears

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensification - the process of becoming more intense
