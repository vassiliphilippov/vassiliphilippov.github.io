bookshelf

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊk.ʃɛlf / (AmE), / ˈbʊk.ʃɛlf / (BrE)
A shelf, typically fitted to a wall or as part of a bookcase, used for storing books.
a shelf for storing books

Examples

She placed her favorite novels on the top bookshelf in her room.
The new bookshelf matched the color of his desk perfectly.
After moving, we realized we needed another bookshelf for all our cookbooks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bookcase - a piece of furniture with shelves for holding books

Etymology

From 'book' + 'shelf', first recorded in the 19th century.
