papa

1. NOUN

/ pəˈpɑ / (AmE), / pəˈpɑː / (BrE)
an informal word for father, especially used by or to young children
informal term for father
informal

Examples

My little girl always calls me papa when she wants something.
Every Sunday, papa would read us stories before bed.
I made a card for papa on Father's Day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dad - an informal word for father
  • Synonym: father - male parent
  • Antonym: mother - female parent

Etymology

From French 'papa', probably imitative of baby talk; widely used in many languages.
