fortieth

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrtiəθ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtiɪθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number matching the number 40 in a sequence; the item in position forty.
number in position forty

Examples

She finished in fortieth place in the marathon, just ahead of her best friend.
This is the fortieth anniversary of the school’s founding.
Only one in every fortieth applicant was accepted into the program.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortieth - adjective: being number forty in a sequence
  • Degree Lower: thirty-ninth - number in position thirty-nine
  • Degree Higher: forty-first - number in position forty-one

Etymology

From 'forty' plus the ordinal suffix '-th'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrtiɪθ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtiɪθ / (BrE)
Coming after thirty-nine others in a sequence; 40th.
ordinal number: 40th

Examples

This year marks the fortieth anniversary of the school's founding.
She finished fortieth in the marathon, just ahead of her personal goal.
The fortieth chapter of the book contains a major plot twist.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty-ninth - coming after thirty-eight others, 39th
  • Degree Higher: forty-first - coming after forty others, 41st

Etymology

From 'forty' + '-eth', the suffix forming ordinal numbers.

Synset POS: s
