refulgence

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈfʌldʒəns / (AmE), / rɪˈfʌldʒəns / (BrE)
A brilliant or radiant brightness; the quality of shining out with light.
radiant brightness; brilliance

Examples

The refulgence of the morning sun lit up the entire valley.
Her smile held a refulgence that seemed to brighten the whole room.
The artist tried to capture the refulgence of the chandelier in her painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiance - the quality of being intensely bright or brilliant

Etymology

From Latin 'refulgentia', from 'refulgēre' (to shine brightly).
