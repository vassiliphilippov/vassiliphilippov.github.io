Damavand

1. PROPN

/ dæməˈvɑːnd / (AmE), / dæməˈvænd / (BrE)
an active volcano in northern Iran, the highest peak in the country and the Middle East.
Iran's highest volcano and mountain

Examples

Mount Damavand is visible from miles around due to its impressive height.
Legends and myths surround Damavand, making it a symbol in Iranian literature.
The snow-capped summit of Damavand attracts climbers from all over the world.

Etymology

From Persian دماوند (Damāvand).
