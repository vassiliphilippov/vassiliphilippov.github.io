declarable

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈklɛrəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈklɛːrəbəl / (BrE)
required to be officially stated or made known, especially to an authority.
required to be declared

Examples

Any declarable income must be reported to tax authorities each year.
The contract outlined which assets are considered declarable under the law.
Failure to list all declarable items on the customs form can result in penalties.

Synset Examples

declarable income

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undeclarable - not required or not able to be declared

Etymology

From Latin 'declarare' (to make clear) plus the suffix '-able'.
