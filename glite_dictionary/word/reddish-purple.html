reddish-purple

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈpɜrpl̩ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈpɜːpl / (BrE)
having a shade of purple that is tinged with red; of a color intermediate between red and purple.
purple color tinged with red

Examples

The evening sky was painted a beautiful reddish-purple as the sun set.
She chose a reddish-purple dress that stood out at the party.
Grapes at this stage have a reddish-purple hue, indicating ripeness.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: purple - a color between red and blue
  • Degree Lower: red - the color at the end of the spectrum next to orange
