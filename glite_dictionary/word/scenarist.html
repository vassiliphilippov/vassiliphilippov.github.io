scenarist

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈnɛərɪst / (AmE), / sɪˈnɑːrɪst / (BrE)
a person who writes screenplays for films or television
screenplay writer

Examples

The acclaimed scenarist received an award for her innovative screenplay.
Many aspiring filmmakers start their careers as a scenarist before moving to directing.
He was hired as the chief scenarist for the studio's new science fiction series.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screenwriter - a person who writes screenplays

Etymology

From French 'scénariste', from 'scénario' (scenario).
