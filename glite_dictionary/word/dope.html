dope

1. NOUN

/ doʊp / (AmE), / dəʊp / (BrE)
Slang term for marijuana.
slang for marijuana
slang

Examples

He was arrested for carrying dope in his backpack.
Some people use dope to relax or relieve stress.
They watched movies and smoked dope all night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weed - slang for marijuana

2. VERB

/ doʊp / (AmE), / dəʊp / (BrE)
to add a small amount of impurities to a semiconductor in order to modify its electrical properties
add impurities to a semiconductor
medical

Examples

To improve conductivity, manufacturers often dope silicon with small amounts of phosphorus.
Modern microchips rely on precisely doped semiconductors for their function.
Adding boron is a common way to dope silicon wafers in the industry.
