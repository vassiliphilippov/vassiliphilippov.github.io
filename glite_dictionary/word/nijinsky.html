Nijinsky

1. PROPN

/ nɪˈʒɪnski / (AmE), / nɪˈʒɪnski / (BrE)
Vaslav Nijinsky (1890–1950), a Russian ballet dancer renowned for his virtuosity and considered one of the greatest dancers of the 20th century.
famous Russian ballet dancer (1890–1950)

Examples

Nijinsky captivated audiences with his remarkable leaps and expressive performances on stage.
The influence of Nijinsky can still be seen in modern ballet choreography today.
Many critics regard Nijinsky as the most talented male dancer of his era.

Etymology

From Russian surname Нижинский (Nizhinsky).
