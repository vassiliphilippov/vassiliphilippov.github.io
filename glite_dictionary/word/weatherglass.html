weatherglass

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðərˌɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈwɛðəˌɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a simple instrument, often a barometer, used to show changes in atmospheric pressure and predict weather changes
simple barometer for weather prediction

Examples

The old sailor consulted his weatherglass before deciding whether to set sail in the morning.
A sudden drop in the weatherglass warned the villagers of an approaching storm.
She inherited a beautiful antique weatherglass from her grandfather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barometer - instrument for measuring atmospheric pressure
