Prince Philip

1. PROPN

/ prɪns ˈfɪlɪp / (AmE), / prɪns ˈfɪlɪp / (BrE)
(1921–2021) The husband of Queen Elizabeth II of the United Kingdom; born Prince Philip of Greece and Denmark, later Duke of Edinburgh.
husband of Queen Elizabeth II

Examples

Many people mourned the death of Prince Philip in 2021.
Prince Philip accompanied the Queen on many official royal tours.
As Duke of Edinburgh, Prince Philip supported numerous charitable organizations.
