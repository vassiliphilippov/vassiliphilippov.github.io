corer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːrɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːrə / (BrE)
A kitchen tool used to remove the core from apples or other fruits.
tool for removing fruit cores

Examples

She used an corer to quickly remove the seeds from the apples.
Make sure to clean your corer after each use for the best results.
This handy corer cuts effortlessly through pears and apples alike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apple corer - a kind of corer specifically designed for apples
