João Pessoa

1. PROPN

/ ʒuˈɑː.oʊ peɪˈsoʊ.ə / (AmE), / ʒuˈaʊ peˈsəʊ.ə / (BrE)
A city in northeastern Brazil, located near the Atlantic Ocean north of Recife, known as the capital of the state of Paraíba.
city in northeastern Brazil

Examples

João Pessoa is famous for its beautiful beaches and historic architecture.
The conference will be held in João Pessoa next year, attracting visitors from around the world.
After visiting Recife, we traveled north to explore João Pessoa.

Etymology

Named after João Pessoa Cavalcanti de Albuquerque, a former governor of Paraíba.
