hustler

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌs.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈhʌs.lə / (BrE)
A prostitute who solicits clients by walking the streets.
street-walking prostitute

Examples

The police arrested a hustler during a late-night patrol downtown.
She started out as a hustler working on city corners.
Rumors spread about the new hustler who quickly found regular clients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: streetwalker - a prostitute who solicits clients in public places

2. NOUN

/ ˈhʌslɚ / (AmE), / ˈhʌslə / (BrE)
a person who is clever and determined in finding ways to overcome difficulties, often using unscrupulous or dishonest methods
shrewd or unscrupulous person

Examples

He's a true hustler, always finding ways to turn situations to his advantage.
The city was full of hustlers looking to make a quick buck.
She admired his drive, but worried he might become a hustler willing to bend the rules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: con artist - a person who deceives others by persuading them to believe something that is not true
  • Synonym: swindler - a person who cheats or defrauds others
  • Antonym: dupe - someone easily deceived or tricked
Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
