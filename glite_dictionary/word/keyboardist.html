keyboardist

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːbɔːrdɪst / (AmE), / ˈkiːbɔːdɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays a keyboard instrument, such as a piano or synthesizer
musician who plays keyboard instruments

Examples

The keyboardist added a beautiful melody to the band's latest song.
During the concert, the keyboardist switched between piano and synthesizer effortlessly.
Many famous rock bands include a talented keyboardist in their lineup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pianist - a person who plays the piano

Etymology

From 'keyboard' + '-ist', formed on the pattern of similar musician terms.
