featherfoil

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðɚˌfɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈfɛðəˌfɔɪl / (BrE)
A Eurasian aquatic herbaceous plant of the genus Hottonia, typically having finely divided, feathery leaves.
aquatic plant of genus Hottonia

Examples

The pond was edged with blooming featherfoil, adding delicate beauty to the water's surface.
Botanists observed that featherfoil thrives in slow-moving freshwater streams and lakes.
She identified the aquatic plant as featherfoil because of its finely divided leaves.

Etymology

From 'feather', referencing the feather-like leaves, and 'foil', meaning leaf or thin sheet.
