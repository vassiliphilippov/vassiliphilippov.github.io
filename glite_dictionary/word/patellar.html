patellar

1. ADJ

/ pəˈtɛlɚ / (AmE), / pəˈtɛlə / (BrE)
relating to the patella (kneecap) or the area around it
relating to the kneecap
medical

Examples

She complained of patellar pain after her run.
The doctor examined the patellar reflex during the check-up.
Physical therapy helped strengthen his patellar tendon.

Synset Examples

patellar tendon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patella - the kneecap bone in the human leg

Etymology

From Latin 'patella' (kneecap) + '-ar' (forming adjectives).
