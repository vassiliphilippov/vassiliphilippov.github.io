veterinarian

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɛtərəˈnɛriən / (AmE), / ˌvɛtərɪˈnɛəriən / (BrE)
A person qualified to treat the diseases and injuries of animals; an animal doctor.
animal doctor; veterinary surgeon

Examples

I took my dog to the veterinarian after she injured her paw.
The veterinarian specializes in caring for exotic animals.
Our local veterinarian offered advice on keeping pets healthy during the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vet - short form of veterinarian; animal doctor
  • Synonym: doctor - medical professional for humans or animals
  • Antonym: physician - medical doctor for humans

Etymology

From Latin 'veterinarius' (of cattle, relating to animals), from 'veterinae' (working animals).
