Sinkie

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋki / (BrE)
an informal term for a person from Singapore
informal term for Singaporean person
informal slang

Examples

Many Sinkies travel abroad during the holiday season to experience different cultures.
He met a group of friendly Sinkies at the international student orientation.
The food festival was organized by some passionate Sinkies living overseas.

Synset Examples

She proudly identifies as a Sinkie from the local community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Singaporean - a person from Singapore

Etymology

From the abbreviation of Singapore ('Sg') + '-kie', a playful suffix.

Examples

Related Terms
