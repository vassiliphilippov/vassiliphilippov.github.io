homeowner

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmˌoʊnər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌəʊnə / (BrE)
A person who owns the house or apartment in which they live, rather than renting it.
person who owns their home

Examples

Many homeowners choose to renovate their kitchens before selling their houses.
The government introduced new tax breaks for first-time homeowners.
As a homeowner, she is responsible for maintaining the garden and repairs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tenant - a person who rents a house or apartment from another
  • Antonym: landlord - a person who rents out property to a tenant

Etymology

From 'home' + 'owner', first attested in late 19th century.
