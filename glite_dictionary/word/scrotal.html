scrotal

1. ADJ

/ ˈskroʊ.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˈskrəʊ.təl / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the scrotum, the pouch of skin containing the testicles in males.
relating to the scrotum
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the patient for any scrotal swelling or abnormalities.
Some mammals possess scrotal testes, while others have internal testes.
A scrotal ultrasound is commonly used to investigate lumps or pain in the area.

Synset Examples

scrotal mammals
scrotal testes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrotum - the pouch of skin containing the testicles

Etymology

From Latin 'scrotum' (a pouch), with the adjectival suffix '-al'.
