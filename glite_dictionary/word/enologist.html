enologist

1. NOUN

/ iˈnɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / iːˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A specialist in the science and study of wine making.
wine making specialist

Examples

The enologist recommended a new fermentation process to improve the wine's flavor.
As an enologist, she travels to vineyards around the world, studying different grape varieties.
The winery hired a skilled enologist to oversee their entire wine production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oenologist - A specialist in wine making (variant spelling)

Etymology

From Greek oinos (wine) + -logist (one who studies).
