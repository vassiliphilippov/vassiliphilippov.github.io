overlarge

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈlɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈlɑːdʒ / (BrE)
excessively large; larger than is necessary or desirable
excessively large

Examples

The sofa seemed overlarge for the small living room.
He chose an overlarge coat that drowned his slim frame.
Some critics felt the sculpture was overlarge and dominated the plaza.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: huge - extremely large
  • Synonym: oversized - bigger than usual or necessary

Etymology

Formed from over- (excessive) + large.
