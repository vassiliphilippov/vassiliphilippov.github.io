purlieu

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜr.lju / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.ljuː / (BrE)
an area lying close to or surrounding a place, especially an outlying district or neighboring region
neighboring or outlying area

Examples

The fox disappeared into the purlieu beyond the riverbank, leaving the hunters behind.
They often strolled through the purlieu of the city, enjoying the quiet streets away from the center.
Many rare birds inhabit the purlieu of the ancient woodland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vicinity - the area near or surrounding a particular place
  • Synonym: environs - the surrounding area or district
  • Antonym: center - the middle point or part

Etymology

From Old French puralee (land on the edge of a forest), from pouraler (to go through), ultimately Latin perambulare (to walk through).
