to streetwalk

1. VERB

/ ˈstriːtˌwɔk / (AmE), / ˈstriːtˌwɔːk / (BrE)
(of a prostitute) to walk the streets in search of customers.
walk the streets to solicit clients (prostitution)

Examples

She used to streetwalk in the city center during her teenage years.
After losing her job, desperation led her to streetwalk to make ends meet.
Authorities patrol the area at night to prevent women from streetwalking.

Synset Examples

The prostitute is street-walking every night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to solicit - to try to obtain customers, especially for prostitution, on the street
