rewardingly

1. ADV

/ rɪˈwɔrdɪŋli / (AmE), / rɪˈwɔːdɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that provides satisfaction, benefit, or a sense of accomplishment.
in a way that is rewarding

Examples

She found the work rewardingly challenging and never lost her enthusiasm.
Volunteering at the shelter was an experience he described as rewardingly meaningful.
The project concluded rewardingly, with the team's efforts recognized by the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rewarding - providing satisfaction or benefit

Etymology

From 'rewarding' + '-ly' (adverb-forming suffix).
