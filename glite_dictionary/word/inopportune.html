inopportune

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnˌɑːpərˈtuːn / (AmE), / ˌɪnˌɒpəˈtjuːn / (BrE)
happening at a time that is not convenient or suitable; ill-timed.
ill-timed or inconvenient

Examples

I'm sorry to call at such an inopportune moment, but it's urgent.
His comment came at the most inopportune time imaginable.
The power outage was particularly inopportune during the live performance.

Synset Examples

arrived at a most inopportune hour
an inopportune visit

Related Terms

  • Antonym: opportune - happening at a convenient or suitable time
  • Synonym: untimely - happening at an unsuitable or premature time

Etymology

From Latin inopportunus, from in- (not) + opportunus (opportune).
