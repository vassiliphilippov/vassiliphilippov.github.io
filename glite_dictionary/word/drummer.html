drummer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrʌmər / (AmE), / ˈdrʌmə / (BrE)
a person who plays a drum, especially as a member of a musical group
person who plays drums

Examples

The drummer kept a steady beat throughout the concert.
She has been the lead drummer in the band for years.
Their new drummer brings a lot of energy to their live shows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: percussionist - a person who plays percussion instruments

Etymology

From 'drum' + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
