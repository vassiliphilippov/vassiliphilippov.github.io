Lomogramma

1. NOUN

/ ˌloʊmoʊˈɡræmə / (AmE), / ˌləʊməʊˈɡræmə / (BrE)
A genus of large climbing ferns native to southeastern Asia.
genus of climbing ferns

Examples

The botanist carefully examined the Lomogramma specimen collected from the rainforest in Borneo.
Species of Lomogramma are known for their distinctive climbing habit and lush green fronds.
Our greenhouse features several rare Lomogramma plants from Southeast Asia.

Etymology

From Greek 'lôma' (fringe, border) and 'gramma' (line, character), referring to the appearance of certain fern structures.
