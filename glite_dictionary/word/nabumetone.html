nabumetone

1. NOUN

/ nəˈbjuːməˌtoʊn / (AmE), / nəˈbjuːmətəʊn / (BrE)
a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve pain and inflammation, especially in arthritis

medical
