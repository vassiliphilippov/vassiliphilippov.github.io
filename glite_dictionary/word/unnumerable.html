unnumerable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈnuːmərəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈnjuːmərəbl / (BrE)
too many to be counted; countless.
too numerous to be counted

Examples

There are unnumerable grains of sand on the beach.
The artist has received unnumerable awards throughout her long career.
His mind raced with unnumerable possibilities as he faced the decision.

Synset Examples

countless hours
an infinite number of reasons
innumerable difficulties
the multitudinous seas
myriad stars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innumerable - too many to be counted; countless
  • Synonym: countless - so many as to be impossible to count
  • Antonym: numerable - able to be counted

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'un-' (not) + 'numerable' (able to be counted).
