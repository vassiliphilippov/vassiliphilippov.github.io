realty

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəlti / (AmE), / ˈrɪəlti / (BrE)
property consisting of land and the buildings on it; real estate.
land and buildings; real estate

Examples

The agent specializes in commercial realty across the city.
Investing in realty has become increasingly popular over the past decade.
They decided to sell their family realty after moving abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: real estate - property consisting of land and buildings

Etymology

From Anglo-French *realté*, from Medieval Latin *realitas* (property, reality), from Latin *realis* (actual).
