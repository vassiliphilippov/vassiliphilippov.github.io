greasily

1. ADV

/ ˈɡriːzɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡriːsɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is covered with or resembling grease; in a greasy manner.
in a greasy manner

Examples

The pan felt greasily slick, making it difficult to hold.
She wiped her hands after touching the greasily wrapped sandwich.
His hair hung greasily over his forehead, in need of a wash.

Synset Examples

the food was greasily unappetizing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greasy - covered with or resembling grease
