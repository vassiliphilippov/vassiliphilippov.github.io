triumphant

1. ADJ

/ traɪˈʌmfənt / (AmE), / traɪˈʌmfənt / (BrE)
feeling or expressing joy, pride, or satisfaction after a victory or success.
feeling or showing victory or success

Examples

She gave a triumphant smile as she crossed the finish line first.
The team returned home feeling triumphant after their championship win.
His triumphant expression showed how much the award meant to him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: victorious - having won a victory; triumphant
  • Antonym: defeated - having been beaten in a contest or battle

Etymology

From Latin 'triumphans', present participle of 'triumphare' meaning 'to triumph'.

/ traɪˈʌmfənt / (AmE), / traɪˈʌmfənt / (BrE)

Examples

His triumphant announcement was met with cheers from the crowd.
