adroitly

1. ADV

/ əˈdrɔɪtli / (AmE), / əˈdrɔɪtli / (BrE)
In a skillful and clever way; with dexterity and resourcefulness.
in a skillful, clever way

Examples

She adroitly navigated the tricky conversation, impressing everyone involved.
The magician adroitly manipulated the cards, leaving the audience in awe.
He adroitly avoided all obstacles during the race and finished first.

Synset Examples

he handled the situation adroitly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skillfully - in a skillful way
  • Antonym: clumsily - in a clumsy or awkward way

Etymology

From 'adroit' + '-ly', with 'adroit' from French 'adroit' (clever, skillful).
