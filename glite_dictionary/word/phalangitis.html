phalangitis

1. NOUN

/ fəˌlæŋˈɡaɪtɪs / (AmE), / fəˌlaŋˈɡaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of a finger or toe
inflammation of a finger or toe
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with phalangitis after he complained of pain and swelling in his toe.
Chronic phalangitis may require both medication and changes in footwear.
Early treatment of phalangitis helps prevent long-term joint damage.

Etymology

From Greek 'phalanx' (bone of finger or toe) + '-itis' (inflammation).
