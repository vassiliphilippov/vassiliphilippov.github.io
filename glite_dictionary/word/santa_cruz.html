Santa Cruz

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæn.tə ˈkɹuz / (AmE), / ˌsæn.tə ˈkɹuːz / (BrE)
A city in western California, located on Monterey Bay, known for its tourism, beaches, and university.
city in western California, USA

Examples

Thousands of visitors flock to Santa Cruz each summer for its famous boardwalk and beaches.
The University of California has a campus located in Santa Cruz.
After moving to Santa Cruz, she quickly fell in love with the city's laid-back atmosphere.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Holy Cross'.
