campmate

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmp.meɪt / (AmE), / ˈkæmp.meɪt / (BrE)
a person who shares a camp with another; someone living in the same camp
someone who shares a camp

Examples

My campmate and I stayed up late telling ghost stories by the fire.
She became close friends with her campmate during a month-long hiking expedition.
After the hike, every campmate helped prepare dinner for the group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roommate - a person who shares a room or apartment with another
  • Synonym: classmate - a person who is in the same class at school or college

Etymology

Formed from 'camp' + 'mate', modeled after similar compounds like 'classmate' and 'roommate'.
