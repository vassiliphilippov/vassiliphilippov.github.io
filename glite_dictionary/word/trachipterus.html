Trachipterus

1. NOUN

/ trəˈkɪptərəs / (AmE), / trəˈkɪptərəs / (BrE)
A genus of elongated, ribbon-like marine fishes in the family Trachipteridae, known as ribbonfishes.
a genus of ribbonfishes

Examples

The genus Trachipterus includes several species of ribbonfishes found in deep ocean waters.
Marine biologists recently discovered a new species within the Trachipterus genus.
The distinctive body shape of Trachipterus makes it easily recognizable among other ribbonfishes.

Etymology

From Greek 'trachys' (rough) and 'pteron' (fin or wing).
