delible

1. ADJ

/ dəˈlɪbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪbəl / (BrE)
able to be deleted, erased, or removed; not permanent.
able to be deleted or erased

Examples

Please use a delible marker on the whiteboard so the writing can be cleaned off easily.
The software saves both permanent and delible files, allowing users to revise their work freely.
Unlike an indelible tattoo, a delible mark can be washed away with water.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indelible - impossible to remove or erase; permanent

Etymology

From Latin 'delibilis', from 'delere' (to delete).
