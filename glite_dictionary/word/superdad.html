superdad

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuː.pɚ.dæd / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pə.dæd / (BrE)
an informal term for a father who manages to combine childcare responsibilities with full-time employment
a father excelling at both work and childcare
informal

Examples

After work, superdad picks up the kids, cooks dinner, and helps with homework every day.
Many people admire him as a superdad because he never misses his children's events despite a busy job.
Balancing board meetings and bedtime stories, he truly earns his reputation as a superdad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supermom - a mother who successfully combines childcare with full-time employment

Etymology

Formed by combining 'super-' (from Latin super, meaning 'above', used as a prefix indicating superiority) with 'dad' (informal for father).
