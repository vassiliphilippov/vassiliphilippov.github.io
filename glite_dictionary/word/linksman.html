linksman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪŋksmən / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋksmən / (BrE)
a man who plays golf, especially on a links course
male golfer

Examples

The skilled linksman approached the eighteenth green with confidence.
Many legendary linksmen have left their mark on this historic course.
As a young linksman, he dreamed of playing in the British Open.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golfer - a person who plays golf
  • Antonym: linkswoman - a woman who plays golf

Etymology

From 'links' (type of golf course) + 'man'.
