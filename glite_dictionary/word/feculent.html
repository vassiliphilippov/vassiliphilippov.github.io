feculent

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɛkjələnt / (AmE), / ˈfiːkjʊlənt / (BrE)
containing or contaminated with waste matter; foul or filthy.
containing waste matter; filthy

Examples

The river had become feculent after years of pollution and neglect.
He recoiled from the feculent odor wafting from the garbage heap.
The laboratory analyzed the feculent water for signs of contamination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filthy - very dirty; covered with filth or grime
  • Antonym: clean - free from dirt or impurities

Etymology

From Latin 'faeculentus', from 'faex' meaning 'sediment, dregs'.
