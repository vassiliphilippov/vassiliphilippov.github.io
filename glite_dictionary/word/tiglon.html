tiglon

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɡlən / (AmE), / ˈtɪɡlən / (BrE)
A hybrid offspring produced by mating a male tiger with a female lion.
offspring of a male tiger and female lion

Examples

The zoo announced the birth of a rare tiglon last week.
Unlike ligers, a tiglon inherits more characteristics from the tiger father.
Scientists study tiglons to understand hybrid genetics in big cats.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: liger - offspring of a male lion and a female tiger

Etymology

Blend of 'tiger' and 'lion'.
