pudding-wife

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊdɪŋ ˌwaɪf / (AmE), / ˈpʊdɪŋ ˌwaɪf / (BrE)
a bluish and bronze wrasse fish found from the Florida Keys to Brazil, known scientifically as Halichoeres radiatus.
a tropical Atlantic wrasse fish

Examples

The diver spotted a pudding-wife swimming among the coral reefs.
Fishermen in the Bahamas often catch the colorful pudding-wife near the shore.
The pudding-wife is easily recognized by its vibrant blue and bronze stripes.
