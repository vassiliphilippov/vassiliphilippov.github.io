Mongol

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈmɒŋɡɒl / (BrE)
a member of the group of nomadic peoples traditionally inhabiting Mongolia and adjacent regions of Central Asia
member of Mongolia's nomadic peoples

Examples

The Mongol Empire was once the largest contiguous empire in history.
Many Mongol families still practice traditional herding on the steppes.
She studied the culture and history of the Mongol people during her travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mongolian - a native or inhabitant of Mongolia

Etymology

From Mongolian *Mongol*, referring to the people of Mongolia.

Examples
