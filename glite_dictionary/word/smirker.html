smirker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɝːkər / (AmE), / ˈsmɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who smiles in a self-satisfied or conceited way, often considered unpleasant or offensive
someone who smirks; conceited smiler

Examples

The smirker in the back row annoyed everyone with his constant air of superiority.
She could not stand being around a smirker who always seemed to find others beneath him.
He came across as a real smirker during the meeting, which irritated his colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smirk - to smile in a self-satisfied or irritating way

Etymology

From the verb 'smirk' + '-er' agent noun suffix.
