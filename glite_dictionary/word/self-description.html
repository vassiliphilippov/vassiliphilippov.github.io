self-description

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf.dɪˈskrɪp.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌself.dɪˈskrɪp.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of describing oneself, especially one's character, abilities, or background.
the act of describing oneself

Examples

Her self-description during the interview was both honest and insightful.
In his self-description, he emphasized his passion for helping others.
The artist's self-description revealed much about her creative process.

Synset Examples

His self-description as an ‘experimenter’ puts him shoulder-to-shoulder with William James and John Dewey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-portrait - a painting, drawing, or photograph that one has done of oneself

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'description'; modeled after the pattern of self- compounds.
