populous

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑːpjələs / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊləs / (BrE)
having a large population; densely populated.
having a large population

Examples

The city became increasingly populous after the construction of new housing developments.
India is one of the most populous countries in the world.
As the area grew more populous, local governments expanded public services.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sparsely populated - having few inhabitants

Etymology

From Latin 'populosus' (full of people), from 'populus' (people).
