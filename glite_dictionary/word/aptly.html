aptly

1. ADV

/ ˈæptli / (AmE), / ˈæptli / (BrE)
in a way that is appropriate or suitable to the situation; with aptness or competence
in an appropriate or competent way

Examples

She aptly summarized the main point of the discussion in one sentence.
The movie was aptly titled, capturing the spirit of the story.
His remarks were aptly worded to avoid offending anyone.

Synset Examples

they worked competently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: competently - in a competent and skillful way
  • Antonym: inaptly - in an inappropriate or unsuitable way

Etymology

From Latin aptus (fit, suitable) via the English adjective 'apt' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
