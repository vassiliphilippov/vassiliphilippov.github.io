Essex

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛs.ɛks / (AmE), / ˈɛs.ɪks / (BrE)
a county in southeastern England, bordering the North Sea and the Thames estuary
county in southeastern England

Examples

Many people commute from Essex to work in London each day.
The coastline of Essex is popular with holidaymakers during the summer.
She grew up in a small village in Essex before moving to the city.

Etymology

From Old English 'Ēastseaxe', meaning 'East Saxons'.
