unpolluted

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.pəˈluː.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.pəˈluː.tɪd / (BrE)
not contaminated or made dirty by harmful substances; clean and pure.
not contaminated; clean, pure

Examples

The air in the remote mountain village remained unpolluted despite nearby industrialization.
She was thrilled to swim in the unpolluted waters of the protected national park.
The unpolluted sky allowed us to see thousands of stars at night.

Synset Examples

unpolluted streams
an uncontaminated lake

Related Terms

  • Antonym: polluted - contaminated or made impure by harmful substances

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'polluted', from Latin 'polluere' (to defile, corrupt).
