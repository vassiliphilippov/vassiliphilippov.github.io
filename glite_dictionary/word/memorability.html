memorability

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛmərəˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌmɛm.ərəˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality of being easy to remember or worth remembering
quality of being worth remembering

Examples

The memorability of the logo contributed greatly to the brand's long-term success.
Good teachers use stories and visuals to enhance the memorability of their lessons.
The film's stunning visuals added to its memorability for audiences worldwide.

Synset Examples

continuous change results in lack of memorability
true memorability of phrase

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memorable - easy to remember; worth remembering
  • Antonym: forgettability - the quality of being easily forgotten

Etymology

From memorable + -ity, modeled on Latin-derived word formation.
