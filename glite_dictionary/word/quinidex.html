Quinidex

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪnɪˌdɛks / (AmE), / ˈkwɪnɪˌdɛks / (BrE)
A trademarked cardiac drug (also sold as Quinora) used to treat certain heart arrhythmias.
cardiac drug for arrhythmias
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Quinidex to manage her heart rhythm disorder.
Patients taking Quinidex should have their heart monitored regularly for side effects.
He experienced fewer palpitations after starting Quinidex as part of his treatment plan.
