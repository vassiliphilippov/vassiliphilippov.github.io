unenergetically

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌɛnəˈdʒetɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner lacking energy or enthusiasm; in a lethargic or sluggish way.
in a manner lacking energy

Examples

After staying up all night, she moved unenergetically through her morning routine.
He responded unenergetically to the coach's instructions, barely lifting a finger.
The children cleaned their rooms unenergetically, clearly wishing they were outside playing.

Synset Examples

he hung around the house lethargically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lethargically - in a sluggish or apathetic manner

Etymology

From 'unenergetic' + '-ally' (adverbial suffix).
