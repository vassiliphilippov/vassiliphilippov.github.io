one-quadrillionth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn kwɑˈdrɪliənθ / (AmE), / wʌn kwɒˈdrɪliənθ / (BrE)
One part of a quadrillion equal parts; the ordinal number corresponding to one divided by a quadrillion (1/1,000,000,000,000,000).
one part in a quadrillion equal parts

Examples

It is highly improbable to win with odds of just one-quadrillionth.
The chance of two identical snowflakes forming is less than one-quadrillionth.
This particle's mass is about one-quadrillionth of a gram.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quadrillionth - one part in a quadrillion equal parts

Etymology

Formed from 'one' and 'quadrillionth' (from French 'quadrillion', patterned after 'million', plus ordinal suffix '-th').
