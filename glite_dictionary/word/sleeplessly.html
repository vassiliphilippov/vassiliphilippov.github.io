sleeplessly

1. ADV

/ ˈsliːpləsli / (AmE), / ˈsliːpləsli / (BrE)
In a way that involves being unable to sleep; without sleeping.
without sleeping

Examples

She stared sleeplessly at the ceiling, waiting for morning to arrive.
After drinking too much coffee, he tossed and turned sleeplessly all night.
The baby cried sleeplessly in her crib, keeping everyone awake.

Synset Examples

he was lying in bed sleeplessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restlessly - in a restless manner; unable to rest or relax
