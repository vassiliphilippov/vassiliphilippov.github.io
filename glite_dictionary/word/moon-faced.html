moon-faced

1. ADJ

/ ˈmun ˌfeɪst / (AmE), / ˈmuːn ˌfeɪst / (BrE)
having a round or full face, resembling the shape of a moon
having a round face

Examples

The little girl was moon-faced and cheerful, her cheeks glowing in the sunlight.
He was a moon-faced child, with bright eyes and a gentle smile.
The actor's moon-faced appearance made his character seem more approachable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: round-faced - having a face that is round in shape

Etymology

Formed from 'moon' + 'faced', comparing the fullness of a face to the roundness of the moon.
