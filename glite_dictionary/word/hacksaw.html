hacksaw

1. NOUN

/ ˈhækˌsɔ / (AmE), / ˈhækˌsɔː / (BrE)
a saw with a fine-toothed blade set in a frame, used with one hand for cutting metal or other hard materials
a hand saw for cutting metal

Examples

He used a hacksaw to cut through the metal pipe.
The mechanic grabbed a hacksaw from his toolbox.
A hacksaw is essential for many home repair projects involving metal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saw - a tool with a serrated blade used for cutting

Etymology

From 'hack' (to cut) and 'saw'; first attested in mid 19th century.
