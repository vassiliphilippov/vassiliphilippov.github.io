endlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛndləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈɛndləsnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having no end or limit; being infinite or seemingly infinite
the state of having no end or limit

Examples

The endlessness of the desert was both beautiful and frightening.
She was overwhelmed by the endlessness of the possibilities ahead of her.
The movie captured the endlessness of childhood summers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infinity - the state or quality of being infinite
  • Antonym: finiteness - the property of having limits or an end

Etymology

From 'endless' + '-ness'.
