telco

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlˌkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɛl.kəʊ / (BrE)
a company that provides telephone and telecommunications services to the public
telephone service provider company

Examples

The major telco announced new data plans for its customers.
Many rural areas still lack adequate service from the local telco.
Competition among telcos has led to lower prices for consumers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telecommunications company - a company that provides telecommunications services

Etymology

Shortened from 'telecommunications company'.
