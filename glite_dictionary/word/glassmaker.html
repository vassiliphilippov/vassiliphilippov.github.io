glassmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlæsˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes glass, especially by hand or in a workshop
person who makes glass

Examples

The glassmaker carefully shaped the glowing molten glass into a beautiful vase.
As a third-generation glassmaker, she has inherited her family's skills and artistry.
The new museum exhibition showcases the work of local glassmakers from the 18th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glassblower - person who shapes molten glass by blowing air into it
  • Synonym: artisan - a worker in a skilled trade, especially one that involves making things by hand
