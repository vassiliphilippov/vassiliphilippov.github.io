counselorship

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊn.sə.lɚ.ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl.ə.ʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or office of a counselor; the state or term of serving as a counselor
position or office of counselor

Examples

She was appointed to the counselorship after many years of dedicated service.
During his counselorship, the school improved its guidance program.
He applied for the vacant counselorship at the law firm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: counselor - a person who gives advice or guidance

Etymology

From 'counselor' + '-ship' (forming nouns denoting state or office).
