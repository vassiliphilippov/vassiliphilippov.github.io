password

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæsˌwɝd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsˌwɜːd / (BrE)
A secret word or phrase used to gain access to a computer system or restricted area, known only to a particular person or group.
secret word/phrase for access

Examples

Please enter your password to log in to the account.
He had to reset his password after forgetting it last week.
Never share your password with anyone to keep your information safe.

Synset Examples

he forgot the password

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passcode - a numerical code used instead of a password

Etymology

Formed from 'pass' + 'word', originally referring to a military challenge and response word.
