lactating

1. ADJ

/ ˈlæk.teɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlak.teɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a female mammal) producing or secreting milk from the mammary glands.
producing or secreting milk
medical

Examples

The lactating cow produces milk several times a day for her calf.
Only lactating mothers should take this supplement, according to the label.
Veterinarians examined the lactating dog and her puppies after they were rescued.

Synset Examples

a wet nurse
a wet cow
lactating cows

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nursing - suckling young; feeding an infant at the breast
  • Antonym: pregnant - having a baby or babies developing inside the body

Etymology

From Latin 'lactare' (to suckle, to nurse), from 'lac, lactis' (milk).
