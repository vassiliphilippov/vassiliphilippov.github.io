stylemark

1. NOUN

/ ˈstaɪlˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlˌmɑːk / (BrE)
a distinctive characteristic or attribute that identifies someone or something
distinctive identifying characteristic

Examples

The designer's use of bold colors became her stylemark within the fashion industry.
A unique brushstroke was the artist's stylemark, instantly recognizable to critics and fans.
That quirky sense of humor is a stylemark of his writing, setting him apart from others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hallmark - a distinctive feature; a mark of authenticity
