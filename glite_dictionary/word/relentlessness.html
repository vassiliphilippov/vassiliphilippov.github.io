relentlessness

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɛntləsnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈlɛntlɪsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being persistent, unyielding, or refusing to relent; mercilessness or unwavering determination
persistent, unyielding determination

Examples

The relentlessness of the storm left the village battered for days.
She admired his relentlessness in pursuing justice for the victims.
Many athletes owe their success to the relentlessness with which they train.

Synset Examples

the relentlessness or their pursuit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determination - the quality of being firm in purpose
  • Antonym: mercy - compassion shown toward others

Etymology

From 'relentless' + '-ness'; 'relentless' from re- (back) + Latin lentus (flexible, slow).
