slopped

1. ADJ

/ slɑpt / (AmE), / slɒpt / (BrE)
Extremely drunk; heavily intoxicated.
very drunk; intoxicated
informal slang

Examples

After so many shots, he was absolutely slopped by midnight.
She stumbled out of the bar, clearly slopped and unable to walk straight.
They laughed about how slopped they all got at the wedding reception.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drunk - intoxicated by alcohol; not sober
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk
