protozoology

1. NOUN

/ ˌproʊtəzoʊˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəʊzəˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of zoology concerned with the study of protozoans
study of protozoans (zoology branch)

Examples

She specialized in protozoology during her graduate studies at the university.
Advances in protozoology have improved our understanding of parasitic diseases.
His research in protozoology contributed significantly to the field of tropical medicine.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: zoology - the scientific study of animals

Etymology

From proto- (first) + zoology, following zoological naming conventions.
