untrimmed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtrɪmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtrɪmd / (BrE)
not cut, clipped, or made neat by trimming; left in its natural or untidy state.
not trimmed; left untidy or natural

Examples

His untrimmed beard gave him a wild appearance.
The lawn looked messy and untrimmed after weeks of neglect.
She admired the garden’s natural beauty, preferring the untrimmed hedges.

Synset Examples

shaggy untrimmed locks

Related Terms

  • Antonym: trimmed - having been made neat or cut by trimming

Etymology

From un- (not) + trimmed (past participle of trim).
