Samoa i Sisifo

1. PROPN

/ sɑːˈmoʊ.ə i siˈsi.foʊ / (AmE), / səˈməʊ.ə i sɪˈsiː.fəʊ / (BrE)
the former official name of Western Samoa, a constitutional monarchy in the western part of the Samoan Islands in the South Pacific, now officially known as Samoa
former official name for Western Samoa

Examples

The country's official name was Samoa i Sisifo before it was changed to Samoa in 1997.
Many historical documents from the 20th century refer to the nation as Samoa i Sisifo.
During the 1980s, Samoa i Sisifo participated in several international sporting events under that name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Samoa - a country comprising the western islands of the Samoan archipelago
  • Synonym: Western Samoa - former name of Samoa before 1997

Etymology

From Samoan, meaning 'Western Samoa' (i Sisifo = 'to the west').
