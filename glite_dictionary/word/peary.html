Peary

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɪəri / (AmE), / ˈpɪəri / (BrE)
(1856–1920) United States Arctic explorer and naval officer, regarded as the first person to reach the North Pole.
U.S. Arctic explorer and naval officer

Examples

Peary claimed to have reached the North Pole in 1909 after a long and perilous journey.
The expeditions led by Peary contributed greatly to our understanding of the Arctic region.
Many historians continue to debate whether Peary's account of the North Pole expedition is entirely accurate.
