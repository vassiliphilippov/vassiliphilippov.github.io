lensman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛnz.mæn / (AmE), / ˈlɛnz.mən / (BrE)
a man whose profession is taking photographs
professional male photographer

Examples

The lensman captured the bride's smile at the perfect moment.
Several well-known lensmen attended the press conference to take pictures for their newspapers.
As a lensman, he travels the world documenting wildlife in its natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photographer - a person who takes photographs, either professionally or as a hobby

Etymology

Formed from 'lens' (camera lens) and 'man'.
