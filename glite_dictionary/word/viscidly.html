viscidly

1. ADV

/ ˈvɪs.ɪd.li / (AmE), / ˈvɪs.ɪd.li / (BrE)
In a sticky or viscous manner.
in a sticky, viscous manner

Examples

The syrup ran viscidly down the sides of the jar, clinging to the glass.
Sweat clung viscidly to his skin in the humid air.
The resin oozed viscidly from the cut in the tree's bark.

Synset Examples

he felt the blood move stickily from his split scalp and trickle down his forehead

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stickily - in a sticky manner

Etymology

From 'viscid' + '-ly'.
