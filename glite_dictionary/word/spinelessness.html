spinelessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪnləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnləsnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of lacking courage, determination, or strong character; an irresolute disposition.
lack of courage or resolve

Examples

His spinelessness in the face of criticism disappointed his supporters.
The team's defeat was blamed on their collective spinelessness during high-pressure moments.
She wished her manager would overcome his spinelessness and stand up for the staff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowardice - lack of bravery or courage
  • Antonym: resolve - firm determination or decisiveness

Etymology

From 'spineless' + '-ness'; 'spineless' formed from 'spine' (backbone) + '-less' (without).
