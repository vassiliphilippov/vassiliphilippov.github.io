garmented

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑrməntɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːməntɪd / (BrE)
dressed or clothed, especially in fine or distinctive attire; often used in combination or formal/literary style
dressed or clothed

Examples

The children arrived garmented in bright costumes for the festival.
He was elegantly garmented in a tailored black suit for the ceremony.
The performers stood garmented in traditional attire before the audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressed - wearing clothes, especially formal or particular attire
  • Synonym: clothed - covered with clothing

Etymology

From 'garment' (Middle English, from Old French 'garnement'), plus the suffix '-ed'.
