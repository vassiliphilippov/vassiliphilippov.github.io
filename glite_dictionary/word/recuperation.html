recuperation

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌkuːpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˌkjuːpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of gradually recovering health and strength after illness, injury, or fatigue, especially through rest
recovery after illness or injury

Examples

After his surgery, several weeks of recuperation were necessary before he could return to work.
The mountain air aided her recuperation from pneumonia.
Strict bed rest is often recommended to speed up recuperation after a serious illness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recovery - the act of regaining health after illness
  • Antonym: relapse - a return of illness after recovery

Etymology

From Latin 'recuperatio', from 'recuperare' (to recover).
