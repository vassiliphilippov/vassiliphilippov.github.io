twenties

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɛntiz / (AmE), / ˈtwɛntiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life between the ages of 20 and 29 inclusive
the age period from 20 to 29

Examples

She spent most of her twenties traveling and working abroad.
Many people experience major life changes in their twenties.
During his twenties, he finished college and started his first job.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: thirties - the period of a person's life between 30 and 39
  • Degree Lower: teens - the period of a person's life between 13 and 19

2. NOUN

/ ˈtwɛntiz / (AmE), / ˈtwɛntɪz / (BrE)
the decade from 1920 to 1929
the decade 1920–1929

Examples

Jazz music became extremely popular during the twenties, shaping an entire cultural era.
Many famous writers and artists emerged in the twenties, including F. Scott Fitzgerald.
The fashion of the twenties was characterized by flapper dresses and shorter hairstyles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Roaring Twenties - the period of prosperity, jazz, and cultural change in the 1920s
  • Degree Higher: thirties - the decade from 1930 to 1939
  • Degree Lower: teens - the decade from 1910 to 1919
