optically

1. ADV

/ ˈɑp.tɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˈɒp.tɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to optics, vision, or light.
in a way relating to optics or vision

Examples

The lens is optically superior to older models.
This crystal can be optically identified under a microscope.
Some animals are optically sensitive to wavelengths humans cannot see.

Synset Examples

optically distorted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visually - in a way relating to sight

Etymology

Formed from 'optic' + '-ally', from Greek 'optikos' (of sight).
