to utilise

1. VERB

/ ˈjuːtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈjuːtɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to make practical or effective use of something for a particular purpose
make use of something

Examples

The new software will utilise our existing database to improve efficiency.
Researchers hope to utilise the latest technology to solve complex problems.
You should utilise all the resources available to you for this project.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: use - to employ for a purpose
  • Synonym: exploit - to make full use of and derive benefit from

Etymology

From French 'utiliser', from Latin 'utilis' meaning 'useful'.
