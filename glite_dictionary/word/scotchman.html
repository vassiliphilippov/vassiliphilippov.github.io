Scotchman

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑtʃmən / (AmE), / ˈskɒtʃmən / (BrE)
a male native or inhabitant of Scotland
male native of Scotland
archaic

Examples

The proud Scotchman recounted tales from his homeland.
As a young Scotchman abroad, he missed the Highlands dearly.
The novel featured a Scotchman who played the pipes at every celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scotsman - a man from Scotland
  • Antonym: Scotchwoman - a female native or inhabitant of Scotland

Etymology

Formed from 'Scotch' (relating to Scotland) + 'man'.
