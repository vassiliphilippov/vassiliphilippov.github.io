sinciput

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪnsɪˌpʌt / (AmE), / ˈsɪnsɪˌpʌt / (BrE)
the front part of the head or skull, including the forehead
front part of the head or skull
medical

Examples

The surgeon made an incision just above the sinciput to access the frontal bone.
Signs of trauma to the sinciput may indicate a forehead injury.
In some animals, the horns arise from the sinciput region of the skull.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forehead - the part of the face above the eyebrows

Etymology

From Latin 'sinciput' (half a head, forehead), from 'semi-' (half) + 'caput' (head).
