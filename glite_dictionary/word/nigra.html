nigra

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪɡrə / (AmE), / ˈnaɪɡrə / (BrE)
(offensive, ethnic slur) An extremely offensive word used as a derogatory term for a Black person.
offensive slur for a Black person
offensive

Examples

He was angered and hurt when someone shouted nigra at him on the street.
The documentary examined the history and use of slurs such as nigra.
It's important to acknowledge that nigra is an offensive and derogatory term.

Synset Examples

only a Black can call another Black a nigga

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nigger - extremely offensive slur for a Black person
  • Degree Lower: negro - outdated term for a Black person, often offensive

Etymology

Variation of the racial slur 'nigger', an altered pronunciation of 'negro' from Spanish and Portuguese 'negro' (black), ultimately from Latin 'niger'.
