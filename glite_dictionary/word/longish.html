longish

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɔŋɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋɪʃ / (BrE)
fairly long; somewhat long in length or duration
somewhat long

Examples

She wore a longish skirt that almost reached her knees.
His hair is longish compared to the other boys in his class.
The meeting ran for a longish time, but not as long as I expected.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: long - having great length
