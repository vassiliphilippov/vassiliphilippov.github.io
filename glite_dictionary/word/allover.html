allover

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔlˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈɔːlˌəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a pattern, color, etc.) covering the entire surface of something; extending uniformly over an area.
covering the entire surface

Examples

The living room walls were decorated with an allover floral pattern.
She prefers dresses with allover prints instead of ones with simple accents.
The painting has an allover wash of color that gives it a soft, blended look.

Synset Examples

an allover pattern
got an allover tan
