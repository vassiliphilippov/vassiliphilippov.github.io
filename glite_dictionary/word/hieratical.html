hieratical

1. ADJ

/ ˌhaɪəˈrætɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪəˈrætɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to priests or the priesthood; priestly.
relating to priests or the priesthood

Examples

The ceremony was marked by hieratical rituals unique to the ancient order.
The scholars analyzed the hieratical nature of the temple's artwork.
He wore hieratical garments symbolizing his spiritual authority.

Synset Examples

priestly (or sacerdotal) vestments
hieratic gestures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: priestly - relating to priests or the priesthood
  • Synonym: sacerdotal - relating to priests or the duties of priests

Etymology

From Late Latin hieraticus, from Greek hieratikos (of priests).
