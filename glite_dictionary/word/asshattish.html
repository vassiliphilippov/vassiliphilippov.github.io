asshattish

1. ADJ

/ ˈæsˌhætɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈasˌhatɪʃ / (BrE)
Displaying rude or foolish behavior characteristic of an 'asshat'; obnoxious or contemptible.
rude or foolish, like an 'asshat'
informal slang offensive

Examples

His asshattish remarks ruined the mood at the party.
I can't stand that kind of asshattish attitude in meetings.
People eventually get tired of his asshattish displays online.

Synset Examples

His asshattish behavior lost him many friends.
I find his asshattish comments quite irritating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asshat - a contemptible or stupid person (slang)
  • Antonym: respectful - showing respect; polite, thoughtful

Etymology

Formed from 'asshat' (slang term) plus '-ish', a suffix meaning 'having the characteristics of'.
