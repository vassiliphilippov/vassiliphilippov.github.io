Californian

1. NOUN

/ ˌkælɪˈfɔrniən / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈfɔːniən / (BrE)
a person who was born in or is a resident of California
person from California

Examples

Many Californians enjoy spending their weekends at the beach.
The company is looking to hire a Californian familiar with local regulations.
After moving to Los Angeles, she quickly felt like a true Californian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Yorker - a native or inhabitant of New York
