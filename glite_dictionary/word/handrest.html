handrest

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˈhændˌrɛst / (BrE)
A support designed to rest or support the hand, often attached to furniture or equipment for comfort or ergonomic purposes.
support for the hand

Examples

The new office chair has a padded handrest for added comfort.
She adjusted the handrest on her keyboard to reduce strain while typing.
Some artists use a handrest when painting delicate details.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armrest - a support for the arm, often attached to a chair
