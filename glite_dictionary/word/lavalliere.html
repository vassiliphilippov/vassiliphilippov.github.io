lavalliere

1. NOUN

/ ləˈvælɪˌɪr / (AmE), / ˌlævəˈlɪə / (BrE)
A jeweled pendant, often ornate, worn on a chain around the neck.
jeweled pendant on a chain

Examples

She wore a sparkling lavalliere to the gala, which caught everyone’s eye.
The antique shop displayed a beautiful Art Nouveau lavalliere in its window.
For her birthday, he gave her a gold lavalliere set with emeralds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pendant - an ornament that hangs from a chain worn around the neck

Etymology

From French Lavallière, after Louise de La Vallière, a mistress of Louis XIV, who popularized the style.
