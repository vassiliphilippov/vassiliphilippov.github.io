Proconsul

1. NOUN

/ proʊˈkɑːnsəl / (AmE), / prəʊˈkɒnsəl / (BrE)
a genus of extinct primitive African primates from the Miocene epoch, sometimes regarded as a subgenus of Dryopithecus
extinct Miocene genus of African primates

Examples

Fossils of Proconsul suggest it was one of the earliest ape-like primates in Africa.
The discovery of Proconsul helped scientists understand primate evolution during the Miocene epoch.
Many paleontologists debate the relationship between Proconsul and later hominids.

Etymology

From Latin 'proconsul' (a governor), name given by paleontologists for its distinguished status among fossil primates.
