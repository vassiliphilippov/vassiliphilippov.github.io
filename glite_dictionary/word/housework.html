housework

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌwɜːk / (BrE)
the regular work of cleaning, tidying, and maintaining a house or home
cleaning and maintaining a house

Examples

She spent her Saturday morning doing housework instead of relaxing.
Many couples share the housework to keep their home clean.
After a long day at the office, he still had housework to finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chores - routine tasks, especially in the home
  • Synonym: housekeeping - the management of household affairs

Etymology

Formed from 'house' + 'work', attested since the 17th century.
