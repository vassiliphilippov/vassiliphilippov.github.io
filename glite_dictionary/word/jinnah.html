Jinnah

1. PROPN

/ dʒɪˈnɑː / (AmE), / dʒɪˈnɑː / (BrE)
Muhammad Ali Jinnah (1876–1948), Indian statesman and the founder of Pakistan as a Muslim state.
founder of Pakistan; Indian statesman

Examples

Jinnah is widely recognized as the driving force behind the creation of Pakistan in 1947.
The legacy of Jinnah continues to influence Pakistani politics and society today.
On the anniversary of his birth, many Pakistanis honor Jinnah with ceremonies and public speeches.
