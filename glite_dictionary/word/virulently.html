virulently

1. ADV

/ ˈvɪrələntli / (AmE), / ˈvɪrʊləntli / (BrE)
In a bitter, hostile, or harshly harmful manner.
in a hostile or harmful way

Examples

She virulently attacked her opponent's character during the debate.
The protesters virulently opposed the new legislation in public statements.
The editor wrote virulently about government corruption in the weekly column.

Synset Examples

an old woman advanced a few paces to shake her fist virulently in my face

Related Terms

  • Synonym: virulent - extremely harmful or hostile
  • Antonym: mildly - in a gentle or moderate manner

Etymology

From 'virulent' (from Latin virulentus 'full of poison') + '-ly' adverbial suffix.
