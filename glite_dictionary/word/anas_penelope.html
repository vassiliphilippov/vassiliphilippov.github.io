Anas penelope

1. NOUN

/ ˌænəs pəˈnɛloʊpi / (AmE), / ˌænəs pəˈnɛləpi / (BrE)
a freshwater duck of Eurasia and northern Africa, commonly known as the Eurasian wigeon, related to mallards and teals
Eurasian wigeon duck species

Examples

Birdwatchers spotted several Anas penelope feeding along the quiet lakeshore.
The migratory pattern of Anas penelope brings it to northern Africa in the winter months.
A field guide can help distinguish Anas penelope from similar duck species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wigeon - common name for several freshwater duck species

Etymology

From Latin; 'Anas' meaning duck and 'penelope' referring to the species name.
