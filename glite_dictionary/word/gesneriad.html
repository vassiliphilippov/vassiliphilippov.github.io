gesneriad

1. NOUN

/ ɡɛzˈnɛriæd / (AmE), / ɡɛzˈnɪəriæd / (BrE)
any plant belonging to the family Gesneriaceae, including small tropical or subtropical shrubs, treelets, or epiphytic vines such as African violets and gloxinia
plant of the Gesneriaceae family

Examples

The African violet is a well-known gesneriad popular among indoor plant enthusiasts.
Many gesneriads thrive in humid, shaded environments similar to their native tropical habitats.
She specializes in cultivating rare gesneriad species from Southeast Asia.

Etymology

From the family name Gesneriaceae, after Swiss naturalist Conrad Gessner.
