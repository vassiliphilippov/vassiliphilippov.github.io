ninety-one

1. ADJ

/ ˌnaɪntiˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌnaɪntiˈwʌn / (BrE)
being one more than ninety; the cardinal number following ninety and preceding ninety-two
the number 91

Examples

There were ninety-one students in the auditorium for the lecture.
The temperature reached ninety-one degrees by midday, making it unusually hot.
He lives at ninety-one Maple Street, just around the corner.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: ninety - the cardinal number 90
  • Degree Higher: ninety-two - the cardinal number 92

2. NUM

/ ˌnaɪntiˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌnaɪntiˈwʌn / (BrE)
The number 91; one more than ninety.
cardinal number: 91

Examples

She finished the race in ninety-one minutes, beating her personal best.
There are ninety-one students enrolled in the science program this year.
The train departed at platform ninety-one just after noon.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: ninety-two - the number 92, one more than ninety-one
