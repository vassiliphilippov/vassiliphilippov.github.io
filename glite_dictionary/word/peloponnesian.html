Peloponnesian

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛləˌpoʊˈniʒən / (AmE), / ˌpɛləˌpɒˈniːziən / (BrE)
of or relating to the Peloponnese, a large peninsula in southern Greece
of or relating to the Peloponnese (Greece)

Examples

The Peloponnesian landscape is famous for its mountainous terrain and ancient ruins.
Many historians have studied the causes and consequences of the Peloponnesian War.
Her research focused on Peloponnesian culture during the classical period.

Synset Examples

Peloponnesian War

Etymology

From Peloponnese + English suffix -ian, meaning 'of or relating to.'
