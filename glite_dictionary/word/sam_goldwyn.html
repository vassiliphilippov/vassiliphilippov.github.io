Sam Goldwyn

1. PROPN

/ sæm ˈɡoʊldwɪn / (AmE), / sam ˈɡəʊldwɪn / (BrE)
(1882–1974) United States film producer, born in Poland, who founded his own film company and later merged with Louis B. Mayer.
American film producer (1882–1974)

Examples

Sam Goldwyn was renowned for his innovative contributions to the American film industry.
Many classic movies bear the unmistakable touch of Sam Goldwyn's production style.
Few producers shaped Hollywood’s golden age as significantly as Sam Goldwyn.
