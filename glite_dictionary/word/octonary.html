octonary

1. NOUN

/ ɑkˈtoʊˌnɛri / (AmE), / ɒkˈtəʊ.nəri / (BrE)
The number eight; the cardinal number equal to seven plus one.
the number eight

Examples

The code uses an octonary system for its calculations.
She grouped the samples into sets of octonary for her experiment.
An octonary sequence is often found in certain mathematical models.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eight - the number eight

Etymology

From Latin 'octonarius' (containing eight), from 'octo' (eight).
