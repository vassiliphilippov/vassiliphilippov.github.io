regimentals

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛdʒəˈmɛntlz / (AmE), / ˌrɛdʒɪˈmɛntlz / (BrE)
the military uniform and insignia worn by members of a regiment
military regiment uniform and insignia

Examples

The officers attended the formal dinner in their finest regimentals.
He wore his old regimentals for the Remembrance Day ceremony.
Their regimentals gleamed with freshly polished brass and vivid sashes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uniform - distinctive clothing worn by members of the same organization

Etymology

From 'regiment,' meaning a military unit, with the suffix '-al' (adjectival), pluralized.
