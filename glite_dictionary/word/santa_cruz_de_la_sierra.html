Santa Cruz de la Sierra

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæn.tə ˈkruːz də lɑ ˈsjɛr.ə / (AmE), / ˌsæn.tə ˈkruːz də lə ˈsjɛə.rə / (BrE)
a city in central Bolivia, the largest and most populous in the country, known for its economic importance and cultural diversity
city in central Bolivia

Examples

Many international companies have opened offices in Santa Cruz de la Sierra in recent years.
The climate in Santa Cruz de la Sierra is tropical, with hot summers and mild winters.
During the festival, visitors flock to Santa Cruz de la Sierra to experience its vibrant culture.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Holy Cross of the Mountain Range'.
