Joan Crawford

1. PROPN

/ dʒoʊn ˈkrɔːfərd / (AmE), / dʒəʊn ˈkrɔːfəd / (BrE)
(1908–1977) a prominent American film actress known for her roles in numerous Hollywood films, especially during the 1930s and 1940s.
American film actress (1908–1977)

Examples

Joan Crawford won the Academy Award for Best Actress for her performance in 'Mildred Pierce' in 1945.
Many classic films from Hollywood's golden age feature Joan Crawford in leading roles.
Despite fierce competition, Joan Crawford maintained her status as a top star for decades.
