shaver

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃeɪvər / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪvə / (BrE)
An electric device used for removing hair, especially from the face or body.
electric device for shaving hair

Examples

He bought a new shaver because his old one stopped working.
The electric shaver gives a smooth and comfortable shave every morning.
Travelers often pack a portable shaver in their luggage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: razor - a device used for removing hair, typically with a blade
