exercise

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛksərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
a task or problem designed to develop skill or understanding, especially as part of learning or practice
task for skill development or practice

Examples

Each chapter ends with several exercises to test your understanding.
The teacher assigned a grammar exercise for homework.
She completed the math exercises before going outside to play.

Synset Examples

you must work the examples at the end of each chapter in the textbook

Related Terms

  • Synonym: problem - a question or puzzle to be solved, usually for practice or learning

Etymology

From Latin exercitium (training, practice), from exercere (to keep busy, to exercise).
