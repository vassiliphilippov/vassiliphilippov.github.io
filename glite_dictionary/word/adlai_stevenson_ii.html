Adlai Stevenson II

1. PROPN

/ ˈæd.leɪ ˌstiː.vən.sən ðə ˈsɛk.ənd / (AmE), / ˈad.leɪ ˌstiː.vən.sən ðə ˈsek.ənd / (BrE)
(1900–1968) An American politician and diplomat, two-time Democratic presidential nominee, and U.S. ambassador to the United Nations.
American politician and diplomat (1900–1968)

Examples

Adlai Stevenson II was twice the Democratic nominee for President of the United States in the 1950s.
As ambassador to the United Nations, Adlai Stevenson II played a crucial role during the Cuban Missile Crisis.
Many historians admire the eloquence and integrity of Adlai Stevenson II throughout his political career.
