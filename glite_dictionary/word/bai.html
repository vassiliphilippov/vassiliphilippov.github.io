Bai

1. NOUN

/ baɪ / (AmE), / baɪ / (BrE)
the Tibeto-Burman language spoken by the Bai people in the Dali region of Yunnan, China.
Tibeto-Burman language of Dali, Yunnan

Examples

The Bai language is primarily spoken in northwestern Yunnan province.
Researchers are documenting the grammar and vocabulary of Bai.
Many places in Dali have signs in both Mandarin and Bai.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tibeto-Burman - a branch of the Sino-Tibetan language family
