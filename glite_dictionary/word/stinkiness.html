stinkiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪŋkinəs / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having a strong and unpleasant smell
state of being smelly or foul-smelling

Examples

We opened the fridge and were overwhelmed by the stinkiness of the forgotten food.
The stinkiness of his old socks made everyone leave the room.
No amount of air freshener could hide the stinkiness coming from the trash can.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stink - a strong unpleasant smell
  • Antonym: fragrance - a pleasant, sweet smell

Etymology

From 'stinky' (smelling bad) + '-ness' (noun suffix indicating state or quality).
