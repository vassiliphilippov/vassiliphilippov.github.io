nonretractile

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.rɪˈtræk.taɪl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈtræk.taɪl / (BrE)
Not capable of being drawn back or retracted.
not able to be retracted

Examples

The claws of the cheetah are nonretractile, unlike those of most other cats.
A nonretractile foreskin may require medical treatment in some cases.
Some insects have nonretractile mouthparts designed for constant feeding.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: retractile - able to be drawn back or retracted

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'non-' (not) + 'retractile' (able to be drawn back).
