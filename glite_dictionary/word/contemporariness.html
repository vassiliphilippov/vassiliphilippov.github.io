contemporariness

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtɛmpəˌrɛri.nəs / (AmE), / kənˈtɛmpərəri.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being contemporary or characteristic of the present time.
state of being contemporary or modern

Examples

The contemporariness of the artwork draws many young visitors to the gallery.
Her writing style has a refreshing contemporariness that appeals to today's readers.
Debates about contemporariness in architecture often focus on the use of innovative materials.

Synset Examples

Most obvious, perhaps, is the contemporariness of her language, for example, when wishing to instil organisational members with ‘a feel for the modern 1990s arm of brewing.’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modernity - the quality of being modern

Etymology

From contemporary + -ness, modeled on similar abstract nouns.
