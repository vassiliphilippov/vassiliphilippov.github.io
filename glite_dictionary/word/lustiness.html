lustiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌstɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈlʌstɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being strong, healthy, and full of energy; vigor
state of being strong and healthy

Examples

The lustiness of the children was evident as they ran around the playground screaming with joy.
After months of illness, his lustiness returned at last, delighting his family and friends.
The lustiness of the crops after the rainfall impressed all the farmers in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vigor - physical strength and good health
  • Antonym: frailty - the condition of being weak and delicate

Etymology

From 'lusty', from Middle English 'lusti', meaning 'pleasant, joyful, vigorous', ultimately from Old Norse 'lust' (pleasure, desire).
