NC

1. NOUN

/ ɛn si / (AmE), / ɛn si / (BrE)
The abbreviation for North Carolina, a state in the southeastern United States and one of the original 13 colonies.
abbreviation for North Carolina, US state

Examples

She moved to NC for college and has lived there ever since.
The shipment will be delivered to Raleigh, NC, next week.
Many people vacation on the coast of NC each summer.
