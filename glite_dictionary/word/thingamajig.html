thingamajig

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɪŋəməˌdʒɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋəməˌdʒɪɡ / (BrE)
a word used to refer to a person or thing whose name one has forgotten, does not know, or does not wish to mention
an unspecified or forgotten object
informal

Examples

Can you hand me that thingamajig over there on the workbench?
I don't know what this thingamajig is supposed to do, but it looks interesting.
Whenever he can't remember the name of a tool, he just calls it a thingamajig.

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gizmo - an object or device whose name is unknown or forgotten
  • Synonym: doodad - a small object, device, or ornament, often unnamed or forgotten

Etymology

Informal formation based on 'thing' and playful suffixation (early 20th century).
