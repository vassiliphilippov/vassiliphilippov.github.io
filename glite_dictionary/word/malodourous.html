malodourous

1. ADJ

/ ˌmælˈoʊdɚəs / (AmE), / ˌmælˈəʊdərəs / (BrE)
Having an unpleasant or foul smell.
having an unpleasant smell

Examples

The malodourous garbage made it impossible to enjoy the backyard barbecue.
Hours after the storm, a malodourous scent lingered in the damp basement.
He grimaced at the malodourous fumes coming from the open chemical container.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malodorous - having an unpleasant odor; foul-smelling
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'malodorous', from Latin 'malus' (bad) + 'odor' (smell).
