anglophil

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋɡləfɪl / (AmE), / ˈaŋɡləfɪl / (BrE)
A person who is very fond of England or English culture, people, and customs.
admirer of England or English culture

Examples

As an anglophil, she filled her home with British antiques and books by English authors.
Many anglophils enjoy celebrating traditional British holidays even if they live abroad.
His friends tease him about being an anglophil because he drinks tea every afternoon and watches English dramas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anglophile - a person who admires England and English things
  • Antonym: anglophobe - a person who dislikes England or English things

Etymology

From Greek 'Anglo-' (English) + '-phil' (lover, friend).
