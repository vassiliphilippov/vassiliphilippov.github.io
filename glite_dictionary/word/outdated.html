outdated

1. ADJ

/ aʊtˈdeɪtɪd / (AmE), / aʊtˈdeɪtɪd / (BrE)
no longer useful or fashionable; old-fashioned or obsolete
no longer useful or fashionable

Examples

Many older hospitals are filled with outdated equipment that needs to be replaced.
The report contains outdated information that is no longer accurate.
She prefers vintage clothes, but avoids anything that looks too outdated.

Synset Examples

obsolete words

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date
  • Antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times

Etymology

From 'out-' (outside, beyond) + 'dated', meaning having a date; pattern after 'out-of-date'.
