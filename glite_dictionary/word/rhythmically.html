rhythmically

1. ADV

/ ˈrɪð.mɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˈrɪð.mɪ.kli / (BrE)
in a rhythmic manner; with a regular repeated pattern of sound or movement
in a rhythmic manner

Examples

The leaves on the tree moved rhythmically in the gentle breeze.
The dancer tapped her feet rhythmically to the beat of the music.
He breathed rhythmically as he fell into a deep sleep.

Synset Examples

the chair rocked rhythmically back and forth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regularly - at uniform intervals or with even repetition; synonym in context of patterns

Etymology

From 'rhythm' (from Greek 'rhythmos') + '-ically', an adverbial suffix.
