assisted

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɪstɪd / (AmE), / əˈsɪstɪd / (BrE)
Receiving help or support; often used to describe actions or services characterized by the presence of assistance.
receiving help or support

Examples

The elderly residents moved into an assisted living facility last year.
She completed the task with assisted technology to help her overcome her disability.
He participated in assisted stretching to improve his flexibility.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unassisted - not receiving help or support
