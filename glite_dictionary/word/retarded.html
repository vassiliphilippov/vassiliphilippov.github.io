retarded

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈtɑrdɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈtɑːdɪd / (BrE)
(offensive) Having slowed or delayed mental, emotional, or physical development; formerly used to describe people with intellectual disabilities.
offensive; delayed development (outdated)
offensive archaic medical

Examples

The institution provided care for retarded children well into the late 20th century.
The law no longer uses the term retarded to describe intellectual disabilities.
She worked at a facility that supported families with retarded members, a term now considered outdated.

Synset Examples

providing a secure and sometimes happy life for the retarded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: developmentally disabled - having intellectual or developmental disabilities
  • Synonym: intellectually disabled - having significantly impaired intellectual ability

Etymology

From Latin retardare 'to make slow, delay.'
