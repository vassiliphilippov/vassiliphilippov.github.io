toweling

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaʊəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaʊəlɪŋ / (BrE)
fabric, usually of cotton or linen, used for making towels
fabric for making towels

Examples

She bought several meters of toweling to make bath towels for her new house.
The hotel uses high-quality toweling for its guest rooms and spa.
He prefers bathrobes made from soft, absorbent toweling fabric.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: towel - a piece of absorbent cloth or paper used for wiping or drying

Etymology

From 'towel' + '-ing' (noun suffix indicating material or action).
