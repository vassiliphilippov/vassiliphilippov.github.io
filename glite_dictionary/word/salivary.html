salivary

1. ADJ

/ ˈsæləˌvɛri / (AmE), / ˈsælɪvəri / (BrE)
relating to or producing saliva.
relating to saliva
medical

Examples

The doctor examined her salivary glands for signs of infection.
Reduced salivary flow can lead to dental problems.
Salivary amylase is an enzyme found in the salivary secretions of the mouth.

Synset Examples

salivary gland

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saliva - the watery fluid secreted in the mouth

Etymology

From Latin 'saliva' (spittle) + English '-ary' (pertaining to).
