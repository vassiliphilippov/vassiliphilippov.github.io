tendinous

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛn.də.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.dɪ.nəs / (BrE)
consisting of tendons or resembling a tendon
made of or like a tendon
medical

Examples

The surgeon noted the tendinous structure connecting the muscle to the bone.
After months of exercise, his arms looked lean and tendinous.
Some fishes have a tendinous band running along their bodies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tendon - a strong band of tissue connecting muscle to bone

Etymology

From Latin tendinosus (sinewy), from 'tendo' (tendon).
