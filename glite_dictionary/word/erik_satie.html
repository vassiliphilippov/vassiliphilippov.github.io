Erik Satie

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛrɪk sæˈti / (AmE), / ˈeɪrɪk sæˈtiː / (BrE)
(1866–1925) French composer known for his experimental approach to music and rejection of the Romantic style.
French composer (1866–1925), known for experimental music

Examples

Many musicians consider Erik Satie a pioneer of minimalism and modernism in classical music.
The gentle piano pieces by Erik Satie often evoke a dreamy and introspective atmosphere.
A concert featuring the works of Erik Satie drew a diverse audience appreciative of his unique style.
