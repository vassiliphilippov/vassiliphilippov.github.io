blameless

1. ADJ

/ ˈbleɪm.ləs / (AmE), / ˈbleɪm.ləs / (BrE)
Not deserving blame; free from guilt or wrongdoing.
free from blame or guilt

Examples

Despite the accusations, she maintained a blameless record throughout her career.
He led a blameless life, always striving to help others and avoid wrongdoing.
They proved the suspect was completely blameless in the incident.

Synset Examples

has lived a blameless life
of irreproachable character
an unimpeachable reputation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: guilty - responsible for a wrongdoing
  • Synonym: innocent - not guilty of a crime or offense

Etymology

From 'blame' + '-less', meaning without blame.
