thanksgiving

1. NOUN

/ θæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / θæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
a short prayer expressing thanks to God, said before a meal
prayer of thanks before a meal

Examples

Before dinner, we all sat quietly while Sarah offered a thanksgiving for the food.
It is customary in their family to say a brief thanksgiving before every meal.
His grandfather led the thanksgiving, expressing gratitude for their gathering and the meal.

Synset Examples

their youngest son said grace

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grace - a short prayer of thanks said before or after a meal

Etymology

From Old English 'þancsagu' (thanksgiving), influenced by Middle English 'thanksgif' (giving of thanks).
