enfolding

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈfoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or process of wrapping or surrounding something closely
the act of wrapping or enclosing

Examples

The enfolding of the forest mist made the morning seem mysterious.
Her enfolding in the warm blanket soothed the child instantly.
There was a gentle enfolding of light as the sun rose above the hill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embracing - the act of holding closely in one's arms
  • Antonym: unfolding - the process of opening or revealing something that was closed

Etymology

From the verb 'enfold' plus the suffix '-ing', forming a noun.
