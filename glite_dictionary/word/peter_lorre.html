Peter Lorre

1. PROPN

/ ˈpiːtər ˈlɔːri / (AmE), / ˈpiːtə ˈlɒri / (BrE)
(1904–1964) Hungarian-born American actor known for playing sinister or eccentric characters in films such as 'M' and 'The Maltese Falcon'.
Hungarian-American actor (1904–1964) known for sinister roles

Examples

Peter Lorre became famous for his chilling performance in the film 'M'.
Film historians often cite Peter Lorre as a master of portraying sinister characters on screen.
Many modern actors admire the unique style that Peter Lorre brought to his roles.
