Bengal

1. PROPN

/ bɛnˈɡɔl / (AmE), / bɛnˈɡɑːl / (BrE)
a region in South Asia, now divided between Bangladesh in the east and the Indian state of West Bengal in the west.
region of South Asia (Bangladesh and West Bengal)

Examples

The fertile plains of Bengal have been known for their rice production for centuries.
He travelled to Bengal to study its diverse wildlife, including the famous Bengal tiger.
After the partition, East Bengal became what is now Bangladesh.

Etymology

From Persian 'Bangāla', probably derived from Sanskrit 'Vaṅga', the ancient name of the region.
