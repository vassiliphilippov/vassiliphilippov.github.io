undulatory

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌn.dʒəˌleɪ.tɔːr.i / (AmE), / ʌnˈdjʊlətəri / (BrE)
having a wave-like form, outline, or motion; moving or shaped in a series of gentle curves or waves
having a wave-like form or motion

Examples

The snake moved in an undulatory pattern across the sand, curving back and forth gracefully.
The undulatory surface of the hills created a beautiful, rippling landscape at sunset.
Her undulatory hair seemed to move like gentle waves as she walked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wavy - having a curving or undulating form

Etymology

From Latin undulatus (wavy), from unda (wave).
