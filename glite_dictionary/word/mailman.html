mailman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlˌman / (BrE)
a man whose job is to deliver mail to people's homes and businesses
man who delivers mail
american

Examples

The mailman delivered the package to my front door this morning.
Every afternoon, the mailman sorts letters before starting his route.
Children waved as the mailman walked down the street carrying his satchel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postman - person who delivers mail (British English)

Etymology

Formed from 'mail' (referring to letters and parcels) and 'man' (a male person). Related to the introduction of organized postal delivery in the United States.
