cyder

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdə / (BrE)
an alcoholic drink made from the fermented juice of apples; variant spelling of 'cider'
variant spelling of cider (apple drink)

Examples

They brewed their own cyder using apples from the orchard.
The tavern served both ale and cyder to its patrons.
He enjoyed a glass of crisp, sweet cyder on a summer afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cider - an alcoholic drink made from fermented apple juice

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'cider', from Old French 'cidre', from Medieval Latin 'sicera' (fermented drink).
