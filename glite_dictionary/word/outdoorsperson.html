outdoorsperson

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊt.dɔrzˌpɝː.sən / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.dɔːzˌpɜː.sən / (BrE)
a person who enjoys and regularly participates in outdoor activities such as hunting, fishing, or camping
person who spends time outdoors

Examples

As an avid outdoorsperson, she spends most weekends hiking and canoeing in the wilderness.
The magazine offers tips for every type of outdoorsperson, from campers to bird-watchers.
He became an accomplished outdoorsperson after years of exploring national parks and forests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outdoorsman - a man who likes outdoor activities
  • Synonym: outdoorswoman - a woman who likes outdoor activities

Etymology

Formed from 'outdoors' + 'person', as a gender-neutral alternative to 'outdoorsman' or 'outdoorswoman'.
