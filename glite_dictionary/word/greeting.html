greeting

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡriːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːtɪŋ / (BrE)
an expression of good will or acknowledgment, especially when meeting someone
expression of good will on meeting

Examples

She gave him a warm greeting as he entered the room.
Holiday greetings were exchanged among the neighbors.
Her cheerful greeting made everyone feel welcome.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salutation - a gesture or utterance made as a greeting or acknowledgment of another's arrival or departure
  • Antonym: farewell - an expression used to say goodbye

Etymology

From Old English *grētung* (a greeting), derived from *grētan* (to greet).
