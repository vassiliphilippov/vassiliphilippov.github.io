Rana pipiens

1. NOUN

/ ˌreɪnə pɪˈpiː.ɛnz / (AmE), / ˌreɪnə pɪˈpiː.ɛnz / (BrE)
a common North American green or brownish frog with white-edged dark oval spots, also known as the northern leopard frog
a spotted North American frog species

Examples

During the biology field trip, we spotted a Rana pipiens by the pond's edge.
The distinctive markings of the Rana pipiens help it blend into its grassy habitat.
Researchers monitor Rana pipiens populations as indicators of environmental health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leopard frog - common name for several North American frogs with spotted patterns

Etymology

From Latin; 'Rana' means frog, and 'pipiens' means peeping or chirping, referring to its call.
