cosmetician

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːz.məˈtɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌkɒz.məˈtɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to give beauty treatments in a beauty salon or parlor
beauty-treatment professional

Examples

Julia has dreamed of becoming a cosmetician since she was a teenager.
The cosmetician recommended a new skin care routine for her client.
Every cosmetician in the salon receives special training in makeup application.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautician - a person whose job is to style hair and provide beauty treatments

Etymology

From French 'cosméticien', derived from 'cosmétique' (cosmetic).

2. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːzməˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a person trained to sell or apply cosmetics professionally
someone who sells or applies cosmetics

Examples

I visited a cosmetician to get advice on skincare products.
The cosmetician recommended a foundation that matched my skin tone perfectly.
She trains as a cosmetician and specializes in bridal makeup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautician - a person whose job is to style hair and apply beauty treatments

Etymology

From French *cosméticien*, from *cosmétique* (cosmetic).
