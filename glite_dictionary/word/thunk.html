thunk

1. NOUN

/ θʌŋk / (AmE), / θʌŋk / (BrE)
a dull, hollow sound made by something hitting a surface
dull, hollow sound

Examples

The branch fell with a loud thunk onto the roof.
She dropped her suitcase, and it landed with a satisfying thunk.
There was a thunk from the next room as the book hit the floor.

Synset Examples

the basketball made a thunk as it hit the rim

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thud - a dull, heavy sound, often made by a falling object
  • Synonym: clunk - a heavy, muffled metallic sound
