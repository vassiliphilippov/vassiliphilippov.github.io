Cristobal Colon

1. PROPN

/ kriˈstoʊbəl koʊˈloʊn / (AmE), / kriˈstəʊbəl kəˈloʊn / (BrE)
the Spanish name for Christopher Columbus, the Italian navigator who discovered the New World in 1492 while sailing for Spain
Spanish name for Christopher Columbus

Examples

The ship was named Cristobal Colon in honor of the famed explorer.
Many Latin American countries celebrate the arrival of Cristobal Colon each October.
In Spanish history books, Cristobal Colon is credited with discovering America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christopher Columbus - Italian navigator who discovered the New World in the service of Spain

Etymology

Spanish form of the explorer’s name, equivalent to Italian Cristoforo Colombo and English Christopher Columbus.
