Humvee

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌm.vi / (AmE), / ˈhʌm.vi / (BrE)
A high-mobility, multipurpose military vehicle with four-wheel drive, commonly used by the U.S. armed forces.
military four-wheel drive vehicle

Examples

The convoy consisted of several Humvees traveling through the desert at sunrise.
During the training exercise, soldiers quickly loaded supplies into the Humvee.
After the war, some surplus Humvees were sold to civilians for off-road use.

Etymology

From the acronym HMMWV (High Mobility Multipurpose Wheeled Vehicle), pronounced as 'Humvee'.
