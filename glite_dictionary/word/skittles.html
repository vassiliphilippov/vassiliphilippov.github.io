skittles

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪt̬əlz / (AmE), / ˈskɪtəlz / (BrE)
A traditional game, chiefly British, in which players roll a ball down an alley to knock over nine wooden pins.
British bowling game with nine pins
british

Examples

We spent the afternoon at the pub, playing a lively game of skittles.
Traditional skittles are still popular in some English villages.
He knocked down all nine pins in one turn of skittles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowling - a game in which players roll a ball to knock down pins

Etymology

From Middle English 'skyttel' (pin, skittle), of Scandinavian origin, related to Old Norse 'skytla' (to shoot, throw).
