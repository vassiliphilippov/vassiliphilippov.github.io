registrant

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdʒɪstrənt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstrənt / (BrE)
A person who is officially entered on a register or list, often acquiring certain rights or privileges as a result.
a person entered on an official register

Examples

Each registrant must provide valid identification upon arrival.
The conference sent confirmation emails to every registrant.
If a registrant cancels, their fee will be refunded in full.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: registrar - an official who maintains a register or official records
  • Degree Lower: applicant - a person who applies for something

Etymology

From 'register' + '-ant', modeled after French 'registrant' and influenced by similar agent nouns.
