Duse

1. PROPN

/ ˈduːzeɪ / (AmE), / ˈduːzeɪ / (BrE)
(1858–1924) Italian actress renowned for her performances in tragic roles, particularly in late 19th and early 20th century theatre.
Italian actress (1858–1924)

Examples

Duse was acclaimed for her emotional depth and naturalism on stage.
Many acting students still study the techniques of Duse to this day.
