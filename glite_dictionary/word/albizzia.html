albizzia

1. NOUN

/ ælˈbɪziə / (AmE), / ælˈbɪziə / (BrE)
Any of various trees of the genus Albizia, known for their feathery leaves and often used as ornamental or shade trees in warm climates.
tree of the genus Albizia

Examples

The garden featured a beautiful albizzia with delicate, fern-like foliage.
Several species of albizzia are valued for their fast growth and shade.
An albizzia tree can transform a landscape with its airy pink flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mimosa - any of various tropical or subtropical plants of the genus Mimosa or related genera

Etymology

Named after Filippo degli Albizzi, an Italian nobleman who introduced the plant to Europe in the 18th century.
