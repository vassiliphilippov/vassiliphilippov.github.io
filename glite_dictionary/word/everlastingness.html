everlastingness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of lasting forever; eternal duration.
the quality of lasting forever

Examples

The everlastingness of the universe has fascinated philosophers for centuries.
She believed in the everlastingness of true love.
Some religions promise the everlastingness of the soul after death.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eternity - infinite or unending time
  • Antonym: impermanence - the state of not lasting forever

Etymology

From 'everlasting' + '-ness'.
