stomachic

1. ADJ

/ stəˈmækɪk / (AmE), / stəˈmækɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affecting the stomach.
relating to the stomach
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed a stomachic medicine to ease her digestive discomfort.
Some herbs are valued for their stomachic properties in traditional medicine.
He suffered from a stomachic disorder that affected his appetite.

Synset Examples

gastric ulcer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gastric - relating to the stomach

Etymology

From Latin stomachicus, from Greek stomachikos (relating to the stomach), from stomachos (stomach).
