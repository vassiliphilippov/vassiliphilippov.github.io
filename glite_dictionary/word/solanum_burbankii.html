Solanum burbankii

1. NOUN

/ soʊˈleɪnəm bɜːrˈbænkiː / (AmE), / səˈleɪnəm bɜːˈbæŋkiː / (BrE)
an improved garden variety of black nightshade with small edible orange or black berries
garden variety of black nightshade

Examples

The gardener planted Solanum burbankii to enjoy the colorful berries throughout the summer.
Some chefs use the berries of Solanum burbankii in unique culinary creations.
Solanum burbankii is favored by some horticulturists for its improved fruit quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black nightshade - a species of nightshade with black berries, some edible

Etymology

From Latin genus name 'Solanum' (nightshade) and named after Luther Burbank, American botanist.
