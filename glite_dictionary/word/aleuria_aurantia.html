Aleuria aurantia

1. NOUN

/ əˈlʊəriə ɔːˈræntiə / (AmE), / əˈljʊəriə ɔːˈrantɪə / (BrE)
A species of fungus with bright orange, cup- or saucer-shaped fruiting bodies, commonly known as the orange peel fungus.
a brightly colored orange cup fungus

Examples

The striking Aleuria aurantia was easily spotted among the leaf litter in the forest.
Mushroom enthusiasts often look for Aleuria aurantia because of its vivid orange color.
You might mistake Aleuria aurantia for bits of orange peel scattered on the ground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange peel fungus - common name for Aleuria aurantia

Etymology

From Latin: genus Aleuria and species aurantia meaning 'orange' in color.
