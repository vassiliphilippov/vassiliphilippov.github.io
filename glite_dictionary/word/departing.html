departing

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈpɑrtɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person, vehicle, or event) that is about to leave or has just left a place.
leaving or going out

Examples

The departing flight was delayed by forty minutes.
Please wait for all departing passengers to exit before boarding.
We waved at the departing train from the platform.

Synset Examples

the departing train
an outward journey
outward-bound ships

Related Terms

  • Antonym: arriving - (of a person, vehicle, or event) coming to a place
  • Synonym: outbound - leaving a place or departing on a journey

Etymology

Present participle of 'depart,' from Old French partir, from Latin partire (to divide, distribute).
