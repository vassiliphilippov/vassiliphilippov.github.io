buntal

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊn.tɑl / (AmE), / ˈbʌn.tæl / (BrE)
a fine, white fiber from the stalks of unopened leaves of talipot palms in the Philippines, used especially for making hats
Philippine palm fiber for hats

Examples

The famous buntal hats are prized for their durability and fine texture.
She bought a buntal weaving kit during her visit to the Philippines.
Local artisans demonstrated how to extract buntal fibers from the palm leaves.

Etymology

From Tagalog 'buntál', referring to the palm fiber.
