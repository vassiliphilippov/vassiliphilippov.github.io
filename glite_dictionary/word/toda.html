2. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊdə / (AmE), / ˈtəʊdə / (BrE)
A member of a pastoral people native to the Nilgiri Hills in southern India.
member of a Nilgiri Hills people, India

Examples

The Toda are renowned for their distinctive oval-shaped huts and rich traditions.
A Toda woman demonstrated their unique embroidery techniques at the cultural exhibition.
Many anthropologists have studied Toda rituals and social customs in the Nilgiri region.
