Aku

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːkuː / (AmE), / ˈɑːkuː / (BrE)
a Kwa language spoken by the Yoruba people in southwestern Nigeria
a Yoruba-related language of Nigeria

Examples

She is fluent in both Yoruba and Aku, enabling her to communicate with diverse communities.
The linguist conducted fieldwork among Aku speakers in southwestern Nigeria.
Researchers are working to preserve the Aku language through documentation and education projects.
