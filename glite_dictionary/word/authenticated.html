authenticated

1. ADJ

/ əˈθɛntɪˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
proved to be genuine or real, especially through official verification or evidence.
proved genuine or real

Examples

The documents were authenticated by a team of experts before the auction.
Only authenticated users can access sensitive information in the database.
She received an authenticated letter confirming her acceptance into the program.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genuine - truly what something is said to be; authentic
  • Antonym: forged - copied fraudulently; fake or counterfeit

Etymology

From the verb 'authenticate', from Medieval Latin 'authenticatus', past participle of 'authenticare' (to make authentic).
