wheeled

1. ADJ

/ wild / (AmE), / wiÀêld / (BrE)
having wheels; equipped with wheels, often used in combination to describe vehicles or machines with a specified number of wheels.
having wheels; equipped with wheels

Examples

The wheeled cart made it easy to transport the heavy boxes across the warehouse.
Modern luggage often comes with a wheeled design for greater convenience while traveling.
They purchased a wheeled walker to help their grandmother move around safely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wheel - A circular object that revolves on an axle and is fixed below a vehicle or other object to enable it to move easily over the ground.

Etymology

From 'wheel' (noun) + '-ed' suffix, indicating possessing or provided with wheels.
