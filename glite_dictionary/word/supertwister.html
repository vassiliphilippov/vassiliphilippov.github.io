supertwister

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːpərˌtwɪstər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌtwɪstə / (BrE)
an exceptionally powerful tornado capable of causing extremely severe damage
extremely powerful tornado

Examples

Supertwisters are fortunately rare, but their impact can be catastrophic when they do occur.
Meteorologists studied the path of the supertwister that swept through the Midwest.
Local officials issued warnings as conditions became favorable for a possible supertwister formation.

Synset Examples

supertwisters are fortunately rare

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tornado - a violent rotating column of air extending from a thunderstorm to the ground

Etymology

Formed from 'super-' (meaning above, beyond, or excessive) + 'twister' (a colloquial term for tornado).
