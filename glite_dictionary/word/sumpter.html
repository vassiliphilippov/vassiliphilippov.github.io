sumpter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmp.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmp.tə / (BrE)
an animal, especially a mule or horse, used for carrying loads or baggage
pack animal for carrying loads
archaic

Examples

The caravan loaded their goods onto the sumpter before setting out across the mountains.
In medieval times, a sumpter was essential for transporting supplies on long journeys.
They watched as the sumpter made its way slowly up the steep trail, carrying heavy baskets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pack animal - an animal used for carrying loads; synonym for sumpter
  • Synonym: beast of burden - an animal used for carrying heavy loads or pulling loads

Etymology

From Old French 'somier' (beast of burden), from Latin 'sagma' (packsaddle).
