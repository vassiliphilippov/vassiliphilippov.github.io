butterbean

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚˌbin / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌbiːn / (BrE)
A small, flat bean with a mild flavor, similar to a lima bean; often used as a vegetable in cooking.
type of edible bean, similar to lima bean

Examples

We added fresh butterbeans to the stew for extra flavor.
My grandmother grows butterbeans every summer in her garden.
The recipe calls for one cup of cooked butterbeans as a side dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lima bean - a flat, edible, white or green bean
