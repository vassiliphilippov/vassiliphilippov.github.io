speakable

1. ADJ

/ ˈspiːkəbəl / (AmE), / ˈspiːkəbl / (BrE)
able to be spoken or expressed in words; capable of being uttered.
able to be spoken or uttered

Examples

The news was finally speakable after years of secrecy.
She put her feelings into speakable words for the first time.
Some topics are still not speakable in polite society.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unspeakable - impossible to express in words; too bad or shocking to be spoken

Etymology

Formed from 'speak' + '-able', an English suffix denoting capability.
