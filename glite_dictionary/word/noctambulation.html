noctambulation

1. NOUN

/ nɑkˌtæmbjuˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / nɒkˌtæmbjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of walking while asleep; sleepwalking
walking while asleep (sleepwalking)
medical

Examples

The patient's history revealed repeated episodes of noctambulation during childhood.
Research has shown that stress may increase the frequency of noctambulation in some individuals.
Doctors advised the family to secure doors and windows to ensure safety during episodes of noctambulation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleepwalking - the act of walking while asleep

Etymology

From Latin 'noct-' (night) + 'ambulatio' (walking), meaning 'night walking'.
