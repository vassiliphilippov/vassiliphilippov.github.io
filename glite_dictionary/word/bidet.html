bidet

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈdeɪ / (AmE), / bɪˈdeɪ / (BrE)
a low basin or bathroom fixture used for washing the genitals and anal area, typically after using the toilet
low basin for washing after using the toilet

Examples

Many European hotels have a bidet in the bathroom next to the toilet.
She had never used a bidet before her trip to France.
The plumber installed a new bidet in our master bathroom.

Etymology

From French 'bidet' (a pony, due to its straddling posture), from Old French 'bider' (to trot).
