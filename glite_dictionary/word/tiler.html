tiler

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪlə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to lay tiles on floors, walls, or roofs
person who lays tiles as a job

Examples

The tiler finished installing the new ceramic floor in the kitchen yesterday.
She hired a professional tiler to redo the bathroom walls.
As a skilled tiler, he takes pride in creating precise and beautiful patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tile setter - a worker who lays tiles

Etymology

From tile + -er (suffix forming agent nouns).
