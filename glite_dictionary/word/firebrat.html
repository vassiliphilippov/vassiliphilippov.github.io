firebrat

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪrˌbræt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəbræt / (BrE)
A small, wingless insect (Thermobia domestica) with a silvery appearance, living in warm, humid places such as near furnaces or water heaters.
a small, silvery insect found in warm, humid places

Examples

We found a firebrat hiding behind the hot water heater in the basement.
Firebrats prefer environments with consistently high temperatures and humidity.
The pest control expert identified the tiny insect as a firebrat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silverfish - a related wingless insect often found in damp places

Etymology

Formed from 'fire' + 'brat', indicating the insect's liking for warmth.
