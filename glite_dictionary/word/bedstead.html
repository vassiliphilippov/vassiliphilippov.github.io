bedstead

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛd.stɛd / (AmE), / ˈbɛd.stɛd / (BrE)
the frame that supports a bed's mattress and base, often with headboard and footboard
the frame of a bed

Examples

The old iron bedstead creaked every time I turned over during the night.
She inherited a Victorian bedstead from her grandmother, which now decorates the guest room.
They bought a wooden bedstead to match the antique dresser in their bedroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bed frame - a frame that supports the mattress and base of a bed

Etymology

From Middle English 'bedsted', from 'bed' + 'sted' (place, stand).
