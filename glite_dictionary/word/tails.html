tails

1. NOUN

/ teɪlz / (AmE), / teɪlz / (BrE)
A man's formal evening suit with a tailcoat, typically worn on very formal occasions.
man's formal evening suit with tailcoat

Examples

The ambassador attended the gala in full tails and a crisp white shirt.
It was the sort of event that required tails, so he rented a suit for the occasion.
He looked quite distinguished in his tails at the opera.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white tie - the most formal evening dress code, typically requiring a tailcoat
  • Degree Lower: tuxedo - less formal evening suit for men, also known as 'dinner jacket'
