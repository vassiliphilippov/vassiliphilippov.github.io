winsome

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪnsəm / (AmE), / ˈwɪnsəm / (BrE)
attractive or appealing in a charming, childlike, or innocent way
charmingly childlike or appealing

Examples

Her winsome smile quickly put everyone at ease in the crowded room.
The child's winsome curiosity charmed the teacher.
He gave a winsome grin that made it hard to stay annoyed with him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charming - pleasant or attractive in a way that delights others
  • Antonym: unattractive - not pleasing or appealing in appearance or manner

Etymology

From Old English 'wynsum' (pleasant, delightful), from 'wynn' (joy) + '-sum' (some).
