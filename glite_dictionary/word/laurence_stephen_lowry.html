Laurence Stephen Lowry

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɔːrəns ˈstiːvən ˈlaʊri / (AmE), / ˈlɒrəns ˈstiːvən ˈlaʊri / (BrE)
(1887–1976) An English painter known for his distinctive urban landscapes and depictions of industrial scenes in North West England.
English painter famous for urban landscapes

Examples

Laurence Stephen Lowry is celebrated for his paintings of industrial scenes populated by matchstick figures.
An exhibition of Laurence Stephen Lowry's work attracted thousands of visitors to the gallery.
Many art critics regard Laurence Stephen Lowry as one of the most distinctive British artists of the twentieth century.
