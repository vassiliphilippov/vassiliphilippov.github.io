SVR

1. NOUN

/ ɛs vi ˈɑr / (AmE), / ɛs vi ˈɑː / (BrE)
the Russian Foreign Intelligence Service, responsible for external intelligence operations and analysis, and international intelligence cooperation
Russian Foreign Intelligence Service

Examples

The SVR has played a significant role in Russia's foreign intelligence operations since the fall of the Soviet Union.
A number of international incidents have been linked to the activities of the SVR.
As a former SVR officer, she had access to sensitive foreign intelligence information.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: FSB - Russian Federal Security Service (internal intelligence)

Etymology

Abbreviation of Russian 'Служба внешней разведки' (Sluzhba vneshney razvedki, 'Service of External Intelligence').
