Heinrich Schliemann

1. PROPN

/ ˈhaɪn.rɪk ˈʃliː.mɑːn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪn.rɪx ˈʃliː.mann / (BrE)
(1822–1890) A German archaeologist best known for discovering the ancient city of Troy and excavating Mycenae.
German archaeologist; discovered Troy

Examples

Heinrich Schliemann is credited with uncovering the legendary city of Troy.
Many of Heinrich Schliemann's archaeological methods are still debated by scholars today.
The gold mask found at Mycenae is often associated with the discoveries of Heinrich Schliemann.
