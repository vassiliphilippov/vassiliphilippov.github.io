A-OK

1. ADJ

/ ˌeɪ.oʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.əʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
in perfect condition or order; completely satisfactory
in perfect condition; completely satisfactory
informal american

Examples

After examining the engine, the mechanic said it was A-OK and ready to go.
The pilot confirmed that all systems were A-OK before takeoff.
Don’t worry about the package—it's A-OK and should arrive on time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellent - very good; of the highest quality
  • Synonym: perfect - flawless; without any faults or defects
  • Antonym: bad - of poor quality; not satisfactory

Etymology

Originated in U.S. astronaut slang, popularized in the 1960s from the phrase 'A-OK', with 'A' thought to stand for 'all'.
