stunning

1. ADJ

/ ˈstʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely impressive, beautiful, or attractive in appearance.
very beautiful or impressive

Examples

The bride looked absolutely stunning in her wedding dress.
They enjoyed a stunning view of the mountains from their hotel room.
Her stunning performance left the audience speechless.

Synset Examples

quite stunning with large dark eyes and a beautiful high-bosomed figure
stunning photographs of Canada's wilderness areas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautiful - pleasing the senses or mind aesthetically
  • Synonym: gorgeous - very beautiful or attractive
  • Antonym: plain - lacking beauty or adornment; not attractive

Etymology

Present participle of 'stun', meaning to astonish or shock.

/ ˈstʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
causing shock, bewilderment, or insensibility; extremely surprising or powerful in effect
causing shock or insensibility

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms
