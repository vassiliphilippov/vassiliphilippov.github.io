mercuric

1. ADJ

/ mərˈkjʊrɪk / (AmE), / məˈkjʊərɪk / (BrE)
containing mercury, especially with a valency of two; relating to or derived from mercury.
containing or relating to mercury
medical

Examples

Mercuric chloride is a highly toxic compound once used as a disinfectant.
The factory was fined for releasing mercuric waste into the river.
Scientists studied the effects of mercuric compounds on aquatic life.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: mercurous - containing mercury with a valency of one

Etymology

From Latin 'mercurius' (Mercury, the Roman god and the element).
