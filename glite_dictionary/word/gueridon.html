gueridon

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡɛrɪˈdɑn / (AmE), / ˌɡɪərɪˈdɒn / (BrE)
A small, usually round, table used especially as a decorative or occasional piece.
small round table

Examples

The ornate gueridon in the hallway displayed a vase of fresh flowers.
They placed their coffee cups on a marble-topped gueridon by the window.
Antique stores often have at least one elegant gueridon for sale.

Etymology

From French 'guéridon', originally a term for a blackamoor figure supporting a stand, later meaning the table itself.
