irreconcilable

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌrɛkənˈsaɪləbəl / (AmE), / ɪˌrɛkənˈsaɪləbəl / (BrE)
(of differences or people) so different that they cannot be brought into agreement or made compatible.
impossible to reconcile; incompatible

Examples

The couple divorced citing irreconcilable differences that could not be resolved.
Their political beliefs were irreconcilable, making cooperation between them impossible.
Some goals are simply irreconcilable with each other in the project timeline.

Synset Examples

irreconcilable differences

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incompatible - unable to exist or work together in harmony
  • Antonym: reconcilable - able to be made compatible or consistent

Etymology

From French irréconciliable, from Latin irreconciliabilis (not able to be reconciled).
