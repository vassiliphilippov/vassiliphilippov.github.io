nonpregnant

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈprɛɡnənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈprɛɡnənt / (BrE)
Not pregnant; not carrying a developing fetus within the body.
not pregnant

Examples

The doctor confirmed that she was nonpregnant after running several tests.
Participation in the study was limited to nonpregnant women.
He asked if the medication was safe for nonpregnant individuals.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pregnant - having a child or young developing inside the body

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' (not) to 'pregnant'.
