amatory

1. ADJ

/ ˈæməˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈamətəri / (BrE)
relating to or expressing sexual love or desire; romantic or erotic.
relating to sexual love or romance

Examples

The novel was filled with amatory intrigue and passionate confessions.
He wrote several amatory poems dedicated to his beloved.
The film's amatory scenes were tastefully handled, avoiding overt explicitness.

Synset Examples

her amatory affairs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amorous - showing, feeling, or relating to sexual desire
  • Synonym: romantic - conducive to or characterized by the expression of love

Etymology

From Latin "amatorius" (relating to a lover), from "amator" (lover), from "amare" (to love).
