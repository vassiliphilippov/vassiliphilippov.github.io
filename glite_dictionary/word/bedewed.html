bedewed

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈduːd / (AmE), / bɪˈdjuːd / (BrE)
covered or made wet with dew; moistened as if by dew.
wet with dew

Examples

The grass was bedewed in the early morning sunlight.
She walked through the bedewed garden, her shoes getting damp.
The petals appeared freshly bedewed after the cool night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moistened - made slightly wet
  • Antonym: dry - not wet or moist; lacking moisture

Etymology

From 'bedew' (to moisten with dew), prefix 'be-' + 'dew'.
