undecipherably

1. ADV

/ ˌʌndɪˈsaɪfɚəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈsaɪfərəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is impossible to read or understand; in an illegible or incomprehensible manner.
in an incomprehensible or illegible way

Examples

The doctor wrote the prescription undecipherably, making it hard for the pharmacist to fill.
The ancient text was undecipherably faded after centuries of weathering.
He mumbled undecipherably under his breath, so no one could catch what he said.

Synset Examples

this student writes illegibly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illegibly - in a way that is difficult or impossible to read

Etymology

From undecipherable (from decipher + -able) + -ly.
