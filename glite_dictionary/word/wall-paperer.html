wall-paperer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔlˌpeɪpəɹɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlˌpeɪpərə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to apply wallpaper to walls as a form of interior decoration
person who applies wallpaper

Examples

The wall-paperer spent the entire morning covering the living room walls with a floral pattern.
After repainting the ceiling, they hired a wall-paperer to finish the renovation.
My grandfather worked as a wall-paperer for over thirty years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decorator - person who decorates interiors

Etymology

From 'wallpaper' + agent suffix '-er'.
