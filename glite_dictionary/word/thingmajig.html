thingmajig

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɪŋməˌdʒɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋməˌdʒɪɡ / (BrE)
an object or device whose name is unknown or has been forgotten
unidentified or forgotten object
informal

Examples

Can you pass me that thingmajig over there on the shelf?
I need a thingmajig to fix the loose screw, but I can't find one.
She pointed to the odd thingmajig and asked if anyone knew what it was.

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thingamajig - thing whose name is forgotten or unknown
  • Synonym: gadget - small mechanical device or tool
  • Synonym: whatchamacallit - thing whose name one cannot recall

Etymology

Alteration of 'thingamajig', a humorous formation from 'thing' + fanciful suffix.
