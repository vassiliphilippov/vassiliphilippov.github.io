stanchly

1. ADV

/ ˈstæntʃli / (AmE), / ˈstɑːntʃli / (BrE)
In a firm, loyal, and determined way; with strong and steadfast support or conviction.
in a firm, loyal, and determined way

Examples

She stanchly supported her colleagues during the crisis despite mounting criticism.
Throughout the trial, he stanchly maintained his innocence.
The organization stanchly opposes any form of discrimination.

Synset Examples

he staunchly defended his principles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staunchly - in a loyal and firm manner (alternative spelling)
  • Synonym: loyally - in a loyal manner

Etymology

From 'staunch' (firm, loyal) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
