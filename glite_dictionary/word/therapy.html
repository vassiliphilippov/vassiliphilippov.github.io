therapy

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
the treatment of physical or mental health problems, especially by medical or psychological means rather than by surgery
treatment for health problems
medical

Examples

After her injury, she received physical therapy to regain strength in her leg.
Meditation is sometimes used as a form of therapy for stress reduction.
The new drug shows promise as an effective therapy for cancer patients.

Synset Examples

the quarterback is undergoing treatment for a knee injury
he tried every treatment the doctors suggested
heat therapy gave the best relief

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treatment - medical care to cure or manage an illness or injury
  • Degree Higher: cure - a treatment that ends a disease permanently

Etymology

From Greek therapeia (healing, medical treatment).
