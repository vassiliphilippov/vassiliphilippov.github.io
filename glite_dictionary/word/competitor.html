competitor

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpɛtɪtɚ / (AmE), / kəmˈpɛtɪtə / (BrE)
a person, team, or company that competes against others, especially in a contest, sport, or business
someone who competes with others

Examples

Our main competitor launched a new product last week.
Every competitor must register before the race begins.
The company is searching for ways to outperform its biggest competitor in the market.

Synset Examples

he had respect for his rivals
he wanted to know what the competition was doing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rival - a person or thing competing with another for the same objective
  • Synonym: opponent - someone who competes against or fights another in a contest, game, or argument

Etymology

From Latin 'competitor' (rival), from 'competere' (to strive together).
