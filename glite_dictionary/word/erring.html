erring

1. ADJ

/ ˈɜrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛːrɪŋ / (BrE)
prone to making mistakes or being in error; capable of going astray
inclined to make mistakes

Examples

He was quick to forgive the erring student for her mistake.
As humans are erring creatures, perfection is rarely attainable.
The judge showed mercy to the erring defendant, hoping he would change his ways.

Synset Examples

all men are error-prone

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fallible - capable of making mistakes or being wrong

Etymology

Present participle of 'err' from Latin 'errare' meaning 'to wander' or 'to make a mistake'.
