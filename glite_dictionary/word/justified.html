justified

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪd / (BrE)
(of printed or digital text) having lines of type aligned evenly along both the left and right margins by adjusting the spacing between words or characters.
text with even left and right margins

Examples

The document was neatly formatted with justified text on every page.
She prefers to use justified alignment for professional reports.
The publisher required all paragraphs to be justified for a cleaner appearance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ragged - having uneven line margins in printed or digital text
