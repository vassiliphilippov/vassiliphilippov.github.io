gibbousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪbəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡɪbəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of bulging out or being protuberant; the condition of projecting from the surrounding surface
quality of bulging or protruding

Examples

The gibbousness of the moon was striking as it shone over the hills.
A tumor's gibbousness may be noted during a physical examination.
The gibbousness of the statue's shoulders gave it a lifelike presence.

Synset Examples

the gun in his pocket made an obvious bulge
the hump of a camel
he stood on the rocky prominence
the occipital protuberance was well developed
the bony excrescence between its horns

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulge - a rounded swelling or protuberance
  • Synonym: protuberance - a thing that protrudes from something else

Etymology

From Latin 'gibbosus' (humped).
