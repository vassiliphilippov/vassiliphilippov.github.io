tinkety-tonk

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪŋ.kə.ti tɑŋk / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋ.kɪ.ti tɒŋk / (BrE)
a light, cheerful sound, especially a lively or tinkling melody
light, cheerful or lively melody

Examples

The tinkety-tonk of the music box brought back childhood memories.
The old piano produced a charming tinkety-tonk that filled the room with energy.
Outside, the tinkety-tonk of bicycle bells could be heard on the busy street.

Synset Examples

The tinkety-tonk sounds of the bells were delightful.
He played a tinkety-tonk tune on his piano.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tinkle - a light, clear, ringing sound
  • Synonym: jingle - a light, ringing sound like small bells
