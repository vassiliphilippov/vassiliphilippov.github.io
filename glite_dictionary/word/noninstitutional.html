noninstitutional

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑːnˌɪn.stɪˈtuː.ʃən.əl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
Not associated with, controlled by, or characteristic of an institution.
not connected with an institution

Examples

The study focused on noninstitutional care options for elderly patients.
She preferred a noninstitutional learning environment that emphasized creativity.
Many homeless individuals live in noninstitutional settings such as shelters or transitional housing.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: institutional - relating to or characteristic of an institution

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' (not) to 'institutional'.
