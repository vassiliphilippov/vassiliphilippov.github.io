featurelessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːtʃərləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈfiːtʃələsnɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of lacking distinct or noticeable features.
lack of distinguishing features

Examples

The featurelessness of the landscape made it difficult to navigate without a map.
Her writing often evokes a sense of featurelessness in modern urban life.
Many critics remarked on the featurelessness of the film's characters.

Synset Examples

I cannot recall in the preceding works of George Eliot anything like the featurelessness of the characters she has drawn here.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: featureless - lacking distinctive features

Etymology

From 'featureless' + '-ness'.
