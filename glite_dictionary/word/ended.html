ended

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛndɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛndɪd / (BrE)
Having come to a conclusion or been brought to a close; finished.
brought to a conclusion; finished

Examples

Their partnership was ended after years of successful collaboration.
The ended project will be reviewed by the board.
When the storm had ended, the sun appeared through the clouds.

Synset Examples

the harvesting was complete
the affair is over, ended, finished
the abruptly terminated interview

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finished - having been completed; no longer in progress
  • Antonym: ongoing - continuing to exist or develop
