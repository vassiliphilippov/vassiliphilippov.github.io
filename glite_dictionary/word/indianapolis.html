Indianapolis

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪndiəˈnæpəlɪs / (AmE), / ˌɪndiəˈnæpəlɪs / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of the U.S. state of Indiana, known for its commercial significance and as the site of the Indianapolis 500 auto race
capital city of Indiana, USA
american

Examples

Many tourists visit Indianapolis each year for the famous auto race.
He grew up in Indianapolis before moving to Chicago.
The convention will be held in downtown Indianapolis this summer.

Etymology

Named after the state of Indiana and the Greek suffix -polis, meaning 'city'.
