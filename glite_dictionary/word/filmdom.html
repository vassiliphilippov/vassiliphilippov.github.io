filmdom

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlmdəm / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmdəm / (BrE)
The people involved in the film industry; the world of films and filmmaking.
the film industry and its people

Examples

Many aspiring actors dream of making it big in filmdom one day.
News of her latest award quickly spread throughout filmdom.
He became a legend in filmdom after directing several iconic movies.

Synset Examples

a star of stage and screen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hollywood - the American film industry, especially as centered in Los Angeles

Etymology

Formed from 'film' + '-dom' (denoting domain or realm).
