imputable

1. ADJ

/ ɪmˈpjuːtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪmˈpjuːtəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be attributed or ascribed to a particular person, cause, or source.
attributable or ascribable to someone or something

Examples

The failure of the project was imputable to poor planning rather than lack of effort.
The accountant argued that the error was not imputable to him but to a software glitch.
His initial success was largely imputable to good timing and the support of his colleagues.

Synset Examples

punctuation errors ascribable to careless proofreading
the cancellation of the concert was due to the rain
the oversight was not imputable to him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attributable - able to be regarded as being caused by
  • Synonym: ascribable - able to be ascribed or assigned to someone or something

Etymology

From Late Latin imputabilis (able to be charged or ascribed), from Latin imputare (to charge, ascribe).
