pig-sticking

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪɡ ˌstɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡ ˌstɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or practice of hunting wild pigs with spears, traditionally considered a form of hunting game
hunting wild pigs with spears

Examples

Many British officers in colonial India participated in pig-sticking as a recreational activity.
He considered pig-sticking to be a thrilling and adventurous pastime.
The tradition of pig-sticking has largely faded in modern times.
