Rossetti

1. PROPN

/ roʊˈsɛti / (AmE), / rəˈsɛti / (BrE)
Dante Gabriel Rossetti (1828–1882), an English poet and painter who was a leading figure in the Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood.
English Pre-Raphaelite poet and painter

Examples

Rossetti was influential both as an artist and as a poet during the Victorian era.
The Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood, co-founded by Rossetti, sought to revive the purity of early Renaissance art.
Art historians often study Rossetti's paintings for their symbolic imagery and vivid colors.
