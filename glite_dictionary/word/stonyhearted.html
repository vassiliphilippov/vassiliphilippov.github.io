stonyhearted

1. ADJ

/ ˈstoʊniˌhɑːrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈstəʊniˌhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Lacking compassion or sympathy; unfeeling towards others.
lacking compassion or sympathy

Examples

The stonyhearted manager dismissed their concerns without a second thought.
She could not believe how stonyhearted he was towards the homeless.
Despite their pleas, the judge remained stonyhearted and unmoved.

Synset Examples

an unfeeling wretch

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartless - showing a lack of compassion or kindness
  • Antonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others

Etymology

From 'stony' + 'hearted', suggesting a heart as hard as stone.
