Lacerta agilis

1. NOUN

/ ləˈsɜrtə əˈdʒɪlɪs / (AmE), / ləˈsɜːtə əˈdʒɪlɪs / (BrE)
a common species of lizard found across Europe and central Asia, known for its agile movements
European and central Asian lizard species

Examples

The Lacerta agilis is often seen basking in the sun on sandy banks throughout Europe.
Scientists study the habitat range of Lacerta agilis to understand environmental changes in the region.
The distinctive green coloration helps Lacerta agilis blend into its surroundings.

Etymology

From Latin: 'lacerta' meaning lizard, 'agilis' meaning quick or nimble.
