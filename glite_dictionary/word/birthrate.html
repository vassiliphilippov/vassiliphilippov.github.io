birthrate

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝθˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθˌreɪt / (BrE)
the number of live births per 1,000 people in a population per year
number of births per 1,000 people per year

Examples

The country's birthrate has steadily declined over the past decade.
Policies were introduced to encourage a higher birthrate in the region.
Japan is facing economic challenges due to its low birthrate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fertility rate - number of children born to a woman over her lifetime
  • Antonym: death rate - number of deaths per 1,000 people per year

Etymology

Formed from 'birth' + 'rate'.
