adoptee

1. NOUN

/ ˌæd.ɑpˈti / (AmE), / ˌæd.ɒpˈtiː / (BrE)
a person who has been adopted, especially as a child
a person who has been adopted

Examples

After years of searching, the adoptee was able to find her birth parents.
As an adoptee, he had many questions about his heritage and background.
The conference brought together adoptees from different countries to share their experiences.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: adopter - a person who adopts someone, especially a child
  • Synonym: adopted child - a child who has been legally taken by someone other than their biological parents

Etymology

Formed from 'adopt' + '-ee', a suffix meaning 'one who is the object of an action'.
