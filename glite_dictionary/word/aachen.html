Aachen

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑkən / (AmE), / ˈɑːkən / (BrE)
A city in western Germany, near the borders with the Netherlands and Belgium, historically known as the northern capital of Charlemagne's empire.
city in western Germany, former capital of Charlemagne

Examples

Tourists flock to Aachen to admire its stunning cathedral, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Located near three countries' borders, Aachen has long served as a place of cultural exchange.
Many European leaders gathered in Aachen to discuss issues of cross-border cooperation.
