twitterverse

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɪtərˌvɜrs / (AmE), / ˈtwɪtəbˌvɜːs / (BrE)
the collective community and activity on the social media platform Twitter.
community or sphere of Twitter users

Examples

Breaking news often spreads quickly throughout the twitterverse.
Many celebrities interact with their fans via the twitterverse.
Debate in the twitterverse can sometimes become heated and divisive.

Etymology

Blend of 'Twitter' (the social media service) and 'universe'.
