Pimlico

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɪm.lɪˌkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɪm.lɪ.kəʊ / (BrE)
A famous horse racing track in Baltimore, Maryland, known as the site of the Preakness Stakes.
US horse racing track; site of the Preakness
american informal

Examples

Every May, thousands gather at Pimlico to watch the Preakness Stakes.
Horse racing fans consider Pimlico a historic and exciting venue.
The crowd at Pimlico erupted as the winning horse crossed the finish line.
