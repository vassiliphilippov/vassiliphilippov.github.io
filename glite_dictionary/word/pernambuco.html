Pernambuco

1. PROPN

/ pər.nəmˈbu.koʊ / (AmE), / ˌpɜː.nəmˈbuː.kəʊ / (BrE)
a city and port in northeastern Brazil, on the Atlantic coast; now known as Recife.
port city in northeastern Brazil

Examples

The historic center of Pernambuco boasts beautiful colonial architecture.
Many ships arrive daily at the busy port in Pernambuco.
Tourists are attracted to the vibrant culture and festivals celebrated in Pernambuco.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Pernambuco', possibly from Tupi 'paranã-puka' (hole in the sea).
