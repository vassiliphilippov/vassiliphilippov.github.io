unrevealed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈviːld / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈviːld / (BrE)
not made known or disclosed; kept secret or hidden from others.
not made public or disclosed

Examples

The true cause of the incident remains unrevealed to this day.
Many chapters in the novel contain unrevealed secrets that drive the plot forward.
His motivation for leaving was never fully unrevealed, leaving friends confused.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: revealed - made known or disclosed
  • Synonym: secret - kept hidden or not known by others

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'revealed', from Latin 'revelare' (to unveil, reveal).
