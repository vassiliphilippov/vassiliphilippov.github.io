horrible

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɔrəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhɒrəbəl / (BrE)
causing horror or shock; extremely unpleasant or disturbing
causing horror; extremely unpleasant

Examples

The movie contained several horrible scenes that haunted me for days.
She witnessed a horrible accident on her way home.
The food had a horrible smell that made everyone feel sick.

Synset Examples

an atrocious automobile accident
a frightful crime of decapitation
an alarming, even horrifying, picture
war is beyond all words horrible
an ugly wound

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrible - very bad or unpleasant; causing terror or horror
  • Synonym: frightful - very unpleasant or shocking; dreadful
  • Antonym: delightful - highly pleasing, causing delight

Etymology

From Latin 'horribilis' (dreadful, horrible), from 'horrere' (to bristle, shudder).
