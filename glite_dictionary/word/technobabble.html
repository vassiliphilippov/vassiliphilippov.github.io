technobabble

1. NOUN

/ ˈtek.nəˌbæb.əl / (AmE), / ˈtɛk.nəʊˌbæb.əl / (BrE)
Technical language or jargon, often from computing or high-tech fields, that is confusing or meaningless to outsiders.
confusing technical jargon

Examples

The IT guy's explanation was just a bunch of technobabble to me.
Science fiction movies are notorious for scenes full of technobabble.
She rolled her eyes at the programmer's technobabble during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jargon - specialized language used by a profession or group

Etymology

Blend of 'technology' and 'babble'.
