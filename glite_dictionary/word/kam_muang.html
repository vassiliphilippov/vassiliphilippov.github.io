Kam Muang

1. NOUN

/ kɑm ˈmuːɑŋ / (AmE), / kæm ˈmuːæŋ / (BrE)
A branch of the Tai languages spoken in several provinces of northern Thailand, including Chiang Mai and Chiang Rai.
language of northern Thailand

Examples

Many people in northern Thailand speak Kam Muang with their family and friends.
He learned Kam Muang to better communicate with locals in Chiang Mai.
The cultural festival featured traditional songs performed in Kam Muang.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Northern Thai - another common name for the Kam Muang language
