to receive

1. VERB

/ rɪˈsiv / (AmE), / rɪˈsiːv / (BrE)
to greet or welcome someone formally upon their arrival
welcome or greet upon arrival

Examples

The ambassador will receive the guests at the main entrance this evening.
She was received warmly by the committee when she arrived at the venue.
Upon entering the hall, he was received by applause from the assembled crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welcome - to greet someone in a warm and friendly way

Etymology

From Old French *receivre*, from Latin *recipere* (to take back, receive).
