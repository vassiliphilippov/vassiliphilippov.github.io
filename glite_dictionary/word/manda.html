Manda

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːndə / (AmE), / ˈmandə / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken in south central India, especially in the Kalahandi district of Odisha
Dravidian language of south central India

Examples

The Manda language is spoken in several villages in the Kalahandi district.
Linguists have been documenting Manda to help preserve its unique vocabulary and grammar.
He grew up speaking Manda at home before learning Odia in school.
