Capra

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæprə / (AmE), / ˈkæprə / (BrE)
a genus of mammals that includes goats and wild goats
genus of goats and wild goats

Examples

The Capra genus includes both domesticated and wild species of goats.
Many mountain goats belong to the Capra genus and inhabit steep, rocky environments.
Scientists studied various members of the Capra genus to understand their adaptation to alpine conditions.

Etymology

From Latin capra (she-goat).
