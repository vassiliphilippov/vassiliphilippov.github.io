trailing

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtreɪ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of pursuing a person or animal by following their tracks or marks
following a trail to pursue

Examples

The dog's trailing skills were vital in finding the lost hikers.
Trailing became more difficult as the rain washed away most of the footprints.
She specialized in the trailing of poachers through dense forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tracking - the act of following tracks or traces
