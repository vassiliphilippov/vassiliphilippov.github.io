lymphangiectasia

1. NOUN

/ lɪmˌfændʒiɪkˈteɪʒiə / (AmE), / lɪmˌfændʒiːɛkˈteɪzɪə / (BrE)
abnormal dilatation of a lymph vessel or vessels
abnormal lymph vessel dilatation
medical

Examples

The diagnosis of lymphangiectasia was confirmed by endoscopic examination and biopsy.
Chronic intestinal lymphangiectasia can lead to protein-losing enteropathy.
Veterinarians treated the dog for lymphangiectasia after noticing persistent swelling in its legs.

Etymology

From Greek *lympha* (lymph) + *angeion* (vessel) + *ektasis* (dilatation).
