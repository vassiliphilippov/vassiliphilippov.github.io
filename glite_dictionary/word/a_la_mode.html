a la mode

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑː lə ˈmoʊd / (AmE), / ˌaː lə ˈməʊd / (BrE)
in the current fashion or style; fashionable
in the current fashion or style

Examples

Her dress was definitely a la mode and caught everyone's attention at the party.
These shoes are considered a la mode this season, so everyone wants a pair.
The designer always ensures his collections are a la mode and appeal to modern tastes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashionable - popular or in fashion
  • Antonym: outdated - no longer in fashion or use

Etymology

From French 'à la mode', meaning 'in the fashion'.
