bounciness

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaʊnsinəs / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnsinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being able to rebound or bounce easily after impact
quality of rebounding or bouncing

Examples

The bounciness of the new basketball made it perfect for playing on the court.
Her hair's natural bounciness gave her style a fresh, lively look.
You can test the mattress's bounciness by pressing down with your hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elasticity - the ability of an object or material to resume its normal shape after being stretched or compressed

Etymology

Formed from 'bouncy' + '-ness' (denoting a state or quality).
