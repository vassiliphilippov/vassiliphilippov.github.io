SADR

1. PROPN

/ ˌɛs.eɪ.diˈɑr / (AmE), / ˌɛs.eɪ.diˈɑː / (BrE)
an acronym for the Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic, a partially recognized state in the Western Sahara region of northwestern Africa
partially recognized state in northwest Africa

Examples

The SADR controls part of the Western Sahara region despite limited international recognition.
Many African Union member states have recognized the SADR as an independent country.
Debates at the United Nations frequently mention the status of the SADR.

Etymology

Acronym from Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic.
