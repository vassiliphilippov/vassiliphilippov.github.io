Lyndon Johnson

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪndən ˈdʒɑnsən / (AmE), / ˈlɪndən ˈdʒɒnsən / (BrE)
the 36th President of the United States (1963–1969), who formerly served as Vice President and succeeded John F. Kennedy after his assassination
