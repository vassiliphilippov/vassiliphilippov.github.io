enthrallingly

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈθrɔlɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈθrɔːlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that captivates or fascinates; in an enthralling manner
in a captivating or fascinating way

Examples

The author described the landscape enthrallingly, making readers feel as though they were actually there.
He enthrallingly recounted his adventures to an eager audience.
The film unfolds enthrallingly from the very first scene.

Synset Examples

she was bewitchingly beautiful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: captivatingly - in a captivating manner
  • Antonym: dully - in a dull or uninteresting manner
