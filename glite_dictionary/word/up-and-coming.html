up-and-coming

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌp ən ˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌp ən ˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
likely to become successful or popular soon; becoming prominent.
likely to achieve success soon

Examples

She is an up-and-coming artist whose work is attracting a lot of attention.
The company hired an up-and-coming marketing specialist to lead their new campaign.
That neighborhood has become popular among up-and-coming professionals looking for affordable housing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promising - showing signs of future success or improvement
