knuckler

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌk.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈnʌk.lə / (BrE)
a baseball pitch thrown with little speed or spin, typically causing unpredictable movement
a type of baseball pitch with little spin

Examples

The pitcher threw a knuckler that baffled the batter with its unpredictable motion.
It's always difficult to hit a knuckler because you never know where it will go.
His signature pitch is the knuckler, which he uses effectively late in the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knuckleball - a baseball pitch thrown so as to minimize spin for erratic movement
