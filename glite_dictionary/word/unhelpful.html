unhelpful

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhɛlp.fəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlp.fəl / (BrE)
Not providing useful assistance or support.
not providing assistance

Examples

The receptionist was unhelpful and refused to answer my questions.
His comments were not only irrelevant but also quite unhelpful to the discussion.
I found the instructions unhelpful and confusing.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: helpful - giving or ready to give help
  • Synonym: useless - not fulfilling or not capable of fulfilling its purpose or function

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'helpful'.
