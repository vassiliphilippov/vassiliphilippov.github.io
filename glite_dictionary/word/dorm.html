dorm

1. NOUN

/ dɔrm / (AmE), / dɔːm / (BrE)
a college or university building where students live while attending classes
student residence in a college or university
informal american

Examples

I spent my first year living in the dorm with three roommates.
The university is renovating the oldest dorm on campus this summer.
He forgot his laptop in the dorm before heading to class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dormitory - a large bedroom for a number of people in a school or institution

Etymology

Short for 'dormitory', from Latin 'dormitorium' (sleeping place).
