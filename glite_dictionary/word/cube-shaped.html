cube-shaped

1. ADJ

/ kjuːb ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / kjuːb ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a cube, with six equal square faces and right angles
having the shape of a cube

Examples

The building had a striking cube-shaped design that stood out from the others.
She handed me a small, cube-shaped box tied with a ribbon.
Scientists examined the cube-shaped crystals under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cubical - having the shape of a cube
  • Antonym: spherical - having the shape of a sphere
