seventeen

1. NUM

/ ˌsɛvənˈtin / (AmE), / ˌsɛvənˈtiːn / (BrE)
the cardinal number 17; the number that is one more than sixteen.
the number 17

Examples

She turned seventeen last week and had a big party to celebrate.
There are seventeen students enrolled in the advanced chemistry class this year.
He found seventeen dollars in his old winter jacket pocket.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sixteen - the number 16
  • Degree Higher: eighteen - the number 18

Etymology

Formed from 'seven' + 'teen' (meaning ten); modeled after other English numerals like 'thirteen' and 'fourteen'.
