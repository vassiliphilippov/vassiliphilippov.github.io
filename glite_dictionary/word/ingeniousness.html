ingeniousness

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʒin.jəs.nəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒiː.ni.əs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being clever, original, and inventive
quality of being clever or inventive

Examples

The ingeniousness of her solution impressed the entire team.
Many admired the ingeniousness behind his latest invention.
We were astonished by the ingeniousness displayed in the architecture of the building.

Synset Examples

a plot of great ingenuity
the cleverness of its design

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ingenuity - the quality of being ingenious; inventiveness

Etymology

From Latin 'ingeniosus' (endowed with talent) + English '-ness' (noun suffix).

2. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʒiːniəsnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒiːniəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being clever, original, and inventive, especially in solving problems or creating things
quality of being clever and inventive

Examples

Her ingeniousness impressed everyone at the robotics competition.
The ingeniousness of the solution saved both time and money.
They admired the ingeniousness that brought the new technology to life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ingenuity - the quality of being clever, original, and inventive

Etymology

From 'ingenious' + '-ness', ultimately from Latin 'ingenium' (inborn talent).
