emeer

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɪr / (AmE), / əˈmɪə / (BrE)
an independent ruler or chieftain, especially in Africa or Arabia; alternative spelling of 'emir'
Muslim ruler or chieftain (alt. spelling)

Examples

The emeer ruled his territory with a blend of tradition and modernity.
Several cities were governed by different emeers during the 19th century.
An emeer often held both political and religious authority in his domain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emir - a Muslim (especially Arab) ruler or prince; standard spelling

Etymology

Variant of 'emir', from Arabic 'amīr' (commander).
