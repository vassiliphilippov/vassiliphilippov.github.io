imaginatively

1. ADV

/ ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪvli / (AmE), / ɪˈmadʒɪnətɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows creativity or original thinking.
with creativity or originality

Examples

The stage was imaginatively lit to convey a sense of wonder.
She solved the problem imaginatively, coming up with a unique and practical solution.
Children often play imaginatively, turning ordinary objects into magical things.

Synset Examples

the room was decorated very imaginatively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creatively - in a way that involves the use of imagination or original ideas
