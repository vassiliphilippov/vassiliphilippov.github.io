Sussex

1. PROPN

/ ˈsʌsɪks / (AmE), / ˈsʌsɪks / (BrE)
a county and former kingdom in southern England, located on the English Channel, now divided into East and West Sussex
county in southern England

Examples

She grew up in Sussex and often visited the coast as a child.
The rolling hills of Sussex are famous for their natural beauty.
Historians have studied the early medieval kingdom of Sussex extensively.

Etymology

From Old English Sūþseaxe, meaning 'South Saxons'.
