lemonwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛm.ənˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈlɛm.ənˌwʊd / (BrE)
A South African evergreen tree with hard, tough wood, often used for tool handles and walking sticks.
South African evergreen with tough wood

Examples

The craftsman selected lemonwood for the tool handles because of its durability.
Botanists studied the growth patterns of lemonwood in its native South African habitat.
Walking sticks made from lemonwood are prized for their strength and resilience.
