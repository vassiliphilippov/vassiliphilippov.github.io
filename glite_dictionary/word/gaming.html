gaming

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of playing games of chance for money, especially in gambling establishments; gambling for stakes
the act of gambling for money or prizes

Examples

The new casino is expected to boost gaming revenue in the city.
Many people oppose the legalization of online gaming due to addiction concerns.
The law strictly regulates gaming to prevent underage participation.

Synset Examples

his gambling cost him a fortune
there was heavy play at the blackjack table

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gambling - the activity of betting money on the outcome of a game or event

Etymology

From Middle English 'gamen' (to play), from Old English 'gamen' (joy, fun, amusement).
