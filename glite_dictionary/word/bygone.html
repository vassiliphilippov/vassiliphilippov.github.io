bygone

1. ADJ

/ ˈbaɪˌɡɔn / (AmE), / ˈbaɪˌɡɒn / (BrE)
belonging to an earlier time; former; well in the past
belonging to an earlier time

Examples

The old castle reminded her of bygone days filled with grandeur and mystery.
He enjoys collecting artifacts from bygone eras.
Many traditions from bygone times are still practiced in rural villages.

Synset Examples

bygone days
dreams of foregone times
sweet memories of gone summers
relics of a departed era

Related Terms

  • Synonym: past - gone by in time and no longer existing
  • Synonym: former - having previously filled a particular role or position
  • Antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times

Etymology

From 'by' (past) + 'gone', indicating something that has passed by.
