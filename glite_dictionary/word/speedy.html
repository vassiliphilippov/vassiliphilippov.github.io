speedy

1. ADJ

/ ˈspiːdi / (AmE), / ˈspiːdi / (BrE)
Moving or occurring quickly; characterized by high speed.
moving or happening quickly

Examples

The company delivered my package in a speedy manner.
She was impressed by the speedy recovery of the athlete after his injury.
We took a speedy route to avoid traffic and arrived early.

Synset Examples

a rapid movement
a speedy car
a speedy errand boy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fast - moving or capable of moving at high speed
  • Synonym: quick - done, proceeding, or occurring with rapidity
  • Antonym: slow - moving or operating at a low speed

Etymology

From Middle English 'spedi', from 'speed' + '-y'.
