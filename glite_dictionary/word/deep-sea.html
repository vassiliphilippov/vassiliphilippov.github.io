deep-sea

1. ADJ

/ ˌdipˈsi / (AmE), / ˌdiːpˈsiː / (BrE)
Relating to or occurring in the deeper parts of the sea, far below the surface.
of or in the deep parts of the sea

Examples

The researchers embarked on a deep-sea expedition to study unique marine life.
Commercial deep-sea fishing is regulated to preserve fish populations.
She specializes in deep-sea exploration and underwater technology.

Synset Examples

deep-sea fishing
deep-sea exploration

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shallow-water - relating to or occurring in waters that are not deep
