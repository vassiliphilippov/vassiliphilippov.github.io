breeder

1. NOUN

/ ˈbriːdər / (AmE), / ˈbriːdə / (BrE)
a person who breeds animals, especially as a profession
person who breeds animals

Examples

The breeder carefully selected the healthiest dogs for her new litter.
Many breeders attend shows to display their best livestock.
He became a successful horse breeder after years of hard work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
  • Antonym: buyer - a person who purchases something (antonym by occupational role)

Etymology

From Old English 'bredan' meaning 'to breed', with agent suffix '-er'.
