Episcopal

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈpɪs.kə.pəl / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪs.kə.pəl / (BrE)
Relating to the Episcopal Church or its members, practices, or hierarchy.
of or relating to the Episcopal Church

Examples

The bishop led the Episcopal service with great dignity.
She has attended an Episcopal church since childhood.
Many Episcopal traditions are rooted in Anglican heritage.

Synset Examples

the Episcopal hierarchy
married by an Episcopalian minister

Etymology

From Late Latin episcopalis, from episcopus ('bishop').
