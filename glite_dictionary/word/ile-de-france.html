Ile-de-France

1. PROPN

/ il də ˈfræns / (AmE), / il də ˈfrɑ̃s / (BrE)
a region in north-central France that includes Paris and its surrounding area
region of France including Paris

Examples

Many tourists visit Ile-de-France every year to see Paris and other famous landmarks.
Economic activity in Ile-de-France is vital to the prosperity of all of France.
She grew up in a small town on the outskirts of Ile-de-France.

Etymology

From French Île-de-France (literally 'Island of France'), the traditional name of the region.
