pyxie

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪk.si / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.si / (BrE)
A creeping evergreen shrub (Pyxidanthera barbulata) with narrow overlapping leaves and early white star-shaped flowers, native to the pine barrens of New Jersey and the Carolinas.
evergreen shrub of US pine barrens

Examples

The rare pyxie blooms early in spring across the New Jersey pine barrens.
Conservationists are concerned about habitat loss for the pyxie in the Carolinas.
White star-shaped flowers cover the ground when the pyxie is in full bloom.
