unenterprising

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈɛn.t̬ɚˌpraɪ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɛn.təˌpraɪ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
not showing initiative or a willingness to take risks; lacking enterprise or ambition.
lacking initiative or ambition

Examples

The team became unenterprising after years of disappointing results.
His unenterprising attitude made him reluctant to seek promotions.
The report criticized the government's unenterprising response to the crisis.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: enterprising - showing initiative and willingness to undertake new projects

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'enterprising' (showing initiative).
