Carducci

1. PROPN

/ kɑrˈdutʃi / (AmE), / kɑːˈduːtʃi / (BrE)
Giosuè Carducci (1835–1907), an Italian poet considered the national poet of modern Italy.
Italian national poet (1835–1907)

Examples

Many Italian schools are named after Carducci to honor his literary contributions.
Carducci's poetry had a significant influence on modern Italian literature.
She wrote her thesis on the works of Carducci and his impact on national identity.
