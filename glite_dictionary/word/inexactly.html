inexactly

1. ADV

/ ɪn.ɪɡˈzæk(t).li / (AmE), / ɪn.ɪɡˈzæk(t).li / (BrE)
In an imprecise or inaccurate manner; not exactly.
in an imprecise manner

Examples

The figures quoted in the report are inexactly calculated and may require revision.
She remembered the details inexactly, so her story changed each time she told it.
The painting imitates nature only inexactly, introducing elements of fantasy.

Synset Examples

he expressed himself imprecisely

Related Terms

  • Antonym: exactly - in a precise and accurate manner
  • Synonym: precisely - with absolute accuracy; exactly

Etymology

Formed from 'inexact' + '-ly'.
