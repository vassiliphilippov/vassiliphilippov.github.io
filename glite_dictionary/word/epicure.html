epicure

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛpɪˌkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪˌkjʊə / (BrE)
a person who takes particular pleasure in fine food and drink, often with refined, discriminating taste
person with refined taste for food and drink

Examples

He is something of an epicure, always seeking out the city's best restaurants.
As a true epicure, she pairs every dish with the perfect wine.
The dinner party was a delight for any epicure, with a menu of gourmet dishes and rare ingredients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gourmet - a person who enjoys and knows a lot about good food and wine
  • Antonym: glutton - a person who eats and drinks excessively

Etymology

From Greek 'Epikouros', name of the philosopher Epicurus, whose followers pursued pleasure as the highest good.
