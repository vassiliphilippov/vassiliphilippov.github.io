hesitater

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛzɪˌteɪtər / (AmE), / ˈhɛzɪˌteɪtə / (BrE)
a person who hesitates, usually out of fear, uncertainty, or indecision
someone who hesitates

Examples

As a known hesitater, she often missed great opportunities simply by waiting too long.
The meeting dragged on because every hesitater in the room couldn't make a decision.
In stressful situations, a hesitater might find it especially hard to take action.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hesitator - a person who hesitates

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'hesitate' + agent noun suffix '-er'.
