rectilineal

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛktɪˈlɪniəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛktɪˈlɪːnɪəl / (BrE)
characterized by straight lines or moving in straight lines
characterized by straight lines

Examples

The artist favored rectilineal designs in his modern sculptures.
Light often travels in a rectilineal path unless reflected or refracted.
Many city streets are laid out in a rectilineal grid pattern.

Synset Examples

rectilinear patterns in wallpaper
the rectilinear propagation of light

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rectilinear - involving or moving in straight lines

Etymology

From Latin 'rectus' (straight) + 'linea' (line).
