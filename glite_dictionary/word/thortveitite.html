thortveitite

1. NOUN

/ θɔrtˈvaɪˌtaɪt / (AmE), / θɔːtˈvaɪ.taɪt / (BrE)
a rare mineral composed of scandium yttrium silicate, an important source of scandium
a rare mineral; scandium source

Examples

The main commercial source of scandium is the mineral thortveitite.
Geologists discovered several new veins of thortveitite in the old mining district.
The crystal structure of thortveitite makes it a unique silicate mineral.

Etymology

Named after Olaus Thortveit, a Norwegian engineer who discovered it.
