Angola

1. PROPN

/ ænˈɡoʊlə / (AmE), / ænˈɡəʊlə / (BrE)
a country in southwestern Africa on the Atlantic Ocean, which gained independence from Portugal in 1975
country in southwestern Africa

Examples

After decades of civil war, Angola has become one of Africa's fastest growing economies.
The capital city of Angola is Luanda, situated along the Atlantic coast.
Portuguese remains the official language of Angola due to its colonial history.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Angola', derived from the title 'ngola', used by kings of the region.
