Bosch

1. PROPN

/ bɑʃ / (AmE), / bɒʃ / (BrE)
(c. 1450–1516) Dutch painter renowned for his fantastical imagery and complex, often surreal religious works.
Dutch painter (c. 1450–1516)

Examples

Bosch's paintings are famous for their vivid imagination and allegorical content.
Many art historians regard Bosch as a precursor to the surrealist movement.
The Garden of Earthly Delights is one of Bosch's most celebrated works.

Etymology

From Dutch surname, notably borne by Hieronymus Bosch.
