short-bodied

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɔrt ˌbɑdid / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌbɒdid / (BrE)
having a body that is noticeably shorter than what is typical for its kind or group
having a noticeably short body

Examples

Some species of fish are short-bodied compared to others in their family.
The short-bodied lizard can squeeze into crevices that longer ones cannot.
Pigeons bred for racing are often short-bodied with broad chests.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-bodied - having a body that is noticeably longer than typical
