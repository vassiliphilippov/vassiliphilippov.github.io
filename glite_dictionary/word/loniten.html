Loniten

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːnɪtən / (AmE), / ˈlɒnɪtən / (BrE)
A trademarked drug (minoxidil) used as a vasodilator to treat severe hypertension.
trademarked antihypertensive drug
medical

Examples

After his diagnosis of severe hypertension, his doctor prescribed Loniten to help control his blood pressure.
Some patients taking Loniten report increased hair growth as an unexpected side effect.
It is important to monitor blood pressure regularly while on Loniten.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rogaine - Trademark for minoxidil as a hair loss treatment
  • Synonym: minoxidil - The generic name for the drug sold as Loniten and Rogaine
