Einstein

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪnstaɪn / (AmE), / ˈaɪnstaɪn / (BrE)
a person who is exceptionally intelligent or original, especially in a particular field; a genius
exceptionally intelligent or original person

Examples

Everyone in the class thinks Sara is an Einstein because of her amazing math skills.
You don't have to be an Einstein to solve this simple puzzle.
Though he tries hard, he's certainly no Einstein when it comes to computers.

Synset Examples

Mozart was a child genius
he's smart but he's no Einstein

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genius - a person of very great intelligence or skill

Etymology

From the surname of the physicist Albert Einstein, whose name became synonymous with genius.
