Arenaria groenlandica

1. NOUN

/ əˌrɛnəriə ɡruːnˈlændɪkə / (AmE), / əˌrɛnəriə ɡruːnˈlændɪkə / (BrE)
A small, cushion-forming flowering plant of northern and alpine regions, commonly known as boreal or alpine sandwort.
a boreal or alpine sandwort plant

Examples

The delicate flowers of Arenaria groenlandica carpet the rocky slopes of the tundra.
Researchers found Arenaria groenlandica thriving in harsh alpine conditions above the tree line.
Hikers were careful not to step on patches of Arenaria groenlandica during their ascent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandwort - a low-growing plant of the genus Arenaria

Etymology

From Latin; 'Arenaria' meaning 'sandwort' and 'groenlandica' meaning 'of Greenland.'
