van Gogh

1. PROPN

/ væn 'ɡoʊ / (AmE), / væn 'ɡɒx / (BrE)
(1853–1890) Dutch post-impressionist painter known for his expressive use of color and bold brushstrokes.
Dutch post-impressionist painter (1853–1890)

Examples

Many art lovers consider van Gogh's 'Starry Night' to be his masterpiece.
The museum features several original paintings by van Gogh.
His unique style was not widely recognized during van Gogh's lifetime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gauguin - French post-impressionist painter and contemporary of van Gogh

Etymology

From Vincent van Gogh, Dutch surname; Vincent Willem van Gogh was the artist's full name.
