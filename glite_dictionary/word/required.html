required

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈkwaɪərd / (AmE), / rɪˈkwaɪəd / (BrE)
officially demanded by rule, law, or authority; obligatory
officially demanded; obligatory

Examples

Wearing a helmet is required when riding a motorcycle in this state.
Completion of the prerequisites is required before enrolling in the course.
All participants must submit the required documents by the deadline.

Synset Examples

in most schools physical education is compulsory
attendance is mandatory
required reading

Related Terms

  • Antonym: optional - not required; left to personal choice
  • Synonym: compulsory - required by law or rule; obligatory
  • Synonym: mandatory - required by law or rules; compulsory

Etymology

Past participle of 'require,' from Old French 'requerre' (to seek, ask), from Latin 'requirere' (to seek, demand).
