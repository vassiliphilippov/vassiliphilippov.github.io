radioactively

1. ADV

/ ˌreɪdi.oʊˈæktɪvli / (AmE), / ˌreɪdi.əˈʊktɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner that involves or relates to radioactivity.
in a way relating to radioactivity

Examples

Some elements decay radioactively, emitting dangerous radiation over time.
The samples were radioactively labeled for tracing within the experiment.
She handled the material carefully because it was known to decay radioactively.

Synset Examples

radioactively labeled

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radioactive - emitting or relating to the emission of ionizing radiation or particles

Etymology

Formed from 'radioactive' + '-ly'. 'Radioactive' from 'radio-' (radiation) + 'active'.
