kneepan

1. NOUN

/ ˈniˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈniːpæn / (BrE)
a small, flat, triangular bone at the front of the knee joint, commonly known as the kneecap, that protects and covers the knee
triangular bone at the front of the knee

Examples

He suffered a fracture to his kneepan while playing football.
The doctor placed a bandage over her kneepan to protect the injury.
Evolutionary adaptations have shaped the kneepan to provide stability and flexibility to the knee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patella - the scientific term for the kneecap

Etymology

Formed from 'knee' + 'pan', referring to the bone's shape.
