pocket-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑːkɪt saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt saɪzd / (BrE)
small enough to fit in a pocket; very small or compact in size.
very small in size; compact

Examples

She keeps a pocket-sized notebook in her bag for jotting down ideas.
The tourist bought a pocket-sized guidebook of the city to carry around easily.
This pocket-sized camera is perfect for traveling light.

Synset Examples

a small business
a newspaper with a modest circulation
small-scale plans
a pocket-size country

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compact - small and efficiently arranged; closely packed
  • Synonym: miniature - very small version of something; tiny

Etymology

Formed from 'pocket' + 'sized', meaning sized to fit a pocket.

2. ADJ

/ ˈpɑːkɪt saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt saɪzd / (BrE)
Small enough to be carried conveniently in a pocket.
small enough to fit in a pocket

Examples

He purchased a pocket-sized camera to take on his travels.
A pocket-sized dictionary can be extremely useful for language learners.

Synset Examples

pocket-size paperbacks

Related Terms
