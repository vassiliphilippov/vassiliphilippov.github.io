uncompartmented

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kəmˈpɑːrt.mən.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəmˈpɑːt.mənt.ɪd / (BrE)
Not divided into separate compartments or isolated units; lacking partitions.
not divided into compartments

Examples

The large, uncompartmented interior of the hall allowed for greater flexibility in design.
Unlike traditional offices, their new workspace is uncompartmented and open.
The container was uncompartmented, making it easy to store bulk items.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: compartmented - divided into separate compartments or sections

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'compartmented'.
