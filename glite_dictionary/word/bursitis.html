bursitis

1. NOUN

/ bɜrˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / bɜːˈsaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of a bursa, especially one in the shoulder, elbow, or knee, causing pain and restricted movement.
inflammation of a bursa (joint sac)
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her shoulder pain as bursitis after a physical examination.
He missed several games due to bursitis in his knee.
Applying ice helped reduce the swelling caused by bursitis.

Etymology

From New Latin, from Medieval Latin bursa (purse, bag) + -itis (inflammation).
