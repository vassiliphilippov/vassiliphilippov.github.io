tentmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛntˌmeɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛntˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs tents
person who makes or repairs tents

Examples

The tentmaker skillfully patched the tear in the canvas before the next expedition.
As a tentmaker, she traveled with the circus, repairing their tents as needed.
Our family business began when my grandfather became a tentmaker in the early twentieth century.
