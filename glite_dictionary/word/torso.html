torso

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔrˌsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːsəʊ / (BrE)
the main part of the human body, not including the head, neck, or limbs
trunk of the body without head or limbs

Examples

The statue was missing its torso and arms.
After the accident, he had severe injuries to his torso.
She kept her back straight, tightening her torso muscles during the exercise.

Synset Examples

they moved their arms and legs and bodies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trunk - the main part of the body, especially the torso, excluding the head and limbs

Etymology

From Italian 'torso' (stalk, trunk), from Latin 'thyrsus' (stalk, stem).
