nosed

1. ADJ

/ noʊzd / (AmE), / nəʊzd / (BrE)
having a nose, especially of a particular kind, often used in compounds (e.g., sharp-nosed) to describe the shape, appearance, or characteristics related to a nose.
having a nose, esp. of a certain kind

Examples

The artist drew a nosed mask, giving it a distinctive profile.
Several nosed species can be found in the region, each adapted for a unique purpose.
The puppy was a black-nosed retriever with an excellent sense of smell.

Related Terms
