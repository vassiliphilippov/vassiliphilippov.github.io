pointrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪntrəl / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntrəl / (BrE)
a small, pointed tool used by engravers for fine detail work
engraver's pointed tool

Examples

The engraver carefully used a pointrel to add intricate details to the metal plate.
She purchased a new pointrel for her printmaking workshop.
Each pointrel in the studio was sharpened before the artists began their work.
