Mindoro

1. PROPN

/ mɪnˈdɔroʊ / (AmE), / mɪnˈdɔːrəʊ / (BrE)
a mountainous island located in the central Philippines, known for its natural beauty and cultural heritage
island in the central Philippines

Examples
