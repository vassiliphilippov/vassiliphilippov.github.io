colobus

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊləbəs / (AmE), / ˈkɒləbəs / (BrE)
an arboreal monkey native to western and central Africa, noted for its long silky fur and reduced thumbs
African arboreal monkey with silky fur

Examples

Several colobus watched us quietly from the treetops.
The black-and-white colobus is well known for its striking appearance.
Researchers observed a group of colobus feeding on young leaves in the forest canopy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colobus monkey - another term for colobus, African arboreal monkey

Etymology

From Greek 'kolobos' meaning 'docked, mutilated', referring to their reduced thumbs.
