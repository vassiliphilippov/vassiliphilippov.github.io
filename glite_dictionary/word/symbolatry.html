symbolatry

1. NOUN

/ sɪmˈbɑːlətri / (AmE), / sɪmˈbɒlətri / (BrE)
the worship or excessive veneration of symbols
worship of symbols

Examples

Some critics argued that the new movement was a form of symbolatry rather than genuine faith.
He accused the institution of descending into symbolatry, placing images above real values.
The ceremony bordered on symbolatry, with every gesture given exaggerated significance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idolatry - the worship of idols

Etymology

From symbol + -latry, from Greek latreia (worship).
