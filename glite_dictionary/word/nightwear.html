nightwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪt.wɛr / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt.wɛə / (BrE)
clothes that are designed to be worn in bed while sleeping.
clothes worn in bed

Examples

She packed her favorite nightwear for the trip and made sure she had something comfortable to sleep in.
Cotton nightwear is ideal during the summer because it is light and breathable.
The store sells a range of nightwear including pyjamas, robes, and nightgowns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pyjamas - loose-fitting nightclothes, typically consisting of a jacket and trousers
  • Synonym: nightgown - a loose dress worn by women and girls in bed

Etymology

Formed from 'night' + 'wear,' similar in construction to 'outerwear' and 'underwear.'
