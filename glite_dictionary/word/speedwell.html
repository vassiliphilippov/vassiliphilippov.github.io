speedwell

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːdˌwɛl / (AmE), / ˈspiːdwɛl / (BrE)
any of several small flowering plants of the genus Veronica, typically with blue or purple flowers and found in temperate regions.
a plant of the genus Veronica

Examples

The meadow was dotted with delicate speedwell among the grasses.
Gardeners often appreciate speedwell for its vibrant blue flowers and easy maintenance.
Some species of speedwell are popular groundcover plants in temperate gardens.

Etymology

From 'speed' (success) and 'well' (health); once used as a wish for good health from its supposed medicinal properties.
