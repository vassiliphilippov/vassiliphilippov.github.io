numdah

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌm.dɑ / (AmE), / ˈnʌm.də / (BrE)
an embroidered rug or mat made from coarse felt, traditionally produced in India.
embroidered Indian felt rug

Examples

The artisan displayed a beautiful numdah with intricate embroidery at the local market.
They decorated the living room floor with a vibrant red numdah from Kashmir.
Collectors often seek out antique numdahs for their unique craftsmanship and patterns.

Etymology

From Hindi नमदा (namdā), meaning 'felt' or 'felt rug'.
