unquestionableness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈkwɛstʃənəblnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɛstʃ(ə)nəblnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being beyond question, doubt, or dispute; certainty or indisputability
state of being unquestionable

Examples

His honesty was marked by an unquestionableness that everyone recognized.
The unquestionableness of her alibi convinced the jury of her innocence.
Financial records provided an unquestionableness that ended all speculation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unquestionability - the state of being unquestionable; certainty

Etymology

From 'unquestionable' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating the quality of being unquestionable.
