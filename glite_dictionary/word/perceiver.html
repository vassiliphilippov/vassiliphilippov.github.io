perceiver

1. NOUN

/ pərˈsiːvər / (AmE), / pəˈsiːvə / (BrE)
a person who becomes aware of things or events through the senses; someone who perceives
someone who perceives or becomes aware

Examples

As the perceiver, she noticed details that everyone else overlooked.
In philosophy, the distinction between the perceiver and the object perceived is often discussed.
Each perceiver brings their own experiences to the interpretation of art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: observer - a person who watches or notices something
  • Synonym: perceiver - someone who perceives or becomes aware

Etymology

From the verb 'perceive' (from Old French percevoir, from Latin percipere), plus suffix '-er'.
