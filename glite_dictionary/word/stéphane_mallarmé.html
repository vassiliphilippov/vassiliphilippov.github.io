Stéphane Mallarmé

1. PROPN

/ steɪˈfɑn mæˈlɑr.meɪ / (AmE), / steɪˈfɑːn mæˈlɑː.meɪ / (BrE)
(1842–1898) A French symbolist poet known for his innovative use of free verse and influential role in modern poetry.
French symbolist poet (1842–1898)

Examples

Stéphane Mallarmé is considered one of the most influential French poets of the 19th century.
Many literary scholars have studied the impact of Stéphane Mallarmé's work on modern poetry.
The poetry of Stéphane Mallarmé is often characterized by its complexity and use of symbolism.

Etymology

From the French given name Stéphane and surname Mallarmé.
