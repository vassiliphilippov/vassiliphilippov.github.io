pure

1. ADJ

/ pjʊr / (AmE), / pjʊə / (BrE)
free from any mixture or discordant qualities; not mixed with anything else, especially in reference to sound, color, or emotion.
free from mixture or discordant qualities

Examples

The choir's voices joined in a pure and harmonious melody that filled the cathedral.
She admired the pure blue of the sky, untouched by clouds or haze.
His intentions were pure, free from any hint of selfishness or ulterior motive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unmixed - not mixed with anything else; pure
  • Antonym: impure - mixed with something else; not pure

Etymology

From Latin 'purus' (clean, clear, unmixed).
