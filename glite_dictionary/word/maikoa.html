maikoa

1. NOUN

/ maɪˈkoʊ.ə / (AmE), / maɪˈkəʊ.ə / (BrE)
A South American plant known for its large, fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers, cultivated in gardens and for ornamental purposes.
South American plant with fragrant flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with the scent of maikoa blooming in the late afternoon sun.
Botanists study the maikoa for its unique trumpet-shaped flowers and aromatic fragrance.
She chose to plant maikoa along the walkway to create a visually striking entrance.
