prizewinning

1. ADJ

/ ˈpraɪzˌwɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpraɪzˌwɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
having won a prize in a competition or contest; awarded as a winner.
having won a prize or award

Examples

Her prizewinning novel was praised by critics around the world.
We enjoyed a tasting of the vineyard's prizewinning wines at the festival.
The prizewinning science project was creative and well researched.

Synset Examples

a champion show dog
a prizewinning wine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: award-winning - having won an award

Etymology

Formed from 'prize' + the present participle of 'win'.
