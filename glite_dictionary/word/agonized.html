agonized

1. ADJ

/ ˈæɡ.əˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈaɡ.ə.naɪzd / (BrE)
expressing or showing extreme pain, suffering, or anguish
expressing great pain or anguish

Examples

She let out an agonized cry when she heard the terrible news.
His agonized expression made it clear he was in severe pain.
The decision left her feeling agonized for days.

Synset Examples

agonized screams

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pained - showing pain or discomfort
  • Synonym: distressed - feeling or showing anxiety or suffering
  • Antonym: calm - not showing agitation or strong emotion

Etymology

From 'agonize' (to suffer agony) + -ed, first recorded in the 17th century; ultimately from Greek 'agōn' (struggle).
