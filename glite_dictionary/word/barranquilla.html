Barranquilla

1. PROPN

/ bɑːˌrɑːnˈkiːjə / (AmE), / bæˌræŋˈkiːjə / (BrE)
a major port city in northern Colombia, located near the Caribbean Sea at the mouth of the Magdalena River
port city in northern Colombia

Examples

The Carnival of Barranquilla is one of the largest and most colorful festivals in Colombia.
She traveled to Barranquilla to study the unique blend of music and culture found there.
Many international ships dock at the busy port of Barranquilla each year.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Barranquilla', diminutive of 'barranca' (ravine), referring to the small ravines near the area.
