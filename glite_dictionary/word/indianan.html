Indianan

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdiːənən / (AmE), / ɪnˈdiːənən / (BrE)
a person who was born in or is a resident of the state of Indiana, USA
person from Indiana, USA

Examples

As an Indianan, she takes pride in her hometown and its traditions.
Many Indianans gather each year at the Indiana State Fair.
He introduced himself as an Indianan when visiting the East Coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hoosier - informal term for a native or resident of Indiana

Etymology

Formed from Indiana, the name of the US state, plus the suffix -an.
