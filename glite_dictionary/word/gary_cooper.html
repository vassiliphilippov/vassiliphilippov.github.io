Gary Cooper

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɛri ˈkuːpər / (AmE), / ˈɡæri ˈkuːpə / (BrE)
(1901–1961) An American film actor known for his portrayals of strong, silent heroes in classic Hollywood movies.
American film actor (1901–1961)

Examples

Gary Cooper starred in many classic Westerns, often playing the rugged individualist.
Her favorite actor growing up was Gary Cooper because of his understated style.
Film historians often cite Gary Cooper as the quintessential American hero of early cinema.
