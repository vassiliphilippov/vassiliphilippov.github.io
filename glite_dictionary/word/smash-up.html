smash-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmæʃ ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈsmæʃ ʌp / (BrE)
a serious accident in which vehicles crash violently into each other
serious vehicle collision or accident

Examples

There was a terrible smash-up on the highway during the morning rush hour.
The police cleared the road after a multi-car smash-up blocked all lanes.
After the smash-up, both vehicles were left completely wrecked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crash - a collision, typically involving vehicles
  • Degree Lower: fender-bender - a minor car accident
