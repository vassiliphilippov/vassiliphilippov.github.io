goon

1. NOUN

/ ɡun / (AmE), / ɡuːn / (BrE)
A foolish or awkward person; someone who behaves in a silly or clumsy way.
a foolish or awkward person
informal

Examples

Don't be such a goon; you nearly knocked over the vase.
He made a goon of himself trying to impress everyone at the party.
Sometimes I feel like a total goon when I trip in public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • Antonym: genius - an exceptionally intelligent person

2. NOUN

/ ɡun / (AmE), / ɡuːn / (BrE)
a violent or aggressive young criminal, often used to refer to a hired thug or gangster
an aggressive young criminal or thug

Examples

The goon threatened anyone who dared to cross his boss.
Police arrested a goon involved in last night's robbery.
That part of the city is run by goons working for various gangs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thug - a violent person, especially a criminal
  • Synonym: gangster - a member of an organized group of violent criminals

Etymology

Possibly from US slang, origin uncertain; popularized in the 20th century.
