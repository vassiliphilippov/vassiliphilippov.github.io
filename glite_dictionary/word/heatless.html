heatless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhiːtləs / (AmE), / ˈhiːtləs / (BrE)
not producing or accompanied by heat; without generating heat.
not producing heat

Examples

The glow from the firefly is a heatless light produced by a chemical reaction.
This lamp uses an LED that emits heatless light, making it ideal for delicate displays.
Scientists studied the heatless energy emitted by certain deep-sea creatures.

Synset Examples

luminescent organisms emit heatless light

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cool - having a low temperature; somewhat cold
  • Degree Higher: hot - having a high degree of heat or temperature
