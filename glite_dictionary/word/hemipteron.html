hemipteron

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈmɪp.təˌrɑn / (AmE), / hɪˈmɪp.tər.ɒn / (BrE)
any insect of the order Hemiptera, characterized by sucking mouthparts and forewings partially thickened and leathery at the base; includes true bugs such as aphids and cicadas.
insect of the order Hemiptera

Examples

The hemipteron feeds by piercing plant tissues with its specialized mouthparts.
Scientists discovered a new species of hemipteron in the Amazon rainforest.
Aphids, a type of hemipteron, can cause significant damage to crops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hemipteran - insect of the order Hemiptera; variant spelling
  • Synonym: bug - general term for insects, especially of the order Hemiptera

Etymology

From Greek hēmi- (half) + pteron (wing), referring to the structure of their wings.
