gratis

1. ADV

/ ˈɡrætɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtɪs / (BrE)
without charge; free of cost
without charge; free of cost

Examples

The hotel offered breakfast gratis to all its guests.
He received legal advice gratis from the firm.
You can get a copy of the report gratis if you request it online.

Synset Examples

I'll give you this gratis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free - without payment; at no cost

Etymology

From Latin 'gratis', ablative of 'gratia' (favor, kindness).

2. ADJ

/ ˈɡrætɪs, ˈɡreɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡrætɪs, ˈɡreɪtɪs / (BrE)
Given or done without payment; costing nothing.
costing nothing; without charge

Examples

The museum offers gratis admission on Sundays to encourage more visitors.
All refreshments at the conference were provided gratis by local sponsors.
As a loyal customer, she received a gratis upgrade to a larger room.

Synset Examples

complimentary tickets
free admission

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free - not costing any money; given without payment
  • Synonym: complimentary - given free, especially as a courtesy or favor

Etymology

From Latin 'gratis', ablative of 'gratia' (favor).
