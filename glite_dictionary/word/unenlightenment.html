unenlightenment

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈlaɪt.ənd.mənt / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈlaɪt.ən.mənt / (BrE)
the state of lacking knowledge, understanding, or awareness; ignorance
lack of knowledge or understanding

Examples

Many conflicts in history have resulted from unenlightenment and misunderstanding between cultures.
The professor's lecture challenged the unenlightenment that had dominated public opinion for years.
Escaping unenlightenment requires curiosity and a willingness to learn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or information
  • Antonym: enlightenment - the state of gaining knowledge or understanding

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'enlightenment', meaning the absence of enlightenment.
