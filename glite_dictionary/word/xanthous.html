xanthous

1. ADJ

/ ˈzænθəs / (AmE), / ˈzanθəs / (BrE)
yellow or yellowish in color; resembling the color of an egg yolk or the hue between green and orange on the spectrum
yellowish or egg-yolk colored

Examples

The painter chose a xanthous hue to brighten the living room walls.
Her hair had a xanthous tint, reminiscent of ripe wheat fields.
In the sunlight, the chick's feathers appeared soft and xanthous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow - of the color between green and orange in the spectrum
  • Degree Lower: lemon - pale yellow color, like the fruit

Etymology

From Greek xanthos (yellow).
