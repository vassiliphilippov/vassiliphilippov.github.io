ILO

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪ ɛl ˈoʊ / (AmE), / ˌaɪ ɛl ˈəʊ / (BrE)
abbreviation for International Labour Organization, a United Nations agency concerned with international labor standards and rights.
UN agency on labor standards

Examples

The ILO sets international labor standards to protect workers' rights.
Many countries follow guidelines provided by the ILO when drafting labor laws.
At the conference, experts discussed the latest ILO report on child labor.

Etymology

Acronym from International Labour Organization.
