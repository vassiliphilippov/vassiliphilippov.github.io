rigidifying

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪdʒɪdəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪdʒɪdɪfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming stiff or rigid
becoming stiff or rigid

Examples

The rigidifying of polymers can greatly affect their strength and durability.
As the mixture cooled, a rigidifying occurred that made it hard to mold.
Prolonged exposure to cold led to the gradual rigidifying of the animal's muscles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stiffening - the process of becoming stiff
