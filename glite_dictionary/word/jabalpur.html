Jabalpur

1. PROPN

/ dʒəˈbɑlpʊr / (AmE), / dʒəˈbælpʊə / (BrE)
a large industrial city in central India, southeast of Delhi, known for manufacturing and historical importance
city in central India

Examples

She was born and raised in Jabalpur before moving abroad for her studies.
Many major manufacturing industries are located in Jabalpur.
The marble rocks near Jabalpur attract tourists from all over the world.
