Nogales

1. PROPN

/ noʊˈɡɑːlɛs / (AmE), / nəʊˈɡɑːlɛs / (BrE)
A town in southern Arizona, United States, located on the border with Mexico opposite Nogales, Mexico.
border town in Arizona, USA

Examples

Many people cross from Mexico into the United States through Nogales each day.
The border fence that divides Nogales is a prominent landmark in the city.
Several historic buildings in Nogales reflect its unique cultural heritage.

Etymology

From Spanish 'nogales', meaning 'walnut trees'.

2. PROPN

/ noʊˈɡɑːleɪs / (AmE), / nəʊˈɡɑːleɪs / (BrE)
a town in northern Mexico, situated on the border with Arizona, United States.
Mexican border town

Examples

They crossed into Mexico at Nogales during their road trip.
Trade between the United States and Mexico is vibrant in Nogales.
The twin cities of Nogales on each side of the border share a unique culture.

Etymology

From Spanish 'nogales', plural of 'nogal' meaning 'walnut tree'.
