cunt

1. NOUN

/ kʌnt / (AmE), / kʌnt / (BrE)
A highly vulgar term for the female genitals, considered extremely offensive in most English-speaking contexts.
vulgar term for female genitals
vulgar offensive

Examples

He was expelled from school for loudly using the word cunt in class.
The novel was controversial for including the explicit term cunt in its dialogue.
She was shocked at how casually he uttered the word cunt during their conversation.

Etymology

From Middle English 'cunte', related to Old Norse 'kunta'; origins predate the 13th century.
