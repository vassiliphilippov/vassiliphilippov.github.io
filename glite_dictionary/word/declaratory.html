declaratory

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈklɛrətɔri / (AmE), / dɪˈklarətəri / (BrE)
stating or making something clear in the form of a declaration, especially in a formal or legal context.
serving as a declaration; formal statement

Examples

The court issued a declaratory judgment outlining the rights of both parties.
A declaratory sentence simply states a fact or opinion.
The organization released a declaratory statement on the new policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: declarative - making a statement or declaration

Etymology

From Late Latin 'declaratorius', from Latin 'declarare' (to declare).
