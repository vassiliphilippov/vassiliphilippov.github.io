Male

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑː.leɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.leɪ / (BrE)
the capital city of the Maldives, located centrally among the islands of the nation.
capital city of the Maldives

Examples

Tourists often visit Male before exploring the other islands of the Maldives.
The population of Male has grown rapidly in recent years.
Flights to the Maldives usually arrive at Male International Airport.

Etymology

From Maldivian Dhivehi މާލެ (Māle).
