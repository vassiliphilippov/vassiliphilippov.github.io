behavioral

1. ADJ

/ bəˈheɪvjɚəl / (AmE), / bɪˈheɪvjərəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving behavior, especially the observable activity of people or animals.
relating to behavior

Examples

The psychologist specializes in behavioral therapy for children with anxiety disorders.
Zookeepers noticed changes in the lion's behavioral patterns after the enclosure was renovated.
Smoking cessation programs often combine medication with behavioral strategies to support long-term quitting.

Synset Examples

behavioral sciences

Related Terms

  • Synonym: behavior - the way in which a person or animal acts

Etymology

From 'behavior' + '-al', forming adjectives meaning 'relating to'.
