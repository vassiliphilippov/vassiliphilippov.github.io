classicistic

1. ADJ

/ klæsɪˈsɪstɪk / (AmE), / klæsɪˈsɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of classicism, especially in art, architecture, or literature
relating to classicism

Examples

The new opera house is designed in a classicistic style reminiscent of ancient Greece.
Her paintings have a distinctly classicistic approach, emphasizing harmony and balance.
During the 19th century, many architects adopted a classicistic aesthetic for public buildings.

Synset Examples

the classicistic tradition

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classical - relating to ancient Greek or Roman principles and style

Etymology

From 'classicism' + '-istic', modeled on similar adjectival forms.
