blogger

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɑɡər / (AmE), / ˈblɒɡə / (BrE)
a person who writes, maintains, or updates a blog, typically publishing content online regularly
person who writes a blog

Examples

The blogger shared her travel experiences with thousands of readers online.
Becoming a successful blogger requires creativity and consistency.
He works as a professional blogger, reviewing technology products.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vlogger - a person who creates and posts video blogs (vlogs)

Etymology

From 'blog' (short for weblog) + '-er', indicating a person who does something.
