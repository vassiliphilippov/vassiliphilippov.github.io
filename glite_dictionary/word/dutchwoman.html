Dutchwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌtʃˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈdʌtʃˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is a native or inhabitant of the Netherlands (formerly known as Holland)
woman from the Netherlands

Examples

The Dutchwoman won the gold medal in speed skating at the Olympics.
As a proud Dutchwoman, she often shared stories about her homeland with her friends.
The author is a Dutchwoman who writes vividly about life in Amsterdam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dutchman - a man who is a native or inhabitant of the Netherlands
  • Synonym: Dutch - relating to the Netherlands, its people, or their language

Etymology

Dutch + woman, referring to a female from the Netherlands.
