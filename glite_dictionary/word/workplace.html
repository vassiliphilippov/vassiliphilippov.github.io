workplace

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːkˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌpleɪs / (BrE)
a place where people do their jobs
location where work is done

Examples

Smoking is not allowed anywhere in the workplace.
Many companies now promote diversity in the workplace.
She enjoys collaborating with her colleagues at her workplace.

Synset Examples

he arrived at work early today

Related Terms

  • Synonym: office - room or building where people work at desks, especially administrative work
  • Synonym: worksite - a place where work, especially physical work, is done
