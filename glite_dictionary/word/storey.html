storey

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔːri / (AmE), / ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
a level of a building consisting of a room or set of rooms at the same height
level or floor of a building
british

Examples

The new office is on the fifth storey of the building.
Their flat occupies the top storey and has a wonderful view of the city.
The museum added a new storey to house its growing collection.

Synset Examples

what level is the office on?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: story - American spelling for 'level of a building'

Etymology

From Middle English 'storie', possibly relating to a row of paintings or windows representing a story on each level.
