doubtless

1. ADV

/ ˈdaʊtləs / (AmE), / ˈdaʊtləs / (BrE)
Without doubt; certainly.
certainly, without doubt

Examples

She will doubtless accept the offer since it matches her expectations.
His explanation was doubtless clear to everyone in the room.
There are doubtless many reasons why the project failed.

Synset Examples

it's undoubtedly very beautiful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undoubtedly - without being open to doubt; certainly
  • Synonym: certainly - without any doubt; surely
  • Antonym: possibly - perhaps; to a degree that is possible

Etymology

From Middle English, formed from 'doubt' + '-less'.
