tenorist

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnərɪst / (AmE), / ˈtɛnərɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays the tenor saxophone, especially in jazz or popular music
a tenor saxophone player

Examples

The renowned tenorist delivered a mesmerizing solo during the concert.
She has played with some of the greatest tenorists in the jazz world.
As a young musician, he dreamed of becoming a celebrated tenorist on the international stage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saxophonist - a person who plays the saxophone

Etymology

From 'tenor' (as in tenor saxophone) + '-ist' (denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something).
