A6

1. NOUN

/ eɪ sɪks / (AmE), / eɪ sɪks / (BrE)
a standard paper size measuring 105 by 148 millimeters, typically used for cards and small booklets
standard paper size (105 × 148 mm)

Examples

I printed the invitations on A6 paper for a compact design.
The notebook comes in A6 size, which fits easily into a small bag.
Please use A6 sheets for your flashcards so they are uniform.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: A5 - standard paper size measuring 148 × 210 mm
  • Degree Lower: A7 - standard paper size measuring 74 × 105 mm

Etymology

Defined by the ISO 216 standard, with 'A' denoting the series and '6' indicating the size within the series.
