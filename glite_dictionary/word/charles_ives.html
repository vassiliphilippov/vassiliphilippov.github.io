Charles Ives

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃɑːrlz aɪvz / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz aɪvz / (BrE)
(1874–1954) American composer known for his innovative use of polytonality and experimental techniques in classical music.
American composer, innovator in classical music

Examples

The music of Charles Ives challenged conventional ideas about harmony and rhythm.
Many consider Charles Ives one of the pioneers of American classical music.
My professor recommended listening to Charles Ives for his groundbreaking orchestral works.
