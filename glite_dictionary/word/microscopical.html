microscopical

1. ADJ

/ ˌmaɪ.krəˈskɑː.pɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪ.krəˈskɒ.pɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to or used in microscopy; concerning the use of microscopes.
relating to microscopy or microscopes

Examples

The scientist conducted a microscopical examination of the sample to detect any cellular abnormalities.
After preparing the slides, a microscopical analysis revealed the presence of bacteria.
Advances in microscopical techniques have significantly improved our understanding of cell structure.

Synset Examples

microscopic analysis
microscopical examination

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microscopic - so small as to be visible only with a microscope

Etymology

Formed from 'microscope' + '-ical', following the pattern of scientific adjectives.
