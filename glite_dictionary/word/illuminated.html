illuminated

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈluːməˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪˈljuːmɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
provided with artificial light; lit up by artificial means
lit up by artificial light

Examples

The garden was beautifully illuminated with strings of fairy lights for the party.
The monument remains illuminated throughout the night so it can be seen from afar.
Several illuminated signs lined the busy downtown street.

Synset Examples

illuminated advertising
looked up at the lighted windows
a brightly lit room
a well-lighted stairwell

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lit - provided with light; illuminated
  • Antonym: dark - without light; not illuminated

Etymology

From Latin 'illuminare' (to light up) via Medieval Latin and Old French.
