haulage

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːlɪdʒ / (BrE)
the act or process of transporting goods, especially by road or rail
transporting goods by road or rail

Examples

The company specializes in haulage of heavy machinery across the country.
Rising fuel prices have affected the costs of haulage in recent years.
Many farmers rely on local haulage firms to transport their crops to market.

Synset Examples

the haul up the hill went very slowly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transportation - the act of transporting something from one location to another
  • Synonym: delivery - the process of bringing goods to a destination

Etymology

From haul + -age, patterned after French analogues like transportage.
