alchemical

1. ADJ

/ ælˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ælˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of alchemy; resembling or pertaining to the philosophy and practices of medieval alchemists.
relating to alchemy

Examples

The alchemical symbols adorned the walls of the ancient laboratory.
His writing possesses an alchemical quality that transforms the ordinary into the extraordinary.
They were fascinated by the alchemical quest to turn base metals into gold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alchemy - medieval forerunner of chemistry, aiming to transform matter

Etymology

From Medieval Latin alchimicus, from alchimia ('alchemy').
