one hundred forty-five

1. NUM

/ wʌn hʌn.drəd ˈfɔr.t̬i faɪv / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌn.drɪd ˈfɔː.ti faɪv / (BrE)
The number that is five more than one hundred forty; 145.
the number 145

Examples

The painting was created one hundred forty-five years ago.
