Pole

1. NOUN

/ poʊl / (AmE), / pəʊl / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Poland
a person from Poland

Examples

She met a young Pole while studying abroad in Warsaw.
Many Poles celebrate traditional holidays with unique customs.
As a proud Pole, he often speaks about his country's history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Polish - relating to Poland, its people, or language

Etymology

From Polish 'Polak', meaning a Polish person.
