grandma

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrænˌmɑ / (AmE), / ˈɡrændmɑː / (BrE)
the mother of one's father or mother; a grandmother
one's grandmother

Examples

My grandma always bakes cookies for us when we visit.
On Sundays, we have lunch with my grandma at her house.
My grandma taught me how to knit when I was a child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandmother - the mother of one's father or mother
  • Antonym: grandpa - the father of one's father or mother; a grandfather

Etymology

Informal shortening of 'grandmother'.
