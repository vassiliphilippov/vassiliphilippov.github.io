unspotted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈspɑt̬ɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɒtɪd / (BrE)
free from any spots, stains, or blemishes; pure or immaculate
without any spots or stains

Examples

She wore an unspotted white dress to the ceremony.
His reputation remained unspotted despite the many accusations.
The artist cleaned the canvas until it was unspotted and ready to use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotless - completely clean; without any spots or stains

Etymology

From un- (not) + spotted (having spots).
