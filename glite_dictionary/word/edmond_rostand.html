Edmond Rostand

1. PROPN

/ ɛdˌmɔ̃ roʊˈstɑːn / (AmE), / ɛdˌmɔ̃ rɒˈstãː / (BrE)
(1868–1918) French dramatist and poet, best known for writing the play Cyrano de Bergerac.
French dramatist and poet (1868–1918)

Examples

The play 'Cyrano de Bergerac' was written by Edmond Rostand in the late nineteenth century.

Edmond Rostand's name is forever linked to the character of Cyrano.
