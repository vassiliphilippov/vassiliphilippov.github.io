Zinacef

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪnəˌsɛf / (AmE), / ˈzɪnəsɛf / (BrE)
a brand name for the antibiotic cefuroxime, used to treat various bacterial infections
antibiotic drug (brand name cefuroxime)
medical
