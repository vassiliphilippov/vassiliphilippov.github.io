asserted

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɝː.tɪd / (AmE), / əˈsɜː.tɪd / (BrE)
confidently stated or declared to be true, especially without proof
declared as true or real

Examples

The asserted benefits of the new policy were called into question by several experts.
He disputed the asserted facts presented in the report.
The asserted ownership of the land remains a matter of legal debate.

Synset Examples

the asserted value of the painting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alleged - claimed or asserted to be true, without proof
  • Antonym: proven - shown to be true by evidence or argument
