helmeted

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛl.mɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɛl.mɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
wearing or equipped with a helmet.
wearing a helmet

Examples

The helmeted motorcyclists waited for the traffic light to change.
A group of helmeted firefighters entered the burning building.
The helmeted soldier stood guard at the entrance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unhelmeted - not wearing a helmet

Etymology

From 'helmet' + '-ed' suffix, denoting possession or presence.
