León

1. PROPN

/ leɪˈoʊn / (AmE), / leɪˈɒn / (BrE)
A city in central Mexico, in the state of Guanajuato, known for its leather industry and as a major economic center.
city in Guanajuato, Mexico

Examples

They visited León to attend the annual leather fair held in the city.
The climate in León is warm and dry for most of the year.
León has grown rapidly due to its thriving shoe manufacturing industry.

Etymology

From Spanish 'León', named after the city of León in Spain; from Latin 'Legio' (legion).

2. PROPN

/ leɪˈoʊn / (AmE), / leɪˈɒn / (BrE)
a historical region and former kingdom in northwestern Spain, once an independent state in the Middle Ages.
historical region and former kingdom in Spain

Examples

The medieval kingdom of León played a central role in the history of Spain.
Many Romanesque churches can be found throughout León and its surrounding region.
During the Reconquista, León was a major center of Christian resistance against Muslim rule.

Etymology

From Spanish 'León', derived from Latin 'Legio', named for a Roman legion stationed there.
