azonal

1. ADJ

/ eɪˈzoʊnəl / (AmE), / eɪˈzəʊnəl / (BrE)
(of a feature or process) not restricted to particular biological, climatic, or geographical zones.
not limited to a zone or region

Examples

The azonal soils are found throughout many different climatic regions.
In this study, we examine the impact of azonal factors on plant growth.

Etymology
