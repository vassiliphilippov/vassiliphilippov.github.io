affectional

1. ADJ

/ əˈfɛkʃənl̩ / (AmE), / əˈfɛkʃənəl / (BrE)
characterized by or relating to feelings of affection or emotion
relating to feelings of affection

Examples

The therapist encouraged an affectional bond between parent and child.
She responded to the news with an affectional display of joy.
An affectional connection can strengthen any relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotional - relating to emotions

Etymology

From 'affection' + '-al', with 'affection' originating from Latin 'affectio' (a disposition, feeling).
