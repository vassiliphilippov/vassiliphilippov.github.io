greenish

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡriːnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnɪʃ / (BrE)
having a tinge or slight shade of green; somewhat green in color
somewhat green in color

Examples

The water in the lake had a greenish tint after the algae bloom.
He chose a greenish sweater that matched his eyes.
The walls were painted a pale, greenish color to create a calming effect.

Synset Examples

a green tree
green fields
green paint

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: green - of the color between blue and yellow; resembling grass
  • Degree Lower: bluish - having a tinge or shade of blue; somewhat blue

Etymology

From 'green' with the suffix '-ish' meaning 'somewhat' or 'like'.
