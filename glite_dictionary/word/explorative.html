explorative

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈsplɔrətɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplɒrətɪv / (BrE)
serving or intended for the purpose of exploration or investigation; related to exploration or discovery.
serving exploration or investigation

Examples

The company conducted an explorative study before entering the new market.
Her approach to art is highly explorative, always seeking new styles and ideas.
The expedition team set out on an explorative mission through uncharted forests.

Synset Examples

an exploratory operation
exploratory reconnaissance
digging an exploratory well in the Gulf of Mexico
exploratory talks between diplomats

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exploratory - serving or intended for exploration or investigation

Etymology

From Latin explorare (to investigate) + English suffix -ive.
