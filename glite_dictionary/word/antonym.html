antonym

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.təˌnɪm / (AmE), / ˈæn.tə.nɪm / (BrE)
a word that has the opposite meaning of another word
word with opposite meaning

Examples

'Happy' is an antonym of 'sad'.
The word 'dark' serves as the antonym of 'light' in this context.
Can you find an antonym for the word 'generous'?

Synset Examples

to him the antonym of ‘gay’ was ‘depressed’

Related Terms

  • Antonym: synonym - a word that has the same or nearly the same meaning as another word

Etymology

From Greek anti- (against) + onoma (name).
