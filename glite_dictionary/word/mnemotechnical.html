mnemotechnical

1. ADJ

/ nɛmoʊtɛkˈnɪkəl / (AmE), / nɛməʊtɛkˈnɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to methods or techniques designed to aid memory
relating to memory aids or techniques

Examples

The teacher used several mnemotechnical tricks to help students remember the periodic table.
Researchers are developing new mnemotechnical methods for patients with memory loss.
Learning languages can be easier with mnemotechnical aids such as rhymes or acronyms.

Synset Examples

mnemonic device

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mnemonic - a device or technique to help remember information

Etymology

From Greek mnēmē (memory) + technical; patterned after 'mnemonic'.
