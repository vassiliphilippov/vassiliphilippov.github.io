beadwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːdˌwɝːk / (AmE), / ˈbiːdˌwɜːk / (BrE)
decorative work or ornamentation created by sewing, weaving, or stringing beads together
decoration using beads

Examples

She wore a dress covered in intricate beadwork for the festival.
The museum displayed traditional beadwork from different cultures around the world.
Making beadwork requires patience and an eye for detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embroidery - decorative needlework using thread or yarn

2. NOUN

/ ˈbidˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈbiːdˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a beaded molding used for edging or decorating furniture or architectural surfaces
beaded molding for furniture or decor

Examples

The cabinetmaker added beadwork along the edges for a decorative touch.
Victorian-era furniture often features intricate beadwork to enhance its elegance.
Restoring the antique chair required careful repair of its damaged beadwork molding.

Etymology

From bead + work, originally referring to objects made by stringing together beads.
