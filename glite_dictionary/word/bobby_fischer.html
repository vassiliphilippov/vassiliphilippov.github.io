Bobby Fischer

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑːbi ˈfɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈbɒbi ˈfɪʃə / (BrE)
(1943–2008) An American chess grandmaster and world chess champion from 1972 to 1975, known for his exceptional skill and eccentric personality.
American chess grandmaster and world champion

Examples

Bobby Fischer stunned the world when he won the World Chess Championship in 1972.
Many consider Bobby Fischer one of the greatest chess players of all time.
The rivalry between Bobby Fischer and Boris Spassky is legendary in the chess community.
