sociologically

1. ADV

/ ˌsoʊsiəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that relates to sociology or considers social relationships and structures.
from a sociological perspective

Examples

Sociologically, the trend reflects changing family structures in urban populations.
The film can be analyzed sociologically to understand its impact on society.
Sociologically speaking, gender roles are shaped by cultural norms and values.

Synset Examples

sociologically speaking, this is an interesting phenomenon

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: psychologically - in a way relating to psychology or the mind

Etymology

From "sociology" + "-ally" (adverbial suffix).
