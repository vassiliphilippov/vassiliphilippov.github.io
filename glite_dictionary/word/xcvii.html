xcvii

1. NUM

/ ɛks si ˈvɪi / (AmE), / ɛks si ˈvɪi / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number ninety-seven (97)
Roman numeral for 97

Examples

The year xcvii of the Roman calendar corresponds to 97 AD.
He labeled the section with the Roman numeral xcvii to indicate it was the ninety-seventh.
The inscription uses xcvii as a marker for the anniversary year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 97 - the number ninety-seven

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10), C (100), V (5), II (2); X before C indicates 10 from 100 (90), V and II for 7.
