Zestril

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɛstrɪl / (AmE), / ˈzɛstrɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked drug used as an ACE inhibitor to treat high blood pressure and after heart attacks; generic name lisinopril.
ACE inhibitor drug for hypertension and heart attacks
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Zestril to help control her high blood pressure.
After his heart attack, he was started on Zestril as part of his treatment plan.
Some patients may experience a dry cough while taking Zestril.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Prinivil - another trade name for the drug lisinopril
  • Synonym: lisinopril - the generic name for Zestril, an ACE inhibitor used to treat hypertension
