frilly

1. ADJ

/ ˈfrɪli / (AmE), / ˈfrɪli / (BrE)
decorated with frills or ruffles; having an ornamental, fancy, or elaborate edge or trim.
decorated with ruffles or frills

Examples

She wore a frilly white dress to the party, which everyone admired.
The pillows were decorated with frilly lace that added a romantic touch to the room.
I prefer simple shirts over those with frilly collars and cuffs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: plain - not decorated with frills; simple in style

Etymology

From 'frill' + '-y', denoting 'full of frills'.
