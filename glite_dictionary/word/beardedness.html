beardedness

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪrdɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈbɪədɪdnəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of having a beard.
the quality of having a beard

Examples

His impressive beardedness made him stand out among the clean-shaven crowd.
The role required an actor whose beardedness gave a sense of wisdom and age.
Questions about beardedness in historical portraits often lead to debates about fashion trends.

Synset Examples

In this scene, the penetrated partner's head is stretched awkwardly out on the floor — placing further emphasis on his beardedness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bearded - having a beard

Etymology

From 'bearded' + '-ness', forming a noun meaning the condition of having a beard.
