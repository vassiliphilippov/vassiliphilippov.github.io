spoke

1. NOUN

/ spoʊk / (AmE), / spəʊk / (BrE)
one of the bars or rods radiating from the center of a wheel to the outer edge, supporting the rim
radial support bar of a wheel

Examples

The bicycle's spoke was bent after the accident.
Each spoke helps distribute the weight evenly on the wheel.
He repaired the damaged spoke with a special tool.

Etymology

From Middle English 'spoke', from Old English 'spāca' (rod of a wheel).
