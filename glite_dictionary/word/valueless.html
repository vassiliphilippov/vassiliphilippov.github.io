valueless

1. ADJ

/ ˈvæl.ju.ləs / (AmE), / ˈvæl.juː.ləs / (BrE)
Having no worth or value; not useful or significant.
having no value; worthless

Examples

After the economic collapse, the currency became completely valueless.
She threw away a box of old, valueless papers.
The painting turned out to be a fake and was therefore valueless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthless - having no value or use
  • Antonym: valuable - having great value

Etymology

Formed from 'value' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without'.
