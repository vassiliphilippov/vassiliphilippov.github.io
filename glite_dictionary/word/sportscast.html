sportscast

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔːrtˌskæst / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtskɑːst / (BrE)
a radio or television broadcast that presents sports news, commentary, or live coverage of sports events
broadcast of sports news or events

Examples

The network will air a live sportscast of tonight's basketball game.
I listened to the sportscast on the radio while driving home.
She started her career as a sportscast reporter for a local TV station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broadcast - a radio or television transmission

Etymology

Blend of 'sports' and 'broadcast.'.

2. VERB

/ ˈspɔrtsˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtskɑːst / (BrE)
to broadcast a sports event, especially on radio or television
broadcast a sports event

Examples

She will sportscast the football game live from the stadium tonight.
He has sportscasted over a hundred matches during his career.
They began sportscasting international tournaments in the 1950s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broadcast - to transmit a program or information by radio or television

Etymology

Blend of 'sports' and 'broadcast'.
