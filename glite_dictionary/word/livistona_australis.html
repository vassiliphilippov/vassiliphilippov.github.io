Livistona australis

1. NOUN

/ lɪˌvɪstənə ɔːˈstreɪlɪs / (AmE), / lɪˌvɪstəʊnə ɔːˈstreɪlɪs / (BrE)
an Australian palm tree with edible leaf buds when young, commonly known as the cabbage-tree palm
Australian cabbage-tree palm species

Examples

The Livistona australis grows widely along the east coast of Australia.
Many bushwalkers can identify the slender trunk and fan-shaped leaves of Livistona australis.
Indigenous Australians traditionally harvested the edible buds of the Livistona australis.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin; genus name 'Livistona' plus 'australis', from Latin meaning 'southern'.
