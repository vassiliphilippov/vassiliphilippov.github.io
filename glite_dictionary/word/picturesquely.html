picturesquely

1. ADV

/ ˌpɪktʃəˈrɛskli / (AmE), / ˌpɪktʃəˈrɛskli / (BrE)
In a way that is visually attractive, charming, or reminiscent of a picture.
in a charming, visually attractive way

Examples

The village is picturesquely situated on a hillside overlooking the river.
She described the old harbor so picturesquely that I could imagine every detail.
The trees were picturesquely reflected in the still lake at dawn.

Synset Examples

in the building trade such a trader is picturesquely described as a ‘brass plate’ merchant

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charmingly - in a charming manner

Etymology

From 'picturesque' plus the adverbial suffix '-ly'. 'Picturesque' from French 'pittoresque', from Italian 'pittoresco', from 'pittore' (painter).
