Banff

1. PROPN

/ bænf / (AmE), / bænf / (BrE)
a resort town in Alberta, Canada, located within Banff National Park in the Canadian Rockies and known for its mountainous surroundings and tourism.
resort town in Alberta, Canada

Examples

She spent her summer vacation hiking and sightseeing in Banff.
Every year, tourists flock to Banff to experience the stunning mountain scenery.
The town of Banff is surrounded by some of the most beautiful landscapes in Canada.

Etymology

Named after Banffshire, Scotland, by Canadian Pacific Railway officials.
