Julius Erving

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒuːliəs ˈɜːrvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːliəs ˈɜːvɪŋ / (BrE)
(born 1950) A famous American basketball player, also known as 'Dr. J', recognized for his career with the ABA and NBA and for revolutionizing the game with his playing style.
noted American basketball player (Dr. J)

Examples

Julius Erving was known for his incredible dunks and high-flying style on the basketball court.
Many consider Julius Erving one of the greatest players in basketball history.
As a role model both on and off the court, Julius Erving inspired countless young athletes.
