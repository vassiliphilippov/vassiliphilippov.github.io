ammo

1. NOUN

/ ˈæmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈaməʊ / (BrE)
Bullets, shells, or other projectiles used in guns; ammunition.
bullets or projectiles for guns

Examples

The soldiers checked their ammo before heading out on patrol.
We're running low on ammo and need to restock soon.
At the firing range, he loaded his ammo carefully into each magazine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ammunition - full term for bullets or explosive projectiles used in weapons

Etymology

Clipping of 'ammunition', which comes from French 'ammunition', alteration of 'munition'.
