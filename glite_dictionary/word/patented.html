patented

1. ADJ

/ ˈpætəntɪd / (AmE), / ˈpætəntɪd / (BrE)
(of a device or process) protected by a government-granted patent, giving exclusive rights to the inventor or patent holder.
protected by a patent

Examples

The company sells several patented products that are not available elsewhere.
He invented a patented process for purifying water.
Only patented technologies may be used in the competition.

Synset Examples

they are patented inventions

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unpatented - not protected by a patent

Etymology

From the verb 'patent', from Latin 'patens' (lying open).
