Jacques Tati

1. PROPN

/ ʒɑk taˈti / (AmE), / ʒak taˈti / (BrE)
(1908–1982) A French filmmaker, actor, and director renowned for his innovative comedies and influential contributions to cinema.
French filmmaker and actor (1908–1982)

Examples

The films of Jacques Tati are celebrated for their visual humor and inventive sound design.
Many critics consider Jacques Tati one of the greatest comic auteurs of the twentieth century.
Jacques Tati's masterpiece 'Playtime' is studied in film schools around the world.
