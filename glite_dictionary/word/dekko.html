dekko

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛk.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɛk.əʊ / (BrE)
A quick look or glance, especially in British slang.
British slang: a look or glance
informal slang british

Examples

Take a dekko at the painting on the wall; it's truly remarkable.
He had a quick dekko through the window before leaving.
If you get a dekko at her new car, you'll be impressed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look - to direct one's gaze in a specified direction
  • Synonym: glance - a quick or brief look

Etymology

From Hindi 'dekho' (look), imperative of 'dekhna' (to look), adopted into British slang during colonial India.
