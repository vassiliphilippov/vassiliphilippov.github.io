63

1. NUM

/ sɪksti θri / (AmE), / sɪksti θri / (BrE)
The number that is three more than sixty; sixty-three.
the number sixty-three

Examples

The team scored 63 points in the final game of the season.
She celebrated her 63rd birthday with family and friends.
Only 63 tickets remain for tonight’s concert.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sixty-two - the number sixty-two (one less than sixty-three)
  • Degree Higher: sixty-four - the number sixty-four (one more than sixty-three)
