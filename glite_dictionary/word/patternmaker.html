patternmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈpætərnˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpætənˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who designs and makes patterns, especially for use in sewing, carpentry, or metalworking
person who makes manufacturing patterns

Examples

The patternmaker created a detailed template for the new dress design.
Before production began, the patternmaker reviewed all measurements to ensure accuracy.
She apprenticed with a skilled patternmaker in a busy industrial workshop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tailor - a person whose occupation is making fitted clothes
  • Synonym: carpenter - a person who makes or repairs wooden objects
