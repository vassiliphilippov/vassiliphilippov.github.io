carte

1. NOUN

/ kɑrt / (AmE), / kɑːt / (BrE)
a menu; a list of the dishes available at a restaurant, typically written or printed
restaurant menu or list of dishes

Examples

The waiter handed us the carte as soon as we sat down.
Some high-end restaurants offer an extensive carte with many options for each course.
She struggled to choose from the French carte, as she didn't speak the language.

Synset Examples

the menu was in French

Related Terms

  • Synonym: menu - a list of the food available in a restaurant

Etymology

From French 'carte' (card, menu); related to Latin 'charta' (paper, map).
