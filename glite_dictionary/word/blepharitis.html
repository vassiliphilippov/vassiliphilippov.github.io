blepharitis

1. NOUN

/ ˌblɛfəˈraɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌblɛfəˈraɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the eyelids, typically causing redness, swelling, irritation, and crusting at the eyelid margins
inflammation of the eyelids
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with blepharitis after complaining of itching and redness around the eyes.
Chronic blepharitis can lead to discomfort and visual disturbances if not properly managed.
Effective hygiene is important in controlling the symptoms of blepharitis.

Etymology

From Greek blepharon (eyelid) + -itis (inflammation).
