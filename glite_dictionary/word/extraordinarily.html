extraordinarily

1. ADV

/ ˌɛkstrəˈɔːrdəˌnɛrəli / (AmE), / ɪkˌstrɔːˈdɪnərɪli / (BrE)
to an unusual or remarkable degree; extremely.
to an unusual or remarkable degree

Examples

The test results were extraordinarily high, surprising even the most experienced scientists.
Her generosity is extraordinarily inspiring to everyone around her.
The weather has been extraordinarily mild this winter, unlike previous years.

Synset Examples

she was inordinately smart
it will be an extraordinarily painful step to negotiate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extremely - to a very great degree
  • Synonym: remarkably - in a way that is worthy of attention
  • Antonym: ordinarily - in a usual or customary way

Etymology

From extraordinary + -ly; 'extraordinary' from Latin 'extraordinarius' (out of the normal course).
