eastside

1. ADJ

/ ˈistˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈiːstˌsaɪd / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the eastern part of a city, especially as a distinct neighborhood or district.
of the eastern part of a city

Examples

Many artists have moved into the eastside neighborhood in recent years.
She lives in an eastside apartment close to the river.
The city invested heavily in revitalizing the eastside business district.

Synset Examples

the eastside silk-stocking district

Related Terms

  • Antonym: westside - of the western part of a city

Etymology

From 'east' + 'side', indicating position within a city.
