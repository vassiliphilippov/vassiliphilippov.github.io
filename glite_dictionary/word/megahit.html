megahit

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛɡəˌhɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛɡəˌhɪt / (BrE)
a product, such as a film, song, book, or show, that achieves exceptional popularity and commercial success
something extremely successful, especially in media or entertainment

Examples

The movie quickly became a megahit, breaking box office records around the world.
Her latest single is a megahit that's playing on every radio station.
After their first book turned into a megahit, the authors were in high demand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blockbuster - a film or other product that is highly successful

Etymology

Formed from 'mega-' (meaning very large or great) and 'hit' (a successful product or performance).
