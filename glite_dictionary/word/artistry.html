artistry

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrtɪstri / (AmE), / ˈɑːtɪstri / (BrE)
a superior skill or ability in creating or performing something, especially in the arts, acquired through study, practice, or observation
superior creative skill or ability

Examples

The pianist's artistry captivated the audience throughout the entire recital.
Her artistry in painting brings every landscape to life with vivid emotion.
We were amazed by the artistry behind the intricate design of the stained glass windows.

Synset Examples

the art of conversation
it's quite an art

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skill - the ability to do something well, usually as a result of experience and training
  • Synonym: craftsmanship - high skill in a particular craft or activity, especially manual work
  • Antonym: incompetence - lack of ability or skill

Etymology

From French 'artisterie' (artistic quality); related to 'artist'.
