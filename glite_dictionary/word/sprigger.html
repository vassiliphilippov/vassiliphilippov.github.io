sprigger

1. NOUN

/ ˈsprɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈsprɪɡə / (BrE)
a worker who strips the stems from moistened tobacco leaves and binds the leaves together into bundles for processing
tobacco leaf stem stripper

Examples

She worked as a sprigger at the tobacco plant during the harvest season.
The job of a sprigger requires nimble fingers and patience.
Many spriggers start their shifts early in the morning to process the day's leaves.
