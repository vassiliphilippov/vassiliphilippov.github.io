refinisher

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈfɪnɪʃər / (AmE), / rɪˈfɪnɪʃə / (BrE)
a skilled worker who restores or applies a new finish to buildings, furniture, or similar objects, especially antiques
person who restores surfaces or finishes

Examples

We hired a professional refinisher to bring our vintage dining table back to life.
As a refinisher, she specializes in restoring old wooden furniture to its former beauty.
The antique chair looked new again thanks to the careful work of the refinisher.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restorer - a person who repairs or renovates works of art, buildings, etc.

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'refinish' + '-er', indicating a person who performs the action.
