p.a.

1. ADV

/ pi eɪ / (AmE), / piː eɪ / (BrE)
(abbreviation) Per annum; for each year, typically referring to an amount of money received or paid annually.
per year (abbreviation, esp. for money)

Examples

The interest rate on the loan is 6% p.a..
The rent is $12,000 p.a., payable in monthly installments.
He receives a pension of €20,000 p.a. from the company.

Synset Examples

he earned $100,000 per annum
we issue six volumes per annum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: per annum - for each year; yearly

Etymology

From Latin 'per annum' meaning 'by the year'.
