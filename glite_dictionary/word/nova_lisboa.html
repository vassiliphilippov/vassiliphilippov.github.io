Nova Lisboa

1. PROPN

/ ˌnoʊ.və liˈzweɪ.dzə / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ.və lɪzˈwiː.bə / (BrE)
a city in the central highlands of Angola, formerly known as Huambo.
city in central Angola

Examples

The train from Luanda to Nova Lisboa offers stunning views of the Angolan countryside.
She grew up in Nova Lisboa before moving to the capital for university.
Nova Lisboa was an important center during the colonial era of Angola.

Etymology

From Portuguese, meaning ‘New Lisbon’ (named during Portuguese colonial rule).
