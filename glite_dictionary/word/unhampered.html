unhampered

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhæmpərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhæmpəd / (BrE)
not slowed, blocked, or interfered with; free and unrestricted in action or progress.
not restricted or interfered with

Examples

She moved through the crowd unhampered by doubt or hesitation.
The river flowed unhampered after the debris was cleared away.
He worked unhampered by unnecessary bureaucracy in the new office.

Synset Examples

an outlet for healthy and unhampered action
a priest unhampered by scruple
the new stock market was unhampered by tradition

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free - not under the control or in the power of another; able to act or be as one wishes
  • Synonym: unrestricted - not restrained or limited
  • Antonym: hampered - impeded or restricted in movement or progress

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + hampered, past participle of 'hamper' (to impede).

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhæmpɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhæmpəd / (BrE)
not restricted, obstructed, or controlled; free to act or progress
not restricted or obstructed

Examples

The artist enjoyed an unhampered creative process in her new studio.
Economic growth was unhampered by government regulations.
He spoke with an unhampered honesty that surprised the audience.

Synset Examples

unhampered dissemination of news
this would give black people the opportunity to live unhampered by racism

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hampered - hindered or obstructed
  • Antonym: restricted - limited in movement, action, or progress
  • Synonym: free - not under the control or in the power of another

Etymology

formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'hampered' (past participle of 'hamper', meaning to hinder or impede)
