unlocated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈloʊˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈləʊkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having a specified or identified location; lacking a particular place or position.
not having a specific location

Examples

The artifact remains unlocated despite years of careful searching by archaeologists.
Several ancient cities mentioned in the texts are still unlocated today.
Our missing package is currently unlocated, according to the postal service.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: located - having a specified or identified location

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'located', from Latin 'locatus' (placed).
