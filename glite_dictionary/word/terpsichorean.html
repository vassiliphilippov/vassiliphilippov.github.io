terpsichorean

1. NOUN

/ tɜrˌpsɪkəˈriən / (AmE), / tɜːpˌsɪkəˈriːən / (BrE)
a professional dancer; someone who performs dance as an art or profession
professional dancer

Examples

The acclaimed terpsichorean performed a mesmerizing solo at the ballet gala.
Many aspire to become a terpsichorean and grace the world's greatest stages.
Each terpsichorean in the troupe brought unique energy to the performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dancer - someone who dances, especially as a profession

Etymology

From Terpsichore, the Muse of dance in Greek mythology, plus '-an'.
