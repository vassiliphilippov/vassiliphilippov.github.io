gratulatory

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrætʃəˌleɪtɔri / (AmE), / ˈɡratjʊlətəri / (BrE)
expressing congratulations or pleasure at someone's success or good fortune
expressing congratulations

Examples

She sent a gratulatory note to her colleague after the promotion announcement.
The mayor delivered a gratulatory speech at the award ceremony.
Many gratulatory messages poured in when the news was made public.

Synset Examples

a congratulatory telegram
the usual congratulatory crowd was conspicuously absent
a gratulatory address

Related Terms

  • Synonym: congratulatory - expressing congratulations or praise for someone's achievement

Etymology

From Latin 'gratulari' (to congratulate) via Late Latin 'gratulatorius'.
