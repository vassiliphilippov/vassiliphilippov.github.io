Yisrael

1. PROPN

/ jisˈraɛl / (AmE), / jisˈraɛl / (BrE)
An alternative Hebrew name for the modern state of Israel, a country in southwestern Asia at the eastern end of the Mediterranean Sea.
the modern state of Israel (Hebrew name)

Examples

The president of Yisrael addressed the United Nations General Assembly yesterday.
Many ancient artifacts have been discovered in Yisrael's archaeological sites.
The official language of Yisrael is Hebrew, reflecting its cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Israel - the State of Israel; a country at the eastern end of the Mediterranean

Etymology

From Hebrew יִשְׂרָאֵל (*Yisra'el*), the Hebrew name for Israel.
