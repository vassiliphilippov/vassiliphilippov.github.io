occiput

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑk.sə.pʊt / (AmE), / ˈɒk.sɪ.pʌt / (BrE)
the back part of the head or skull
back part of the head or skull
medical

Examples

He received a blow to the occiput during the accident.
The surgeon made an incision near the base of the occiput.
Babies sometimes develop a flat spot on their occiput from lying on their backs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nape - the back of the neck

Etymology

From Latin *occiput*, from *ob-* (against) + *caput* (head).
