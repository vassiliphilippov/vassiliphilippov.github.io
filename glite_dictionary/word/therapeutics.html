therapeutics

1. NOUN

/ ˌθɛrəˈpjutɪks / (AmE), / ˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪks / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the treatment and cure of diseases
treatment of diseases in medicine
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in therapeutics during her medical studies.
Modern therapeutics combines medication, surgery, and other methods to improve patient health.
Advancements in therapeutics have dramatically increased survival rates for many diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: therapy - treatment intended to relieve or heal a disorder

Etymology

From Greek therapeuein (to take care of, treat).
