No.

1. NOUN

/ noʊ / (AmE), / nəʊ / (BrE)
An abbreviation for 'number,' used to indicate a particular position in a series or sequence.
abbreviation for 'number', used in sequences

Examples

Please write your apartment No. here on the form.
The winning ticket was No. 245.
My office is on the third floor, No. 17.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: number - A numeral or symbol indicating quantity or sequence

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'numero', ablative of 'numerus' meaning 'number'.
