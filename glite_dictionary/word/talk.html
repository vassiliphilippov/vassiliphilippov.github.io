talk

1. NOUN

/ tɔk / (AmE), / tɔːk / (BrE)
an act of speaking or discussion, especially on a particular topic; conversation or discourse
conversation or discussion

Examples

There was a lot of talk about the new regulations at the meeting.
His latest novel sparked considerable talk among critics and readers alike.
The community gathered for a talk on environmental issues.

Synset Examples

his poetry contains much talk about love and anger

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discussion - the act of talking about something with another person or a group
  • Synonym: conversation - an informal exchange of ideas by spoken words

Etymology

From Middle English 'talken', probably a back-formation from 'talking'.

Source: WordNet
