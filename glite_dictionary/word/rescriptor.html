Rescriptor

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈskrɪptər / (AmE), / rɪˈskrɪptə / (BrE)
A proprietary name for delavirdine, a non-nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitor used to treat HIV and AIDS.
anti-HIV drug (delavirdine)
medical american

Examples

The doctor prescribed Rescriptor as part of his antiretroviral therapy.
Patients taking Rescriptor should follow the dosage instructions carefully.
Rescriptor is often combined with other medications to treat HIV infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delavirdine - a non-nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitor used as an antiretroviral drug
