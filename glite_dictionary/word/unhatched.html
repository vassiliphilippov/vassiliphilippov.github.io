unhatched

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhætʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈhætʃt / (BrE)
(of an egg) not yet having produced a young animal; not yet emerged from the egg.
not yet emerged from an egg

Examples

The museum displayed several unhatched dinosaur eggs in a glass case.
Scientists monitored the temperature of the unhatched eggs carefully to ensure proper development.
She was fascinated by the tiny heartbeat visible in the unhatched chick's egg.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hatched - having emerged from an egg

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'hatched'.
