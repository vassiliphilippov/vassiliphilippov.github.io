rolypoliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊliˌpoʊlinəs / (AmE), / ˈrəʊliˌpəʊlinɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being plump and round in shape, especially in a pleasing or endearing way
the quality of being plump and round

Examples

Her rolypoliness made her the most huggable child in the family.
The artist captured the kitten's rolypoliness with soft, curving lines.
His rolypoliness didn’t stop him from running faster than anyone else on the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plumpness - the quality of being plump or full-bodied

Etymology

From 'roly-poly' (plump and round) + '-ness' (forming nouns of quality or state).
