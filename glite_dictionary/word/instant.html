instant

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.stənt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.stənt / (BrE)
a very brief moment; a particular point in time
a particular point in time

Examples

The bell rang at the exact instant she stepped outside.
He realized in that instant that he had made a mistake.
An accident can happen in an instant if you're not careful.

Synset Examples

the moment he arrived the party began

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moment - a very short period of time
  • Degree Lower: second - a very short period of time; 1/60th of a minute

Etymology

From Latin 'instans, instantis' (present, urgent), from 'instare' (to be present).
