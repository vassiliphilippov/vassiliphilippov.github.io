sunstruck

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌnˌstrʌk / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌstrʌk / (BrE)
Illuminated by sunlight; bathed in or bright with sunlight.
illuminated by sunlight

Examples

The hiker paused to admire the sunstruck peaks in the distance.
Her hair gleamed sunstruck as she stood in the open doorway.
The painting captures a sunstruck landscape filled with vibrant colors.

Synset Examples

the sunlit slopes of the canyon
violet valleys and the sunstruck ridges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunlit - illuminated by sunlight

Etymology

Formed from 'sun' and 'struck', indicating something struck or touched by the sun's light.
