Schweiz

1. PROPN

/ ʃvaɪts / (AmE), / ʃvaɪts / (BrE)
The German name for Switzerland, a landlocked federal republic in central Europe.
German name for Switzerland

Examples

He was born in Schweiz and later moved to the United States.
Many tourists visit Schweiz for its stunning Alpine scenery.
The official languages of Schweiz include German, French, Italian, and Romansh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Switzerland - a landlocked country in central Europe

Etymology

German 'Schweiz', from the canton of Schwyz, one of the founding members of the Swiss Confederation.
