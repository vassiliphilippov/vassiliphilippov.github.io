Mark

1. PROPN

/ mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
the second and shortest of the four Gospels in the New Testament, recounting the life and teachings of Jesus Christ
the shortest Gospel in the New Testament

Examples

Many scholars believe that Mark was the earliest Gospel written.
We studied the parables in Mark for our theology class.
The Gospel of Mark emphasizes the actions and miracles of Jesus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Matthew - the first Gospel in the New Testament
  • Synonym: Luke - the third Gospel in the New Testament
  • Synonym: John - the fourth Gospel in the New Testament
