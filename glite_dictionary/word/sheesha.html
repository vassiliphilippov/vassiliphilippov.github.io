sheesha

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃiːʃə / (AmE), / ˈʃiːʃə / (BrE)
an oriental water pipe for smoking tobacco, having a long flexible tube connected to a container of water through which the smoke is cooled before being inhaled
water pipe for smoking tobacco

Examples

He enjoyed smoking sheesha at the outdoor café every evening with his friends.
Many tourists visit lounges in Istanbul to try traditional sheesha flavors.
The aroma of apple and mint sheesha filled the air at the party.

Synset Examples

a bipolar world with the hookah and Turkish coffee versus hamburgers and Coca Cola

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hookah - a water pipe for smoking tobacco, especially in Middle Eastern cultures

Etymology

From Arabic 'shīsha' (glass, bottle), via Urdu/Hindi 'śīśā' (glass, glass bottle).
