spelldown

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛlˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈspɛl.daʊn / (BrE)
A contest in which participants are eliminated when they fail to spell a word correctly; a spelling competition using an elimination format.
spelling elimination contest

Examples

Our school will host a spelldown next week to determine the best speller in each grade.
She was eliminated in the second round of the spelldown when she missed a tricky word.
The annual spelldown attracted students from all over the district.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spelling bee - a competition in which children try to spell words correctly

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'countdown' and 'spelling bee', combining 'spell' and 'down' (elimination).
