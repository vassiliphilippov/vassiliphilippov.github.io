counterpane

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊntərˌpeɪn / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌpeɪn / (BrE)
a decorative cover for a bed, especially a quilt or bedspread
decorative bed cover

Examples

The handmade counterpane brought a touch of elegance to the guest room.
She tucked the sheets neatly under the counterpane before leaving for work.
A patchwork counterpane covered the old wooden bed in the cottage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedspread - a decorative cloth placed over a bed for warmth or decoration
  • Synonym: quilt - a warm bed covering made of padding enclosed between layers of fabric, stitched together

Etymology

From Middle English 'countrepointe', from Old French 'cointrepoint', from Medieval Latin 'contra punctum' (stitched quilt, lit. 'stitched against').
