rapturous

1. ADJ

/ ˈræp.tʃɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˈrap.tjʊə.rəs / (BrE)
Experiencing or expressing great joy, delight, or enthusiasm.
feeling or showing great joy or delight

Examples

The audience broke into rapturous applause after the inspiring performance.
She gave him a rapturous welcome when he returned home from his trip.
They listened in rapturous silence as the orchestra played the final movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstatic - feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness
  • Synonym: delighted - greatly pleased
  • Antonym: miserable - feeling very unhappy or uncomfortable

Etymology

From rapture (from Latin raptus, 'seized, carried away') + -ous.
