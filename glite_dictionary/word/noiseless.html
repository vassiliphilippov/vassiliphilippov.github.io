noiseless

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɔɪs.ləs / (AmE), / ˈnɔɪs.lɪs / (BrE)
making no sound; completely silent or without noise
making no sound

Examples

The cat walked across the room in a noiseless manner, barely disturbing the air.
She opened the door noiselessly so as not to wake the baby.
The new electric car was almost noiseless at low speed.

Synset Examples

th' inaudible and noiseless foot of time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silent - not making or accompanied by any sound
  • Synonym: quiet - making little or no noise
  • Antonym: noisy - making a lot of noise

Etymology

From 'noise' + '-less' (suffix meaning 'without').
