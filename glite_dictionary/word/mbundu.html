Mbundu

1. NOUN

/ əmˈbʊndu / (AmE), / əmˈbʊndu / (BrE)
A member of an ethnic group native to western Angola who speak Umbundu.
an ethnic group of western Angola

Examples

The Mbundu are known for their rich cultural traditions in Angola.
Anthropologists have studied the history and social structure of the Mbundu.
Many Mbundu communities continue to speak Umbundu as their primary language.
