soochong

1. NOUN

/ suːˈtʃʌŋ / (AmE), / suːˈtʃɒŋ / (BrE)
A fine quality black tea originally produced in China.
type of Chinese black tea

Examples

She ordered a cup of soochong after her meal, savoring the delicate aroma.
The shop carries several varieties of soochong, prized for its refined taste.
I prefer soochong over other black teas because of its smooth flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lapsang souchong - a type of Chinese black tea with a smoky flavor

Etymology

From Chinese souchong, meaning 'small kind' or 'subvariety', referring to a grading of tea leaves.
