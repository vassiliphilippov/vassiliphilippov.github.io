year-around

1. ADJ

/ jɪr əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / jɪə əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
operating or continuing throughout the entire year; not limited to a particular season.
throughout the entire year

Examples

This park offers year-around activities for both locals and tourists.
The community center operates year-around, providing services and programs in every season.
She has a year-around job, unlike many seasonal workers in the area.

Synset Examples

a year-round resort
a year-round job

Related Terms

  • Synonym: year-round - lasting or available during all seasons of the year
