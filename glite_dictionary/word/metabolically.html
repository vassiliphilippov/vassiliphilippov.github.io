metabolically

1. ADV

/ məˌtæbəˈlɪkli / (AmE), / mɛtəˈbɒlɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to metabolism or the chemical processes within a living organism.
in a way relating to metabolism
medical

Examples

The patient was found to be metabolically stable after the treatment.
Some species are metabolically adapted to survive in extremely cold environments.
This compound is metabolically active in the liver.

Synset Examples

metabolically important substances

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metabolic - relating to metabolism

Etymology

From 'metabolic' (from Greek 'metabolē' meaning change) + '-ally'.
