Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Blok

1. PROPN

/ əˈlɛksɑːndər ˌɑːlɛkˈsandrəvɪtʃ ˈblɒk / (AmE), / əˈlɛksəndə ˌælɪkˈsandrəvɪtʃ ˈblɒk / (BrE)
(1880–1921) Russian poet, one of the most significant figures of Russian symbolism, known for works such as 'The Twelve' and 'Verses About the Beautiful Lady'.
Russian poet (1880–1921)

Examples

Many literature courses include the poetry of Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Blok among their Russian Symbolist readings.
The influence of Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Blok can be seen in the works of later twentieth-century poets.
In his lecture, the professor quoted Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Blok to illustrate the themes of revolution in Russian poetry.
