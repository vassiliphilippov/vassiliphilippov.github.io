Portulaca

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɔr.tʃəˈleɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˌpɔː.tjʊˈlɑː.kə / (BrE)
A genus of mainly tropical, fleshy or trailing herbs, including species commonly known as purslane.
a genus of tropical fleshy herbs

Examples

The garden was filled with colorful Portulaca blooming in the summer heat.
Many species within the Portulaca genus are valued as ornamental plants.
Researchers studied the drought resistance of Portulaca in arid climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purslane - a common name for edible species in the genus Portulaca

Etymology

From Latin 'portulaca' (purslane).
