Tibet

1. PROPN

/ tɪˈbɛt / (AmE), / tɪˈbɛt / (BrE)
an autonomous region of China, situated in the Himalayas, known for its unique culture, history, and high-altitude plateau
autonomous Himalayan region of China

Examples

Many travelers dream of visiting Tibet to experience its breathtaking landscapes and spiritual culture.
After years of living in Tibet, she developed a deep respect for its traditions.
The Dalai Lama is a prominent spiritual leader from Tibet.

Etymology

From Tibetan 'Bod', via Mandarin 'Xīzàng' (西藏), anglicized as 'Tibet'.
