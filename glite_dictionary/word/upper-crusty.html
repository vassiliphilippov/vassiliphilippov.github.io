upper-crusty

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌpɚˈkrʌsti / (AmE), / ˌʌpəˈkrʌsti / (BrE)
(informal) Characteristic of or resembling the upper class, especially in a manner that is snobbish or elitist.
snobbish; upper-class mannered
informal

Examples

The upper-crusty guests looked down on the casual dress code.
His upper-crusty demeanor made him seem unapproachable.
She was criticized for her upper-crusty attitude.

Synset Examples

The upper-crusty guests looked down on the casual dress code.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aristocratic - belonging to or typical of the aristocracy
  • Synonym: snobbish - having or showing the attitude of people who think they are better than others

Etymology

Formed from 'upper crust' (meaning upper class, originally in the 19th century) + '-y' suffix.
