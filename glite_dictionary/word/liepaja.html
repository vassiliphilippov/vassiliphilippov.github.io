Liepaja

1. PROPN

/ liˈɑːpɑːjɑː / (AmE), / liˈɑːpajə / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Latvia situated on the Baltic Sea, known for its port and cultural history.
city in southwestern Latvia on the Baltic Sea

Examples

Many tourists visit Liepaja to enjoy its beautiful beaches and lively music festivals.
The port of Liepaja plays a significant role in Latvia’s economy.
During our trip to Liepaja, we explored its historic city center and seaside parks.

Etymology

From Latvian 'Liepāja', possibly derived from 'liepa' meaning 'linden tree.'
