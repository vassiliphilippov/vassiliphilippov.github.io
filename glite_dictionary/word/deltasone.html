Deltasone

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛltəˌsoʊn / (AmE), / ˈdɛltəˌsəʊn / (BrE)
a trademarked drug name for prednisone, a synthetic corticosteroid used as an anti-inflammatory and immunosuppressant, especially to treat arthritis and other inflammatory conditions
trade name for prednisone, anti-inflammatory drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Deltasone to reduce the swelling in his joints.
After starting Deltasone, she noticed her symptoms began to improve within a few days.
Long-term use of Deltasone may lead to certain side effects, so regular monitoring is important.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prednisone - the generic form of Deltasone, a synthetic corticosteroid drug
