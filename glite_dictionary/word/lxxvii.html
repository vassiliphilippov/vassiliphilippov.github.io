LXXVII

1. NUM

/ ɛl sɛks sɛvən ˈsɛvəti ˈsɛvən / (AmE), / ɛl sɛks ˈsɛvən ˈsɛv(ə)nti ˈsɛv(ə)n / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number seventy-seven (77)
Roman numeral for 77

Examples

The ancient manuscript was dated LXXVII in the Roman calendar system.
Her favorite chapter in the epic poem is numbered LXXVII.
There were exactly LXXVII contestants in the first round.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seventy-seven - the number 77

Etymology

From Latin: L = 50, X = 10, V = 5, II = 2, totaling 77.
