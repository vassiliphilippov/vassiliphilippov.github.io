Hyaena brunnea

1. NOUN

/ haɪˈiːnə ˈbruːniə / (AmE), / haɪˈiːnə ˈbruːniːə / (BrE)
A species of hyena found in southern Africa, also known as the brown hyena, characterized by its shaggy coat and nocturnal habits.
brown hyena of southern Africa

Examples

The Hyaena brunnea is mainly found in the arid regions of southern Africa.
Researchers recently tracked a Hyaena brunnea pack to study its nocturnal behavior.
Unlike other hyenas, the Hyaena brunnea has a shaggy, dark-brown coat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brown hyena - Common name for Hyaena brunnea

Etymology

From Latin; 'Hyaena' from Greek 'hyaina' (female pig; hyena), and 'brunnea' meaning 'brown' in Latin.
