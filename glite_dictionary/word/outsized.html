outsized

1. ADJ

/ ˈaʊt.saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.saɪzd / (BrE)
unusually large in size, larger than what is considered typical for its kind
exceptionally large; larger than usual

Examples

The actress arrived in an outsized hat that drew everyone's attention.
He lives in an outsized mansion on the edge of town.
The company delivered an outsized cheque to the winner for publicity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oversized - of a size larger than is necessary or usual
  • Antonym: undersized - smaller than normal for its kind

Etymology

From 'out-' (outside) + 'sized', formed in English to mean outside the normal size.
