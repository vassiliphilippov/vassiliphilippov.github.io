faceless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfeɪsləs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪslɪs / (BrE)
lacking distinguishing features or identity; anonymous or unremarkable.
lacking identity or distinguishing features

Examples

The new office building seemed faceless compared to the historic structures nearby.
The committee was criticized for being run by a group of faceless officials.
She felt lost in the faceless crowd of the big city.

Synset Examples

a faceless apparition
the faceless accusers of the police state

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anonymous - without any name acknowledged, as if unknown or unidentifiable
  • Antonym: distinguished - having an identity or notable features; easily recognizable
