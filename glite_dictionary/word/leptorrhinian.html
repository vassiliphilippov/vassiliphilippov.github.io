leptorrhinian

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɛp.təˈrɪni.ən / (AmE), / ˌlɛp.təˈrɪn.i.ən / (BrE)
having a long, narrow nose; relating to or characterized by a slender nasal shape.
having a long, narrow nose

Examples

The ancient statue depicts a leptorrhinian profile with delicate features.
Scientists noted the leptorrhinian shape of the fossilized skull.
Among the population, leptorrhinian characteristics are relatively rare.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: platyrhinian - having a broad, flat nose

Etymology

From Greek leptos (slender, thin) + rhis, rhin- (nose) + -ian.
