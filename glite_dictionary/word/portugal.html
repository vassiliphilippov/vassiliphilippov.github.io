Portugal

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɔrtʃəɡəl / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtʃʊɡəl / (BrE)
a country in southwestern Europe on the Iberian Peninsula, known for its rich maritime history and former global empire
country in southwestern Europe

Examples

I spent my summer vacation exploring the historic cities of Portugal.
Many of the world's famous explorers, such as Vasco da Gama, came from Portugal.
Portugal is renowned for its delicious cuisine and beautiful Atlantic coastline.

Etymology

From Latin 'Portus Cale', an ancient settlement in the area; the name evolved into 'Portugal'.
