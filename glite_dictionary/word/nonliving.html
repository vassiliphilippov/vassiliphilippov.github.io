nonliving

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
Not alive; not endowed with life or living characteristics.
not alive; inanimate

Examples

The museum display includes both nonliving minerals and fossilized plants.
Water and sunlight are nonliving components of an ecosystem.
Scientists study both living and nonliving things to understand how nature works.

Synset Examples

the inorganic world is inanimate
inanimate objects

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inanimate - not alive, especially not in the manner of animals and humans
  • Antonym: living - having life; alive

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'non-' (not) to 'living'.
