Kwangchow

1. PROPN

/ kwɑŋˈtʃoʊ / (AmE), / kwæŋˈtʃaʊ / (BrE)
an older spelling of Guangzhou, a major city and port in southern China; the capital of Guangdong province
older name for Guangzhou, major city in China

Examples

Many exporters in Kwangchow take advantage of its busy deep-water port.
The architecture of Kwangchow reflects both ancient traditions and modern influences.
Older maps of China often use the name Kwangchow instead of Guangzhou.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Guangzhou - major city and port in southern China; capital of Guangdong province

Etymology

Wade-Giles romanization of Mandarin 廣州 (Guǎngzhōu)
