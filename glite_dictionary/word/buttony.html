buttony

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌt̬əni / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəni / (BrE)
decorated or ornamented with many buttons
decorated with many buttons

Examples

She wore a buttony jacket that drew everyone's attention.
The designer's new collection features buttony dresses and coats.
His buttony shirt reminded me of styles from a bygone era.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buttoned - having buttons, especially fastened

2. ADJ

/ ˈbʌt̬ni / (AmE), / ˈbʌtni / (BrE)
small, round, and shiny like a button or bead
small, round, and shiny, like a button

Examples

The doll had buttony black eyes that seemed to follow me around the room.
His puppy's buttony nose was cold and wet when it sniffed my hand.
She admired the bird's buttony eyes, which sparkled in the sunlight.

Synset Examples

bright beady eyes
black buttony eyes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beady - small, round, and glittering (like a bead)

Etymology

From 'button' + '-y', forming an adjective meaning 'like a button'.
