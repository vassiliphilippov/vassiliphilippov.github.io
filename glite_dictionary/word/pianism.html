pianism

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpɪənɪzəm / (BrE)
the technique or style of playing the piano, especially as displayed by a particular pianist
piano playing technique or style

Examples

Her pianism is admired for its subtlety and virtuosity.
Critics praised the composer's unique approach to pianism.
The concert showcased different styles of pianism from around the world.

Synset Examples

a program of pianism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piano playing - the act of playing the piano

Etymology

From French pianisme, from 'piano' + '-ism' denoting a practice or style.
