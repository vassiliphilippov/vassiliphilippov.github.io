zestfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɛst.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈzɛst.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
a quality of being full of enthusiasm and energy; vigorous enjoyment or eagerness
enthusiastic enjoyment; energy

Examples

Her zestfulness for life inspired everyone around her to be more positive.
The children's zestfulness made the event lively and unforgettable.
Even in her old age, she maintains a remarkable zestfulness that people admire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiasm - intense and eager enjoyment or interest
  • Antonym: apathy - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern

Etymology

From 'zest' (great enthusiasm or energy) + '-fulness' (state or quality).
