NSF

1. NOUN

/ ɛn ɛs ɛf / (AmE), / ɛn ɛs ɛf / (BrE)
(in the US) an independent federal agency that supports research and education in science and engineering.
US science funding agency (National Science Foundation)
american

Examples

After winning a grant from the NSF, her laboratory expanded its research projects on renewable energy.
The NSF plays a crucial role in advancing scientific knowledge in the United States.
Applicants must submit their proposals to the NSF by the end of March.

Etymology

Abbreviation of National Science Foundation, established 1950.
