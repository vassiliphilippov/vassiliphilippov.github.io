sugarless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʊɡɚləs / (AmE), / ˈsʊɡələs / (BrE)
(of food or drink) not containing any sugar.
not containing sugar

Examples

I've switched to sugarless gum to protect my teeth.
Their sugarless cookies are popular among people with dietary restrictions.
She always prefers sugarless tea, even in the morning.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sugary - containing or tasting of sugar
  • Synonym: sugar-free - not containing sugar
