Life Saver

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪf ˌseɪvər / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf ˌseɪvə / (BrE)
A brand of ring-shaped hard candy with a hole in the center, resembling a life preserver.
ring-shaped hard candy
american

Examples

She offered me a cherry Life Saver from her purse.
The kids love to eat Life Savers during road trips.
I haven't had a Life Saver since I was in school.

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to a life preserver (life saver) ring; introduced as a brand in the United States in 1912.
