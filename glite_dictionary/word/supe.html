Supe

1. NOUN

/ suːp / (AmE), / suːp / (BrE)
A member of a group of superheroes, especially in fictional works.
member of a group of superheroes
informal

Examples

He always admired the Supes for their courage and strength.
Rumors spread quickly whenever one of the Supes appeared in public.
In the series, being a Supe comes with both great power and responsibility.

Synset Examples

He always dreamed of being one of the Supes in the league.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superhero - a fictional character with superhuman powers

Etymology

Shortened form of 'superhero.'
