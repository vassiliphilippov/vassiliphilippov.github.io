Little Horn

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪɾl hɔrn / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl hɔːn / (BrE)
a river flowing from northern Wyoming into the Bighorn River in southern Montana, historically noted as the site of Custer's Last Stand in 1876.
river in Wyoming and Montana, historic site

Examples

The Little Horn River is famous for its connection to the Battle of the Little Bighorn.
We visited the Little Horn during our tour of Montana's historic sites.
The troops followed the Little Horn to reach the battlefield.
