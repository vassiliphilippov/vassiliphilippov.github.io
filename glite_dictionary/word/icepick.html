icepick

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪsˌpɪk / (AmE), / ˈaɪs.pɪk / (BrE)
a tool with a sharp pointed steel rod used for breaking up blocks of ice
tool for breaking ice blocks

Examples

He used an icepick to break the frozen block in the cooler.
The bartender skillfully chipped cubes from the ice with an icepick.
She accidentally dropped the icepick, and it clattered loudly on the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pickaxe - a large heavy tool used for breaking hard surfaces
