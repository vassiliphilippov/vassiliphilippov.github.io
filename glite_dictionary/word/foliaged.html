foliaged

1. ADJ

/ ˈfoʊ.li.ɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.li.ɪdʒd / (BrE)
Bearing or having numerous leaves.
having many leaves

Examples

The foliaged branches created dense shade in the garden.
Every spring, the hills become lush and foliaged overnight.
Artists love painting the richly foliaged trees of the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leafy - having many leaves

Etymology

From 'foliage' (from Latin folium 'leaf') + -ed suffix forming adjectives.
