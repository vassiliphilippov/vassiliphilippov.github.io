isotopic

1. ADJ

/ ˌaɪsəˈtoʊpɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈtɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or denoting isotopes or their relations.
relating to isotopes

Examples

The isotopic composition of water can provide valuable information about climate history.
Recent studies have used isotopic analysis to trace the origins of ancient artifacts.
Scientists measured the isotopic ratios in the soil samples to better understand the environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: isotope - each of two or more forms of the same element with equal protons but different neutrons

Etymology

From isotope + -ic, where 'isotope' is from Greek isos (equal) + topos (place).
