gustative

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡʌsteɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈɡʌsteɪtɪv / (BrE)
relating to or connected with the sense of taste
relating to the sense of taste
medical

Examples

The tongue has specialized gustative cells that detect different tastes.
This medicine can temporarily alter your gustative function, making foods taste unusual.
Researchers measured the gustative response of participants to various flavors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gustatory - relating to the sense of taste

Etymology

From French gustatif, from Latin gustatus (sense of taste).
