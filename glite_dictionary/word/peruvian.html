Peruvian

1. ADJ

/ pəˈruːviən / (AmE), / pəˈruːviən / (BrE)
of or relating to Peru or its people
from or related to Peru

Examples

The museum displayed a rare collection of Peruvian textiles dating back centuries.
She prepared a traditional Peruvian dish for the cultural festival.
The Peruvian artwork reflects ancient indigenous influences.

Synset Examples

Peruvian artifacts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Peru - a country in western South America

Etymology

From Peru + -ian, denoting belonging or relating to.

WordNet ID: oewn-02977699-a

2. NOUN

/ pəˈruːviən / (AmE), / pəˈruːviən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Peru.
a person from Peru

Examples

The Peruvian welcomed us warmly to his hometown in the Andes.
Many Peruvians celebrate Inti Raymi, the festival of the sun.
She became friends with a Peruvian during her travels in South America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Peru - a country in western South America

Etymology

From Peru + -ian, denoting nationality or association.

Synset POS: n
