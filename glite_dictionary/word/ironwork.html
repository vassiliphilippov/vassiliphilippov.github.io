ironwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.ɚnˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.ənˌwɜːk / (BrE)
objects or architectural features made of iron, such as railings, gratings, or decorative fittings
objects or features made of iron

Examples

The old gate was adorned with elaborate ironwork that had been restored to its former glory.
Skilled blacksmiths contributed beautiful ironwork to the renovation of the building.
Much of the historic ironwork in the city center dates back to the Victorian era.

Synset Examples

the houses had much ornamental ironwork

Etymology

From 'iron' + 'work', referring to crafted objects made of iron.
