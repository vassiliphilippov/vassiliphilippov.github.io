altruistic

1. ADJ

/ ælˈtruːɪstɪk / (AmE), / alˈtruːɪstɪk / (BrE)
showing a selfless concern for the well-being of others; unselfish.
showing selfless concern for others

Examples

Her altruistic actions inspired others to volunteer at the homeless shelter.
He is known for his altruistic nature and willingness to help anyone in need.
The altruistic doctor devoted her life to treating patients in remote villages.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: selfish - lacking consideration for others; concerned chiefly with one's own personal profit or pleasure
  • Synonym: generous - willing to give more of something than is strictly necessary or expected

Etymology

From French 'altruiste', from Italian 'altrui' (somebody else), from Latin 'alter' (other).
