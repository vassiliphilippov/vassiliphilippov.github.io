riblike

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪbˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɪb.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the shape or structure of a rib
resembling a rib

Examples

The fossil showed a series of riblike structures along its side.
Some cacti have riblike ridges running from top to bottom.
The architect designed riblike supports for the new building.
