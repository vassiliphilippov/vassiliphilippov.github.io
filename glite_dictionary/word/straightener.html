straightener

1. NOUN

/ ˈstreɪtnər / (AmE), / ˈstreɪtənə / (BrE)
a device or tool that is used to make something straight, especially for straightening hair or other materials
device for making things straight

Examples

She uses a straightener every morning to style her hair before work.
This new straightener heats up quickly and gives a smooth finish.
He borrowed his sister's straightener to prepare for the formal event.

Synset Examples

a hair straightener

Related Terms

  • Antonym: curler - a device for curling hair

Etymology

From 'straighten' (to make straight) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
