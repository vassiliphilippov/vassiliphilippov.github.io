measurer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛʒərər / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒ(ə)rə / (BrE)
a person who measures something, especially as part of their job or a particular task
a person who measures

Examples

The measurer carefully recorded the length of each sample in his notebook.
As an official measurer at the competition, she ensured every piece was exactly the right size.
Their project required a skilled measurer to assess the dimensions accurately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: observer - a person who watches or notices something
  • Synonym: assessor - a person who evaluates or estimates the nature, ability, or quality of something

Etymology

From measure + -er, denoting one who performs an action.
