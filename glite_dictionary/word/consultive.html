consultive

1. ADJ

/ kənˈsʌltɪv / (AmE), / kənˈsʌltɪv / (BrE)
giving advice or having the function of advising; advisory
giving advice; advisory

Examples

The committee serves a purely consultive role in university decisions.
She was appointed to a consultive position on the board.
The organization's consultive council meets quarterly to advise the management team.

Synset Examples

an advisory memorandum
his function was purely consultative

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advisory - having the power or function of giving advice
  • Synonym: consultative - having the function of giving advice; advisory

Etymology

From 'consult' + '-ive', patterned after 'consultative'.
