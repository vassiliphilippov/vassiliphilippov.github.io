unknown

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈnoʊn / (AmE), / ʌnˈnəʊn / (BrE)
A variable in mathematics whose value is not known and must be found as a solution to an equation.
math variable to solve for

Examples

In this equation, the unknown is represented by the letter x.
Solving for the unknown is the main goal in algebraic problems.
Let us assign a value to each unknown to check if the equation is satisfied.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: variable - a symbol that represents a quantity in mathematics
