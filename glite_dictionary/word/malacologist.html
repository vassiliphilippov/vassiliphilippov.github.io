malacologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæl.əˈkɑː.lə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌmæl.əˈkɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
a zoologist who specializes in the study of mollusks
zoologist specializing in mollusks
medical

Examples

The museum hired a malacologist to curate its mollusk collection.
As a malacologist, Dr. Kim has discovered several new species of snails.
Our team consulted a malacologist for advice on preserving freshwater mussels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoologist - a scientist who studies animals

Etymology

From malacology + -ist; malacology from Greek 'malakos' (soft).
