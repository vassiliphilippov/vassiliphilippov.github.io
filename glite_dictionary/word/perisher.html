perisher

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛrɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈpɛrɪʃə / (BrE)
(informal, British) A mischievous or troublesome person, especially a child; sometimes used to mean a disagreeable person.
mischievous or troublesome person
informal british

Examples

That little perisher broke my favorite vase again.
He's a lovable perisher who always finds himself in trouble at school.
You noisy perisher, stop running down the hallway!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bounder - an objectionable or ill-mannered person
