fiddling

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪd.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪd.lɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal) Small and of little importance; trivial.
of little importance; trivial
informal

Examples

She spent hours arguing over a fiddling detail that didn't really matter.
The amount he was owed was so fiddling that he didn't bother to collect it.
Arguing about fiddling sums of money wastes everyone's time.

Synset Examples

a fiddling sum of money

a little (or small) matter
a dispute over niggling details
limited to petty enterprises
piffling efforts
giving a police officer a free meal may be against the law, but it seems to be a picayune infraction

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petty - of little importance; trivial
  • Synonym: trivial - of little value or importance
  • Antonym: significant - important; likely to have an effect
