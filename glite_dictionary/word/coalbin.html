coalbin

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊlˌbɪn / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlˌbɪn / (BrE)
a bin or container used for storing coal, especially for domestic use
container for storing coal

Examples

He lifted the lid of the coalbin to check how much coal was left for the stove.
In the old house, the coalbin was kept in the basement near the furnace.
After the delivery, the coalbin was full and ready for the coming winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coal box - a box for holding coal
