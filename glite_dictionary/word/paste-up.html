paste-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪstˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪstˌʌp / (BrE)
a layout in which text and images are physically arranged and affixed to a backing, traditionally used in preparing material for printing or publication
physical layout of text and images for printing

Examples

The designer created a detailed paste-up for the magazine cover before it went to print.
They reviewed the paste-up to ensure all the captions and images were in the correct place.
Old publishing offices often had walls covered in paste-ups for proofreading and layout approval.

Synset Examples

they showed him a paste-up of the book jacket

Related Terms

  • Synonym: layout - the way in which text and pictures are arranged, especially on a printed page

Etymology

From 'paste' + 'up', referring to the action of pasting elements onto a board.
