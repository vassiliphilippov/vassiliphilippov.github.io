numerousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːmərəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈnjuːmərəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being numerous; a large quantity or number
the state of being numerous

Examples

The numerousness of species in the rainforest is astonishing.
She was overwhelmed by the numerousness of her student assignments this week.
The numerousness of stars visible on a clear night inspires awe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multitude - a very large number of people or things
  • Antonym: fewness - the quality of being few in number

Etymology

Formed from 'numerous' + '-ness', patterned after 'numerous' from Latin 'numerosus' (numerous).
