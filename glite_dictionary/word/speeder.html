speeder

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːdər / (AmE), / ˈspiːdə / (BrE)
a person who drives faster than the allowed speed limit
driver exceeding speed limit

Examples

The police pulled over the speeder for going 30 miles over the limit.
Many speeders are caught by traffic cameras near school zones.
As a known speeder, he received several tickets in one month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speedster - a person who drives fast, often exceeding speed limits
  • Antonym: speed limit - the maximum legal speed for vehicles on a road

Etymology

From 'speed' + agentive suffix '-er'.
