L-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛl ˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈɛl ˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape or form of the letter L, typically with two straight segments joined at a right angle.
in the shape of the letter L

Examples

The new office has an L-shaped desk that fits perfectly in the corner.
They bought an L-shaped sofa for the living room to maximize space.
The kitchen was remodeled with an L-shaped counter for better efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: U-shaped - having the shape of the letter U

Etymology

Formed from the letter 'L' plus 'shaped'.
