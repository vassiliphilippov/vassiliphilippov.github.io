denturist

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛn.tʃə.rɪst / (AmE), / ˈdɛn.tjʊ.rɪst / (BrE)
a person who is trained and licensed to make and fit dentures and other dental appliances
maker of dentures and dental appliances
medical

Examples

After losing her teeth, she visited a denturist to be fitted for new dentures.
The denturist crafted a bridge that matched the patient's natural teeth perfectly.
He trained as a denturist after completing his studies in dental technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prosthodontist - a dentist who specializes in making artificial replacements for teeth

Etymology

From 'denture' (from French, meaning 'set of teeth') + '-ist' (denoting a person).
