goofball

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːfˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈɡuːfbɔːl / (BrE)
a silly or ridiculous person, especially one who amuses others through absurd behavior
a silly or ridiculous person
informal american

Examples

Stop being such a goofball and pay attention to the instructions.
My brother is a total goofball who always makes everyone laugh at parties.
She acted like a goofball to cheer her friends up after a tough day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clown - a person who amuses others by ridiculous behavior
  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently

Etymology

From 'goof' (fool) + 'ball', originally US slang, mid-20th century.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡufˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈɡuːfbɔːl / (BrE)
a silly or foolish person, often acting in a comical or incompetent way
a silly or foolish person
informal

Examples

My brother can be such a goofball when he's trying to make everyone laugh.
Stop acting like a goofball and take this seriously for a minute.
She loves being around goofballs who keep the mood light and fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • Synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or lacking good judgment
  • Antonym: genius - a person of extraordinary intelligence

Etymology

From "goof" (slang for 'foolish person') + "ball" (term of endearment in slang compounds), U.S. slang, mid-20th century.

3. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːfˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈɡuːfˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a silly or ridiculous way, often to make others laugh
a silly or ridiculous person
informal

Examples

Everyone knows Tom is the office goofball, always pulling funny faces during meetings.
My little sister can be such a goofball when she dances around the kitchen.
Despite being a brilliant scientist, he's known as a goofball at parties.

Synset Examples

He's such a goofball, always making everyone laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clown - a person who amuses others by ridiculous behavior
  • Synonym: joker - a person fond of joking or behaving in a playful way

Probably from 'goof' (fool, clown) + 'ball' (used in slang for eccentric people).
