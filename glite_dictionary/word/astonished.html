astonished

1. ADJ

/ əˈstɑːnɪʃt / (AmE), / əˈstɒnɪʃt / (BrE)
very surprised or amazed by something unexpected or difficult to believe.
very surprised; amazed

Examples

She was astonished to hear that she had won the first prize.
The children looked astonished when they saw the birthday cake appear from nowhere.
I was astonished by how quickly the weather changed in the mountains.

Synset Examples

an amazed audience gave the magician a standing ovation
I stood enthralled, astonished by the vastness and majesty of the cathedral
astounded viewers wept at the pictures from the Oklahoma City bombing
stood in stunned silence
stunned scientists found not one but at least three viruses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amazed - very surprised or filled with wonder
  • Synonym: astounded - greatly surprised or shocked
  • Synonym: stupefied - so surprised or shocked that you cannot think or feel properly

Etymology

From the verb 'astonish', which comes from Old French 'estoner', from Latin 'extonare' (to thunder out).
