pensively

1. ADV

/ ˈpɛnsɪvli / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows deep or serious thought, often with a hint of sadness.
in a thoughtful or reflective manner

Examples

She gazed pensively out the window, lost in memories of her childhood.
He sat pensively at his desk, considering the question for several minutes before answering.
The writer paused pensively, searching for the right words to describe the scene.

Synset Examples

pensively he stared at the painting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtfully - in a thoughtful manner
  • Antonym: cheerfully - in a cheerful manner

Etymology

From 'pensive' (from Old French *pensif*, from Latin *pensare* 'to weigh, ponder') + '-ly'.
