certificated

1. ADJ

/ sərˈtɪfɪˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / səˈtɪfɪkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
officially recognized or authorized by a certificate; having received a certificate, especially to perform a professional duty.
officially authorized by certificate

Examples

All flight instructors must be certificated by the aviation authority.
She is a certificated teacher with years of classroom experience.
Only certificated electricians are allowed to install this type of wiring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certified - having official certification or authorization

Etymology

From certificate + -ed; modeled after similar past participle adjectives.
