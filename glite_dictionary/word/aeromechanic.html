aeromechanic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛroʊməˈkænɪk / (AmE), / ˌeərəʊmɪˈkænɪk / (BrE)
relating to aerodynamics or the mechanical aspects of air and gases, especially as they relate to objects moving through them
relating to aerodynamics or mechanics of air

Examples

The aeromechanic properties of the new aircraft were rigorously tested in a wind tunnel.
Engineers examined the aeromechanic features to improve fuel efficiency.
Her research focuses on aeromechanic principles in the design of drones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aerodynamic - relating to the properties of moving air and solid bodies in the air

Etymology

From aero- (air) + mechanic (relating to machines or mechanics).
