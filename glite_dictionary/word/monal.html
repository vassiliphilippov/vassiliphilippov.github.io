monal

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊ.nəl / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.nəl / (BrE)
a brilliantly colored pheasant native to the mountainous forests of southern Asia, especially the Himalayas
a brilliantly colored Asian pheasant

Examples

The Himalayan monal is renowned for its dazzling plumage and iridescent colors.
Birdwatchers traveled far to catch a glimpse of the elusive monal in its natural habitat.
In Nepal, the monal holds cultural significance and appears on local currency.

Etymology

From Nepali 'munaal', referring to the Himalayan monal.
