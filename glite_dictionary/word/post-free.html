post-free

1. ADJ

/ ˌpoʊstˈfri / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈfriː / (BrE)
(of mail) sent with the postage paid by the sender, so that the recipient does not have to pay for delivery.
with postage paid by sender

Examples

All items will be sent post-free within the country.
If you reply before the deadline, we will ship your order post-free.
Books ordered from this catalog are delivered post-free to your address.

Synset Examples

I will send it post-paid

Related Terms

  • Synonym: post-paid - with postage paid by the sender

Etymology

From 'post' (mail) + 'free' (without cost); used since the 19th century for postage arrangements.
