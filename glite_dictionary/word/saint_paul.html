Saint Paul

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt pɔl / (AmE), / seɪnt pɔːl / (BrE)
the capital city of Minnesota, located in the southeastern part of the state on the Mississippi River, adjacent to Minneapolis; one of the Twin Cities.
capital city of Minnesota, USA

Examples

After moving to Saint Paul, she quickly adapted to life in the Twin Cities.
The weather in Saint Paul can be very cold during the winter months.
Many historic buildings are preserved in downtown Saint Paul.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Minneapolis - largest city in Minnesota and the other Twin City
  • Synonym: Twin Cities - the metropolitan area formed by Minneapolis and Saint Paul

Etymology

Named after Paul the Apostle; from French 'Saint Paul'.
