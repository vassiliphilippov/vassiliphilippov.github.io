lumma

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːmə / (AmE), / ˈlʌmə / (BrE)
A former monetary unit in Armenia, equal to one hundredth of a dram.
old Armenian monetary unit

Examples

The price was listed as 250 lummas in the old Armenian market.
In historical records, a lumma was a tiny fraction of the dram.
Collectors sometimes seek out coins denominated in lummas from Armenia's past.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: dram - the basic monetary unit of Armenia
