washables

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑʃəbəlz / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃəbəlz / (BrE)
garments or household items that can be washed, especially by machine laundering
items that can be washed by laundering

Examples

All the washables are sorted into separate piles before doing the laundry.
Please check the label to see if the item belongs with the washables.
Our hotel offers laundry service for your washables during your stay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicates - fragile garments requiring gentle washing
