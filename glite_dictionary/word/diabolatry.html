diabolatry

1. NOUN

/ daɪˈæbələtri / (AmE), / daɪˈæbələtri / (BrE)
the worship of devils or Satan, often involving specific rites or acts
worship of devils

Examples

The cult was accused of diabolatry and other occult practices.
Legends of medieval diabolatry have inspired many works of fiction.
Authorities investigated the ritual site after rumors of diabolatry spread through the town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idolatry - the worship of idols
  • Synonym: demonolatry - the worship of demons

Etymology

From Greek 'diabolos' (devil) + '-latry' (worship).
