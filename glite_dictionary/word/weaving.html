weaving

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of making fabric by interlacing threads or yarns.
making fabric by interlacing threads

Examples

Traditional weaving techniques have been passed down through generations in many cultures.
The museum features a display of ancient weaving tools and fabrics.
She took up weaving as a hobby, creating colorful scarves and tapestries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knitting - the craft of forming fabric by interlacing yarn with needles

Etymology

From Old English *wefan* ('to weave').
