goldamned

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡoʊlˌdæmd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊlˌdamd / (BrE)
(informal) Used to express annoyance or emphasis, often as a milder substitute for 'goddamned'.
expressing annoyance; mild euphemism for 'goddamned'
informal

Examples

I can't believe that goldamned printer jammed again right before my deadline.
She tore up the goldamned check in frustration.
Why does the goldamned traffic always have to be so bad on Mondays?

Synset Examples

That goldamned machine broke down again!
He cursed the goldamned weather during the storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goddamned - used for emphasis or to express anger (vulgar)

Etymology

Formed as a euphemistic alteration of 'goddamned', substituting 'gold' for 'god' to avoid profanity.
