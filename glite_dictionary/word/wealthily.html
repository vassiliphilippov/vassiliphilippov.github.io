wealthily

1. ADV

/ ˈwɛlθɪli / (AmE), / ˈwɛlθɪli / (BrE)
In a wealthy or rich manner; with great wealth.
in a wealthy manner

Examples

The couple lived wealthily in a mansion overlooking the city.
She dressed wealthily for the gala, wearing diamonds and designer gowns.
The estate was wealthily decorated with fine art and luxurious furnishings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: richly - in a rich manner; synonym for 'wealthily'
