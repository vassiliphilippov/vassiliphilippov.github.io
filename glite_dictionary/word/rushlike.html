rushlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌʃˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a rush or sedge in appearance, structure, or nature.
resembling a rush or sedge

Examples

The plant had tall, slender, rushlike stems growing along the water's edge.
Her design featured a rushlike texture that gave the basket a natural appearance.
Some grasses have rushlike leaves that are difficult to distinguish from true rushes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rushy - abounding in or covered with rushes

Etymology

From 'rush' (a plant of the family Juncaceae) + '-like' (having the characteristics of).
