sheepshead

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃiːpsˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpshɛd / (BrE)
a large food fish of the western Atlantic, found along the eastern coast of the United States and Mexico
an Atlantic food fish

Examples

Fishermen along the Gulf coast often catch sheepshead near rocky jetties.
The restaurant specializes in grilled sheepshead with local vegetables.
We spotted a large sheepshead swimming close to the pier.

Etymology

The name refers to the fish's teeth, which resemble those of a sheep.
