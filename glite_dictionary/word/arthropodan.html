arthropodan

1. ADJ

/ ɑːrˈθrɑːp.ə.dən / (AmE), / ɑːˈθrɒp.əd.ən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of invertebrates of the phylum Arthropoda, such as insects, arachnids, and crustaceans.
relating to arthropods (invertebrates)
medical

Examples

The arthropodan exoskeleton provides both protection and support for the animal.
Many arthropodan species undergo metamorphosis during their life cycles.
The scientist delivered a lecture on arthropodan evolution and diversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arthropod - an invertebrate animal of the phylum Arthropoda

Etymology

From 'Arthropoda', the phylum name, plus the suffix '-an' forming adjectives and nouns.
