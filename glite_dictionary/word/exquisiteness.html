exquisiteness

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈskwɪzɪtnəs / (AmE), / ɪkˈskwɪzɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely beautiful, delicate, or refined
extreme beauty or delicacy

Examples

The exquisiteness of the jewelry took everyone's breath away.
She spoke with a calm exquisiteness that captivated the audience.
The exquisiteness of the painting lies in its delicate brushstrokes and subtle colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - a quality that gives pleasure to the senses or exalts the mind or spirit
  • Antonym: ugliness - the quality of being unpleasant to look at; lack of beauty

Etymology

From Latin 'exquisitus' (carefully sought out) via French 'exquis'.
