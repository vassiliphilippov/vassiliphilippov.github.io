neckwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛkˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈnɛkˌwɛə / (BrE)
items of clothing worn around the neck, such as ties and scarves
clothing worn about the neck

Examples

He chose a colorful neckwear to match his suit for the party.
Scarves and ties are common types of neckwear worn in winter and at formal events.
The store sells a variety of neckwear, including bow ties and cravats.

Etymology

From 'neck' + 'wear', indicating items worn around the neck.
