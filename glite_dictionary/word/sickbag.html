sickbag

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪkˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈsɪkˌbaɡ / (BrE)
a bag provided, especially on airplanes, for passengers to use if they feel the need to vomit due to motion sickness
bag for vomiting, especially on airplanes

Examples

If you start to feel nauseous during the flight, there's a sickbag in the seat pocket in front of you.
The stewardess handed a sickbag to the passenger who looked unwell.
He reached for a sickbag just in time as the turbulence got worse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air sickness bag - a bag used for vomiting due to motion sickness, especially on planes
  • Synonym: emesis bag - medical bag for collecting vomit
