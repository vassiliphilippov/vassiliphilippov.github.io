to debone

1. VERB

/ diˈboʊn / (AmE), / diˈbəʊn / (BrE)
to remove the bones from meat or fish
remove bones from meat or fish

Examples

The chef will debone the chicken before marinating it.
It's often easier to debone fish after cooking, but some prefer to remove bones beforehand.
Learning how to debone meat properly is a useful kitchen skill.

Synset Examples

bone the turkey before roasting it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bone - remove the bones from (meat or fish)

Etymology

de- (prefix expressing removal) + bone (noun).
