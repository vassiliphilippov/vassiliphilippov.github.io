listener

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪsənər / (AmE), / ˈlɪsənə / (BrE)
a person who pays attention to and hears what is being said
person who listens attentively

Examples

She is a good listener who always makes time for her friends.
Radio stations often try to engage their listeners with interactive programs.
As a listener, he was both patient and thoughtful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hearer - a person who hears something
  • Antonym: speaker - a person who speaks

Etymology

From listen + -er, dating from the late 16th century.
