fifty-fifty

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɪfti ˈfɪfti / (AmE), / ˌfɪfti ˈfɪfti / (BrE)
equally divided between two parties; having equal chances or shares.
equally divided; equal chances

Examples

We decided on a fifty-fifty split of the profits from the project.
The chances of success are fifty-fifty at this point.
They reached a fifty-fifty agreement on who would do the housework.

Synset Examples

even amounts of butter and sugar
on even terms
it was a fifty-fifty (or even) split
had a fifty-fifty (or even) chance
an even fight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: even - equal in amount or balanced
  • Antonym: unequal - not equal; uneven in amount or distribution

Etymology

From the numbers fifty and fifty, indicating an equal split of 100 into two parts.
