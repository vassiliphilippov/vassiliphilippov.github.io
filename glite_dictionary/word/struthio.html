Struthio

1. PROPN

/ struːˈθaɪoʊ / (AmE), / struːˈθiːəʊ / (BrE)
The genus comprising the African ostriches, large flightless birds native to Africa.
genus of African ostriches

Examples

The genus Struthio includes the largest living birds on Earth, the ostriches.
Fossil records show that Struthio once lived in parts of Asia as well as Africa.
In zoological classification, Struthio is the only extant genus of the Struthionidae family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ostrich - a large flightless bird native to Africa

Etymology

From Latin *struthio* (ostrich), from Ancient Greek *strouthíōn* (ostrich).
