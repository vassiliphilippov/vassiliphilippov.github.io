meaningfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːnɪŋfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈmiːnɪŋfəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having significance, purpose, or value
the quality of being significant or valuable

Examples

The meaningfulness of their work inspired the whole team to contribute their best efforts.
She searched for greater meaningfulness in her daily routine and relationships.
Researchers are studying the meaningfulness of dreams in human psychology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: significance - the quality of being important or of giving meaning
  • Antonym: meaninglessness - the quality of lacking meaning, purpose, or value

Etymology

From 'meaningful' + '-ness', formed in English.
