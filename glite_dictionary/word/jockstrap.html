jockstrap

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɑkˌstræp / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒkˌstræp / (BrE)
a piece of men's undergarment worn to support the genitals during vigorous physical activity, especially in sport
athletic support garment for men

Examples

He wore a jockstrap during football practice for extra protection.
The coach reminded all the players to bring their jockstraps to the game.
Some athletes find the jockstrap uncomfortable, but it is important for safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athletic supporter - another term for jockstrap

Etymology

From 'jock' (slang for 'athlete' or 'penis') + 'strap'.
