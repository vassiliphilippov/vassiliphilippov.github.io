sectorial

1. ADJ

/ sɛkˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / sɛkˈtɔːrɪəl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a sector, especially in shape or form.
relating to a sector in shape or form

Examples

The pie chart was divided into sectorial segments for each department.
They constructed a sectorial box to study angular distribution patterns.
The building's sectorial design made efficient use of its corner location.

Synset Examples

a sectorial box

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sector - a part or division of a whole, especially in mathematics or business

Etymology

From sector + -ial, patterned after Latin adjectives ending in -ialis.
