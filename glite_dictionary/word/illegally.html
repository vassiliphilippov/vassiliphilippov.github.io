illegally

1. ADV

/ ɪˈliɡəli / (AmE), / ɪˈliːɡəli / (BrE)
In a manner that is not allowed by law.
in an unlawful way

Examples

The company was fined after disposing of hazardous materials illegally.
They entered the country illegally, bypassing the border checkpoint.
Selling these products illegally can result in severe penalties.

Synset Examples

they dumped the waste illegally

Related Terms

  • Antonym: legally - in a manner permitted by law

Etymology

From illegal + -ly, forming the adverb from the adjective.
