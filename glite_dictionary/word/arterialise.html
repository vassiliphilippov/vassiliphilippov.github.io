to arterialise

1. VERB

/ ɑːrˈtɪriəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriəlaɪz / (BrE)
(of blood) to become, or cause to become, arterial blood by oxygenation.
convert venous blood into arterial blood
medical british

Examples

During respiration, the lungs arterialise the venous blood passing through them.
The process to arterialise blood is critical in extracorporeal membrane oxygenation therapy.
Some medical devices are designed specifically to arterialise venous samples for analysis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to arterialize - To change venous blood into arterial blood (American spelling)

Etymology

From artery + -al + -ise (British spelling), meaning to make like arterial blood.
