Poinciana gilliesii

1. NOUN

/ pɔɪnˈsiːənə ɡɪlˈiːziːaɪ / (AmE), / pɔɪnˈsiːənə ɡɪlˈiːziːaɪ / (BrE)
a tropical shrub or small tree with bright orange or red flowers, sometimes classified in the genus Poinciana
tropical shrub with orange or red flowers

Examples

The vivid blossoms of the Poinciana gilliesii adorn many tropical gardens around the world.
Gardeners prize Poinciana gilliesii for its striking orange-red flowers and feathery foliage.
During summer, the Poinciana gilliesii attracts butterflies and hummingbirds with its bright blooms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bird of paradise bush - another common name for Poinciana gilliesii

Etymology

From the genus name 'Poinciana', honoring Philippe de Longvilliers de Poincy, and the species name after Robert Gillies.
