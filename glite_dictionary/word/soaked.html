soaked

1. ADJ

/ soʊkt / (AmE), / səʊkt / (BrE)
Extremely drunk; intoxicated to a high degree.
very drunk; intoxicated
informal

Examples

He got absolutely soaked at the party last night and could barely walk home.
After a few pints, she was already feeling soaked.
By midnight, most of the guests were totally soaked and singing loudly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drunk - intoxicated by alcohol
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol
