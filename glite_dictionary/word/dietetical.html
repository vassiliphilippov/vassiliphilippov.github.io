dietetical

1. ADJ

/ ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to diet or the regulation of food intake, especially for health or medical reasons.
relating to diet or food regulation

Examples

The dietetical value of the meal was carefully considered by the nutritionist.
She received specialized dietetical advice after her diagnosis.
Their conference included experts in dietetical science and nutrition.

Synset Examples

dietary restrictions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dietetic - relating to diet, especially specific regimens

Etymology

From Greek *diaitētikos* (pertaining to diet) via Late Latin *dieteticus*.
