Le Havre

1. PROPN

/ lə ˈhɑvrə / (AmE), / lə ˈhɑːvʁ(ə) / (BrE)
a port city in northern France on the English Channel, situated at the mouth of the Seine River
French port city on the English Channel

Examples

The cruise ship made a stop at Le Havre before continuing to Paris.
During World War II, Le Havre suffered significant damage from bombing raids.
Exports from Le Havre play a vital role in the French economy.

Etymology

French, meaning 'the harbour'.
