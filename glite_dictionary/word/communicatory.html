communicatory

1. ADJ

/ kəˈmjunɪkəˌtɔri / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪkətəri / (BrE)
able or inclined to communicate; relating to communication
relating to communication

Examples

The teacher adopted a communicatory approach to encourage classroom discussions.
Effective leadership often requires strong communicatory skills.
Her communicatory abilities made her an excellent spokesperson for the organization.

Synset Examples

was a communicative person and quickly told all she knew

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communicative - willing, ready, or able to talk or impart information

Etymology

From Latin 'communicare' (to share) + English suffix '-ory'.
