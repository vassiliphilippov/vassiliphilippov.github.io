derivation

/ ˌdɛrɪˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛrɪˈveɪʃən / (BrE)

Examples

The derivation of water from the river ensured the irrigation of nearby fields.
Engineers designed a canal for the derivation of water into the agricultural zone.
During the dry season, the derivation system helps provide sufficient water for crops.

Etymology

From Latin derivatio (a drawing off), from de- (from) + rivus (stream).
