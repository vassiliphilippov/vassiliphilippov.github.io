doglike

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɔgˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɒgˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling a dog in appearance or character, especially in loyalty or devotion.
resembling a dog, especially in loyalty

Examples

The child's doglike devotion to her father was touching.
He followed his mentor with a doglike loyalty, never questioning a command.
Her doglike eagerness to please made her popular among her colleagues.

Synset Examples

a doglike affection

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canine - relating to or resembling a dog

Etymology

Formed from 'dog' + '-like', denoting resemblance.
