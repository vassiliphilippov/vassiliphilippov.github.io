Nguyen Tat Thanh

1. PROPN

/ ŋwiˈɛn ˈtæt ˈtæn / (AmE), / ŋwiˈɛn ˈtæt ˈtæn / (BrE)
The birth name of Ho Chi Minh (1890–1969), the Vietnamese communist statesman and revolutionary leader who fought against Japanese, French, and South Vietnamese forces.
birth name of Ho Chi Minh, Vietnamese leader

Examples

Few people realize that Nguyen Tat Thanh was the original name of Ho Chi Minh.
The early activism of Nguyen Tat Thanh laid the foundation for Vietnam's struggle for independence.
In history class, we learned how Nguyen Tat Thanh played a vital role in shaping modern Vietnam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ho Chi Minh - Vietnamese communist leader (1890–1969), also known as Nguyen Tat Thanh
