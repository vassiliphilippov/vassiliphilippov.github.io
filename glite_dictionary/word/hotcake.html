hotcake

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑtˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌkeɪk / (BrE)
A thin, flat cake made from batter and cooked on a griddle or frying pan, typically served hot.
thin, flat cake cooked on a griddle

Examples

He flipped the hotcake in the skillet until it was golden brown on both sides.
They served stacks of hotcakes with maple syrup at the breakfast diner.
The smell of freshly made hotcakes filled the kitchen every Sunday morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pancake - thin, flat cake of batter cooked on a griddle
