self-produced

1. ADJ

/ sɛlf prəˈduːst / (AmE), / sɛlf prəˈdjuːst / (BrE)
Originating or created by oneself; produced without external help.
created by oneself

Examples

The artist's latest album is entirely self-produced and showcases his unique style.
Many small business owners take pride in their self-produced products.
Her self-produced films have won awards at several international festivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-made - having become successful or rich by one's own efforts
