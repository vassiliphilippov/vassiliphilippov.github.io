Trento

1. PROPN

/ ˈtrɛn.toʊ / (AmE), / ˈtrɛn.təʊ / (BrE)
A city in northern Italy on the River Adige, known as the site of the Council of Trent.
city in northern Italy; Council of Trent site

Examples

The Council of Trent was held in Trento during the 16th century.
Many tourists visit Trento to explore its medieval old town and stunning mountain scenery.
She moved to Trento for a research program at the local university.

Etymology

From Italian 'Trento', from Latin 'Tridentum', the ancient name of the city.
