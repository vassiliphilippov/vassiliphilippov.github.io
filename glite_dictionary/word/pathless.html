pathless

1. ADJ

/ ˈpæθ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːθ.ləs / (BrE)
(of a place) having no paths or tracks; lacking any defined or walkable ways.
without paths or tracks

Examples

The hikers ventured into the pathless forest, relying on their compass to find the way.
Snow covered the fields, making the landscape seem completely pathless after the storm.
Explorers faced many dangers crossing the pathless deserts in search of new routes.

Synset Examples

trackless wilderness
roadless areas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trackless - without tracks or roads
  • Synonym: roadless - without roads or roadways

Etymology

From 'path' + '-less', with '-less' meaning 'without'.
