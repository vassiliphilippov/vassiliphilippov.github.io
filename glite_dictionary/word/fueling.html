fueling

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of supplying or taking on fuel, especially to a vehicle, engine, or machine.
supplying or taking on fuel

Examples

The fueling of the aircraft was completed before sunrise.
Proper fueling procedures are essential for the safety of the ship.
The pit crew managed fueling quickly during the race.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refueling - the act of supplying fuel again

Etymology

From 'fuel' (Middle English, from Old French, from Late Latin *focalis*), with the suffix '-ing' forming a noun of action.
