vegetational

1. ADJ

/ ˌvɛdʒəˈteɪʃənl / (AmE), / ˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪʃənl / (BrE)
relating to vegetation or composed of plants.
relating to vegetation

Examples

The researchers surveyed different vegetational zones within the national park.
Changes in climate have led to significant vegetational shifts across the continent.
Satellite imagery helps analyze the vegetational cover of remote landscapes.

Synset Examples

regions rich in vegetal products
vegetational cover
the decaying vegetative layer covering a forest floor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetative - relating to vegetation or plant growth

Etymology

From 'vegetation' + '-al', forming an adjective meaning 'pertaining to vegetation'.
