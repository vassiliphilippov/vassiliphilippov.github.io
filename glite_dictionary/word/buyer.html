buyer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who buys goods or services
person who purchases something

Examples

The buyer agreed to pay the full asking price for the car.
A first-time home buyer should always have a property inspected before purchase.
The store offered a discount to every buyer during its anniversary sale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purchaser - a person who buys something
  • Antonym: seller - a person who sells something

Etymology

From Middle English 'biour', from 'buy' + '-er'.
