San Pablo

1. PROPN

/ sæn ˈpɑbloʊ / (AmE), / sæn ˈpæbləʊ / (BrE)
A town in western California, located to the north of Oakland on the eastern shore of San Francisco Bay.
town in California, USA

Examples

She grew up in San Pablo and often visited the nearby bay.
The new community center in San Pablo has become a local gathering place.
Many people commute from San Pablo to jobs in Oakland and San Francisco.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Saint Paul'.
