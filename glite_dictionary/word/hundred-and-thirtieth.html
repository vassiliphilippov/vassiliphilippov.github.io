hundred-and-thirtieth

1. ADJ

/ ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈθɜrtiəθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈθɜːtiəθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred and thirty; 130th in a sequence
ordinal number: 130th

Examples

She finished in hundred-and-thirtieth place during the marathon.
This is the hundred-and-thirtieth anniversary of the school's founding.
I've read the hundred-and-thirtieth page, and the story is still exciting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one hundred and thirtieth - alternative spelling for the ordinal 130th
  • Degree Lower: hundred-and-twenty-ninth - ordinal for 129th in a sequence
  • Degree Higher: hundred-and-thirty-first - ordinal for 131st in a sequence
