fearlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈfɪr.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈfɪə.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows no fear; bravely or courageously.
in a fearless or brave manner

Examples

She spoke fearlessly in front of the large audience, impressing everyone with her confidence.
The journalist fearlessly reported from the conflict zone despite the risks involved.
They fearlessly challenged the unfair policy, refusing to back down.

Synset Examples

fearlessly, he led the troops into combat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bravely - in a brave manner; showing courage
  • Antonym: timidly - in a timid manner; lacking courage or confidence

Etymology

From "fearless" (without fear) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).
