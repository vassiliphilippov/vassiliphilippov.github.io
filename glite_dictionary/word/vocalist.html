vocalist

1. NOUN

/ ˈvoʊkəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈvəʊkəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who sings, especially as a performer in a band or group
person who sings, especially professionally

Examples

The vocalist captivated the audience with her powerful voice.
He joined the band as the lead vocalist last summer.
Many rock bands have a separate vocalist and instrumentalists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: singer - a person who sings, especially professionally
  • Antonym: instrumentalist - a person who plays a musical instrument

Etymology

From vocal + -ist, patterned after other words for practitioners like 'pianist'.
