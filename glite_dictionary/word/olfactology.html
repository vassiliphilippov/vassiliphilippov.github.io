olfactology

/ ˌɑl.fækˈtɑl.ə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌɒl.fækˈtɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of smells or the sense of smell
scientific study of smells or smell
medical

Examples

She pursued a doctorate in olfactology to better understand how aroma affects human behavior.

Etymology
