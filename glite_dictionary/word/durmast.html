durmast

1. NOUN

/ dɜrˈmæst / (AmE), / ˈdɜːmɑːst / (BrE)
a deciduous European oak (Quercus petraea) valued for its tough, elastic wood
a European oak tree species

Examples

The furniture was crafted from solid durmast, giving it both beauty and durability.
Carpenters prize durmast for its resilience in construction projects.
Ancient forests of durmast oaks once covered large parts of Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oak - a type of tree or its wood, from the genus Quercus
