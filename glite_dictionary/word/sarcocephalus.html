1. NOUN

/ ˌsɑːrkoʊˈsɛfələs / (AmE), / ˌsɑːkəˈsɛfələs / (BrE)
A genus of tropical African trees and shrubs in the family Rubiaceae.
genus of tropical African trees and shrubs

Examples
