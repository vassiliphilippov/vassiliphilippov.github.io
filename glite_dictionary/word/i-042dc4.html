i

1. NOUN

/ aɪ / (AmE), / aɪ / (BrE)
The ninth letter of the English alphabet.
the ninth letter of the alphabet

Examples

The word 'insect' begins with the letter i.
She wrote her name, making sure the i was dotted clearly.
The alphabet song lists i after 'h' and before 'j'.

Etymology

From Latin letter 'i', from Greek 'iota', from Phoenician 'yodh'.
