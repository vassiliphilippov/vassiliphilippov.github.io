odontology

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊdənˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌɒdɒnˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the structure, development, and diseases of the teeth.
study of teeth and their diseases
medical

Examples

She specialized in odontology after graduating from medical school.
Advances in odontology have greatly improved dental health treatments.
His research in forensic odontology helped identify the remains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dentistry - the profession or science dealing with the prevention and treatment of dental diseases

Etymology

From Greek odous, odont- (tooth) + -logy (study).
