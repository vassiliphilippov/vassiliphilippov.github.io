Serb

1. NOUN

/ sɜrb / (AmE), / sɜːb / (BrE)
a member of a Slavic ethnic group originating from Serbia and neighboring regions
member of a Slavic ethnic group from Serbia

Examples

He is a proud Serb who celebrates his heritage every year.
Many Serbs emigrated to other countries during the 20th century.
The Serb community organized a cultural festival in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Croat - member of a Slavic ethnic group from Croatia
  • Synonym: Serbian - relating to Serbia or its people

Etymology

From Serbian 'Srbin', ultimately from Slavic roots denoting kinship or tribe.
