Malian

1. ADJ

/ ˈmæliən / (AmE), / ˈmɑːliən / (BrE)
of or relating to Mali or its people or culture.
of or relating to Mali or its people

Examples

Traditional Malian music is recognized around the world for its rich rhythms and melodies.
The Malian government announced new agricultural reforms this year.
Visitors often admire the unique architecture found in Malian cities like Timbuktu.

Synset Examples

Malian deserts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mali - a country in West Africa

Etymology

From Mali, the name of the West African country, with English adjectival suffix -an.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːliən / (AmE), / ˈmæliən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Mali, a country in West Africa
person from Mali

Examples

There is a large Malian community living in Paris.
The Malian greeted us warmly and offered us tea.
She studied the art and history of ancient Malian empires.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mali - a country in West Africa

Etymology

From Mali, the name of the country, plus the suffix -an denoting nationality or origin.
