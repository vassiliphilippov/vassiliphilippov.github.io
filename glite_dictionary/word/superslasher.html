superslasher

1. NOUN

/ suːpərˌslæʃər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌslæʃə / (BrE)
a large, swift carnivorous dinosaur of the early Cretaceous period, notable for a 15-inch slashing claw on each hind foot
large Cretaceous carnivorous dinosaur

Examples

The fossilized remains of a superslasher were discovered in Mongolia.
Paleontologists believe the superslasher used its deadly claw to hunt large prey.
A life-size model of the superslasher is on display at the natural history museum.
