redevelopment

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːdəˈvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːdɪˈvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
the process of improving an area, building, or system by making major changes or rebuilding
renewal or improvement of an area or system

Examples

The city approved a large redevelopment project for the old industrial district.
The redevelopment of the waterfront transformed the area into a vibrant destination.
After years of neglect, the neighborhood is undergoing significant redevelopment.

Synset Examples

they are pursuing a general program of renovation to the entire property
a major overhaul of the healthcare system was proposed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: renovation - the act of restoring something to a good condition
  • Synonym: overhaul - a thorough repair or renovation

Etymology

Formed from 're-' (again) + 'development'.
