musingly

1. ADV

/ ˈmjuːzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows deep thought or reflection; thoughtfully or meditatively.
in a thoughtful or reflective manner

Examples

She stared out of the window musingly, lost in her own thoughts.
He replied musingly, as if considering every word carefully.
The old man smiled musingly, recalling memories from his youth.

Synset Examples

‘It's funny about that bar,’ he said musingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtfully - in a thoughtful manner
  • Synonym: absentmindedly - in a manner lost in thought

Etymology

From "musing" (reflective, meditative) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
