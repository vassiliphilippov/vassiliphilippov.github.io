waterlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtərˌlɛsnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌlɛsnəs / (BrE)
the state or condition of being without water; absence of water
state of being without water

Examples

The waterlessness of the desert made it impossible for plants to survive.
After weeks of drought, villagers worried about the increasing waterlessness of their wells.
Scientists studied the effects of waterlessness on local ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dryness - the state of being dry; lack of moisture

Etymology

From 'water' + '-less' (without) + '-ness' (state or quality).
