rundown

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌnˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈrʌn.daʊn / (BrE)
a summary or brief report giving the main points of something, especially a situation, event, or process
summary of main points

Examples

Let me give you a quick rundown of today's meeting before we start.
The news anchor provided a rundown of the top stories this evening.
Before the trial ended, the lawyer gave the jury a final rundown of the evidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
  • Synonym: overview - a general summary or review
