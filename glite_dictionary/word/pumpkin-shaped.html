pumpkin-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌmpkɪn ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈpʌmpkɪn ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having an equatorial diameter greater than the polar diameter; shaped like a pumpkin, being flattened at the poles.
shaped like a pumpkin; flattened at the poles

Examples

The spacecraft's heat shield had a distinctive pumpkin-shaped design to improve stability during descent.
Certain squash varieties are naturally pumpkin-shaped with broad, rounded bodies and flattened tops and bottoms.
The artist created a pumpkin-shaped lantern for the fall festival.
