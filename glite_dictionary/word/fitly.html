fitly

1. ADV

/ ˈfɪt.li / (AmE), / ˈfɪt.li / (BrE)
In a way that is appropriate or suitable for the circumstances.
in an appropriate or suitable manner

Examples

The ceremony was fitly conducted with dignity and respect.
He was fitly rewarded for his outstanding contributions to the community.
Her speech was fitly timed, coming just when encouragement was needed.

Synset Examples

he was appropriately dressed
If you don't behave properly, you'll have to leave!
I met the junior senator from Illinois and I was duly impressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appropriately - in a way that is suitable or fitting for a particular situation
  • Synonym: properly - in a correct or suitable way
  • Antonym: inappropriately - in a way that is not suitable for the circumstances

Etymology

From 'fit' + '-ly', modeled after similar adverbs formed from adjectives.
