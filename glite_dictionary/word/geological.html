geological

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒiːəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of the Earth's physical structure, history, and processes
relating to geology

Examples

The region is famous for its geological formations and unique rock structures.
Scientists conducted a geological survey to better understand earthquake risks in the area.
Her research focuses on geological changes that occurred millions of years ago.

Synset Examples

geological formations
geologic forces

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geologic - relating to geology (alternate form)
  • Antonym: geographical - relating to geography, the study of Earth's surface

Etymology

From geology + -ical; geology from Greek geo- (earth) + -logia (study).
