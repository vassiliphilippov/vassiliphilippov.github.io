motorcade

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊ.tɚˌkeɪd / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.tə.keɪd / (BrE)
a procession of motor vehicles, typically for an important person or event
procession of motor vehicles

Examples

The president's motorcade brought traffic in the city to a standstill.
Spectators lined the streets to watch the motorcade pass by.
Security was tight along the route of the official motorcade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: procession - an organized group or line of people or vehicles moving together

Etymology

Blend of 'motor' and 'cavalcade'.
