camping

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæm.pɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkæm.pɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of staying and sleeping outdoors, usually in tents, for recreation.
activity of staying outdoors in tents

Examples

We went camping in the mountains for a week last summer.
Many families enjoy camping as a way to spend time together in nature.
She bought a new tent for her next camping trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camp - to set up or stay in a camp

Etymology

From the verb 'camp', ultimately from French 'camp', from Latin 'campus' (field).
