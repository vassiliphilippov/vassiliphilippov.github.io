vulpecular

1. ADJ

/ vʌlˈpɛkjələr / (AmE), / vʌlˈpɛkjʊlə / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a fox.
resembling a fox

Examples

Her vulpecular expression made everyone suspect she had a secret.
The animal's vulpecular tail was bushy and red.
He admired the vulpecular grace of the fox as it darted through the woods.

Synset Examples

vulpine cunning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vulpine - related to or resembling a fox

Etymology

Formed from Latin 'vulpes' (fox) with scientific/Latin suffix '-cular'.
