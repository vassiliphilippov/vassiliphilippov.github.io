Colinus virginianus

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈlaɪnəs vɜrdʒɪˈeɪnəs / (AmE), / kɒˈlaɪnəs vɜːdʒɪˈeɪnəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the northern bobwhite, a game bird native to eastern and central United States.
scientific name for northern bobwhite

Examples

Wildlife photographers often seek out Colinus virginianus in grassland habitats during the nesting season.
The Colinus virginianus population has faced decline due to habitat loss and changes in land use.
Hunters in the southern United States prize the Colinus virginianus for its challenging flight and delicious meat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: northern bobwhite - a small North American quail, widely known as a game bird

Etymology

From Latin: 'Colinus', the genus name, and 'virginianus', meaning 'of Virginia', indicating the species' native region.
