tightfisted

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪtˌfɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtˌfɪstɪd / (BrE)
unwilling to spend or give money; stingy or miserly
unwilling to spend money

Examples

My grandfather is so tightfisted that he reused tea bags to save money.
The company was known for its tightfisted approach to employee salaries.
Despite his wealth, he remained tightfisted and rarely donated to charity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stingy - unwilling to spend or give money
  • Antonym: generous - willing to give more money or help than is usual or expected

Etymology

Formed from 'tight' (close, parsimonious) and 'fisted' (gripped; with reference to holding money tightly).
