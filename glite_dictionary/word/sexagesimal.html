sexagesimal

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛk.səˈdʒɛs.ə.məl / (AmE), / ˌsɛk.səˈdʒɛs.ɪ.məl / (BrE)
relating to or based on the number sixty; using a numerical system with sixty as its base.
relating to base 60 or the number sixty

Examples

Ancient Babylonian mathematicians used a sexagesimal system for calculations.
The division of a circle into 360 degrees reflects a sexagesimal influence.
Minutes and seconds in timekeeping are still based on the sexagesimal principle.

Synset Examples

the sexagesimal divisions of hours and degrees

Related Terms

  • Antonym: decimal - relating to or based on the number ten or a system of numbers that uses ten as its base

Etymology

From Latin sexagesimus (sixtieth), from sexaginta (sixty).
