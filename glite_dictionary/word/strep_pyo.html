Strep pyo

1. NOUN

/ stɹɛp ˈpaɪ.oʊ / (AmE), / stɹɛp ˈpʌɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
An informal, abbreviated term for Streptococcus pyogenes, a species of bacteria that causes various infections in humans.
abbreviation for Streptococcus pyogenes
medical informal

Examples

The doctor suspected that Strep pyo was responsible for the patient's sore throat.
After the cut became red and swollen, tests confirmed a Strep pyo infection.
Medical staff are trained to recognize symptoms caused by Strep pyo in children.

Synset Examples

He was treated for a wound infected by Strep pyo.
Strep pyo is a common cause of skin infections.
Strep pyo can lead to more severe health issues if untreated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Streptococcus pyogenes - the bacterium species known for causing infections such as strep throat and impetigo

Etymology

Shortened from 'Streptococcus pyogenes', from Greek 'streptos' (twisted) and 'kokkos' (berry) referring to the bacterium's shape, and 'pyogenes' from Greek 'pyo' (pus) and 'genes' (producing).
