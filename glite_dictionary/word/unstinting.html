unstinting

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈstɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
giving something generously and without holding back; not restricting or limiting in amount.
very generous; without holding back

Examples

Her unstinting support helped the charity to succeed beyond all expectations.
The teacher was known for his unstinting dedication to his students.
They received unstinting praise from their colleagues after the successful presentation.

Synset Examples

distributed gifts with a lavish hand
the critics were lavish in their praise
a munificent gift
his father gave him a half-dollar and his mother a quarter and he thought them munificent
unsparing generosity
his unstinted devotion
called for unstinting aid to Britain
lavish praise

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generous - willing to give more of something than is strictly necessary
  • Antonym: stingy - unwilling to give or spend; ungenerous

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'stint' (to restrict), meaning 'not restricting'.
