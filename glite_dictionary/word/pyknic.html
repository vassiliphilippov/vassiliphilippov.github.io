pyknic

1. ADJ

/ pɪkˈnɪk / (AmE), / pɪkˈnɪk / (BrE)
having a short and thick, fleshy body build, especially as classified in somatotyping.
having a thick, fleshy build

Examples

Despite being pyknic, she excelled in athletics and amazed everyone with her speed.
The pyknic man enjoyed a hearty meal and good company at the village festival.
Some older psychological theories classified people as pyknic based on their physique.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: athletic - having a strong, muscular body type

Etymology

From Greek 'pyknos' (thick, compact).
