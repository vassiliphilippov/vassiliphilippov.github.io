wally

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːli / (AmE), / ˈwɒli / (BrE)
A person who is silly, inept, or regarded as stupid.
silly or inept person
informal british

Examples

Everyone thought he was a wally for forgetting his own birthday.
Don’t be such a wally—that’s not how you do it!
She felt like a complete wally when she tripped in front of everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently; a silly person
  • Synonym: idiot - a person of very low intelligence or common sense
