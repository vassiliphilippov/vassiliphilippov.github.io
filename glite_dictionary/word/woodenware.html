woodenware

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdənˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈwʊdənˌweə / (BrE)
Domestic utensils, containers, or other articles made of wood.
household items made of wood

Examples

She collects antique woodenware from different regions around the world.
The kitchen was filled with simple woodenware, giving it a rustic charm.
They displayed handmade woodenware at the craft fair last weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tableware - dishes and utensils used for serving and eating food
  • Antonym: silverware - dishes, cutlery or utensils made of silver or similar metals
