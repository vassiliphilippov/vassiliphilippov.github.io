ingesta

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʒɛstə / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɛstə / (BrE)
food and drink that have been taken into the body through the mouth, especially in medical or scientific contexts.
substances taken into the body by mouth
medical

Examples

The patient's ingesta were carefully measured to monitor nutrient intake.
Doctors analyzed the ingesta to identify potential sources of infection.
Veterinarians must consider the ingesta when diagnosing digestive issues in animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ingest - to take food, drink, or another substance into the body by swallowing or absorbing it

Etymology

From Latin 'ingesta', plural of 'ingestum' (that which is carried in), from 'ingerere' (to carry in).
