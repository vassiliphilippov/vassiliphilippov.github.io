spirally

1. ADV

/ ˈspaɪrəli / (AmE), / ˈspaɪrəli / (BrE)
In a spiral manner or shape; forming or moving in spirals.
in a spiral pattern or form

Examples

The tendrils grew spirally up the trellis, creating a beautiful pattern.
The columns were decorated spirally along their shafts with intricate carvings.
Smoke rose spirally from the campfire, drifting into the morning sky.

Synset Examples

spirally fluted handles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiral - having the shape or form of a spiral

Etymology

From 'spiral' + '-ly', forming an adverb meaning 'in a spiral manner'.
