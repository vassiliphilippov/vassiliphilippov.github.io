unappetising

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈæpəˌtaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈapɪtʌɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
Not attractive or appealing to the appetite in appearance, smell, or taste.
not appealing to the appetite
british

Examples

The cafeteria served an unappetising stew that most students avoided.
Despite its unappetising appearance, the dish actually tasted quite good.
She wrinkled her nose at the unappetising leftovers in the fridge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unappetizing - Not attractive or appealing to the appetite (US spelling)
  • Antonym: appetising - Appealing to the appetite in appearance, smell, or taste

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'appetising', meaning 'not appetising'.
