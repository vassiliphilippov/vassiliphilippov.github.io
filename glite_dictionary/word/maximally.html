maximally

1. ADV

/ ˈmæk.sɪ.mə.li / (AmE), / ˈmaks.ɪ.mə.li / (BrE)
to the greatest or highest degree possible.
to the greatest degree

Examples

The medication was adjusted so that its effects were maximally beneficial.
The new security system is maximally efficient during peak hours.
To achieve the best results, the machine must operate maximally under optimal conditions.

Synset Examples

the cells maximally responsive to lines in this orientation will fire

Related Terms

  • Antonym: minimally - to the least or smallest degree possible

Etymology

From maximal + -ly, with 'maximal' from Latin 'maximus' (greatest).
