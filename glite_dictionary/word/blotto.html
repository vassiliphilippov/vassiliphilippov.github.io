blotto

1. ADJ

/ ˈblɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈblɒtəʊ / (BrE)
Extremely drunk; intoxicated to the point of being unable to function normally.
extremely drunk
informal

Examples

After five glasses of wine, he was absolutely blotto and couldn't find his keys.
She stumbled out of the bar, clearly blotto and giggling uncontrollably.
You should have seen Tim last night—he was so blotto he forgot where he parked his car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drunk - intoxicated with alcohol
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol

Etymology

Of unknown origin, possibly from the word 'blot' (meaning to stain or soak); popular in early 20th-century slang.
