genealogical

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒiːniəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːniəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study or tracing of lines of family descent and ancestry.
relating to family ancestry

Examples

She spent years compiling her family's genealogical records.
The museum contains a large collection of genealogical charts and documents.
He joined an online forum to discuss genealogical research techniques with others.

Synset Examples

genealogical records

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ancestral - related to ancestors or ancestry
  • Antonym: descendant - a person descended from a particular ancestor

Etymology

From genealogy, based on Greek 'genealogia' (tracing of descent) and the suffix '-ical.'
