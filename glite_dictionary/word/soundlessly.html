soundlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈsaʊndləsli / (AmE), / ˈsaʊndləsli / (BrE)
In a way that makes no sound; silently or noiselessly.
without making any sound

Examples

The cat moved soundlessly across the carpet, barely disturbing a thread.
She closed the door soundlessly so as not to wake the baby.
Snow fell soundlessly on the empty street, muffling every sound.

Synset Examples

he stood up soundlessly and speechlessly and glided across the hallway and through a door

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silently - without making any sound; quietly
  • Antonym: noisily - making a lot of noise or sound
