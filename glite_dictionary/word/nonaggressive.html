nonaggressive

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.əˈɡrɛs.ɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈɡrɛs.ɪv / (BrE)
not aggressive; not inclined to attack, fight, or act forcefully
not aggressive or forceful

Examples

The new puppy is surprisingly nonaggressive and gentle with children.
He prefers a nonaggressive approach to negotiating business deals.
Some medications are prescribed to encourage more nonaggressive behavior in patients.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: aggressive - inclined or ready to attack or confront; assertive or forceful

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' (not) to 'aggressive'.
