noncivilized

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈsɪvəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsɪvəˌlaɪzd / (BrE)
not having a high level of culture or social development; lacking the characteristics of a civilized society.
lacking advanced culture or development

Examples

The documentary explored the traditions of a noncivilized tribe deep in the rainforest.
Many early explorers wrongly described unfamiliar cultures as noncivilized.
Some argued that the absence of written language made a society noncivilized in Western eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncivilized - not civilized; lacking manners or social development
  • Antonym: civilized - having an advanced state of social development

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'non-' meaning 'not' plus 'civilized'.
