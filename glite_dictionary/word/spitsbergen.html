Spitsbergen

1. PROPN

/ spɪtsˈbɜːrɡən / (AmE), / spɪtsˈbɜːɡən / (BrE)
the largest island in the Svalbard archipelago, located in the Arctic Ocean, north of mainland Norway.
largest island in Svalbard archipelago, Norway

Examples

Polar bears can often be found on Spitsbergen during the summer months.
Scientists travel to Spitsbergen to study its unique Arctic ecosystems.
Tourists visit Spitsbergen to witness the midnight sun and dramatic fjords.

Etymology

From Dutch 'spitsbergen', meaning 'pointed mountains'.
