beseechingly

1. ADV

/ bɪˈsiːtʃɪŋli / (AmE), / bɪˈsiːtʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows you are begging or pleading intensely for something
with intense pleading or begging

Examples

She looked at him beseechingly, hoping he would reconsider his decision.
The child reached out beseechingly for her mother's hand as they crossed the busy street.
He spoke beseechingly, trying to convince the council to support his proposal.

Synset Examples

‘You must help me,’ she said imploringly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imploringly - in a pleading or begging manner

Etymology

From 'beseech' + '-ing' + '-ly'; 'beseech' from Middle English 'besechen', from Old English 'besēcan'.
