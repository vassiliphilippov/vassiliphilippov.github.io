dagame

1. NOUN

/ dəˈɡɑːmeɪ / (AmE), / dəˈɡɑːmeɪ / (BrE)
A tropical American tree (Calycophyllum candidissimum) whose tough, elastic wood is used for making tool handles and other items.
tropical tree with tough, elastic wood

Examples

The handle of the machete was made from sturdy dagame wood.
In Central America, dagame is valued for its exceptionally resilient timber.
Carpenters often choose dagame for tool handles due to its strength and elasticity.
