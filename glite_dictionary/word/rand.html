Rand

1. PROPN

/ rænd / (AmE), / rænd / (BrE)
a rocky region in southern Transvaal, northeastern South Africa, known for its rich gold, coal, and manganese deposits
gold-rich region of South Africa

Examples

The discovery of gold in the Rand transformed the economy of South Africa.
Many mining towns developed rapidly along the Rand during the late nineteenth century.
The Rand remains an important economic hub due to its mineral wealth.

Etymology

From Afrikaans 'Rand', meaning 'ridge'.
