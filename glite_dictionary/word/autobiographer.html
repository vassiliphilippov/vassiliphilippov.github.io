autobiographer

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtoʊbaɪˈɑɡrəfər / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊbaɪˈɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person who writes an account of their own life
person who writes their own biography

Examples

The famous autobiographer spent years recording every detail of her extraordinary life.
As an autobiographer, he provided unique insights into his personal struggles and triumphs.
Many politicians become autobiographers after leaving office, hoping to shape their own legacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biographer - a person who writes someone's biography

Etymology

From 'autobiography', from Greek 'autos' (self) + 'bios' (life) + 'graphein' (to write).
