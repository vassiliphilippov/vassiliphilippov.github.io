aqua

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːkwə, ˈækwə / (AmE), / ˈakwə, ˈɑːkwə / (BrE)
A light bluish-green color, resembling that of water.
bluish-green color

Examples

The pale aqua walls made the room feel cool and inviting.
She wore a dress in shimmering aqua that caught everyone's attention.
They chose aqua for their wedding theme because it reminded them of the ocean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turquoise - a blue-green color, similar to aqua
  • Degree Lower: teal - a dark greenish-blue color
  • Degree Higher: cyan - a greenish-blue color, between blue and green on the spectrum

Etymology

From Latin 'aqua' meaning 'water'.
