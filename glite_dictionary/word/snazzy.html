snazzy

1. ADJ

/ ˈsnæzi / (AmE), / ˈsnæzi / (BrE)
Brightly stylish or attractive in a flashy way.
flashily stylish or attractive

Examples

She wore a snazzy red jacket to the party.
His snazzy new car turned heads on the street.
The restaurant has a snazzy design with bold colors and modern lighting.

Synset Examples

a snazzy outfit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stylish - fashionably elegant and sophisticated
  • Synonym: flashy - bright or showy in a way that attracts attention
  • Antonym: plain - lacking ornament or adornment
