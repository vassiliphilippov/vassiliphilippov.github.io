letch

1. NOUN

/ lɛtʃ / (AmE), / lɛtʃ / (BrE)
a man who is regarded as having strong or excessive sexual desires, often behaving in a lecherous or inappropriate way
man with strong sexual desires
informal slang

Examples

He avoided sitting next to the office letch during meetings.
She complained about a letch who kept making inappropriate comments at the gym.
Some people whisper that the old landlord is a bit of a letch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lecher - a man given to excessive sexual indulgence

Etymology

Alteration of 'lecher' (from Old French lechier), influenced by slang formation.
