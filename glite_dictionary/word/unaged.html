unaged

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈeɪdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈeɪdʒd / (BrE)
Not having been subjected to an aging process.
not subjected to aging

Examples

The distillery produces both unaged and aged versions of its whiskey.
This cheese is unaged, giving it a mild and creamy flavor.
Some spirits, like unaged rum, are bottled soon after distillation.

Synset Examples

vodka is an unaged liquor from Russia

Related Terms

  • Antonym: aged - having been subjected to an aging process
