secretaryship

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkrəˌtɛrɪʃɪp / (AmE), / sɪˈkrɛtəriʃɪp / (BrE)
the position, office, or term of being a secretary
the office or role of a secretary

Examples

His secretaryship lasted for more than a decade before he chose to retire.
Following her successful secretaryship, she was promoted to department head.
He was widely praised for his diligent secretaryship during a challenging period for the company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secretary - a person employed to assist with correspondence, records, and administrative work

Etymology

secretary + -ship, denoting office or condition.
