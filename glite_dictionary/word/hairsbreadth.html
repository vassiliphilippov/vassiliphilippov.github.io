hairsbreadth

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrzˌbrɛdθ / (AmE), / ˈheəzˌbrɛdθ / (BrE)
A very small distance or space; the width of a hair.
a very small distance

Examples

She missed the bus by a hairsbreadth and had to wait for the next one.
The car stopped within a hairsbreadth of the edge of the cliff.
He escaped punishment by a hairsbreadth thanks to new evidence.

Synset Examples

they escaped by a hair's breadth
they lost the election by a whisker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whisker - a very small amount or margin

Etymology

From 'hair's breadth', referring literally to the width of a single hair.
