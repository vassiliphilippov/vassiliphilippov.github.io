starchlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɑrtʃˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtʃˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of starch
resembling starch

Examples

The sauce thickened to a starchlike consistency as it cooked.
Some synthetic fabrics have a starchlike stiffness when new.
This powder leaves a starchlike residue on the surface after cleaning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starchy - containing starch or resembling starch
