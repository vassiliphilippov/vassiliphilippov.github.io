Joan Miró

1. PROPN

/ hoʊˌɑn miˈroʊ / (AmE), / hwɑːn mɪˈrəʊ / (BrE)
(1893–1983) a Spanish surrealist painter and sculptor known for his imaginative, often abstract works featuring vivid colors and playful shapes.
Spanish surrealist painter and sculptor

Examples

The works of Joan Miró inspired a generation of modern artists.
A new exhibition features paintings by Joan Miró at the city museum.
Many art critics praise Joan Miró for his imaginative use of color and form.
