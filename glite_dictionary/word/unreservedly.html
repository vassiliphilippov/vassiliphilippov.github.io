unreservedly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈzɜrvɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈzɜːvɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that is completely without hesitation or limitation; openly and without holding back.
completely without reservation

Examples

She unreservedly apologized for her mistake, taking full responsibility for her actions.
I unreservedly support your decision to pursue a new career path.
The professor unreservedly praised the student's groundbreaking research.

Synset Examples

I can unreservedly recommend this restaurant!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wholeheartedly - in a way that shows complete sincerity and commitment

Etymology

Formed from 'unreserved' + '-ly'.
