forty-fifth

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔrti ˈfɪfθ / (AmE), / ˌfɔːti ˈfɪfθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number that is equal to forty-five in position in a sequence
ordinal number; position 45th

Examples

This is the forty-fifth anniversary of the foundation of our school.
She lives on the forty-fifth floor of the tower.
