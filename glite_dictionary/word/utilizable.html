utilizable

1. ADJ

/ ˈjuː.tɪ.laɪ.zə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈjuː.tɪ.laɪ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
Capable of being put to practical or profitable use; able to be utilized.
able to be used effectively

Examples

The company invested in equipment that was fully utilizable in a variety of manufacturing processes.
After the renovation, the basement space became much more utilizable for storage and meetings.
Many alternative energy sources are not yet economically utilizable on a large scale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: usable - able to be used
  • Antonym: unusable - not able to be used
  • Degree Higher: useful - able to be used for a practical purpose

Etymology

Formed from 'utilize' + '-able', patterned after 'usable'.
