lukewarmly

1. ADV

/ ˌluːkˈwɔrmli / (AmE), / ˌljuːkˈwɔːmli / (BrE)
In a way that shows only mild enthusiasm or interest; without strong feeling or excitement.
in an unenthusiastic or mild manner

Examples

The announcement was lukewarmly received by investors, who had hoped for stronger results.
She lukewarmly agreed to join us for dinner, showing little enthusiasm.
The proposal was lukewarmly endorsed by the committee, without much excitement or strong support.

Synset Examples

he was lukewarmly received by his relatives

Related Terms

  • Antonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows strong excitement or interest
  • Degree Higher: warmly - in a friendly or enthusiastic manner
  • Degree Lower: coldly - in an unfriendly or unemotional manner
