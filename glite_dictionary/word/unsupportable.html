unsupportable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.səˈpɔːr.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.səˈpɔː.tə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be defended, justified, or maintained; indefensible.
cannot be defended or justified

Examples

His theory was ultimately found to be unsupportable by the available evidence.
The company made unsupportable claims about the effectiveness of its product.
Such a position is morally and logically unsupportable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indefensible - not able to be defended against attack or criticism
  • Antonym: tenable - able to be defended or maintained

Etymology

From un- (not) + support + -able.
