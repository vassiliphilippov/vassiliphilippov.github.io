Alexander Pushkin

1. PROPN

/ ˌæləɡˈzændɚ ˈpʊʃkɪn / (AmE), / ˌalɪɡˈzandə ˈpʊʃkɪn / (BrE)
(1799–1837) A renowned Russian poet, playwright, and novelist, widely considered the founder of modern Russian literature.
famous Russian poet and author

Examples

Many scholars regard Alexander Pushkin as the greatest Russian poet of the 19th century.
The works of Alexander Pushkin have been translated into dozens of languages worldwide.
At the museum, there was a special exhibition about Alexander Pushkin and his influence on literature.
