P. T. Barnum

1. PROPN

/ pi ti ˈbɑrnəm / (AmE), / piː tiː ˈbɑːnəm / (BrE)
(1810–1891) An American showman and entertainer best known for popularizing the modern circus and founding the Barnum & Bailey Circus.
American circus showman (1810–1891)

Examples

Many credit P. T. Barnum with revolutionizing live entertainment in the 19th century.
P. T. Barnum was famous for his elaborate hoaxes and promotional stunts.
The influence of P. T. Barnum can still be seen in today's circus performances.
