defencelessness

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛnsləsnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnsləsnɪs / (BrE)
The state of being unable to protect oneself from harm or attack; helplessness.
state of being helpless or unprotected

Examples

His defencelessness made the situation even more tragic.
The villagers' defencelessness against the invaders was painfully obvious.
She was moved by the defencelessness of the stray animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helplessness - the state of being unable to help oneself or act without assistance
  • Antonym: security - the state of being free from danger or threat

Etymology

From 'defenceless' (without protection) + '-ness' (forming a noun).
