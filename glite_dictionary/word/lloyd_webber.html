Lloyd Webber

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɔɪd ˈwɛbər / (AmE), / ˈlɔɪd ˈwɛbə / (BrE)
(Andrew) Lloyd Webber, an English composer known for his successful musicals such as 'The Phantom of the Opera' and 'Cats'.
English composer of musicals

Examples

Lloyd Webber wrote the music for some of the most popular musicals of all time.
Many theater lovers admire the works of Lloyd Webber and attend his shows worldwide.
Lloyd Webber collaborated with Tim Rice on several successful productions.
