metrosexual

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛtroʊˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtrəʊˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
A heterosexual man who spends a significant amount of time and money on his appearance and lifestyle, typically following trends in fashion and grooming.
fashion-conscious heterosexual man

Examples

As a self-confessed metrosexual, he never misses a new skincare launch.
Some people still stereotype a metrosexual as being vain, but he just likes to look good.
The rise of the metrosexual has influenced men's fashion brands worldwide.

Etymology

Blend of 'metropolitan' and 'heterosexual'; popularized in the 1990s.
