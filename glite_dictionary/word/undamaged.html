undamaged

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdæmɪdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdamɪdʒd / (BrE)
not harmed or spoiled; remaining in original, sound condition.
not harmed or spoiled

Examples

Despite the accident, the package arrived undamaged.
The painting remained undamaged after the fire, astonishing everyone.
Please return the item undamaged for a full refund.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: damaged - harmed or spoiled; not in original condition

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + damaged (past participle of damage).
