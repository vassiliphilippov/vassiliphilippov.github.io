fogbank

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔɡˌbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡˌbaŋk / (BrE)
a large, dense mass of fog, especially as seen on the sea or at sea from a distance
large mass of sea fog

Examples

A thick fogbank obscured the coastline from the sailors' view.
We could see a distant fogbank rolling in over the ocean.
The lighthouse beam disappeared into the fogbank, leaving the harbor shrouded in mist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fog - thick cloud of tiny water droplets near the ground or sea
