roller-skater

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊlər ˌskeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə ˌskeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who roller skates, especially as a sport or recreational activity
person who roller skates

Examples

The roller-skater glided gracefully across the park path.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a professional roller-skater.
Several roller-skaters participated in the city marathon this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skater - a person who skates on ice or roller skates
