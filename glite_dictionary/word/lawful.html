lawful

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɔfəɫ / (AmE), / ˈlɔːf(ə)l / (BrE)
Allowed by law; legal; conforming to the law.
permitted by law
legal

Examples

Only lawful residents are entitled to these benefits.
The committee insisted on lawful procedures throughout the investigation.
He assured everyone that their protest would remain completely lawful.

Synset Examples

lawful methods of dissent

Related Terms

  • Antonym: illegal - forbidden by law; not lawful
  • Synonym: legal - allowed by law

Etymology

From Old English 'lagu' (law) + '-ful' (full of).

recognized or permitted by law

Examples
