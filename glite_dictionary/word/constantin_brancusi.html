Constantin Brancusi

1. PROPN

/ kɑn.stænˈtin brɒnˈkuː.si / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.stænˈtiːn ˌbrænˈkuː.ʃi / (BrE)
(1876–1957) A Romanian sculptor renowned for his pioneering abstract sculptures, often inspired by animal forms.
Romanian sculptor known for abstract works

Examples

Constantin Brancusi revolutionized modern sculpture with his abstract interpretations of natural forms.
The museum featured a major exhibition of Constantin Brancusi's works.
Many consider Constantin Brancusi a leading figure in twentieth-century art.
