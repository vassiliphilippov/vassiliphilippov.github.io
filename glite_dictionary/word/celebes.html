Celebes

1. PROPN

/ səˈliːbiːz / (AmE), / səˈliːbiːz / (BrE)
a mountainous island in eastern Indonesia, now more commonly known as Sulawesi
island in eastern Indonesia (now Sulawesi)

Examples

The island of Celebes is famous for its unique biodiversity, including many endemic species.
European explorers first reached Celebes in the early 16th century.
Coffee from Celebes has long been prized for its distinctive flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sulawesi - current Indonesian name for Celebes, the same island

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Celebes', the name given by early European navigators.
