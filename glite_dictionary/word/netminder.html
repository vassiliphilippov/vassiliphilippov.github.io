netminder

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛtˌmaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌmaɪndə / (BrE)
a soccer or hockey player whose job is to protect the goal from being scored on; a goalkeeper.
goalkeeper in soccer or hockey

Examples

The team's netminder made an incredible save in the final seconds of the game.
When the defense failed, the netminder stepped up to block the shot.
The coach praised his rookie netminder for keeping the score close.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goalkeeper - player who prevents the ball or puck from entering the goal
  • Synonym: goaltender - player who protects the goal in ice hockey

Etymology

From 'net' (goal net) + 'minder' (someone who minds or guards).
