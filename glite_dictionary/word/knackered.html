knackered

1. ADJ

/ ˈnækərd / (AmE), / ˈnækəd / (BrE)
Extremely tired or exhausted.
extremely tired; exhausted
informal british

Examples

After running the marathon, I was completely knackered and could barely stand.
He looked knackered after working a double shift at the hospital.
By the end of the week, everyone felt knackered from all the late nights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - extremely tired; lacking energy
  • Antonym: energized - full of energy and enthusiasm

Etymology

From the British slang 'knacker', referring to someone who slaughters old horses, suggesting something worn out or useless.
