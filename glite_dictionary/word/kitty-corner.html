kitty-corner

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪtiˌkɔrnər / (AmE), / ˈkɪtiˌkɔːnə / (BrE)
In a position diagonally opposite, especially across an intersection or space; on a diagonal line from something else.
diagonally opposite; on a diagonal line
informal american

Examples

The bakery is kitty-corner from the post office.
A café sits kitty-corner to the bank on the main street.
She parked her car kitty-corner from the library entrance.

Synset Examples

set off in a catty-corner direction across the vacant lot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diagonal - joining two nonadjacent corners of a figure or space
  • Synonym: catty-corner - diagonally opposite, variant spelling

Etymology

Alteration of 'cater-cornered,' from French 'quatre' (four) and English 'corner.'
