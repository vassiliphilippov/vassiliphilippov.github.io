Magh

1. NOUN

/ mɑːɡ / (AmE), / mɑːɡ / (BrE)
The eleventh month of the Hindu calendar, typically corresponding to January in the Gregorian calendar.
eleventh month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

The festival of Makar Sankranti is celebrated in the month of Magh.
Many Hindu weddings are scheduled during Magh, a period considered auspicious for ceremonies.
In 2022, Magh began in mid-January and ended in mid-February according to the lunar calendar.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Magha', the name of a lunar asterism associated with this month.
