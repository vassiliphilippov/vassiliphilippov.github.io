Heinlein

1. PROPN

/ ˈhaɪnˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪn.laɪn / (BrE)
(1907–1988) An American writer known as one of the most influential authors of science fiction.
American science fiction writer

Examples

He considered Heinlein one of his greatest influences as a science fiction writer.
Heinlein's novels often explore complex social and political themes.
