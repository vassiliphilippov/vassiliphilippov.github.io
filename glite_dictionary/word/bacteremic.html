bacteremic

1. ADJ

/ bæk.tɚˈiː.mɪk / (AmE), / bæk.təˈriː.mɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by the presence of bacteria in the bloodstream (bacteremia)
relating to bacteremia (bacteria in the bloodstream)
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed as bacteremic following blood cultures.
Early antibiotic treatment is crucial for bacteremic infections.
Children with fever and a cough are less likely to be bacteremic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacteremia - the presence of bacteria in the blood

Etymology

From 'bacteremia', with '-ic' forming an adjective; 'bacteremia' from Greek 'bakterion' (a small stick, bacterium) and 'haima' (blood).
