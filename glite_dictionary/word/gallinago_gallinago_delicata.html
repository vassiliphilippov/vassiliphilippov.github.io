Gallinago gallinago delicata

1. NOUN

/ ɡælɪˈneɪɡoʊ ɡælɪˈneɪɡoʊ dɛlɪˈkɑːtə / (AmE), / ɡalɪˈneɪɡəʊ galɪˈneɪɡəʊ dɛlɪˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
A subspecies of snipe found in North America, commonly referred to as the American snipe.
North American subspecies of snipe

Examples

Birdwatchers often travel to wetlands in spring to observe the Gallinago gallinago delicata during its migration.
The Gallinago gallinago delicata is noted for its distinctive winnowing display in flight.
Researchers banded several Gallinago gallinago delicata to study their breeding behaviors in North America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: American snipe - Common name for Gallinago gallinago delicata

Etymology

From Latin: 'Gallinago' (a snipe), 'delicata' meaning delicate or dainty.
