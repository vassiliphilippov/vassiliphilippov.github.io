Albert Speer

1. PROPN

/ ˈæl.bɚt spɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈæl.bət spɪə / (BrE)
(1905–1981) German architect and Nazi official who served as Minister of Armaments and War Production under Adolf Hitler.
German Nazi architect and government minister

Examples

Albert Speer was known for designing monumental buildings during the Nazi regime.
Many historians debate the legacy of Albert Speer and his role in World War II.
