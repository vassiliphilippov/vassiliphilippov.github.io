rijsttaffel

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪstˌtɑːfəl / (AmE), / ˈraɪstəˌtɑːfəl / (BrE)
An Indonesian meal made up of many different dishes and sauces served with rice, usually eaten as a feast.
Indonesian meal of mixed dishes with rice

Examples

For her birthday, she invited all her friends to share a traditional rijsttaffel with dozens of flavorful dishes.
The restaurant specializes in rijsttaffel, offering a variety of Indonesian curries and side dishes.
Our experience with rijsttaffel introduced us to new spices and textures we had never tasted before.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smorgasbord - a wide variety of foods served as a meal, especially in Swedish cuisine

Etymology

From Dutch 'rijsttafel', meaning 'rice table'.
