crazily

1. ADV

/ ˈkreɪ.zə.li / (AmE), / ˈkreɪ.zɪ.li / (BrE)
In a wild, insane, or extremely foolish manner.
in an insane or wild manner

Examples

He was laughing crazily at the movie for no apparent reason.
The wind howled crazily through the abandoned building.
She danced crazily at the party, not caring who was watching.

Synset Examples

she behaved insanely
he behaves crazily when he is off his medication
the witch cackled madly
screaming dementedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insanely - in an insane or wildly foolish manner
  • Antonym: calmly - in a calm or composed manner

Etymology

From the adjective "crazy" + adverbial suffix "-ly".
