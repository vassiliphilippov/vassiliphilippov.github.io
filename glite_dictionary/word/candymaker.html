candymaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn.diˌmeɪ.kər / (AmE), / ˈkæn.diˌmeɪ.kə / (BrE)
a person who makes candies and other sweets, either professionally or as a hobby
person who makes candies

Examples

The local candymaker created a new variety of chocolate truffles for the holidays.
As a skilled candymaker, she knows exactly how to temper chocolate to perfection.
The children loved visiting the old-fashioned shop run by the town's famous candymaker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confectioner - a person who makes or sells sweets and chocolates

Etymology

From 'candy' + 'maker', both of English origin.
