weightily

1. ADV

/ ˈweɪ.t̬ɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈweɪ.tɪ.li / (BrE)
In a solemn, serious, or significant manner.
in a solemn or serious manner

Examples

She spoke weightily about the challenges facing the company.
The judge considered the evidence weightily before announcing his verdict.
The committee commented weightily on the importance of ethics in research.

Synset Examples

the speech was weighty and it was weightily delivered

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seriously - in a serious manner
  • Antonym: lightly - in a light or unserious manner
Source: WordNet

2. ADV

/ ˈweɪtɪli / (AmE), / ˈweɪtɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that is very heavy or suggests great weight.
in a heavy or ponderous manner

Examples

She moved weightily across the dance floor, her steps slow and deliberate.
The elephant trod weightily through the mud, sinking with each stride.
After the long hike, he collapsed weightily into the nearest chair.

Synset Examples

she moved weightily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heavily - in a heavy manner

Etymology

From 'weighty' + '-ly'.
