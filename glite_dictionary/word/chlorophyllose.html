chlorophyllose

1. ADJ

/ klɔːˈrɑːfɪˌloʊs / (AmE), / klɔːˈrɪfɪˌləʊs / (BrE)
Relating to or containing chlorophyll; having the nature of or being like chlorophyll.
relating to chlorophyll

Examples

Leaves display a distinct chlorophyllose hue during the peak of the growing season.
The chlorophyllose content of the algae was measured in the laboratory.
Certain fungi exhibit chlorophyllose tissues, unlike most of their relatives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chlorophyllous - relating to or containing chlorophyll

Etymology

From 'chlorophyll' + '-ose', forming adjectives relating to the presence of chlorophyll.
