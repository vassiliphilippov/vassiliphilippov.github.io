salacity

1. NOUN

/ səˈlæsəti / (AmE), / səˈlæsəti / (BrE)
the quality of expressing or showing obscene or lascivious behavior
obscene or lascivious behavior

Examples

The film was criticized for the salacity of certain scenes.
His speech was marked by unexpected salacity that shocked the audience.
Tabloids are often filled with stories of celebrity salacity and scandal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lewdness - the state or quality of being lewd or sexually indecent
  • Antonym: modesty - the quality of being modest or reserved, especially in behavior or speech

Etymology

From Latin 'salacitas', from 'salax' (lustful).
