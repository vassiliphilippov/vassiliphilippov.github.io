signalwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡnəlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female railway worker responsible for operating signals and managing points in a railway yard.
female railway signals operator

Examples

The signalwoman ensured the train switched tracks safely.
After years of experience, she was promoted to chief signalwoman in the yard.
As the only signalwoman on her team, she handled every shift with professionalism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signalman - a railroad employee in charge of signals and points
  • Synonym: signaller - a person responsible for operating signals (gender-neutral)

Etymology

Formed from 'signalman' by replacing 'man' with 'woman'.
