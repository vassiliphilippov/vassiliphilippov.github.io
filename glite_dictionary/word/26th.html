26th

1. ADJ

/ twɛn.tiˈsɪksθ / (AmE), / twɛn.tiˈsɪksθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the twenty-fifth in position; being number twenty-six in a sequence.
ordinal number; next after twenty-fifth

Examples

Her birthday falls on the 26th of May every year.
The team finished in 26th place at the competition.
He celebrated his 26th wedding anniversary last weekend.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 25th - coming next after the twenty-fourth in position
  • Degree Higher: 27th - coming next after the twenty-sixth in position
