trigon

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪˌɡɑn / (AmE), / ˈtrʌɪɡɒn / (BrE)
(Astrology) One of four groups in the zodiac, each containing three signs that are 120 degrees apart from each other.
group of three zodiac signs in astrology

Examples

In traditional astrology, a trigon represents an elemental grouping such as fire or water signs.
Each trigon consists of three signs spaced evenly around the zodiac wheel.
The fire trigon includes Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius.

Etymology

From Latin trigonum (triangle), from Greek trigōnon (triangle).

three-sided polygon; triangle
