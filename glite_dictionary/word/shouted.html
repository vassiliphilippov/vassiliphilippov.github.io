shouted

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃaʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊtɪd / (BrE)
(of words or speech) spoken loudly with force or emotion, especially in a shout.
spoken forcefully in a shout

Examples

The coach's shouted instructions could be heard across the entire field.
Everyone was startled by her shouted response during the meeting.
His shouted warnings saved the hikers from danger.

Synset Examples

his shouted words of encouragement could be heard over the crowd noises

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yelled - spoken or uttered with a yell
  • Antonym: whispered - spoken very softly or quietly

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'shout', from Middle English 'shouten'.
