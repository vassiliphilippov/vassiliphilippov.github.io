Yamamoto

1. PROPN

/ jɑːməˈmoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / jæməˈməʊtəʊ / (BrE)
(1884–1943) Japanese admiral who planned the attack on Pearl Harbor during World War II.
Japanese admiral; planned Pearl Harbor attack

Examples

Yamamoto was instrumental in devising Japan's naval strategies during World War II.
The actions of Admiral Yamamoto had a significant impact on the outcome of the Pacific War.
Historians continue to debate Yamamoto's legacy as a military leader.

Etymology

Japanese family name.
