John Roebling

1. PROPN

/ dʒɑn ˈroʊblɪŋ / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈrəʊblɪŋ / (BrE)
(1806–1869) United States engineer, born in Germany, who designed and began construction of the Brooklyn Bridge.
German-American engineer; designed Brooklyn Bridge

Examples

John Roebling was the visionary engineer behind the Brooklyn Bridge.
The innovative ideas of John Roebling revolutionized suspension bridge construction in the 19th century.
Many engineering students study the work of John Roebling for inspiration.
