Assuan

1. PROPN

/ əˈswɑn / (AmE), / əˈswɑːn / (BrE)
an ancient city on the Nile in southern Egypt, known for its historical significance and location near the Aswan dams.
ancient city on the Nile in Egypt

Examples

Many tourists visit Assuan to see the impressive ancient temples nearby.
The construction of the dam near Assuan changed the landscape of the Nile.
Assuan has long been a strategic site for controlling trade along the Nile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aswan - modern name for the ancient city of Assuan in Egypt

Etymology

Variant of 'Aswan' from Arabic 'Aswān'.
