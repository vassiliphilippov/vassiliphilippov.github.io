salable

1. ADJ

/ ˈseɪləbəl / (AmE), / ˈseɪləbəl / (BrE)
Able to be sold; suitable or fit for sale.
able to be sold

Examples

The store had to remove products that were not salable due to damage.
Only salable items should be displayed on the shelves.
Her paintings became highly salable after her first major exhibition.

Synset Examples

saleable at a low price

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unsalable - not able to be sold
  • Synonym: marketable - able or suitable to be sold or marketed

Etymology

From Latin 'salre' (to leap), via obsolete French 'salable', later influenced by 'sale'.
