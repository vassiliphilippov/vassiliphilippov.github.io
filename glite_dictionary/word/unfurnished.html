unfurnished

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɝːnɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɜːnɪʃt / (BrE)
(of a house, apartment, or room) not supplied with furniture.
without furniture

Examples

They decided to rent an unfurnished apartment to save on costs.
The office was still unfurnished when the new employees arrived.
We moved into an unfurnished house and had to buy all new furniture.

Synset Examples

an unfurnished apartment

Related Terms

  • Antonym: furnished - (of a place) supplied with furniture

Etymology

From un- (not) + furnish (from Old French fournir, meaning 'to furnish, supply').
