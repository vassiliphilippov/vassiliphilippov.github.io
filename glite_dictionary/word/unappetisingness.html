unappetisingness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnˈæpəˌtaɪzɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈæpɪtʌɪzɪŋnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being unappetising; lacking appeal to the appetite.
state of being unappetising

Examples

The unappetisingness of the food made everyone lose interest in dinner.
Despite the restaurant's reputation, the unappetisingness of the meal was disappointing.
He complained about the unappetisingness of the leftovers in the fridge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unappetising - not appealing to the appetite
  • Antonym: appetisingness - the property of stimulating the appetite

Etymology

From 'unappetising' + '-ness', where 'unappetising' means not appealing to the appetite, from 'appetising' + prefix 'un-'.
