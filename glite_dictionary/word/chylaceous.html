chylaceous

1. ADJ

/ kaɪˈleɪʃəs / (AmE), / kaɪˈleɪʃəs / (BrE)
Consisting of chyle or having the properties of chyle.
relating to or resembling chyle
medical

Examples

The surgeon noted the presence of chylaceous fluid in the abdominal cavity.
A chylaceous effusion may indicate damage to the lymphatic system.
Laboratory analysis confirmed that the sample was distinctly chylaceous in appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chylous - relating to or resembling chyle
  • Degree Lower: serous - relating to serum or resembling serum

Etymology

From chyle + -aceous (from Latin -āceus, forming adjectives meaning 'of the nature of').
