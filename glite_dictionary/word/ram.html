Ram

1. NOUN

/ ræm / (AmE), / ræm / (BrE)
The first sign of the zodiac, also known as Aries, which the sun enters at the vernal equinox (about March 21 to April 19).
first zodiac sign (Aries)

Examples

In astrology, being born under the Ram is said to make a person energetic and pioneering.
She reads horoscopes daily and was thrilled to learn her sign is the Ram.
People born under the Ram are thought to be bold and confident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aries - the first sign of the zodiac, represented by a ram

Etymology

From Latin 'aries' meaning ram, used as a symbol for the zodiac sign.

2. NOUN

/ ræm / (AmE), / ræm / (BrE)
(Astrology) A person born when the sun is in the sign of Aries, traditionally said to have characteristics associated with that sign.
person born under Aries (astrology)

Examples

Beatrice is a proud Ram, always diving headfirst into new adventures.
As a Ram, he is known for his enthusiasm and leadership qualities.
The horoscope said that every Ram could expect big changes this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aries - the zodiac sign symbolized by a ram

Etymology

From the symbolic association of Aries (Latin for 'ram') with the constellation and zodiac sign Aries.
