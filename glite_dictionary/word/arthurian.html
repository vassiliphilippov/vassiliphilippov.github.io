Arthurian

1. ADJ

/ ɑːrˈθʊriən / (AmE), / ɑːˈθjʊəriən / (BrE)
relating to King Arthur or the legends of the Knights of the Round Table
relating to King Arthur

Examples

Many scholars study the Arthurian legends to better understand medieval literature.
The film presents an Arthurian world filled with magic and chivalry.
Her thesis examines the influence of Arthurian myths on modern fantasy novels.

Etymology

From King Arthur, legendary British leader, plus the suffix -ian.
