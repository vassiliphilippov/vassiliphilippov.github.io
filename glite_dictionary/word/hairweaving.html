hairweaving

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌwiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈheəˌwiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of integrating a hairpiece with someone's own hair by interweaving them to create a natural appearance
integration of a hairpiece with real hair

Examples

After years of thinning hair, she decided to try hairweaving to boost her confidence.
Many salons now offer advanced hairweaving techniques that look incredibly natural.
He specializes in hairweaving for clients experiencing hair loss due to medical treatments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair extension - artificial hair added to natural hair for length or fullness
  • Degree Lower: wig - covering of artificial or natural hair worn on the head

Etymology

From 'hair' + 'weaving' (process of interlacing threads), reflecting the technique's method.
