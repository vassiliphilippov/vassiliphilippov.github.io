nonretractable

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnrɪˈtræktəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnrɪˈtræktəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be drawn back or withdrawn; incapable of being retracted.
impossible to retract

Examples

The device features a nonretractable cord for safety reasons.
Some species of cat have nonretractable claws.
His shield was nonretractable, making it difficult to transport.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: retractable - able to be drawn back or withdrawn

Etymology

Formed from 'non-' (not) + 'retractable' (able to be withdrawn or pulled back).
