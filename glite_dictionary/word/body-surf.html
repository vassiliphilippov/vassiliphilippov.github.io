to body-surf

1. VERB

/ ˈbɑdi ˌsɝf / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌsɜːf / (BrE)
to ride on the crest of a wave without using a surfboard, propelled only by one's own body.
ride a wave without a board

Examples

He loves to body-surf when the waves are high in the summer.
Do you know how to body-surf safely at the beach?
Many children enjoy body-surfing near the shore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surf - to ride on a wave using a surfboard
  • Synonym: bodysurfing - the activity of body-surfing; riding waves without a surfboard

Etymology

From 'body' (the human form) + 'surf' (to ride on waves), patterned after 'surf' in surfing terminology.
