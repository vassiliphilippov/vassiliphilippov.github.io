disclike

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɪsˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɪsˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having a flat, circular shape; resembling a disc.
having a flat, circular shape

Examples

The microorganism has a disclike structure visible under the microscope.
The shield was disclike in appearance, making it easy to spot among other artifacts.
Astronomers observed a disclike formation of dust around the distant star.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disc-shaped - shaped like a disc; flat and circular

Etymology

Formed from 'disc' + 'like', meaning similar to a disc.
