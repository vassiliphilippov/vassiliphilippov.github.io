horrified

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɔr.əˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɒr.ɪˌfaɪd / (BrE)
experiencing or showing intense shock, fear, or disgust
stricken with horror or fear

Examples

She was horrified to discover the mess in the kitchen.
The news left everyone horrified and speechless.
He looked horrified when he heard the terrible story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrified - extremely frightened
  • Antonym: delighted - experiencing great pleasure

Etymology

From Latin 'horrere' (to shudder, bristle) via French and English 'horror'.
