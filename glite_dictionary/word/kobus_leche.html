Kobus leche

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊbəs ˈleɪkeɪ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊbəs ˈlɛtʃiː / (BrE)
A tawny-colored African antelope species that inhabits wet grassy plains; also known as the lechwe.
tawny African antelope species

Examples

The Kobus leche is often seen grazing near rivers and floodplains in southern Africa.
Wildlife reserves are working to protect the threatened Kobus leche from habitat loss.
A guide pointed out a herd of Kobus leche in the distance as we drove through the savannah.

Etymology

From modern Latin, genus and species names coined in scientific taxonomy.
