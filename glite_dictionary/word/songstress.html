songstress

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔŋ.strəs / (AmE), / ˈsɒŋ.strəs / (BrE)
a female singer, especially one who performs popular songs
female singer, especially of popular songs

Examples

The acclaimed songstress captivated the audience with her powerful vocals and heartfelt lyrics.
As a young songstress, she dreamed of performing on big stages across the world.
The magazine featured an exclusive interview with the rising songstress about her new album.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: singer - a person who sings, especially professionally
  • Synonym: songster - a singer, especially a male or unspecified gender

Etymology

Formed from 'song' and the feminine suffix '-stress', modelled on words like 'poetess'.
