adenocarcinomatous

1. ADJ

/ ˌæd.ə.noʊˌkɑr.sɪˈnoʊ.mə.təs / (AmE), / ˌæd.ɪ.nəʊˌkɑː.sɪˈnəʊ.mə.təs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an adenocarcinoma (a type of malignant tumor formed from glandular structures)
relating to adenocarcinoma tumor
medical

Examples

The biopsy revealed adenocarcinomatous changes in the lung tissue.
The patient was diagnosed with an adenocarcinomatous lesion in the colon.
Pathologists noted the adenocarcinomatous growth pattern under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adenocarcinoma - a malignant tumor formed from glandular structures in epithelial tissue

Etymology

Formed from 'adenocarcinoma' (from Greek 'adēn' gland + 'karkinos' crab, cancer + '-oma' tumor) and suffix '-ous'.
