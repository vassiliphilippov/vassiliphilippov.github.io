Bill Gates

1. PROPN

/ bɪl ɡeɪts / (AmE), / bɪl ɡeɪts / (BrE)
A prominent American computer entrepreneur, co-founder of Microsoft Corporation, and major philanthropist, known for being one of the world's richest individuals.
American computer entrepreneur and philanthropist

Examples

Bill Gates founded Microsoft and became one of the world's wealthiest people.
Many people admire Bill Gates not only for his business success but for his charitable work.
Bill Gates has donated billions of dollars to global health and education initiatives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Steve Jobs - American computer entrepreneur and co-founder of Apple Inc.
  • Synonym: philanthropist - a person who seeks to promote the welfare of others, especially by donating money
