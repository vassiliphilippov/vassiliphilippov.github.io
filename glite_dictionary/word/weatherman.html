weatherman

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛð.ɚ.mæn / (AmE), / ˈwɛð.ə.mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to forecast and report the weather, especially on television or radio
male weather forecaster

Examples

The weatherman predicted heavy rain for the weekend.
Every morning, I watch the weatherman give the latest forecasts on TV.
The weatherman warned people to stay indoors during the storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather forecaster - a person who predicts the weather
  • Antonym: weatherwoman - a woman whose job is to forecast and report the weather
