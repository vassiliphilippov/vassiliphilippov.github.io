oxheart

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑksˌhɑrt / (AmE), / ˈɒksˌhɑːt / (BrE)
a cultivated variety of sweet cherry with large, heart-shaped, juicy fruit
a type of sweet, heart-shaped cherry

Examples

The farmer picked ripe oxheart cherries from the tree for the local market.
Her favorite dessert is a tart made with fresh oxheart cherries.
In early summer, oxheart cherries are often featured at fruit festivals.

Etymology

Named for its resemblance in shape to an ox's heart.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɑksˌhɑrt / (AmE), / ˈɒksˌhɑːt / (BrE)
A large, heart-shaped sweet cherry with soft flesh, often used for eating fresh or canning.
large heart-shaped sweet cherry

Examples

The farmer displayed a basket of ripe oxheart cherries at the market.
I prefer oxheart cherries because of their size and juicy texture.
The recipe called for a pound of fresh oxheart cherries to make the jam.
