Saraswati

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɑːrəˈswɑːti / (AmE), / ˌsærəˈswɑːti / (BrE)
(in Hinduism) the goddess of learning, wisdom, music, and the arts.
Hindu goddess of learning and arts

Examples

Many students pray to Saraswati before their exams for wisdom and success.
The festival of Saraswati Puja is celebrated with music and art performances across India.
In Hindu mythology, Saraswati is often depicted holding a veena and sacred texts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sarasvati - Another transliteration of Saraswati, Hindu goddess of knowledge and arts

Etymology

From Sanskrit सरस्वती (Sarasvatī), the name of the goddess.
