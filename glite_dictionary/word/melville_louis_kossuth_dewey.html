Melville Louis Kossuth Dewey

1. PROPN

/ mɛlˈvɪl ˈluːɪs kɒˈsuːθ ˈduːi / (AmE), / mɛlˈvɪl ˈluːɪs kɒˈsuːθ ˈdjuːi / (BrE)
(1851–1931) United States librarian known for creating the Dewey Decimal Classification system.
U.S. librarian; creator of Dewey Decimal System

Examples

Melville Louis Kossuth Dewey revolutionized library organization with his decimal system.
Many librarians consider Melville Louis Kossuth Dewey one of the most influential figures in their field.
The legacy of Melville Louis Kossuth Dewey continues in modern library science curricula.
