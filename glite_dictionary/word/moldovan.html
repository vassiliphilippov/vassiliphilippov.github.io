Moldovan

1. ADJ

/ moʊlˈdoʊvən / (AmE), / mɒlˈdəʊvən / (BrE)
of or relating to Moldova, its people, or its culture
relating to Moldova or its people

Examples

The Moldovan cuisine is diverse and influenced by neighboring countries.
She enjoyed listening to traditional Moldovan folk music at the festival.
A Moldovan artist will be exhibiting his paintings in London next month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Moldavia - former historical region related to modern Moldova

Etymology

From Moldova, the name of the country in Eastern Europe, with English adjectival suffix -an.
