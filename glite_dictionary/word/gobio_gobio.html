Gobio gobio

1. NOUN

/ ˌgoʊ.bi.oʊ ˈgoʊ.bi.oʊ / (AmE), / ˌgəʊ.bɪ.əʊ ˈgəʊ.bɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
a small slender European freshwater fish, also known as the gudgeon, often used as bait by anglers
a small European freshwater fish (gudgeon)

Examples

Anglers frequently use Gobio gobio as live bait for larger predatory fish.
The clear streams of central Europe are home to the Gobio gobio.
During the biology field trip, students identified Gobio gobio among the local fish species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gudgeon - another name for Gobio gobio, a small freshwater fish

Etymology

Scientific name from Latin, where 'Gobio' refers to gudgeon.
