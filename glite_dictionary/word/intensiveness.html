intensiveness

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɛnsɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being intense or involving a high degree or concentration
the quality of being intense

Examples

The intensiveness of the training program surprised all the new employees.
Many students struggled with the intensiveness of the summer course.
The intensiveness of her studies paid off when she graduated with top honors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensity - the quality of being intense
  • Antonym: mildness - the quality or state of being mild
  • Degree Higher: intensity - the quality of being intense

Etymology

From 'intensive' + '-ness', with 'intensive' from Latin 'intensus' (stretched, strained).
