middle-of-the-road

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɪd.əl.əv.ðəˈroʊd / (AmE), / ˌmɪd.əl.əv.ðəˈrəʊd / (BrE)
not holding extreme views or supporting either side strongly; moderate in views, policies, or approach, especially in politics.
moderate; not extreme in views

Examples

The party adopted a middle-of-the-road stance to appeal to a wider range of voters.
His opinions are generally middle-of-the-road, avoiding the far left or right.
She preferred a middle-of-the-road approach when deciding on policy changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moderate - holding moderate views or positions
