incestuously

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈsɛstʃuəsli / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛstjʊəsli / (BrE)
In a manner involving or relating to incest; by means of incest.
in an incestuous manner

Examples

The children were incestuously conceived, which led to legal complications.
Some monarchies in history were criticized for incestuously marrying within the family.
The novel explores a community where members incestuously maintain powerful family ties.

Synset Examples

incestuously conceived

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incestuous - involving or relating to incest

Etymology

From 'incestuous' + '-ly'. 'Incestuous' from Latin 'incestuōsus' (unlawful, incestuous) from 'incestum'.
