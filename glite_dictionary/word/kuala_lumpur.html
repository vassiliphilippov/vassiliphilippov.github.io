Kuala Lumpur

1. PROPN

/ kuˈɑlə luˈmpʊr / (AmE), / kwɑːlə lʊmˈpʊə / (BrE)
the largest city in Malaysia, formerly the national capital until 2005, now the seat of the Malaysian Parliament
largest city in Malaysia

Examples

Thousands of tourists visit Kuala Lumpur each year for its lively markets and modern skyline.
The Petronas Towers are among the most famous landmarks in Kuala Lumpur.
He moved to Kuala Lumpur for a job opportunity after graduating from university.

Etymology

From Malay, meaning 'muddy confluence.'
