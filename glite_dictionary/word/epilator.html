epilator

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛpəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪleɪtə / (BrE)
a device used to remove unwanted hair from the body by mechanically grasping and pulling it out
device for removing unwanted hair

Examples

She bought a new epilator to keep her legs smooth for the summer.
Using an epilator can be more effective than shaving because it removes hair at the root.
Some people find the sensation of an epilator uncomfortable at first, but it becomes easier over time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: depilatory - a substance or device used to remove hair

Etymology

From Latin 'epilare', meaning 'to remove hair', from 'e-' (out) + 'pilus' (hair).
