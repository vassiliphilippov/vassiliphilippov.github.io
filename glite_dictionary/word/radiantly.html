radiantly

1. ADV

/ ˈreɪ.di.ənt.li / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.di.ənt.li / (BrE)
In a bright, joyful, or glowing manner; emitting radiance or happiness.
in a bright, joyful manner

Examples

She beamed radiantly when she received the good news.
The garden glowed radiantly in the morning sunlight.
He smiled radiantly as he accepted the award.

Synset Examples

the bride smiled radiantly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brilliantly - in a very bright or dazzling manner
  • Antonym: dull - lacking brightness or vividness (adverb: dully)

Etymology

From radiant + -ly; radiant from Latin radiāns (shining), -ly forming adverbs.
