visibleness

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪzəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈvɪzəbəlnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or degree of being visible; the state of being able to be seen.
quality of being visible

Examples

The visibleness of the mountain peak depended on the weather conditions.
Poor lighting affected the visibleness of the warning signs along the road.
He adjusted the font size to enhance the visibleness of the text on the screen.

Synset Examples

low visibility caused by fog

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visibility - the state of being able to see or be seen

Etymology

Formed from 'visible' + '-ness'; 'visible' from Latin 'visibilis' (that can be seen).
