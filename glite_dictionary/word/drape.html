drape

1. NOUN

/ dreɪp / (AmE), / dreɪp / (BrE)
A sterile cloth used to cover a patient during a medical examination or surgery to reduce the risk of contamination.
sterile medical covering for patients
medical

Examples

The surgeon carefully placed a drape over the patient's abdomen before beginning the procedure.
Nurses ensured all drapes were properly sterilized before the operation.
A sterile drape is essential to maintain a clean field during surgery.
