Kelantan

1. PROPN

/ kəˈlɑːnˌtɑn / (AmE), / kɛˈlæntɑːn / (BrE)
a state in the northeast of Peninsular Malaysia, known as a sultanate and one of the 13 states forming the Federation of Malaysia
state in northeast Malaysia

Examples

She traveled to Kelantan to explore its traditional arts and crafts.
Kelantan is known for its unique culture and delicious local cuisine.
The annual kite-flying festival in Kelantan attracts visitors from all over the country.

state of northeastern Malaysia

Examples

The food in Kelantan is known for its unique blend of Thai and Malay flavors.
