melted

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛltɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɛltɪd / (BrE)
having changed from a solid to a liquid state, usually by heating
changed from solid to liquid

Examples

The cheese became gooey and delicious when it was melted on the pizza.
They used melted chocolate to coat the strawberries for dessert.
The path was slippery with melted snow after the sun came out.

Synset Examples

rivers filled to overflowing by melted snow

Related Terms

  • Antonym: solid - having a firm, stable form; not liquid or gas
  • Degree Higher: liquid - flowing freely like water, not solid or gas
