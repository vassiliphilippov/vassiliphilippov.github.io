intraspecies

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntrəˈspiːʃiz / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈspiːʃiːz / (BrE)
Occurring or existing within a single species; involving members of the same species.
within a single species
medical

Examples

The researchers studied intraspecies communication among dolphins in coastal waters.
Competition for food is often more intense in intraspecies interactions than between different species.
Certain diseases can spread quickly through intraspecies contact in dense animal populations.

Synset Examples

intraspecific competition

Related Terms

  • Antonym: interspecies - occurring or existing between different species

Etymology

From Latin 'intra-' (within) and 'species' (appearance, kind, biological taxon).
