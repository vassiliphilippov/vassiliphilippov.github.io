teetotalist

1. NOUN

/ tiˈtoʊtəlɪst / (AmE), / tiːˈtəʊtəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who abstains completely from alcoholic drinks
person who abstains from alcohol

Examples

As a proud teetotalist, she always declined offers of wine at dinner parties.
The club attracted many teetotalists who preferred socializing without alcohol.
He became a teetotalist after witnessing the effects of alcoholism in his family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol
  • Synonym: abstainer - a person who refrains from something, especially alcohol

Etymology

From 'teetotal,' meaning total abstinence, plus '-ist' (person who practices).
