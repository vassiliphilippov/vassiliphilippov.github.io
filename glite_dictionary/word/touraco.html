touraco

1. NOUN

/ tuːˈreɪkoʊ / (AmE), / tjʊəˈrɑːkəʊ / (BrE)
A large, brightly crested bird of Africa, notable for its vivid plumage and unique coloring, belonging to the family Musophagidae.
large, crested African bird

Examples

The vibrant feathers of the touraco make it stand out among African birds.
We spotted a flock of touracos in the rainforest canopy during our safari.
A touraco flew by, its distinctive red and green wings flashing in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turaco - alternative spelling of touraco, same bird family

Etymology

From French 'touraco', probably from a West African language.
