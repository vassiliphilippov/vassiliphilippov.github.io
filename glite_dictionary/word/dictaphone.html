Dictaphone

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪktəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈdɪktəfəʊn / (BrE)
a small tape recorder used to record and play back speech, especially for making notes or dictation
device for recording dictation

Examples

The secretary used a Dictaphone to record her boss's instructions.
After speaking into the Dictaphone, he handed the tape to his assistant for transcription.
Doctors often dictate medical notes on a Dictaphone during their rounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tape recorder - a machine for recording and playing back sound on magnetic tape

Etymology

Originally a trademark from 'dictate' + 'phone', denoting a device to record spoken words.
