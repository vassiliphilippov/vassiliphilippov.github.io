Dushanbe

1. PROPN

/ duːˈʃɑːnbeɪ / (AmE), / dʊˈʃænbɛ / (BrE)
the capital city of Tajikistan, formerly known as Stalinabad from 1926 to 1991
capital of Tajikistan

Examples

I visited Dushanbe during my trip to Central Asia last year.
The conference was held in Dushanbe, attracting delegates from around the world.
After gaining independence, the city reverted to its original name, Dushanbe.

2. PROPN

the capital city of Tajikistan, formerly known as Stalinabad from 1926 to 1991
capital city of Tajikistan

Examples

She traveled to Dushanbe to attend an international conference.
After independence, Dushanbe became a vibrant cultural center in Tajikistan.
The climate of Dushanbe is characterized by hot summers and mild winters.

Etymology

From Tajik, meaning 'Monday', traditionally the day of the weekly market held in the area.
