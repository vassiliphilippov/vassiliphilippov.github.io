Hertfordshire

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɜrt.fərd.ʃɪr / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt.fəd.ʃɪə / (BrE)
a county in southern England, north of London, bordered by Bedfordshire, Cambridgeshire, Essex, and Buckinghamshire
county in southern England

Examples

She moved to Hertfordshire to enjoy a quieter countryside lifestyle.
Many historic estates can be found throughout Hertfordshire.
The train journey from London to Hertfordshire is less than an hour.

Etymology

Named after its county town Hertford, which comes from Old English 'heort ford' (hart ford, a ford frequented by deer).
