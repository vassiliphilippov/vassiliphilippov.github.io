annoyingly

1. ADV

/ əˈnɔɪ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / əˈnɔɪ.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that causes irritation or annoyance.
in an irritating or annoying way

Examples

The dog barked annoyingly every time someone walked by the house.
She annoyingly interrupted our conversation to answer her phone.
He annoyingly taps his pen during meetings, which distracts everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irritatingly - in an irritating manner
  • Antonym: pleasantly - in a pleasing or enjoyable way
