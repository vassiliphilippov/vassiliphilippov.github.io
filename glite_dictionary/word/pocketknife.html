pocketknife

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪtˌnaɪf / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪtˌnaɪf / (BrE)
A small knife with a blade that folds into the handle, designed to be carried in the pocket.
small folding knife for carrying in a pocket

Examples

He always carries a pocketknife with him when hiking in the mountains.
With a quick flick, she opened her pocketknife to cut the rope.
My grandfather gave me his old pocketknife as a birthday present.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penknife - small folding knife, often used for sharpening pens or other small tasks

Etymology

From 'pocket' (a small pouch) and 'knife', indicating a knife small enough to be kept in a pocket.
