imbibing

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈbaɪbɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪmˈbaɪbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of drinking, especially taking in liquids by drinking
the act of drinking liquids

Examples

He was criticized for his constant imbibing at social events.
The imbibing of water is essential for good health.
During the festivities, imbibing was encouraged, but moderation was advised.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drinking - the act of taking in liquid through the mouth

Etymology

From Latin 'imbibere' (to drink in).
