organisational

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃənl / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃənl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an organization or its structure.
relating to an organization
british

Examples

The company underwent a major organisational change to improve efficiency.
