nick

/ nɪk / (AmE), / nɪk / (BrE)
(of horses) to cut or reset the tail muscles, especially to make the tail more erect for appearance.

Examples

The groom decided to nick the horse's tail to achieve a more fashionable look.

Veterinarians debated whether nicking causes unnecessary pain to animals.
