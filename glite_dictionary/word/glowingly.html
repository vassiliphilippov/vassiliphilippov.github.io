glowingly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡloʊɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that expresses enthusiastic praise or admiration
in an enthusiastic or admiring manner

Examples

The author was glowingly recommended by several respected critics.
She spoke glowingly of her experiences traveling abroad.
The restaurant was glowingly reviewed in last week's newspaper.

Synset Examples

in her letter she praised him glowingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows intense enjoyment or interest
  • Antonym: critically - in a way that expresses disapproval
