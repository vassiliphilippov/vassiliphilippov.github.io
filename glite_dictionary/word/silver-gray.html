silver-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvər ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
of a grey color that resembles silver in appearance or brightness.
grey resembling silver

Examples

The cat's silver-gray fur shimmered in the sunlight.
She wore a silver-gray dress to the evening gala.
His hair turned silver-gray as he grew older.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - a color between black and white; a neutral color
  • Degree Higher: silver - a shiny, metallic gray-white color
