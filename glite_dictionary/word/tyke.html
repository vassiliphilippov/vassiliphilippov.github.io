tyke

1. NOUN

/ taɪk / (AmE), / taɪk / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Yorkshire, England (used chiefly in informal or affectionate contexts)
native of Yorkshire, England
informal british

Examples

He's a proud tyke who supports the local football team every weekend.
Many tykes cherish their strong Yorkshire identity and traditions.
Despite living in London for years, she still considers herself a tyke at heart.

Etymology

Originally a term for a dog, later applied dialectally in Yorkshire to refer to locals, especially as a nickname.
