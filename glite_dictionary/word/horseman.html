horseman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrs.mən / (AmE), / ˈhɔːs.mən / (BrE)
A man whose occupation is breeding and looking after horses.
man who breeds and cares for horses

Examples

The estate's chief horseman was responsible for the breeding program.
As a skilled horseman, he took pride in the care of the stables.
The young horseman learned everything about raising thoroughbreds from his father.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: horsewoman - a woman who breeds and cares for horses
  • Synonym: groom - a person employed to take care of horses

Etymology

From Old English horsman, from hors (horse) + man (man).

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɔːrsmən / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsmən / (BrE)
a man who rides horses well, especially as a skilled rider
a skilled male horse rider

Examples

He was known throughout the region as an exceptional horseman and trainer.
As a young horseman, he competed in many show jumping events.
The legendary horseman led his troops into battle on horseback.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equestrian - a person skilled in horseback riding

Etymology

From Middle English horsman, from horse + man.
