Mauritian

1. NOUN

/ mɔˈrɪʃən / (AmE), / mɒˈrɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Mauritius.
native or inhabitant of Mauritius

Examples

The Mauritian greeted us warmly as we arrived on the island.
Many Mauritians speak several languages fluently, including English and French.
She befriended a Mauritian during her studies abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mauritius - an island country in the Indian Ocean

Etymology

From Mauritius + -ian (suffix forming demonyms and adjectives).
