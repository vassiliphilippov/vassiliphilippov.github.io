to fellate

1. VERB

/ fəˈleɪt / (AmE), / fəˈleɪt / (BrE)
to perform oral stimulation of the penis for sexual gratification
perform oral sex on (penis)
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that to fellate means to provide oral stimulation to the penis.
Some scenes in the film were controversial because characters were shown fellating.
The expert discussed cultural attitudes toward those who fellate their partners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fellatio - oral stimulation of the penis

Etymology

From Latin 'fellare' (to suck).
