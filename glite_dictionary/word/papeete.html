Papeete

1. PROPN

/ pɑːˈpeɪ.eɪt / (AmE), / paˈpeɪ.eɪt / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of French Polynesia, located on the northwestern coast of Tahiti
capital of French Polynesia (Tahiti)

Examples

We flew into Papeete before traveling to the smaller islands of French Polynesia.
Papeete is known for its vibrant market and waterfront promenade.
Cruise ships often dock at Papeete when visiting Tahiti.

Etymology

From Tahitian Papeʻete, meaning 'water from a basket.'
