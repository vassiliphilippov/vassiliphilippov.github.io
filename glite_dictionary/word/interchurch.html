interchurch

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˈtʃɜrtʃ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈtʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
existing or occurring between different churches or denominations; involving the cooperation or interaction of more than one church
occurring between churches or denominations

Examples

The conference focused on interchurch dialogue to promote unity among Christian communities.
An interchurch committee was formed to address local social issues.
She attended an interchurch service that brought together people from multiple congregations.

Synset Examples

interchurch aid
interdenominational cooperation between Methodists and Presbyterians

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interdenominational - between or involving different religious denominations

Etymology

From inter- (between) + church.
