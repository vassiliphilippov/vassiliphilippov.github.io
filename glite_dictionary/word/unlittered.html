unlittered

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlɪtɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪtəd / (BrE)
free from litter or extraneous material; clean or uncluttered
free from litter or clutter

Examples

After the volunteers cleaned the park, the paths were completely unlittered.
She admired the unlittered design of the modern living room.
The mountain trails remain unlittered thanks to strict regulations.

Synset Examples

an uncluttered room
the unlittered shoulders of the road

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cluttered - filled with clutter; disorganized or untidy
  • Antonym: littered - covered or scattered with litter or debris

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'littered'.
