anomalist

1. NOUN

/ əˈnɑː.mə.lɪst / (AmE), / əˈnɒ.mə.lɪst / (BrE)
a person who is especially interested in anomalous, unusual, or exceptional phenomena or cases
someone interested in unusual phenomena

Examples

As an anomalist, he devoted his career to investigating unexplained disappearances.
The magazine caters to the anomalist who craves stories about UFOs and mysterious animals.
Many anomalists attend conferences to discuss their latest theories on rare phenomena.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paranormalist - someone interested in paranormal phenomena

Etymology

From anomaly + -ist, where 'anomaly' is from Greek 'anōmalia' (unevenness).
