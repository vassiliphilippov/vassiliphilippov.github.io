mommy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈmɒmi / (BrE)
(informal) A child's word for mother; mom.
informal term for mother
informal

Examples

The little boy ran to his mommy when he scraped his knee.
Could you ask your mommy if you can stay for dinner?
I made this picture for my mommy at school today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mom - informal American term for mother
  • Synonym: mum - informal British term for mother
  • Synonym: mother - female parent
  • Antonym: daddy - informal term for father

Etymology

Diminutive formation from 'mom', probably from 'mamma', imitative of a child's speech.
