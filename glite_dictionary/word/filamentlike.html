filamentlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪləməntˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɪləməntˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Thin and resembling a filament or thread; threadlike in form or appearance.
resembling a thread; threadlike

Examples

Under the microscope, the algae appeared filamentlike and stretched across the slide.
The spider produced a filamentlike silk to build its web.
Researchers observed filamentlike structures in the tissue sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: threadlike - resembling a thread; thin and long

Etymology

From 'filament' (from Latin 'filamentum', thread) + 'like'.
