unaffectedness

1. NOUN

/ ʌn.əˈfɛk.tɪd.nəs / (AmE), / ʌn.əˈfɛk.tɪd.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being natural and sincere; lack of pretence or artificiality in manner or appearance
naturalness; lack of pretence

Examples

Her unaffectedness made everyone feel comfortable and at ease during the meeting.
Despite his success, he maintained a sense of unaffectedness that won him many friends.
The actor was praised for the unaffectedness of her performance, which felt genuine and real.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naturalness - the quality of being natural or genuine
  • Antonym: artificiality - the quality of being artificial or not natural

Etymology

From un- (not) + affected + -ness (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
