Nyamwezi

1. NOUN

/ njɑːmˈweɪzi / (AmE), / njæmˈwiːzi / (BrE)
A Bantu language spoken in central Tanzania.
Bantu language of central Tanzania

Examples

Many people in central Tanzania speak Nyamwezi as their first language.
Linguists have studied the grammar and structure of Nyamwezi extensively.
She learned to greet local villagers in Nyamwezi during her stay in Tanzania.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Swahili - another Bantu language of East Africa

Etymology

From the Swahili 'WaNyamwezi,' meaning 'people of the moon.'
