tsoris

1. NOUN

/ ˈtsɔːrɪs / (AmE), / ˈtsɒrɪs / (BrE)
trouble, suffering, or distress; personal difficulties or problems, especially of a persistent or aggravating nature.
trouble or distress
informal

Examples

After all the tsoris with his job, he finally decided to change careers.
We've had enough tsoris this year to last a lifetime.
She called her friend to vent about the tsoris her family was going through.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trouble - difficulty or distress
  • Synonym: misery - a state or feeling of great physical or mental distress

Etymology

From Yiddish צוריס ‎(tsóres), meaning 'troubles', itself from Hebrew צָרוֹת ‎(tsarót, 'troubles').
