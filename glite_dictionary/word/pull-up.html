pull-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊl ʌp / (AmE), / ˈpʊl ʌp / (BrE)
An exercise in which a person pulls themselves up while hanging from a horizontal bar until their chin is level with the bar.
exercise lifting oneself on a bar

Examples

He can do ten pull-ups without stopping.
She improves her strength by adding more pull-ups to her workout routine.
At the gym, I watched him perform a perfect pull-up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chin-up - similar exercise with palms facing toward you
