Tom Thumb

1. PROPN

/ tɑm θʌm / (AmE), / tɒm θʌm / (BrE)
an imaginary character in English folklore, famous for being extremely small in size, no taller than his father's thumb
tiny hero of English folklore

Examples

As a child, he loved hearing stories about Tom Thumb and his adventures with giants.
The play was based on the tale of Tom Thumb, thrilling both children and adults in the audience.
In literature, Tom Thumb represents the triumph of cleverness over size.
