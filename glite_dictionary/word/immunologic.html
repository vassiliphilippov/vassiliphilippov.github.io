immunologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪmjənəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪmjʊnəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to immunology or the immune system
relating to immunology
medical

Examples

The patient showed an immunologic response to the vaccine.
Researchers are studying the immunologic mechanisms behind allergies.
Some diseases are caused by abnormal immunologic reactions in the body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immunological - relating to immunology (variant form)

Etymology

From immune + -logic (from Greek -logikos, relating to study or science), after immunology.
