transphilic

1. ADJ

/ trænsˈfɪlɪk / (AmE), / tranzˈfɪlɪk / (BrE)
supportive of transgender people or transgender rights
supportive of transgender rights

Examples

Her transphilic stance has made her a prominent figure in the LGBTQ+ community.
The new policy reflects a transphilic perspective, ensuring equal rights for all.
Many activists praise the organization's transphilic initiatives and outreach programs.

Synset Examples

Her transphilic views are evident in her writings and speeches.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: transphobic - having or showing a dislike of or prejudice against transgender people

Etymology

Formed from 'trans-' (relating to transgender) and '-philic' (from Greek 'philos', meaning 'loving' or 'fond of').
