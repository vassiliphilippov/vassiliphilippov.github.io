dermatologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɜr.mə.təˈlɑ.dʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌdɜː.mə.təˈlɒ.dʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study or treatment of the skin and its diseases
relating to skin or dermatology
medical

Examples

The patient was referred to a dermatologic specialist for an evaluation of her rash.
Several dermatologic treatments are available for persistent acne.
This clinic is well known for its advanced dermatologic procedures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dermatological - relating to dermatology (variant spelling)

Etymology

From Greek 'derma' (skin) + '-logic', from 'logia' (study).
