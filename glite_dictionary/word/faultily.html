faultily

1. ADV

/ ˈfɔːl.tɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈfɔːl.tɪ.li / (BrE)
In a mistaken, incorrect, or defective manner.
in a mistaken or incorrect manner

Examples

The machine operated faultily due to a manufacturing defect.
He was faultily blamed for the error, even though he was not responsible.
The report was faultily compiled, resulting in several inaccuracies.

Synset Examples

these statements were faultily attributed to me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incorrectly - in an incorrect or mistaken way
  • Antonym: correctly - in an accurate or proper way

Etymology

From 'faulty' + '-ly'; 'faulty' from Old French 'faute' (fault, lack).
