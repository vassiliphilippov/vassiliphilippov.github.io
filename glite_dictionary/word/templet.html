templet

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛm.plɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɛm.plɪt / (BrE)
A model or pattern used as a standard for making comparisons or copying objects.
model or standard for comparison

Examples

The architect used a templet to ensure each window was identical.
This sculpture was made by following the templet provided by the artist.
You should check your work against the original templet for accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: template - A pattern or mold used as a guide for shaping or replicating objects

Etymology

Variant of 'template', from French 'templet', diminutive of 'temple' (a piece for shaping).
