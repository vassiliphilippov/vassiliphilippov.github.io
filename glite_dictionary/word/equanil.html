Equanil

1. NOUN

/ ˈekwəˌnɪl / (AmE), / ˈekwəˌnɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked sedative and tranquilizer (generic name meprobamate) used to treat muscle tension and anxiety.
sedative drug, trademark for meprobamate
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Equanil to help manage her anxiety symptoms.
Patients taking Equanil should follow their physician's instructions closely.
In the 1950s, Equanil became a popular medication for treating nervous conditions.
