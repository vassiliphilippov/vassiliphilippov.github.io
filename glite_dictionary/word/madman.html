madman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmad.man / (BrE)
a man who is insane or behaves in a wildly foolish or dangerous way
insane or wildly foolish man

Examples

The neighbors regarded him as a madman after his bizarre outburst.
Driven by ambition, he worked like a madman to complete the project on time.
In old stories, the madman often lives alone at the edge of town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunatic - a person who is crazy or mentally ill
  • Synonym: maniac - a person who behaves in a wild or violent way, especially due to mental illness
  • Antonym: sane person - someone of sound mind; not insane

Etymology

From mad + man, attested since the 16th century.
