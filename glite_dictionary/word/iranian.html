Iranian

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈreɪniən / (AmE), / ɪˈreɪniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Iran
person from Iran

Examples

An Iranian artist won an international award for his latest film.
Many Iranians celebrate Nowruz, the Persian New Year, with special traditions.
The Iranian community in London has grown significantly over the past decade.

Synset Examples

the majority of Irani are Persian Shiite Muslims

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian - a native or inhabitant of Persia (Iran), especially one who speaks Persian

Etymology

From Iran + -ian, denoting association or origin.

2. ADJ

/ ɪˈreɪniən / (AmE), / ɪˈreɪniən / (BrE)
of or relating to Iran, its people, language, or culture
of or relating to Iran or its people

Examples

The Iranian government announced new policies today.
She specializes in Iranian history and art.
Many Iranian films have gained international recognition.

Synset Examples

Iranian mountains
Iranian security police

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian - relating to Persia (historical name for Iran), its people, or language

Etymology

From Iran + -ian (suffix denoting belonging or relating to).
