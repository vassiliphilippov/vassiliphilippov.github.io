drugless

1. ADJ

/ ˈdrʌɡləs / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡləs / (BrE)
(of a treatment or therapy) not involving the use of drugs.
not involving drugs (usually about therapy)

Examples

The clinic specializes in drugless treatments for chronic pain.
Many patients prefer drugless therapies such as acupuncture or massage.
He advocates a drugless approach to mental health care whenever possible.

Synset Examples

drugless therapy

Related Terms

  • Antonym: medicinal - relating to or having the properties of a medicine
