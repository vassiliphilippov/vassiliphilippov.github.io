gondolier

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡɑn.dəˈlɪr / (AmE), / ˌɡɒn.dəˈlɪə / (BrE)
a person, especially in Venice, whose job is to row and steer a gondola
Venetian boatman who rows a gondola

Examples

The gondolier skillfully navigated the narrow canals of Venice.
Tourists often tip their gondolier for singing traditional Italian songs during the ride.
Becoming a licensed gondolier in Venice requires years of training and apprenticeship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boatman - a person who operates or works on a boat

Etymology

From Italian *gondoliere*, from *gondola* (a type of boat).
