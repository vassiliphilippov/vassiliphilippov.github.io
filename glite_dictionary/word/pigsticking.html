pigsticking

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪɡˌstɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡˌstɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the hunting of wild boar with spears, traditionally as a sport
sport of hunting wild boar with spears

Examples

He traveled to India to experience pigsticking among the British officers stationed there.
The annual pigsticking event drew hunters from across the region.
She wrote a book on the history and customs of pigsticking in colonial times.

Etymology

From 'pig' + 'sticking', referring to the act of spearing wild pigs (boars).
