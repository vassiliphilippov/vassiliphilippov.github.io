priory

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪəri / (AmE), / ˈpraɪəri / (BrE)
a religious residence that is a monastery governed by a prior or a convent governed by a prioress
monastery or convent governed by a prior/prioress

Examples

The nuns at the priory host a charity bake sale every autumn.
After the dissolution of the priory, the building was converted into a private residence.
He found peace and solitude living within the walls of the ancient priory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monastery - a building where monks live and worship
  • Synonym: convent - a building where nuns live and worship

Etymology

From Old French *prioré*, from Medieval Latin *prioratus* (‘office of a prior’).
