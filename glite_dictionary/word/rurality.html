rurality

1. NOUN

/ ruˈræləti / (AmE), / rʊəˈralɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being rural; the characteristic or traits of the countryside rather than the city
quality of being rural

Examples

Many people are attracted to the rurality of small country villages.
The author describes the rurality of the landscape with vivid imagery.
Efforts to preserve rurality are vital for maintaining local traditions.

Synset Examples

a place with the rurality of a turnip field

Related Terms

  • Antonym: urbanity - the quality or characteristics of city life

Etymology

Formed from 'rural' and the suffix '-ity', from Latin 'ruralis' (of the countryside).
