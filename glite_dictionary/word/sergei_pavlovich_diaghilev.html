Sergei Pavlovich Diaghilev

1. PROPN

/ sərˈɡeɪ ˌpɑːvləvɪtʃ diˈɑːɡɪlɛv / (AmE), / səˈɡeɪ ˌpɑːvləvɪtʃ dɪˈɑːɡɪlɛv / (BrE)
(1872–1929) Russian ballet impresario who founded the Ballets Russes and introduced Russian ballet to Western Europe.
Russian ballet impresario and founder of Ballets Russes

Examples

Without Sergei Pavlovich Diaghilev, the world of modern ballet would look very different today.
The innovative productions of Sergei Pavlovich Diaghilev captivated audiences throughout Europe in the early twentieth century.
Many famous dancers and composers collaborated with Sergei Pavlovich Diaghilev during his leadership of the Ballets Russes.
