boob

1. NOUN

/ buːb / (AmE), / buːb / (BrE)
a person who is considered stupid or lacking in good judgment; a fool
a foolish or stupid person
informal

Examples

Don't be such a boob—read the instructions before you start!
He felt like a real boob when he realized he had been tricked.
The movie was full of slapstick and boobs making silly mistakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking in judgment or sense
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability

Etymology

Probably of imitative origin, first used in early 20th-century American English.

2. NOUN

/ buːb / (AmE), / buːb / (BrE)
a slang term for either of the two soft, fleshy, milk-producing organs on the chest of a woman; a breast
slang term for a woman's breast
slang

Examples

She wore a low-cut dress that showed a bit of her boob.
He made a silly joke about getting sand on his boobs at the beach.
The nurse explained the importance of checking each boob for unusual lumps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breast - either of the two soft, fleshy organs on a woman's chest

Etymology

Possibly from baby talk or an alteration of 'booby'.
