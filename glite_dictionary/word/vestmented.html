vestmented

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛstməntɪd / (AmE), / ˈvɛstməntɪd / (BrE)
Dressed in ceremonial garments, especially ecclesiastical vestments.
wearing ceremonial (esp. clerical) garments

Examples

The bishop appeared vestmented for the solemn high mass.
All the priests were vestmented in white and gold for the ceremony.
The vestmented clergy processed slowly down the aisle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vested - clothed in vestments, often used similarly

Etymology

From 'vestment', from Old French vestement, from Latin vestimentum (clothing, garment).
