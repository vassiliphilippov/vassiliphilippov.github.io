blue-white

1. ADJ

/ bluː waɪt / (AmE), / bluː waɪt / (BrE)
(of a color) white with a slight tinge of blue.
white tinged with blue

Examples

The diamond has a blue-white brilliance that makes it particularly valuable.
She painted the ceiling a blue-white to create a feeling of coolness and light.
The glacier appeared blue-white against the dark mountains.
