Calcutta

1. PROPN

/ kælˈkʌtə / (AmE), / kælˈkʌtə / (BrE)
the former official name for Kolkata, a major city in eastern India known for its cultural heritage, historical significance, and large population.
former official name for Kolkata, major Indian city

Examples

She spent two years teaching English in Calcutta before returning home.
Calcutta is famous for its vibrant festivals and colonial architecture.
The book explores the social challenges faced by residents of Calcutta in the twentieth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kolkata - current official name of Calcutta, city in eastern India

Etymology

From Bengali কলিকাতা (Kôlikata); anglicized to Calcutta during British colonial period.
