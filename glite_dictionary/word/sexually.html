sexually

1. ADV

/ ˈsɛkʃuəli / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to sex or by means of sexual activity.
involving sex or sexual activity

Examples

Many plants can reproduce sexually as well as asexually, depending on the conditions.
He was accused of behaving sexually inappropriately toward several colleagues.
Some diseases can be transmitted sexually, so precautions are important.

Synset Examples

reproduce sexually

Related Terms

  • Antonym: asexually - not involving sexual activity; without sex

Etymology

From 'sexual' + '-ly' adverb suffix.

2. ADV

/ ˈsɛkʃuəli / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to sex, sexual activity, or sexuality.
in a sexual way or relating to sex

Examples

The two characters are sexually attracted to each other.
This behavior is considered sexually inappropriate in the workplace.
Some plants reproduce sexually rather than asexually.

Synset Examples

sexually ambiguous

Related Terms

  • Antonym: asexually - in a way that does not involve sexual activity or sexuality

Etymology

From 'sexual' + '-ly' (adverb forming suffix).
