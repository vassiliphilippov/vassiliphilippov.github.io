hereto

1. ADV

/ hɪrˈtuː / (AmE), / hɪəˈtuː / (BrE)
to this document or writing—used especially in legal or formal contexts to refer to something attached or referred to in the current document.
to this document (legal, formal)
legal

Examples

The financial statements hereto attached form part of the agreement.
Please refer to the appendix hereto for supporting evidence.
The schedules hereto incorporated shall be binding upon all parties.

Synset Examples

the charts hereto attached

Etymology

From Middle English, combining 'here' and 'to.'
