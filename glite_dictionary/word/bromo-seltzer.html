Bromo-Seltzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊmoʊ ˈsɛltsər / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊməʊ ˈsɛltsə / (BrE)
a brand of effervescent antacid and pain reliever, used especially for indigestion and headache
antacid and pain reliever (brand)

Examples

After a heavy meal, she took a Bromo-Seltzer to settle her stomach.
He always kept a bottle of Bromo-Seltzer in his medicine cabinet for headaches.
The advertisement claimed that Bromo-Seltzer could quickly relieve indigestion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alka-Seltzer - brand of effervescent antacid and pain reliever

Etymology

Trademark, originally adopted by the Emerson Drug Company for a medicine containing sodium bromide and acetanilide, later sold as an antacid.
