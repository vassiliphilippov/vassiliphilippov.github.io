2. NOUN

/ laʊˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / laʊˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
a person from Laos, or a member of the Buddhist ethnic group native to Laos and northeastern Thailand who speaks the Lao language.
a person from Laos or of Lao ethnicity

Examples

The Laotian community in the city celebrates their traditional festivals every spring.
As a Laotian, she is fluent in both Lao and Thai languages.
Many Laotians practice Theravada Buddhism as part of their cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lao - a member of the same ethnic group; often used synonymously

Etymology

From French 'laotien', from 'Laos' + '-ien' (equivalent to English '-ian').

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09739190-n
Synset POS: n
