liquidizer

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪk.wə.daɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪk.wɪ.daɪ.zə / (BrE)
an electric kitchen appliance with rotating blades used to mix, chop, or purée foods and liquids
electric mixer for blending foods
british

Examples

She poured the soup into the liquidizer to make it smooth and creamy.
After chopping the fruit, add it to the liquidizer with some yogurt for a quick smoothie.
The recipe suggests using a liquidizer to blend the ingredients together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blender - an electric machine for mixing and puréeing foods

Etymology

From 'liquid' + '-izer', indicating a device that turns things into liquid.
