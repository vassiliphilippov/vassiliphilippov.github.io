volant

1. ADJ

/ ˈvoʊ.lənt / (AmE), / ˈvɒl.ənt / (BrE)
(of a bird or animal) having the wings extended as if in flight, especially in heraldry or zoological illustration.
with wings extended as if flying

Examples

The family crest features an eagle volant over a mountain.
In the illustration, the crane is depicted volant, its wings spread wide.
The artist drew several birds volant in the upper corner of the canvas.

Etymology

From French 'volant', present participle of 'voler' (to fly), from Latin 'volare'.
