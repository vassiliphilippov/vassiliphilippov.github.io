monkey-ass

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋki ˌæs / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ˌas / (BrE)
(slang) A foolish or annoying person; used as an insult or to express exasperation.
foolish or annoying person (slang)
slang offensive

Examples

Stop being such a monkey-ass and help us out.
Nobody wants to work with a monkey-ass who keeps making trouble.
She called him a monkey-ass after he ruined the mood with his jokes.

Synset Examples

Don't be a monkey-ass; just admit your mistake.
He acted like a monkey-ass during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a stupid or foolish person
  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person (slang)
