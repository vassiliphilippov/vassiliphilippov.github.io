coiffeur

1. NOUN

/ kwɑːˈfɜr / (AmE), / kwɑːˈfɜː / (BrE)
a male hairdresser, especially one who works in an upscale salon
male hairdresser (often upscale)

Examples

The coiffeur styled her hair in an elegant updo for the gala.
He regularly visits the same coiffeur in Paris for a fresh haircut.
The renowned coiffeur opened a new salon downtown, attracting a high-end clientele.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairdresser - a person whose job is to cut, style, and color hair
  • Antonym: coiffeuse - a female hairdresser

Etymology

From French coiffeur (male hairdresser), from coiffer (to do hair).
