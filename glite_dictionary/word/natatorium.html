natatorium

1. NOUN

/ ˌnæt̬əˈtɔriəm / (AmE), / ˌnatəˈtɔːrɪəm / (BrE)
An indoor swimming pool, especially one designed for public or competitive use.
indoor swimming pool facility

Examples

The university's new natatorium features an Olympic-size pool and diving platforms.
Many swim teams hold their practices in the local natatorium during the winter months.
The public natatorium was renovated to include accessible locker rooms and spectator seating.

Synset Examples

‘swimming bath’ is a British term

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimming pool - a structure designed for swimming; often outdoors
  • Synonym: swimming bath - British term for a public indoor swimming facility

Etymology

From Latin 'natare' (to swim), via Modern Latin 'natatorium' (swimming place).
