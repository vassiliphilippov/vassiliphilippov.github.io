spaced-out

1. ADJ

/ ˈspeɪst aʊt / (AmE), / ˈspeɪst aʊt / (BrE)
confused or inattentive, as if under the influence of drugs or completely absorbed elsewhere; dazed or disoriented
confused or dazed; inattentive
informal

Examples

After working all night, she felt completely spaced-out during the meeting.
He looked so spaced-out that I wondered if he was listening at all.
The medication left him feeling spaced-out and disconnected from reality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dazed - unable to think or react properly; stunned or confused
  • Antonym: lucid - expressed clearly; easy to understand; showing clear thinking

2. ADJ

/ ˌspeɪstˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˌspeɪstˈaʊt / (BrE)
in a stupor or dazed state, as if under the influence of drugs; inattentive or not fully aware of one's surroundings
dazed or stupefied, as by drugs
informal

Examples

After staying up all night, he felt completely spaced-out at work the next day.
She looked spaced-out and barely responded when I spoke to her.
The medication made him so spaced-out that he forgot where he was.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dazed - unable to think clearly
  • Antonym: alert - quick to notice and respond; attentive

Synset POS: s
