Crocethia

1. NOUN

/ kroʊˈsiːθiə / (AmE), / krəʊˈsiːθiə / (BrE)
A genus of birds in the family Scolopacidae, containing the sanderling.
genus of shorebirds (sanderling)

Examples

The sanderling is the only species currently classified in the genus Crocethia.
Researchers recently reviewed the classification of birds in Crocethia.
The genus Crocethia is recognized in ornithological records worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanderling - a small shorebird of the genus Crocethia
