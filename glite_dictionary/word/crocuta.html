Crocuta

1. NOUN

/ kroʊˈkjuːtə / (AmE), / krəʊˈkjuːtə / (BrE)
a genus of hyenas in the family Hyaenidae, which includes the spotted hyena
genus of hyenas

Examples

The spotted hyena, or Crocuta crocuta, is native to sub-Saharan Africa.
Researchers studied the behavior of Crocuta in their natural habitat.
Fossils of the genus Crocuta have been found in both Africa and Eurasia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted hyena - a large, carnivorous hyena species native to Africa, member of Crocuta

Etymology

From Latin 'crocuta', from Greek 'krokottas', an ancient name for the hyena.
