unromantic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.roʊˈmæn.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rəʊˈmæn.tɪk / (BrE)
Not expressing, involving, or exciting sexual love or romance; lacking romance or romantic qualities.
not romantic; lacking romance

Examples

She found the idea of a spreadsheet date night terribly unromantic.
His unromantic suggestion was to order pizza and watch TV together.
The room had an unromantic atmosphere, with harsh lighting and plain décor.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: romantic - expressive of or conducive to romance
  • Synonym: dispassionate - not influenced by strong emotion

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' + 'romantic'.
