sculptress

1. NOUN

/ ˈskʌlp.trɪs / (AmE), / ˈskʌlp.trɪs / (BrE)
a female sculptor
female sculptor

Examples

The renowned sculptress presented her latest bronze statue at the gallery opening.
Many early 20th-century exhibitions featured works by pioneering sculptresses.
She aspired to become a sculptress after seeing famous statues in Italy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculptor - a person who makes sculptures

Etymology

Formed from "sculptor" with the feminine suffix -ess, following the tradition of gendered occupational nouns.
