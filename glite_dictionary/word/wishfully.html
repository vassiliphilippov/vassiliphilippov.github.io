wishfully

1. ADV

/ ˈwɪʃfəli / (AmE), / ˈwɪʃfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows longing or hope for something that is unlikely or unattainable.
in a wishful or longing manner

Examples

She gazed wishfully at the beautiful dress in the store window.
He wishfully imagined a future where he could travel the world.
The children looked wishfully at the plate of cookies on the counter.

Synset Examples

he wishfully indulged in dreams of fame

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wishful - having or showing a longing or desire for something
  • Synonym: yearningly - in a manner full of yearning or longing
  • Antonym: realistically - in a way that accepts things as they are
