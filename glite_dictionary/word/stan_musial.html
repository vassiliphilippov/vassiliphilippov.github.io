Stan Musial

1. PROPN

/ stæn ˈmjuːziəl / (AmE), / stæn ˈmjuːziəl / (BrE)
(1920–2013) United States baseball player regarded as one of the greatest hitters in Major League Baseball history.
legendary American baseball player

Examples

Stan Musial played his entire major league career with the St. Louis Cardinals.
Many fans consider Stan Musial one of the best hitters in baseball history.
The legacy of Stan Musial continues to inspire young athletes today.
