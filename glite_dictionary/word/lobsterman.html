lobsterman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːbstərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstəmən / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, whose occupation is catching lobsters
person who catches lobsters for a living

Examples

The lobsterman set out before dawn to check his traps along the rugged coastline.
Generations of lobstermen have worked these waters, relying on traditional methods.
She interviewed a veteran lobsterman about the challenges of sustainable fishing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lobster fisherman - a person whose job is to catch lobsters
