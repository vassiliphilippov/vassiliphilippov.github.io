Henrik Ibsen

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɛnˌrɪk ˈɪb.sən / (AmE), / ˈhɛn.rɪk ˈɪb.sən / (BrE)
(1828–1906) a Norwegian playwright and poet, widely regarded as a founder of modern realistic drama, known for plays addressing social and political issues.
Norwegian playwright and poet (1828–1906)

Examples
