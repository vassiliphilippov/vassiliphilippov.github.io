Villahermosa

1. PROPN

/ ˌviː.jɑː.erˈmoʊ.sə / (AmE), / ˌviː.jɑː.ɛˈmɒ.sə / (BrE)
the capital city of the Mexican state of Tabasco, located in southeastern Mexico, known for its rich history and as a regional economic center
capital city of Tabasco, Mexico

Examples

Many tourists visit Villahermosa to explore its museums and nearby archaeological sites.
The business conference will be held in Villahermosa next spring.
After the hurricane, Villahermosa became a key center for relief operations.

Etymology

From Spanish 'villa' (town or village) and 'hermosa' (beautiful), meaning 'beautiful town'.
