divorcee

1. NOUN

/ dɪˌvɔrˈseɪ / (AmE), / dɪˌvɔːˈseɪ / (BrE)
A woman who is divorced or separated from her husband.
a divorced or separated woman

Examples

After the breakup, she became a divorcee and started a new chapter in her life.
The club was popular among both widows and divorcees seeking companionship.
As a divorcee, she found certain social situations challenging at first.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divorced woman - a woman who is no longer married after a legal divorce

Etymology

From French 'divorcée', feminine past participle of 'divorcer' (to divorce).
