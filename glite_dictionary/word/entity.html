entity

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛn.t̬ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈen.tɪ.ti / (BrE)
a thing with distinct and independent existence, either living or nonliving
something that exists independently

Examples

The company became a separate entity after the reorganization.
Some philosophers debate whether the mind is an independent entity from the body.
Every living entity in the ecosystem plays a unique role.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: being - a living or existing thing

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'entitas', from Latin 'ens' (being, thing that is).
