buff-colored

1. ADJ

/ bʌf ˈkʌlərd / (AmE), / bʌf ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
having a pale yellow-brown or tawny color resembling buff leather
pale yellow-brown (buff) in color

Examples

The jacket was made of soft buff-colored suede.
We chose a buff-colored paint for the living room walls.
The bird’s buff-colored feathers helped it blend into the sandy background.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buff - a light yellow-brown color

Etymology

From 'buff' meaning a light yellow-brown color, originally referring to buffalo leather.
