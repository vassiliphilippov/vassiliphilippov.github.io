engaged

/ ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (BrE)
(of a person or service) hired or employed for a particular job or task.

Examples

/ ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (BrE)
busy doing something; having one's attention or mind fully occupied with a particular activity or interest.

Examples

During the meeting, he remained fully engaged in the discussion.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occupied - busy or actively involved in something
  • Synonym: absorbed - fully involved or immersed in an activity or thought
  • Antonym: available - not occupied; free to do something

Etymology

From Old French *engager* (to pledge, involve), from en- (in) + gage (a pledge).
