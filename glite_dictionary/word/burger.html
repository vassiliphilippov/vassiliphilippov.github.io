burger

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡə / (BrE)
A sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of minced beef, typically served in a split bun with various condiments and toppings.
sandwich with beef patty in a bun

Examples

He ordered a burger with extra cheese and no onions for lunch.
The new restaurant on Main Street serves the best burgers in town.
Would you like fries with your burger today?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamburger - a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground beef in a bun
  • Degree Higher: cheeseburger - a burger with a slice of cheese melted on the patty

Etymology

Shortened from 'hamburger', which itself is named after the German city of Hamburg.
