to bonk

1. VERB

/ bɑŋk / (AmE), / bɒŋk / (BrE)
to hit someone or something hard, often in a way that makes a sound
hit someone or something hard
informal

Examples

He accidentally bonked his head on the open cupboard door.
If you bonk the drum too hard, it might tear.
The child bonked his friend with a foam sword during playtime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smack - to hit someone or something with a sharp slap or blow
  • Synonym: strike - to hit forcibly or deliberately
  • Antonym: caress - to touch or stroke gently
