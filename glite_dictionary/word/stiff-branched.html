stiff-branched

1. ADJ

/ stɪf bræntʃt / (AmE), / stɪf brɑːntʃt / (BrE)
(of a plant or tree) having rigid or firm branches that do not bend easily.
having rigid or firm branches

Examples

The stiff-branched shrubs stood firm against the strong winds.
This stiff-branched variety is often used as a windbreak in gardens.
The naturalist noted the stiff-branched structure of the young pine tree.
