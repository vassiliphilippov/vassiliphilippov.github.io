toe-tapper

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ ˌtæpər / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ ˌtæpə / (BrE)
a song with a lively rhythm that makes people want to tap their toes or dance
catchy, dance-inspiring song
informal

Examples

The band's new single is a real toe-tapper that gets everyone out on the dance floor.
Every party playlist needs at least one classic toe-tapper to liven up the mood.
Her latest album is full of toe-tappers that you can't help but move to.

Synset Examples

I couldn't help but dance to that toe-tapper at the party.
Her music was so catchy, it turned into a toe-tapper.
That song is a real toe-tapper; I love it!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catchy tune - song with an easily memorable melody
