undecomposable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndiˈkɑmpəzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndiˈkɒmpəzəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be separated or analyzed into simpler parts; incapable of further division or analysis.
incapable of further division or analysis

Examples

Despite using various scientific techniques, the compound remained undecomposable by ordinary means.
Philosophers sometimes refer to basic sensations as undecomposable elements of consciousness.
The mathematician claimed the problem was undecomposable and could not be split into smaller cases.

Synset Examples

a feeling is a simple and undecomposable mental state
this weight of evidence is something mystical and unanalyzable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indivisible - impossible to divide or separate
  • Antonym: analyzable - able to be analyzed or separated into parts

Etymology

From un- (not) + decomposable (able to be decomposed), following the pattern of English negatives.
