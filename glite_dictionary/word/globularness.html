globularness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlɑːbjələrnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒbjʊlənɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being globular; the roundness or spherical shape of something.
quality of being globular or spherical

Examples

The globularness of the marble made it perfect for rolling across the floor.
Scientists studied the globularness of the tiny organism under the microscope.
The artist admired the globularness of the perfectly round sculpture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sphericity - the quality of being spherical or like a sphere
  • Antonym: angularity - the quality of being angular rather than rounded

Etymology

From 'globular' + '-ness' (Old French or Latin globe, late Latin globularis + Old English -ness).
