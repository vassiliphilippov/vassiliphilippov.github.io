europhilia

1. NOUN

/ jʊˌroʊˈfɪliə / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊˈfɪliə / (BrE)
Admiration for Europe, Europeans, or the European Union.
admiration for Europe or Europeans

Examples

Academic discussions of europhilia often focus on its cultural and political implications.
She was accused of europhilia after advocating for closer ties with European institutions.
The government’s policies reflected a strong sense of europhilia among its members.

Synset Examples

The other challenge is that this open and liberal Euroscepticism may be an even more elite project than liberal Europhilia.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: europhobe - person who fears or dislikes Europe or Europeans
  • Synonym: francophilia - admiration for France or French people

Etymology

From Greek 'eurōpē' (Europe) + '-philia' (love, fondness).
