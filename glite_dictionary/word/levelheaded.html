levelheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɛvəlˈhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˌlɛv.əlˈhɛd.ɪd / (BrE)
having or showing an ability to think clearly and make good decisions, especially in difficult situations
sensible and calm; good judgment

Examples

Her levelheaded response to the crisis impressed everyone on the team.
You need to be levelheaded when making important financial decisions.
Despite the chaos, he remained levelheaded and calmly directed everyone to safety.

Synset Examples

healthy scepticism
a healthy fear of rattlesnakes
the healthy attitude of French laws
healthy relations between labor and management
an intelligent solution
a sound approach to the problem
sound advice
no sound explanation for his decision

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensible - showing good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: reasonable - based on good sense or logic
  • Antonym: irrational - not logical or reasonable

Etymology

Formed from 'level' + 'headed'; 'level' meaning even or balanced, and 'headed' referring to the mind or temperament.
