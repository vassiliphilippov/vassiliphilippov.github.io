fancifully

1. ADV

/ ˈfæn.sə.fəli / (AmE), / ˈfæn.sɪ.fəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows imagination or is unusual and decorative, often in an unrealistic or whimsical manner.
in an imaginative or whimsical way

Examples

The room was fancifully decorated with paper lanterns and colorful streamers.
She fancifully described her dream as a journey through a candy forest.
The chef fancifully arranged the desserts to look like tiny fairy houses.

Synset Examples

the Christmas tree was fancifully decorated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imaginatively - in an imaginative manner
  • Antonym: plainly - in a simple or unadorned manner
