Udmurt

1. NOUN

/ ˈudˌmʊrt / (AmE), / ˈuːdmʊət / (BrE)
the Finnic language spoken by the Udmurt people, primarily in the Udmurt Republic of Russia
a Finnic language of Russia

Examples

She is studying the Udmurt language at university.
Many older villagers still speak Udmurt as their first language.
Bilingual signs in both Russian and Udmurt are common in Izhevsk.

Etymology

From Russian Удму́рт (Udmúrt), the self-designation of the people.

2. NOUN

/ ʊdˈmʊrt / (AmE), / ʊdˈmɜːt / (BrE)
a member of a Finno-Ugric-speaking people living in eastern European Russia, primarily in the Udmurt Republic.
member of a Finno-Ugric people of Russia

Examples

The Udmurt are known for their distinctive folk music and traditional dress.
She learned about the history and customs of the Udmurt during her visit to the region.
Many Udmurt live in the republic that bears their name in Russia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finno-Ugric - relating to the Finno-Ugric peoples or their languages

Etymology

From Russian Удму́рт (Udmúrt), the self-designation of the people.
