Irani

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈrɑni / (AmE), / ɪˈrɑːni / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Iran
native or inhabitant of Iran

Examples

The museum features art by contemporary Irani artists.

Etymology
