pushcart

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊʃˌkɑrt / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃˌkɑːt / (BrE)
a small cart with wheels that is pushed by hand, used for carrying goods, especially in markets or on the street
hand-pushed cart for goods

Examples

Vendors sold fresh fruit from a brightly painted pushcart on the corner.
He loaded his groceries into the pushcart and wheeled it to the car.
The old pushcart creaked as it rolled over the cobblestone street.

Synset Examples

he used a handcart to carry the rocks away
their pushcart was piled high with groceries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handcart - a small cart pushed or pulled by hand

Etymology

From 'push' + 'cart'.
