cocobolo

1. NOUN

/ koʊ.kəˈboʊ.loʊ / (AmE), / kəʊ.kəˈbəʊ.ləʊ / (BrE)
a tropical hardwood tree of Central America, valued for its richly colored, dense wood, often used in fine woodworking
tropical hardwood tree valued for timber

Examples

The furniture maker chose cocobolo for its beautiful grain and vibrant color.
Because of its durability and striking appearance, cocobolo is highly prized among woodworkers.
The knife handle was crafted from rare cocobolo wood.

Etymology

From Spanish 'cocobolo', referring to the tree and its wood.
