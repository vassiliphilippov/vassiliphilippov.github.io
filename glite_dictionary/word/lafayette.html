Lafayette

1. PROPN

/ ˌlæfiˈɛt, ˌlɑːfi- / (AmE), / ˌlæfiˈɛt, ˌlɑːfi- / (BrE)
A city in south central Louisiana, United States, known for its Acadian heritage.
city in Louisiana, USA

Examples

Many tourists visit Lafayette to experience its vibrant Cajun culture and cuisine.
The university in Lafayette attracts students from across the country.
After moving to Louisiana, she settled in Lafayette and quickly became part of the community.

Etymology

Named after Marquis de Lafayette, a French general and hero of the American Revolutionary War.
