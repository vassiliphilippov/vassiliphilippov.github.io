astrological

1. ADJ

/ ˌæstəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌæstəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with astrology, especially the interpretation of celestial bodies in relation to human affairs
relating to astrology

Examples

She consulted an astrological chart before making major life decisions.
The magazine publishes daily astrological predictions for each zodiac sign.
His interest in astrological phenomena led him to study the movements of the stars.

Synset Examples

astrological chart

Related Terms

  • Antonym: astronomical - relating to astronomy, the scientific study of celestial objects

Etymology

From late Latin astrologicus, from Greek astrologikos, from astrologia (astrology).
