Paducah

1. PROPN

/ pəˈduːkə / (AmE), / pəˈduːkə / (BrE)
A city in western Kentucky, United States, located on the Ohio River.
city in western Kentucky, USA

Examples

She grew up in Paducah before moving to the city for college.
Severe floods once threatened much of Paducah's historic downtown area.
The quilt festival in Paducah attracts visitors from across the country every year.

Etymology

Named after the Padouca, an old term for the Comanche people.
