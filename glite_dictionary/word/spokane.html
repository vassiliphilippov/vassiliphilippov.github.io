Spokane

1. PROPN

/ spoʊˈkæn / (AmE), / spəʊˈkæn / (BrE)
A city in eastern Washington State, near the border with Idaho.
city in eastern Washington, U.S.

Examples

She grew up in Spokane before moving to Seattle for college.
The river running through Spokane is a popular spot for kayaking and fishing.
Last summer, we visited Spokane for a family reunion.

Etymology

From the name of the Spokane people, a Native American tribe in the region.
