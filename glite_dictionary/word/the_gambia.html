The Gambia

1. PROPN

/ ðə ˈɡæm.bi.ə / (AmE), / ði ˈɡæm.bi.ə / (BrE)
a country in West Africa, consisting of a narrow strip of land along the Gambia River, almost entirely surrounded by Senegal
country in West Africa

Examples

She traveled to The Gambia to study its unique wildlife along the river.
The Gambia gained independence from Britain in 1965.
Many tourists visit The Gambia for its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture.

Etymology

Named after the Gambia River, which flows through the country.
