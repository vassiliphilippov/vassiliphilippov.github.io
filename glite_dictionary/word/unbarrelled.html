unbarrelled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbærəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈbærəld / (BrE)
Not contained or kept in a barrel.
not contained in a barrel

Examples

The unbarrelled wine was stored in glass containers instead of wooden barrels.
The unbarrelled oil proved more susceptible to contamination.
Freshly squeezed juice is often sold unbarrelled for immediate consumption.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: barrelled - (of a substance or item) kept or contained in a barrel
