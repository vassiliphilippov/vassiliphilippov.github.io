Capone

1. PROPN

/ kəˈpoʊn / (AmE), / kəˈpəʊn / (BrE)
Alphonse 'Al' Capone (1899–1947), an American gangster who led organized crime in Chicago during the Prohibition era.
notorious American gangster (1899–1947)

Examples

Capone became infamous for his role in organized crime during the Prohibition era.
Many films and books have been inspired by the life of Capone.
The authorities eventually arrested Capone for tax evasion, not for his violent crimes.
