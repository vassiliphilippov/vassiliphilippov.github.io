Anaheim

1. PROPN

/ ˈænəˌhaɪm / (AmE), / ˈænəhaɪm / (BrE)
a city in southern California, southeast of Los Angeles, known as the site of Disneyland
city in southern California, home of Disneyland

Examples

We spent our vacation in Anaheim visiting theme parks and museums.
The baseball team, the Angels, plays its home games in Anaheim.
Many families travel to Anaheim each year to visit Disneyland.

Etymology

From German, combining "Ana" (from the nearby Santa Ana River) and "heim" (home).
