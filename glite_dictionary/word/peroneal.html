peroneal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛrəˈniːəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛrəˈniːəl / (BrE)
relating to the fibula or the outer part of the leg below the knee
of the fibula or outer lower leg
medical

Examples

The peroneal nerve runs along the outer part of the lower leg.
An injury to the peroneal tendons can cause pain and swelling near the ankle.
Doctors examined his peroneal muscles after he reported weakness in his foot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fibular - relating to the fibula bone

Etymology

From Greek peronaios (of the fibula), from perone (pin, fibula).
