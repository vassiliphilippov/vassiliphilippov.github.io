centilitre

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntɪˌliːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛntɪliːtə / (BrE)
A metric unit of volume equal to one hundredth of a litre (0.01 litre).
one hundredth of a litre (metric unit)

Examples

The bottle contains 50 centilitres of water.
He measured out exactly five centilitres of vinegar for the recipe.
Soft drink cans in Europe often have a volume marked in centilitres.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: millilitre - a metric unit of volume equal to one thousandth of a litre
  • Degree Higher: litre - the basic metric unit of volume

Etymology

From French 'centilitre', from Latin 'centum' (hundred) + French 'litre'.
