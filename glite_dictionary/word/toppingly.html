toppingly

1. ADV

/ ˈtɑpɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtɒpɪŋli / (BrE)
In an excellent or superb manner; extremely well.
excellently; superbly
archaic

Examples

The actors performed toppingly in last night's production of Hamlet.
She looked toppingly elegant at the gala, turning heads wherever she went.
This cake tastes toppingly delicious, just like the ones my grandmother used to make.

Synset Examples

her voice is superbly disciplined
the colors changed wondrously slowly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superbly - in an excellent way; extremely well
  • Synonym: excellently - in an excellent or outstanding manner

Etymology

From 'topping' (outstanding) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
