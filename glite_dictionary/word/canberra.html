Canberra

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæn.bɚ.ə / (AmE), / ˈkæn.bərə / (BrE)
the capital city of Australia, located in southeastern Australia
capital of Australia

Examples

Many government offices are based in Canberra, making it an important political center.
She traveled from Melbourne to Canberra for a business meeting.
The National Gallery of Australia is located in Canberra.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Sydney - largest city in Australia

Etymology

Named after the Ngunnawal word 'Kambera' or 'Canberry', meaning 'meeting place'.
