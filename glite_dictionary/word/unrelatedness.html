unrelatedness

1. NOUN

/ ʌn.rɪˈleɪ.tɪd.nəs / (AmE), / ʌn.rɪˈleɪ.tɪd.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of not being connected or related to something else
lack of connection or relation

Examples

The unrelatedness of their arguments made the discussion confusing for everyone involved.
He was surprised by the apparent unrelatedness of her comments to the main topic.
Scientific studies often require establishing the unrelatedness of variables before making conclusions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: relatedness - the state of being connected or related

Etymology

From 'unrelated' + '-ness', forming a noun meaning the state of being unrelated.
