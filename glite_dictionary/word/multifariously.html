multifariously

1. ADV

/ ˌmʌl.tɪˈfer.i.əs.li / (AmE), / ˌmʌl.tɪˈfeə.ri.əs.li / (BrE)
In many and various ways; diversely.
In many diverse ways

Examples

The museum displays its collection multifariously, appealing to visitors of all ages.
Her talents allow her to contribute multifariously to the success of the project.
This issue has been interpreted multifariously by scholars from different backgrounds.

Synset Examples

the alternatives that are variously represented by the participants
the speakers treated the subject most diversely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diversely - in various or different ways

Etymology

From 'multifarious' + '-ly'; 'multifarious' from Latin multifarius (having many forms).
