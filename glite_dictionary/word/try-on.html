try-on

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪ ˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ ɒn / (BrE)
an act of putting on clothes to see if they fit or suit you
act of trying clothes for fit

Examples

She took several dresses into the changing room for a try-on.
The try-on was successful, so he decided to buy the jacket.
There is a special area in the store set aside for try-ons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fitting - trying on clothes, especially at a shop
