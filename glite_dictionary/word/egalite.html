egalite

1. NOUN

/ eɪˌgælɪˈteɪ / (AmE), / eɪˌgælɪˈteɪ / (BrE)
The principle of social and political equality, especially as a political ideal.
social and political equality

Examples

The ideal of egalite was central to the French Revolution.
Many activists continue to fight for egalite in modern societies.
In his speech, he emphasized the importance of liberty, egalite, and fraternity.

Synset Examples

egality represents an extreme leveling of society

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equality - the state of being equal, especially in status, rights, and opportunities

Etymology

From French 'égalité' meaning 'equality'.
