rotated

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊˌteɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊˌteɪtɪd / (BrE)
turned around a central point or axis
turned around an axis

Examples

The image was rotated 90 degrees to fit the screen.
The Earth is rotated on its axis once every 24 hours.
He rotated the handle to open the valve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spun - turned rapidly or continuously around an axis
  • Antonym: stationary - not moving or turning

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'rotate', from Latin 'rotatus', past participle of 'rotare' (to turn in a wheel).
