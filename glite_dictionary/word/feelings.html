feelings

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːlɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
a person's emotional or moral sensitivity, especially relating to their principles or sense of dignity
emotional or moral sensitivity

Examples

Try not to hurt his feelings with harsh criticism.
Her feelings were deeply wounded by their insensitive comments.
It's important to consider other people's feelings before making jokes.

Synset Examples

the remark hurt his feelings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotions - strong feelings such as joy, anger, or sadness
  • Antonym: insensitivity - lack of concern for others' feelings; not easily offended or emotionally affected

Etymology

Plural of 'feeling', from Old English 'fēlan' (to feel) + -ing.
