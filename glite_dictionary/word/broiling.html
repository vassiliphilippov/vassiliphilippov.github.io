broiling

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɔɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɔɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of cooking food by exposing it to direct radiant heat, such as over a fire or under a grill
method of cooking by direct heat

Examples

Broiling is a popular technique for cooking steaks quickly and evenly.
The chef recommended broiling the fish for a crisp finish.
Some ovens have a special setting designed specifically for broiling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grilling - cooking food over direct heat, especially outdoors on a grill
  • Synonym: roasting - cooking food using dry heat, usually in an oven or over fire

Etymology

From the verb 'broil', ultimately from Old French *bruler* (to burn).
