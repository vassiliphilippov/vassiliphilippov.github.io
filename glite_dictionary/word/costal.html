costal

1. ADJ

/ ˈkoʊstl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkɒstl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or situated near a rib or the ribs, especially in anatomy or zoology.
relating to a rib
medical

Examples

The costal cartilage connects the ribs to the sternum.
During the examination, the doctor pressed gently on the costal margin.
A fracture in the costal region can make breathing painful.

Etymology

From Latin costalis (of the ribs), from costa (rib).
