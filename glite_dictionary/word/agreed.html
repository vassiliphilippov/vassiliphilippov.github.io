agreed

1. ADJ

/ əˈɡrid / (AmE), / əˈɡriːd / (BrE)
having the same opinion or being of one mind; united in viewpoint
sharing the same opinion or view

Examples

The committee was agreed that more time was needed to complete the work.
Both parties were agreed on the terms of the contract before signing it.
We are all agreed that this solution is the best way forward.

Synset Examples

agreed in their distrust of authority

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unanimous - fully in agreement; of one mind
  • Antonym: divided - having differing opinions; not in agreement

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'agree,' from Old French 'agreer'.
