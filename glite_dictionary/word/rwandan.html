Rwandan

1. NOUN

/ ruːˈɑːn.dən / (AmE), / ruːˈæn.dən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Rwanda
native or resident of Rwanda

Examples

The museum features art from a well-known Rwandan.
Many Rwandans speak more than one language fluently.
She became friends with a Rwandan while studying abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rwanda - a country in East-Central Africa

/ ruˈɑːndən / (AmE), / ruˈændən / (BrE)
