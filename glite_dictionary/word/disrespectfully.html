disrespectfully

1. ADV

/ ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktfəli / (AmE), / ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows a lack of respect.
in a disrespectful way

Examples

The student disrespectfully interrupted the teacher during her lecture.
He spoke disrespectfully to his manager, which cost him his job.
She rolled her eyes disrespectfully when her mother gave her advice.

Synset Examples

he treats his parents rather disrespectfully

Related Terms

  • Antonym: respectfully - in a manner that shows respect
  • Synonym: rudely - in an impolite or offensive manner

Etymology

From 'disrespectful' + '-ly', where 'disrespectful' comes from 'disrespect' (lack of respect) + '-ful'.
