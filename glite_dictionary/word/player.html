player

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who plays a musical instrument professionally
professional musician

Examples

The jazz player captivated the audience with his trumpet solo.
She is a renowned violin player in the city orchestra.
The guitar player released his first solo album last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musician - a person who is skilled in playing music

Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who performs in the theater; an actor or actress
theatrical performer; actor or actress

Examples

The leading player captivated the audience with her performance.
Several famous players attended the opening night of the play.
He dreamed of becoming a successful player on the stage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actor - a person who acts in plays or movies
  • Synonym: actress - a female actor

Etymology

From Middle English *pleiere*, from Old French *pleier* (to play) + -er.
