leftism

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛftˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈlɛftɪzəm / (BrE)
belief in or support of the political left and its principles, such as social equality and government intervention in the economy
support of left-wing political principles

Examples

Her commitment to leftism led her to support progressive social policies.
Over the years, the magazine became known for its strong leftism.
Debates between advocates of leftism and right-wing ideologies are common in politics.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rightism - support of right-wing political principles
  • Synonym: leftist - a person who supports left-wing political principles

Etymology

From 'left' (the political left) + '-ism' (forming nouns denoting ideologies).
