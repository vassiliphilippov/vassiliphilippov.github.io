orgasm

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrˌɡæzəm / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡaz(ə)m / (BrE)
the climax of sexual excitement, characterized by intense pleasure and physiological changes
sexual climax; peak of sexual pleasure

Examples

She experienced an intense orgasm that left her breathless.
The study focused on factors that affect the likelihood of reaching orgasm.
He was embarrassed to discuss problems with achieving orgasm with his doctor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climax - the most intense or exciting part of an experience, especially during sex

Etymology

From Greek ‘orgasmos’ (excitement, swelling), from ‘organ’ (to swell, be excited).
