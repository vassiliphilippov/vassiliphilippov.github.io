divertingly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈvɝtɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪˈvɜːtɪŋli / (BrE)
in an entertaining and amusing manner; so as to provide enjoyment or amusement
in an entertaining way

Examples

The teacher explained the lesson divertingly, keeping even the youngest students engaged.
The film moves divertingly from scene to scene, never losing the audience's attention.
She recounted her travels divertingly, with witty stories and vivid descriptions.

Synset Examples

Hollywood has grown too sophisticated to turn out anything really amusingly bad these days

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amusingly - in a manner that causes laughter or amusement
  • Antonym: tediously - in a boring or monotonous manner

Etymology

From 'diverting' (from Latin 'divertere', to turn aside) + '-ly'.
