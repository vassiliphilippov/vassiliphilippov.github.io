Abyssinia

1. PROPN

/ ˌæbɪˈsɪniə / (AmE), / ˌabɪˈsɪnɪə / (BrE)
the former name for Ethiopia, a country in northeastern Africa
former name for Ethiopia

Examples

In the 19th century, Abyssinia was known for its rich culture and history.
European explorers wrote extensively about their travels in Abyssinia.
The kingdom of Abyssinia played a significant role in regional politics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ethiopia - a country in northeastern Africa, formerly known as Abyssinia

Etymology

From Latin 'Abyssinia', derived from Arabic 'al-Ḥabash', referring to the Habesha people.
