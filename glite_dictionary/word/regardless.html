regardless

1. ADV

/ rəˈɡɑrd.ləs / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɑːd.ləs / (BrE)
Despite the prevailing circumstances or potential drawbacks; in spite of everything.
in spite of everything

Examples

She continued her studies regardless of the obstacles she faced.
They decided to go for a hike regardless of the weather forecast.
The concert went ahead regardless, even after the major sponsor pulled out.

Synset Examples

he carried on regardless of the difficulties

Related Terms

  • Synonym: despite - without being affected by; in defiance of
  • Synonym: notwithstanding - in spite of; although

Etymology

From 'regard' + '-less', meaning 'without regard'.
