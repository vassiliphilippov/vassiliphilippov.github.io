agrarian

1. ADJ

/ əˈɡrɛriən / (AmE), / əˈɡreəriən / (BrE)
relating to land, farming, or the cultivation of fields and agriculture
relating to farming or agriculture

Examples

The government introduced new policies to support agrarian communities and small farmers.
Many conflicts in history have arisen due to agrarian reform and land distribution.
The museum features exhibits on agrarian life in the 19th century.

Synset Examples

an agrarian (or agricultural) society
farming communities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agricultural - relating to agriculture or farming

Etymology

From Latin 'agrarius' (relating to land), from 'ager' (field).
