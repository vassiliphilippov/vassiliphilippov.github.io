variedness

1. NOUN

/ ˈværidnəs / (AmE), / ˈveərɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being varied; the presence of diversity or differences within something
the state of being varied or diverse

Examples

The variedness of the landscape attracts many tourists each year.
We appreciate the variedness of opinions shared in the discussion.
Her cooking is famous for its variedness and inventive flavors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: variety - the quality or state of being different or diverse

Etymology

From 'varied' + '-ness', formed from Latin 'varius' (varied).
