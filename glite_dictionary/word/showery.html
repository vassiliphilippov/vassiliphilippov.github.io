showery

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃaʊ.ə.ri / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊ.əri / (BrE)
(of weather) marked by frequent or intermittent brief periods of rain.
weather with frequent rain showers

Examples

The forecast predicts showery conditions throughout the afternoon.
It was a showery spring day, with bursts of rain alternating with sunshine.
Despite the showery weather, the outdoor event went ahead as planned.

Synset Examples

showery weather
rainy days

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rainy - (of weather) having a lot of rain

Etymology

From 'shower' (rainfall) + '-y' (adjectival suffix).
