raimentless

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪ.mənt.ləs / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.mənt.ləs / (BrE)
without clothing; not wearing any garments, especially used in literary or poetic contexts.
without clothing; unclothed
archaic

Examples

The statue stood raimentless in the moonlight, exposed to every eye.
After the storm, the trees appeared almost raimentless, stripped bare of their leaves.
In myth and legend, gods sometimes walk the earth raimentless among mortals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naked - without any clothes; not covered by clothing
  • Antonym: clothed - wearing clothes; covered with garments

Etymology

Formed from 'raiment' (Middle English, from Old French remaindre ‘to remain’, applied to clothes that remain) + suffix '-less'.
