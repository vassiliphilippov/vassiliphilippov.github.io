far-reaching

1. ADJ

/ fɑr ˈritʃɪŋ / (AmE), / fɑː ˈriːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
Having a wide range or significant influence or effect.
having wide or significant impact

Examples

The new law could have far-reaching consequences for the entire country.
Her scientific discoveries are expected to be far-reaching in their impact on future generations.
The company made far-reaching changes to its structure after the merger.

Synset Examples

The ramification of his decision was far-reaching.
The effects of the atomic bomb were devastating and far-reaching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extensive - having wide scope or influence
  • Antonym: limited - restricted in range or influence
