emergence

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɜrdʒəns / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒəns / (BrE)
the process of coming into view or becoming visible or known after being concealed
the act of becoming visible or known

Examples

The emergence of new technology has changed the way we communicate.
After hours of discussion, a consensus began to emergence among the group.
Scientists were fascinated by the sudden emergence of the rare butterfly species in the area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appearance - the act of becoming visible or present
  • Antonym: disappearance - the act of ceasing to be visible or present

Etymology

From Latin emergere (to rise up, come forth).
