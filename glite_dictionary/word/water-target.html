water-target

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˌtɑrɡət / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌtɑːɡɪt / (BrE)
An aquatic plant with floating oval leaves and purple flowers, typically found in lakes and slow-moving streams; sometimes cultivated in aquariums.
