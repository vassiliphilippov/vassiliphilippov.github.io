openly

1. ADV

/ ˈoʊpənli / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənli / (BrE)
In a way that is not concealed or secret; in a frank or public manner.
in a frank or public manner

Examples

She openly criticized the new policy in front of the entire team.
The couple openly discussed their relationship issues with friends.
He openly admitted his mistake during the meeting.

Synset Examples

he openly flaunted his affection for his sister

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publicly - in a manner observable by people in general; not in private
  • Antonym: secretly - in a way that is not seen or known by others

Etymology

From open + -ly, forming the adverb from the adjective 'open'.
