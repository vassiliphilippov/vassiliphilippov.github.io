moo

1. NOUN

/ muː / (AmE), / muː / (BrE)
the characteristic deep, long sound made by a cow or bull
sound made by a cow or bull

Examples

The quiet of the farm was broken only by the occasional moo from the fields.
A loud moo echoed through the barn as the cows waited to be fed.
Children laughed as they tried to imitate the moo of a passing cow.

Etymology

Imitative of the sound made by cattle.

2. VERB

/ muː / (AmE), / muː / (BrE)
(of a cow or similar animal) to make a long, low sound characteristic of bovines.
make a cow-like low sound

Examples

The cows often moo loudly in the early morning.
As we walked past the farm, a calf suddenly mooed at us.
She could hear the herd mooing contentedly in the fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: low - (of a cow) to make a deep sound
