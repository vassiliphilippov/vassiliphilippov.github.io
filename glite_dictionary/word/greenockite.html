greenockite

1. NOUN

/ ɡrɪˈnɑːkaɪt / (AmE), / ɡrɪˈnɒkaɪt / (BrE)
a rare yellowish mineral consisting of cadmium sulfide, which is the principal ore of cadmium
a rare yellow cadmium mineral

Examples

Greenockite is the only significant ore mineral of cadmium found in nature.
The distinctive yellow color of greenockite makes it easily recognizable to mineralogists.
Specimens of greenockite are highly prized by collectors for their rarity and striking appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cadmium sulfide - chemical compound forming greenockite

Etymology

Named after Lord Greenock, a British military officer; -ite suffix denotes minerals.
