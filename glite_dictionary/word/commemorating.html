commemorating

1. ADJ

/ kəˈmɛməˌreɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / kəˈmɛməreɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
serving as a remembrance or in honor of something or someone; intended as a commemoration.
serving as a remembrance or honor

Examples

The city installed a commemorating statue in the town square to honor the founder.
They held a commemorating ceremony on the anniversary of the historic event.
A commemorating plaque was unveiled at the site of the memorial.

Synset Examples

a commemorative plaque

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commemorative - serving to commemorate; intended as a memorial

Etymology

From Latin commemorare (to call to mind), via English verb commemorate + -ing.
