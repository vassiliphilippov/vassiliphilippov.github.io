cornfield

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈkɔːn.fiːld / (BrE)
A field in which corn is grown.
field where corn is grown

Examples

The cornfield stretched as far as the eye could see under the summer sun.
After the rain, the cornfield looked especially green and vibrant.
They took a shortcut through the cornfield to reach the old barn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maize field - field where maize is grown
