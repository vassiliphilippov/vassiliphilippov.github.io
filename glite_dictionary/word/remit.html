remit

1. VERB

/ rɪˈmɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈmɪt / (BrE)
to send money as a payment for something, especially formally or from a distance
send money as payment

Examples

Please remit the exact amount to the address listed on the invoice.
He remits funds to his family abroad every month.
All students must remit their tuition fees before the deadline.

Synset Examples

remit $25

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pay - to give money that is due for goods or services
  • Antonym: to receive - to be given something, such as money, opposite sense

Etymology

From Latin remittere (send back, relax).
