rustless

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌstləs / (AmE), / ˈrʌstləs / (BrE)
Not affected by rust; free from rust.
not affected by rust

Examples

The stainless steel sink remained rustless even after years of use.
He preferred using rustless nails for outdoor construction to ensure durability.
The company guarantees that all their tools are completely rustless.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rusty - covered with or affected by rust
