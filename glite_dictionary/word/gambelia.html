Gambelia

1. NOUN

/ ɡæmˈbɛliə / (AmE), / ɡamˈbiːlɪə / (BrE)
A genus of large, agile lizards known as leopard lizards, native to arid regions of western North America.
genus of leopard lizards

Examples

The genus Gambelia includes the well-known blunt-nosed leopard lizard of California.
Researchers observed several species of Gambelia basking on desert rocks.
Gambelia lizards can leap considerable distances while hunting for prey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leopard lizard - a large, spotted lizard of western North America in the genus Gambelia

Etymology

Named after the American naturalist William Gambel (1823–1849).
