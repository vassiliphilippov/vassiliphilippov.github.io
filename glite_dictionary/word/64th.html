64th

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪksti ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪksti ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number that is in position sixty-four in a sequence; coming after sixty-third.
ordinal number for sixty-four

Examples

She finished in 64th place in the marathon, a personal best for her.
The 64th annual film festival attracted filmmakers from all over the world.
His desk is on the 64th floor of the skyscraper.
