unexpected

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪkˈspɛktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈspɛktɪd / (BrE)
Not anticipated or foreseen; happening without warning or as a surprise.
not anticipated; surprising

Examples

The storm caused an unexpected delay to the flight schedule.
She received an unexpected gift from an old friend.
The discovery of the ancient ruins was completely unexpected.

Synset Examples

unexpected guests
unexpected news

Related Terms

  • Antonym: expected - regarded as likely to happen; anticipated
  • Synonym: surprising - causing surprise; astonishing

Etymology

From un- (not) + expected, based on Latin expectare (to await).
