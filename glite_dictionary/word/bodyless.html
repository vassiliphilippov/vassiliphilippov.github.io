bodyless

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑdi.ləs / (AmE), / ˈbɒdɪ.ləs / (BrE)
having no body or main part; lacking a physical form or trunk
having no body or main part

Examples

The villain appeared as a bodyless voice echoing through the chamber.
A single bodyless head floated inside the mysterious jar.
Her fears became bodyless shadows haunting her dreams.

Synset Examples

a bodiless head

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disembodied - lacking a physical body; not having a material form

Etymology

From 'body' + '-less', with '-less' meaning 'without'.
