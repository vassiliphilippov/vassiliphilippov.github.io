speedskater

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːdˌskeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈspiːdˌskeɪtə / (BrE)
an athlete who competes in ice skating races, usually around an oval track
athlete who races on ice skates

Examples

The speedskater trained intensively for the upcoming Winter Olympics.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a professional speedskater.
The crowd cheered as the leading speedskater crossed the finish line.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: figure skater - an ice-skater who performs spins, jumps, and choreographed routines

Etymology

From 'speed' + 'skater', meaning one who skates fast.
