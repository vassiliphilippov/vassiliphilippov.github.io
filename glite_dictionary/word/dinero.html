dinero

1. NOUN

/ diˈneɪroʊ / (AmE), / diˈneərəʊ / (BrE)
(informal) Money; cash, used especially as slang or with a playful reference to Spanish.
informal slang for money
informal slang

Examples

I need some dinero before we hit the shops.
He worked all summer just to make some dinero.
They offered plenty of dinero for that old car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: money - general term for currency or medium of exchange
  • Synonym: cash - money in the form of coins or notes

Etymology

From Spanish 'dinero' (money), ultimately from Latin 'denarius' (Roman coin).
