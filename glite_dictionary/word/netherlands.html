Netherlands

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɛðərləndz / (AmE), / ˈnɛðələndz / (BrE)
A country in western Europe on the North Sea, known officially as a constitutional monarchy; much of its land lies below sea level.
country in western Europe

Examples

Tulips are one of the most famous exports of the Netherlands.
The Netherlands is renowned for its windmills and canals.
She traveled to the Netherlands to study at a university in Amsterdam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Holland - an informal or historical name for part of the Netherlands

Etymology

From Dutch 'Nederland', meaning 'low country'; 'nether-' (lower) + 'lands'.
