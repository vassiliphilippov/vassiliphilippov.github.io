rocketry

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːkətri / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪtri / (BrE)
the science and technology of designing, building, and operating rockets
science and technology of rockets

Examples

He studied rocketry at university, eventually joining a space research program.
Advancements in rocketry have made space exploration more accessible.
Her passion for rocketry began after watching a documentary on the Apollo missions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astronautics - the science of the construction and operation of vehicles for travel in space

Etymology

From 'rocket' + '-ry', denoting a field of study or work.
