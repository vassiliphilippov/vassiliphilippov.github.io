Saudi

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaʊdi / (AmE), / ˈsaʊdi / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Saudi Arabia.
person from Saudi Arabia

Examples

The Saudi welcomed us warmly when we arrived in Riyadh.
Many Saudis travel abroad for higher education every year.
She met a Saudi at the international business conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Saudi Arabian - a person from Saudi Arabia

Etymology

Shortened from Saudi Arabian, from the country's ruling dynasty, the House of Saud.

Synset POS: n

2. ADJ

/ ˈsaʊdi / (AmE), / ˈsɔːdi / (BrE)
of or relating to Saudi Arabia or its people.
relating to Saudi Arabia or its people

Examples

The Saudi government announced new reforms last year.
Many Saudi students choose to study abroad in Europe and the United States.
She enjoyed learning about Saudi traditions and celebrations.

Synset Examples

the Saudi-Arabian desert
the Saudi royal family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Saudi Arabian - of or relating to Saudi Arabia or its people

Etymology

From Saudi Arabia, named after the ruling Al Saud family.
