swordswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔrdzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈsɔːdzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is skilled at fencing or sword fighting.
skilled female fencer

Examples

She became a renowned swordswoman after years of training with the national fencing team.
The story follows a fearless swordswoman who defends her village from invaders.
As the only swordswoman in the competition, she inspired many young girls to take up fencing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swordsman - a man skilled at fencing or sword fighting
  • Synonym: fencer - a person skilled in fencing

Etymology

From 'sword' + 'woman', modeled after 'swordsman'.
