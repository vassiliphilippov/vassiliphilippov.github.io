Norfolk

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɔrfək / (AmE), / ˈnɔːfək / (BrE)
a port city in southeastern Virginia, United States, located on the Elizabeth River at the mouth of Chesapeake Bay; known as the headquarters of the Atlantic fleet of the United States Navy.
city in southeastern Virginia, USA

Examples

The naval base in Norfolk is the largest of its kind in the world.
She moved to Norfolk to work at the shipyard.
Norfolk is famous for its bustling port and historic waterfront.

Etymology

Named after Norfolk, a county in England.
