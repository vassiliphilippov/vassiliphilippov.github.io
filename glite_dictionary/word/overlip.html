overlip

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvərlɪp / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəlɪp / (BrE)
the upper lip, especially as distinguished from the lower lip
the upper lip

Examples

The actor's prominent overlip made his facial expressions especially noticeable.
During the dental exam, the dentist carefully examined the patient's overlip for any abnormalities.
The painting emphasized the unique curve of her overlip against the gentle light.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underlip - the lower lip

Etymology

Formed from 'over' (upper) + 'lip'.
