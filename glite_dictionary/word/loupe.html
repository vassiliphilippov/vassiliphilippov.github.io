loupe

1. NOUN

/ lup / (AmE), / luÀêp / (BrE)
a small magnifying glass, typically set in a frame or eyepiece, used especially by jewelers and watchmakers to closely inspect small objects
small jeweler's magnifying glass

Examples

The jeweler examined the diamond closely with his loupe.
She used a loupe to inspect the tiny details on the watch's face.
A loupe is an essential tool in the gemstone trade.

Etymology

From French 'loupe' meaning 'magnifying glass'.
