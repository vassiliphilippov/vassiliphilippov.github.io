nerveroot

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɜːrˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvəˌruːt / (BrE)
A woodland orchid of eastern North America, once common, with rose-pink flowers.
woodland orchid with rose-pink flowers (N. America)

Examples

The delicate nerveroot blooms in shady woodland areas every spring.
Historically, the nerveroot was abundant, but it has become increasingly rare due to habitat loss.
Botanists are working to protect the remaining populations of nerveroot in the wild.
