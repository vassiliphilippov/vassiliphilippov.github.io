Jewishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːɪʃnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being Jewish, especially in relation to culture, religion, or ethnicity.
quality or state of being Jewish

Examples

Her Jewishness was an important part of her identity throughout her life.
The play explores themes of Jewishness and assimilation in twentieth-century America.
He spoke about how Jewishness has influenced his worldview and career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Judaism - the religion, philosophy, and way of life of the Jewish people
