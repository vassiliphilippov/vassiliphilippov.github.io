Internal ID: pet|oewn-10011405-n
a special loved one

Reason

This sense of 'pet' as 'a special loved one' is too general and not specific to the actual noun 'pet' as understood in standard English. The primary meaning of 'pet' is an animal kept for companionship, not a person who is loved, which would be covered by more precise words like 'sweetheart' or 'darling.' This concept is not relevant for inclusion as a separate dictionary entry.
