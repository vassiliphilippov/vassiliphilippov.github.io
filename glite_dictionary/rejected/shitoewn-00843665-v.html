Synset POS: v
Internal ID: shit|oewn-00843665-v
give away information about somebody

Synset Examples

He told on his classmate who had cheated on the exam

Reason

This sense of 'shit' (meaning 'to give away information about somebody') is extremely rare, nonstandard, and not supported by major dictionaries like Oxford. The common standard verb for this sense is 'to tell on' or 'to inform on.' Including it would not be helpful for learners and does not meet criteria for inclusion in a reputable dictionary.
