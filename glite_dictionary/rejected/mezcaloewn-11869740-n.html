Reason

The sense described refers to a specific type of cactus (commonly called peyote or Lophophora williamsii), but the headword 'mezcal' is primarily known as an alcoholic beverage. The sense as 'a small spineless globe-shaped cactus' is not a standard or widely recognized meaning for 'mezcal' in English; thus, this entry would not commonly appear in Oxford or similar dictionaries. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
