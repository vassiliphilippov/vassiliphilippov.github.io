freeze down

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00446666-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: freeze down|oewn-00446666-v
change from a liquid to a solid when cold

Synset Examples

Water freezes at 32 degrees Fahrenheit

Reason

The phrasal verb 'freeze down' is not commonly used or recognized in standard English for describing the process of changing from liquid to solid. The standard verb 'freeze' covers this meaning. This phrase would not appear as a headword or entry in major English dictionaries like Oxford.
