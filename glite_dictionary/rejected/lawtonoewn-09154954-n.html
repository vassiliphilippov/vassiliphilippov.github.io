Lawton

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09154954-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Lawton|oewn-09154954-n
a town in southwest Oklahoma

Reason

'Lawton' in this sense refers to a specific place (a town in Oklahoma), but its synset is as a general place-name. Standard English dictionaries generally do not include names of most towns or cities unless there is widespread international significance or figurative use. This term is more appropriate for a geographical dictionary or gazetteer.
