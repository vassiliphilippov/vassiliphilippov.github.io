designate

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: v
Internal ID: designate|oewn-00748465-v
decree or designate beforehand

Synset Examples

She was destined to become a great pianist

Reason

This synset refers to a highly specific sense of 'designate' meaning 'to decree or determine beforehand,' which is more accurately captured by words like 'destine' or 'ordain.' The example sentence uses 'destined,' not 'designate.' This meaning does not correspond to standard or common uses of 'designate' as they appear in major English dictionaries. Therefore, this specific concept should not be included as an entry for 'designate.'
