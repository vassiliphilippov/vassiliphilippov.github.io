meeter

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09631032-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: meeter|oewn-09631032-n
a person who is present and participates in a meeting

Synset Examples

he was a regular attender at department meetings
the gathering satisfied both organizers and attendees

Reason

The term 'meeter' in this sense is not standard or recognized in English; common terms are 'attendee,' 'participant,' or 'attender.' 'Meeter' is not used in reputable dictionaries for this meaning, so it should not be included.
