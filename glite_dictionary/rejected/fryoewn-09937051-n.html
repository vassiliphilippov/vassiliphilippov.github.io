Reason

The sense 'fry' as a young person is obscure and not in current general use in English. The Oxford Dictionary does not list 'fry' in this sense for people; it is not relevant or familiar enough to be included as a dictionary headword in this meaning. The main recognized senses relate to cooking or to young fish, but not young humans.
