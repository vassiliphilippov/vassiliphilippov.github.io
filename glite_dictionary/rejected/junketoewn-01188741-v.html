junket

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01188741-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: junket|oewn-01188741-v
provide a feast or banquet for

Reason

The verb sense 'junket' meaning 'to provide a feast or banquet for' is extremely rare and outdated in contemporary English, and the noun and a more common verb sense ('to go on an official trip, especially one funded by someone else') would be included instead. This rare verb sense does not meet inclusion standards for modern dictionaries such as Oxford.
