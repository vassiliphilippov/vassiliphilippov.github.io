Reason

The concept 'utilised' as 'made useful' is an inflected form of the verb 'utilise' and not a distinct lexical item. Dictionary entries are typically for the base form ('utilise' or 'utilized' in American spelling), not for participle or inflected forms unless they have a separate, unique meaning. 'Utilised' does not have a meaning different from 'utilise', so it should not have its own dictionary entry.
