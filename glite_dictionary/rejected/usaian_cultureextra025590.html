Reason

The term 'USAian culture' is not a standard or commonly accepted English headword. The standard term is 'American culture' for the culture of the United States. 'USAian' is rarely used and does not appear in major English dictionaries. The synset here is also overly general, tied to the concept of 'culture' as it relates to the U.S.; thus, this would not be included in a standard English dictionary such as Oxford.
