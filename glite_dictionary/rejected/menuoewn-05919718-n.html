menu

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05919718-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: menu|oewn-05919718-n
an agenda of things to do

Synset Examples

they worked rapidly down the menu of reports

Reason

This sense of 'menu' as 'an agenda of things to do' is not commonly used in this way in standard English. The standard definition of 'menu' refers to a list of food or drink options or a list of selectable options in computing interfaces, not a general agenda of tasks. This sense is too rare and does not meet typical dictionary inclusion standards.
