in the closet

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: in the closet|extra.018217
inside a storage space or cupboard

Synset Examples

He keeps his old toys in the closet.

Reason

The phrase 'in the closet' in this sense simply means 'inside a storage space or cupboard' and is a literal use of 'in' plus 'closet.' This is not an idiomatic or compound expression; it is a direct, compositional phrase that does not warrant a separate dictionary entry beyond the headwords 'in' and 'closet.'
