spergy comment

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: spergy comment|extra.023508
a comment perceived as socially awkward

Synset Examples

He made a spergy comment that made everyone laugh.

Reason

The concept 'spergy comment' is derived from 'spergy', which itself is an offensive slang term related to 'Asperger's syndrome' or autistic traits. It is a highly informal and offensive slang term that is not appropriate for inclusion in a standard English-English dictionary, especially as a collocation with 'comment'. It is not an established phrase that would appear as a dictionary entry in Oxford or similar dictionaries. Including it could perpetuate stigma and would not be educationally or socially responsible.
