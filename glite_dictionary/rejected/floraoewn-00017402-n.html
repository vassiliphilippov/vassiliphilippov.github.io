flora

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00017402-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: flora|oewn-00017402-n
(botany) a living organism lacking the power of locomotion

Reason

This sense of 'flora' as 'a living organism lacking the power of locomotion' is too broad and general, referring to 'plant' or broadly to entities that do not move, and is not the specific, conventional sense of 'flora' as used in dictionaries. Such a general biological definition does not reflect standard usage, so it should not be included.
