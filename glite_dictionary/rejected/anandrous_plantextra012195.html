a plant lacking water

Synset Examples

In anandrous plants, the absence of water was evident.

Reason

The term 'anandrous plant' is incorrectly defined in this synset as 'a plant lacking water.' In botanical usage, 'anandrous' refers to a plant lacking stamens (male reproductive organs), not to water content. This incorrect or nonstandard synset does not represent an established meaning accepted in major dictionaries (like the Oxford Dictionary), nor does it correspond to a standard English term in common use. Therefore, it should not be included as presented.
