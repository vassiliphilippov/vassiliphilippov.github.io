nebulose

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00785533-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: nebulose|oewn-00785533-s
lacking definite form or limits

Synset Examples

gropes among cloudy issues toward a feeble conclusion
nebulous distinction between pride and conceit

Reason

The concept 'nebulose' is extremely rare, archaic, and not used in contemporary English. The standard and much more common English adjective is 'nebulous'. 'Nebulose' would not appear as a headword in standard modern dictionaries such as Oxford, and is thus excluded.
