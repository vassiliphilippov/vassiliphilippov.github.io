Bohemian

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10173755-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Bohemian|oewn-10173755-n
a member of a people with dark skin and hair who speak Romany and who traditionally live by seasonal work and fortunetelling; they are believed to have originated in northern India but now are living on all continents (but mostly in Europe, North Africa, and North America)

Reason

This sense of 'Bohemian' refers to an ethnic group (the Roma people) but the term is considered outdated and problematic in this context. Modern dictionaries discourage using 'Bohemian' to refer to the Romani people, as it can be incorrect and offensive. Instead, the primary meanings of 'Bohemian' relate to the region of Bohemia or a person with an unconventional lifestyle, not an ethnic group. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
