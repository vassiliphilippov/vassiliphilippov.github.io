Annam

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09185985-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Annam|oewn-09185985-n
a communist state in Indochina on the South China Sea; achieved independence from France in 1945

Reason

The synset here refers to 'Annam' as a modern communist state in Indochina, but Annam is not the name of the present-day country (Vietnam); it is a historical region of Vietnam. The word 'Annam' as the name of a current country would not be included as a main dictionary entry, as it is now archaic and not used to refer to the state described. Instead, 'Vietnam' is the proper headword for that context. Thus, this sense is not appropriate for inclusion according to the guidelines.
