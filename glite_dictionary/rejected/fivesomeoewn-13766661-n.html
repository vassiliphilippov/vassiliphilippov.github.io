the cardinal number that is the sum of four and one

Reason

The concept 'fivesome' with the sense 'the cardinal number that is the sum of four and one' is not appropriate for inclusion in a standard English-English dictionary. The standard term for the number 5 is 'five'; 'fivesome' in English usually refers to a group of five people/objects, not the number itself. Including it with this meaning would be misleading and does not reflect common real-world usage.
