Reason

The noun sense of 'forbidding' as 'an official prohibition or edict against something' is not commonly used in contemporary English and does not appear in standard dictionaries like Oxford as a noun. The usual noun forms are 'forbid' (verb) or 'forbiddance' or 'prohibition' (nouns), not 'forbidding'. Therefore, this sense should not be included in the dictionary.
