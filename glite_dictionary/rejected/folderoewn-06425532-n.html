folder

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06425532-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: folder|oewn-06425532-n
a small book usually having a paper cover

Reason

This sense ('folder' as a small book with a paper cover) is extremely rare and not standard; 'folder' generally refers to a container for papers, not a small book. The concept is too obscure and would not appear as such in a standard English-English dictionary, such as Oxford.
