some other

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02077592-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: some other|oewn-02077592-s
any of various alternatives; some other; entity, singular, other pronoun; quantifier, singular, another

Synset Examples

put it off to another (or some other) day

Reason

'some other' in this sense is a generic quantifier phrase composed of 'some' + 'other' and does not represent a distinct lexical item or idiom. It would not have its own entry in standard dictionaries such as Oxford; its meaning is fully compositional from its parts.
