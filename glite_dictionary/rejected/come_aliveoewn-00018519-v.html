stop sleeping

Synset Examples

She woke up to the sound of the alarm clock

Reason

The phrase 'come alive' in the sense of 'stop sleeping' is not in standard use; native speakers do not use 'come alive' to mean 'wake up' or 'stop sleeping.' Its typical use means 'become lively or exciting,' but not 'stop sleeping.' Therefore, this sense should not be included in the dictionary.
