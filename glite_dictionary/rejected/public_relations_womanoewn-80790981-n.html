public relations woman

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-80790981-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: public relations woman|oewn-80790981-n
a woman employed to arrange publicity (for a firm or a public figure)

Reason

This concept refers to a gendered version of a profession ('public relations woman') that is simply the female equivalent of 'public relations person' or 'public relations officer'. It is not a standard dictionary entry and is too specific, as dictionaries generally do not list gendered forms separately unless they are common established terms (which this is not).
