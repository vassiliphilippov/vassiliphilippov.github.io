Synset Examples

Reason

This sense of 'crop' meaning 'to feed (as in a meadow or pasture)' is outdated and extremely rare, and 'crop' is not commonly used as a verb with this meaning in modern English. The primary term for this action is 'graze.' Therefore, this sense is too obscure for inclusion in a general dictionary.
