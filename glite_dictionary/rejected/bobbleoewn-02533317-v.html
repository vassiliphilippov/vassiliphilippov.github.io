bobble

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02533317-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: bobble|oewn-02533317-v
make a mess of, destroy or ruin

Synset Examples

I botched the dinner and we had to eat out
the pianist screwed up the difficult passage in the second movement

Reason

The sense of 'bobble' meaning 'make a mess of, destroy or ruin' is extremely rare and nonstandard. The common usage of 'bobble' as a verb is 'to move with a bouncing or jerking motion' or 'to fumble', especially with regard to catching something. The 'botch' or 'ruin' sense is not widely attested in reputable dictionaries and would not appear as a main entry or common definition. Therefore, it should not be included.
