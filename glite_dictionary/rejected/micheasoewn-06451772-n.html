Micheas

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06451772-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Micheas|oewn-06451772-n
an Old Testament book telling the prophecies of Micah foretelling the destruction of Jerusalem

Reason

The term 'Micheas' refers to the Book of Micah in the Old Testament, but in English it is not commonly used (the standard English form is 'Micah'). 'Micheas' is a Latin or alternate form and would not appear as a headword in a standard English dictionary such as Oxford; therefore, it should not be included.
