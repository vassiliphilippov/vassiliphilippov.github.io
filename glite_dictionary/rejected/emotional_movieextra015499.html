a film designed to evoke strong feelings

Synset Examples

Reason

The concept 'emotional movie' is a compositional phrase: it simply combines the adjective 'emotional' with the noun 'movie' and means 'a movie that evokes emotion.' This is not an established compound or set phrase in English, and would not appear as a separate entry in Oxford or similar dictionaries. Such descriptive, transparent combinations do not merit inclusion as headwords.
