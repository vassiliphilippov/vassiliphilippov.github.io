vet

Synset POS: v
Internal ID: vet|oewn-00080342-v
provide (a person) with medical care

Reason

This sense of 'vet' as 'provide (a person) with medical care' is not standard usage in English. 'Vet' as a verb commonly means 'to examine or check something or someone carefully,' and as a noun, it refers to 'veterinarian' or 'veteran.' The use of 'vet' specifically to mean 'provide a person with medical care' is not recognized in standard dictionaries and would not appear in Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, it should not be included.
