Reason

This sense of 'exchange' as 'to exchange a penalty for a less severe one' is not a standard or widely recognized meaning in English dictionaries. The usual legal term for reducing a penalty is 'commute,' and 'exchange' is not used with this specific meaning in mainstream usage. Therefore, this sense should not be included as a standalone entry.
