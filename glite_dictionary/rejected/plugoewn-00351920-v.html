Reason

This verb sense ('plug' meaning 'to persist in working hard') only appears in the dictionary as part of the phrasal verb 'plug away'. The base form 'plug' with this meaning does not appear on its own in natural usage. Therefore, the concept is too closely tied to the phrasal verb and should not be included as a stand-alone dictionary entry for the verb 'plug' with this sense.
