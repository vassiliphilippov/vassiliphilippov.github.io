fly in a flock

Synset Examples

flighting wild geese

Reason

While 'flight' is a legitimate verb in the sense of 'to fly in a flock', this usage is archaic and extremely rare in modern English. It would not appear as a primary or even secondary definition in contemporary dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary, and most speakers and writers are unfamiliar with this verb sense. It is therefore not suitable for general dictionary inclusion.
