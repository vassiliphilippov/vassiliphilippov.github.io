Laws

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06463561-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Laws|oewn-06463561-n
the first of three divisions of the Hebrew Scriptures comprising the first five books of the Hebrew Bible considered as a unit

Reason

This sense refers to 'Laws' as a synonym for the division of the Hebrew Scriptures more commonly known as the 'Torah' or 'Pentateuch', which is a proper noun or specific title and not a general English word or meaning of 'laws'. This usage is extremely rare and would not appear as an independent entry in a standard English-English dictionary like Oxford. If included, it would be a sense note under 'Pentateuch' or 'Torah', not 'laws'.
