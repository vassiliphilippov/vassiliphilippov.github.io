the capital and largest city of Turkmenistan

Reason

The concept 'capital of Turkmenistan' refers to a general role or status (i.e., the city that is the capital of Turkmenistan), not to a specific proper noun or standalone lexical entry. Dictionaries typically include the actual city name (e.g., Ashgabat), not this descriptive phrase. Including it would be too general and is not standard practice for English-language dictionaries like Oxford.
