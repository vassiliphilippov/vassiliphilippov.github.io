mainframe

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02998952-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: mainframe|oewn-02998952-n
(computer science) the part of a computer (a microprocessor chip) that does most of the data processing

Synset Examples

the CPU and the memory form the central part of a computer to which the peripherals are attached

Reason

The synset definition is incorrect for 'mainframe.' The described sense refers to the CPU or central processing unit of a computer, specifically the microprocessor chip. 'Mainframe' in standard English refers to a large, powerful computer used mainly by large organizations. This sense would not be included in a dictionary under the headword 'mainframe.'
