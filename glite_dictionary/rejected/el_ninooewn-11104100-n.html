El Nino

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11104100-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: El Nino|oewn-11104100-n
the Christ child

Reason

This concept corresponds to the Spanish phrase 'El Niño' meaning 'the Christ child', but in standard English usage 'El Niño' most commonly refers to the climate phenomenon, not to the Christ child. The sense 'the Christ child' is not in common English usage, so it should not be included in the dictionary as an English entry.
