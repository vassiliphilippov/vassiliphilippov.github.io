crystallization

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09283372-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: crystallization|oewn-09283372-n
a rock formed by the solidification of a substance; has regularly repeating internal structure; external plane faces

Reason

This sense of 'crystallization' does not correspond with standard English usage; 'crystallization' refers to the process of forming crystals, not to the crystal itself (the rock). The synset describes the object (a crystal), not the process, and this sense is too general and not aligned with dictionary treatment of the term.
