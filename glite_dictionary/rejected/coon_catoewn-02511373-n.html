coon cat

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02511373-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: coon cat|oewn-02511373-n
raccoon-like omnivorous mammal of Mexico and the southwestern United States having a long bushy tail with black and white rings

Reason

The synset relates to the animal species, but 'coon cat' here refers incorrectly to a raccoon-like mammal native to Mexico and the southwestern U.S., which is actually a 'ringtail' or 'bassarisk.' 'Coon cat' in common English refers to the Maine Coon cat, a domestic cat breed, not a wild mammal. This sense is misleading and not suitable for a general English dictionary entry.
