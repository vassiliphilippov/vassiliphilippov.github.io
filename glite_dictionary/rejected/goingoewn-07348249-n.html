Synset Examples

Reason

Although the sense description refers to 'going' as a euphemism for death, in standard English usage, 'going' alone is not commonly or idiomatically used to mean 'death' (unlike 'passing' or 'passing away'). This would not appear as a standalone entry or primary sense for 'going' in the Oxford Dictionary. Thus, it should be excluded.
