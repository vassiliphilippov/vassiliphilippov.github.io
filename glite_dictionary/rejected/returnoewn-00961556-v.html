Reason

This synset is about a very general sense of 'return' as 'go back to something earlier', which is already well covered under the main verb senses of 'return'. As described, it's not a distinct enough sense for a separate dictionary entry; more specific usages like 'hark back' may merit their own entries, but not this vague, general sense.
