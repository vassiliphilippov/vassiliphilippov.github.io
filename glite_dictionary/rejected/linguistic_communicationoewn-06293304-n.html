linguistic communication

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06293304-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: linguistic communication|oewn-06293304-n
a systematic means of communicating by the use of sounds or conventional symbols

Synset Examples

he taught foreign languages
the language introduced is standard throughout the text
the speed with which a program can be executed depends on the language in which it is written

Reason

The synset refers to the general concept of language or linguistic communication as a system but the headword 'linguistic communication' itself is not standard dictionary headword and would not appear as a primary entry in a dictionary like Oxford. The standard entry is 'language' for this sense.
