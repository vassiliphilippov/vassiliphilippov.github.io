attractor

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10052401-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: attractor|oewn-10052401-n
an entertainer who attracts large audiences

Synset Examples

he was the biggest drawing card they had

Reason

The synset refers to a very rare sense of 'attractor' meaning 'an entertainer who attracts large audiences.' This sense is not in common use; standard dictionaries do not list 'attractor' in this sense (the common senses are 'something or someone that attracts,' especially in science or mathematics as in 'strange attractor'). The specific show-business sense is better represented by terms like 'drawing card' or 'headline act.' Therefore, this concept should not be included as a dictionary entry.
