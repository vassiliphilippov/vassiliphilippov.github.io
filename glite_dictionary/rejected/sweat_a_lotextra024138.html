Synset Examples

Reason

The phrase 'sweat a lot' is a transparent combination of the verb 'sweat' and the adverb 'a lot'. It does not have an idiomatic or fixed meaning beyond what the literal meanings of its parts provide. Such straightforward verb-adverb collocations are not included in standard dictionaries unless they are idiomatic or have a specific usage that differs from the sum of their parts.
