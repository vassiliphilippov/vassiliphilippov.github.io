censure

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14437397-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: censure|oewn-14437397-n
the state of being excommunicated

Reason

This sense of 'censure' as 'the state of being excommunicated' is rare and obsolete. In contemporary English usage, 'censure' usually refers to strong disapproval or formal criticism, not excommunication. Therefore, this sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry for modern learners.
