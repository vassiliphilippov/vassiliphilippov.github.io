a class of synthetic detergents in which the molecules do not ionize in aqueous solutions

Reason

The concept 'anionic' as described here is a noun referring to a class of synthetic detergents, but this sense is not standard or common in English dictionaries. 'Anionic' is widely used as an adjective to describe substances that behave as anions, but not as a noun for a detergent class. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate dictionary entry in this noun sense.
