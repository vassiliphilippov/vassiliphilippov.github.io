take

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01934453-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: take|oewn-01934453-v
proceed along in a vehicle

Synset Examples

We drive the turnpike to work

Reason

This sense of 'take' is too context-specific and not a standard meaning that would appear as its own sense in a major dictionary. The generic verb 'take' does mean to use a route or means of transport, but 'proceed along in a vehicle' as in 'We drive the turnpike to work' does not correspond to a separate dictionary sense for 'take.' The actual sense is covered under 'take (a road, route, etc.)' meaning 'to travel on or follow,' which is already an established sense in dictionaries. The current definition is redundant and overly narrow.
