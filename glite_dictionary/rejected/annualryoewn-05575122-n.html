annualry

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: annualry|oewn-05575122-n
the third finger (especially of the left hand)

Reason

The word 'annualry' for the third finger (ring finger) is obsolete and not in contemporary usage; standard dictionaries like Oxford do not list it, and the concept as defined here is rare and archaic. The word 'ring finger' is used for this concept in modern English.
