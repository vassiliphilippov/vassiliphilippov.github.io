middling

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00036138-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: middling|oewn-00036138-r
to certain extent or degree

Synset Examples

pretty big
pretty bad
jolly decent of him
the shoes are priced reasonably
he is fairly clever with computers

Reason

The adverbial sense of 'middling' as 'to a certain extent' is not current in standard English and is not commonly used or recognized as an adverb meaning 'fairly' or 'reasonably.' The dictionary should not include this adverbial use, as it is at best obsolete or dialectal, and would not appear in a major dictionary like Oxford. The examples given actually fit 'fairly' or 'reasonably,' not 'middling.'
