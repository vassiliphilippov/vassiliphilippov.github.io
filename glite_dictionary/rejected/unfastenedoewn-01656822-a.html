affording unobstructed entrance and exit; not shut or closed

Synset Examples

Reason

The concept 'unfastened' with the sense 'affording unobstructed entrance and exit; not shut or closed' is too broad for this specific word. The correct headword for this synset is 'open', which is commonly used in this sense. 'Unfastened' typically refers to something not secured by fasteners (e.g., buttons, zippers) and does not naturally mean 'not shut or closed' in the context of doors or entrances. Therefore, this concept should be excluded from the dictionary in this sense.
