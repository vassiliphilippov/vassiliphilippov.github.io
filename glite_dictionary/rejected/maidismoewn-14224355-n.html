maidism

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14224355-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: maidism|oewn-14224355-n
a disease caused by deficiency of niacin or tryptophan (or by a defect in the metabolic conversion of tryptophan to niacin); characterized by gastrointestinal disturbances and erythema and nervous or mental disorders; may be caused by malnutrition or alcoholism or other nutritional impairments

Reason

The term 'maidism' is an extremely rare and obsolete synonym for pellagra, and does not appear in modern English dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. It has essentially disappeared from contemporary English and is not relevant or useful for learners. It is also not a recognizable technical or historical term outside of highly specialized contexts.
