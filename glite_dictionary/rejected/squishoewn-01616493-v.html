Synset POS: v

put (a liquid) into a container or another place by means of a squirting action

Reason

This specific sense of "squish" (meaning 'to put a liquid into a container by means of a squirting action') is not a standard or widely recognized English verb usage. In dictionaries like Oxford, 'squish' refers to crushing, squeezing, or compressing something with a soft sound or to a soft, squishy feeling. The described sense is better matched by verbs like 'squirt' or 'squeeze.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
