Reason

The concept 'Mich.' is an abbreviation for 'Michigan' (the U.S. state), and standard English dictionaries typically do not include state abbreviations as dictionary headwords. The full noun 'Michigan' would be included, but its abbreviation 'Mich.' is better handled in a separate section for geographical abbreviations, not as a main entry.
