Reason

The word 'hydraulicly' is a nonstandard or incorrect spelling of 'hydraulically.' It does not appear in major dictionaries like Oxford, and the correct and widely accepted adverbial form is 'hydraulically.' Therefore, it should not be included as a headword in the dictionary.
