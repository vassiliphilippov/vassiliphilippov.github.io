hamper

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04188289-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: hamper|oewn-04188289-n
a restraint that confines or restricts freedom (especially something used to tie down or restrain a prisoner)

Reason

This specific sense of 'hamper' as a noun meaning a physical restraint or shackle is archaic and not in current use. In modern English, 'hamper' as a noun most commonly refers to a basket for laundry or food. The sense described here would not appear in a current Oxford Dictionary.
