conjunctive

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06336819-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: conjunctive|oewn-06336819-n
an uninflected function word that serves to conjoin words or phrases or clauses or sentences

Reason

The noun sense "conjunctive" as an uninflected function word is too specialized and rarely used; in standard English, the commonly recognized term is "conjunction" (the part of speech), not "conjunctive" as a noun. The adjective form (as in 'conjunctive adverb') would be included, but not this noun sense.
