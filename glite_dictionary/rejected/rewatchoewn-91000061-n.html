to watch a show or television one more time

Reason

The synset describes 'rewatch' as a noun meaning 'to watch a show or television one more time,' which is not standard usage. 'Rewatch' is commonly used as a verb, not a noun. The sense given here is a misapplication, so the noun form with this meaning does not warrant inclusion as a separate entry.
