Hopei

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08743759-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Hopei|oewn-08743759-n
a populous province in northeastern China

Reason

'Hopei' is an obsolete romanization of 'Hebei', the current standard spelling for the province in northeastern China. Modern English dictionaries use 'Hebei', so 'Hopei' as a lemma for the current province is archaic and not standard. This concept does not need a contemporary dictionary entry.
