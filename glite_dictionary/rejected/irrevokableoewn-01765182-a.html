irrevokable

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01765182-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: irrevokable|oewn-01765182-a
incapable of being retracted or revoked

Synset Examples

firm and irrevocable is my doom

Reason

The standard and widely accepted spelling for this word is 'irrevocable,' not 'irrevokable.' The form 'irrevokable' is a rare, nonstandard, or erroneous variant and does not appear as a headword in major English dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate dictionary entry.
