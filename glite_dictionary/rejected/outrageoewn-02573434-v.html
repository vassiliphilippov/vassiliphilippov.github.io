outrage

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02573434-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: outrage|oewn-02573434-v
force (someone) to have sex against their will

Synset Examples

The woman was raped on her way home at night

Reason

The concept 'outrage' as a verb meaning 'to force someone to have sex against their will' is archaic and no longer current in modern English; the standard verb is 'rape.' This sense does not appear in contemporary dictionaries and should not be included. The entry would be confusing and misleading for users, as 'outrage' is not used in this way today.
