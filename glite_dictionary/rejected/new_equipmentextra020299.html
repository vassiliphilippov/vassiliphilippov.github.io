new equipment

modern or updated tools and machinery

Synset Examples

We need new equipment for the gym to attract members.

Reason

The concept 'new equipment' combines an adjective and a noun in a transparent, compositional way, and is not a fixed phrase or idiom with meaning beyond its parts. It is simply the noun 'equipment' modified by 'new.' Such combinations are not included in major dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary unless they form idioms or set phrases, which is not the case here.
