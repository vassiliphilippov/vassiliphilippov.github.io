sensify

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00273190-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: sensify|oewn-00273190-v
make sensitive or aware

Synset Examples

He was not sensitized to her emotional needs

Reason

The verb 'sensify' is not in standard contemporary usage and is not found in major English dictionaries. The sense 'make sensitive or aware' is more accurately rendered by the verb 'sensitize.' Including 'sensify' would not be appropriate for a general English dictionary akin to the Oxford Dictionary, as it is not established in common or authoritative usage.
