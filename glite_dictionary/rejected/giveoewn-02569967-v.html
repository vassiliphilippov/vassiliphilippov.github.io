Reason

This synset is a verbal sense of 'give' in a specialized legal context meaning 'to execute and deliver' (e.g., 'give bond'), but it is not a distinct sense from the general 'give' definition of 'to hand over or provide.' The specific legal phrase 'give bond' would be better covered under 'bond' or as part of a phrasal entry. Including this highly technical nuance as a standalone dictionary sense would be unnecessarily granular for general learners. The concept is not sufficiently distinct for individual dictionary entry beyond the main senses of 'give.'
