Samson

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09898168-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Samson|oewn-09898168-n
a large and strong and heavyset man

Synset Examples

he was a bull of a man
a thick-skinned bruiser ready to give as good as he got

Reason

The synset describes a type of person (a large, strong man) and uses 'Samson' as an analogy to the biblical figure, but in modern English, 'Samson' is primarily a proper noun referring to the biblical character or used as a given name. It is not commonly used generically as a common noun to mean 'a large, strong man,' and would not be included as such in major dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
