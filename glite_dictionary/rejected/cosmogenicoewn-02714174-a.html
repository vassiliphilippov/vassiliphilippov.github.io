cosmogenic

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02714174-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: cosmogenic|oewn-02714174-a
pertaining to the branch of astronomy dealing with the origin and history and structure and dynamics of the universe

Synset Examples

cosmologic science
cosmological redshift
cosmogonic theories of the origin of the universe

Reason

The synset definition actually refers more closely to 'cosmological' or 'cosmologic', not 'cosmogenic'. Additionally, 'cosmogenic' properly means 'produced by cosmic rays' or 'originating in outer space', not specifically pertaining to the origin and history of the universe. The provided synset is too broad/general and not closely related to the headword 'cosmogenic'. This sense should not be included.
