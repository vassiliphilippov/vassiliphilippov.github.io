smuttiness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14522556-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: smuttiness|oewn-14522556-n
the state of being dirty with soot

Reason

The sense 'smuttiness' as 'the state of being dirty with soot' is rare and highly specific. In modern and general usage, 'smuttiness' overwhelmingly refers to something obscene or indecent rather than being dirty with soot. The general sense 'the state of being dirty with soot' would not be included as a primary dictionary entry in contemporary dictionaries. This meaning is obsolete and not useful for the modern learner.
