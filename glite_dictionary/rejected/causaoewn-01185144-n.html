causa

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01185144-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: causa|oewn-01185144-n
a comprehensive term for any proceeding in a court of law whereby an individual seeks a legal remedy

Synset Examples

the family brought suit against the landlord

Reason

The term 'causa' is not commonly used in modern English legal language. The concept refers to a legal proceeding (such as 'case', 'lawsuit', 'suit', or 'action') and does not appear as an established English headword in standard English dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, it should not be included.
