Synset Examples

Reason

The phrase 'may falter' is simply a modal verb ('may') followed by the verb 'falter' and is not a fixed expression or a lexical unit. The meaning is compositional and does not qualify as a dictionary entry. Only 'falter' as a headword would be included in a dictionary, not the phrase 'may falter.'
