Synset POS: v
Internal ID: plume|oewn-01394734-v
clean with one's bill

Synset Examples

The birds preened

Reason

The verb sense of 'plume' meaning 'to clean with one's bill' is only used in reference to birds and is far less common than the synonym 'preen.' It is quite rare and specialized, and would not typically appear in general English dictionaries like Oxford under this sense. Therefore, it should be excluded.
