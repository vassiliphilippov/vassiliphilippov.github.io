steaming

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00424753-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: steaming|oewn-00424753-r
(used of heat) extremely

Synset Examples

the casserole was piping hot

Reason

The sense of 'steaming' as an adverb meaning 'extremely' or as an intensifier for heat is extremely rare and not commonly recognized as a standalone adverb in modern English. The example 'piping hot' does not use 'steaming' in this adverbial sense. As such, it would not be included in a standard dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary in this form. The primary use of 'steaming' is as a present participle/adjective (e.g., 'steaming vegetables', 'steaming hot'). Therefore, this sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
