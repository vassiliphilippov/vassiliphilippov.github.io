abo identity

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: abo identity|extra.000154
Aboriginal identity, often relating to culture and heritage

Synset Examples

She embraced her abo identity through art and storytelling.

Reason

The concept 'abo identity' incorporates an abbreviation 'abo' that is widely considered offensive and derogatory in reference to Aboriginal people. Such phrases are not standard or acceptable in published English dictionaries like Oxford, and the inclusion of this entry would not be appropriate outside of very specific offensive language documentation. Furthermore, the term is rarely used outside pejorative contexts and is not recognized as an established standalone noun phrase meaning 'Aboriginal identity.'
