Reason

This sense of 'honest' as 'not forged' is an extremely rare, archaic, and contextually specialized usage that would not appear as a primary or secondary definition in a standard English-English dictionary such as Oxford. The typical senses of 'honest' relate to truthfulness and integrity, not genuineness of physical objects or currency. Therefore, this concept should not be included.
