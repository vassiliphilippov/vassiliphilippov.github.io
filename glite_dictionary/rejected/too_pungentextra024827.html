too pungent

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: too pungent|extra.024827
overly strong or intense in smell

Synset Examples

Her perfume was too pungent for my taste.

Reason

'Too pungent' is not a dictionary headword or set phrase; it is a compositional phrase made from 'too' (an intensifier) and 'pungent' (a normal adjective). Dictionaries list 'pungent', not every possible intensifier construction.
