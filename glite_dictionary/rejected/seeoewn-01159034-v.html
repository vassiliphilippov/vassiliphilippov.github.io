match or meet

Synset Examples

I saw the bet of one of my fellow players

Reason

This sense of 'see' as 'match or meet,' specifically in the context of betting (as in poker), is highly specialized and does not commonly appear in general dictionaries as a separate sense. Instead, most dictionaries list the sense 'to match a bet' under the poker term 'see (a bet),' not as a general meaning of the verb 'see.' This is generally covered under specialized gaming or poker terminology, not as a main entry for 'see.'
