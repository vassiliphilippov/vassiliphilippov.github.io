beginner

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10127072-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: beginner|oewn-10127072-n
a person who founds or establishes some institution

Synset Examples

George Washington is the father of his country

Reason

The sense description and example are mismatched: 'beginner' does not mean a person who founds or establishes an institution (that would be 'founder' or 'originator'). This synset is not related to the headword 'beginner' in standard English usage. Therefore, it should be excluded.
