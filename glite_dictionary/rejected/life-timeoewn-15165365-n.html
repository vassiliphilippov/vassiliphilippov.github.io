life-time

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15165365-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: life-time|oewn-15165365-n
the period during which something is functional (as between birth and death)

Synset Examples

the battery had a short life
he lived a long and happy life

Reason

The synset described here is the general concept of 'period during which something is functional,' which is usually covered under the main entry for 'life' rather than a distinct entry for 'life-time' (which is an uncommon spelling; the standard form is 'lifetime'). Thus, the concept as given is too general and does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
