Euphorbia litchi

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-12764112-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Euphorbia litchi|oewn-12764112-n
tree of southeastern Asia to Australia grown primarily for its sweet edible fruit resembling litchi nuts; sometimes placed in genera Euphorbia or Nephelium

Reason

This concept should not be included because the synset is for a plant species (Euphorbia litchi) but the main description and botanical reference seem to actually refer to 'litchi' or 'lychee' (Litchi chinensis), and the placement in genera Euphorbia is not botanically accurate. Euphorbia is a large and unrelated genus. This appears to be a misattributed/botanical error and would not appear as a dictionary headword in major English dictionaries.
