food that is somewhat spicy

Synset Examples

He preferred spicish foods that tingled his taste buds.

Reason

The term 'spicish' is an extremely rare, informal, and nonstandard formation based on the word 'spicy.' It does not appear in standard dictionaries, is not widely recognized, and is not a commonly accepted word in English. Its synset (describing food that is 'somewhat spicy') is covered by words like 'mildly spicy' or 'slightly spicy.' Therefore, it should not be included in a standard English-English dictionary modeled after Oxford.
