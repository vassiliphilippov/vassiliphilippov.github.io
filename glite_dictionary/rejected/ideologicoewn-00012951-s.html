ideologic

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00012951-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: ideologic|oewn-00012951-s
concerned with or suggestive of ideas

Synset Examples

ideological application of a theory
the drama's symbolism was very ideological

Reason

The concept 'ideologic' as an adjective is not commonly used in modern English; the standard and widely accepted form is 'ideological.' 'Ideologic' is a rare variant and would not typically appear in major dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, it should be excluded.
