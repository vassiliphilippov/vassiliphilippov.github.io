endurance

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13985502-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: endurance|oewn-13985502-n
a state of surviving; remaining alive

Reason

This sense of 'endurance' as merely 'a state of surviving; remaining alive' is too generic and does not reflect how the word is actually used in English. In modern dictionaries, 'endurance' is defined in terms of the ability to withstand hardship, stress, or prolonged effort, rather than simply the state of being alive or surviving. Therefore, this concept should not be included for this sense.
