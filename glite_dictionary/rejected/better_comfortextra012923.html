better comfort

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: better comfort|extra.012923
increased ease or relaxation

Synset Examples

He wore soft shoes to the dance for better comfort.

Reason

'Better comfort' is a noun phrase formed by combining the comparative 'better' with 'comfort', but it is not an established compound or idiom and does not have a separate meaning from the sum of its parts. It simply refers to greater comfort. Such phrases are not included as standalone dictionary entries in quality dictionaries like Oxford.
