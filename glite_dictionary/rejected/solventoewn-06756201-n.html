solvent

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06756201-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: solvent|oewn-06756201-n
a statement that solves a problem or explains how to solve the problem

Synset Examples

they were trying to find a peaceful solution
the answers were in the back of the book
he computed the result to four decimal places

Reason

The concept described corresponds to the meaning of 'solution' or 'answer', not 'solvent'. 'Solvent' is not used in English with the sense of 'a statement that solves a problem or explains how to solve the problem'; that sense is captured by 'solution'. This is a mismatched synset for the headword and should be excluded.
