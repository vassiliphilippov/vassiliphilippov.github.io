7th president of the US; successfully defended New Orleans from the British in 1815; expanded the power of the presidency (1767-1845)

Reason

The concept 'Jackson' in this sense refers specifically to an individual person, Andrew Jackson, whose synset in WordNet is simply 'person.' Proper names of individual historical figures whose meanings are just their identity (i.e., the person, not a specialist term or phrase) are not typically included as dictionary headwords in dictionaries like Oxford. These are either entries in biographical reference works or encyclopedias, not standard narratives dictionaries.
