institution

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00241051-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: institution|oewn-00241051-n
the act of starting something for the first time; introducing something new

Synset Examples

she looked forward to her initiation as an adult
the foundation of a new scientific society

Reason

This synset refers to 'institution' as the act of starting or founding something for the first time (an event or action), which is not a primary or widely recognized sense of 'institution' in modern English dictionaries. The core noun 'institution' regularly refers to an established organization or a practice/custom, not the founding act itself, so this sense is too obscure and not standard for common dictionary inclusion.
