avoirdupois

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05006566-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: avoirdupois|oewn-05006566-n
excess bodily weight

Synset Examples

she disliked fatness in herself as well as in others

Reason

The sense 'avoirdupois' meaning 'excess bodily weight' is extremely rare and obsolete in modern English. In contemporary usage, 'avoirdupois' almost exclusively refers to the system of weights used in the UK and US. The sense relating to body weight is archaic and would not appear in current Oxford-style dictionaries.
