renter

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10540397-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: renter|oewn-10540397-n
an owner of property who receives payment for its use by another person

Reason

This sense defines 'renter' as an owner who rents property to others, but standard English usage defines 'renter' as a person who rents and occupies property (the tenant), not the owner (who is called a 'landlord' or 'lessor'). Including this erroneous sense would mislead learners. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
