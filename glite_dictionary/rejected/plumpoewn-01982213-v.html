Synset POS: v
Internal ID: plump|oewn-01982213-v
drop sharply

Synset Examples

The stock market plummeted

Reason

The verb sense 'plump' meaning 'drop sharply' is obsolete and not in contemporary general or even specialized use (the standard word is 'plummet'). This usage would not appear in Oxford Dictionary and is not helpful for most learners.
