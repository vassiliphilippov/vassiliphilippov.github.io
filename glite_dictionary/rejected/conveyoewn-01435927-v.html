convey

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01435927-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: convey|oewn-01435927-v
go or come after and bring or take back

Synset Examples

Get me those books over there, please
Could you bring the wine?
The dog fetched the hat

Reason

This synset describes the sense of physically bringing or fetching something, but the headword 'convey' is not used in standard English to mean 'go or come after and bring or take back.' The example sentences all illustrate 'fetch', 'bring', 'get', but not 'convey'. Therefore, this concept is too general and not relevant to the actual usage or meaning of 'convey'. It should not be included.
