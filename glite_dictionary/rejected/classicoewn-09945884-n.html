Reason

This sense of 'classic' refers to 'an artist who has created classic works', which is not an established or standard meaning in modern English. The noun 'classic' as used for a person/artist is not in common use and does not appear in major dictionaries like Oxford. The primary senses of 'classic' as a noun are for works themselves ('a classic') or as an adjective. Therefore, it does not merit inclusion as an independent dictionary entry.
