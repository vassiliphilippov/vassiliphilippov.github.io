Krakau

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09005783-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Krakau|oewn-09005783-n
an industrial city in southern Poland on the Vistula

Reason

'Krakau' is the German name for the city known in English as 'Krakow.' English dictionaries would include 'Krakow,' not the German exonym 'Krakau.' Therefore, 'Krakau' should be excluded from an English-English dictionary.
