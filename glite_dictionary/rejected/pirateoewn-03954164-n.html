pirate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03954164-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: pirate|oewn-03954164-n
a ship that is manned by pirates

Reason

This concept ('pirate' as 'a ship that is manned by pirates') is too general and does not correspond to standard dictionary usage. In English, 'pirate' typically refers to a person, not a ship; the term for such a ship would be 'pirate ship.' As such, including 'pirate' with this sense would be confusing and is not found in major English dictionaries.
