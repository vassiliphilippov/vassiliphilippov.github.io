discomfited

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07962721-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: discomfited|oewn-07962721-n
people who are defeated

Synset Examples

the Romans had no pity for the defeated

Reason

The synset is for 'people who are defeated', but 'discomfited' is not used as a plural noun meaning 'defeated people' in standard English. It is mainly used as a verb or adjective (to discomfit, or as an adjective describing someone who is uneasy or embarrassed). Therefore, this noun sense should not be included.
