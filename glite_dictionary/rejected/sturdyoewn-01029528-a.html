Reason

This synset relates to the meaning 'not making concessions', which is better captured by the word 'uncompromising' rather than 'sturdy'. In standard usage, 'sturdy' refers to physical strength or firmness. The sense given is not a common or recognized definition for 'sturdy' in contemporary English dictionaries, so it should be excluded.
