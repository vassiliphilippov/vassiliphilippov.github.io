Reason

The synset is overly general and refers to 'Bearer of the Sword' as an organization, but the WordNet sense provided is simply a definition for the specific group 'Abu Sayyaf.' 'Bearer of the Sword' does not appear as a recognized name or term in standard English dictionaries for this group. Major English dictionaries (such as Oxford Dictionary) do not have an entry for 'Bearer of the Sword' as a specific organization; instead, this is just a literal translation of 'Abu Sayyaf.' The concept is not widely recognized or established enough under this English phrase to merit inclusion.
