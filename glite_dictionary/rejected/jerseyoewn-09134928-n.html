Jersey

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09134928-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Jersey|oewn-09134928-n
a Mid-Atlantic state on the Atlantic; one of the original 13 colonies

Reason

This sense refers to 'New Jersey,' a U.S. state. The headword 'Jersey' alone does not commonly refer to the state; 'New Jersey' is the established and correct usage. Therefore, this concept is too general and would not appear under the headword 'Jersey' in a dictionary for this meaning.
