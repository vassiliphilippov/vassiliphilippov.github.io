Reason

The concept 'cow sociology' refers to a very specific academic study area (the social behavior in cattle) formed by combining 'cow' and 'sociology.' It is not a set term or widely recognized phrase, nor would it appear as a headword in authoritative dictionaries like Oxford. Such phrases are typically handled as the sum of their parts, and this synset is too specific and compositional.
