Synset POS: v
Internal ID: apprise|oewn-00316406-v
increase the value of

Synset Examples

The Germans want to appreciate the Deutsche Mark

Reason

The verb 'apprise' meaning 'to increase the value of' is not standard and does not appear in reputable dictionaries in this sense. The example provided refers to 'appreciate' (to increase the value of a currency), not 'apprise'. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
