strong duty

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: strong duty|extra.023909
a significant moral obligation

Synset Examples

She felt a strong duty to volunteer at the shelter.

Reason

The concept 'strong duty' is a compositional phrase made up of the adjective 'strong' modifying the noun 'duty'. This is not an idiomatic or fixed expression, and its meaning is straightforwardly derived from its parts. Such adjective-noun combinations are too general and would not appear as separate entries in the Oxford Dictionary.
