lightly

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00392845-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: lightly|oewn-00392845-r
with few burdens

Synset Examples

experienced travellers travel light

Reason

This concept is too general and not meaningfully distinct from the main adverbial uses of 'light' or 'lightly'. The synset describes a manner of traveling with few burdens ('travel light'), but 'lightly' is not standardly used in this sense; the usual phrase is 'travel light'. Since 'lightly' does not commonly mean 'with few burdens' on its own, this sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
