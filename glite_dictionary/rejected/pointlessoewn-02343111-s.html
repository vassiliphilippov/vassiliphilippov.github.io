Synset Examples

a scoreless inning

Reason

The synset 'having no points scores' for 'pointless' is highly specific and rarely used. In standard English, 'scoreless' is the usual term for describing a game or period where no points have been scored. The main dictionary entry for 'pointless' means 'having no purpose or meaning,' which is already covered. The specialized sports sense does not need a separate entry for general dictionaries and does not appear in Oxford or similar sources for 'pointless.'
