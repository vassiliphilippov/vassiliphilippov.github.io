band

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08257090-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: band|oewn-08257090-n
an unofficial association of people or groups

Synset Examples

the smart set goes there
they were an angry lot

Reason

This synset refers to 'band' as a general association or group of people, which is too vague and unspecific. The examples use words like 'set' and 'lot' for similar senses. 'Band' is commonly defined in the sense of a group of musicians or a group united for a particular purpose, but this broad 'unofficial association of people or groups' usage is too general to warrant a dictionary entry under 'band.' It would not appear as such in a quality dictionary.
