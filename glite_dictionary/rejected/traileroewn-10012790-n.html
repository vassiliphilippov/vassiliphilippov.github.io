trailer

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10012790-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: trailer|oewn-10012790-n
someone who takes more time than necessary; someone who lags behind

Reason

The sense of 'trailer' meaning 'someone who lags behind' is extremely rare and not commonly used in modern English; this sense is not current and would not appear in major dictionaries like Oxford. The headword 'trailer' is much more commonly used for 'vehicle for hauling' or 'preview for a film.' This sense is too obscure and not dictionary-worthy for learners.
