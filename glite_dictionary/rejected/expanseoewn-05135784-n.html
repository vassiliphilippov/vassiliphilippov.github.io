expanse

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05135784-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: expanse|oewn-05135784-n
the extent of a 2-dimensional surface enclosed within a boundary

Synset Examples

the area of a rectangle
it was about 500 square feet in area

Reason

This sense of 'expanse' is defined as 'the extent of a 2-dimensional surface enclosed within a boundary', which specifically refers to the mathematical concept of 'area'. The usual lexical sense of 'expanse' refers to a wide and continuous surface or stretch, not the measurable quantity of area. This mathematical meaning is covered by the headword 'area', not 'expanse'. Therefore, this concept does not need a separate entry for 'expanse' in the dictionary.
