Reason

The sense 'antique' as 'an elderly man' is extremely rare, archaic, and not attested as a standard or recognizable English usage. Major dictionaries do not record 'antique' with this meaning. The usual senses of 'antique' relate to objects from a previous era, not people. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
