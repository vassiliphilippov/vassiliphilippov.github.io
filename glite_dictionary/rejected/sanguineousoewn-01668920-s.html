Synset POS: s
Internal ID: sanguineous|oewn-01668920-s
confidently optimistic and cheerful

Reason

This synset represents 'sanguineous' as an adjective meaning 'confidently optimistic and cheerful', but the standard and widely used English word for that sense is 'sanguine'. 'Sanguineous' in modern English primarily means 'relating to blood' and is used in technical or medical contexts. Its use for 'cheerful and optimistic' is extremely rare and archaic, so this concept does not merit a dictionary entry. The proper entry for this meaning would be 'sanguine' (adj.), not 'sanguineous'.
