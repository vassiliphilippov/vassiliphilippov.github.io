Sir Alexander Mackenzie

Canadian explorer (born in England) who explored the Mackenzie River and who was first to cross North America by land north of Mexico (1764-1820)

Reason

The synset here is 'person', which is too general and relates only to the fact that Sir Alexander Mackenzie is a person. Biographical entries for individuals with only generic hypernyms like 'person' are not included in dictionaries like Oxford; they are found in encyclopedias instead.
