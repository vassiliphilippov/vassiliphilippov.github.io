skulking

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00742570-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: skulking|oewn-00742570-n
evading duty or work by pretending to be incapacitated

Synset Examples

they developed a test to detect malingering

Reason

The concept described by this synset (evading duty or work by pretending incapacity) is actually more accurately captured by the noun 'malingering'. The noun 'skulking' primarily refers to moving stealthily or hiding, and is not generally used to specifically mean feigning incapacity to avoid work. Therefore, this particular synset meaning is too narrow/specific to be an accepted sense of 'skulking' in a general-purpose English dictionary.
