comprehend

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02110960-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: comprehend|oewn-02110960-v
to become aware of through the senses

Synset Examples

I could perceive the ship coming over the horizon

Reason

This synset describes 'comprehend' as 'to become aware of through the senses', but in standard usage 'comprehend' means 'to understand' rather than 'to perceive through the senses'. The sense described here is better suited to 'perceive' or 'sense', not 'comprehend'. Therefore, this specific concept is too semantically distant from the actual headword meaning and should not be included in the dictionary under 'comprehend'.
