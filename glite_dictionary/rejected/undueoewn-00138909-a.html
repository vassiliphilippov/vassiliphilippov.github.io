undue

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00138909-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: undue|oewn-00138909-a
not yet payable

Synset Examples

an undue loan

Reason

The sense of 'undue' as 'not yet payable' is highly technical, obsolete, and rarely used outside historical or legal contexts. It is not a standard or recognizable meaning for general English learners and would not typically appear with this sense in modern dictionaries. The commonly understood sense of 'undue' (meaning 'excessive' or 'inappropriate') is kept elsewhere.
