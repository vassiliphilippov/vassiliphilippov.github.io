driveller

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10053388-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: driveller|oewn-10053388-n
a person who dribbles

Synset Examples

that baby is a dribbler; he needs a bib

Reason

The concept 'driveller' here is defined as a person who dribbles (saliva), but this word is extremely rare and not in common usage in this literal sense. The common and correct term is 'dribbler', especially for babies and sports. 'Driveller' is sometimes used for someone who talks nonsense (from 'drivel'), which is a different sense. Therefore, as defined here, it should not be included as it is not a standard or recognized usage for modern learners.
