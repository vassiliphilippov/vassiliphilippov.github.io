Reason

The concept 'K.E.' refers to 'kinetic energy,' and as an abbreviation written with periods, it is not commonly used as a standalone entry in major English dictionaries. The full term 'kinetic energy' should be included instead. 'K.E.' as an abbreviation is too technical and context-specific for general dictionaries unless it is exceptionally widespread, which is not the case here.
