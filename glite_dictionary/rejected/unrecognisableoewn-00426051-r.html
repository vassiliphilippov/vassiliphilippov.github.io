WordNet ID: oewn-00426051-r
Synset POS: r

beyond recognition; in an unrecognizable manner

Synset Examples

he had unrecognizably aged

Reason

This entry is for the adverbial form 'unrecognisably', not the adjective 'unrecognisable'. The adverb form is a straightforward derivation of the adjective and follows regular patterns in English. Such derived adverbs (with -ly) are typically not given separate dictionary entries unless they have unique usage or meaning. 'Unrecognisably' simply means 'in an unrecognisable manner,' which is directly deducible. Therefore, it does not warrant a standalone entry in a dictionary like Oxford.
