ducking

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00278413-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: ducking|oewn-00278413-n
the act of wetting something by submerging it

Reason

This sense of 'ducking' as 'the act of wetting something by submerging it' is highly technical, obscure, and not currently used in general English. It is not a distinct sense that would appear in major dictionaries like Oxford; the verb 'to duck' (submerge quickly) is covered, but the nominal usage for this particular meaning is not common enough for inclusion.
