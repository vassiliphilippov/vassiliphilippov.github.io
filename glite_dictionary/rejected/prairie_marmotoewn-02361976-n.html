prairie marmot

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02361976-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: prairie marmot|oewn-02361976-n
any of several rodents of North American prairies living in large complex burrows having a barking cry

Reason

The synset definition refers to 'prairie marmot', but the common and standard term for this animal is 'prairie dog'. 'Prairie marmot' is an incorrect or extremely rare synonym and would not appear as a headword in Oxford Dictionary; readers should be directed to the proper term 'prairie dog'.
