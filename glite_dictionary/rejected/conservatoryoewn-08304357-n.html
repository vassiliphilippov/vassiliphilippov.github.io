conservatory

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08304357-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: conservatory|oewn-08304357-n
the faculty and students of a school specializing in one of the fine arts

Reason

This sense of 'conservatory' as referring specifically to the faculty and students of a school specializing in the fine arts is not commonly recognized as a primary or distinctive meaning in major English dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary. Typically, 'conservatory' refers to the institution or the building itself rather than its collective members. The sense is overly general ('the people of an institution') and does not merit a separate dictionary entry.
