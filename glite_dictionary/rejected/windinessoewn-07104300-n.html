boring verbosity

Reason

The sense of 'windiness' meaning 'boring verbosity' is attested and meaningful. However, in this context, the main headword 'windiness' would be defined as 'the quality or state of being windy' (related to wind), and the figurative sense meaning 'verbosity' is relatively rare, archaic, and is almost never used in modern English. The main synset associated here appears too general and not prominent enough for dictionary inclusion under this figurative usage. It does not warrant a separate modern dictionary entry in line with Oxford standards.
