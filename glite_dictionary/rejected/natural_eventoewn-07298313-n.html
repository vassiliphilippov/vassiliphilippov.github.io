natural event

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07298313-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: natural event|oewn-07298313-n
an event that happens

Reason

The concept "natural event" in this context is defined too generally as simply "an event that happens," which does not relate to the more specific and established sense of 'natural event' (e.g., a flood, earthquake, etc., as opposed to man-made events) that would appear in a standard dictionary. Here, it is just a generic label for any event, which is too broad and not lexically distinct. Therefore, it should be excluded.
