Reason

This is a very narrow, specialized verb sense ("to invest money in government securities") that is not commonly recognized or regularly distinguished as a separate verb meaning of 'fund' in standard dictionaries. Main dictionaries list 'fund' as providing financial resources, especially for a purpose or project, but do not specifically define it as 'to invest in government securities.' As such, this sense is too narrow for a general-purpose dictionary and does not warrant a separate entry.
