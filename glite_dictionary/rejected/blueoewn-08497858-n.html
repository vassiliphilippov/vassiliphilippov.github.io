blue

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08497858-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: blue|oewn-08497858-n
any organization or party whose uniforms or badges are blue

Synset Examples

the Union army was a vast blue

Reason

This concept refers to 'blue' as a generic label for any group whose uniform is blue, which is too broad and not a distinct sense that would be included in standard dictionaries like Oxford. This figurative, context-dependent usage does not warrant a separate entry.
