Reason

The sense 'footer' meaning 'a person who travels by foot' is archaic and extremely rare in modern or even historical English; it does not appear in standard dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The common meaning of 'footer' in English relates to the bottom section of a page or a type of kick in football, not a pedestrian. Therefore, this sense should not be included in a contemporary English dictionary.
