adjunct

Synset POS: s
Internal ID: adjunct|oewn-02363453-s
furnishing added support

Synset Examples

an ancillary pump
an adjuvant discipline to forms of mysticism
The mind and emotions are auxiliary to each other

Reason

The sense described ("furnishing added support") is broader than the headword "adjunct" and actually relates to the adjectives "ancillary","adjuvant", and "auxiliary" as used attributively. 'Adjunct' as an adjective (s, attributive) is extremely rare or obsolete in this sense, and does not merit a dictionary entry in standard modern English. This sense would not appear in the Oxford Dictionary under 'adjunct' as an adjective.
