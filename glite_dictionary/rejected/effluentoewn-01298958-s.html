WordNet ID: oewn-01298958-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: effluent|oewn-01298958-s
that is flowing outward

Reason

This sense of 'effluent' as an adjective meaning 'flowing outward' is archaic and extremely rarely used in modern English. The noun sense of 'effluent' (waste liquid released into the environment) is included in dictionaries, but this participial/adjunct use as an adjective is not in current usage and would not typically appear in a modern dictionary for learners.
