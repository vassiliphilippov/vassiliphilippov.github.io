Synset POS: s
Internal ID: existing|oewn-01853029-s
existing in something specified

Synset Examples

depletion of the oxygen existing in the bloodstream

Reason

This sense is an attributive participial adjective from the verb 'exist' meaning 'present in something specified.' This is a function of the main adjective 'existing' which is already covered under its general adjectival sense, not as a separate dictionary entry for 'existing in [something]'. The synset is too context-dependent and not a distinct dictionary sense suitable for a headword entry.
