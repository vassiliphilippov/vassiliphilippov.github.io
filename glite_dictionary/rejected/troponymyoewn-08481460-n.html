troponymy

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08481460-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: troponymy|oewn-08481460-n
the place names of a region or a language considered collectively

Reason

The definition provided for 'troponymy' is actually the definition for 'toponymy' (the study or collection of place names), and 'troponymy' as a noun is not a recognized lexical item in general English. In linguistics, 'troponymy' refers to a semantic relation between verbs (where one verb is a specific manner of another), but not to place names. Thus, this concept is either a misattributed term or an error and should be filtered out.
