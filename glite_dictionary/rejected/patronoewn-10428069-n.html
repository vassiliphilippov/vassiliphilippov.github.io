patron

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10428069-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: patron|oewn-10428069-n
the proprietor of an inn

Reason

The concept 'patron' as 'the proprietor of an inn' is archaic and extremely rare in modern English. In current usage, 'patron' almost always refers to a customer or supporter, not an owner or innkeeper. This sense would not typically appear as a separate entry in contemporary English dictionaries such as Oxford unless marked very specifically as archaic and regional. Therefore, it should be excluded.
