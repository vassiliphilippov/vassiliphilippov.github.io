Synset Examples

Reason

The phrase 'all that remained' functions as a compositional phrase rather than a fixed expression or idiom. Its meaning is the sum of its parts ('all' + 'that remained'), and it is not a lexicalized, dictionary-worthy unit. Major dictionaries do not include such non-idiomatic, purely compositional phrases.
