do it to someone

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: do it to someone|extra.015125
have an effect on someone

Synset Examples

Too much coffee? That'll do it to you.

Reason

This concept is an informal phrasal use of 'do it to someone,' but it is not an established phrasal verb or idiom commonly listed in major dictionaries. The synset is too general and refers to a meaning (having an effect) that can be inferred from the normal use of 'do' and 'it.' Therefore, it does not meet the criteria for inclusion.
