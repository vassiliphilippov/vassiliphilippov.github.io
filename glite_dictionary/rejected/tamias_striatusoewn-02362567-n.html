Tamias striatus

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02362567-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Tamias striatus|oewn-02362567-n
small striped semiterrestrial eastern American squirrel with cheek pouches

Reason

The concept 'Tamias striatus' is the scientific (Latin) name for the eastern chipmunk. Dictionaries like Oxford include the common English word 'chipmunk', not the binomial Latin name as a headword. Therefore, this concept should be excluded as it would not appear in an English-English dictionary under this form.
