season

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00295646-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: season|oewn-00295646-v
make more temperate, acceptable, or suitable by adding something else

Synset Examples

she tempered her criticism

Reason

The synset is for the verb 'season' in the sense of 'make more temperate or suitable by adding something else,' but the given example ('she tempered her criticism') and the definition actually fit the verb 'temper.' The use of 'season' in this very general, abstract sense is not current, standard, or recognizable and would not appear in a modern English dictionary; only 'season' as in 'add flavor' is standard as a verb. Therefore, this sense does not merit a dictionary entry for 'season.'
