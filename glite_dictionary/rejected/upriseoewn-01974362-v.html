Reason

The use of 'uprise' to mean 'come up, of celestial bodies' (e.g., the sun 'uprising') is archaic and extremely rare in modern English. The primary and standard verb in this context is 'rise.' Including this very rare and antiquated usage would not be helpful to most learners and would not be found as a headword sense in modern English dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
