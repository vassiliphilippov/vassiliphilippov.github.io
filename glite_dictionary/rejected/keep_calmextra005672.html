keep calm

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: keep calm|extra.005672
to remain calm or composed

Synset Examples

The trainer used a muzzle to keep the aggressive dog calm.
Just breathe and stay calm during the interview.

Reason

The concept 'keep calm' is a free combination (verb + adjective) and does not constitute a fixed phrase with a special meaning beyond the meanings of 'keep' and 'calm.' Such transparent collocations are not usually included as separate entries in major dictionaries like Oxford, unless they form an idiom (e.g., 'keep calm and carry on'). Therefore, it should not be included.
