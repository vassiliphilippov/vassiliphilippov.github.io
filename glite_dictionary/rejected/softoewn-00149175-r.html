soft

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00149175-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: soft|oewn-00149175-r
in a relaxed manner; or without hardship

Synset Examples

just wanted to take it easy
the judge went easy on the young defendant

Reason

This concept represents the adverbial sense of 'easy' as in 'take it easy' or 'go easy', but is connected to 'soft' only synonymously in rare or fixed expressions. The synset is too general and not specifically tied to 'soft' as an adverb in standard modern usage. This adverbial sense would not have an entry for 'soft' itself in a standard dictionary like Oxford; 'easy' would be the headword.
