place

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08665191-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: place|oewn-08665191-n
a space reserved for sitting (as in a theater or on a train or airplane)

Synset Examples

he booked their seats in advance
he sat in someone else's place

Reason

The synset is too general; it refers to 'place' in the sense of a reserved seat, but 'place' has many broader senses. Dictionary entries would not define a separate, specific sub-sense solely for 'a seat or spot reserved for someone,' as this usage is compositional and not unique or idiomatic. This sense can be covered under general meanings of 'place.'
