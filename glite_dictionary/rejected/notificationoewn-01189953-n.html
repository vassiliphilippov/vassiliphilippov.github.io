notification

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01189953-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: notification|oewn-01189953-n
an accusation of crime made by a grand jury on its own initiative

Reason

The synset described here refers to a legal accusation made by a grand jury on its own initiative (technically, an 'indictment' or a 'presentment'), not the general noun 'notification.' This sense is too specialized, obsolete, and not directly tied to the standard or modern use of 'notification.' The main use of 'notification' in English is as an act of informing or a message informing someone of something, not a grand jury accusation. This legal sense does not appear in general-use dictionaries for 'notification.'
