Hindian culture

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: Hindian culture|extra.017837
the culture associated with Hinduism

Synset Examples

The Hindian culture has a rich history of traditions.

Reason

The term 'Hindian culture' is not a widely recognized or standard term in English; the appropriate and established terms are 'Hindu culture' or 'Indian culture.' 'Hindian' is not commonly used and does not appear in authoritative dictionaries, so it should be excluded.
