steady down

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00416323-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: steady down|oewn-00416323-v
become settled or established and stable in one's residence or life style

Synset Examples

He finally settled down

Reason

The concept 'steady down' is essentially a variant or confused form of the much more standard and common phrasal verb 'settle down'. 'Steady down' is not in standard usage with this meaning in contemporary English and would not appear as a headword or main entry in reputable dictionaries like Oxford. The actual phrase in use for this sense is 'settle down', which should be included instead.
