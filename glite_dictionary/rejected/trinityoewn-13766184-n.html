trinity

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13766184-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: trinity|oewn-13766184-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of one and one and one

Reason

This concept represents the cardinal number three, but 'trinity' is not used in standard English to refer to the number three itself; it is primarily used for the theological concept or to refer to a group of three closely related things. The sense of 'trinity' as merely the number three would not appear in a reputable dictionary as a standalone definition.
