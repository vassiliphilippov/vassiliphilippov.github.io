Revere

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11278491-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Revere|oewn-11278491-n
American silversmith remembered for his midnight ride (celebrated in a poem by Longfellow) to warn the colonists in Lexington and Concord that British troops were coming (1735-1818)

Reason

The synset here is 'person' (a specific individual, Paul Revere). Standard English-English dictionaries like Oxford typically do not include proper names of historical figures unless they have become dictionary words in their own right (e.g., 'sandwich'). 'Revere' in this sense is a proper noun referring to a person, so it does not meet the inclusion criteria.
