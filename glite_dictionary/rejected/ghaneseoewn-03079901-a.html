Ghanese

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03079901-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: Ghanese|oewn-03079901-a
of or relating to or characteristic of Ghana or its people or language

Synset Examples

Ghanaian cocoa production

Reason

The correct demonym and adjective for something or someone from Ghana is 'Ghanaian', not 'Ghanese'. 'Ghanese' is not standard or commonly accepted in modern English and would not appear in major dictionaries like Oxford. This concept should be excluded.
