Reason

This sense of 'under' is highly context-dependent and adverbial in a way that does not represent a distinct, dictionary-worthy meaning separate from the general spatial preposition/adverb meanings of 'under.' There is no distinct, commonly recognized entry for 'under' as 'below the horizon' in major dictionaries; it is a normal use of the core word. Thus, it is too context-specific to be listed as a separate entry.
