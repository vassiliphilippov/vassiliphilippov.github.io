aline

Synset POS: v

place in a line or arrange so as to be parallel or straight

Synset Examples

align the car with the curb
align the sheets of paper on the table

Reason

The sense described by 'aline' as 'place in a line or arrange so as to be parallel or straight' is an alternative spelling or infrequently used form of 'align.' 'Align' is the standard and widely used headword. 'Aline' is extremely rare and obsolete in modern English, and would not appear as a primary verb entry in current general-use dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, it should not be included as its own dictionary entry.
