nervus

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05481998-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: nervus|oewn-05481998-n
any bundle of nerve fibers running to various organs and tissues of the body

Reason

The term 'nervus' is Latin and not used as an English word. In English, 'nerve' is the correct term for a bundle of nerve fibers. 'Nervus' may appear in anatomical Latin names (e.g., nervus opticus), but would not be included as a dictionary headword in an English-English dictionary like Oxford. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
