spying

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00151907-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: spying|oewn-00151907-n
the act of detecting something; catching sight of something

Reason

'Spying' as 'the act of detecting something; catching sight of something' is an overly general sense that is not specific to the word and not how 'spying' is used in modern English. The standard sense—'the act of secretly watching or gathering information', often related to espionage—should be included, but this particular concept is too broad and does not correspond to actual dictionary usage. Therefore, it should be excluded.
