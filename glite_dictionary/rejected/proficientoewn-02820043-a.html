proficient

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02820043-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: proficient|oewn-02820043-a
of or relating to technique or proficiency in a practical skill

Synset Examples

his technical innovation was his brushwork
the technical dazzle of her dancing

Reason

This sense of 'proficient' is not appropriate; the sense described is actually for 'technical', as in 'technical skill', which refers to technique or proficiency, not to 'proficient' itself. 'Proficient' relates to being skilled or competent, not to technique, so this particular sense-synset should be excluded.
