so

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00119259-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: so|oewn-00119259-r
in a manner that facilitates

Synset Examples

he observed the snakes so he could describe their behavior
he stooped down so he could pick up his hat

Reason

This sense of 'so' as an adverb meaning 'in a manner that facilitates' is too general and does not relate directly to a specific, dictionary-worthy sense distinct from its primary usage as an adverb, conjunction, or interjection. The example sentences actually use 'so' as a conjunction ('so that'), not as an independent adverb. This meaning would not be included as a separate entry in a standard dictionary like Oxford. Therefore, it should be excluded.
