punctuate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01015376-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: punctuate|oewn-01015376-v
to stress, single out as important

Synset Examples

Dr. Jones emphasizes exercise in addition to a change in diet

Reason

The synset 'to stress, single out as important' is not the primary meaning of 'punctuate' in general English usage. Instead, 'punctuate' is primarily used to mean 'to insert punctuation marks.' The sense here as 'to emphasize' is rare, nonstandard, and would not be listed in a typical English dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary under 'punctuate.'
