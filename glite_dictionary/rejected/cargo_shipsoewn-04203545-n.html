cargo ships

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04203545-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: cargo ships|oewn-04203545-n
conveyance provided by the ships belonging to one country or industry

Reason

This concept refers to a general category of conveyance as provided by 'ships belonging to one country or industry,' which is too broad and abstract. The headword 'cargo ships' normally refers to a type of ship, not to the collective conveyance system of a nation or industry. This sense does not align with what would appear as a discrete entry in a standard English dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary. Only the main sense of 'cargo ship' (a ship designed to carry goods) is lexically relevant.
