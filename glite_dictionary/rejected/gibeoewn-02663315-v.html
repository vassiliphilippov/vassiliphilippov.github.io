gibe

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02663315-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: gibe|oewn-02663315-v
be compatible, similar or consistent; coincide in their characteristics

Synset Examples

The two stories don't agree in many details
The handwriting checks with the signature on the form
The suspect's fingerprints don't match those on the gun

Reason

This sense of 'gibe' ('be compatible, similar or consistent') is not contemporary, standard, or attested in major dictionaries; 'gibe' in modern English functions as a noun or verb meaning to mock or taunt, not to match or coincide. This sense appears to be a confusion with 'jibe,' which does mean 'to be in accord' (especially in American English). Therefore, including this sense under 'gibe' would be incorrect and misleading.
