capital of the state of Minnesota; located in southeastern Minnesota on the Mississippi river adjacent to Minneapolis; one of the Twin Cities

Reason

The synset 'capital of Minnesota' is a general descriptor for the political status of a place, not a specific place name. Synsets like this, which describe a city by its property as a 'capital', are too general and would not be listed as entries in a dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary. Instead, the actual place name (e.g., 'Saint Paul') is the entry that would be included in a dictionary.
