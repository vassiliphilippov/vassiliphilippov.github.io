pearl

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13924278-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: pearl|oewn-13924278-n
a shape that is spherical and small

Synset Examples

he studied the shapes of low-viscosity drops
beads of sweat on his forehead

Reason

This concept refers to a very general meaning of 'pearl', specifically the shape (a small sphere), which is not a distinct dictionary sense but rather a technical or metaphorical extension. The usual dictionary senses of 'pearl' are as a gemstone (the nacreous product of a mollusk) or a metaphor for something precious or beautiful. The general sense of 'a small spherical shape' does not warrant a separate entry in a standard English dictionary like Oxford. Therefore, it should be excluded.
