Reason

This sense of 'evoke' (deduce a principle or construe a meaning) does not correspond to the common usage of the word 'evoke'. It appears too specific and is not standard for this headword in major English dictionaries. Typical meanings of 'evoke' involve bringing to mind or eliciting a response, not deducing or construing, so this sense should not be included.
