Reason

The concept 'newgen of entrepreneur' is not a standard or established term in English, nor is 'newgen' (short for 'new generation') used widely enough or in a form that would be included in a dictionary like Oxford. The synset is also too general, simply referring to 'a new generation of business innovators', which is sufficiently described by existing words like 'entrepreneur' and 'new generation of entrepreneurs.' Including this would not add distinct lexical value.
