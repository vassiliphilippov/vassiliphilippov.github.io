need

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00023953-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: need|oewn-00023953-n
the psychological feature that arouses an organism to action toward a desired goal; the reason for the action; that which gives purpose and direction to behavior

Synset Examples

we did not understand his motivation
he acted with the best of motives

Reason

The synset refers to the general psychological concept of 'motivation' or 'motive', which is distinct from common dictionary uses of 'need' (as in necessity or requirement). The definition and examples do not reflect conventional meanings of 'need' as a noun in standard English dictionaries. Therefore, this concept is too general and not appropriately linked to the headword 'need' for inclusion.
