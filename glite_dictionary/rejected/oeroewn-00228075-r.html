throughout a period of time

Synset Examples

stay over the weekend

Reason

The synset described as 'throughout a period of time' with example 'stay over the weekend' is linked to the adverb 'over,' not 'o'er.' 'O'er' is a poetic contraction of 'over,' but it is not commonly used with this adverbial temporal sense. In modern dictionaries, this usage is associated with 'over,' and 'o'er' is typically only included in its archaic/poetic sense of 'over' as a preposition. This particular synset is too general and mismatched for 'o'er.'
