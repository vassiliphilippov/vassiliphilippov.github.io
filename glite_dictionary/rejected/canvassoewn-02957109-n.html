canvass

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: canvass|oewn-02957109-n
a heavy, closely woven fabric

Reason

This concept represents the noun 'canvass' with the sense of 'a heavy, closely woven fabric', but the correct spelling for the fabric is 'canvas'. 'Canvass' as a noun relates to solicitation or survey, not fabric. Therefore, this particular sense for 'canvass' is too broad/confused and should not be included.
