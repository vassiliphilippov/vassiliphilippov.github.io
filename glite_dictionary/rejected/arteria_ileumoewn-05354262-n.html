arteria ileum

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05354262-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: arteria ileum|oewn-05354262-n
branch of the superior mesenteric artery that supplies the ileum

Reason

The concept 'arteria ileum' is a technical anatomical term (Latin) for a branch of the superior mesenteric artery supplying the ileum, but this exact term is not used in standard English medical terminology. Instead, the correct English term is 'ileal artery'. As such, the term 'arteria ileum' would not appear in general or even standard medical English dictionaries. Therefore, it should be excluded.
