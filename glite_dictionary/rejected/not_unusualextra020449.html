not unusual

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: not unusual|extra.020449
common or expected behavior

Synset Examples

It's ununusual for him to arrive late to meetings.

Reason

The phrase 'not unusual' is a compositional and transparent negation of 'unusual,' and would not be included as a stand-alone entry in standard English dictionaries like Oxford. Its meaning is fully derived from 'not' + 'unusual.'
