to make the subject of dispute, contention, or litigation

Synset Examples

They contested the outcome of the race

Reason

The verb 'repugn' in the sense of 'to make the subject of dispute, contention, or litigation' is obsolete and extremely rare in modern English usage. This meaning is not attested in contemporary dictionaries and is not encountered in modern texts. It would not appear in a standard English-English dictionary like Oxford Dictionary. Instead, the common verb for this meaning is 'contest.'
