communicate despite

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: communicate despite|extra.014262
to exchange information or ideas regardless of obstacles

Synset Examples

They learned to communicate despite her deafness.

Reason

The concept 'communicate despite' is not a fixed phrase, idiom, or recognized compound; it is simply a combination of the verb 'communicate' and the preposition 'despite', which any competent speaker can compose grammatically for many verbs. This is too general and would not appear as a stand-alone entry in Oxford Dictionary.
