Synset Examples

Reason

The concept 'take her word for it' is a specific instantiation of a general idiom 'take someone's word for it.' Dictionary entries should describe the general idiom rather than versions with a specific pronoun. The concept is too specific and does not constitute a unique idiom entry in standard dictionaries.
