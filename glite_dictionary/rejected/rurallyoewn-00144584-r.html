Reason

The adverb 'rurally' is a straightforward derivative of 'rural' made by adding the '-ly' suffix. It is rare and not commonly attested in natural English usage; standard adverbial forms such as 'in a rural manner' or using 'rural' as a modifier are more idiomatic. Major dictionaries like Oxford and Merriam-Webster do not include 'rurally,' indicating it is not a standard entry. Therefore, it should not be included.
