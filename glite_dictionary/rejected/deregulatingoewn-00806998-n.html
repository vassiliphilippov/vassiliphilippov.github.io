Reason

The concept 'deregulating' is presented as a noun meaning 'the act of freeing from regulation,' but in standard English, 'deregulating' is the present participle or gerund form of the verb 'deregulate.' Dictionaries typically include the noun 'deregulation' for this meaning, not the participle/gerund. Therefore, this concept should be excluded as a standalone dictionary entry.
