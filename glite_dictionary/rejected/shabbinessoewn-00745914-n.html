shabbiness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00745914-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: shabbiness|oewn-00745914-n
an unjust act

Reason

This sense of 'shabbiness' as 'an unjust act' is not commonly used in modern English and would not appear as a primary or standalone meaning in major dictionaries like Oxford. The primary uses of 'shabbiness' relate to physical condition (e.g., being in poor condition or neglected), not to unjust acts. This sense is too obscure and not sufficiently attested for inclusion.
