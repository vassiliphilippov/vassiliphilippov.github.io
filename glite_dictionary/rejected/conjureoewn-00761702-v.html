conjure

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00761702-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: conjure|oewn-00761702-v
ask for or request earnestly

Synset Examples

The prophet bid all people to become good persons

Reason

This sense of 'conjure' (meaning 'ask for or request earnestly') is very rare and archaic, and is not commonly used in modern English. In standard dictionaries, 'conjure' typically refers to performing magic tricks or calling upon supernatural powers. The usage in the example ('The prophet bid all people...') more naturally corresponds to 'urge' or 'beseech,' not to current usage of 'conjure.' Therefore, this particular sense should not be included as a standalone entry.
