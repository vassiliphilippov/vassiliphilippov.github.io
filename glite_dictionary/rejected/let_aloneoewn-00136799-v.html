let alone

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00136799-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: let alone|oewn-00136799-v
leave unchanged or undisturbed or refrain from taking

Synset Examples

leave it as is
leave the young fawn alone
leave the flowers that you see in the park behind

Reason

This synset refers to the very broad sense of 'leave' meaning 'to let be, not disturb', which is much more general than the specific phrasal expression 'let alone' that commonly appears in dictionaries. The concept here is too general and not specifically tied to a dictionary entry for 'let alone'. It matches the verb 'leave' rather than a fixed phrase or unique usage.
