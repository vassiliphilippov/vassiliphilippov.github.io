day-after-day

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01975230-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: day-after-day|oewn-01975230-s
of or belonging to or occurring every day

Synset Examples

daily routine
a daily paper

Reason

The synset describes the concept of something occurring every day, but 'day-after-day' is not commonly used as a standard adjective for this meaning. In standard usage, 'daily' is the established adjective for something that happens every day. Therefore, 'day-after-day' with this meaning does not warrant a separate dictionary entry; it is better covered by 'daily'.
