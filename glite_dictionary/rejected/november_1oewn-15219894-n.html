a Christian feast day honoring all the saints; first observed in 835

Reason

The synset for 'November 1' here corresponds to it as a calendar date, and its meaning as 'All Saints' Day' is better covered under that specific holiday name. Dictionary entries for days of the year as dates (e.g., 'January 1', 'November 2') typically do not appear in major English dictionaries unless referring to widely recognized holidays or events known primarily by the date (e.g., 'July 4' for Americans). 'November 1' as a calendar date is too general and not suitable as a headword under these criteria.
