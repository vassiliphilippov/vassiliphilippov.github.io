Cambrian

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03141741-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: Cambrian|oewn-03141741-a
of or relating to or characteristic of Wales or its people or their language

Synset Examples

the Welsh coast
Welsh syntax

Reason

The synset describes 'Cambrian' as relating to Wales or its people or language. However, in standard usage, 'Cambrian' as an adjective for 'Welsh' is archaic and extremely rare; 'Welsh' is the standard term. 'Cambrian' is chiefly known as a geological term (referring to the Cambrian period), and the usage for 'of or relating to Wales' does not appear in modern general dictionaries except perhaps as a specialized or archaic term. Therefore, this sense is excluded.
