close to

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00007414-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: close to|oewn-00007414-r
(of quantities) imprecise but fairly close to correct

Synset Examples

lasted approximately an hour
in just about a minute
he's about 30 years old
I've had about all I can stand
we meet about once a month
some forty people came
weighs around a hundred pounds
roughly $3,000
holds 3 gallons, more or less
20 or so people were at the party

Reason

The synset is for a general adverbial sense of approximate quantity, where 'close to' as a set phrase is not the primary or natural adverb used in this context in standard English ('about', 'approximately', 'around', etc. are standard). The adverbial 'close to' for 'approximately' is not common enough as a standalone adverb entry for quantities, nor does it usually appear as a dictionary headword for this sense. Therefore, 'close to' in this sense does not merit a stand-alone dictionary entry.
