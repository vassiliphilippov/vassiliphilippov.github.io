Mont.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09130786-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Mont.|oewn-09130786-n
a state in northwestern United States on the Canadian border

Reason

'Mont.' is an abbreviation for 'Montana' in this context. Standard dictionaries do not generally include state abbreviations as headwords unless they are commonly used as words in their own right. The synset is for the U.S. state, but 'Mont.' as an abbreviation is too specialized and not treated as an independent lexical entry in major dictionaries.
