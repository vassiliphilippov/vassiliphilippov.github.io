sick

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02333753-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: sick|oewn-02333753-s
(of light) lacking in intensity or brightness; dim or feeble

Synset Examples

the pale light of a half moon
a pale sun
the late afternoon light coming through the el tracks fell in pale oblongs on the street
a pallid sky
the pale (or wan) stars
the wan light of dawn

Reason

The sense of 'sick' in this context is not commonly recognized in modern English as describing dim or feeble light. The examples provided actually represent senses more accurately expressed by 'pale,' 'wan,' or 'pallid.' 'Sick' is not used to describe light lacking brightness or intensity in standard usage, and such a meaning would not appear in a major English-English dictionary under 'sick.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
