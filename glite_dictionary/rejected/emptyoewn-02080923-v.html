Reason

The verb sense 'empty' meaning 'leave behind empty; move out of' as in 'vacate' is extremely rare and not in standard use; the usual verb 'empty' means 'remove contents from.' This specific sense is not attested in major dictionaries nor used naturally in modern English. The example provided ('vacate your office') uses 'vacate', not 'empty',' and such a usage of 'empty' would not be included in Oxford Dictionary.
