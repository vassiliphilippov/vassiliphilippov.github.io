venous blood vessel

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05426061-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: venous blood vessel|oewn-05426061-n
a blood vessel that carries blood from the capillaries toward the heart

Synset Examples

all veins except the pulmonary vein carry unaerated blood

Reason

The concept 'venous blood vessel' is a general technical term describing any blood vessel that returns blood to the heart (i.e., a vein), but in standard usage, 'vein' is the dictionary headword. 'Venous blood vessel' is not established as a common entry; it is usually explained under 'vein', so it should not be included as a separate dictionary entry.
