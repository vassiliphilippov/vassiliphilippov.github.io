Synset Examples

he commented the paper of his colleague

Reason

The sense 'notice' meaning 'make or write a comment on' is not a standard, contemporary English usage. It is not reflected in modern dictionaries and would not appear in the Oxford Dictionary in this form. The headword 'notice' as a verb does not commonly carry this meaning in present-day English; the correct verb is 'comment.' Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
