turned funny

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: turned funny|extra.025242
changed to a humorous tone

Synset Examples

The conversation turned funny when they mentioned vajay.

Reason

"Turned funny" is not an established idiom, compound, or fixed expression in English. The sense is compositional: "turned" + "funny" = became humorous. It would not appear as such in major English dictionaries, only as part of normal phrasal verb use plus an adjective. Therefore, it is too general and should not be included.
