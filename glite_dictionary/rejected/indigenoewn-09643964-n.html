indigen

Synset POS: n

an indigenous person who was born in a particular place

Synset Examples

the art of the natives of the northwest coast

Reason

While 'indigen' refers to an indigenous person and is a valid noun, it is extremely rare and not in standard use in modern English. The term is not found in reputable dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster, and the common terms for this concept are 'native' or 'indigenous person.' Therefore, it does not warrant inclusion as a headword in a general English dictionary.
