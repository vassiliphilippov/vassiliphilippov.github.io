atomic number 36

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14667094-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: atomic number 36|oewn-14667094-n
a colorless element that is one of the six inert gasses; occurs in trace amounts in air

Reason

The concept 'atomic number 36' is too general and refers to the category of elements by atomic number, not the specific element itself. In a standard dictionary, entry would be under 'krypton', the chemical element with symbol Kr, rather than its atomic number.
