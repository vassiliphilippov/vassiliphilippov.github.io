crisp

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01280991-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: crisp|oewn-01280991-v
make wrinkles or creases on a smooth surface; make a pressed, folded or wrinkled line in; ‘crisp’ is archaic

Synset Examples

The dress got wrinkled
crease the paper like this to make a crane

Reason

This sense of 'crisp' as a verb meaning 'to make wrinkles or creases' is archaic and not in current use. It would not appear as a separate verb entry in modern dictionaries such as Oxford; contemporary dictionaries cover this meaning via 'crease' or 'crimp'. Therefore, it should be excluded.
