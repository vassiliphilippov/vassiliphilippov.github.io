someone who complains frequently

Synset Examples

He was a langer, always complaining about everything.

Reason

The term 'langer' in this sense as 'someone who complains frequently' is extremely rare, regional, and not widely attested in standard English. It does not appear in major dictionaries like Oxford, and its usage is not widespread enough to merit inclusion. Its sense as a synonym for 'complainer' is not standard.
