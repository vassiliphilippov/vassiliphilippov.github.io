Tao

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09867693-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Tao|oewn-09867693-n
an adherent of any branch of Taoism

Reason

The concept 'Tao' in the sense of 'an adherent of any branch of Taoism' is not commonly used in English to refer to a person. In standard English usage, 'Tao' usually refers to the philosophical principle or path in Taoism, not its followers. The proper term for an adherent is 'Taoist.' Therefore, this sense is too specific and not representative of how the word 'Tao' is used in English. Including it would be confusing or misleading.
