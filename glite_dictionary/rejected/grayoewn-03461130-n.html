Reason

This synset refers to a specific, rare usage of 'gray/grey' as a noun meaning clothing of a grey color. This is not a common or standard usage in modern English dictionaries like Oxford, as 'gray' is usually included as a noun for the color itself, not for clothing. Therefore, this sense should be excluded from the dictionary.
