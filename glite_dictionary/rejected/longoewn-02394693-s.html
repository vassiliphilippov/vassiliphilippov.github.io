Synset POS: s
Internal ID: long|oewn-02394693-s
of relatively great height

Synset Examples

a race of long gaunt men
looked out the long French windows

Reason

The synset "of relatively great height" for 'long' is too close to the general sense of 'long' meaning 'of great length' and is applied rarely, often in specific collocations. The usual term for 'of great height' is 'tall'. This specific sense is too narrow, marginal, and not a standalone meaning that would be given its own entry in a dictionary like Oxford unless as a usage note. It is not a distinct meaning for dictionary purposes.
