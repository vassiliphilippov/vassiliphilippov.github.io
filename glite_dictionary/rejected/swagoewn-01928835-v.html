swag

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01928835-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: swag|oewn-01928835-v
walk as if unable to control one's movements

Synset Examples

The drunken man staggered into the room

Reason

This sense of 'swag' as a verb meaning 'walk as if unable to control one's movements' is extremely rare and not in current use; major dictionaries (including Oxford) do not list it in this sense. The example given is more accurately associated with 'stagger.' This archaic or obsolete sense does not merit inclusion in a modern learner's dictionary.
