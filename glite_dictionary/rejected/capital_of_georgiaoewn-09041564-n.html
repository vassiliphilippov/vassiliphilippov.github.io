capital of Georgia

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09041564-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capital of Georgia|oewn-09041564-n
the capital and largest city of Georgia on the Kura river

Reason

The concept 'capital of Georgia' in this context refers to the capital city of the country Georgia (on the Kura river, thus Tbilisi), but as a general phrase rather than a proper noun or established name. Oxford-style dictionaries include the established name 'Tbilisi', not the descriptive phrase 'capital of Georgia', so this phrase is too general and not included as a dictionary headword.
