South American nation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08720216-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: South American nation|oewn-08720216-n
any one of the countries occupying the South American continent

Reason

The concept 'South American nation' is too general and refers to any country that is a nation in South America; this type of geographical categorical phrase would not appear as a headword in a standard dictionary such as the Oxford Dictionary. Instead, dictionaries typically have entries for the names of the individual countries or for the continent itself.
