circumstance

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14536419-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: circumstance|oewn-14536419-n
the set of facts or circumstances that surround a situation or event

Synset Examples

the historical context

Reason

This synset ('the set of facts or circumstances that surround a situation or event') refers to 'context,' not 'circumstance.' Including it under 'circumstance' would be misleading, as the specific sense is for 'context' as shown by the example.
