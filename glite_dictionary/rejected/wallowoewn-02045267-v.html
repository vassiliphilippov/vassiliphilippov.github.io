wallow

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02045267-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: wallow|oewn-02045267-v
rise up as if in waves

Synset Examples

smoke billowed up into the sky

Reason

The sense described ("rise up as if in waves") is more appropriate for the word "billow," not "wallow." This is either an error or an extremely rare/archaic usage of "wallow" that would not appear in a standard modern dictionary like Oxford. Hence, it should be excluded.
