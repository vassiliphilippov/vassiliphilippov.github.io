limited

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03311330-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: limited|oewn-03311330-n
public transport consisting of a fast train or bus that makes only a few scheduled stops

Synset Examples

he caught the express to New York

Reason

The synset describes 'limited' as a type of fast train or bus making only a few stops, but this sense is very rare in modern English and the standard word for this meaning is 'express'. 'Limited' in this sense would not appear as a headword or primary sense in a contemporary dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary; it would be considered archaic or regional at best and is not in general use. Therefore, it should be excluded.
