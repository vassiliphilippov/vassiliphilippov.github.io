Cebion

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-92460019-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Cebion|oewn-92460019-n
a medicine containing ascorbic acid, available in a number of countries worldwide.

Reason

Cebion is a brand name for a vitamin C (ascorbic acid) supplement and does not have independent lexical significance outside being a trademarked product. Brand names of common pharmaceuticals or supplements are generally not included in standard English dictionaries unless they gain generic status or broad cultural relevance.
