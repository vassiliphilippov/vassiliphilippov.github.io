Bermudas

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08728511-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Bermudas|oewn-08728511-n
a group of islands in the Atlantic off the Carolina coast; British colony; a popular resort

Reason

The synset is for a geographical entity (the islands of Bermuda), but the headword 'Bermudas' is simply a plural form and not the canonical or most common dictionary entry for this place (which is 'Bermuda'). Standard dictionaries would have an entry under 'Bermuda' as a proper noun for the territory, not 'Bermudas'. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
