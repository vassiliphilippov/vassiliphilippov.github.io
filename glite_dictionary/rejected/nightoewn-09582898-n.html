Roman goddess of night; daughter of Erebus; counterpart of Greek Nyx

Reason

This concept refers to 'Night' as the Roman goddess, but in English dictionaries, such mythological figures are listed under their specific names (e.g., 'Nox' for Roman mythology and 'Nyx' for Greek mythology). The word 'Night' is not used as a proper noun to denote this deity in English; therefore, it is too general and not appropriate for inclusion as an entry for a mythological figure.
