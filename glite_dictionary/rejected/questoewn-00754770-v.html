quest

Synset POS: v
Internal ID: quest|oewn-00754770-v
express the need or desire for

Synset Examples

She requested an extra bed in her room
She called for room service
when you call, always ask for Mary

Reason

The concept 'quest' as a verb meaning 'express the need or desire for' is archaic and essentially obsolete in modern English; it is not used in this sense in contemporary language and appears in dictionaries only as a historical/archival form. The usual verb for this sense is 'request' or 'ask for'. Therefore, this specific sense of 'quest' should not be included.
