Reason

The concept 'subsidiarity' with the sense 'secondary importance' is too general and not specific to the headword. The primary and widely recognized meaning of 'subsidiarity' is as a legal or political principle, not as 'secondary importance.' This sense would not appear as a separate entry in a standard dictionary, nor would it be useful or recognizable for learners.
