attracter

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10052401-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: attracter|oewn-10052401-n
an entertainer who attracts large audiences

Synset Examples

he was the biggest drawing card they had

Reason

The concept 'attracter' in this sense refers generically to an entertainer who attracts large audiences, but the term itself is not commonly used with this meaning in standard English and does not appear in major dictionaries like Oxford as a primary noun for such entertainers. The more natural term used is 'draw' or 'drawing card'. Therefore, it should be excluded.
