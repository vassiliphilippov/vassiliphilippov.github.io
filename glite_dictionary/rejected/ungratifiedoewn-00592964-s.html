ungratified

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00592964-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: ungratified|oewn-00592964-s
worried and uneasy

Reason

The concept 'ungratified' with the sense 'worried and uneasy' is not accurate or commonly recognized. 'Ungratified' generally means 'not satisfied' or 'not fulfilled' and does not clearly or widely mean 'worried and uneasy.' Including this as a separate entry would be misleading. Therefore, it should not be included as a distinct dictionary concept for this sense.
