to rip or break with little effort

Synset Examples

The slimsy fabric tore easily in the wind.

Reason

The concept 'tear easily' is a descriptive phrase rather than a fixed expression or established idiom, compound, or phrasal verb. It does not have a separate dictionary entry in major English dictionaries; instead, it is a combination of the verb 'tear' and the adverb 'easily.' Learners can use the meanings of the two individual words to understand the phrase. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate headword.
