agitate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01897981-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: agitate|oewn-01897981-v
move very slightly

Synset Examples

He shifted in his seat

Reason

This sense of 'agitate', meaning 'to move very slightly', is too general and does not reflect standard usage of the word in modern English dictionaries. The typical meanings of 'agitate' involve stirring or disturbing something considerably, or causing anxiety, not small or slight movement. Including this sense would be confusing and not helpful to learners.
