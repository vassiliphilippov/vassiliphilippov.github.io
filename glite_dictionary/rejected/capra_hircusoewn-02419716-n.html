Capra hircus

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02419716-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Capra hircus|oewn-02419716-n
any of various breeds of goat raised for milk or meat or wool

Reason

The concept 'Capra hircus' refers to the scientific Latin name for the domestic goat and its synset is equivalent to the general category 'goat.' Entries in an English-English dictionary typically include the common noun 'goat' rather than the binomial scientific name, unless there is a widespread English usage for the Latin term, which there is not in this case.
