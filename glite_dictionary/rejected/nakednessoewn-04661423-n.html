nakedness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04661423-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: nakedness|oewn-04661423-n
characterized by an attitude of ready accessibility (especially about one's actions or purposes); without concealment; not secretive

Reason

This sense of 'nakedness' as 'ready accessibility, without concealment, not secretive' is an extended, abstract meaning rarely used in this form; the primary dictionary sense of 'nakedness' refers to the state of being without clothes. This abstract meaning is not common enough or standard enough to merit its own entry in a typical English-English dictionary like Oxford.
