parroket

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01823844-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: parroket|oewn-01823844-n
any of numerous small slender long-tailed parrots

Reason

The spelling 'parroket' is a rare, obsolete variant of 'parakeet.' Modern dictionaries use 'parakeet;' 'parroket' is not in common current usage and would not appear as a main headword in contemporary dictionaries.
