Synset Examples

Reason

The concept 'for anything' with the sense 'under any circumstances' is a general idiomatic intensifier, but it is not a lexicalized phrase or fixed expression that would appear as a headword in dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary. Such emphasis is better covered under entries for idioms like 'for love or money.' Therefore, this is excluded.
