nought

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13764498-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: nought|oewn-13764498-n
a mathematical element that when added to another number yields the same number

Reason

This sense refers to the mathematical concept of the additive identity (zero), but as described, it is too general for 'nought'; 'nought' in standard English refers specifically to 'zero' (the number 0), not the abstract identity element from algebra. The mathematical additive identity sense is more appropriately covered under 'zero.' Thus, this particular concept does not need a separate dictionary entry for 'nought.'
