a midwestern state in north central United States

Reason

"Wisc." is an abbreviation for Wisconsin, and while abbreviations for state names are common, major English-English dictionaries (such as Oxford) do not typically include most state name abbreviations as headword entries, unless the abbreviation itself has become a standard lexical item outside postal or technical contexts. In this case, "Wisc." is not in standard widespread use as a dictionary entry; its synset is the general concept of the state (not the abbreviation as a word), so it should be excluded.
