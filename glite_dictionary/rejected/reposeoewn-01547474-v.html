Synset Examples

lay the books on the table

Reason

The verb sense of 'repose' meaning 'to put in a horizontal position' is archaic and extremely rare in modern English; in contemporary usage, 'repose' as a verb primarily means 'to rest' or 'lie at rest,' usually in a literary or formal context. The 'to put in a horizontal position' meaning is not current and does not appear in current Oxford Dictionary entries. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
