Prakrit

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06986165-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Prakrit|oewn-06986165-n
any of the modern Indic languages

Reason

This sense of 'Prakrit' as 'any of the modern Indic languages' is too broad and not specific to the headword. 'Prakrit' is not used in English to generally mean 'modern Indic languages,' but rather as a term for a group of ancient Middle Indo-Aryan languages. Therefore, this sense is not appropriate for inclusion in a general English dictionary.
