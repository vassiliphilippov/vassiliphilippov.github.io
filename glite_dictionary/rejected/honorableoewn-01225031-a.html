honorable

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01225031-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: honorable|oewn-01225031-a
not disposed to cheat or defraud; not deceptive or fraudulent; marked by truth

Synset Examples

honest lawyers
honest reporting
gave honest answers
honest reporting

Reason

The synset described here actually corresponds to the headword 'honest', not 'honorable'. It is too general and does not reflect the distinct meaning of 'honorable', as it refers to the broader quality of not cheating, deceiving, or defrauding, which belongs under 'honest'. Therefore, it should be excluded.
