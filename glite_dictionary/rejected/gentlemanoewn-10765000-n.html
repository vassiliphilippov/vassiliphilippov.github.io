gentleman

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10765000-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: gentleman|oewn-10765000-n
a manservant who acts as a personal attendant to their employer

Synset Examples

Jeeves was Bertie Wooster's man

Reason

This sense of 'gentleman' as a manservant is archaic and extremely rare; it does not appear as a distinct entry in contemporary dictionaries like Oxford. In modern English, 'gentleman' chiefly refers to a man of good manners or social position, not a servant.
