capitalization

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00953737-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capitalization|oewn-00953737-n
the act of capitalizing on an opportunity

Reason

This concept uses 'capitalization' in the sense of 'taking advantage of an opportunity', but this meaning is not standard or widely recognized in contemporary English dictionaries. 'Capitalization' is almost always used to refer to finance (providing capital) or writing (using uppercase letters). The sense of 'taking advantage of an opportunity' is properly served by the verb 'capitalize (on)'. Therefore, this noun sense is too nonstandard and rarely used to merit inclusion.
