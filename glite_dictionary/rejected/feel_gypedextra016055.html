feel gyped

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: feel gyped|extra.016055
to feel cheated or deceived

Synset Examples

He felt gyped after the concert was canceled.

Reason

Do not include; 'gyped' is a misspelled, offensive, and deprecated spelling of 'gypped,' which itself is considered offensive due to its ethnic slur origins and is not appropriate for inclusion as a neutral dictionary entry. The phrasal structure 'feel gyped' offers no standalone meaning beyond the verb. Appropriate modern alternatives exist (e.g., 'feel cheated').
