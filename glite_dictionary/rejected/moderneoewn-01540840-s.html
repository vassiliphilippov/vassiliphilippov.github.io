moderne

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01540840-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: moderne|oewn-01540840-s
of or relating to a popularization of art deco that used bright colors and rectangular shapes

Reason

The concept 'moderne' as described here refers to an artistic style variant (Art Moderne), but is not commonly encountered as a distinct English adjective. Its usage as an English adjective is rare and often replaced by 'Art Moderne' or simply 'moderne' as a noun. Therefore, it is too obscure in this adjectival sense for a general English dictionary, and may be better covered under entries for 'Art Deco' or 'Art Moderne' as a style or noun, rather than as an ordinary English adjective.
