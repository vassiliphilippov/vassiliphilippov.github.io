specialise

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01023982-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: specialise|oewn-01023982-v
be specific about

Synset Examples

Could you please specify your criticism of my paper?

Reason

This sense of 'specialise' as 'be specific about' is not widely recognized in standard English. Instead, 'specify' is the correct verb for this sense. 'Specialise' is more commonly used to mean 'focus on a particular area or subject.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
