agriculture

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08092123-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: agriculture|oewn-08092123-n
the class of people engaged in growing food

Reason

This sense refers to 'agriculture' as a class of people, which is not a standard or commonly recognized meaning of the word in English. In standard dictionaries, 'agriculture' is defined as the practice or science of farming, not as the group of people who farm. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
