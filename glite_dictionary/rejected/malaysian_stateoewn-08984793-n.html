Reason

The synset 'Malaysian state' is too general and simply means 'a state in Malaysia'; such compositional terms (geographical + state) are not included as separate dictionary entries in Oxford or similar dictionaries. It does not represent a distinct lexicalized concept beyond the sum of its parts.
