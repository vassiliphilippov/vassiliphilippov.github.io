Internal ID: latest blockbuster|extra.018863
the most recent popular film

Synset Examples

We spent the evening at the cinema watching the latest blockbuster.

Reason

The synset 'the most recent popular film' is too general and refers to any 'most recent blockbuster'. The phrase 'latest blockbuster' is a compositional and transparent adjective-noun combination ('latest' + 'blockbuster'), so it does not merit a dictionary entry. Dictionaries would include 'blockbuster' and perhaps 'latest' but not this specific compositional phrase.
