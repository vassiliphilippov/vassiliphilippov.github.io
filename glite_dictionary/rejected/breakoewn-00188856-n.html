break

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00188856-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: break|oewn-00188856-n
any frame in which a bowler fails to make a strike or spare

Synset Examples

the break in the eighth frame cost him the match

Reason

This sense of 'break' is highly specific to bowling and not widely recognized outside of specialized terminology. It does not have broad enough usage to appear as a standalone sense in general dictionaries like Oxford; such sources do not typically include this meaning under 'break.'
