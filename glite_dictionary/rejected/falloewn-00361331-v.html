fall

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00361331-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: fall|oewn-00361331-v
be born, used chiefly of lambs

Synset Examples

The lambs fell in the afternoon

Reason

This usage of 'fall' meaning 'to be born' is archaic, highly specialized, and almost exclusively used in historical or regional dialects regarding livestock, especially lambing. It is extremely rare in modern usage and would not be included in a general-purpose dictionary such as the Oxford Dictionary today.
