one-year

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00681291-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: one-year|oewn-00681291-a
completing its life cycle within a year

Synset Examples

a border of annual flowering plants

Reason

The synset describes the concept 'completing its life cycle within a year,' but the headword 'one-year' is not an established adjective in standard English for this meaning. The standard adjective is 'annual,' which already appears in dictionaries. 'One-year' is not used as an adjective to mean this and therefore should not have a separate entry.
