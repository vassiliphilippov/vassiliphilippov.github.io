take this stress

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: take this stress|extra.024234
handle or endure this stress

Synset Examples

I can't take this stress; I need no more.

Reason

This is a compositional usage of the common verb 'take' with the noun 'stress'; it is not an idiom, phrasal verb, or set phrase that would appear as a separate lexical entry in a dictionary. It means 'endure this stress,' and both words function with their standard meanings.
