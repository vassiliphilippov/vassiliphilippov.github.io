place under suspicion or cast doubt upon

Synset Examples

sully someone's reputation

Reason

This verbal sense of 'corrupt' as 'place under suspicion or cast doubt upon' is highly specialized and rare, not standard usage in contemporary English. The main dictionary meaning of 'corrupt' relates to making something dishonest, impure, or morally degenerate, but not specifically to 'casting doubt upon.' This sense is more appropriately captured by words like 'sully' or 'taint.' Therefore, it should not be included as a separate dictionary entry.
