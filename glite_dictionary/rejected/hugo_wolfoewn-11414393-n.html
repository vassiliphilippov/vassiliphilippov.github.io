Reason

The synset 'Austrian composer' is too general and simply refers to 'person'. English dictionaries like Oxford do not include biographical entries for proper names unless they have specific, established secondary meanings in English. As 'Hugo Wolf' is just a proper name for an individual, not a term with a meaning beyond the person, it should be excluded.
