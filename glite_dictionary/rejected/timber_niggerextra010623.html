Reason

The concept 'timber nigger' is a highly offensive racial slur, and while dictionaries include documentation of offensive language, this term is extremely rare, contextually isolated, and primarily a historical insult. Most major dictionaries do not include it outside of historical or specialist lexicons due to its obscurity and egregious offensiveness. Inclusion is not justified under standard lexicographical criteria for general-purpose dictionaries.
