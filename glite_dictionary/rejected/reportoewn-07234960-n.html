report

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07234960-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: report|oewn-07234960-n
a written evaluation of a student's scholarship and deportment

Synset Examples

his father signed his report card

Reason

The synset refers to a very specific educational usage ('written evaluation of a student's scholarship and deportment') and overlaps with senses for 'report' already present in major dictionaries, but the canonical English term for this context is 'report card.' Including this sense as 'report' alone is misleading and not standard dictionary practice in English-English dictionaries. The headword 'report' with this sense would not be listed separately from its general meaning of 'a written or spoken account.'
