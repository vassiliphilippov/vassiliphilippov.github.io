Synset POS: n
Internal ID: natural elevation|oewn-09389214-n
a raised or elevated geological formation

Reason

The synset 'natural elevation' is overly general and not commonly used as a dictionary headword. It refers broadly to any geological formation that is raised, such as hills, mountains, and plateaus, but is not itself a lexical item one would expect to find as an entry in standard dictionaries like Oxford. This concept is therefore too generic for inclusion.
