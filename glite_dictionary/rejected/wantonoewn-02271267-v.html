wanton

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02271267-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: wanton|oewn-02271267-v
spend wastefully

Synset Examples

wanton one's money away

Reason

This verbal sense of 'wanton' meaning 'to spend wastefully' is archaic, extremely rare, and not used in contemporary English. It does not commonly appear as a verb in modern dictionaries, so it should be excluded.
