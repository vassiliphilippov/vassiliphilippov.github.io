inveterate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00142067-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: inveterate|oewn-00142067-r
in a habitual and longstanding manner

Synset Examples

smoking chronically

Reason

The adverbial sense 'in a habitual and longstanding manner' is not in common use and does not have a standard standalone adverb form ('inveterately') in English. The primary usage of 'inveterate' is as an adjective. Adverbial use is extremely rare and would not appear in major dictionaries like Oxford.
