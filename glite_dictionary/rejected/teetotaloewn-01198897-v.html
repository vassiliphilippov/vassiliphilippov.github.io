teetotal

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01198897-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: teetotal|oewn-01198897-v
practice teetotalism and abstain from the consumption of alcoholic beverages

Reason

The verb 'teetotal' (to practice teetotalism or abstain from alcohol) is extremely rare and not widely recognized or used in modern English. Major dictionaries such as Oxford do not include 'teetotal' as a verb; the usage is almost exclusively as an adjective ('teetotal') and the noun ('teetotaler'). Therefore, it should not be included as a verb in the dictionary.
