'Chaenopsis ocellata' is a scientific binomial name for a species (bluespotted jawfish). Standard English dictionaries do not typically include Latin scientific names unless they are commonly used in English as common nouns. Inclusion would be better suited for a specialized biological or zoological dictionary, not a general English dictionary.
