office

Synset Examples

Reason

This sense of 'office' is extremely general and not related to the typical headword meaning; it refers to abstract concepts like 'function,' 'role,' or 'part,' which are broader and not specific to 'office' as commonly used in English. Such a general sense should be excluded as per guidelines.
