Synset Examples

the police nabbed the suspected criminals

Reason

The sense of 'cop' meaning 'to take into custody' is not widely recognized or standard in contemporary English dictionaries. The use in the given example ('the police nabbed the suspected criminals') aligns more closely with 'nab' or 'arrest'. 'Cop' is more commonly used as a noun meaning 'police officer' and as a verb meaning 'to obtain' or 'to receive', rather than specifically meaning 'to take into custody'. Including this sense would not be appropriate as it is not commonly attested or familiar to most speakers.
