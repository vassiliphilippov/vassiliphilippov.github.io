Reason

This sense of 'offering' refers to the very general act of making an offer or proposal, which is too broad and abstract for a practical dictionary entry under this specific term. In modern usage, 'offering' more commonly refers to something presented (e.g. a gift, contribution, or product). The synset here is about the speech act of offering, which is general and not specific to 'offering.' Therefore, it does not meet criteria for a standalone dictionary entry for learners.
