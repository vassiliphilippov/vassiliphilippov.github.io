Reason

This sense of 'overhaul' as 'travel past' or 'overtake' is rare and largely obsolete in modern English. In current usage, 'overhaul' as a verb primarily means to repair or renovate something thoroughly. The sense described here would not typically appear in a modern Oxford-style English dictionary, and is too obscure for general learners.
