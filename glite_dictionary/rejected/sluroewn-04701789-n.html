Reason

The sense 'slur' as 'a blemish made by dirt' is not a recognized or standard usage in contemporary English, and this meaning is obsolete or not attested in reputable dictionaries such as Oxford. The usual meanings of 'slur' relate to an insulting remark or a musical notation, not a physical stain or smudge. Therefore, this concept should not be included in the dictionary.
