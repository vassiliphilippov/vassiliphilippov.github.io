gun

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02673313-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: gun|oewn-02673313-n
a pedal that controls the throttle valve

Synset Examples

he stepped on the gas

Reason

This sense of 'gun' as a noun referring to the accelerator pedal (throttle control) is extremely rare and not standard. The usual term for this control in vehicles is 'gas pedal' (US) or 'accelerator (pedal)' (UK). 'Gun' in this sense is not widely recognized nor would it appear as an independent entry in a major English dictionary like Oxford. Therefore, it should not be included.
