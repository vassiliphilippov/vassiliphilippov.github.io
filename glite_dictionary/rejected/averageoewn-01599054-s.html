average

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01599054-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: average|oewn-01599054-s
relating to or constituting the middle value of an ordered set of values (or the average of the middle two in a set with an even number of values)

Synset Examples

the median value of 17, 20, and 36 is 20
the median income for the year was $15,000

Reason

This sense of 'average' is actually the mathematical sense of 'median', i.e., the middle value in an ordered set, rather than the general sense of 'average' (arithmetic mean). 'Average' in standard dictionaries does not typically carry this meaning (it belongs under 'median'), so this concept should not be included under 'average'.
