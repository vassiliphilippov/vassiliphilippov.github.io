stay

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01196647-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: stay|oewn-01196647-v
overcome or allay

Synset Examples

quell my hunger

Reason

This sense of 'stay' as 'overcome or allay' (as in 'quell my hunger') is archaic and no longer used in contemporary English. The general verb entry for 'stay' meaning 'to remain' or 'to stop' is included in dictionaries, but this very rare and obsolete sense does not merit a separate entry in a modern learners' dictionary.
