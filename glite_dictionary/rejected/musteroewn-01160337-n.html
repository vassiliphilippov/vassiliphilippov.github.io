muster

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01160337-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: muster|oewn-01160337-n
compulsory military service

Reason

The noun sense of 'muster' as 'compulsory military service' is either archaic or misapplied. In contemporary English, 'muster' as a noun usually refers to a gathering or assembly, especially of troops, but not the concept of compulsory military service itself. The sense given overlaps with 'conscription', which is the standard term for compulsory military service. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a stand-alone dictionary entry for 'muster'.
