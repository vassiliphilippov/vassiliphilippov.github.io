blind date

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09879381-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: blind date|oewn-09879381-n
a participant in a blind date (someone you meet for the first time when you have a date with them)

Reason

This concept refers to a participant in a blind date, not the common sense of 'blind date' as the social event itself. The headword 'blind date' is usually defined as the event, not as a person. Defining it as 'a participant in a blind date' is too obscure and not standard usage found in reputable dictionaries.
