the quality of being at hand when needed

Reason

The word 'availableness' is a rare, mostly obsolete term for the quality of being available; the standard modern term is 'availability'. 'Availableness' would not appear in major current English dictionaries, except perhaps as an archaic or dated form. Therefore, it should not be included as a dictionary entry.
