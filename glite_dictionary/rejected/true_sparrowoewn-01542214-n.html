true sparrow

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01542214-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: true sparrow|oewn-01542214-n
any of several small dull-colored singing birds feeding on seeds or insects

Reason

The concept 'true sparrow' is a technical or biological categorization distinguishing typical sparrows from other birds called 'sparrows' in different regions, but it is not a common term found in general English dictionaries such as Oxford. It is too specific and not widely used in general language.
