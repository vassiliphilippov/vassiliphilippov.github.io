bedaze

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02119913-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: bedaze|oewn-02119913-v
overcome as with astonishment or disbelief

Synset Examples

The news stunned her

Reason

The term 'bedaze' is an archaic and extremely rare variant of 'daze' that is not commonly used in modern English, nor is it recognized in standard contemporary dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The main sense ('overcome as with astonishment or disbelief') is fully covered by the much more common verb 'daze' or 'stun.' Therefore, it does not merit a standalone dictionary entry.
