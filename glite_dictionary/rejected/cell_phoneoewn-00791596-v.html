cell phone

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00791596-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: cell phone|oewn-00791596-v
call up by using a cellular phone

Synset Examples

If the train is late, I will cell phone you

Reason

The sense 'cell phone' as a verb (meaning to call someone using a cellular phone) is not established usage in standard English. 'Cell phone' is overwhelmingly used as a noun. This verbal usage is extremely rare and nonstandard, so it should not be included in an English-English dictionary.
