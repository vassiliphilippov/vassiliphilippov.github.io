the battle in which Attila the Hun was defeated by the Romans and Visigoths in 451

Reason

The concept describes a historical event (the Battle of Chalons-sur-Marne, also known as the Battle of the Catalaunian Plains) but the headword 'Chalons-sur-Marne' itself is primarily a proper noun referring to a place, not a specific event. Including it as a word entry with a general meaning of 'battle' is too broad/general and not appropriate for an English dictionary. If anything, the event should be entered under 'Battle of Chalons' or similar, not as 'Chalons-sur-Marne.'
