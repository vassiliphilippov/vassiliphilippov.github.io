pass the salt

WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: pass the salt|extra.007666
to hand the salt to someone

Synset Examples

Hun, can you pass me the salt?
Make sure to pass the salt, please.

Reason

'Pass the salt' is simply an ordinary phrase formed by combining 'pass' (verb) and 'the salt' (noun phrase), where 'pass' is used in a very literal, basic sense ('to hand something to someone'). There is no idiomatic or special meaning here; the meaning is fully compositional and transparent. Such common, literal verb-object collocations are not included in general dictionaries like Oxford Dictionary unless they have an idiomatic or distinct fixed meaning, which this does not.
