Reason

The sense 'not made dim or less bright' refers to the general, compositional meaning expressed by the adjective 'undimmed,' which is formed transparently from 'dim.' This is a straightforward compositional negation rather than an independent sense of 'bright,' and such derived negations (e.g., 'undimmed') are not typically given standalone entries in major English dictionaries for this basic sense. The synset itself is too general and does not represent a distinct concept that merits a separate dictionary entry under 'bright.'
