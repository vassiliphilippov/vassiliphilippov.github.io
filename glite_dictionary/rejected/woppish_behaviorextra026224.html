Moreover, 'woppish' seems to be a constructed derivation from 'wop', an offensive ethnic slur. Including 'woppish behavior' is not appropriate as it is both unidiomatic and derived from a term that is offensive, and it would not be found as a headword or compound entry in authoritative dictionaries like Oxford.
