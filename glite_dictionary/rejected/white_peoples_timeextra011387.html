White People's time

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: White People's time|extra.011387
a cultural reference to a perceived lack of punctuality or differing perceptions of time punctuality

Synset Examples

They arrived late, operating on White People's time.
Getting there at 9 means White People's time, not 8.

Reason

The synset is a cultural stereotype phrase that does not have widespread, standard usage, nor does it appear in reputable dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary. The phrase could also be considered culturally insensitive and derives from a general term without a discrete, commonly recognized meaning. Therefore, it is not suitable for inclusion as an independent dictionary entry.
