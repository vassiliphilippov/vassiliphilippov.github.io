mildew

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13537379-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: mildew|oewn-13537379-n
the process of becoming mildewed

Reason

This sense describes 'mildew' as the process of becoming mildewed rather than as a substance. 'Mildew' is not commonly used in standard English to refer to the process itself; it refers to a type of fungus growing on surfaces. The sense as a process is too marginal and would not appear as a separate entry in a standard dictionary like Oxford; the main noun sense of 'mildew' (the fungus itself) is the only one included.
