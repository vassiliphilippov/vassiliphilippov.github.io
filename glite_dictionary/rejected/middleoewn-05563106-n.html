middle

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05563106-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: middle|oewn-05563106-n
the middle area of the human torso (usually in front)

Synset Examples

young American women believe that a bare midriff is fashionable

Reason

This sense of 'middle' refers specifically to the midriff, which is a separate, commonly used noun in English for the front area of the human torso. The word 'middle' as a noun in this sense is very rare and nonstandard; the term 'midriff' is the appropriate and standard dictionary entry. Therefore, this particular concept of 'middle' should not be included as its own dictionary entry.
