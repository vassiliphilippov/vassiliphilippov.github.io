unreal

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01575756-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: unreal|oewn-01575756-a
contrived by art rather than nature

Synset Examples

artificial flowers
artificial flavoring
an artificial diamond
artificial fibers
artificial sweeteners

Reason

This concept refers to things that are artificial or man-made rather than natural, typically covered under the adjective 'artificial'. While 'unreal' can imply something is not natural, it is rarely used to directly mean 'artificial' in standard English usage. Dictionaries like Oxford would list 'artificial' for this sense, not 'unreal', so this particular synset should be excluded.
