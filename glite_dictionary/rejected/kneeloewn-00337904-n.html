supporting yourself on your knees

Reason

This sense of 'kneel' as a noun ('supporting yourself on your knees') is not standard in modern English; 'kneel' is primarily a verb. The noun form is extremely rare and not recognized in major dictionaries such as Oxford, so this sense should not be included.
