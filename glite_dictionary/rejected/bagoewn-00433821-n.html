bag

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00433821-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: bag|oewn-00433821-n
an activity that you like or at which you are superior

Synset Examples

chemistry is not my cup of tea
his bag now is learning to play golf
marriage was scarcely his dish

Reason

The concept refers to a sense of 'bag' as an activity or interest, but this figurative use (meaning 'specialty, area of interest or expertise') is now rare and chiefly historical or slang, not widely encountered in contemporary English. Standard dictionaries sometimes list it as slang but note it's dated. The phrase is more commonly encountered as 'not my bag' rather than as 'bag' alone. For clarity and dictionary relevance, and since this use is extremely uncommon today, it should be excluded.
