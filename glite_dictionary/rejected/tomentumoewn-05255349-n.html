tomentum

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05255349-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: tomentum|oewn-05255349-n
a network of tiny blood vessels between the cerebral surface of the pia mater and the cerebral cortex

Reason

This definition of 'tomentum' is an extremely specific technical sense referring to a structure in the brain anatomy that is not commonly known or found in general or even many specialized dictionaries outside a particular field. In most dictionaries, 'tomentum' is defined as a botanical term (a covering of dense, matted, woolly hairs on plant parts), not as a network of blood vessels. Including this highly specialized neuroscience sense is beyond the scope of a standard English-English dictionary similar to Oxford, as it would not be encountered by most users.
