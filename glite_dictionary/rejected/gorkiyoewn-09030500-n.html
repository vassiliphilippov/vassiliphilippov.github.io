Gorkiy

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09030500-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Gorkiy|oewn-09030500-n
an industrial city in the European part of Russia; birthplace of Maksim Gorky

Reason

While 'Gorkiy' is the former name of the Russian city now known as Nizhny Novgorod, in contemporary English dictionaries proper names of cities are typically included only under their current standard names. 'Gorkiy' is not the current or most widely used name, and its sense as simply an 'industrial city' is now obsolete. Therefore, it does not warrant its own entry as a headword in a general English-English dictionary.
