Reason

This synset represents a general linguistic classification ('Austronesian languages spoken by Australian aborigines') and does not refer to a distinct word sense of 'Australian' as used in English dictionaries. In English, 'Australian' as a noun usually refers to a person from Australia, and as an adjective, it means 'relating to Australia.' The sense here is overly broad and not specific to the headword, thus should be excluded according to the guidelines.
