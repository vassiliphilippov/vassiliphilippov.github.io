forgo

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02308311-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: forgo|oewn-02308311-v
lose (something) or lose the right to (something) by some error, offense, or crime

Synset Examples

you've forfeited your right to name your successor
forfeited property

Reason

This synset describes the legal or formal process of losing a right or property due to an offense or error, which is accurately captured by the verb 'forfeit,' not 'forgo.' 'Forgo' means to give up voluntarily, whereas this sense is about involuntary loss. Therefore, including this sense under 'forgo' would be misleading. The headword and synset are mismatched.
