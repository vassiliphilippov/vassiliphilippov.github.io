Reason

The adverb 'untruly' is extremely rare and not in use in modern English. This word does not appear in standard dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster, most likely because 'untruly' is not used in contemporary speech or writing, and its meaning can be conveyed by 'falsely' or 'untruthfully.' Therefore, it should not be included in a current English-English dictionary.
