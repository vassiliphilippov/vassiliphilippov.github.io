no

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00051219-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: no|oewn-00051219-r
referring to the degree to which a certain quality is present

Synset Examples

he was no heavier than a child

Reason

The synset is too general and does not correspond to a dictionary-worthy sense specific to 'no' as an adverb in this comparative construction. In this context, 'no' is functioning as a modifier of degree (as in 'no heavier than'), which is best treated as a grammatical feature within the entry for 'no' rather than as a separate dictionary entry. This abstract, highly grammatical use would not receive a separate entry in a standard English-English dictionary such as Oxford.
