incumbency

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01133746-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: incumbency|oewn-01133746-n
a duty that is incumbent upon you

Reason

This sense of 'incumbency' refers to 'a duty that is incumbent upon you,' which is now a rare or archaic usage. The primary, modern dictionary sense of 'incumbency' is as the period during which one holds an office or position. The general sense of 'duty' is not in current common use and is too general, not specific to the headword, so it should be excluded.
