telephoner

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09907469-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: telephoner|oewn-09907469-n
the person initiating a telephone call

Synset Examples

there were so many callers that he finally disconnected the telephone

Reason

The term 'telephoner' is not established in standard English usage to refer to the person initiating a telephone call. Standard dictionaries do not list 'telephoner' with this meaning, and the more common term is 'caller'. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
