book of words

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: book of words|extra.001259
a reference book containing meanings and vocabulary of words

Synset Examples

She consulted her book of words for inspiration.
The dictionary is my favorite book of words.
He turned to his book of words during the game.

Reason

The term 'book of words' simply refers to any book containing words, specifically dictionaries or similar references. This is a descriptive phrase, not an established compound or idiom commonly found as a dictionary headword in major dictionaries like Oxford. It is too general and not distinct enough to merit its own entry.
