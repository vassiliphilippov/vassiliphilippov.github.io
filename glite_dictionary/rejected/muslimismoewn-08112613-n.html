Muslimism

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08112613-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Muslimism|oewn-08112613-n
the civilization of Muslims collectively which is governed by the Muslim religion

Synset Examples

Islam is predominant in northern Africa, the Middle East, Pakistan, and Indonesia

Reason

The term 'Muslimism' is extremely rare, nonstandard, and not used by either Muslims or academic sources; standard references use 'Islam' or 'Islamic civilization.' This term does not appear in major English dictionaries and could be considered a misnomer. Therefore, it should not be included as a dictionary entry.
