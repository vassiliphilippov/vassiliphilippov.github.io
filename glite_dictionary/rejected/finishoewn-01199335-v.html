finish

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01199335-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: finish|oewn-01199335-v
finish eating all the food on one's plate or on the table

Synset Examples

She polished off the remaining potatoes

Reason

This sense uses 'finish' in the general way of 'eat all of (something)' and does not represent a distinct idiomatic or specialized sense outside the verb 'finish' meaning 'consume completely' or 'bring to an end.' Such general uses are already covered under the main verb and do not require a separate entry. The example shows a collocational use, not a true new sense.
