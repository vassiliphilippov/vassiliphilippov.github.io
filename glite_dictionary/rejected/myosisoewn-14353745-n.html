myosis

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14353745-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: myosis|oewn-14353745-n
an acute infectious disease occurring in epidemic form and featuring paroxysms of pain (usually in the chest)

Reason

This concept should be excluded because the synset for 'myosis' here actually describes an acute infectious disease, not the established medical term. In standard medical and dictionary usage, 'myosis' (or 'miosis') refers to the constriction of the pupil, and the disease meaning is either obsolete or erroneous. No recognized dictionary uses 'myosis' for an infectious disease. Therefore, it does not meet inclusion criteria.
