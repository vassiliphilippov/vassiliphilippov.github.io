roo bar

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: roo bar|extra.008720
a popular bar or pub, often with a lively atmosphere, particularly in Australia

Synset Examples

We enjoyed drinks at the trendy roo bar downtown.
The roo bar was packed with lively patrons.

Reason

The concept 'roo bar' as a popular bar or pub is not widely attested in English dictionaries or common usage; it appears to be a local or nonstandard expression. Its main established meaning is as a protective bar fitted to vehicles, especially in Australia, not as a drinking establishment. Therefore, it should not be included as defined here.
