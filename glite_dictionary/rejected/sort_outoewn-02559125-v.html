sort out

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02559125-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: sort out|oewn-02559125-v
punish in order to gain control or enforce obedience

Synset Examples

The teacher disciplined the pupils rather frequently

Reason

The phrasal verb 'sort out' is valid for inclusion, but this particular sense ('punish in order to gain control or enforce obedience') is not a standard or recognized usage in modern English. Standard dictionaries do not list 'sort out' as meaning directly 'punish'; rather, it means 'resolve', 'organize', or 'deal with'. Therefore, this sense does not warrant its own dictionary entry.
