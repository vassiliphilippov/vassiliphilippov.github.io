Ubuntard community

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: Ubuntard community|extra.025324
community of Ubuntu Linux users

Synset Examples

The Ubuntard community is very supportive of new users.

Reason

The term 'Ubuntard community' uses a highly offensive and derogatory formation based on '-tard,' and is not recognized as a formal or legitimate term referring to the Ubuntu Linux user community. Additionally, its sense is tied to a slur, making it inappropriate for a standard English dictionary entry. Only established, non-derogatory terms for the community (such as 'Ubuntu community') would be considered for inclusion.
