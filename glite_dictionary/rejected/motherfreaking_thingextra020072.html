motherfreaking thing

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: motherfreaking thing|extra.020072
a vulgar expression of disbelief or frustration

Synset Examples

I can't believe she said that motherfreaking thing!

Reason

The phrase 'motherfreaking thing' is not an established idiom or lexicalized expression; it is a transparent euphemistic variant of 'motherfucking thing', formed with a common process of euphemism substitution. Such phrases are created productively and do not warrant their own dictionary entry, as would be the case in major dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, it should be excluded.
