whipping

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01178709-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: whipping|oewn-01178709-n
the act of overcoming or outdoing

Reason

This sense of 'whipping' as 'the act of overcoming or outdoing' is extremely rare and not in regular modern use. Standard dictionaries do not typically include this meaning, as 'whipping' is almost always used for the act of beating or a physical punishment, not in the sense of simply defeating or outdoing. Therefore, it should be excluded.
