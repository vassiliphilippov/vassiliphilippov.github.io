Synset Examples

Reason

The phrase 'cockroach infestation' in this context is being used metaphorically to refer to any overwhelming and unpleasant situation, but this metaphorical extension is not established enough to be included as a fixed dictionary entry like an idiom. The literal sense ('infestation by cockroaches') is already covered by the separate words, and the metaphorical sense is too rare and nonstandard for inclusion.
