endorsement

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07195032-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: endorsement|oewn-07195032-n
a speech seconding a motion

Synset Examples

do I hear a second?

Reason

This sense of 'endorsement' refers specifically to a parliamentary or meeting procedure, meaning a speech seconding a motion. However, the headword 'endorsement' is not commonly used for this in general English; 'second' or 'seconding' is used. 'Endorsement' in this context is uncommon and not standard enough to be included as a dictionary sense.
