Synset POS: n
Internal ID: version|oewn-05937299-n
a mental representation of the meaning or significance of something

Reason

This sense of 'version' as 'a mental representation of the meaning or significance of something' is too general and abstract. In real usage, 'version' refers to a particular form or variant of something, not to a person's mental concept of meaning. This concept would not typically appear in a reputable dictionary like Oxford under this sense; it is too broad to be useful for learners.
