rack up

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: v
Internal ID: rack up|oewn-00453349-v
supply a rack with feed for (horses or other animals)

Reason

This sense of 'rack up' is a highly specific, archaic, technical usage relating to feeding animals and is not in common contemporary use. It would not appear in a modern learner's dictionary like Oxford; 'rack up' is primarily used informally today in the sense of accumulating or achieving something. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
