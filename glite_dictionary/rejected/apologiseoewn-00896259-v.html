apologise

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00896259-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: apologise|oewn-00896259-v
defend, explain, clear away, or make excuses for by reasoning

Synset Examples

rationalize the child's seemingly crazy behavior
he rationalized his lack of success

Reason

The sense described here relates to 'apologise' meaning to rationalize, defend, or make excuses for something, which is not the standard or common meaning of 'apologise' in modern English dictionaries. That meaning is better covered by words like 'rationalize' or 'justify,' and 'apologise' is not typically used in this way in contemporary English. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
