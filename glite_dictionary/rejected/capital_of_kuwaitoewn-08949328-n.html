a seaport on the Persian Gulf and capital of Kuwait

Reason

The synset refers to a place (city) that is only described by the generic category 'capital of Kuwait,' without a proper noun headword. Dictionaries would include the city's proper name, which is 'Kuwait City,' not the descriptive phrase 'capital of Kuwait.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
