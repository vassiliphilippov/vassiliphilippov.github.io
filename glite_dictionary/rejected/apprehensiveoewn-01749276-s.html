Synset Examples

Reason

This sense of 'apprehensive' (meaning 'quick to understand') is archaic and extremely rare in modern English. In contemporary dictionaries, 'apprehensive' is universally defined as 'anxious or fearful that something bad will happen.' The provided sense would not appear in modern Oxford or comparable dictionaries, so it should be excluded.
