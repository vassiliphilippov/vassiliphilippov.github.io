naturalisation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04794552-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: naturalisation|oewn-04794552-n
the quality of being brought into conformity with nature

Reason

This specific sense ('the quality of being brought into conformity with nature') is too obscure and not idiomatic; 'naturalisation' is overwhelmingly attested and defined as the process of granting citizenship or adapting to a new environment. This rare sense would not appear in authoritative dictionaries like Oxford, so it should be excluded.
