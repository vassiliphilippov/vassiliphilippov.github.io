cruff of the pastry

Source: Idiom List

the outer layer or crust of pastry

Synset Examples

The cruff of the pastry was perfectly flaky.

Reason

"Cruff" is not a recognized standard English word for the outer layer or crust of pastry. The correct term is "crust." This is likely a typographical error or very rare dialectal usage not included in major dictionaries such as Oxford Dictionary. It does not merit a separate entry.
