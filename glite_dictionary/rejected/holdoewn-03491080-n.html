hold

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03491080-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: hold|oewn-03491080-n
the appendage to an object that is designed to be held in order to use or move it

Synset Examples

he grabbed the hammer by the handle
it was an old briefcase but it still had a good grip

Reason

The synset (the appendage designed for holding an object) corresponds to the meaning of 'handle' or 'grip', not 'hold'. 'Hold' as a noun does not commonly refer to a physical appendage on an object; therefore, this meaning of 'hold' should not be included in the dictionary under this headword.
