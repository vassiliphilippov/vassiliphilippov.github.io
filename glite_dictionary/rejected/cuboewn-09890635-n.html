cub

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09890635-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: cub|oewn-09890635-n
a male child (a familiar term of address to a boy)

Reason

This sense of 'cub' as a familiar term of address to a boy is extremely rare and not current; it is not in standard modern use and does not appear as a noun for a male child in major dictionaries like Oxford. The primary senses of 'cub' relate to young animals or, figuratively, to an inexperienced person. It should not be included as a dictionary entry for this meaning.
