Reason

The concept 'honoring' in the sense of 'conformity with law or custom or practice etc.' is not a commonly used noun in English. The gerund form 'honoring' is mainly used as the act of showing respect or recognition, not as a noun for conformity. This sense is too abstract and not standard usage, so it would not appear in a major English dictionary.
