get together

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02439823-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: get together|oewn-02439823-v
become part of; become a member of a group or organization

Synset Examples

He joined the Communist Party as a young man

Reason

The sense description (to become part of; to join an organization) does not correspond to the canonical meaning of 'get together,' which typically means 'to meet' or 'to assemble.' This sense relates more closely to the verb 'join.' Thus, the synset is too generic and mismatched for 'get together' and should be excluded.
