Reason

The synset meaning 'pass from physical life and lose all bodily attributes and functions necessary to sustain life' is too general for the verb 'choke.' While 'choke' can cause death, it is not itself a synonym for 'die,' and this sense does not directly relate to the lexical meaning of 'choke.' This entry would not appear in an Oxford Dictionary for 'choke' with this direct meaning. The lexeme-meaning pairing is not appropriate for inclusion.
