allow the other (baseball) team to score

Synset Examples

give up a run

Reason

This sense of "allow" is a specific sports usage (especially baseball), meaning 'to give up runs,' but it does not differ significantly from the general sense of 'allow' (to permit something to happen, in this case, to permit the other team to score). Dictionaries typically do not include very narrow, sport-specific sub-senses separately unless they are idiomatic or have a substantially different meaning. This sense would not be included separately in Oxford Dictionary but be subsumed under the main verb entry for 'allow.'
