Synset Examples

Reason

This concept refers specifically to the sense 'affirm or declare as an attribute or quality of', which is expressed in the example as 'predicate.' This is a rare, technical, and somewhat archaic sense that is not directly related to the much more common verb 'proclaim.' In modern English usage, 'proclaim' does not typically mean 'to predicate, to declare as an attribute.' To avoid confusion and keep the dictionary focused and relevant, this particular WordNet synset for 'proclaim' should not be included.
