charge

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02353564-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: charge|oewn-02353564-v
cause to be admitted; of persons to an institution

Synset Examples

After the second episode, she had to be committed
he was committed to prison

Reason

The sense 'charge' meaning 'cause to be admitted; of persons to an institution' is not idiomatic or established in English. The examples use the verb 'commit' rather than 'charge' for this meaning, and 'charge' is not used in standard English to mean 'admit someone to an institution.' Therefore, this concept should not be included.
