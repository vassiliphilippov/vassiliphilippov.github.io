Reason

The sense description refers to the broader usage of 'check' or 'check out,' meaning to examine. 'Check into' is not used in standard English for this specific sense; it more commonly means 'to register at' (e.g., a hotel). The sample sentences do not actually use 'check into,' but rather 'check out' and 'check.' Therefore, the synset does not accurately reflect the headword and should be excluded.
