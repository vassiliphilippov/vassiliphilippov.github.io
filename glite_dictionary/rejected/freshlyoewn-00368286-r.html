freshly

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00368286-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: freshly|oewn-00368286-r
in an impudent or impertinent manner

Synset Examples

a lean, swarthy fellow was peering through the window, grinning impudently

Reason

The adverb 'freshly' meaning 'in an impudent or impertinent manner' is extremely rare and archaic, and is not used in contemporary English in this sense. Modern dictionaries (including Oxford) do not list this as a current meaning; 'freshly' is almost exclusively used to mean 'recently.' Therefore, this sense does not warrant a separate entry.
