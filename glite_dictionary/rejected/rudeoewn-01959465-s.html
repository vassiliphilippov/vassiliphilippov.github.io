rude

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01959465-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: rude|oewn-01959465-s
(used especially of commodities) being unprocessed or manufactured using only simple or minimal processes

Synset Examples

natural yogurt

bales of rude cotton

Reason

This sense of 'rude'—meaning unprocessed or minimally processed, especially for commodities—is archaic and no longer in current use for general English. Modern dictionaries no longer list 'rude' with this sense, and it would not appear as an active sense in the Oxford Dictionary except perhaps as an etymological note or in extremely historical contexts. Therefore, it should not be included as a headword or sense in a contemporary English-English dictionary.
