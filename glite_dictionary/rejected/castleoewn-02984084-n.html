castle

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02984084-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: castle|oewn-02984084-n
(chess) the piece that can move any number of unoccupied squares in a direction parallel to the sides of the chessboard

Reason

This sense of 'castle' refers to the chess piece normally called 'rook'. In modern English, 'castle' as a noun meaning the chess piece is considered inaccurate or informal; most reputable dictionaries list 'rook' as the proper term, sometimes mentioning 'castle' as an informal or lay usage. Since 'castle' as a chess piece would not typically appear as a main entry with this definition in an authoritative dictionary, it should not be included as a primary sense.
