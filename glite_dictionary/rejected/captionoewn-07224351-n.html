caption

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07224351-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: caption|oewn-07224351-n
taking exception; especially a quibble based on a captious argument

Synset Examples

a mere caption unworthy of a reply

Reason

This sense of 'caption' as 'taking exception; especially a quibble based on a captious argument' is archaic and exceedingly rare in contemporary English. It would not be included in a modern general-use English dictionary such as the Oxford Dictionary. The primary sense of 'caption' today relates to titles or brief explanations, not this obsolete legalistic meaning.
