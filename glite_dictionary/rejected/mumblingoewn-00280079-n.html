mumbling

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00280079-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: mumbling|oewn-00280079-n
ineffectual chewing (as if without teeth)

Reason

Although 'mumbling' as a noun meaning 'ineffectual chewing' exists in WordNet, this usage is extremely rare and not found in standard modern dictionaries like Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The primary, contemporary meaning of 'mumbling' relates to speaking indistinctly, not to chewing. Therefore, this sense would not be included in a modern English-English dictionary.
