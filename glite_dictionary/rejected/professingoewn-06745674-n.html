professing

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06745674-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: professing|oewn-06745674-n
an open avowal (true or false) of some belief or opinion

Synset Examples

a profession of disagreement

Reason

The sense corresponds to the noun 'profession' (as in 'profession of faith'), not to 'professing'. The word 'professing' is the present participle form of the verb 'profess', and is not used as a noun in standard English. Therefore, this noun sense of 'professing' should be excluded as it would not appear as a headword in a standard English dictionary for this meaning.
