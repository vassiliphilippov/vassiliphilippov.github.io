Reason

The sense 'a person who slips or slides because of loss of traction' for 'skidder' is extremely rare and not established in standard usage. 'Skidder' as a noun is primarily known as a type of logging vehicle, not as a label for a person slipping. This sense would not appear in Oxford Dictionary and is not relevant for inclusion.
