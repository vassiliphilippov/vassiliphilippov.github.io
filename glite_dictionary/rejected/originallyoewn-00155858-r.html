originally

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00155858-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: originally|oewn-00155858-r
in an manner suggesting originality

Reason

This sense of 'originally' as 'in a manner suggesting originality' is not standard usage; 'originally' is typically used to mean 'from the beginning' or 'in the first place.' As defined here, it is redundant with 'originally' (in the sense of origin), and the adverbial form meaning 'in an original manner' is almost always 'originally' used informally or incorrectly where 'in an original way' or 'creatively' should be used. Therefore, this particular concept does not require a separate dictionary entry.
