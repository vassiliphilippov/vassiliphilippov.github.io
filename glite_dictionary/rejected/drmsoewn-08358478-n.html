DRMS

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08358478-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: DRMS|oewn-08358478-n
the organization in the Defense Logistics Agency that inventories and evaluates and sells reusable United States government surplus

Reason

The synset 'organization' is too general, referring only to its role as an organization. Acronyms for highly specific, internal governmental departments like 'DRMS' (Defense Reutilization and Marketing Service) are not included in general English dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary unless they have broader, commonly recognized usage. This term is highly specialized and not in general use.
