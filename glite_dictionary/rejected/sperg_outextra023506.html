sperg out

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: sperg out|extra.023506
to become overly excited or obsessive

Synset Examples

He tends to sperg out when discussing his favorite topics.

Reason

The expression 'sperg out' is based on a slang/derogatory shortening of 'Asperger's' and is considered offensive and inappropriate. Additionally, it would not be included in authoritative dictionaries like Oxford due to its origin as an ableist slur and its insensitive nature.
