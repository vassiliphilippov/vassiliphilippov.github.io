antediluvian

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09815335-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: antediluvian|oewn-09815335-n
any of the early patriarchs who lived prior to the Noachian deluge

Reason

The sense refers to a very specific biblical/historical figure (early patriarchs before the flood), which is too narrow, and the term 'antediluvian' is generally used as an adjective meaning 'extremely old' or 'from before the flood.' This specific noun sense would not commonly warrant a separate dictionary entry as it does not appear as a headword in standard dictionaries in this sense.
