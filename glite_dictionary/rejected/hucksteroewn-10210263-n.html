huckster

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10210263-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: huckster|oewn-10210263-n
a person who writes radio or tv advertisements

Reason

The sense of 'huckster' as 'a person who writes radio or tv advertisements' is not standard or widely recognized in modern English. The primary accepted meanings of 'huckster' relate to a person who sells goods aggressively or a peddler, not specifically to advertising writers. Therefore, this sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
