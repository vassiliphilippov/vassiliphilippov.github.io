kill trees

to cause trees to die or be removed, often by cutting them down

Reason

The concept 'kill trees' is a general collocation (verb + noun) rather than a fixed compound, phrasal verb, or idiom. It simply combines the common verb 'kill' with the noun 'trees', and its meaning is transparent and compositional. Such combinations do not merit a separate dictionary entry in an English-English dictionary, following Oxford practice.
