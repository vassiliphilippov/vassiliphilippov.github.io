box standard

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: box standard|extra.001323
a standard of average quality or the basic level of features

Synset Examples

He delivered a box standard performance at the competition.
The new model exceeds the box standard features.

Reason

The phrase "box standard" appears to be a common misspelling or mishearing of the established British term "bog-standard" (meaning ordinary or basic), and is not a recognized or widely accepted term in standard English dictionaries. It therefore should not be included as a headword.
