skeleton

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07321099-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: skeleton|oewn-07321099-n
a scandal that is kept secret

Synset Examples

there must be a skeleton somewhere in that family's closet

Reason

This sense of 'skeleton' as 'a scandal that is kept secret' is idiomatic and almost always used within the phrase 'skeleton in the closet/cupboard'. The noun 'skeleton' on its own does not typically carry this meaning outside these set phrases, so including 'skeleton' with this synset would be misleading and too general. The appropriate entry is 'skeleton in the closet/cupboard' as a compound or idiom, not the standalone word 'skeleton' for this sense.
