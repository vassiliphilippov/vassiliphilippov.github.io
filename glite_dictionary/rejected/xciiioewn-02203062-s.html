being three more than ninety

Reason

'xciii' refers to the Roman numeral for ninety-three. While Roman numerals are found in dictionaries, individual numerals beyond the most commonly used ones are typically not included as separate entries in English dictionaries. The meaning is not unique to 'xciii'; it is simply the Roman numeral for 93, making it too general and not warranting a standalone dictionary entry.
