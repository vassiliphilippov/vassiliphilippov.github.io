acres

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13267561-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: acres|oewn-13267561-n
extensive landed property (especially in the country) retained by the owner for their own use

Synset Examples

the family owned a large estate on Long Island

Reason

This sense of 'acres' as 'extensive landed property' is too general and does not correspond to a standard lexical entry for 'acres' in reputable English dictionaries. 'Acres' in standard dictionaries refers to units of area or, sometimes informally, to a large expanse of land, but not specifically to landed estates. The synset meaning overlaps with more precise terms like 'estate' or 'property,' so it should be excluded.
