Reason

The synset refers to 'the music of Bach' as a generalization, which is not a separate dictionary headword. The canonical headword 'Bach' primarily appears as a proper noun referring to the composer Johann Sebastian Bach and his family, not as a mass noun for their music. This usage does not warrant a separate dictionary entry beyond the existing entry for the composer's name.
