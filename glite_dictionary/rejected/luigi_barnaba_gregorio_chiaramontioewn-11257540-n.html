Luigi Barnaba Gregorio Chiaramonti

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11257540-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Luigi Barnaba Gregorio Chiaramonti|oewn-11257540-n
Italian pope from 1800 to 1823 who was humiliated by Napoleon and taken prisoner in 1809; he concluded a concordat with Napoleon and crowned him emperor of France; he returned to Rome in 1814 (1740-1823)

Reason

The synset here is purely 'person' related to the proper name 'Luigi Barnaba Gregorio Chiaramonti'. English dictionaries like Oxford would instead include the papal name 'Pius VII', which is the commonly used name. Including birth names of popes is not standard dictionary practice unless historically significant on their own.
