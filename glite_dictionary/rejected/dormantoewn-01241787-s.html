Reason

This concept is a specific, technical adjectival sense describing the posture of certain animals (especially felines) but is too narrow and specialized to merit a separate dictionary entry under 'dormant'. The general adjectival sense of 'dormant' ('inactive', 'in a state of rest or sleep') is already captured in standard dictionaries, and this nuanced usage would be illustrated as an example of the general meaning, not as a distinct headword or definition.
