Suisse

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09054023-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Suisse|oewn-09054023-n
a landlocked federal republic in central Europe

Reason

'Suisse' is the French name for Switzerland. In English dictionaries, the canonical name for the country is 'Switzerland.' 'Suisse' may be mentioned as a foreign variant or in etymology, but it should not have its own main entry in an English-English dictionary.
