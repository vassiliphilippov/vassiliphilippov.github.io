espouse

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02494483-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: espouse|oewn-02494483-v
take in marriage; married

Reason

The sense of 'espouse' meaning 'to take in marriage' or 'married' is now archaic and extremely rare in modern English; the primary contemporary meaning is 'to adopt or support (a cause, belief, etc.)'. The marital sense would not appear as a main entry in a modern English-English dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary.
