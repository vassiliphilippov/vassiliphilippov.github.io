inexplicably

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00035878-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: inexplicably|oewn-00035878-r
in a manner differing from the usual or expected

Synset Examples

had a curiously husky voice
he's behaving rather peculiarly

Reason

The synset described here ("in a manner differing from the usual or expected") is better captured by adverbs like 'peculiarly' or 'curiously', not 'inexplicably'. 'Inexplicably' uniquely means 'in a way that cannot be explained', which is a different sense. Therefore, the synset is not sufficiently related to the headword 'inexplicably', and should not be included under this meaning.
