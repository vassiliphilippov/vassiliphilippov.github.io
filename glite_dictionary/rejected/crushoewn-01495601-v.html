Reason

This sense of 'crush' as specifically meaning 'to jam or bruise' is too narrow and is not distinct from the main dictionary sense of 'crush' (to compress so as to damage, distort, or bruise), which is already included in all major dictionaries. The given synset is not specific or unique enough for a separate entry and is redundantly covered by the main verb sense.
