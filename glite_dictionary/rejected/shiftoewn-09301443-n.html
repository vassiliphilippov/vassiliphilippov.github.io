shift

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09301443-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: shift|oewn-09301443-n
(geology) a crack in the earth's crust resulting from the displacement of one side with respect to the other

Synset Examples

they built it right over a geological fault
he studied the faulting of the earth's crust

Reason

The synset refers to a geological feature (a crack in the earth's crust) more commonly called a 'fault.' The use of 'shift' in this context is extremely rare or obsolete, and 'shift' does not conventionally mean 'geological fault' in standard modern English usage. Dictionaries like Oxford list 'fault' for this concept, not 'shift.' Therefore, this sense should not be included.
