acculturation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05993821-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: acculturation|oewn-05993821-n
all the knowledge and values shared by a society

Reason

This concept should not be included under 'acculturation' with the meaning 'all the knowledge and values shared by a society'. That definition more accurately describes 'culture', not 'acculturation'. 'Acculturation' refers specifically to the process by which individuals or groups adopt the cultural traits of another group. Therefore, this synset is too general and not specific to the headword's true meaning.
