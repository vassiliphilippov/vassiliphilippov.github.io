facial

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02735794-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: facial|oewn-02735794-a
of or pertaining to the outside surface of an object

Reason

This sense of "facial" (meaning 'of or pertaining to the outside surface of an object') is too general and not directly related to the established headword 'facial' as used in English. The standard sense of 'facial' relates to the face (of a person or animal), not to outside surfaces of objects in general. Thus, this concept does not warrant a dictionary entry under 'facial.'
