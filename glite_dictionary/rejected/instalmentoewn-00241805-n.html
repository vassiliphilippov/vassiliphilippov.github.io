instalment

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00241805-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: instalment|oewn-00241805-n
the act of installing something (as equipment)

Synset Examples

the telephone installation took only a few minutes

Reason

This sense of 'instalment' is not correct; the correct term for the act of installing equipment is 'installation.' 'Instalment' in standard English refers to one of several parts of a payment or narrative, not the act of installing. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
