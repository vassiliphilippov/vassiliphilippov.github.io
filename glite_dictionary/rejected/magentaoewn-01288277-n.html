Magenta

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01288277-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Magenta|oewn-01288277-n
a battle in 1859 in which the French and Sardinian forces under Napoleon III defeated the Austrians under Francis Joseph I

Reason

The synset here is a battle (event), but the headword 'Magenta' is commonly known as a color, not as the name of the battle except in historical encyclopedias. As an English dictionary entry, 'Magenta' as a battle is too specific and functions as a proper noun for a singular historical event, and would not generally appear as a dictionary entry except perhaps in a specialized historical or encyclopedic context.
