Reason

The synset 'replying' with the headword 'respondent' is not directly relevant to the noun 'respondent' as commonly defined. The narrowly adjectival sense ('replying') is very rare and not commonly attested in this word form; in modern English, 'respondent' is almost always a noun meaning 'a person who replies to something' (e.g., in surveys or legal cases). The adjective 'answering' is the appropriate form here, and 'respondent' is not used this way. Therefore, this specific adjectival concept should be excluded.
