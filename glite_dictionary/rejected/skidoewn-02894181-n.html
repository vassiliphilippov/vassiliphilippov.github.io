skid

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02894181-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: skid|oewn-02894181-n
a restraint provided when the brake linings are moved hydraulically against the brake drum to retard the wheel's rotation

Reason

This sense of 'skid' is too specialized and does not correspond to how the word 'skid' is generally used in standard English. The meaning relates to a specific component or function of a brake system in vehicles but 'skid' is not a standard term for a brake or brake restraint in general dictionaries like Oxford. It is not a general-use word with this sense, so it should be excluded.
