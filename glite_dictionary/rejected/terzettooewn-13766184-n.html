terzetto

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13766184-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: terzetto|oewn-13766184-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of one and one and one

Reason

The sense describes the cardinal number three, but 'terzetto' in English refers to a trio or ensemble of three musicians, particularly in musical contexts, not directly to the number three. The sense here is not sufficiently distinct from 'three' to be included as a separate dictionary entry. The concept is too general and is not a standard English word for the number three.
