like Sherlock

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: like Sherlock|extra.019204
as clever or insightful as the detective Sherlock Holmes

Synset Examples

He felt like Sherlock solving the mystery.

Reason

The phrase 'like Sherlock' is an ordinary simile based on cultural knowledge of Sherlock Holmes. It is not a fixed expression or idiom warranting a dictionary entry and would not appear as a headword in standard dictionaries such as Oxford Dictionary.
