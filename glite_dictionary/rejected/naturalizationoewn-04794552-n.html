the quality of being brought into conformity with nature

Reason

This sense of 'naturalization'—'the quality of being brought into conformity with nature'—is too rare and not commonly used. It refers to an abstract, dated sense that does not occur in contemporary or even formal English. The word 'naturalization' as commonly used refers to the process of becoming a citizen or introducing species to a new environment. Therefore, this sense should not be included in a modern English-English dictionary.
