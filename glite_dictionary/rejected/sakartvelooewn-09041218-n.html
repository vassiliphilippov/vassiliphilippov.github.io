Sakartvelo

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Sakartvelo|oewn-09041218-n
a republic in Asia Minor on the Black Sea separated from Russia by the Caucasus mountains; formerly an Asian soviet but became independent in 1991

Reason

The headword 'Sakartvelo' is a transliteration of the Georgian name for Georgia (the country), but in English, the standard term is 'Georgia'. 'Sakartvelo' is not commonly used in English, so it would not typically appear as a headword in an English-English dictionary.
