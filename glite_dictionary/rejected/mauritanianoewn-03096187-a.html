Reason

The synset is incorrectly aligned; 'Mauritanian' actually refers to Mauritania (a country in northwest Africa), not Mauritius (an island nation in the Indian Ocean). The definition and sense provided here are not accurate for 'Mauritanian', and this error would not appear in a reliable dictionary like Oxford. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
