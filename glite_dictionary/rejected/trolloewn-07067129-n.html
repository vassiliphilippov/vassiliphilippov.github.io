troll

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07067129-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: troll|oewn-07067129-n
a partsong in which voices follow each other; one voice starts and others join in one after another until all are singing different parts of the song at the same time

Synset Examples

they enjoyed singing rounds

Reason

This sense of 'troll' as a 'partsong in which voices follow each other' is exceedingly rare and outdated; the commonly recognized term for this musical form is 'round'. 'Troll' in this sense would not appear in a modern English dictionary such as Oxford, and therefore should be excluded.
