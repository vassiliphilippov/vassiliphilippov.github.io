idleness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05147351-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: idleness|oewn-05147351-n
the quality of lacking substance or value

Synset Examples

the groundlessness of their report was quickly recognized

Reason

The sense described does not relate to the headword 'idleness.' Instead, it refers generally to 'the quality of lacking substance or value,' which is not a standard or recognized meaning of 'idleness.' 'Idleness' commonly means the state of being idle, i.e., not active or not working, but not 'lacking substance or value.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
