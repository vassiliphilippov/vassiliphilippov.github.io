natural covering

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09280855-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: natural covering|oewn-09280855-n
a natural object that covers or envelops

Synset Examples

under a covering of dust
the fox was flushed from its cover

Reason

The synset 'natural covering' is a very general term for any naturally occurring material or structure that covers something (e.g., skin, bark, rind). It is not commonly used as its own lexicalized term in English and would not appear as a headword in an Oxford Dictionary. The examples demonstrate the use of 'covering' and 'cover,' which are dictionary words, but 'natural covering' is a technical/WordNet-specific label, not an attested lexical entry.
