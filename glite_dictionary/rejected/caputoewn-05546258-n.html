caput

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05546258-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: caput|oewn-05546258-n
the upper part of the human body or the front part of the body in animals; contains the face and brains

Synset Examples

he stuck his head out the window

Reason

The synset refers to the anatomical part 'head', but 'caput' is not used in modern English as a common noun for this meaning. It is a Latin term, not standard in English except as a technical or historical reference, and does not appear as its own headword with this sense in standard English dictionaries.
