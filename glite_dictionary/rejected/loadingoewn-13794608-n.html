loading

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13794608-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: loading|oewn-13794608-n
a quantity that can be processed or transported at one time

Synset Examples

the system broke down under excessive loads

Reason

This sense of 'loading' corresponds to 'load' or 'loads' as 'the quantity that can be processed or transported at one time.' The canonical term for this meaning is 'load', not 'loading', and dictionaries do not usually define 'loading' in this way. 'Loading' more commonly refers to the process of putting things into a vehicle or container, or to weight imposed on a structure, but not to a 'quantity' in this sense. Therefore, this sense of 'loading' should not be included.
