Reason

The sense 'wallow' meaning 'be ecstatic with joy' is not attested as a standard or recognisable meaning in contemporary English; 'wallow' more commonly means to indulge excessively in an emotion or to roll about (as in mud or water), not specifically referring to ecstasy or joy.
