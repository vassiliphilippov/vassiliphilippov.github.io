ceratin

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14756651-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: ceratin|oewn-14756651-n
a fibrous scleroprotein that occurs in the outer layer of the skin and in horny tissues such as hair, feathers, nails, and hooves

Reason

The headword 'ceratin' appears to be a misspelling or very rare variant of 'keratin', which is the standard term for this protein. Dictionaries, including Oxford, do not typically list 'ceratin' as a main entry, only 'keratin'. Therefore, it should not be included.
