Reason

The sense 'a large-scale farming enterprise' of 'agriculture' is not a commonly used meaning. 'Agriculture' in standard usage refers to the practice or science of farming, not the specific farm (which would be 'farm' or 'plantation'). Therefore, this synset is too unusual and should not be included as a standalone dictionary entry for this sense.
