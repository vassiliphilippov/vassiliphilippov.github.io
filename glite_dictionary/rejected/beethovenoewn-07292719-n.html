Beethoven

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07292719-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Beethoven|oewn-07292719-n
the music of Beethoven

Synset Examples

he enjoyed Beethoven most of all

Reason

The concept 'Beethoven' in the sense of 'the music of Beethoven' is too general and does not represent a separate dictionary entry; it refers to the works of Beethoven, the composer. Oxford dictionaries would treat 'Beethoven' as a proper noun referring to the person, not as a countable or mass noun for his music.
