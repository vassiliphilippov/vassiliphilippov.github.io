drum

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07398075-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: drum|oewn-07398075-n
the sound of a drum

Synset Examples

he could hear the drums before he heard the fifes

Reason

This synset refers not to the musical instrument itself, but specifically to the sound made by a drum. The use of 'drum' in this sense (to mean 'the sound of a drum') is rare and does not merit a separate dictionary entry in a standard English-English dictionary. Typical dictionaries define 'drum' as the instrument, not the sound produced by it. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
