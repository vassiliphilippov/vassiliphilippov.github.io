Reason

The concept 'lxxiv' refers to the Roman numeral for 74 (being four more than seventy). In most modern English dictionaries, Roman numerals are not included as separate lexical entries except for major numerals (I, V, X, etc.) or when used in specific names or titles. 'lxxiv' is a numeral representation, not a word or phrasal expression, and does not warrant its own entry. Therefore, it should not be included.
