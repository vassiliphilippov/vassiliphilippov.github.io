equal day and night

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: equal day and night|extra.015590
period of equal duration of day and night

Synset Examples

The equinox signifies equal day and night length.

Reason

The phrase 'equal day and night' describes a period when day and night are of equal length, but this is a general descriptive phrase, not a fixed lexical item or idiomatic expression. The proper term for this phenomenon is 'equinox,' which would be included in a dictionary. 'Equal day and night' is too descriptive and not a headword in standard dictionaries.
