White Russian

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09715375-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: White Russian|oewn-09715375-n
a native or inhabitant of Byelorussia

Reason

The meaning 'a native or inhabitant of Byelorussia' for 'White Russian' is obsolete and misleading. In modern English, 'White Russian' primarily refers either to a cocktail or to a supporter of the anti-Bolshevik movement, not to inhabitants of Belarus. The general label for an inhabitant of Belarus is 'Belarusian.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded as it is not representative of standard dictionary practice.
