Reason

The sense 'run' as 'the act of testing something' is too specialized and not a common or independent noun meaning in general English. In most cases, the word 'trial,' 'test,' or 'experiment' would be used, and 'run' in this sense is technical jargon specific to laboratory or computing contexts. It does not warrant a separate dictionary entry for general learners.
