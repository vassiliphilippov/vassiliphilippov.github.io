in a beastly manner

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00281857-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: in a beastly manner|oewn-00281857-r
in the manner of a beast

Synset Examples

she treated her husband bestially

Reason

The headword 'in a beastly manner' is an adverbial phrase directly corresponding to the adjectival form 'beastly' and the adverb 'beastlily' or 'bestially', all of which would be separate dictionary entries. This phrase is too transparently compositional and not idiomatic; such transparently formed adverbial phrases do not merit dictionary entries in major English dictionaries. It would not appear as a separate entry in Oxford Dictionary.
