Synset POS: n
Internal ID: collectivist|oewn-10638692-n
a person who belongs to the political left

Reason

This sense ('a person who belongs to the political left') is too broad and does not accurately capture a specific or widely-used meaning for 'collectivist' as a noun. In standard English dictionaries, 'collectivist' is defined as a person who advocates or follows collectivism (the principle of collective ownership or control). The broader sense describing anyone on the political left is not standard and is too general, so it should be excluded.
