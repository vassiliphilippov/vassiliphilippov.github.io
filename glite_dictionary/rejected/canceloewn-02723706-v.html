Synset Examples

Reason

This sense of 'cancel' as 'make up for' (i.e., 'offset' or 'counterbalance') is not common in modern standard English and does not represent a primary or widely recognized meaning. The main senses of 'cancel' relate to annulment, deletion, or calling off something, which are included in major dictionaries. This sense is better captured by the verb 'offset' or 'counterbalance', and the usage is not sufficiently distinct or common for inclusion as a separate main entry for 'cancel'.
