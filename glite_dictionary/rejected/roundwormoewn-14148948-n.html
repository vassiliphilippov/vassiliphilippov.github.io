roundworm

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14148948-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: roundworm|oewn-14148948-n
infections of the skin or nails caused by fungi and appearing as itching circular patches

Reason

This sense describes 'roundworm' as a fungal infection of the skin or nails, but this is an error. 'Roundworm' refers to a type of parasitic worm, not a skin infection. The described skin infection is commonly called 'ringworm', which is unrelated to actual worms. Therefore, this sense does not correspond to a correct or relevant meaning of 'roundworm' and should not be included.
