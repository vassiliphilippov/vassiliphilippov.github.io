Arminius

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10842566-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Arminius|oewn-10842566-n
Dutch Protestant theologian who founded Arminianism which opposed the absolute predestinarianism of John Calvin (1560-1609)

Reason

Arminius in this sense refers to an individual person (Jacobus Arminius), and the WordNet synset here is simply 'person' with a biographical note. Since dictionary entries do not generally include all personal names unless the person is of extraordinary historical or cultural influence that changes ordinary language, and since the Oxford Dictionary does not typically include individual historical figures unless they are eponymous or archetypal, this concept should be excluded.
