cinque

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13766661-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: cinque|oewn-13766661-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of four and one

Reason

The word 'cinque' refers to the number five, but this form is archaic and is not used in modern English except in specialized or historical contexts (e.g., in cards or heraldry). The general meaning 'the number five' is much better covered by the headword 'five'. Including 'cinque' for the core meaning 'the cardinal number that is the sum of four and one' is unnecessary for a modern English dictionary user.
