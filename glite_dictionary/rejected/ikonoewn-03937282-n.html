ikon

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03937282-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: ikon|oewn-03937282-n
a visual representation (of an object or scene or person or abstraction) produced on a surface

Synset Examples

they showed us the pictures of their wedding
a movie is a series of images projected so rapidly that the eye integrates them

Reason

The word 'ikon' in this sense is just an archaic or alternative spelling of 'icon,' and is not commonly used in modern English to mean a general 'visual representation' (the standard term is 'image,' 'picture,' or 'icon'). The lemma here ('ikon') is not standard, and its general synset does not match a real contemporary dictionary sense under this spelling. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
