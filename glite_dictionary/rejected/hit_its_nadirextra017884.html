Source: Idiom List

reached its lowest point

Synset Examples

The economy hit its nadir before starting to recover.

Reason

The phrase 'hit its nadir' is a compositional and predictable use of the noun 'nadir' with the verb 'hit.' It is not an idiom or set phrase with meaning beyond its parts, and would not be included as a separate entry in major dictionaries like Oxford. The main entry should be under 'nadir.'
