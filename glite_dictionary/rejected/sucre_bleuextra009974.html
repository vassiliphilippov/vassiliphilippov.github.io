sucre bleu

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: sucre bleu|extra.009974
an expression of surprise or disbelief; an expression of shock or surprise (French origin)

Synset Examples

Sucre bleu! I can't believe you did that!
She gasped, 'Sucre bleu! What a surprise!'
When he lost the match, he exclaimed, 'Sucre bleu!'

Reason

The expression 'sucre bleu' is a French interjection, not standard in English usage. It is not found in reputable English dictionaries as a recognized English phrase. It only appears in English when used as a foreignism, not as part of native English vocabulary. Thus, it should be excluded from an English-English dictionary.
