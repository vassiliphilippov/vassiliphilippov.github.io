crucify

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01805829-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: crucify|oewn-01805829-v
hold within limits and control

Synset Examples

subdue one's appetites
mortify the flesh

Reason

This sense of 'crucify' (meaning to 'hold within limits and control') is extremely rare, archaic, and not attested in contemporary or even historical general English usage as a standard meaning of 'crucify.' It is a highly figurative or misapplied extension sometimes associated with 'mortify' (as in to 'mortify the flesh'), but not standard enough to warrant a dictionary entry under this meaning.
