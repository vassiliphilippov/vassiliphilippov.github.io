bring up

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01978076-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: bring up|oewn-01978076-v
raise from a lower to a higher position

Synset Examples

Raise your hands
Lift a load

Reason

This sense of 'bring up' ('raise from a lower to a higher position') is not a standard or accepted usage in modern English dictionaries; the correct verbs for this meaning are 'raise' or 'lift'. 'Bring up' commonly means 'to mention', 'to raise a child', or 'to vomit', but not physically lifting something. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
