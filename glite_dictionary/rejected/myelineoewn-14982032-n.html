myeline

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14982032-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: myeline|oewn-14982032-n
a white fatty substance that forms a medullary sheath around the axis cylinder of some nerve fibers

Reason

The concept 'myeline' appears to be a misspelling or obsolete form of 'myelin', which is the standard term for the white fatty substance that forms a sheath around some nerve fibers. Only the standard term 'myelin' should be included, as 'myeline' does not appear in current reputable dictionaries as an established headword.
