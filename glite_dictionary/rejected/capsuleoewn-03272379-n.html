capsule

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03272379-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capsule|oewn-03272379-n
a pilot's seat in an airplane that can be forcibly ejected in the case of an emergency; then the pilot descends by parachute

Reason

This specific sense of 'capsule' referring to a pilot's ejection seat is not a general or commonly recognized meaning of the word. In aviation, 'ejection seat' or 'escape capsule' is used, but 'capsule' alone for this meaning is not sufficiently established in standard English dictionaries. Therefore, it should be excluded.
