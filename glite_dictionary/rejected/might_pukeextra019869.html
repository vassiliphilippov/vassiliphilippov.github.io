Source: Idiom List

to feel nauseous or sick

Synset Examples

He felt like he might puke after the roller coaster.

Reason

The concept 'might puke' is a phrase using the modal 'might' with the verb 'puke.' 'Puke' itself is an established dictionary word (slang for 'vomit'), but 'might puke' as a phrase is simply a particular usage of the verb with a modal and does not represent a fixed phrasal verb or idiom. No dictionary would have a separate entry for 'might puke.' Therefore, it should not be included as a headword entry.
