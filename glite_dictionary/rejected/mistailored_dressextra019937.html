Synset Examples

She wore a mistailored dress that looked unflattering.

Reason

The term 'mistailored dress' is a descriptive phrase composed of 'mis-' (meaning 'badly' or 'wrongly') and 'tailored dress,' but it is not a fixed lexical item or widely recognized compound that would appear as an entry in major English dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary. It describes an instance of a badly tailored dress rather than a term with an established meaning.
