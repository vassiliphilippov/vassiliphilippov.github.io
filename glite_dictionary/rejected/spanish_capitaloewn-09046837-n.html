Spanish capital

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09046837-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Spanish capital|oewn-09046837-n
the capital and largest city situated centrally in Spain; home of an outstanding art museum

Reason

Although 'Spanish capital' refers to Madrid, as a phrase it is too general and simply denotes 'the capital of Spain', which would not have its own dictionary entry. Dictionaries typically include the proper noun 'Madrid', not the descriptive phrase 'Spanish capital'. Therefore, it should be excluded.
