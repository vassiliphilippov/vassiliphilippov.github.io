unleaded

WordNet ID: oewn-01660946-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: unleaded|oewn-01660946-s
not having leads between the lines

Reason

This sense of 'unleaded' (meaning 'not having leads between the lines') refers only to a technical compositional property in printing or typography, and is extremely rare and specialized. It does not represent a meaning in general use, nor would it appear as a distinct sense in a general dictionary like Oxford. The commonly encountered sense of 'unleaded' as 'not containing lead' (especially in the context of petrol/gasoline) is the main dictionary entry. Therefore, this rare typographical sense should be excluded.
