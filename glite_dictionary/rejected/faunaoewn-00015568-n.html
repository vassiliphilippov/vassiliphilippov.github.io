fauna

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00015568-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: fauna|oewn-00015568-n
a living organism characterized by voluntary movement

Reason

The synset description ('a living organism characterized by voluntary movement') corresponds to the very general biological concept 'animal' or 'organism'; it is not specific to the headword 'fauna'. The correct dictionary entry for 'fauna' is 'the animals of a given region, habitat, or time', not the general biological definition. Therefore, this sense should be excluded as it is too general and not related specifically to 'fauna'.
