the Tuesday that occurs second in a given month

Synset Examples

The report is due on the second Tuesday of the week.

Reason

The concept 'second Tuesday' refers to a general construct formed by combining the ordinal 'second' and the weekday 'Tuesday'. This is a compositional phrase, not a fixed expression or idiom, nor does it have a special meaning beyond the sum of its parts. Such temporal references are typically not included as headwords in major dictionaries.
