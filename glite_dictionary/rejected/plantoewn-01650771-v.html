Synset POS: v
Internal ID: plant|oewn-01650771-v
set up or lay the groundwork for

Synset Examples

establish a new department

Reason

This sense of 'plant' as 'set up or lay the groundwork for' is too general and does not correspond to a standard English usage. The verb 'to plant' is not used to mean 'establish a new department' in regular English; that is the sense of 'establish', not 'plant'. Therefore, this concept should not be included in a standard English-English dictionary.
