fare

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05919718-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: fare|oewn-05919718-n
an agenda of things to do

Synset Examples

they worked rapidly down the menu of reports

Reason

This sense of 'fare' referring to 'an agenda of things to do' is not in standard English usage; the example sentence actually uses 'menu' metaphorically for 'list,' and 'fare' is not commonly used in this sense. Therefore, this concept should not be included.
