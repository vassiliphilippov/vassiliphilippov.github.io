oftenness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15303267-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: oftenness|oewn-15303267-n
the number of occurrences within a given time period

Synset Examples

the frequency of modulation was 40 cycles per second
the frequency of his seizures increased as he grew older

Reason

While 'oftenness' is a grammatically possible noun, it is extremely rare and not commonly accepted or attested in reputable dictionaries such as Oxford. The standard term for this concept is 'frequency,' which is universally used and recognized in both general and technical contexts. 'Oftenness' does not warrant inclusion as it would not be expected in a serious English dictionary.
