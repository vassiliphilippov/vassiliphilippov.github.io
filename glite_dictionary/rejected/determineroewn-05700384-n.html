determiner

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05700384-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: determiner|oewn-05700384-n
a determining or causal element or factor

Synset Examples

education is an important determinant of one's outlook on life

Reason

The synset given ('a determining or causal element or factor') for 'determiner' refers to a general sense of something that determines, but in modern English 'determiner' is primarily used as a grammatical term. This general sense is far less common, rarely used, and would not typically appear as a main entry in major dictionaries under this definition. The typical word for this meaning is 'determinant.' Therefore, this sense should not be included in a standard dictionary entry for 'determiner.'
