scented

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01061878-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: scented|oewn-01061878-a
having the sense of smell

Synset Examples

keen-scented hounds

Reason

This sense of 'scented' (meaning 'having the sense of smell') is archaic and almost never used in modern English, where 'scented' almost always means 'having a scent/fragrance.' The main dictionaries do not include this physical sense as a separate, current entry. Standard learners' dictionaries would not include this meaning.
