strand

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02026332-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: strand|oewn-02026332-v
bring to the ground

Synset Examples

the storm grounded the ship

Reason

This sense of 'strand' as 'to bring to the ground' is not a standard or recognized usage in English dictionaries like Oxford; the example sentence actually pertains to 'ground' as a verb, not 'strand'. 'Strand' typically means to leave someone or something without the means to move from somewhere, especially in the context of being stuck or left behind, but not 'bring to the ground.' Therefore, this sense is too far removed from accepted dictionary definitions and should be excluded.
