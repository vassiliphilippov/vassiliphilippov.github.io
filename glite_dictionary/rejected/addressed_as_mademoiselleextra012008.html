addressed as mademoiselle

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: addressed as mademoiselle|extra.012008
to call someone 'miss' in French

Synset Examples

The waiter addressed her as mademoiselle with a smile.

Reason

This concept refers to the action or process of being addressed as 'mademoiselle', which is too general and metalinguistic to be an entry in an English-English dictionary. The actual word 'mademoiselle' would be included, but not the general act of being addressed in that way.
