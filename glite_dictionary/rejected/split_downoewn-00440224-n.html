Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00440224-n
Synset POS: n

a decrease in the number of outstanding shares of a corporation without changing the shareholders' equity

Reason

The synset here ('a decrease in the number of outstanding shares of a corporation without changing the shareholders' equity') refers to a technical finance event, usually called a 'reverse split' or 'reverse stock split' in financial English. The phrase 'split down' is not a standard dictionary headword for this meaning and would not have a standalone entry in the Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, it should be excluded.
