harsh

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02238735-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: harsh|oewn-02238735-a
of textures that are rough to the touch or substances consisting of relatively large particles

Synset Examples

coarse meal
coarse sand
a coarse weave

Reason

This sense of 'harsh' actually describes 'coarse' (of textures that are rough or made of large particles), not 'harsh' itself. This is too broad/general and does not represent a sense of 'harsh' that warrants its own Oxford-style entry. Therefore, it should not be included.
