animation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13984978-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: animation|oewn-13984978-n
the condition of living or the state of being alive

Synset Examples

while there's life there's hope
life depends on many chemical and physical processes

Reason

This sense of "animation"—meaning the condition of living or being alive—is overly general and does not reflect standard usage. In current English, "animation" typically refers to motion or to the creation of moving images, not to the state of being alive itself. Furthermore, this sense is extremely rare and is not commonly found in contemporary dictionaries. Therefore, it should not be included.
