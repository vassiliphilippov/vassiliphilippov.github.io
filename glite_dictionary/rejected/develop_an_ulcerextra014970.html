Synset POS:
Internal ID: develop an ulcer|extra.014970
form a sore in the stomach

Synset Examples

He developed an ulcer due to stress from work.

Reason

The concept 'develop an ulcer' is a verb phrase formed from the verb 'develop' and the noun 'ulcer'. It describes the act of forming an ulcer, but does not constitute an established phrasal verb or idiomatic phrase. Both 'develop' (in the sense of 'begin to have or suffer from') and 'ulcer' are standard entries; this combination is semantically transparent and does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
