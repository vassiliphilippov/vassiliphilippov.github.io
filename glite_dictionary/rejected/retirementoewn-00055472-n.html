retirement

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: retirement|oewn-00055472-n
withdrawal for prayer and study and meditation

Synset Examples

the religious retreat is a form of vacation activity

Reason

This synset refers to a very specific and rare sense of 'retirement' meaning 'withdrawal for prayer and study and meditation', which is not attested as a common or recognizable usage of the word in general or standard English. In ordinary usage, 'retirement' refers to the act of stopping work, not this concept, and this meaning would not appear as a separate entry in Oxford Dictionary.
