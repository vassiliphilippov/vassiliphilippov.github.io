nasale

WordNet ID: oewn-00987625-v
Synset POS: v

speak in a nasal voice

Synset Examples

‘Come here,’ he nasaled

Reason

The verb 'nasale' as 'to speak in a nasal voice' is not an established or recognized verb in modern English and would not appear in Oxford or similar dictionaries. It is not standard usage and the sense is adequately covered by related adjectives like 'nasal' and the verb phrase 'speak nasally'. Therefore, it should not be included.
