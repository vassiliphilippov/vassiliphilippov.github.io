Reason

The concept 'order Colymbiformes' is a technical scientific taxonomic category (order) and is not in common use in general English dictionaries. Most modern taxonomy now classifies grebes under the order Podicipediformes, and 'Colymbiformes' is considered outdated. Therefore, this term is too technical and outdated for general dictionary inclusion.
