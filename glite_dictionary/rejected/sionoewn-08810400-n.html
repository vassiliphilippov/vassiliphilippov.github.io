Sion

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08810400-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Sion|oewn-08810400-n
Jewish republic in southwestern Asia at eastern end of Mediterranean; formerly part of Palestine

Reason

The synset refers to a country (Israel), but the headword 'Sion' is not commonly used in English to mean 'Israel' as a geographical or political entity. It would not appear as such in standard English dictionaries except as an alternative/spelling for 'Zion' in a religious or historical context, not as a modern country. The synset is not sufficiently related to the specific sense of 'Sion' as an entry-worthy term for the state of Israel.
