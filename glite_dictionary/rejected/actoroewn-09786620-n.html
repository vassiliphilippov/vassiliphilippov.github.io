actor

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09786620-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: actor|oewn-09786620-n
a person who acts and gets things done

Synset Examples

he's a principal actor in this affair
when you want something done, get a doer
he's a miracle worker

Reason

This sense of 'actor' is too general and refers to a person who takes action or gets things done, which is distinct from the primary, commonly understood sense of 'actor' (a performer in theatre, film, etc.). This sense would not typically have a separate entry in a standard dictionary like Oxford Dictionary, as it overlaps with broader terms like 'doer' or 'agent.' Therefore, it should be excluded.
