Synset Examples

Reason

This sense of 'nip' as 'the property of being moderately cold' is too uncommon and does not reflect standard usage in modern English. In contemporary dictionaries, 'nip' is not cited as a noun meaning 'chilliness' or 'moderate cold,' so it should not be included as a headword in this sense.
