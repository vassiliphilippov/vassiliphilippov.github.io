specific

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05826326-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: specific|oewn-05826326-n
a fact about some part (as opposed to general)

Synset Examples

he always reasons from the particular to the general

Reason

The noun use of 'specific' meaning 'a fact about some part' is extremely rare, is not generally used in modern English, and is not widely recognized as a standalone noun in this sense. The word 'specifics' (plural) is common as a noun, but 'specific' (singular, as a countable noun for 'a fact about some part') would not appear as a main entry in standard dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, this sense should not be included.
