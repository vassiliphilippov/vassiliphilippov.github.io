Reason

This sense of 'be' (have life, be alive) is too specific and does not represent a distinct dictionary entry. Oxford dictionaries do not list this particular sense as a separate entry, as it is a figurative extension of the general existential verb 'be'. The headword 'be' itself is included with its usual primary senses, but this narrow sense (used to mean 'be alive') is reflected in example sentences, not as its own sense.
