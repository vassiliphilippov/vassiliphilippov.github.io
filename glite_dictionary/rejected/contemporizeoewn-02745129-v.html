contemporize

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02745129-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: contemporize|oewn-02745129-v
happen at the same time

Reason

The sense 'happen at the same time' for 'contemporize' is extremely rare and not attested in modern English usage. 'Contemporize' is sometimes used as a verb meaning 'to make contemporary' or 'to bring up to date,' but virtually never to indicate simultaneity (which is instead conveyed by 'coincide' or 'synchronize'). Therefore, this sense does not warrant inclusion as a dictionary entry.
