leave the building

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: leave the building|extra.018931
to cease to exist or be present

Synset Examples

The excitement has left the building since the announcement.

Reason

The given sense uses 'leave the building' metaphorically to mean that something has ceased to exist or departed, but this is not an established idiom in English. The phrase is most commonly used in the idiom 'Elvis has left the building,' rather than as a general verb phrase. Therefore, it does not warrant a dictionary entry on its own.
