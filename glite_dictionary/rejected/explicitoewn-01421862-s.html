Internal ID: explicit|oewn-01421862-s
in accordance with fact or the primary meaning of a term

Reason

This sense of 'explicit' as 'in accordance with fact or the primary meaning of a term' is too abstract and technical, relating to logical or philosophical terminology. The main, widely-used senses of 'explicit'—such as 'stated clearly and in detail' or 'leaving no room for confusion or doubt'—are covered elsewhere. This specific sense is unlikely to appear as a separate entry in general English dictionaries like Oxford.
