hymenopter

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02208922-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: hymenopter|oewn-02208922-n
insects having two pairs of membranous wings and an ovipositor specialized for stinging or piercing

Reason

The term 'hymenopter' is not standard in English and is not used in authoritative dictionaries; the correct term is 'hymenopteran'. Therefore, this variant should not be included as a headword in a general English-English dictionary.
