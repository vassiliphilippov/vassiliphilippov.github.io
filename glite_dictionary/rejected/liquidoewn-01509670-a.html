liquid

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01509670-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: liquid|oewn-01509670-a
changed from a solid to a liquid state

Synset Examples

rivers filled to overflowing by melted snow

Reason

This synset refers to a participial adjective indicating the state of being in liquid form (e.g., "melted"). However, 'liquid' is not used in contemporary English as an adjective meaning 'melted' or 'changed from solid to liquid' in this context. Instead, 'melted' or 'molten' would be standard. This sense is too rare and is not a meaning of 'liquid' that would warrant its own dictionary entry in a modern English-English dictionary.
