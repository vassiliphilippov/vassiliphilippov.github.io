mirasol

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11999182-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: mirasol|oewn-11999182-n
annual sunflower grown for silage and for its seeds which are a source of oil; common throughout United States and much of North America

Reason

The concept 'mirasol' refers to a type of sunflower but is not commonly used in English; the standard English term is 'sunflower.' 'Mirasol' is a Spanish word and not established as an English headword. As such, it does not belong in an English-English dictionary like Oxford unless as a rare loanword, which it is not in this context.
