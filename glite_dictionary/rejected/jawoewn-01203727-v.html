jaw

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01203727-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: jaw|oewn-01203727-v
chew (food); to bite and grind with the teeth

Synset Examples

He jawed his bubble gum
Chew your food and don't swallow it!
The cows were masticating the grass

Reason

The sense of 'jaw' as a verb meaning 'to chew' is obsolete and extremely rare, and is not included in modern dictionaries like Oxford. The common verb 'jaw' means 'to talk or chatter persistently.' Therefore, this sense is excluded as it would not appear in a standard learner's dictionary.
