wistaria

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-12601205-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: wistaria|oewn-12601205-n
any flowering vine of the genus Wisteria

Reason

'Wistaria' is an alternative, less common spelling of 'Wisteria'. In modern usage and in respected dictionaries like Oxford, 'wistaria' is generally not listed as a separate headword, but is mentioned as a variant spelling within the main entry for 'wisteria'. The concept is not sufficiently distinct or in use to warrant a separate dictionary entry.
