overthrow

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00555887-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: overthrow|oewn-00555887-n
the act of disturbing the mind or body

Synset Examples

his carelessness could have caused an ecological upset
she was unprepared for this sudden overthrow of their normal way of living

Reason

This sense of 'overthrow' as 'the act of disturbing the mind or body' is too rare and not recognized in general usage. The synset is overly broad and does not correspond to a meaning of 'overthrow' that would appear in modern English dictionaries. Primary definitions focus on removal from power or throwing down, not general mental or bodily disturbance.
