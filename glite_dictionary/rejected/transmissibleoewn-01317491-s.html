transmissible

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01317491-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: transmissible|oewn-01317491-s
occurring among members of a family usually by heredity

Synset Examples

an inherited disease
familial traits
genetically transmitted features

Reason

The synset definition for this sense ("occurring among members of a family usually by heredity") refers to the idea of something being familial or hereditary, not to the main or only valid meaning of 'transmissible' (which relates to something able to be passed from one individual to another, typically by infection). In modern usage, 'transmissible' is not used to mean 'hereditary' or 'familial,' so this sense should not be included as a dictionary entry for 'transmissible.' Instead, 'transmissible' rightfully refers to diseases or traits that can be transmitted (passed on), usually from person to person, not specifically by heredity.
