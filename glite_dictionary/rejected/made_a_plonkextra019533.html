Synset Examples

Reason

The phrase 'made a plonk' refers to making a plonking sound and is not a set idiom or established dictionary entry. It simply combines the verb 'made' with the onomatopoeic noun 'plonk', which is already a word for a sound. The concept is too specific and descriptive, rather than lexical, and would not be included as a separate entry in a major dictionary such as Oxford.
