misdemean

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02522868-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: misdemean|oewn-02522868-v
behave badly

Synset Examples

The children misbehaved all morning

Reason

The concept 'misdemean' as a verb meaning 'to behave badly' is extremely rare and archaic in modern English, and is not commonly found in contemporary dictionaries. Its usage has been largely replaced by 'misbehave.' Therefore, it is not suitable for inclusion in a general English-English dictionary aimed at modern learners.
