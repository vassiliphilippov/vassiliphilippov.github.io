double cream

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07867419-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: double cream|oewn-07867419-n
fresh soft French cheese containing at least 60% fat

Reason

The synset described refers to a type of soft French cheese with high fat content, not the common English use of 'double cream' as a rich cream. This use is not standard or widely recognized in English dictionaries, and the synset is not sufficiently specific to the headword as used in English.
