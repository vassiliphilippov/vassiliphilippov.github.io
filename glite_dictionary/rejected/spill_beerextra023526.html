Reason

The concept 'spill beer' is a transparent, literal combination of 'spill' (verb) and 'beer' (noun) with no idiomatic or non-compositional meaning. It simply means to accidentally make beer fall or escape its container, which is the regular sense of 'spill' + direct object, appearing in countless similar contexts (e.g., 'spill coffee', 'spill water'). Such combinations are not typically included in major English dictionaries as distinct entries.
