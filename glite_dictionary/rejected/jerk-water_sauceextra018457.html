Reason

The concept 'jerk-water sauce' is not a widely recognized or established term in English. It does not appear in reputable dictionaries, and appears to be a blend or misinterpretation of 'jerk sauce' (a well-known Jamaican spicy sauce) and the unrelated term 'jerkwater' (meaning trivial or insignificant, often as a modifier for a small town). 'Jerk-water sauce' is not standard usage and does not warrant its own dictionary entry.
