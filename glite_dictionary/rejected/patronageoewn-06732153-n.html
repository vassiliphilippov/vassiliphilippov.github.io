patronage

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06732153-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: patronage|oewn-06732153-n
a communication that indicates lack of respect by patronizing the recipient

Reason

This sense of 'patronage' (meaning a communication that is patronizing or disrespectful) is not a standard or widely recognized usage in modern English dictionaries such as Oxford Dictionary. 'Patronage' is not ordinarily used to mean 'a communication that indicates lack of respect by patronizing the recipient.' The typical senses of 'patronage' refer to support, sponsorship, or the power to grant favors. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
