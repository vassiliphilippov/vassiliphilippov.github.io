prolongation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05140800-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: prolongation|oewn-05140800-n
amount or degree or range to which something extends

Synset Examples

the wire has an extension of 50 feet

Reason

The synset here refers to the very general sense of 'amount or degree or range to which something extends' (as in length, scope, etc.), which is too broad and not strongly tied to the specific headword 'prolongation'. In standard dictionaries, 'prolongation' refers to the action or result of making something longer in time, not simply the range or amount of extension. Therefore, this sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry for 'prolongation'.
