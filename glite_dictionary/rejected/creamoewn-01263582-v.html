Reason

This verbal sense of 'cream' meaning 'to remove from the surface' (as in skimming cream from milk) is extremely rare, archaic, and not in modern use as an independent verb outside very technical or context-specific references. Standard dictionaries like Oxford do not usually list 'cream' as a verb in this sense, as 'skim' is the natural choice for this action. Therefore, this sense is too obscure for general dictionary inclusion.
