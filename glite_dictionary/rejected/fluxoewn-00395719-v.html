Synset Examples

Reason

The sense of 'flux' as a verb meaning 'mix together different elements' is extremely rare, archaic, and not in contemporary use. Modern dictionaries do not list 'flux' as a verb in this sense; more common verbs for this meaning are 'blend' or 'mix'. Including this sense would mislead learners. Therefore, this concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
