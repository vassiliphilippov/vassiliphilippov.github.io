go along

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02076777-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: go along|oewn-02076777-v
for time to move forward

Synset Examples

three years elapsed

Reason

The synset sense for 'go along' here refers to a very general sense of time passing (as in 'elapse'), but the phrase 'go along' is not idiomatically or commonly used to indicate the passage of time in standard English. It is not a phrasal verb for 'time to move forward' in the way 'elapse' is. Therefore, this concept does not warrant its own dictionary entry.
