chachka

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09924983-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: chachka|oewn-09924983-n
(Yiddish) an attractive, unconventional woman

Reason

The synset 'chachka' here refers to an attractive, unconventional woman (Yiddish), but in standard English dictionaries, this term does not appear as a common noun for this meaning. 'Chachka' (or 'tchotchke') more often refers to a small trinket or knick-knack. It is not attested in modern English usage as a term for a woman, so it should be excluded.
