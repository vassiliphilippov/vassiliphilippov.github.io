huitre

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07802140-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: huitre|oewn-07802140-n
edible body of any of numerous oysters

Reason

The concept 'huitre' refers to the edible body of oysters, but 'huitre' is the French word for 'oyster'. In English dictionaries, the headword should be 'oyster', which is already included. Therefore, 'huitre' does not represent an English headword and should be excluded.
