graph

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06831828-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: graph|oewn-06831828-n
a written symbol that is used to represent speech

Synset Examples

the Greek alphabet has 24 characters

Reason

This sense of 'graph' (as a 'written symbol that is used to represent speech') is archaic and extremely rare. In modern English, this meaning is almost always covered by 'letter', 'character', or 'grapheme', and 'graph' is overwhelmingly used for its familiar mathematical meanings (such as 'a diagram showing the relationship between variable quantities'). Thus, this sense should not be included in a general English-English dictionary like Oxford, as it does not match contemporary usage.
