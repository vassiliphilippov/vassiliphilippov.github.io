Synset POS: v

provide with clothes or put clothes on

Synset Examples

Parents must feed and dress their child

Reason

Although 'habilitate' exists in English, its use to mean 'to provide with clothes or to dress' is obsolete or extremely rare. Standard English overwhelmingly uses 'dress' or 'clothe' for this sense. This verb with this meaning would not appear as a main entry or in current use in Oxford Dictionary, and including it would not benefit learners.
