a family of mollusks

Reason

The concept 'mollusk family' in this sense refers to the taxonomic rank 'family' within the group of mollusks. This is too general and technical, describing a biological category that is not specific to a named group; dictionaries typically include individual family names or the general senses 'mollusk' and 'family' separately, not their combination as a set phrase.
