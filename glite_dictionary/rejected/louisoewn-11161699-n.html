Louis

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11161699-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Louis|oewn-11161699-n
United States prizefighter who was world heavyweight champion for 12 years (1914-1981)

Reason

This sense refers to 'Louis' as a person (Joe Louis, the boxer) and the synset is 'person', which is too general. Individual people are not usually included unless they are eponymous or have become common nouns or adjectives, which is not the case here.
