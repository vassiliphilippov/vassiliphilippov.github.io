monetary unit in Papua New Guinea

Reason

'Papuan monetary unit' is an overly generic term referring broadly to the monetary unit in Papua New Guinea and does not correspond to a dictionary headword. In English dictionaries, entries are for the names of currencies themselves (e.g., 'kina', 'toea'), not for phrases like 'Papuan monetary unit'. This concept is too general and would not appear as a dictionary entry in Oxford Dictionary.
