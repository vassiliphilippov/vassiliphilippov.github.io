indicatory

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02098448-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: indicatory|oewn-02098448-s
(usually followed by ‘of’) pointing out or revealing clearly

Synset Examples

actions indicative of fear

Reason

The word 'indicatory' is extremely rare and is almost never used in contemporary or standard English. The commonly used adjective fulfilling this meaning is 'indicative.' 'Indicatory' appears only in highly specialized or obsolete contexts, and is not found in major modern dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. Therefore, it should not be included as a headword in a general English dictionary.
