frequent prostitutes

Reason

The sense 'wench' as a verb meaning 'to frequent prostitutes' is exceedingly rare, archaic, and almost obsolete. It is not commonly found in modern usage, and the verb form does not appear in current reputable dictionaries such as the Oxford English Dictionary or Merriam-Webster. Only the noun form 'wench' (meaning a young woman or servant girl, sometimes with pejorative or archaic overtones) is commonly listed. Therefore, this verb sense should be excluded from a modern English-English dictionary.
