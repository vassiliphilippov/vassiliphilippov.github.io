capital of Spain

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09046837-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capital of Spain|oewn-09046837-n
the capital and largest city situated centrally in Spain; home of an outstanding art museum

Reason

The synset is a generic description of a city as a 'capital', not the proper noun for the city itself. English dictionaries do not list 'capital of Spain' as a headword; they include 'Madrid', which is the actual proper noun for the capital. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
