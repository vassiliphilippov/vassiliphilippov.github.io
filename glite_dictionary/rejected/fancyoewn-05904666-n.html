fancy

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05904666-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: fancy|oewn-05904666-n
something many people believe that is false

Synset Examples

they have the illusion that I am very wealthy

Reason

The synset refers to the meaning 'something many people believe that is false', which is a rare or archaic use of 'fancy' as a noun. In modern usage, this sense is not common and would not generally appear as a separate main noun sense in a contemporary dictionary like Oxford. Primary meanings of 'fancy' as a noun are about imagination, liking, or ornamental detail, not about collective false beliefs or illusions.
