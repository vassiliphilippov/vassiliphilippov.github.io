manhood

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04734196-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: manhood|oewn-04734196-n
the quality of being human

Synset Examples

he feared the speedy decline of all manhood

Reason

This sense of "manhood" as simply "the quality of being human" is too general and not specific to the headword; English usage normally reserves "manhood" for meanings related to being male or to masculinity. The general idea of being human is covered under words like "humanity" or "humankind." Therefore, this concept should not be included under "manhood."
