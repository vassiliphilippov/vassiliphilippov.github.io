actually

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00151061-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: actually|oewn-00151061-r
at present

Synset Examples

the transmission screen shows the picture that is actually on the air

Reason

This synset of 'actually' is defined as 'at present', which is not a conventional or widely recognized sense. Standard dictionaries do not list 'actually' as simply meaning 'at present'; its primary senses involve 'in fact' or 'really', or to emphasize reality as opposed to appearance. Therefore, this meaning is too narrow, context-specific, and not dictionary-worthy as a stand-alone sense.
