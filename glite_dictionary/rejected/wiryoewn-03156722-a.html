of or relating to wire

Reason

The sense 'of or relating to wire' for 'wiry' is too general and not commonly used. In standard English dictionaries, 'wiry' is almost exclusively defined as 'resembling wire in form or texture' or 'lean and strong,' not in reference to being related to actual wire, which would be rare and non-idiomatic. Therefore, this sense does not need a separate entry.
