seaward

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00466390-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: seaward|oewn-00466390-a
(of winds) coming from the land

Synset Examples

offshore winds

Reason

This concept defines 'seaward' as an adjective meaning '(of winds) coming from the land,' but in standard English usage 'seaward' generally means 'toward the sea.' The sense described here is overly specific and possibly a confusion with 'offshore.' This usage does not merit a separate dictionary entry as it is not standard or widely recognized in modern English, nor does it represent the main or an accepted secondary sense of the word.
