unvoluntary

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02531409-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: unvoluntary|oewn-02531409-a
not subject to the control of the will

Synset Examples

involuntary manslaughter
involuntary servitude
an involuntary shudder
It (becoming a hero) was involuntary. They sank my boat

Reason

The concept 'unvoluntary' is not standard English; the correct and commonly used term is 'involuntary.' 'Unvoluntary' does not appear in reputable dictionaries and is not idiomatic. The examples provided all use 'involuntary.' Therefore, it should not be included in a standard English-English dictionary.
