lour

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00268741-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: lour|oewn-00268741-v
make lower or quieter

Synset Examples

turn down the volume of a radio

Reason

The verb 'lour' is not used with the meaning 'make lower or quieter' in English. It appears to be a misattribution. In reputable dictionaries, 'lour' typically means 'to look sullen or threatening' or 'to frown.' The concept described here ('make lower or quieter', as in lowering volume) is not associated with 'lour.' Therefore, it should not be included in the dictionary for this meaning.
