Chief Executive

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00598380-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Chief Executive|oewn-00598380-n
the office of the United States head of state

Synset Examples

a President is elected every four years

Reason

This sense refers broadly to the office rather than a specific, established meaning of 'Chief Executive' as a dictionary entry. The phrase 'Chief Executive' as a title or a person should be included, but this synset refers only to the office of U.S. head of state, which is covered by 'President'. It is too general and not a distinct headword for a dictionary.
