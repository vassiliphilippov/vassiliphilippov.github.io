change her mind

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: change her mind|extra.001904
to alter one's decision or opinion

Synset Examples

She might change her mind about the vacation.
She's not likely to change her mind this late.

Reason

The phrase 'change her mind' is simply a specific grammatical instance of the general phrasal verb 'change one's mind', which is the canonical dictionary entry. Dictionary entries would be for the generalizable form, not the specific inflection 'her'. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a separate headword.
