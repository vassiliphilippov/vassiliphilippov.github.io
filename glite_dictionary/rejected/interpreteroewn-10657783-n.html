interpreter

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10657783-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: interpreter|oewn-10657783-n
an advocate who represents someone else's policy or purpose

Synset Examples

the meeting was attended by spokespersons for all the major organs of government

Reason

This sense of 'interpreter' is too general and does not reflect standard or recognized usage in English; it refers to someone who advocates or represents another's policy, which is more accurately and commonly described by words like 'spokesperson', 'advocate', or 'representative'. This meaning is not found in Oxford or other standard dictionaries under 'interpreter', so it should be excluded.
