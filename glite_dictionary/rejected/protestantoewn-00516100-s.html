Reason

The adjective sense 'protestant' meaning 'protesting' is rarely or never used in contemporary English. The common meaning of 'Protestant' as relating to a branch of Christianity would be included, but not this obsolete/extremely rare participial sense. It would not appear as a standalone entry in standard dictionaries like Oxford.
