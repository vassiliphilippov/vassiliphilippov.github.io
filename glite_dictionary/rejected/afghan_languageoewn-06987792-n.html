Afghan language

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06987792-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Afghan language|oewn-06987792-n
an Iranian language spoken in Afghanistan and Pakistan; the official language of Afghanistan

Reason

The synset 'Afghan language' is equivalent to the more common and proper term 'Pashto' (or occasionally 'Dari' as the other official Afghan language). 'Afghan language' as a noun phrase is not standard usage in English dictionaries; established dictionaries do not record an entry for it. Appropriate dictionary entries are for 'Pashto' and 'Dari'. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
