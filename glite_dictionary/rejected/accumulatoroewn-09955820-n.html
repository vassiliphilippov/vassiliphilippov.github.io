accumulator

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09955820-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: accumulator|oewn-09955820-n
a person who is employed to collect payments (as for rent or taxes)

Reason

This sense of 'accumulator' as 'a person who is employed to collect payments' is obsolete and not used in modern English. The contemporary use of 'accumulator' refers to a device or person that collects or accumulates something, such as energy or items. Such a person is more commonly referred to as a 'collector' rather than 'accumulator.' Therefore, this sense does not warrant a dictionary entry.
