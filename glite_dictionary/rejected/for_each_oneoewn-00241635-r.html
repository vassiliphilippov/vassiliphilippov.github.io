to or from every one of two or more (considered individually)

Synset Examples

Reason

The concept 'for each one' as an adverbial phrase simply expresses a distributive sense, usually covered by the standard adverb 'each.' Dictionary entries typically list 'each' as the main headword, with explanatory phrases in the definition and examples. The phrase 'for each one' is not a fixed idiom or unique semantic unit but a compositional phrase, so it does not warrant a separate entry in a dictionary like Oxford.
