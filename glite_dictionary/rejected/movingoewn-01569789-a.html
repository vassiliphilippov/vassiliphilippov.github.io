moving

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01569789-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: moving|oewn-01569789-a
used of a series of photographs presented so as to create the illusion of motion

Synset Examples

Her ambition was to be in moving pictures or ‘the movies’

Reason

This sense of 'moving' is a specialized, archaic adjectival use referring to 'moving pictures', which is now obsolete outside of set phrases and historical contexts. It is not a distinctive, current meaning of 'moving' as an adjective that would appear as a separate sense in most modern dictionaries. The primary senses of 'moving' (as causing emotion or in motion) are already included elsewhere; this niche cinematic sense is adequately covered under 'moving picture'.
