Reason

The synset describes a highly abstract and idiomatic use ("receive a specified treatment") which does not correspond to the core sense or an established dictionary sense of 'obtain'. Standard dictionaries like Oxford do not list 'obtain' with this meaning (e.g., to receive an interpretation or treatment). It is a more general semantic pattern in English and not a distinct sense of the verb 'obtain' suitable for a dictionary entry.
