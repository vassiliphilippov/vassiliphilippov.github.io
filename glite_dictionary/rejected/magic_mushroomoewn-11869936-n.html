magic mushroom

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11869936-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: magic mushroom|oewn-11869936-n
the button-shaped top of the mescal cactus; a source of psilocybin

Reason

The synset refers to the button-shaped top of the mescal cactus, which is known as peyote, not a 'magic mushroom.' The headword 'magic mushroom' in English refers specifically to certain species of fungi containing psilocybin, not to the cactus top. This sense is too general and mismatched and should not be included under 'magic mushroom.'
