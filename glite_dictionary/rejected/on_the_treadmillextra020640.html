on the treadmill

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: on the treadmill|extra.020640
using a treadmill for exercise

Synset Examples

She spends an hour daily on the treadmill.

Reason

The concept 'on the treadmill' in this sense simply uses the prepositional phrase to describe being physically on a piece of exercise equipment. It is not a set phrase, idiom, or phrasal verb with special meaning beyond the literal. Such prepositional phrases are too compositional and do not warrant separate dictionary entries in a standard English dictionary like Oxford.
