nowadays

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15144478-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: nowadays|oewn-15144478-n
the period of time that is happening now; any continuous stretch of time including the moment of speech

Synset Examples

that is enough for the present
he lives in the present with no thought of tomorrow

Reason

The synset described ('any continuous stretch of time including the moment of speech') matches the noun sense of 'present', not 'nowadays'. 'Nowadays' is not used as a noun but as an adverb meaning 'at the present time, in contrast with the past'. Therefore, this concept should be excluded because the synset is not truly related to the headword.
