Benzoin

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11726589-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Benzoin|oewn-11726589-n
used in some classifications for the American spicebush and certain other plants often included in the genus Lindera

Reason

This sense of 'benzoin' is an obscure botanical usage referring to certain plants often included in the genus Lindera, which is not the commonly used sense and is too technical and rare for general English dictionaries like Oxford. Modern dictionaries define 'benzoin' primarily as a resin or compound, not as a synonym for 'spicebush.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
