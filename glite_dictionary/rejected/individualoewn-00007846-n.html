Reason

The described synset refers to 'individual' used as an assertive existential pronoun or as a general quantifier, which is too generic and not directly related to the headword 'individual' as a dictionary entry. Such grammatical functions and synonyms to 'person' are either dictionary entries under 'individual' as a noun or represented by other determiners/pronouns (e.g., 'one', 'someone'). Including this concept would be redundant and not meaningful as its own entry.
