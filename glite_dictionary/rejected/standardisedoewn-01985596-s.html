standardised

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01985596-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: standardised|oewn-01985596-s
capable of replacing or changing places with something else; permitting mutual substitution without loss of function or suitability

Synset Examples

interchangeable electric outlets
interchangeable parts

Reason

The concept as described refers to 'interchangeable' and not specifically to 'standardised.' The synset meaning is about being able to substitute or replace without loss of function, which is properly captured by 'interchangeable,' a word that already appears in dictionaries with this sense. 'Standardised' is not the correct headword for this definition, so it should be excluded.
