Reason

The lemma 'unconfessed' in this sense refers to 'people who have not confessed', which is not a standard noun in English. 'Unconfessed' is typically used as an adjective, not as a noun referring to people. This noun usage is too rare and not established enough to warrant a dictionary entry as a headword. Thus, this concept should be excluded.
