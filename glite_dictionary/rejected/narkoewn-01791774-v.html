nark

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01791774-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: nark|oewn-01791774-v
cause annoyance in; disturb, especially by minor irritations

Synset Examples

Mosquitoes buzzing in my ear really bothers me
It irritates me that she never closes the door after she leaves

Reason

The verb 'nark' in the sense of 'cause annoyance in; disturb, especially by minor irritations' is very rare and largely regional/informal in this usage. It does not commonly appear in standard dictionaries like Oxford in this sense; instead, 'nark' is much more often used as a noun (meaning 'informer') or as a verb meaning 'to inform to the authorities.' Thus, this concept is not suitable for inclusion as a general dictionary entry for most learners.
