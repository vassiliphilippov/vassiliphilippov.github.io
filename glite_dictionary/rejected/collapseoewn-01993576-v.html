Reason

The sense 'collapse' meaning 'cause to burst' is not a standard or recognized meaning of the verb 'collapse' in contemporary English dictionaries. This seems to be a confusion or mapping error, as 'collapse' does not generally mean 'to cause something (like a pipe) to burst.' Therefore, this sense should not be included as it would not appear in a standard dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary.
