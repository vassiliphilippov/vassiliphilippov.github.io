Reason

The synset described ('especially' meaning 'in a special manner') is not related to the usual adverb 'especially' in modern English, which means 'particularly' or 'above all.' The sense 'in a special manner' would be more precisely conveyed by 'specially.' Therefore, this particular concept is too broad or not directly related to the headword and should not be included.
