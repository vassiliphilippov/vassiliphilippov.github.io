gutter

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10172529-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: gutter|oewn-10172529-n
a worker who guts things (fish or buildings or cars etc.)

Reason

This sense describes 'gutter' as a person who guts things, but this usage is extremely rare and not widely recognized in modern or historical English dictionaries. The common senses of 'gutter' refer to a channel for carrying off water or as a verb meaning to cut open and remove the insides, especially of fish, but not as an agent noun. Therefore, it should be excluded.
