representative

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05828980-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: representative|oewn-05828980-n
an item of information that is typical of a class or group

Synset Examples

this patient provides a typical example of the syndrome
there is an example on page 10

Reason

The concept for 'representative' here refers to an 'item of information that is typical of a class or group,' which is more precisely covered by the sense of 'example' or 'specimen.' 'Representative' is not commonly used as a noun in this exact meaning in everyday English; rather, its main noun sense is 'a person representing others.' This usage would not be separately included in a standard dictionary like Oxford.
