sporadicalness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-92418999-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: sporadicalness|oewn-92418999-n
The quality of being rare and scattered in occurrence.

Synset Examples

The sporadicalness of the collections meant that one master could not rely on an overlap to coordinate a takeover.

Reason

The term 'sporadicalness' is a highly uncommon and nonstandard derivative of 'sporadic.' Standard English dictionaries use 'sporadicity' or 'sporadicness' (though even those are rare), but 'sporadicalness' is almost never used in reputable sources and does not warrant an entry in a mainstream dictionary.
