protagonist

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10697152-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: protagonist|oewn-10697152-n
a person who backs a politician or a team etc.

Synset Examples

all their supporters came out for the game
they are friends of the library

Reason

The synset described refers to 'supporter' or 'backer', not the main accepted meaning of 'protagonist'. The primary sense of 'protagonist' is a leading character or main figure, especially in a literary or dramatic work. The meaning given here is not standard for 'protagonist' and does not appear in major dictionaries. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
