resplend

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02739328-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: resplend|oewn-02739328-v
be resplendent or radiant; to shine brightly

Synset Examples

The Queen's garments resplended in velvet and jewels

Reason

The verb 'resplend' is extremely rare and archaic, not used in modern English; standard usage is 'resplendent' (an adjective). This word does not appear in contemporary dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster even as archaic or obsolete, and thus should not be included.
