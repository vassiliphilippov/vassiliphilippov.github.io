Justice

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08152324-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Justice|oewn-08152324-n
the United States federal department responsible for enforcing federal laws (including the enforcement of all civil rights legislation); created in 1870

Reason

This concept refers to a specific governmental department (the United States Department of Justice) but is labelled only as 'Justice'; this is too general and does not represent a dictionary sense of the noun 'justice.' The dictionary should instead include 'Department of Justice' or 'Justice Department' for this meaning.
