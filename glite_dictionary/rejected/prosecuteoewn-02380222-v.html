prosecute

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02380222-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: prosecute|oewn-02380222-v
carry out or participate in an activity; be involved in

Synset Examples

She pursued many activities
They engaged in a discussion

Reason

This sense of 'prosecute' (to carry out or participate in an activity; be involved in) is far too broad and not the common or dictionary-recognized use of 'prosecute.' Standard dictionaries focus on its legal sense, not this generic usage, which is better covered by verbs like 'pursue,' 'participate,' or 'engage.' It would not be included as a primary sense in Oxford or similar dictionaries.
