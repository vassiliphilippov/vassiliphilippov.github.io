Reason

This sense of 'priority' as 'preceding in time' is too general and not commonly used in modern English with this meaning. In contemporary usage, 'priority' is primarily used to refer to something regarded as more important, or the right to proceed before others. The sense described here is obsolete or extremely rare and would not appear as a distinct entry in a modern dictionary.
