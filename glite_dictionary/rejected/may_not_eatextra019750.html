may not eat

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: may not eat|extra.019750
prohibited from eating

Synset Examples

He said you mayn't eat in the library.

Reason

The concept 'may not eat' is simply a combination of the modal verb 'may' in the negative form with the verb 'eat', indicating prohibition. This construction is regular and fully compositional, and would not appear as a separate dictionary entry in standard dictionaries. The contraction 'mayn't' is extremely rare and nonstandard, but even so, 'may not' is not an idiom or lexicalized phrase. Therefore, this concept should not be included.
