load

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04558661-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: load|oewn-04558661-n
the front part of a guided missile or rocket or torpedo that carries the nuclear or explosive charge or the chemical or biological agents

Reason

This concept defines 'load' in a very narrow, technical sense referring specifically to the explosive or agent-carrying part of a missile or torpedo. However, in general use and major dictionaries, this sense is typically covered by more specific terms like 'warhead' or 'payload', not 'load' by itself. Including this would be misleading as 'load' alone does not commonly refer to this concept in standard English.
