serial

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06633954-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: serial|oewn-06633954-n
a serialized set of programs

Synset Examples

a comedy series
the Masterworks concert series

Reason

The concept given refers to 'serial' as a set of programs (such as a TV or radio series), but the typical dictionary entry for this sense would be under the noun 'series' rather than 'serial'. The noun 'serial' generally denotes something published or broadcast in parts rather than the set as a whole. The synset is too general for the headword 'serial' and is better represented by the term 'series.' Therefore, this concept should not be included for 'serial' as a headword.
