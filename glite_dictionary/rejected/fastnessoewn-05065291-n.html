fastness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05065291-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: fastness|oewn-05065291-n
a rate (usually rapid) at which something happens

Synset Examples

the project advanced with gratifying speed

Reason

This sense of 'fastness' ('a rate (usually rapid) at which something happens') is rarely or never used in modern English; the standard noun for this concept is 'speed'. In Oxford and other major dictionaries, 'fastness' is not defined as 'speed' but as a stronghold or place of safety. Therefore, this usage should be excluded.
