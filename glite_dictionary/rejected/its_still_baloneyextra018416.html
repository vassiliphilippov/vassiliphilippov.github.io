it's still baloney

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: it's still baloney|extra.018416
it's still nonsense or untrue

Synset Examples

Her excuses were weak; no matter how thin you slice it, it's still baloney.

Reason

The concept 'it's still baloney' refers to a general statement that something is nonsense, derived from the slang use of 'baloney.' However, 'it's still baloney' is not a fixed idiom or well-attested phrase; rather, 'baloney' itself in the sense of 'nonsense' is the dictionary-worthy headword. The example is a creative, disposable expression and not a well-established idiom or entry as found in standard dictionaries.
