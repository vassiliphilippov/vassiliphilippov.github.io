lien

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05341015-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: lien|oewn-05341015-n
a large dark-red oval organ on the left side of the body between the stomach and the diaphragm; produces cells involved in immune responses

Reason

This synset of 'lien' refers specifically to the anatomical organ 'spleen', which is a technical/medical term. However, in English, 'lien' is overwhelmingly used as a legal term referring to a legal right to keep possession of property. The use of 'lien' to mean 'spleen' is now archaic, extremely rare, and not included in modern general English dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary. This concept is either obsolete or technical Latin, so it should not be included in a general English-English dictionary.
