optimize

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00011845-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: optimize|oewn-00011845-v
act as an optimist and take a sunny view of the world

Reason

This sense of 'optimize'—meaning to act as an optimist or to take a sunny view of the world—is not standard and does not appear in reputable English dictionaries like Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The common meaning of 'optimize' relates to making the best or most effective use of something, not to the disposition of optimism. Therefore, this nonstandard, confusing sense should be excluded.
