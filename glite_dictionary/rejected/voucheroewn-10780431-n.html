voucher

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10780431-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: voucher|oewn-10780431-n
someone who vouches for another or for the correctness of a statement

Reason

This sense of 'voucher' as 'someone who vouches for another or for the correctness of a statement' is extremely rare or archaic in modern English. The primary and widespread use of 'voucher' today is as a noun meaning a coupon or receipt, not as a person. Therefore, this person-based sense should not be included in a modern learner's dictionary.
