suspect

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09781524-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: suspect|oewn-09781524-n
a person or institution against whom an action is brought in a court of law; the person being sued or accused

Reason

This synset is too general and not specific to 'suspect.' It describes any person or institution against whom legal action is brought (such as 'defendant'), not specifically a 'suspect,' which refers to a person thought to have committed a crime. Therefore, this concept should not be included for 'suspect.'
