sophistication

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05903745-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: sophistication|oewn-05903745-n
a deliberately invalid argument displaying ingenuity in reasoning in the hope of deceiving someone

Reason

This sense of 'sophistication' as a deliberately invalid argument is archaic and not widely used in modern English. The headword 'sophistication' in modern usage refers to refinement, complexity, or worldliness, not to fallacious reasoning. This concept does not correspond to any main or commonly recognized dictionary meaning and would not appear in a major dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary under 'sophistication.'
