trundle

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04498840-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: trundle|oewn-04498840-n
small wheel or roller

Reason

The sense 'trundle' meaning a 'small wheel or roller' is obsolete and extremely rare; it does not appear in major modern dictionaries like Oxford in this sense. In current English, 'trundle' is known as a verb or as a noun meaning a type of bed on wheels (trundle bed), but not as a general noun for a small wheel or roller.
