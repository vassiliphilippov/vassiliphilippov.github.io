mind

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05770146-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: mind|oewn-05770146-n
recall or remembrance

Synset Examples

it came to mind

Reason

This sense of 'mind' as recall or remembrance is too general and not distinct in modern dictionary usage; the phrase 'it came to mind' uses 'mind' in its standard sense of 'the faculty of thought,' not as an independent noun meaning 'recall' or 'remembrance.' Therefore, this sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
