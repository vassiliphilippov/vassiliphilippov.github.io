Synset Examples

Reason

The synset is 'any liquid suitable for drinking,' but the headword 'drinkable' is almost exclusively used as an adjective, not as a noun. The noun sense 'drinkable' meaning 'a drink' is not standard and would not appear in major English dictionaries like Oxford. This noun sense is rare, nonstandard, and not dictionary-worthy.
