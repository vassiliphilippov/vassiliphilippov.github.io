last word

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04820943-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: last word|oewn-04820943-n
elegance by virtue of being fashionable

Reason

The synset refers to a highly idiomatic and dated usage of 'last word' meaning 'elegance by virtue of being fashionable,' which is not common or relevant in modern English and would not appear as a primary sense in Oxford Dictionary entries today. The main phrase 'last word' is usually included in dictionaries in the sense of 'the most modern or best example of something,' but not primarily or solely as 'elegance by virtue of being fashionable.'
