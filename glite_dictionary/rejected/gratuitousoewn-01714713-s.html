gratuitous

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01714713-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: gratuitous|oewn-01714713-s
costing nothing

Synset Examples

complimentary tickets
free admission

Reason

This sense of 'gratuitous' meaning 'costing nothing' is now archaic and rarely used. In modern English, 'gratis' or 'complimentary' are used for this meaning, and 'gratuitous' most commonly means 'uncalled for' or 'unjustified.' Including this sense would not reflect current English usage.
