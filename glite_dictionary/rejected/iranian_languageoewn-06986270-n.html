Reason

The synset for 'Iranian language' in this context refers to a general language family, but the definition provided is for 'the modern Persian language spoken in Iran', which is more accurately called 'Persian' or 'Farsi'. 'Iranian language' as a dictionary entry would be too broad and potentially misleading. The proper entry for the language spoken in Iran is 'Persian' or 'Farsi', not 'Iranian language'. Therefore, this concept as stated should not be included.
