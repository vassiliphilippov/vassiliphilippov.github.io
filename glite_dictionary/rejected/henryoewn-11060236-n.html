Reason

The synset for 'Henry' here corresponds to a general proper noun meaning 'person', specifically the historical figure Joseph Henry. As with other personal names whose synsets only relate to 'person', standard English dictionaries like Oxford typically do not include biographical entries for individuals unless they have become common nouns or adjectives. This is primarily encyclopedic rather than lexicographical content.
