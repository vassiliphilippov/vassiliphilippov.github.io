Wilno

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09036643-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Wilno|oewn-09036643-n
the capital and largest city of Lithuania; located in southeastern Lithuania

Reason

'Wilno' is an alternative or historical name (primarily the Polish name) for Vilnius, the capital of Lithuania. The standard English name is 'Vilnius', and 'Wilno' is not commonly used in English. Including 'Wilno' as a main dictionary entry would not fit standard dictionary practice; instead, it could be mentioned as an alternative name within the entry for 'Vilnius'. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
