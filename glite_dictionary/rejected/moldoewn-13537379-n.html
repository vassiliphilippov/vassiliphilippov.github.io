the process of becoming mildewed

Reason

This sense describes 'mold' as an abstract process ('the process of becoming mildewed'), which is not a standalone meaning commonly listed in major English dictionaries. The main senses are as a noun (the fungus itself, or a shaped form) or as a verb (to shape something), but not the process as such. This is too abstract and not specific to the headword as commonly used.
