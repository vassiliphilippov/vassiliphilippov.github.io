overstep

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02674870-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: overstep|oewn-02674870-v
be superior or better than some standard

Synset Examples

She exceeded our expectations
She topped her performance of last year

Reason

The concept 'overstep' with the sense 'be superior or better than some standard' does not correspond to the main usage of the word 'overstep.' In standard English (and Oxford Dictionary usage), 'overstep' means 'to go beyond what is permitted or acceptable,' not 'to exceed or surpass' as in 'exceed' or 'surpass.' Since the synset relates to a more general meaning not attached to 'overstep' in standard usage, this sense should be excluded from the dictionary.
