Synset Examples

The candles are tapered

Reason

This sense of 'point' is too narrow, rare, and technical (meaning 'to make pointed, to give a point to,' as in tapering a candle). It is not a commonly recognized or general sense in contemporary English, nor would it typically have its own dictionary entry in most learners' dictionaries. The usual verb sense of 'point' (to indicate something with a gesture) is included, but this specific sense is highly specialized and obsolete.
