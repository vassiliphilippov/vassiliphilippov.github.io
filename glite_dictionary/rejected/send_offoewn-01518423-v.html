send off

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01518423-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: send off|oewn-01518423-v
throw, send, or cast forward

Synset Examples

project a missile

Reason

The sense of 'send off' here is overly general, essentially meaning 'to send' or 'to project,' and is not a commonly recognized or independently idiomatic phrasal verb in standard English usage in this sense. This usage would not appear as a separate entry in Oxford Dictionary; the main senses of 'send off' relate to farewells or dispatching someone or something on a journey, not specifically to throwing or projecting forward. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
