content

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04354303-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: content|oewn-04354303-n
something (a person or object or scene) selected by an artist or photographer for graphic representation

Synset Examples

a moving picture of a train is more dramatic than a still picture of the same subject

Reason

This sense of 'content' refers to a general concept ('something selected by an artist or photographer') but is too broad and generic. This exact sense is rarely used as a dictionary word with this specific meaning; 'content' in standard dictionaries more commonly refers to 'what is contained', 'subject matter', or 'digital media material.' This sense would not appear as a headword or major sub-entry in a dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary.
