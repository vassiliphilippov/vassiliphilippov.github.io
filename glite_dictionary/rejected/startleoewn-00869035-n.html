startle

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00869035-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: startle|oewn-00869035-n
a sudden involuntary movement

Synset Examples

he awoke with a start

Reason

The synset for 'startle' here refers to a sudden involuntary movement, which is already more commonly expressed by the noun 'start' (as in 'with a start'). 'Startle' as a noun is rare, nonstandard, or obsolete. Modern dictionaries (e.g., Oxford, Merriam-Webster) do not list 'startle' as a noun in this sense, but do include the verb 'to startle.' Therefore, it should not be kept as a dictionary headword in this sense.
