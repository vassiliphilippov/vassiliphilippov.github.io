exudate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00067770-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: exudate|oewn-00067770-v
release (a liquid) in drops or small quantities

Synset Examples

exude sweat through the pores

Reason

The verb 'exudate' meaning 'to release (a liquid) in drops or small quantities' is not established or recognized in standard English usage; the correct verb form is 'exude.' 'Exudate' is used as a noun in medical/scientific contexts, but not as a verb. Therefore, this verb sense should not be included in the dictionary.
