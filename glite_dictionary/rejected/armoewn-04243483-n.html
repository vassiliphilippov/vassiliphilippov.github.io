arm

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04243483-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: arm|oewn-04243483-n
the part of a garment that is attached at the armhole and that provides a cloth covering for the arm

Reason

This sense of 'arm' refers to the sleeve of a garment, but in standard English, the part of a garment covering the arm is referred to as a 'sleeve,' not an 'arm.' The synset definition is not commonly used with 'arm' in this context and would not appear as a standalone entry in standard dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
