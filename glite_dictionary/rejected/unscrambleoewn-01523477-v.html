Synset Examples

Reason

This sense of 'unscramble' (to become or cause to become undone by separating the fibers or threads) is not a standard meaning of 'unscramble'. In modern English and reputable dictionaries, 'unscramble' refers to restoring order to something scrambled, such as a code or mixed-up information, not to separating fibers or threads (which is instead covered by 'unravel'). This concept should not be included as it is not attested or recognized for 'unscramble'.
