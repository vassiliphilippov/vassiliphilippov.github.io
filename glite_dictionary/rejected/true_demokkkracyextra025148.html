Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a

Internal ID: true demokkkracy|extra.025148
a flawed or inauthentic form of democracy

Synset Examples

They claimed their system was a true demokkkracy.

Reason

The term 'true demokkkracy' is a nonce word and a deliberate misspelling of 'true democracy' used to criticize or mock perceived flawed democracy, but it is not an established word or phrase in English, nor would it appear in a reputable dictionary like Oxford. Its spelling also deliberately incorporates 'kkk' for provocative effect, which is not dictionary-worthy.
