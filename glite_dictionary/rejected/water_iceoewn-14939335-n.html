Synset POS: n

water frozen in the solid state

Synset Examples

Americans like ice in their drinks

Reason

The concept 'water ice' in this synset simply refers to ice as frozen water, which is already covered by the common noun 'ice'. The term is not materially distinct from 'ice' in general use and does not warrant a separate entry. Additionally, most dictionaries treat 'ice' (frozen water) as the canonical headword; 'water ice' as a variant is not widely used or necessary for general learners.
