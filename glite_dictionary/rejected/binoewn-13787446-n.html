the quantity contained in a bin

Reason

The concept refers to the specific sense 'the quantity contained in a bin,' which is an uncommon and context-specific usage. Standard dictionaries do not typically include such container-quantity senses unless they are widely used as measure words or units (e.g., 'a cup of', 'a barrel of'). 'Bin' in this sense does not have a distinct, established usage separate from the ordinary meaning of the container itself. Therefore, this specific sense should be excluded.
