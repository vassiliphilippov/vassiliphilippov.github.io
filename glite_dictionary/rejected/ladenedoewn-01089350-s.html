ladened

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01089350-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: ladened|oewn-01089350-s
filled with a great quantity

Synset Examples

a tray loaded with dishes
table laden with food
‘ladened’ is not current usage

Reason

The concept 'ladened' is a nonstandard or archaic variant of 'laden' and is not current usage, as noted in the example. Standard dictionaries would list the correct form 'laden'. This form should not be included as a headword.
