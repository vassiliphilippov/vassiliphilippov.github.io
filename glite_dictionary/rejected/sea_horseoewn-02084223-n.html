sea horse

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02084223-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: sea horse|oewn-02084223-n
either of two large northern marine mammals having ivory tusks and tough hide over thick blubber

Reason

This sense of 'sea horse' refers to marine mammals (likely walrus), but this is not a standard or commonly recognized meaning of 'sea horse' in modern English dictionaries. 'Sea horse' generally refers to the small fish of the genus Hippocampus. This synset is too general and does not correctly correspond to the expected headword meaning for inclusion.
