tiptoe

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: tiptoe|oewn-05584935-n
the tip of a toe

Reason

The concept 'tiptoe' as 'the tip of a toe' is too specific, rare, and not meaningfully distinct in contemporary usage. Dictionaries usually do not define 'tiptoe' as a noun specifically referring to 'the tip of a toe'; rather, it is commonly used as a verb (to walk quietly on the balls of one's feet) or as a noun meaning the act or state of being on one's toes. The sense given is not standard and would not appear in Oxford Dictionary.
