break

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09301443-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: break|oewn-09301443-n
(geology) a crack in the earth's crust resulting from the displacement of one side with respect to the other

Synset Examples

they built it right over a geological fault
he studied the faulting of the earth's crust

Reason

This sense of 'break' refers specifically to a geological crack in the Earth's crust, but the standard and widely accepted term for this in English is 'fault.' 'Break' is not commonly used in modern geology or general English to refer to this concept, and it would not appear as a primary sense in standard English dictionaries like Oxford for this meaning. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
