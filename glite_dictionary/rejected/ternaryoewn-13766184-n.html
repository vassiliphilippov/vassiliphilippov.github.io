the cardinal number that is the sum of one and one and one

Reason

This sense of 'ternary' as a synonym for the number three is very rarely used in contemporary English and does not appear in major dictionaries in this sense. The usual noun for the number is 'three,' and 'ternary' is primarily used as an adjective (such as in mathematics, meaning 'involving three parts or elements'). Therefore, this noun sense should not be included in a modern English-English dictionary.
