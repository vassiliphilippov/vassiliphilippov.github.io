forego

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02308311-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: forego|oewn-02308311-v
lose (something) or lose the right to (something) by some error, offense, or crime

Synset Examples

you've forfeited your right to name your successor
forfeited property

Reason

This concept describes 'forego' with the meaning 'lose (something) or lose the right to (something)', but in modern English, this sense is represented by 'forfeit'. The standard verb 'forego' (also spelled as 'forgo') means 'to go before' or 'to abstain from'. This synset appears to be a confusion or overlap with 'forfeit', which is a separate verb. Therefore, including 'forego' with this sense would not reflect contemporary or correct dictionary usage.
