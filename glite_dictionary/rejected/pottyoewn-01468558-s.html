potty

marked by foolish or unreasoning fondness

Synset Examples

he was infatuated with her
Narcissus was a beautiful Greek youth who became enamored of his own reflection

Reason

This sense of 'potty' as 'marked by foolish or unreasoning fondness' is not attested in modern standard English dictionaries like Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The examples given are more linked to words like 'infatuated' or 'enamored.' 'Potty' is not used with this meaning in contemporary English; its common uses relate to silliness (informal: crazy) or to children's toilet training. Therefore, this sense should not be included.
