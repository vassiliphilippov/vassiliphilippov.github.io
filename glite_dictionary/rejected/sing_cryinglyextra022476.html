sing cryingly

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: sing cryingly|extra.022476
singing in a sorrowful or emotional manner

Synset Examples

The children sang cryingly at the memorial service.

Reason

The concept 'sing cryingly' uses the rare and unidiomatic adverb 'cryingly', which is not established in modern standard English and does not appear in reputable dictionaries like Oxford. The meaning can be expressed more naturally with 'sing sorrowfully', 'sing tearfully', or 'sing while crying'. Therefore, it should not be included as a dictionary entry.
