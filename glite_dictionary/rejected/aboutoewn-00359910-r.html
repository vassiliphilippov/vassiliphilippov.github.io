in rotation or succession

Synset Examples

turn about is fair play

Reason

This adverbial sense of 'about' ('in rotation or succession') is highly archaic and found only in fixed, fossilized expressions such as 'turn about is fair play.' It is not used in modern English as a general adverb with this meaning, and thus does not warrant a separate entry outside of those specific idioms. The general preposition and adverb senses of 'about' are covered elsewhere.
