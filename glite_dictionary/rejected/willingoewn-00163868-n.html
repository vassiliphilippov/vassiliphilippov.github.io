the act of making a choice

Synset Examples

followed my father of my own volition

Reason

The noun sense 'willing' as 'the act of making a choice' is extremely rare and not attested in standard modern English. In standard usage, 'willing' is an adjective, whereas the noun forms relevant to this meaning are 'will' or 'volition.' This noun sense would not appear in Oxford or major dictionaries, so it should be excluded.
