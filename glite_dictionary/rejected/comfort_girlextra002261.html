comfort girl

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: comfort girl|extra.002261
a girl providing emotional support

Synset Examples

She played the role of the comfort girl in the film.
In the story, the comfort girl helped him heal emotionally.
He often called her his comfort girl during tough times.

Reason

The synset 'comfort girl' here refers simply to a girl providing emotional support, which is too general and does not represent a commonly recognized term with an independent entry in standard English dictionaries such as Oxford. Additionally, 'comfort girl' is far more widely known as an offensive historical euphemism for women forced into sexual slavery by the Japanese military in WWII, but that meaning is not the one in this synset. The generic meaning given here is not dictionary-worthy as a separate entry.
