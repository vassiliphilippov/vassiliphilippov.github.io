Reason

The concept 'hero of the sea' is a descriptive noun phrase rather than an established lexical item or recognized compound. It is formed transparently from 'hero' and 'sea,' and does not have a distinct meaning from its component words. Such phrases are generally not included in standard dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary unless they are widely used set phrases or idioms, which this is not.
