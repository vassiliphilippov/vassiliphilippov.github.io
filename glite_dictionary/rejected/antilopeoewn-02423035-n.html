Reason

'Antilope' as a dictionary entry is not appropriate, since it refers to the genus of the blackbuck and is a Latin scientific name, not a common English word. Oxford and similar dictionaries do not generally include generic Latin genus names unless they have become established as common English nouns (which 'Antilope' has not).
