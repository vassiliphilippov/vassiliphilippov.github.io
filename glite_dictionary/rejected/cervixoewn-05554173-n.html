cervix

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05554173-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: cervix|oewn-05554173-n
the part of an organism (human or animal) that connects the head to the rest of the body

Synset Examples

he admired her long graceful neck
the horse won by a neck

Reason

This sense of 'cervix' as 'neck' is not used in modern English; 'cervix' in contemporary English almost exclusively refers to the lower part of the uterus in female mammals. This general sense ('neck' connecting head and body) is archaic, rare, and does not appear in major modern dictionaries outside specialized or historical contexts. Therefore, it should not be included as a main dictionary entry.
