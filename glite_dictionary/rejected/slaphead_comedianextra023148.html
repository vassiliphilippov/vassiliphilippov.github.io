slaphead comedian

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: slaphead comedian|extra.023148
bald comedian, often humorously self-deprecating

Synset Examples

The slaphead comedian had everyone laughing at his jokes.

Reason

The phrase 'slaphead comedian' is not a standard dictionary entry; it is a descriptive phrase combining 'slaphead' (a slang term for a bald person) and 'comedian.' This is too specific and compositional to warrant its own dictionary entry. Oxford and similar dictionaries would include 'slaphead,' but not 'slaphead comedian.'
