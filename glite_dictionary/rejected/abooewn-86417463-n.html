Abo

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-86417463-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Abo|oewn-86417463-n
a dark-skinned member of a race of people living in Australia when Europeans arrived

Reason

'Abo' refers to a member of the Australian Aboriginal people but is considered highly offensive and derogatory. While it may be attested in some older dictionaries, most modern dictionaries exclude or heavily mark it as an offensive ethnic slur. The term is not included according to current mainstream dictionary policies due to its extremely offensive nature.
