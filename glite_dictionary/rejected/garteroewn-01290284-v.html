fasten with or as if with a garter

Reason

The verb sense 'to garter' (meaning to fasten with or as if with a garter) is extremely rare and not in current standard usage. Most major modern dictionaries do not include this verb form, and it is not likely to be encountered by learners or general readers. Only the noun sense (the band worn around the leg) is in common use.
