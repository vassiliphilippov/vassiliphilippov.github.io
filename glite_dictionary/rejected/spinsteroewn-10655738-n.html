spinster

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10655738-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: spinster|oewn-10655738-n
someone who spins (who twists fibers into threads)

Reason

This sense of 'spinster' (meaning someone who spins thread) is archaic and extremely rare; the word is now almost exclusively used to mean an unmarried woman (usually older). The technical referent for someone who spins is 'spinner.' Thus, this sense would not typically appear in a modern English dictionary entry for 'spinster'.
