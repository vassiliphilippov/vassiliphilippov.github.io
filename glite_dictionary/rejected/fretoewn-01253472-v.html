Reason

The sense 'fret' as 'cause friction' in the context of 'my sweater scratches' is extremely rare and does not correspond to how 'fret' is standardly used in modern English. This usage is too technical/archaic and does not appear in reputable dictionaries like Oxford under this sense. The commonly used senses relate to worry, erosion, or the ridges on a guitar. Therefore, it should be excluded.
