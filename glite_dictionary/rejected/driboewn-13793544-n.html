drib

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13793544-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: drib|oewn-13793544-n
a small indefinite quantity (especially of a liquid)

Synset Examples

he had a drop too much to drink
a drop of each sample was analyzed
there is not a drop of pity in that man
years afterward, they would pay the blood-money, driblet by driblet

Reason

The sense of 'drib' as 'a small indefinite quantity (especially of a liquid)' is extremely rare and not standard in modern English. This form is almost entirely replaced by 'driblet', 'drop', or 'dribble'. Its use does not warrant inclusion in a contemporary learner's dictionary like Oxford; it is not encountered in general usage or even in literary/historical contexts.
