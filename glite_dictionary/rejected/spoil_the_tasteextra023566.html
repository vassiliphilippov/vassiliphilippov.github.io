Reason

The concept 'spoil the taste' is a verb phrase composed transparently of 'spoil' + 'the taste', and its meaning (ruin the flavor of food) is directly deducible from the meanings of its parts. Such transparent verbal phrases are typically not included as separate dictionary entries in Oxford-style dictionaries, unless they are idiomatic or non-compositional, which is not the case here.
