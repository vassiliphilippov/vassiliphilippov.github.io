Internal ID: spreading|oewn-06263270-n
the opening of a subject to widespread discussion and debate

Reason

Although the noun 'spreading' is attested, this specific sense ('the opening of a subject to widespread discussion and debate') is extremely rare, nonstandard, and not included in major dictionaries. In common usage, 'spreading' as a noun refers to the act of spreading, not to the abstract concept of debate or discussion. Therefore, this concept is too narrow and unused for inclusion as a standard dictionary entry.
