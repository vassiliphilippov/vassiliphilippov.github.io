Reason

The synset for 'Hawaiian capital' refers to the city of Honolulu, but the phrase 'Hawaiian capital' itself is a descriptive reference and not a standard headword or proper noun that would appear as an entry in a dictionary. Dictionaries would have an entry for 'Honolulu' rather than 'Hawaiian capital.' Therefore, it should be excluded.
