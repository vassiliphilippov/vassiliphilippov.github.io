Internal ID: unsuasible|oewn-02372813-s
not susceptible to persuasion

Reason

The word 'unsuasible' is extremely rare and archaic, and is not found in modern standard dictionaries like Oxford. Its meaning is completely covered by the more widely used and recognized adjective 'unsuadable' or 'unpersuadable.' Therefore, it does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
