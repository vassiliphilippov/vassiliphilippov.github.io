Reason

The concept 'Skoplje' refers to a specific place (the capital city of North Macedonia) and is a proper noun. However, 'Skoplje' is an obsolete or alternative spelling of 'Skopje,' which is the standard form used in modern English. Dictionaries like Oxford would list the most current and widely accepted spelling, which is 'Skopje.' Therefore, including 'Skoplje' is unnecessary and potentially confusing for learners.
