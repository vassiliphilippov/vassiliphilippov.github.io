Synset Examples

Reason

The verb sense of 'blackguard' meaning 'to subject to laughter or ridicule' is extremely rare, archaic, and not in current standard usage. Major English dictionaries, including the Oxford Dictionary, do not include 'blackguard' as a commonly used verb with this meaning. The noun form ('a scoundrel or villain') is in dictionaries, but not this verb sense. Therefore, it should be excluded.
