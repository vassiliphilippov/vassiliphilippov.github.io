Angora

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09063076-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Angora|oewn-09063076-n
the capital of Turkey; located in west-central Turkey; it was formerly known as Angora and is the home of Angora goats

Reason

The synset is 'the capital of Turkey', which is a general category (place, city), and only the former name 'Angora' is listed. The actual modern entry would be 'Ankara', not 'Angora', so this concept is too general for inclusion as a dictionary entry for 'Angora'.
