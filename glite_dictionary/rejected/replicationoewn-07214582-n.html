replication

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07214582-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: replication|oewn-07214582-n
a quick reply to a question or remark (especially a witty or critical one)

Synset Examples

it brought a sharp rejoinder from the teacher

Reason

This sense of 'replication' as a quick or witty reply is not a common or recognizable usage in modern English and does not appear in reputable dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The usual meanings of 'replication' relate to copying, reproduction, or scientific procedures, not to verbal replies. Therefore, this sense should be excluded as not dictionary-worthy.
