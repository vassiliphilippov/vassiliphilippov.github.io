medium to tall fir of western North America having a conic crown and branches in tiers; leaves smell of orange when crushed

Reason

This concept refers to a specific botanical description of a fir tree (Abies concolor or similar), not the common noun 'Christmas tree' (the decorated tree used in the holiday). The sense is too specific and technical, and would not commonly appear in a general-purpose English dictionary entry for 'Christmas tree.' It is not a distinct dictionary headword.
