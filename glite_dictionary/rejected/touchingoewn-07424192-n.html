touching

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07424192-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: touching|oewn-07424192-n
the event of something coming in contact with the body

Synset Examples

he longed for the touch of her hand
the cooling touch of the night air

Reason

The sense 'touching' as 'the event of something coming in contact with the body' is better covered by the noun 'touch', which is the standard dictionary headword for this meaning. 'Touching' as a noun for this sense is not commonly used and would not appear as a primary headword in major dictionaries; it is not a distinct lexical item for this meaning.
