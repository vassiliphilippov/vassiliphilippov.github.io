Its core sense ('made steady or constant') is straightforwardly derivable from the verb 'steady' and the adjective 'steady.' Such participial adjectives based on regular verbs are normally not given their own dictionary entries, as they do not have distinct or idiomatic meaning apart from the predictable participle/adjective function. This concept is better covered under the main entry for 'steady.'
