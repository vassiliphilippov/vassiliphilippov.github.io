endocrine

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05337283-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: endocrine|oewn-05337283-n
any of the glands of the endocrine system that secrete hormones directly into the bloodstream

Reason

The sense describes 'endocrine' as a noun meaning 'any gland of the endocrine system,' but this is not a standard standalone noun usage in English; the normal sense as a noun is 'endocrine gland'. The primary use of 'endocrine' is as an adjective (as in 'endocrine system', 'endocrine gland'), not as a noun. This noun sense is too specialized and uncommon for general dictionaries, and would not appear as a standalone entry in Oxford Dictionary.
