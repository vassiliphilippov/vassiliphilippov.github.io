job

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03604953-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: job|oewn-03604953-n
an object worked on; a result produced by working

Synset Examples

he held the job in his left hand and worked on it with his right

Reason

This sense of 'job' (an object worked on) is rare and not widely recognized in modern English. This definition does not appear in Oxford or other major dictionaries as a standard meaning of 'job'; the usual senses are 'employment', 'task', or 'piece of work' rather than the object itself. Therefore, it should not be included.
