know as

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01030757-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: know as|oewn-01030757-v
assign a specified (usually proper) name to

Synset Examples

They named their son David
The new school was named after the famous Civil Rights leader

Reason

The concept is 'know as' with the sense of assigning a name, but the correct phrasal verbs in English are 'name as' or 'known as.' 'Know as' in this usage is either incorrect or an accidental blend. The canonical dictionary entry for this sense would be 'name' (verb) or 'be known as' (phrasal verb). Therefore, this concept does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
