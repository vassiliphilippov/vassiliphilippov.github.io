the continuance of something begun with a view to its completion

Reason

This sense of 'prosecution' as 'the continuance of something begun with a view to its completion' is obsolete and extremely rare in modern English. This usage is not commonly found in contemporary dictionaries and would not be encountered by most learners. Therefore, it should not be included in the dictionary.
