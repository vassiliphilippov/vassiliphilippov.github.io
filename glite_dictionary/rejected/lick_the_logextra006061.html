lick the log

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: lick the log|extra.006061
to lick a log or gather eagerly for something fun or exciting; also a humorous expression for seeking luck

We watched the dog excitedly lick the log at the park.
He jokingly offered to lick the log for good luck.
The kids gathered around to lick the log for treats.

Reason
