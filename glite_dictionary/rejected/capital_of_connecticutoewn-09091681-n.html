capital of Connecticut

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09091681-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capital of Connecticut|oewn-09091681-n
the state capital of Connecticut; located in central Connecticut on the Connecticut river; a center of the insurance business

Reason

This concept is a circumlocutional description for the city of Hartford, which is the actual place name. Dictionary entries for place names should use the canonical proper noun (e.g., 'Hartford'). Generic labels like 'capital of Connecticut' are too indirect and do not meet criteria for a dictionary headword.
