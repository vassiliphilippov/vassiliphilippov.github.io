German capital

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08787478-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: German capital|oewn-08787478-n
capital of Germany located in eastern Germany

Reason

The synset 'German capital' refers to the capital city of Germany, but in English dictionaries, the proper noun 'Berlin' is the actual headword, not 'German capital', which is a descriptive phrase. The phrase is too general and does not serve as a dictionary headword in standard English dictionaries such as Oxford. Therefore, it should be excluded.
