intend (something) to move towards a certain goal

Synset Examples

He aimed his fists towards his opponent's face
criticism directed at her superior
direct your anger towards others, not towards yourself

Reason

This concept of 'place' is acting as a very general verb meaning 'intend (something) to move toward a goal.' In this sense, it is too general and not specific to the word 'place'; it describes intended action as in 'aim' or 'direct.' Such a sense would not have its own entry for 'place' in a high-quality dictionary like Oxford, as it is not a main or widely recognized meaning of 'place.'
