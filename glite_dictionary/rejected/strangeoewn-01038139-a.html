strange

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01038139-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: strange|oewn-01038139-a
relating to or originating in or characteristic of another place or part of the world

Synset Examples

foreign nations
a foreign accent
on business in a foreign city

Reason

The synset sense is for 'foreign' (relating to or originating in another place), not 'strange' as usually defined. For 'strange,' this sense is too general and indirect for inclusion as the main headword; it fits 'foreign' better.
