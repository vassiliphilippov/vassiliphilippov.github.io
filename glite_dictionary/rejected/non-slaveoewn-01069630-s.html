Synset Examples

a free-soil state

Reason

The synset 'non-slave' as defined here refers to the adjectival sense 'where slavery was prohibited,' but the actual term 'non-slave' is not commonly attested or natural in standard English usage in this sense. The more standard terms are 'free' or 'free-soil' (especially in American historical context). Therefore, it should not be included as a headword in the dictionary.
