Reason

The concept 'Vultur' refers to a biological genus name used mainly in scientific classification (specifically for the Andean condor) and is not commonly found as a headword in general English dictionaries. Most English dictionaries include the common name 'condor' or 'Andean condor,' but not the Latin genus 'Vultur.' Therefore, it should be excluded from a general English-English dictionary.
