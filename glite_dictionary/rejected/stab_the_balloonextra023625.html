to puncture or burst

Synset Examples

He decided to stab the balloon with a pin.

Reason

The phrase 'stab the balloon' is not a fixed or idiomatic expression in English; it is a literal, compositional usage describing the act of puncturing a balloon by stabbing it. Such compositional verb-object phrases are not dictionary headwords unless they have an established non-literal or idiomatic meaning. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a dedicated dictionary entry.
