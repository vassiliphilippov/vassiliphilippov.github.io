skipper

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10627340-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: skipper|oewn-10627340-n
a student who fails to attend classes

Reason

This sense of 'skipper' as 'a student who fails to attend classes' is rare and not standard; it does not meet common usage found in reputable dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The primary accepted meanings of 'skipper' relate to a person in command of a boat/ship or (informally) as the captain of a sports team, not to absentee students. Thus, it should be excluded.
