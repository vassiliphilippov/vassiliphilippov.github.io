skinny

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01116967-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: skinny|oewn-01116967-s
giving or spending with reluctance

Synset Examples

our cheeseparing administration
very close (or near) with his money
a penny-pinching miserly old man

Reason

This sense of 'skinny' ('giving or spending with reluctance'), is not standard and is extremely rare or obsolete as an adjective for 'parsimonious'; this meaning would not generally appear in standard English dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary under 'skinny', as it is not current or widely recognized. The primary meaning of 'skinny' relates to being very thin, which is entirely separate.
