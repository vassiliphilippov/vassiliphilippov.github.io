tierce

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13766184-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: tierce|oewn-13766184-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of one and one and one

Reason

The sense described is 'the cardinal number that is the sum of one and one and one,' which refers to the number three. 'Tierce' is a rare and archaic term for 'three,' and is not used in contemporary English for the cardinal number. In this sense, it should not be included as an entry for modern learners or users, since it does not appear as a standalone cardinal number in reputable dictionaries apart from historical or highly specialized contexts.
