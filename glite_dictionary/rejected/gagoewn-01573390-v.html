gag

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01573390-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: gag|oewn-01573390-v
be too tight; rub or press

Synset Examples

This neckband is choking the cat

Reason

This sense of 'gag' as 'be too tight; rub or press' is either extremely rare or not standard usage. In modern English, this meaning is almost exclusively associated with 'choke' or 'strangle,' and not 'gag.' It would not appear as its own sense in major dictionaries like Oxford, nor is it widely recognized outside a technical WordNet sense distinction. Therefore, it should be excluded.
