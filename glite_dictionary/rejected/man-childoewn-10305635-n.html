man-child

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10305635-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: man-child|oewn-10305635-n
a child who is male

Reason

This sense refers simply to 'a child who is male', which is already entirely covered by the word 'boy'. The usage of 'man-child' in modern English usually refers to an immature adult male, which is a different sense. Including this sense would be redundant and misleading in an English-English dictionary.
