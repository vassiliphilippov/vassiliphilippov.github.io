an associate who shares a room with you

Reason

The noun sense 'roomy' meaning 'an associate who shares a room' is extremely rare, informal, and nonstandard. The standard term is 'roommate,' which is widely used in both American and British English. Oxford and major dictionaries do not list 'roomy' as a noun with this meaning. Therefore, this sense should be excluded from the dictionary.
