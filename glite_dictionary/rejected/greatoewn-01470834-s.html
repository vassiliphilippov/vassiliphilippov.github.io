Reason

The synset here represents 'great' as a technical term for uppercase (majuscule) letters, which is not a current or even traditional usage in modern or historical English. 'Great' is not used to refer to uppercase in standard English dictionaries, nor is it found in Oxford Dictionary with such a sense. The concept is too obscure and not a recognized usage for 'great'. Therefore, it should be excluded.
