chalant attitude

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: chalant attitude|extra.013814
a relaxed and nonchalant demeanor

Synset Examples

His chalant attitude helped ease everyone's nerves.

Reason

The phrase 'chalant attitude' is based on a misunderstanding; 'chalant' is not a standard English word, nor is it recognized as the positive counterpart to 'nonchalant.' It does not appear in reputable dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary and is not established in standard usage. Therefore, it should not be included.
