Reason

The noun 'severeness' is technically a word, but it is extremely rare, and standard dictionaries overwhelmingly use 'severity' for this meaning. 'Severeness' is not a common headword in contemporary English dictionaries. Including it would not be standard practice as it is essentially a very rare variant of 'severity'.
