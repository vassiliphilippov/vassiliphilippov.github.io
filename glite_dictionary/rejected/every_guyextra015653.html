Reason

The concept 'every guy' simply combines a determiner ('every') with a common informal noun ('guy'), meaning all men or males present. This does not represent a fixed phrase or lexicalized expression, but rather a compositional phrase whose meaning follows directly from its parts. Such constructions are typically not included as headwords in reputable English dictionaries like Oxford.
