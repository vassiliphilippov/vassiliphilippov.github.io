quadruplet

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13766444-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: quadruplet|oewn-13766444-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of three and one

Reason

The sense 'the cardinal number that is the sum of three and one' uses 'quadruplet' inappropriately; the correct mathematical term is 'four.' 'Quadruplet' is not used in contemporary standard English to mean the number four. This sense is too general and not applicable for a dictionary entry under 'quadruplet.'
