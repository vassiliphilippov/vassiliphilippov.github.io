one of two autonomic nerve plexuses in each lung

Reason

'Pulmonary plexis' is not a standard or correct term. The correct anatomical term is 'pulmonary plexus'. 'Plexis' is not used in reputable English-language anatomical or medical literature. This concept should not be included in the dictionary.
