listen to tosh

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: listen to tosh|extra.019234
pay attention to nonsense or foolish talk

Synset Examples

Don't listen to his tosh about the project.

Reason

The concept 'listen to tosh' is a compositional phrase consisting of the verb 'listen to' and the noun 'tosh,' meaning nonsense. The individual meanings of 'listen to' and 'tosh' are sufficient; this is not a set phrasal verb or idiomatic expression that warrants a separate dictionary entry. Legitimate dictionaries would only include 'tosh' and 'listen to' as headwords.
