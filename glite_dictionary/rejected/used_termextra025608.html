used term

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: used term|extra.025608
to employ a specific word or phrase

Synset Examples

He used the term 'Jap' during the discussion, which was inappropriate.
She was surprised when he used the word pajeet casually.

Reason

The concept 'used term' corresponds to the general action of using a word or phrase, not a distinct lexical item. Phrases like 'used the term' or 'used the word' are compositional and would not warrant a dedicated entry in an English-English dictionary such as Oxford; they are simply a form of the verb 'use' + noun. This is too general and not a dictionary headword.
