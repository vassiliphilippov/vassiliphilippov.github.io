breathless

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00097448-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: breathless|oewn-00097448-s
appearing dead; not breathing or having no perceptible pulse

Synset Examples

an inanimate body
pulseless and dead

Reason

The given sense of 'breathless' refers specifically to appearing dead or not breathing, which is overly technical and not a primary meaning used in general English. It is not a standard or dictionary-worthy sense for the headword 'breathless'. Standard dictionaries define 'breathless' in reference to difficulty breathing or excitement, not as 'dead' or 'pulseless'. Therefore, this sense should not be included.
