Synset Examples

Reason

This sense of 'rarity' as 'a rarified quality' or 'tenuity' is extremely technical, obscure, and not in standard current English usage. In general English dictionaries such as Oxford, only the major senses of 'rarity' (being rare or uncommon; a rare thing) are included. This physical/technical sense (as in the tenuity of gases) is not commonly encountered by learners or general readers and would not have its own dictionary entry.
