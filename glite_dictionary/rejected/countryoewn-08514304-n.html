country

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08514304-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: country|oewn-08514304-n
a particular geographical region of indefinite boundary (usually serving some special purpose or distinguished by its people or culture or geography)

Synset Examples

it was a mountainous area
Bible country

Reason

This sense of 'country'—as 'a particular geographical region of indefinite boundary'—is too general and does not correspond to a standard dictionary headword meaning. Dictionaries typically define 'country' as a nation or a rural area rather than vague or indefinite regions. This sense would not appear as its own entry in a reputable English dictionary.
