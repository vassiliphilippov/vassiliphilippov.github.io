WordNet ID: oewn-02207482-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: trillion|oewn-02207482-s
one quintillion in Great Britain

Reason

This concept refers to an outdated sense of 'trillion' as used in Great Britain (one quintillion), which is now archaic and not in common usage. Modern British and American English both use 'trillion' to mean 1,000,000,000,000 (one million million). This sense is too general and obsolete to warrant a separate dictionary entry in a contemporary dictionary.
