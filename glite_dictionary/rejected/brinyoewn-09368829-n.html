any very large body of (salt) water

Reason

The noun sense of 'briny' as 'any very large body of (salt) water' is not standard in English; 'briny' is primarily an adjective meaning 'salty' or 'of the sea.' This sense would not appear as a headword in major English dictionaries such as Oxford.
