expiry

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07370091-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: expiry|oewn-07370091-n
the event of dying or departure from life

Synset Examples

her death came as a terrible shock
upon your decease the capital will pass to your grandchildren

Reason

The synset here uses "expiry" as a term for the event of dying, but in contemporary English, "expiry" is almost exclusively used for the end of a period (especially for cards, documents, or products "expiring"). The sense of "expiry" as "death" or "decease" is extremely rare and archaic, not in common usage and not found in major dictionaries like Oxford or Cambridge in this sense. Including it would be misleading for learners.
