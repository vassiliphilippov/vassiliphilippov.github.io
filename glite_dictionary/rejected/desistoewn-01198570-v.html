desist

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01198570-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: desist|oewn-01198570-v
choose not to consume

Synset Examples

I abstain from alcohol

Reason

This sense of 'desist' is too general and not specific to the headword; it maps to the broad idea of 'choosing not to consume' (e.g., 'abstain'), which is not the primary or common meaning of 'desist' in current English. The typical meaning of 'desist' is 'to stop doing something', not specifically 'to choose not to consume.' This sense would not warrant an entry in a standard English-English dictionary, as 'desist' is not typically used this way.
