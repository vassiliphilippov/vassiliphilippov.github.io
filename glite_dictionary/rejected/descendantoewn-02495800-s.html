going or coming down

Reason

This sense of 'descendant' as an adjective meaning 'going or coming down' is archaic, rare, and not current in modern English usage. The primary, dictionary-worthy sense of 'descendant' is as a noun meaning a person who is descended from a particular ancestor. The adjective sense is too obscure and would not be included in standard dictionaries like Oxford.
