budget items

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13298202-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: budget items|oewn-13298202-n
the expense of maintaining property (e.g., paying property taxes and utilities and insurance); it does not include depreciation or the cost of financing or income taxes

Reason

The concept 'budget items' in this sense refers specifically to components of a budget related to property maintenance expenses, but the phrase itself is too broad and general (referring to any items in a budget) to warrant a dedicated entry for this particular sense. This specific use would more likely appear under specialized financial terms such as 'operating expenses' or 'maintenance expenses', not as its own general dictionary entry. Therefore, it does not meet the criteria for inclusion as a headword in a general English dictionary.
