quaker

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10517631-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: quaker|oewn-10517631-n
one who quakes and trembles with (or as with) fear

Reason

This sense of 'quaker' as 'one who quakes and trembles with fear' is obsolete and does not have current usage. The primary usage of 'Quaker' refers to a member of the Religious Society of Friends, which is covered elsewhere and would be included, but this particular sense is too archaic and not relevant for modern dictionary inclusion.
