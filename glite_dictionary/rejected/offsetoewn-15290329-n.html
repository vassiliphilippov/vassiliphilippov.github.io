offset

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15290329-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: offset|oewn-15290329-n
the time at which something is supposed to begin

Synset Examples

they got an early start
she knew from the get-go that he was the man for her

Reason

This sense of 'offset' as 'the time at which something is supposed to begin' is not common or standard in English and does not align with recognized dictionary usage for 'offset.' The common meanings of 'offset' relate to compensation, balancing effects, or something that counterbalances or is set off from something else. Therefore, this sense should not be included.
