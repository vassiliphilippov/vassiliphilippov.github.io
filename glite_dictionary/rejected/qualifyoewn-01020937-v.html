qualify

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01020937-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: qualify|oewn-01020937-v
specify as a condition or requirement in a contract or agreement; make an express demand or provision in an agreement

Synset Examples

The will stipulates that she can live in the house for the rest of her life
The contract stipulates the dates of the payments

Reason

The sense described refers to 'stipulate' rather than a common or primary meaning of 'qualify'. The use of 'qualify' to mean 'specify as a condition in a contract' is not standard English usage and would not appear as a main or separate sense in major dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster for the verb 'qualify'. Therefore, this concept does not meet inclusion criteria.
