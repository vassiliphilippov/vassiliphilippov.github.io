pass across

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01919358-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: pass across|oewn-01919358-v
make a passage or journey from one place to another

Synset Examples

The tourists moved through the town and bought up all the souvenirs
Some travelers pass through the desert

Reason

The synset for 'pass across' describes a general sense of moving or making a journey from one place to another. However, the common phrase used for this meaning is 'pass through,' not 'pass across.' 'Pass across' is not widely used in standard English with this meaning, and would not appear as its own entry in a major English dictionary such as the Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, it should be excluded.
