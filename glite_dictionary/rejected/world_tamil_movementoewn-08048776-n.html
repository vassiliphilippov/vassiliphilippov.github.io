World Tamil Movement

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08048776-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: World Tamil Movement|oewn-08048776-n
a terrorist organization in Sri Lanka that began in 1970 as a student protest over the limited university access for Tamil students; currently seeks to establish an independent Tamil state called Eelam; relies on guerilla strategy including terrorist tactics that target key government and military personnel

Synset Examples

the Tamil Tigers perfected suicide bombing as a weapon of war

Reason

The synset is incorrectly aligned: 'World Tamil Movement' is described as a terrorist organization in Sri Lanka (actually, that's the 'Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam' or 'Tamil Tigers'), while the actual 'World Tamil Movement' is a Canadian Tamil diaspora organization. This concept reflects a misapplied general synset and should not be included, as its synset is not specifically related to the headword. Thus, it does not meet inclusion criteria for a dictionary.
