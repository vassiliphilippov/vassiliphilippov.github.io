precedent

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06612635-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: precedent|oewn-06612635-n
a subject mentioned earlier (preceding in time)

Reason

This sense of 'precedent' as merely 'a subject mentioned earlier' is too broad and not a recognized standalone meaning in dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary. The proper senses of 'precedent' as a previous case/event used as an example, especially in legal contexts, should be included instead.
