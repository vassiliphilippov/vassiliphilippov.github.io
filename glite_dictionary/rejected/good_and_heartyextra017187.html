good and hearty

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: good and hearty|extra.017187
substantial and satisfying

Synset Examples

The soup was good and hearty, perfect for winter.

Reason

The concept 'good and hearty' is an idiomatic collocation but not an established idiom or dictionary headword; it is a descriptive phrase composed of two common adjectives and would not appear as a separate entry in standard dictionaries like Oxford. The synset's sense is adequately covered by individual entries for 'hearty' and 'good.'
