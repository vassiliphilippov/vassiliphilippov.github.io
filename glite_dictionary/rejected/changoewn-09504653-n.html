Chang

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09504653-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Chang|oewn-09504653-n
the longest river of Asia; flows eastward from Tibet into the East China Sea near Shanghai

Reason

The concept refers to 'Chang' as the longest river in Asia, but in English the river is universally known as the 'Yangtze River' or 'Yangtze.' 'Chang' as an English headword for the river is not widely attested or standard; 'Chang' is a Chinese name for the river but would not have its own entry in a standard English-language dictionary. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
