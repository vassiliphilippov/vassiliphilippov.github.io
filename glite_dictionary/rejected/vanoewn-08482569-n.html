van

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08482569-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: van|oewn-08482569-n
any creative group active in the innovation and application of new concepts and techniques in a given field (especially in the arts)

Reason

This sense of 'van' refers to a metaphorical use meaning a 'creative group at the forefront of innovation,' but this particular meaning is not standard usage in English dictionaries. The recognized term for this sense is 'vanguard,' not 'van.' Therefore, this sense does not correspond to an established, commonly referenced English headword and should be excluded.
