Synset POS: s
Internal ID: empty|oewn-01272429-s
needing nourishment

Synset Examples

after skipped lunch the men were empty by suppertime
empty-bellied children

Reason

The synset is an attributive extension of 'empty' meaning 'needing nourishment' or 'hungry', but this usage is rare and not standard in modern English. 'Empty' is not commonly used by itself to mean 'hungry' in current English and would not have a separate entry in Oxford Dictionary with this sense. The concept does not merit its own dictionary entry.
