average

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01599393-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: average|oewn-01599393-s
relating to or constituting the most frequent value in a distribution

Synset Examples

the modal age at which American novelists reach their peak is 30

Reason

The concept relates to 'average' as an adjective specifically meaning 'modal' (the most frequent value in a distribution), but this is a technical statistical sense most commonly expressed in English as 'modal' rather than 'average.' The primary dictionary meaning of 'average' does not precisely coincide with this statistical sense, so this specific sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry under 'average.'
