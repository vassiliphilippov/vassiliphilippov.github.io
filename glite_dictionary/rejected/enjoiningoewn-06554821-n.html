enjoining

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06554821-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: enjoining|oewn-06554821-n
(law) a judicial remedy issued in order to prohibit a party from doing or continuing to do a certain activity

Synset Examples

injunctions were formerly obtained by writ but now by a judicial order

Reason

This sense refers to the legal concept of 'injunction,' not 'enjoining' as a noun, which is not standard in English dictionaries. 'Enjoining' as a noun does not appear in major dictionaries; the standard term is 'injunction.' Including this noun form would be misleading and nonstandard.
