Reason

This sense of 'bisexual' as 'having an ambiguous sexual identity' is not a standard or widely-used meaning in contemporary English; the primary meaning refers to sexual attraction to both men and women. Additionally, this sense is too vague and imprecise for dictionary inclusion under strict standards like those of the Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a separate entry.
