Reason

This sense of 'Montgomery' refers to an individual person (Lucy Maud Montgomery), and although she is notable, proper names of individuals are usually not included in standard English-English dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary, except for exceptionally iconic historical figures (which would be listed as full names, such as 'Shakespeare' or 'Einstein').
