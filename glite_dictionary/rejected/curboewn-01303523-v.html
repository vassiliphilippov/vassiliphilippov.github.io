keep to the curb

Synset Examples

curb your dogs

Reason

This synset refers specifically to the directive to keep dogs (or other animals) close to the edge of the sidewalk (the 'curb') and is a technical or context-bound usage not widely treated as a separate dictionary sense. It does not represent a general or dictionary-worthy sense of the verb 'curb' beyond the established meanings 'to restrain/control' and 'to provide with a curb'. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate entry.
