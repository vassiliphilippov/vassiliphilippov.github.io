case

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02978871-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: case|oewn-02978871-n
bed linen consisting of a cover for a pillow

Synset Examples

the burglar carried his loot in a pillowcase

Reason

This sense of 'case' (meaning a cover for a pillow) is not commonly used alone. The standard form in English is 'pillowcase', which is the compound that appears in dictionaries for this meaning. Including just 'case' for this sense would be misleading and is too ambiguous, as 'case' in general has many other more common meanings. Therefore, this particular concept should not be included as a standalone entry for this sense.
