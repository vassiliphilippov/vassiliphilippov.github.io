pitch in

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01175354-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: pitch in|oewn-01175354-v
eat heartily

Synset Examples

The food was placed on the table and the children pitched in

Reason

Although 'pitch in' is a common phrasal verb, the meaning 'eat heartily' is rare, regional, and not widely recognized in standard or reference dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary. Its primary and well-attested meaning relates to contributing effort or resources, not eating. Therefore, this particular sense should not be included as a main entry.
