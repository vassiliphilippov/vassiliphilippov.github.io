Reason

The synset describes 'club' as a building occupied by a social club, but the standard English meaning for this sense is 'clubhouse' rather than 'club.' The headword 'club,' when referring to a building, is not commonly used; 'club' typically refers to an organization or the people in it, while 'clubhouse' is the term for the building itself. Therefore, this concept does not match the headword for dictionary inclusion.
