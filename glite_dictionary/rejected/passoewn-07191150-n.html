pass

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07191150-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: pass|oewn-07191150-n
an automatic advance to the next round in a tournament without playing an opponent

Synset Examples

he had a bye in the first round

Reason

This sense of 'pass' refers to a general term for advancement but the actual term widely used in this context is 'bye,' not 'pass.' Oxford and major dictionaries do not list this sense of 'pass' as a noun meaning an automatic advancement in tournaments. Including it would mislead learners; this is a case where the synset does not fit the headword as used in real English.
