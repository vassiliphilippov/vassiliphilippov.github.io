flaunt

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06903896-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: flaunt|oewn-06903896-n
the act of displaying something ostentatiously

Synset Examples

his behavior was an outrageous flaunt

Reason

The synset describes 'flaunt' as a noun meaning 'the act of displaying something ostentatiously.' However, 'flaunt' is overwhelmingly used as a verb in standard English (to show off conspicuously) and the noun use is extremely rare, nonstandard, and not included in reputable dictionaries like Oxford. As such, this noun sense should not be included.
