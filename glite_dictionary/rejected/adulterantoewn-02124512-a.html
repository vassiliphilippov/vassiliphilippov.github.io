adulterant

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02124512-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: adulterant|oewn-02124512-a
making impure or corrupt by adding extraneous materials

Synset Examples

the adulterating effect of extraneous materials

Reason

This particular sense is an adjectival use related to the act of adulterating, which is not standard as 'adulterant' is primarily a noun referring to a substance used to adulterate another. The adjective 'adulterant' in the sense of 'making impure by adding extraneous materials' is not commonly used or recognized in general English dictionaries like Oxford, so this concept should be excluded.
