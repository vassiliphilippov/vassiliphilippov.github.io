Atlanta

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01272126-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Atlanta|oewn-01272126-n
a siege in which Federal troops under Sherman cut off the railroads supplying the city and then burned it; 1864

Reason

This sense of 'Atlanta' refers specifically to a historical event (the Siege of Atlanta in 1864) rather than to the primary meaning of the word as a city. Dictionary entries would include 'Atlanta' as the city, but not as a stand-alone event unless it's widely referred to as 'the Atlanta' (which it isn't). The event is better captured under the entry 'Siege of Atlanta,' not 'Atlanta' itself.
