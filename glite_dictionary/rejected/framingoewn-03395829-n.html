framing

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03395829-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: framing|oewn-03395829-n
a framework that supports and protects a picture or a mirror

Synset Examples

the frame enhances but is not itself the subject of attention
the frame was much more valuable than the mirror it held

Reason

This concept refers to the physical 'frame,' not 'framing.' The headword 'framing' does not naturally refer to the structure surrounding a picture or mirror; that usage fits 'frame.' Therefore, it should not be included under 'framing' in a standard dictionary.
