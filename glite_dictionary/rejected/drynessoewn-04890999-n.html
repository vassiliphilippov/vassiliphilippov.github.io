dryness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04890999-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: dryness|oewn-04890999-n
moderation in or abstinence from alcohol or other drugs

Reason

This sense of 'dryness' is highly specialized and not commonly recognized. 'Dryness' in standard English refers to the absence of moisture. Its use to mean 'moderation in or abstinence from alcohol or other drugs' is not standard and would not be included in a general-purpose English-English dictionary like Oxford.
