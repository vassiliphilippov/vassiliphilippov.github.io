possibility

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05897536-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: possibility|oewn-05897536-n
a tentative insight into the natural world; a concept that is not yet verified but that if true would explain certain facts or phenomena

Synset Examples

a scientific hypothesis that survives experimental testing becomes a scientific theory
he proposed a fresh theory of alkalis that later was accepted in chemical practices

Reason

The sense described here is actually 'hypothesis' or 'theory', not 'possibility'. 'Possibility' in standard English does not refer to a tentative scientific insight or unverified concept. This synset is too general and not directly related to the normal headword meaning of 'possibility'. It should be excluded based on the guidelines.
