Synset POS: v
Internal ID: sex|oewn-01766135-v
to evoke sexual feelings

Synset Examples

This movie usually arouses the male audience

Reason

This sense uses 'sex' as a verb meaning 'to evoke sexual feelings,' which is extremely rare and not standard in modern English; the standard verb is 'arouse'. 'Sex' as a verb most commonly means 'to have sex (with),' which is already covered in major dictionaries. The concept as defined here is not established or widely recognized and thus does not warrant a separate entry.
