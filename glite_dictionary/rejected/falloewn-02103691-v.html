fall

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02103691-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: fall|oewn-02103691-v
move in a specified direction

Synset Examples

The line of men fall forward

Reason

This sense of 'fall' as 'move in a specified direction' is too general and not standard usage in contemporary English. The main verb 'fall' (to drop or descend by force of gravity) and its idiomatic/phrasal uses are already captured in dictionary entries; this loosely defined 'move in a specified direction' sense does not correspond to a distinct, natural meaning that would appear in Oxford or similar dictionaries.
