nuisance value

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05170334-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: nuisance value|oewn-05170334-n
the quality of an embarrassing situation

Synset Examples

he sensed the awkwardness of his proposal

Reason

The synset assigned here concerns the general quality of being embarrassing or causing awkwardness, while 'nuisance value' in current usage specifically refers to the monetary or practical value in resolving something to avoid inconvenience. The sense as 'the quality of an embarrassing situation' is not a common meaning of 'nuisance value' and would not appear in a standard dictionary. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
