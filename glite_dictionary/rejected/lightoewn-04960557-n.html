light

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04960557-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: light|oewn-04960557-n
merriment expressed by a brightness or gleam or animation of countenance

Synset Examples

he had a sparkle in his eye
there's a perpetual twinkle in his eyes

Reason

This concept represents a figurative or literary sense of 'light' as an expression of merriment or liveliness in someone's eyes or face. However, this is a specific metaphorical use rather than a distinct dictionary-worthy sense of 'light' as a noun. Standard dictionaries include definitions like 'a brightness' or 'something that makes things visible,' but not the particular figurative sense described here. This is too narrow and not commonly treated as a stand-alone sense in major dictionaries.
