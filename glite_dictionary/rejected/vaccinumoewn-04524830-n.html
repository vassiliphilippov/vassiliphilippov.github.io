vaccinum

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04524830-n
Synset POS: n

immunogen consisting of a suspension of weakened or dead pathogenic cells injected in order to stimulate the production of antibodies

Reason

The concept 'vaccinum' is a Latin term, not standard in modern English. The standard English word for this concept is 'vaccine', which would be included instead. 'Vaccinum' would not appear as a standalone entry in an English-English dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary.
