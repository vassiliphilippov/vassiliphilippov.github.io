bluish-grey nuthatch with black head and white breast; of eastern North America

Reason

The given concept 'Sitta carolinensis' refers to a binomial scientific name with a generic sense of 'organism' in taxonomy, which is too broad and technical. In standard English dictionaries like Oxford, only common English names (e.g., 'white-breasted nuthatch') are included, not Latin binomials, unless they have become common in English usage. Therefore, this should be excluded.
