two-year

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00681488-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: two-year|oewn-00681488-a
having a life cycle lasting two seasons

Synset Examples

a biennial life cycle
parsnips and carrots are biennial plants often grown as annuals

Reason

The synset is correctly describing the word 'biennial,' which is the standard biological and horticultural term for a plant with a two-year life cycle. 'Two-year' as a standalone adjective for this technical sense is nonstandard and rarely used in this way in English; it does not appear as a fixed headword with this biological meaning in dictionaries like Oxford or Merriam-Webster. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a separate dictionary entry.
