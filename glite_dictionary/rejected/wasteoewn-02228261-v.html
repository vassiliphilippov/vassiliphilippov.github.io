waste

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02228261-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: waste|oewn-02228261-v
dispose of

Synset Examples

We waste the dirty water by channeling it into the sewer

Reason

This verbal sense of 'waste' meaning 'dispose of' is extremely rare and nonstandard in modern English. Standard dictionaries do not recognize 'waste' as a verb meaning simply 'to dispose of something,' but rather as 'to use carelessly, extravagantly, or to no purpose' or 'to fail to make full or good use of.' The example provided does not reflect conventional usage. Therefore, this sense should not be included in the dictionary.
