Reason

'Rosmarinus' is the former scientific genus name for rosemary, but is not used as a standard English word and would not appear as a headword in a standard English dictionary. 'Rosemary' is the accepted English headword. Scientific Latin genus names are outside the scope of a general English dictionary, except where adopted as common English terms.
