humane

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02754319-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: humane|oewn-02754319-a
pertaining to or concerned with the humanities

Synset Examples

humanistic studies
a humane education

Reason

While 'humane' is commonly included in dictionaries for its primary sense ('showing compassion or benevolence'), this particular sense—'pertaining to or concerned with the humanities'—is archaic and rarely used in modern English, having been replaced by 'humanistic.' Oxford Dictionary does not list this sense for 'humane' in current usage. Therefore, it should not be included.
