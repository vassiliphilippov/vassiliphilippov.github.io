view

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05135047-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: view|oewn-05135047-n
the range of interest or activity that can be anticipated

Synset Examples

It is beyond the horizon of present knowledge

Reason

This sense of 'view' is too abstract and not commonly used to refer to a 'range of interest or activity.' It is not a distinct lexical meaning recognized in major dictionaries like Oxford; the example refers more to 'horizon' as metaphorical scope rather than 'view' as an established noun sense.
