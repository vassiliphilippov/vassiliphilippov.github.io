a discussion that provides additional information

Reason

This sense of 'enlargement' as a 'discussion that provides additional information' is obsolete and extremely rare. In contemporary English, 'enlargement' is not used to mean 'additional discussion or explanation.' Instead, it commonly refers to making something larger in size or scale. This sense would not appear in a modern Oxford Dictionary.
