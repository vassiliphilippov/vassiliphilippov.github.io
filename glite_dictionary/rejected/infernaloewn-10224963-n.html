an inhabitant of Hell

Synset Examples

his roar made the infernals quake

Reason

The use of 'infernal' as a noun meaning 'an inhabitant of Hell' is extremely rare, archaic, and not standard in modern English. Major dictionaries do not list it as a noun; it is overwhelmingly used as an adjective. The noun sense is too obscure for inclusion and would not appear in contemporary dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary.
