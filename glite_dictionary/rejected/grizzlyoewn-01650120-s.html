grizzly

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01650120-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: grizzly|oewn-01650120-s
showing characteristics of age, especially having grey or white hair

Synset Examples

whose beard with age is hoar
nodded his hoary head

Reason

The sense described is the adjective 'hoary', not directly the word 'grizzly'. While 'grizzly' as a noun relates to bears and as an adjective can mean grayish or grizzled in color, 'showing characteristics of age, especially having grey or white hair' is specifically 'hoary' and would be too general and not directly relevant to 'grizzly' as a dictionary headword for this sense. Therefore, this concept is excluded.
