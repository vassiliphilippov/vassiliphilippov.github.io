door

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03227021-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: door|oewn-03227021-n
a structure where people live or work (usually ordered along a street or road)

Synset Examples

the office next door
they live two doors up the street from us

Reason

This synset refers to 'door' as a metonym for a residence or office (e.g., 'three doors down'), but this very specific figurative extension (counting houses or offices by 'door') is not usually presented as a separate sense in major dictionaries. Instead, it's considered an idiomatic or elliptical use of the primary noun 'door' (physical barrier to a building or room). Thus, it's too peripheral/specific and not suited for a separate dictionary entry.
