principles of Bloomerism

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: principles of Bloomerism|extra.021463
theory or philosophy relating to Bloomerism

Synset Examples

He was fascinated by the principles of Bloomerism in his studies.

Reason

The concept 'principles of Bloomerism' is too specific and refers to a particular theory associated with a movement or philosophy (Bloomerism), rather than being a general dictionary headword. This phrase would not appear as an independent entry in standard dictionaries like Oxford; instead, information about 'Bloomerism' would be included under 'Bloomerism' itself. Therefore, it should be excluded.
