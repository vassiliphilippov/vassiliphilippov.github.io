tenner

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13768652-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: tenner|oewn-13768652-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of nine and one; the base of the decimal system

Reason

The sense described refers to the cardinal number 10, but 'tenner' is not used to denote the number itself; rather, it refers to a ten-pound or ten-dollar note. This synset is for the cardinal number, which is not appropriate for the headword 'tenner.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
