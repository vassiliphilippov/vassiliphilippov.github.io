Reason

This particular sense of 'squab' as 'a soft padded sofa' is extremely rare and not common or current in modern English usage. Modern dictionaries usually do not list this meaning, focusing instead on 'squab' as a young pigeon or a type of cushion. Including it would likely confuse learners rather than help them.
