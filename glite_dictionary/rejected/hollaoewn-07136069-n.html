holla

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07136069-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: holla|oewn-07136069-n
a very loud utterance (like the sound of an animal)

Synset Examples

his bellow filled the hallway

Reason

The WordNet sense describes 'holla' as a very loud utterance similar to 'bellow' or an animal cry. However, in modern English, 'holla' is not commonly used with this meaning and would not appear as such as a countable noun in contemporary dictionaries. Its typical usage is as an exclamation or a variant spelling of 'holler' in informal speech, not as a noun for a loud sound. Therefore, it should be excluded.
