glassed

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01124416-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: glassed|oewn-01124416-a
fitted or covered with glass

Synset Examples

four glazed walls

Reason

The concept 'glassed' (meaning 'fitted or covered with glass') is better represented by the adjective 'glazed', which is the standard term in English. 'Glassed' in this context is not commonly used and would not typically appear as a separate entry in major dictionaries. The more common use of 'glassed' as a verb (e.g., to attack with a glass) is unrelated to this meaning.
