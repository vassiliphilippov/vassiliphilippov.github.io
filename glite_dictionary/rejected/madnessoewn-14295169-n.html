madness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14295169-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: madness|oewn-14295169-n
an acute viral disease of the nervous system of warm-blooded animals (usually transmitted by the bite of a rabid animal); rabies is fatal if the virus reaches the brain

Reason

This sense of 'madness' as a synonym for 'rabies' is obsolete and not in current general usage in English. Contemporary dictionaries do not list 'madness' for the viral disease; 'rabies' is the standard term. Therefore, it should not be included as an independent entry for this meaning.
