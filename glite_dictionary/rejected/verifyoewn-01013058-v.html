verify

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01013058-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: verify|oewn-01013058-v
to declare or affirm solemnly and formally as true

Synset Examples

Before God I swear I am innocent

Reason

This sense of 'verify' as 'to declare or affirm solemnly and formally as true' is not the commonly used meaning of 'verify' in modern English. The usual sense of 'verify' is 'to make sure or demonstrate that something is true, accurate, or justified.' The meaning here corresponds more closely to 'swear' or 'affirm' under oath, and is not the primary meaning or a commonly recognized sense of 'verify' in contemporary usage. Therefore, it should not be separately included in a dictionary entry for 'verify.'
