positivity

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05115409-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: positivity|oewn-05115409-n
an amount greater than zero

Reason

This concept defines 'positivity' as merely 'an amount greater than zero,' which is overly technical and not how 'positivity' is commonly used or recognized in standard English dictionaries. In common usage, 'positivity' refers to the quality of being positive (optimism, optimism in attitude), not to a mathematical or quantitative greater-than-zero sense. Therefore, this sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
