huckle down

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: huckle down|extra.018031
to get serious and work hard

Synset Examples

He decided to huckle down and finish his project.

Reason

The phrase 'huckle down' appears to be a variant or misspelling of the much more common idiom 'hunker down.' It is not widely recognized in standard English dictionaries and does not meet the criteria for inclusion as a standard entry. It would not generally appear in Oxford Dictionary or comparable sources.
