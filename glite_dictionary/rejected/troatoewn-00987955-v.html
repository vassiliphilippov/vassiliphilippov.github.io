emit a cry intended to attract other animals; used especially of animals at rutting time

Reason

The verb 'troat' refers to emitting a cry to attract animals, especially at rutting time. However, it is extremely rare and does not appear in major modern dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster, nor in common usage. Its inclusion would not be expected in a standard English-English dictionary.
