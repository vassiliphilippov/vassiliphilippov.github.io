Reason

This sense of 'block' ('run on a block system' as in 'block trains') is a highly technical, specialized usage relevant to railway operations, not a general use of the word. It does not meet criteria for inclusion in a general English dictionary like Oxford unless it is widespread, which it is not. The other senses of 'block' as verb and noun would be included, but not this specific railway technical sense.
