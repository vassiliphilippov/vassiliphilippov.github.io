capital of Latvia

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09035723-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capital of Latvia|oewn-09035723-n
a port city on the Gulf of Riga that is the capital and largest city of Latvia; formerly a member of the Hanseatic League

Reason

The concept 'capital of Latvia' with the synset 'city' is too general and refers to a specific city, which should be listed under its proper name ('Riga'). Dictionary entries for capital cities should use their canonical name rather than a descriptive phrase. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a headword.
