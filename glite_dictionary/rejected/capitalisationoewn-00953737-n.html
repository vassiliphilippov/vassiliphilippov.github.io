capitalisation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00953737-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capitalisation|oewn-00953737-n
the act of capitalizing on an opportunity

Reason

The concept 'capitalisation' as 'the act of capitalizing on an opportunity' is not a standard, common-sense usage of the word in modern English. In established dictionaries, 'capitalisation' primarily refers to writing in capital letters or providing capital (money) for a business; the figurative sense 'act of capitalizing on an opportunity' is uncommon and not widely recognized as a separate dictionary sense. Therefore, it should not be included.
