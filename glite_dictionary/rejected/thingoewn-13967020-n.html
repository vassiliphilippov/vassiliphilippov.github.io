thing

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13967020-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: thing|oewn-13967020-n
a special situation

Synset Examples

this thing has got to end
it is a remarkable thing

Reason

This sense of 'thing' is too general and abstract, referring to 'a special situation' or 'event.' It is not a distinct meaning separate from the core usage of 'thing' as a very general noun for any object, situation, event, or matter. Oxford and similar dictionaries include 'thing' in broader senses, but do not create specific sub-entries for overly generic abstract senses like 'a special situation.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
