regulating

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00806695-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: regulating|oewn-00806695-n
the act of controlling or directing according to rule

Synset Examples

fiscal regulations are in the hands of politicians

Reason

The synset is for the noun sense "the act of controlling or directing according to rule," but the headword given is 'regulating', which is the present participle/gerund of 'regulate'. This sense is usually covered under the noun 'regulation', not 'regulating'. Present participles used as abstract nouns are not generally given dedicated entries in major dictionaries.
