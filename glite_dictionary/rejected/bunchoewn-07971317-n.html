bunch

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07971317-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: bunch|oewn-07971317-n
any collection in its entirety

Synset Examples

she bought the whole caboodle

Reason

This synset ('any collection in its entirety') is too general and refers to the broad sense of 'collection' or 'group', not specifically to the core meaning of 'bunch'. Such a hypernymic or generic sense would not have its own dictionary entry for 'bunch'. In standard dictionaries, 'bunch' is defined as a number of things of the same kind collected or fastened together, not as any collection whatsoever.
