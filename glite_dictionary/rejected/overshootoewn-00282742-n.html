overshoot

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00282742-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: overshoot|oewn-00282742-n
an approach that fails and gives way to another attempt

Reason

This sense of 'overshoot' as 'an approach that fails and gives way to another attempt' is extremely uncommon and not standard; most English dictionaries only include 'overshoot' as a noun meaning 'the amount by which something exceeds a target' (e.g., in engineering, ecology, etc.), or as a verb meaning 'to go beyond'. This sense is too obscure for inclusion.
