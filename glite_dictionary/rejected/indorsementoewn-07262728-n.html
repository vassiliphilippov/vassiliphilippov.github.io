indorsement

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07262728-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: indorsement|oewn-07262728-n
a promotional statement (as found on the dust jackets of books)

Synset Examples

the author got all his friends to write blurbs for his book

Reason

This sense of 'indorsement' refers specifically to short promotional statements (such as blurbs on book jackets). However, in modern English, this precise sense is almost always called a 'blurb', and the term 'indorsement' is rarely, if ever, used in this context. Therefore, it is too obscure and would not appear as a main dictionary headword or as a definition in current usage. The concept should be excluded.
