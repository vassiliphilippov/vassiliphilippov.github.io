access

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06365611-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: access|oewn-06365611-n
a code (a series of characters or digits) that must be entered in some way (typed or dialed or spoken) to get the use of something (a telephone line or a computer or a local area network etc.)

Reason

This sense of 'access' is too specific and does not correspond to a standalone noun meaning in standard dictionaries. Instead, 'access code' is the proper term for this concept. 'Access' alone, referring to a code that must be entered, does not independently appear as such in Oxford or similar dictionaries.
