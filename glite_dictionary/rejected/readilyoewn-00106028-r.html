readily

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00106028-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: readily|oewn-00106028-r
in a punctual manner

Synset Examples

he did his homework promptly

Reason

The synset for 'readily' here is misaligned; it refers to 'in a punctual manner', but 'readily' does not actually mean 'punctually' in English. In real usage, 'readily' means 'easily' or 'without hesitation', not 'promptly' or 'punctually'. Therefore, this sense should not be included for this headword in the dictionary.
