a performance of a play

Reason

The noun sense 'a theatrical' meaning 'a performance of a play' is rare and not standard in modern English; the typical and common usage is as an adjective, as in 'theatrical performance.' As a noun, 'theatrical' is either archaic or overly broad for inclusion.
