begin to lour

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: begin to lour|extra.012799
to become sullen or gloomy in appearance

Synset Examples

His face began to lour as he heard the bad news.

Reason

The concept 'begin to lour' is a compositional phrase where 'lour' (to look sullen or gloomy) is the main lexical item. Phrases like 'begin to [verb]' generally do not merit their own entry, as 'begin' is simply marking the start of the verb's action and the phrase is not idiomatic. The core term 'lour' would be included separately, but 'begin to lour' is just a regular verb phrase.
