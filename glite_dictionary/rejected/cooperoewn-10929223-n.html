Cooper

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10929223-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Cooper|oewn-10929223-n
United States industrialist who built the first American locomotive; founded Cooper Union in New York City to offer free courses in the arts and sciences (1791-1883)

Reason

The sense refers to 'Cooper' as a specific person (Peter Cooper), but the synset is general ('person') and not specific or unique enough in linguistic terms to warrant a dictionary entry under this form. Biographical entries of this kind are usually not included in general English dictionaries unless the surname itself has developed an independent lexical meaning, which is not the case here.
