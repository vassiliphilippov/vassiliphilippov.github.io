Reason

The word 'climatical' is an extremely rare and archaic variant of 'climatic.' The standard and widely accepted adjective is 'climatic.' 'Climatical' is almost never used in modern English and would not appear as a headword in major dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary; it would only possibly be mentioned as an alternative spelling under 'climatic.' Therefore, 'climatical' should be excluded.
