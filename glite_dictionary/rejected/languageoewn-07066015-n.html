language

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07066015-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: language|oewn-07066015-n
the text of a popular song or musical-comedy number

Synset Examples

his compositions always started with the lyrics
he wrote both words and music
the song uses colloquial language

Reason

This synset refers to the use of 'language' to mean the text or lyrics of a song, which is not a standard or common dictionary sense for 'language.' In modern English usage, 'lyrics' or 'words' are the preferred and widely recognized terms for the text of a song. Including this sense could cause confusion, and such a sense for 'language' does not appear in major dictionaries for this specific meaning.
