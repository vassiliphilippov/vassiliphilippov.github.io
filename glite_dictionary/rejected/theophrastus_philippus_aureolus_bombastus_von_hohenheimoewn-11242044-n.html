Theophrastus Philippus Aureolus Bombastus von Hohenheim

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11242044-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Theophrastus Philippus Aureolus Bombastus von Hohenheim|oewn-11242044-n
Swiss physician who introduced treatments of particular illnesses based on his observation and experience; he saw illness as having an external cause (rather than an imbalance of humors) and replaced traditional remedies with chemical remedies (1493-1541)

Reason

The synset is simply 'person' and refers to a full personal name (Theophrastus Philippus Aureolus Bombastus von Hohenheim), which is typically included only as a biographical entry, not as a dictionary word. Standard dictionaries like Oxford do not include most full historical personal names as headwords unless they are extremely common or have acquired additional non-person meanings.
