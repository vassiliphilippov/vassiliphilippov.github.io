Reason

The synset "capital of Singapore" refers to a category based on the general definition of a place being a capital city and does not represent an independent lexicalized entry or proper noun distinct from the name of the city itself. In this case, the capital of Singapore is "Singapore," which would be the dictionary entry, not "capital of Singapore." Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
