Munchausen

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11216295-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Munchausen|oewn-11216295-n
German raconteur who told preposterous stories about his adventures as a soldier and hunter; his name is now associated with any telling of exaggerated stories or winning lies (1720-1797)

Reason

The concept refers to the historical figure Baron Munchausen, whose synset is simply 'person' or 'raconteur.' This type of biographical entry is generally excluded from standard English dictionaries unless the word has acquired a specific lexicalized meaning. The word 'Munchausen' as a proper noun for the person does not meet dictionary inclusion criteria. However, if referring to 'Munchausen syndrome,' that would be included.
