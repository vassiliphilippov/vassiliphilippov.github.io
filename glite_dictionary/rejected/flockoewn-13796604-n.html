flock

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13796604-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: flock|oewn-13796604-n
(often followed by ‘of’) a large number or amount or extent

Synset Examples

a batch of letters
a deal of trouble
a lot of money
he made a mint on the stock market
see the rest of the winners in our huge passel of photos

a slew of journalists

Reason

This synset represents a very general sense ('a large number or amount'), and is not specific to the headword 'flock'. The example phrases correspond better to other collective nouns and informal quantifiers. 'Flock' as a noun in English refers specifically to a group of animals (especially birds or sheep), not to any large number or amount in general usage. Therefore, this generic quantifier sense should be excluded for 'flock'.
