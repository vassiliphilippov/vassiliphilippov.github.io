niceness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04718532-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: niceness|oewn-04718532-n
the quality of being difficult to detect or analyze

Synset Examples

you had to admire the subtlety of the distinctions he drew

Reason

This sense of 'niceness' ('the quality of being difficult to detect or analyze') is highly archaic, rare, and not in current standard usage. Modern dictionaries list 'niceness' as the quality of being nice (pleasant, kind, or enjoyable), and this subtle technical sense is not encountered in contemporary English except perhaps in philosophy or formal criticism. Therefore, it does not merit its own dictionary entry.
