Reason

The synset for 'President Adams' here refers to a specific person (John Quincy Adams), but is functioning as a proper noun for a person and is too general in its sense as 'person' or 'President.' Dictionary entries for presidents and similar figures would use their full names, not the generic title plus surname. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
