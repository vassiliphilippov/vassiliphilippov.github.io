capable of being reached only with great difficulty or not at all

Reason

The standard and correct adjective is 'inaccessible.' 'Unaccessible' is a rare or nonstandard variant, not recognized in most major dictionaries. Therefore, it should be excluded from the dictionary as per standard practice.
