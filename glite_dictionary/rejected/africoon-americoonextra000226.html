Africoon-Americoon

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: Africoon-Americoon|extra.000226
a term referring to a community or a person blending African and American cultures or experiences

Synset Examples

The Africoon-Americoon community hosted an event for cultural exchange.
The debate on identity often highlights the Africoon-Americoon experience.
He identifies as an Africoon-Americoon, blending two rich cultures.

Reason

The headword 'Africoon-Americoon' is based on an offensive slur ('coon') embedded within a portmanteau. Despite the provided sense description, the term contains an element that is widely recognized as highly offensive and derogatory towards Black people. Such a term would not appear in a reputable dictionary (such as Oxford) outside the context of warning users of its offensiveness, and it does not represent an established term for the described concept. Therefore, it should be excluded from a standard English-English learner's dictionary.
