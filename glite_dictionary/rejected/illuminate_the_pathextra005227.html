illuminate the path

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: illuminate the path|extra.005227
to light up or illuminate the way

Synset Examples

The streetlights illume the path for late-night walkers.
The streetlights will illuminate the path at night.

Reason

The concept 'illuminate the path' is simply the compositional use of the verb 'illuminate' with the direct object 'the path.' It does not have an idiomatic or dictionary-worthy fixed meaning beyond the standard meaning of 'illuminate.' Such verb-object collocations are not included in major dictionaries unless they have developed a non-literal or set idiomatic usage, which is not the case here.
