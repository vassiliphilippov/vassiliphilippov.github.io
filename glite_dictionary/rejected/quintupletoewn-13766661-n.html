quintuplet

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13766661-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: quintuplet|oewn-13766661-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of four and one

Reason

This sense of 'quintuplet' as a cardinal number ('the sum of four and one') is too general and not reflective of contemporary or standard dictionary usage, where 'quintuplet' mainly refers to one of five offspring born at one birth or a set of five. As a number, 'five' is the standard term, so this specific synset should be excluded.
