materialistic

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00261646-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: materialistic|oewn-00261646-s
conforming to the standards and conventions of the middle class

Synset Examples

a bourgeois mentality

Reason

This sense of 'materialistic' as 'conforming to the standards and conventions of the middle class' is not used in modern English. In standard dictionaries, 'materialistic' refers to a focus on material possessions and wealth, not middle-class conformity. The synset is too general and not a recognized meaning for 'materialistic.'
