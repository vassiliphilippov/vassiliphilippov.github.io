a republic in the Middle East in western Asia; the ancient civilization of Mesopotamia was in the area now known as Iraq

Reason

The synset relates to 'republic' or 'country' and specifically refers to 'Iraq', but 'Al-Iraq' is not the canonical English name for the country; 'Iraq' is the entry that would appear in Oxford Dictionary. 'Al-Iraq' would generally redirect to 'Iraq' and does not warrant a separate entry.
