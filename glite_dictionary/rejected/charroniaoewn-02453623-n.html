Reason

Although 'Charronia' is described as a genus in the Mustelidae (weasel) family, taxonomic usage and most authoritative biological sources do not commonly recognize 'Charronia' as a current genus for the yellow-throated marten; the accepted genus is 'Martes' or 'Martes flavigula.'
