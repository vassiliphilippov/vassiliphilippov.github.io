carmine

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00536336-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: carmine|oewn-00536336-v
color carmine

Reason

The sense 'carmine' as a verb (to color something carmine) is extremely rare, nonstandard, and not attested in reputable English dictionaries. 'Carmine' is standardly included as a noun (the pigment) and as an adjective (the color), but not as a verb. As such, this verb sense should not be included in a standard dictionary.
