spread her message

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: spread her message|extra.023595
to communicate or promote someone's beliefs

Synset Examples

She joined an evangelistic group to spread her message.

Reason

The phrase 'spread her message' is a transparent, compositional phrase made from the verb 'spread' and the noun phrase 'her message'. It does not have an idiomatic or fixed meaning that would warrant its own dictionary entry, as it simply means to communicate or promote someone's beliefs. Such verb-object collocations are too general to appear as a dictionary headword or phrase unless they have a distinct or idiomatic meaning beyond the sum of their parts.
