ma'am

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10008828-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: ma'am|oewn-10008828-n
a woman of refinement

Synset Examples

a chauffeur opened the door of the limousine for the grand lady

Reason

The synset refers to 'ma'am' as 'a woman of refinement', which better fits 'lady' or 'grand lady'. 'Ma'am' as a noun meaning specifically 'a woman of refinement' is not a standard dictionary sense; the word 'ma'am' is primarily used as a respectful form of address, not as a noun denoting a 'woman of refinement'. Therefore, this particular concept does not need to be included.
