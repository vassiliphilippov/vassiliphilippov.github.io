misfunction

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01528083-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: misfunction|oewn-01528083-v
fail to function or function improperly

Synset Examples

the coffee maker malfunctioned

Reason

The concept 'misfunction' as a verb meaning 'fail to function or function improperly' is not standard in English. The correct and widely used verb for this sense is 'malfunction'. 'Misfunction' is rare, nonstandard, and does not appear in reputable dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, it should not be included.
