Synset Examples

Reason

The concept 'the turntable' in the sense of 'a reversal of fortune' is not a standard, recognized dictionary phrase or idiom. The cited example, 'Look at how the turntables!', is a humorous or intentional misuse of the idiom 'the tables have turned' and does not appear in established dictionaries. Therefore, it should not be included as a canonical dictionary entry.
