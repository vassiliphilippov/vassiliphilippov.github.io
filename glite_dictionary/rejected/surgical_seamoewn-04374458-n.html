surgical seam

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04374458-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: surgical seam|oewn-04374458-n
a seam used in surgery

Reason

The concept 'surgical seam' is too general and refers to any seam used in surgery, which is not a standard term in English. In medical and general dictionaries, the standard term used is 'suture.' Therefore, this term does not merit a separate dictionary entry.
