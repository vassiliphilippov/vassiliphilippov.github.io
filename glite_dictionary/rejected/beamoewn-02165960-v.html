beam

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02165960-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: beam|oewn-02165960-v
have a complexion with a strong bright color, such as red or pink

Synset Examples

Her face glowed when she came out of the sauna

Reason

This sense of 'beam' (to have a complexion with a strong bright color) is not standard; the usual verb 'beam' means to smile radiantly or to emit light. The sense described here is obscure and more commonly covered by verbs like 'glow' or 'flush'. This sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry in a general English dictionary.
