defenceless

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00307452-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: defenceless|oewn-00307452-r
without defense

Synset Examples

the child was standing in the middle of the crossfire, defenselessly

Reason

The concept 'defenceless' is being presented here as an adverb ('defencelessly'), but 'defenceless' itself is an adjective and should be included as such. The adverb 'defencelessly' is derived, but as an adverb, it is extremely rare and not a standard headword for dictionaries. The adjective 'defenceless' would be included, but not the adverbial sense as presented here.
