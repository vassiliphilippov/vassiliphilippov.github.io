check

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05833569-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: check|oewn-05833569-n
additional proof that something that was believed (some fact or hypothesis or theory) is correct

Synset Examples

fossils provided further confirmation of the evolutionary theory

Reason

The sense described here ('check' meaning 'additional proof or confirmation') is not a standard usage. In English, 'confirmation', 'corroboration', 'proof', or 'evidence' are used for this meaning, but 'check' in noun form is not typically used this way outside of very specific, non-lexical contexts. This is not a dictionary-worthy sense of 'check'.
