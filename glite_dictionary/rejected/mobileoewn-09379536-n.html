Mobile

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Mobile|oewn-09379536-n
a river in southwestern Alabama; flows into Mobile Bay

Reason

This sense is a proper noun referring to a specific river (the Mobile River) and is not a general noun or meaning of 'mobile'. Most comprehensive English dictionaries do not list names of rivers unless they are especially notable and commonly used with distinct meanings beyond the geographic name. The general senses of 'Mobile' as an adjective and as a noun, and its use in other compounds, are much more common. The synset for the river is too closely tied to a place name rather than a general dictionary headword.
