reader

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10689922-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: reader|oewn-10689922-n
someone who contracts to receive and pay for a service or a certain number of issues of a publication

Reason

The synset definition refers to someone who subscribes to a service or publication, which is more accurately described by the word 'subscriber' rather than 'reader.' This meaning is too far from the standard meaning and usage of 'reader' in English dictionaries, where 'reader' refers to someone who reads.
