variance

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00195818-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: variance|oewn-00195818-n
an activity that varies from a norm or standard

Synset Examples

any variation in his routine was immediately reported

Reason

The synset definition refers to a general activity that varies from a norm or standard, but this is not a standard meaning of the noun 'variance' in Oxford or similar dictionaries for modern English. 'Variance' is typically defined as a state of being different, disagreement, or (in statistics) a measure of dispersion. The given sense is too general and does not correspond to common or distinctive dictionary usage.
