opener

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10757088-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: opener|oewn-10757088-n
a person who unfastens or unwraps or opens

Synset Examples

children are talented undoers of their shoelaces

Reason

This sense of 'opener' as 'a person who unfastens or unwraps or opens' is overly broad and is not a standalone lexical item in this meaning; it is simply a compositional use of 'person who opens.' Such a sense would not be listed in a standard English dictionary, which instead lists 'opener' as a device or thing used to open something, or in idiomatic senses.
