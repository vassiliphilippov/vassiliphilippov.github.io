ride on a plank

Reason

The verb 'teetertotter' meaning 'to ride on a plank' is extremely rare and not established as a standard English verb. The noun 'teeter-totter' is common, but the verb form is virtually unattested in reputable dictionaries and is not widely used. Therefore, this sense should not be included in the dictionary.
