phlegm

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04643310-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: phlegm|oewn-04643310-n
inactivity; showing an unusual lack of energy

Synset Examples

the general appearance of sluggishness alarmed his friends

Reason

This sense of 'phlegm' as 'inactivity' or 'unusual lack of energy' is archaic and extremely rare in modern English. It would not typically appear in a contemporary learner's dictionary like Oxford, except perhaps as a historical note, and is not a distinct headword or primary sense today. The main modern meaning of 'phlegm' is the medical one (thick mucus). Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
