Tamil Pokkisham

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: Tamil Pokkisham|extra.010242
a cultural reference to a Tamil bookstore or platform

Synset Examples

He found a rare book at Tamil Pokkisham last week.
She discovered her favorite author at Tamil Pokkisham.

Reason

The concept 'Tamil Pokkisham' refers to a specific organization or platform (a Tamil bookstore) and not to a generalizable English vocabulary item. It does not have an independent, general dictionary sense and would not be included in standard dictionaries like Oxford, which exclude names of individual organizations unless they have become genericized (which is not the case here).
