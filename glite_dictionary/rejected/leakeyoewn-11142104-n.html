Leakey

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11142104-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Leakey|oewn-11142104-n
English paleontologist (son of Louis Leakey and Mary Leakey) who continued the work of his parents; he was appointed director of a wildlife preserve in Kenya but resigned under political pressure (born in 1944)

Reason

This sense of 'Leakey' refers to a specific individual (Richard Leakey) and would be categorized as a proper noun referring to a person. Major dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary generally do not include individual people's names unless they have become eponymous or refer to something else. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
