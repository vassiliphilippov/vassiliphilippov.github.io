vapor

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13593639-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: vapor|oewn-13593639-n
the process of becoming a vapor

Reason

This sense of 'vapor' (as 'the process of becoming a vapor') is not standard or attested in reputable dictionaries; the noun 'vapor' refers to the substance (gas) itself, not the process, which is termed 'vaporization' or 'evaporation.' Thus, this concept should not be included.
