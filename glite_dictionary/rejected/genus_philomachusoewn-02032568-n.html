Reason

The concept 'genus Philomachus' refers to a specific scientific taxonomic group and is now considered outdated, as the ruff and its allies are often placed in the genus Calidris or Philomachus is treated as a synonym for Calidris. Additionally, such scientific genus-level names are often not included in general English dictionaries like Oxford, unless the genus is widely used in non-specialist contexts. 'Philomachus' is a technical zoological term, not a common English noun.
