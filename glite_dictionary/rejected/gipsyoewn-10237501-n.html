gipsy

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10237501-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: gipsy|oewn-10237501-n
a laborer who moves from place to place as demanded by employment

Synset Examples

itinerant traders

Reason

The synset describes a general concept of a migratory laborer, not specifically related to the headword 'gipsy'. 'Gipsy' in this context is a dated and often problematic term for Romani people, and using it solely to mean an itinerant worker is not accurate in contemporary English usage. The sense is too general and potentially misleading, so it should be excluded.
