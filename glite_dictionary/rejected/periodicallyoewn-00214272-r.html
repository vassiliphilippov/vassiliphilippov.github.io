periodically

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00214272-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: periodically|oewn-00214272-r
in a sporadic manner

Synset Examples

he only works sporadically

Reason

The synset refers to 'periodically' in the sense of 'sporadically', but 'periodically' is not commonly or correctly used to mean 'in a sporadic manner'; 'sporadically' would be the appropriate entry for this meaning. Including 'periodically' with this sense would likely confuse learners, as standard English dictionaries define 'periodically' as 'at regular intervals', which is nearly the opposite of 'in a sporadic manner'. Therefore, this concept should not be included.
