Nederland

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08968866-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Nederland|oewn-08968866-n
a constitutional monarchy in western Europe on the North Sea; half the country lies below sea level

Reason

The synset for 'Nederland' is 'a constitutional monarchy in western Europe,' which refers to the country commonly known in English as 'the Netherlands.' The English dictionary headword should be 'Netherlands,' not 'Nederland' (which is the Dutch name and only appears as a foreign spelling or rarely as an exonym). Standard English dictionaries do not generally include non-anglicized country names as dictionary entries unless they are widely used in English. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
