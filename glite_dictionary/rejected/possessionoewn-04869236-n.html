possession

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04869236-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: possession|oewn-04869236-n
the trait of resolutely controlling your own behavior

Reason

This sense of 'possession' as 'the trait of resolutely controlling your own behavior' is extremely rare and not commonly found in modern English usage or in standard dictionaries. The main senses of 'possession' concern ownership, property, or spiritual possession, not self-control. Including this would confuse learners, as standard dictionaries never list this meaning for 'possession.'
