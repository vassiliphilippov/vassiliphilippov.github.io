Reason

The sense 'a representation by picture or portraiture' for 'portraying' is not attested as a standard noun in English usage. 'Portraying' is primarily the present participle form of the verb 'portray,' and in nominal construction, the standard noun is 'portrayal.' This noun sense is too general and not used independently as a countable noun in typical usage. It would not appear in a major English dictionary under this form.
