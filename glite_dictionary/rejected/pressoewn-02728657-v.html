be urgent

Synset Examples

Reason

This synset is an extremely rare or obsolete verbal sense of 'press' meaning 'to be urgent', not commonly used in contemporary English. The modern adjective 'pressing' is used to mean 'urgent', but 'press' as a verb with this meaning is virtually never used. As such, this sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
