Reason

The concept 'go deep' in the sense of 'extend in importance or range' is too general and does not constitute a well-established dictionary entry with this specific meaning in English. The phrase is used, but not as a distinct, commonly recognized idiom or phrasal verb relating to this abstract sense, especially as illustrated by the example 'His accomplishments go far'. Instead, 'go far' is typical for this meaning, and 'go deep' more commonly refers to literal or metaphorical depth (e.g., knowledge, emotion). Therefore, it should not be included.
