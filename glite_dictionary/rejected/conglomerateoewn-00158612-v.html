conglomerate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00158612-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: conglomerate|oewn-00158612-v
increase in amount by collecting or gathering

Synset Examples

Journals are accumulating in my office
The work keeps piling up

Reason

The verb sense of 'conglomerate' meaning 'to increase in amount by collecting or gathering' is not commonly used in modern English. Instead, 'accumulate' or 'pile up' are the standard verbs for this meaning. This sense does not appear in Oxford or major dictionaries under 'conglomerate' as a verb in this usage. Therefore, it should be excluded.
