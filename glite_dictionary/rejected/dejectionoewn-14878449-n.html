dejection

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14878449-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: dejection|oewn-14878449-n
solid excretory product evacuated from the bowels

Reason

This sense of 'dejection'—meaning 'solid excretory product'—is excessively rare, archaic, and not found in modern English dictionaries. The common and accepted meaning of 'dejection' is 'a state of sadness or depression.' Including this sense would be misleading and unnecessary for learners.
