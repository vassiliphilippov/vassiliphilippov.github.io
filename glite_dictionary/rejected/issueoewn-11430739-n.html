issue

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11430739-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: issue|oewn-11430739-n
a phenomenon that follows and is caused by some previous phenomenon

Synset Examples

the magnetic effect was greater when the rod was lengthwise
his decision had depressing consequences for business
he acted very wise after the event

Reason

This sense of 'issue' as 'a phenomenon that follows and is caused by some previous phenomenon' is too broad and general; it does not represent a standard, recognizable usage of the word 'issue' as seen in reputable dictionaries. In this context, words like 'consequence,' 'result,' or 'effect' are appropriate, but 'issue' is not clearly used in modern or common English this way. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
