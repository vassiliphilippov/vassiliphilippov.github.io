Crocodile River

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09362418-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Crocodile River|oewn-09362418-n
the second largest river in Africa that drains into the Indian Ocean; flows into the Indian Ocean

Reason

The synset sense is about a specific river (the second largest river in Africa draining into the Indian Ocean), but the main river corresponding to this definition is the Limpopo River, not commonly called 'Crocodile River.' 'Crocodile River' is a name shared by several distinct rivers in southern Africa. As such, this sense is too general and/or inaccurate for inclusion as a dictionary entry for 'Crocodile River.' It is not a well-accepted standalone geographical term meeting dictionary criteria.
