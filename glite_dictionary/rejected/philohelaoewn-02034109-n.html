Reason

Philohela refers to a former genus name in taxonomy and is no longer a valid or commonly used term in modern English; 'American woodcock' is the relevant common name. This concept is too technical, obscure, and outdated for inclusion in a general-purpose English dictionary like Oxford. It would be more appropriately included in a specialist ornithological or biological glossary.
