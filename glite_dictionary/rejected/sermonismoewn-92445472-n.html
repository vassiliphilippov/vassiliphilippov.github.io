Synset Examples

Although in one sense Abelard's attempt must be profoundly respected, yet practically no consequences worth mentioning have matured from it; for he was able to establish no mediatory psychological function beyond conceptualism or sermonism, which is merely a revised edition, altogether one-sided and intellectual, of the ancient Logos conception.

Reason

The concept 'sermonism', as described, refers specifically to a historical philosophical doctrine associated with Abelard and is extremely rare, essentially obsolete except in specialist academic discourse. It does not have general English usage and would not appear in a typical English dictionary such as the Oxford Dictionary.
