sailing

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00304321-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: sailing|oewn-00304321-n
the activity of flying a glider

Reason

This sense of 'sailing' (as the activity of flying a glider) is too rare and specialized. The primary and ordinary dictionary meaning of 'sailing' is traveling on water in a boat using sails. The glider-flying sense does not appear in major dictionaries, nor is it commonly used, so it should be excluded.
