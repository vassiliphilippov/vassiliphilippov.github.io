Reason

The synset defined as 'in a levelheaded manner' is not commonly associated with 'healthily.' In standard English dictionaries, 'healthily' refers to something done in a healthy way, especially relating to health or diet. The sense 'in a levelheaded manner' is overly abstract and not attested in current, real-world usage. Therefore, this concept should not be included in the dictionary.
