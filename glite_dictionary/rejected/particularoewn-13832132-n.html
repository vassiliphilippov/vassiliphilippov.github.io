Reason

The synset describing 'particular' as a noun meaning 'a small part that can be considered separately from the whole' is archaic and rarely used in modern English. Contemporary dictionaries primarily list 'particular' as an adjective, and the noun form with this meaning does not appear in standard modern usage. Therefore, this sense should not be included as a separate headword.
