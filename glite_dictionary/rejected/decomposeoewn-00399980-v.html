decompose

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00399980-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: decompose|oewn-00399980-v
lose a stored charge, magnetic flux, or current

Synset Examples

the particles disintegrated during the nuclear fission process

Reason

This sense of 'decompose' relates to highly technical/physical meanings involving the loss of charge, magnetic flux, or current, which is both very technical and rarely appears as a main dictionary meaning outside physics/engineering contexts. Additionally, it does not match the usual dictionary sense of 'decompose' as break down into simpler elements, rot, or decay. This sense is too specialized and not appropriate for a general English dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary.
