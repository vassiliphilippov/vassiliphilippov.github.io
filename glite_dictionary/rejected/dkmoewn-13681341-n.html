a metric unit of length equal to ten meters

Reason

'dkm' stands for 'dekameter', a metric unit of length. However, the abbreviation 'dkm' is rarely, if ever, used in standard English. Dictionaries typically include the full form 'dekameter' (or 'decametre' in British English), but not the uncommon or obsolete abbreviation 'dkm'. Furthermore, this unit itself is very rarely used in English-speaking contexts.
