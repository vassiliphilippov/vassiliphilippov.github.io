source

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09430745-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: source|oewn-09430745-n
anything (a person or animal or plant or substance) in which an infectious agent normally lives and multiplies

Synset Examples

an infectious agent depends on a reservoir for its survival

Reason

This synset describes 'source' as it relates specifically to epidemiology, corresponding closely to the technical sense often covered by the word 'reservoir' in medical contexts. It is too technical and overlaps with a more precise term already covered ('reservoir'). The general noun 'source' in its medical/epidemiological sense is not distinct or frequent enough to warrant a separate dictionary headword entry for this synset. Therefore, it should not be included as a standalone dictionary entry for 'source'.
