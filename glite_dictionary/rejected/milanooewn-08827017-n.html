Milano

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08827017-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Milano|oewn-08827017-n
the capital of Lombardy in northern Italy; has been an international center of trade and industry since the Middle Ages

Reason

The headword 'Milano' refers to the city known as 'Milan' in English. In standard English dictionaries, the English form 'Milan' is given as the main entry. 'Milano' is not the standard English usage but the Italian name for the city. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a dictionary entry in an English-English dictionary.
