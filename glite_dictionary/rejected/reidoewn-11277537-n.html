Reid

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11277537-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Reid|oewn-11277537-n
Scottish philosopher of common sense who opposed the ideas of David Hume (1710-1796)

Reason

The synset is 'person' and refers to 'Reid' as a specific individual (Thomas Reid) rather than a meaningful dictionary headword. Dictionaries like Oxford would instead have an entry for 'Thomas Reid', not just 'Reid'. This usage is too general and not suitable as an independent dictionary entry.
