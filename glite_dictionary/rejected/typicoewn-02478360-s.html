typic

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02478360-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: typic|oewn-02478360-s
being or serving as an illustration of a type

Synset Examples

the free discussion that is emblematic of democracy
an action exemplary of his conduct

Reason

The concept 'typic' as 'being or serving as an illustration of a type' is not in common modern usage and is not standard in contemporary English dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The synonyms 'emblematic' and 'exemplary' are the standard forms for this meaning. 'Typic' would only appear, if at all, as an archaic or highly specialized technical term, so it should not be included as a main dictionary entry.
