milkweed

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: milkweed|oewn-12039504-n
annual Eurasian sow thistle with soft spiny leaves and rayed yellow flower heads

Reason

The synset described refers specifically to an 'annual Eurasian sow thistle' rather than the common dictionary meaning of 'milkweed' (plants of the genus Asclepias). This is a botanical misalignment; this specific sense would not be included under 'milkweed' in a standard English dictionary.
