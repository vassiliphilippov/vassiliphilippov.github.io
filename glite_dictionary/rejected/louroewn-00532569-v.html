Reason

The synset described here relates to the verb 'lower' in the sense of 'to set lower' (for example, to lower a rating or lower expectations). The headword provided is 'lour', which is a different, much rarer word with a distinct meaning (usually 'to frown' or 'to look sullen'), and is not synonymous with 'lower' in this context. Therefore, this concept should not be included as 'lour' with the sense 'to set lower'.
