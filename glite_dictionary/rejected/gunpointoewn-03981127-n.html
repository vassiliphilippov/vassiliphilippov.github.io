gunpoint

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03981127-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: gunpoint|oewn-03981127-n
the gun muzzle's direction

Synset Examples

he held me up at the point of a gun

Reason

The synset 'the gun muzzle's direction' is too general and not specific to 'gunpoint' as an independent lexical item. In natural English, 'gunpoint' is used in the idiom 'at gunpoint', meaning 'under threat of being shot', not as a noun meaning the physical direction of a gun muzzle. This sense would not appear as a separate headword or meaning in a standard English dictionary.
