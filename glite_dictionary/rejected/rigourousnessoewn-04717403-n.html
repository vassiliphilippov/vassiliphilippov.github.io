Reason

The concept 'rigourousness' is assigned here to a broad, abstract sense ('something hard to endure'), which is too general and not specifically associated with the headword. The more standard dictionary sense of 'rigourousness' is 'the quality of being rigorous,' not 'something hard to endure.' Since this synset is not closely relevant to 'rigourousness' as it would appear in standard dictionaries, it should be excluded.
