bargainer

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10740102-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: bargainer|oewn-10740102-n
someone who purchases and maintains an inventory of goods to be sold

Reason

The concept 'bargainer' with the sense of 'someone who purchases and maintains an inventory of goods to be sold' is too broad and does not accurately reflect actual usage of 'bargainer' in English. Typically, 'bargainer' refers to someone who negotiates or makes bargains, not specifically to someone managing inventory for resale. This sense would not appear in major English dictionaries under this form. Therefore, it should not be included.
