thumb

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03872399-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: thumb|oewn-03872399-n
a convex molding having a cross section in the form of a quarter of a circle or of an ellipse

Reason

This sense of 'thumb' refers to a very specialized architectural/woodworking term for a particular molding shape, better known as 'ovolo.' This use is obscure and not commonly found in general English dictionaries such as Oxford; it is restricted to technical glossaries. Also, most dictionary users will not expect this meaning under 'thumb.'
