Reason

The verb 'sonnet' meaning 'to compose a sonnet' is extremely rare and not widely accepted or attested in modern English. Major dictionaries such as Oxford do not include it as a verb. Only the noun 'sonnet' is standard. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
