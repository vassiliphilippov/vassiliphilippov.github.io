Reason

The term 'uncamphorated' is an extremely rare and highly technical word, essentially meaning 'not containing camphor.' It is formed by adding the negative prefix 'un-' to 'camphorated,' which itself is scarcely used outside of specialized, mostly outdated medical or pharmaceutical contexts. 'Uncamphorated' as an adjective would not be found in major modern dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary and does not have general usage in contemporary or even historical English. Thus, it is too marginal for inclusion.
