imitative

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01120122-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: imitative|oewn-01120122-a
not genuine; imitating something superior

Synset Examples

counterfeit emotion
counterfeit money
counterfeit works of art
a counterfeit prince

Reason

The synset meaning is 'not genuine; imitating something superior,' which is more accurately covered by 'counterfeit' or 'fake.' 'Imitative' in mainstream English typically means 'copying or mimicking' rather than 'not genuine' or 'fraudulent,' and does not directly mean 'counterfeit.' Therefore, this specific sense mapping is too broad/incorrect for 'imitative.' It should not be included as a direct dictionary entry for 'imitative' in this sense.
