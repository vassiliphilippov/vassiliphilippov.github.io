canvass

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02957504-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: canvass|oewn-02957504-n
an oil painting on canvas fabric

Reason

This sense of 'canvass' (an oil painting on canvas fabric) is not standard or attested in reputable dictionaries; moreover, the correct spelling for the painting sense is 'canvas', not 'canvass'. 'Canvass' as a noun typically refers only to the act of soliciting votes. This is either a misspelling or an extremely rare/obsolete usage that does not warrant dictionary entry.
