Vilno

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09036643-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Vilno|oewn-09036643-n
the capital and largest city of Lithuania; located in southeastern Lithuania

Reason

The synset here refers generally to a 'city' (a place), and 'Vilno' is an outdated or alternative name for the city now universally known as 'Vilnius.' Modern English dictionaries, such as Oxford, use the current official name 'Vilnius.' Thus, 'Vilno' as a headword for the modern capital of Lithuania is obsolete and does not warrant its own entry beyond an etymology or alternative name note under 'Vilnius.'
