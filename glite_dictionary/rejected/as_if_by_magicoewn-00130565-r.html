as if by magic

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00130565-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: as if by magic|oewn-00130565-r
in a magical manner

Synset Examples

it disappeared magically

Reason

The concept 'as if by magic' here refers to the adverbial sense 'in a magical manner', which is synonymous with 'magically'. As an adverbial phrase describing manner, it is not an idiom with distinctive meaning; instead, it is a compositional phrase that would not have a standalone entry in major dictionaries like Oxford. Related senses such as the adverb 'magically' should be included instead.
