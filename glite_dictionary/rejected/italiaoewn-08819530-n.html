Italia

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Italia|oewn-08819530-n
a republic in southern Europe on the Italian Peninsula; was the core of the Roman Republic and the Roman Empire between the 4th century BC and the 5th century AD

Reason

The synset is for 'republic' (i.e., a political entity), while the headword 'Italia' is simply the Italian name for the country 'Italy'. In standard English dictionaries, 'Italia' is only included as a foreign or historical form, and the proper entry would be for 'Italy'. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
