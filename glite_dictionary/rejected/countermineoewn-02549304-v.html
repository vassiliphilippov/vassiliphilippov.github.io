countermine

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: v
Internal ID: countermine|oewn-02549304-v
destroy property or hinder normal operations

Synset Examples

The Resistance sabotaged railroad operations during the war

Reason

The concept 'countermine' as a verb meaning 'destroy property or hinder normal operations' corresponds to the verb 'sabotage,' and 'countermine' as a verb in this sense is extremely rare, archaic, and not in common use in modern English. Major dictionaries do not include 'countermine' as a verb with this sense. Therefore, it should not be included in a modern English-English dictionary.
