gabardine

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03361927-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: gabardine|oewn-03361927-n
(usually in the plural) trousers made of flannel or gabardine or tweed or white cloth

Reason

This sense refers specifically to trousers made of gabardine or similar fabrics, but 'gabardine' is not commonly used in modern standard English to mean trousers themselves; the main sense refers to the fabric. The synset is too narrow and not widely used as a standalone noun meaning 'trousers' in contemporary English dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary. Standard dictionary entries include 'gabardine' as a type of fabric, not as trousers.
