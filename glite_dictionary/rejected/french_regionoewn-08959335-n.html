Reason

The sense 'French region' refers to France's regional administrative divisions but is a descriptive phrase, not a standard lexicalized entry in English. It is too generic and refers to a category (region of France) which would not appear with its own entry in a typical English dictionary. Instead, 'region' would be defined, and 'region of France' would be clear contextually.
