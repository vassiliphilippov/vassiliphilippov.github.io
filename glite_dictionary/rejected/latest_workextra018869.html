latest work

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: latest work|extra.018869
most recent artistic creation

Synset Examples

The artist's latest work was displayed on Sheetpost.

Reason

'Latest work' is a compositional phrase combining 'latest' and 'work', and refers generally to the most recent creation by someone, but there is no idiomatic or specialized meaning beyond that construction. Such compositional phrases are usually not included as headwords in dictionaries unless they have a special or established meaning. This phrase is too general and could apply to any type of creation or labor, lacking sufficient dictionary relevance as a fixed expression.
