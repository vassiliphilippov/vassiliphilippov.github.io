forgo

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02718318-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: forgo|oewn-02718318-v
be earlier in time; go back further

Synset Examples

Stone tools precede bronze tools

Reason

The synset meaning 'be earlier in time; go back further' (as in 'precede') does not naturally align with the canonical meaning of 'forgo' in standard English, which means 'to do without' or 'to abstain from.' This sense should not be included, as it is too general and not a standard usage of the headword 'forgo.'
