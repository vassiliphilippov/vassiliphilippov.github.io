gimmick

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05172200-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: gimmick|oewn-05172200-n
a drawback or difficulty that is not readily evident

Synset Examples

it sounds good but what's the catch?

Reason

The synset is associated with 'gimmick' but actually refers to the sense of 'catch' meaning 'a hidden drawback or difficulty.' This sense does not properly pertain to 'gimmick'; 'gimmick' does not commonly mean a hidden drawback. Therefore, this is an overly general or incorrect mapping and should not be included as a dictionary entry for 'gimmick.'
