capital of Thailand

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09059355-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capital of Thailand|oewn-09059355-n
the capital and largest city and chief port of Thailand; a leading city in southeastern Asia; noted for Buddhist architecture

Reason

The concept 'capital of Thailand' refers generally to the role of a city as a capital, and not to its specific proper noun (Bangkok). As a descriptive noun phrase for any capital city, it is too general and would not be listed as a standalone entry in a standard dictionary like Oxford; the actual city name 'Bangkok' would be included, but not 'capital of Thailand' as a dictionary headword.
