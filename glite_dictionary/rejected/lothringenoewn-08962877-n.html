Lothringen

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08962877-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Lothringen|oewn-08962877-n
an eastern French region rich in iron-ore deposits

Reason

The headword 'Lothringen' is the German name for the region known in English as 'Lorraine.' Only the English variant ('Lorraine') would appear in an English-English dictionary. 'Lothringen' is not standard in English usage for this geographic entity.
