Reason

This sense of 'super' is an attributive modifier meaning 'including more than a specified category', used technically or rarely and not as a standard independent dictionary entry. It is not a headword sense that would appear in Oxford Dictionary with this definition; typical dictionary entries for 'super' focus on 'excellent', 'superintendent' (as a noun), or as a prefix (meaning above, over). This specialized adjectival usage is too narrow and not independently dictionary-worthy.
