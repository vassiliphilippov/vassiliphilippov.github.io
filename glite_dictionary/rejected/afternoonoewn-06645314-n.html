afternoon

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06645314-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: afternoon|oewn-06645314-n
a conventional expression of greeting or farewell, used to wish someone a good afternoon

Reason

This concept refers to a conventional greeting or farewell using 'afternoon' as a shorthand for 'good afternoon'. As a standalone noun with this sense, 'afternoon' is not commonly used as a greeting in modern standard English dictionaries (such as the Oxford Dictionary). Instead, the noun 'afternoon' generally refers to the period of the day. The use as a greeting ('Afternoon!' meaning 'Good afternoon!') is colloquial and not sufficiently distinct to merit its own entry apart from usage notes under 'good afternoon'. Therefore, this sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
