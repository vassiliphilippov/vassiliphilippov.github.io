Reason

This concept corresponds to 'hard' as an adverb meaning 'indulging excessively' (e.g., 'drink hard'), but this usage is quite rare and archaic. Modern dictionaries rarely list 'hard' as an adverb with this sense, except as part of fixed expressions such as 'drink hard' or 'play hard'. The adverb normally means 'with a lot of effort' or 'intensely', so this specific 'excessive indulgence' meaning is too marginal to warrant its own entry.
