plyers

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03973308-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: plyers|oewn-03973308-n
a gripping hand tool with two hinged arms and (usually) serrated jaws

Reason

The correct spelling of the tool is 'pliers.' 'Plyers' is either a common misspelling or a very rare variant, and would not appear as a headword in standard dictionaries like Oxford. Only 'pliers' should be included.
