predication

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06745976-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: predication|oewn-06745976-n
(logic) a declaration of something self-evident; something that can be assumed as the basis for argument

Reason

The synset described—'a declaration of something self-evident'—is actually the definition for 'axiom' or 'postulate', not 'predication'. The correct sense of 'predication' in logic and grammar refers to the act of asserting or attributing a property to a subject. Thus, this concept as described does not correspond to the actual usage or meaning of 'predication' and is too general to include as a dictionary entry under this headword.
