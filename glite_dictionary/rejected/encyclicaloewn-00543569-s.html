encyclical

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00543569-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: encyclical|oewn-00543569-s
intended for wide distribution

Synset Examples

an encyclical letter

Reason

The synset here ('intended for wide distribution') describes an attributive sense (describing something as being for wide distribution), but the concept 'encyclical' is almost always used as a noun (a papal letter sent to all bishops) or, rarely, as a specific kind of letter/adjective. The attributive sense here is too narrow and depends on the noun sense. In dictionaries, the primary inclusion is for the noun or proper adjective related to the papal document, not the generalized adjective 'intended for wide distribution.' Therefore, this adjectival sense is too derived and rare to merit a separate dictionary entry.
