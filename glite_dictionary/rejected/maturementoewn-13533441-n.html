maturement

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13533441-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: maturement|oewn-13533441-n
coming to full development; becoming mature

Reason

The term 'maturement' is not used in contemporary English and is not standard; 'maturation' or simply 'maturity' is the accepted noun for the process of coming to full development. This word would not appear in Oxford or other reputable dictionaries.
