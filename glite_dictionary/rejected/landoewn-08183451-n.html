Reason

This synset for 'land' refers generally to 'the people who live in a nation or country,' which is more accurately covered by the nouns 'nation,' 'country,' or 'people.' 'Land' is not naturally or commonly used in modern standard English with this meaning. Therefore, it does not merit its own entry for this sense in an English-English dictionary.
