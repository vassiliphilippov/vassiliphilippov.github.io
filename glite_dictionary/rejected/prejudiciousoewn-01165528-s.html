prejudicious

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01165528-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: prejudicious|oewn-01165528-s
(sometimes followed by ‘to’) causing harm or injury

Synset Examples

damaging to career and reputation
the reporter's coverage resulted in prejudicial publicity for the defendant

Reason

The word 'prejudicious' is extremely rare, nonstandard, and does not appear in major authoritative dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The correct and commonly used form is 'prejudicial', which carries the intended meaning. Therefore, 'prejudicious' does not meet the criteria for inclusion as a standalone headword in a standard English dictionary.
