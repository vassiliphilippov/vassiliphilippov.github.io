good cup of coffee

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a

Internal ID: good cup of coffee|extra.011714
a satisfying serving of coffee

Synset Examples

Dere's nothing like a good cup of coffee.

Reason

The concept 'good cup of coffee' describes a generic, compositional phrase referring to a serving of coffee that is pleasing or satisfying. This is not an idiomatic or fixed expression, nor is it a compound or phrasal verb with a meaning beyond its parts. Such collocations are too general and do not merit a separate dictionary entry in the manner of the Oxford Dictionary.
