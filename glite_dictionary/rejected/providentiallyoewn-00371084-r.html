providentially

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00371084-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: providentially|oewn-00371084-r
in a prudent manner

Synset Examples

I had allotted my own bedroom for necking, prudently removing both the bed and the key, and taken both myself and my typewriter into my son's bedroom.

Reason

The sense of 'providentially' defined as 'in a prudent manner' is incorrect. 'Providentially' means 'by divine providence; as if by fate or good fortune,' not 'prudently' or 'in a careful manner.' This sense is either an error or a mis-mapping in WordNet and would not be included as such in a standard dictionary. The correct adverb for 'in a prudent manner' is 'prudently.'
