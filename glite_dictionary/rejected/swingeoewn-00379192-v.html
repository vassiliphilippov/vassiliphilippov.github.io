swinge

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00379192-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: swinge|oewn-00379192-v
burn superficially or lightly

Synset Examples

I singed my eyebrows

Reason

The concept 'swinge' as 'burn superficially or lightly' is not the standard or current usage; the correct and common verb with this meaning is 'singe.' 'Swinge' is obsolete with this sense and not found in modern usage or standard dictionaries under this meaning. Therefore, it should be excluded.
