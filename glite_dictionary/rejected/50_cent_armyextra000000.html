Reason

The concept '50 cent army' in the sense of 'a small but effective group or team' is not widely recognized in standard English dictionaries and appears to be a nonstandard or very rare usage. The more established use of '50 cent army' refers to Chinese internet commentators paid to influence public opinion online, which is a proper noun. As such, this sense does not warrant inclusion as a dictionary entry similar to Oxford Dictionary standards.
