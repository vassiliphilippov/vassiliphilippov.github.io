the music of Stravinsky

Synset Examples

Stravinsky no longer causes riots in the streets

Reason

The concept refers to 'the music of Stravinsky', which is a usage pattern seen with many composers' names (e.g., 'Mozart', 'Beethoven'), but as a dictionary headword, 'Stravinsky' is only included as a proper noun for the composer himself, not as a distinct noun for his music. The synset is too general and not a separate lexicalized sense; it does not warrant its own dictionary entry.
