Reason

The synset describes 'nestling' as 'a young person of either sex,' but 'nestling' is not used in standard English with this meaning. Its standard usage refers to a young bird that has not yet left the nest. Therefore, this concept does not reflect a valid or widely recognized linguistic sense of 'nestling' as applied to children and should not be included in the dictionary.
