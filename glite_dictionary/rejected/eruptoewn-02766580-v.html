Reason

This synset meaning for 'erupt' is not standard; 'erupt' in reference to fire/burning (i.e., 'start to burn' or 'burst into flames') is not idiomatic in modern English and is not an established sense in standard dictionaries. The typical senses of 'erupt' refer to volcanic activity or sudden outbursts, not specifically ignition or bursting into flames. Therefore, this concept is excluded as it would not appear in Oxford Dictionary under this meaning.
