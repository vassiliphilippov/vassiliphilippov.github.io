cow man

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09921470-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: cow man|oewn-09921470-n
a man who raises (or tends) cattle

Reason

The term 'cow man' is not a standard or widely recognized English word; 'cowman' (as one word) is the standard form and is included in major dictionaries. The spaced form 'cow man' is not established enough for a dictionary entry. Furthermore, the concept is already covered by 'cowman' or 'cattleman.'
