incurring

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00079037-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: incurring|oewn-00079037-n
acquiring or coming into something (usually undesirable)

Synset Examples

incurring debts is easier than paying them

Reason

The concept 'incurring' as a noun is not standard in English; 'incur' is a verb, and 'incurring' is its present participle or verbal noun (gerund) form. However, as a dictionary entry, 'incurring' as a noun is too rare and not established usage, so it does not merit a separate dictionary entry.
