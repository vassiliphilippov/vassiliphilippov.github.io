allspice

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11721333-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: allspice|oewn-11721333-n
deciduous shrubs having aromatic bark; eastern China; southwestern and eastern United States

Reason

This sense refers to a general category of deciduous shrubs with aromatic bark found in various regions, which is too broad and not directly related to the headword 'allspice' as commonly understood (which refers to a specific spice or tree, Pimenta dioica). This general botanical sense for 'allspice' does not warrant inclusion as a distinct dictionary entry.
