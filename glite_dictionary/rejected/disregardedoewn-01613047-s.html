disregarded

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01613047-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: disregarded|oewn-01613047-s
not noticed inadvertently

Synset Examples

her aching muscles forgotten she danced all night
he was scolded for his forgotten chores

Reason

The concept 'disregarded' here refers to the participial adjective sense meaning 'not noticed inadvertently', which is too close to the very general idea of 'overlooked' or 'forgotten'. This particular synset is not the primary or most distinctive sense of 'disregarded' as an adjective; it is better covered by words like 'forgotten', which is used directly in the examples. Therefore, this sense is too general and not distinct enough to be included as a standalone dictionary entry for 'disregarded'.
