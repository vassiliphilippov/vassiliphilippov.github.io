cut up

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00484732-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: cut up|oewn-00484732-v
separate into isolated compartments or categories

Synset Examples

You cannot compartmentalize your life like this!

Reason

The sense 'cut up' meaning 'separate into isolated compartments or categories' is very rare and not widely recognized as an established meaning in standard English dictionaries; it is not a common or idiomatic use of 'cut up' as a phrasal verb. The example uses 'compartmentalize', which is the standard word for this action. This sense does not warrant a separate entry in an English-English dictionary.
