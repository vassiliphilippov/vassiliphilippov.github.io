Dae-Han-Min-Gook

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08974384-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Dae-Han-Min-Gook|oewn-08974384-n
an Asian peninsula (off Manchuria) separating the Yellow Sea and the Sea of Japan; the Korean name is Dae-Han-Min-Gook or Han-Gook

Reason

The concept "Dae-Han-Min-Gook" refers to the Korean name for Korea, but the synset provided is for the geographical peninsula (Korea/Korean Peninsula), which is generally represented in English as "Korea" or "the Republic of Korea". The term "Dae-Han-Min-Gook" is just the native name and is not commonly used in English texts as a headword. Therefore, it should not be included as a stand-alone headword in an English-English dictionary.
