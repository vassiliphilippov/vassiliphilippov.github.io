pean

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06706894-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: pean|oewn-06706894-n
a formal expression of praise

Reason

The sense described as 'a formal expression of praise' actually refers to 'paean', which is the correct and established English word for this concept. 'Pean' as such is not recognized as a standard English noun for this meaning; it is likely a misspelling or misreading of 'paean'. Therefore, this concept should not be included in the dictionary.
