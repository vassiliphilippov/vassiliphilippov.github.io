gleaming

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07427078-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: gleaming|oewn-07427078-n
a flash of light (especially reflected light)

Reason

The noun sense of 'gleaming' as 'a flash of light' is not standard in contemporary English and does not appear in major dictionaries for this form; 'gleam' is used instead. Therefore, it should not be included as a noun entry.
