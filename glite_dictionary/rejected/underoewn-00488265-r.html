Reason

The adverbial use of 'under' meaning 'further down' is a specific editorial or reference convention, not a general English word sense; it is too specialized, technical, and does not appear as a distinct word meaning in general dictionaries like Oxford. It functions as a heading or pointer in reference works, not as an independent lexical meaning.
