key up

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: key up|extra.005728
to prepare or finalize a document or input data

Synset Examples

I had to key up the report before submission.
Make sure to key up all the data accurately.

Reason

The phrasal verb "key up" with the sense of preparing or finalizing a document or inputting data is not widely recognized or established in standard English; it is not attested in major dictionaries with this meaning. The usual term for entering data is "key in." Therefore, this sense should not be included as a standalone dictionary entry.
