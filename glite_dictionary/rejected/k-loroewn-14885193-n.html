salt of potassium (KCl) (trade names K-Dur 20, Kaochlor and K-lor and Klorvess and K-lyte); taken in tablet form to treat potassium deficiency

Reason

K-lor is a trademarked trade name for a specific brand of potassium chloride supplement, not a generic term. Such proprietary names, unless broadly used in general language, are usually excluded from general-purpose dictionaries like Oxford. The definition is also very specific and technical, relevant mainly in medical or pharmacological contexts.
