pouch

WordNet ID: oewn-13937488-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: pouch|oewn-13937488-n
an enclosed space

Synset Examples

the trapped miners found a pocket of air

Reason

This synset refers to 'pouch' in the highly general sense of 'an enclosed space,' exemplified by 'a pocket of air.' This is not a distinct or commonly recognized usage of 'pouch' as a noun in standard English dictionaries, as the primary senses relate to a small bag or sac. This generalized meaning does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
