Reason

This sense is a very marginal, slang, and regionally variable insult which is not widely recognized or standard. The use of 'goose' to mean 'a stupid incompetent man' is rare and not established enough to merit a separate entry in a standard English dictionary such as Oxford. More common senses of 'goose' as a bird and as a verb are covered elsewhere.
