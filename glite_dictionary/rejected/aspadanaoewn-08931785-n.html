city in central Iran; former capital of Persia

Reason

Aspadana is an old or alternative name for the city of Isfahan. The synset is too general and does not represent a stand-alone, widely used English headword, as contemporary dictionaries would define Isfahan, not Aspadana. This term is mostly of scholarly or historical interest and not in common current English usage as a headword.
