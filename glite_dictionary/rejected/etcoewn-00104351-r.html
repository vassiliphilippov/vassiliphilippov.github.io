etc.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00104351-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: etc.|oewn-00104351-r
continuing in the same way

Reason

The sense description is for an adverbial use of 'etc.', meaning 'continuing in the same way', but 'etc.' is an abbreviation for 'et cetera' and functions as a discourse marker meaning 'and so on'. Its part of speech is not usually classified as an adverb, and it is already adequately covered under its primary use as an abbreviation. This adverbial synset is too general, and does not warrant a separate entry.
