location

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00156307-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: location|oewn-00156307-n
a determination of the place where something is

Synset Examples

he got a good fix on the target

Reason

This particular synset of 'location' refers to the technical or abstract sense: the determination of where something is, rather than the more widely used noun sense (a place or position). This is a highly general and abstract usage, more of a metalinguistic or technical interpretation than an independent dictionary-worthy sense for the general learner. The ordinary dictionary sense 'a place or position' should be included, but not this more technical/abstract synset.
