apprize

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00316996-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: apprize|oewn-00316996-v
gain in value

Synset Examples

The yen appreciated again!

Reason

This sense of 'apprize' is actually a mix-up with 'appreciate' (to gain in value). The verb 'apprize' means to inform or notify, while the financial meaning (to increase in value) properly belongs to 'appreciate'. Therefore, this concept is not appropriate for dictionary inclusion under 'apprize', as it is too general and not meaningfully related to the headword.
