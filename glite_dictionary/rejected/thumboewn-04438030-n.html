thumb

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04438030-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: thumb|oewn-04438030-n
the part of a glove that provides a covering for the thumb

Reason

This sense refers specifically to the part of a glove that covers the thumb. It is a specialized, compositional usage rather than an established dictionary sense. Standard dictionaries do not define 'thumb' as 'the part of a glove for the thumb', since this is directly deducible from the primary sense of 'thumb' and the structure of gloves. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate entry.
