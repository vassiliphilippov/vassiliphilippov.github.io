manakin

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10310901-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: manakin|oewn-10310901-n
a woman who wears clothes to display fashions

Synset Examples

she was too fat to be a mannequin

Reason

This sense of 'manakin' is an error or confusion with 'mannequin,' which refers to a dummy used to display clothes or, figuratively, to a person (often a woman) who models fashions. The term 'manakin' itself properly refers to a small tropical bird and not a woman displaying fashions. The main word relevant to this sense is 'mannequin,' which would be included, but not this mistaken or archaic usage of 'manakin.'
