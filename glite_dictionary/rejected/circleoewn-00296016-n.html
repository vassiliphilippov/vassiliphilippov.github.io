Synset POS: n
Internal ID: circle|oewn-00296016-n
movement once around a course

Synset Examples

he drove an extra lap just for insurance

Reason

The sense 'movement once around a course' is described with the headword 'circle', but in common and dictionary usage, 'circle' as a noun does not refer specifically to a lap or circuit in racing or athletics. That meaning is normally covered by the word 'lap', not 'circle'. Therefore, this particular sense as 'movement once around a course' is too specific and not standard usage of 'circle' as a noun. It should not be included as a separate entry in the dictionary.
