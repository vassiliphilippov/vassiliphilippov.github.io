left the windows down

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: left the windows down|extra.018958
did not close the car windows

Synset Examples

It started raining, and shit, we left the windows down.

Reason

The phrase 'left the windows down' is a compositional and literal usage meaning someone did not raise or close the car windows. It is not an idiom, phrasal verb, or fixed expression with independent meaning beyond the sum of its parts. This would not have a separate entry in a typical dictionary like Oxford.
