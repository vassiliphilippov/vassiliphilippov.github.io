bear on

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02685709-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: bear on|oewn-02685709-v
keep or maintain in unaltered condition; cause to remain or last

Synset Examples

preserve the peace in the family
continue the family tradition
Carry on the old traditions

Reason

The synset meaning 'keep or maintain in unaltered condition' is not idiomatically or naturally expressed by 'bear on' in English; the examples fit verbs like 'preserve' or 'carry on.' 'Bear on' more commonly means 'be relevant to' or 'affect,' which is a separate sense. This sense would not appear as an entry or subsense for 'bear on' in a modern English dictionary.
