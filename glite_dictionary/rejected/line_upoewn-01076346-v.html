line up

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01076346-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: line up|oewn-01076346-v
take one's position before a kick-off

Reason

This sense of 'line up' is a very narrowly defined usage specific to sports (taking position before a kick-off), but the general phrasal verb 'line up' (to stand in a row, to arrange in line, etc.) is the dictionary-worthy sense. The narrowly sports-related sense is subsumed under the broader meanings and is not commonly listed as a separate dictionary entry. Therefore, this specific sense does not qualify for inclusion as a distinct entry.
