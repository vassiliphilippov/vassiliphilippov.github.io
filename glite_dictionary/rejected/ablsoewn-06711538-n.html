Reason

The concept 'ABLS' corresponds to an abbreviation for 'Bachelor of Library Science', specifically a particular degree name. Abbreviations for degree titles like this, unless widely used as a common noun, do not typically appear as headwords in major English-English dictionaries such as Oxford. Including every such academic abbreviation would lead to an overabundance of technical entries not used in general English. Therefore, it should be excluded.
