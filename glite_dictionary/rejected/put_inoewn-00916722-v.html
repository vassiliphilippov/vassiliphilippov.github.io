put in

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00916722-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: put in|oewn-00916722-v
to insert between other elements

Synset Examples

She interjected clever remarks

Reason

The phrasal verb 'put in' in the sense of 'to insert between other elements' is very rare and not a recognized dictionary meaning; standard meanings of 'put in' refer to placing, installing, or submitting (e.g., 'put in an application'), not interjecting among elements. The example sentence given better fits 'interject' than a dictionary entry for 'put in.' Therefore, this sense is not appropriate for an English-English dictionary and should be excluded.
