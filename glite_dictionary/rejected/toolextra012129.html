tool

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: tool|extra.012129
everything necessary for a task

Synset Examples

His workbox is filled with all the tools he needs.

Reason

This sense is too broad and general ("everything necessary for a task") and is not a standard or distinct dictionary meaning for 'tool.' Instead, 'tool' in dictionaries is primarily defined as an instrument or implement used to carry out a particular function. The example provided conflates the collection (like a workbox) with the individual 'tools.' Therefore, this concept does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
