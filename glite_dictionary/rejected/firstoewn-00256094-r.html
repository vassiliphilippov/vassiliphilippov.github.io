first

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00256094-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: first|oewn-00256094-r
prominently forward

Synset Examples

he put his best foot foremost

Reason

The synset refers to the adverbial use of "first" meaning 'prominently forward,' but this usage ('foremost', as in 'first and foremost') is archaic and much less common than the standard uses of 'first' meaning 'before all others' in order or time. This specific adverbial nuance does not merit a separate dictionary entry in a modern dictionary, as it overlaps with 'foremost.'
