strings

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06783666-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: strings|oewn-06783666-n
(usually plural) a statement of what is required as part of an agreement

Synset Examples

the contract set out the conditions of the lease
the terms of the treaty were generous

Reason

The sense described as 'a statement of what is required as part of an agreement,' is actually properly covered by 'terms' or 'conditions' rather than 'strings.' 'Strings' is not used in standard English to mean contractual terms, so this definition is either erroneous, archaic, or extremely rare. This concept should not be included as a dictionary entry for 'strings.'
