Jugoslavija

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08834088-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Jugoslavija|oewn-08834088-n
a mountainous republic in southeastern Europe bordering on the Adriatic Sea; formed from two of the six republics that made up Yugoslavia until 1992; Serbia and Montenegro were known as the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia until 2003 when they adopted the name of the Union of Serbia and Montenegro

Reason

The synset refers to a country proper noun ('Jugoslavija' as 'Yugoslavia', specifying a former republic). However, the lemma 'Jugoslavija' is not the canonical English form (instead, 'Yugoslavia' is), and in current English dictionaries foreign names are only included if they are in use in English. 'Jugoslavija' is the transliteration of the name in Serbo-Croatian, not standard in English. Therefore, this is too language-specific and not suitable for an English-English dictionary entry.
