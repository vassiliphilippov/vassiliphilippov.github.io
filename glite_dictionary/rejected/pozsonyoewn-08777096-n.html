capital and largest city of Slovakia

Reason

The synset for 'Pozsony' is 'capital and largest city of Slovakia'. However, 'Pozsony' is the Hungarian name for the city known in English as 'Bratislava'. Standard English dictionaries use 'Bratislava' as the headword; 'Pozsony' may be noted as an alternative foreign-language name but is not used as the main or common English term. Thus, by Oxford Dictionary standards, it would appear only as a variant or historical note under 'Bratislava', not as a main entry.
