swob

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01235758-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: swob|oewn-01235758-v
apply (usually a liquid) to a surface

Synset Examples

dab the wall with paint

Reason

The verb 'swob' meaning 'to apply (usually a liquid) to a surface' is highly obsolete and no longer used in standard contemporary English. Modern usage overwhelmingly prefers 'dab' or 'swab' for this sense. Its inclusion would not be helpful for most learners and does not meet the criteria for modern dictionary inclusion, except as an extremely rare or archaic form. Therefore, it should be excluded.
