forbear

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10122138-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: forbear|oewn-10122138-n
a person from whom you are descended

Reason

This sense of 'forbear' as 'a person from whom you are descended' (ancestor, forebear) is extremely rare and archaic in this spelling; the modern and standard form is 'forebear'. The spelling 'forbear' as a noun meaning ancestor does not commonly appear in modern English dictionaries and would not be expected in an Oxford-style dictionary. The verb 'forbear' (to refrain) is a different word. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
