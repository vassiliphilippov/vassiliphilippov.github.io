indifferent

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01197871-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: indifferent|oewn-01197871-s
(usually followed by ‘to’) unwilling or refusing to pay heed

Synset Examples

deaf to her warnings

Reason

The synset 'indifferent' in the sense of 'deaf to' (unwilling or refusing to pay heed) is a general, metaphorical extension of 'indifferent,' which primarily means 'having no particular interest or sympathy.' The usage here is uncommon, and the concept is usually captured by 'indifferent to,' which is already covered under the main sense of 'indifferent.' It does not warrant a separate dictionary entry as described; the specific sense 'deaf to' is included within broader definitions of indifference.
