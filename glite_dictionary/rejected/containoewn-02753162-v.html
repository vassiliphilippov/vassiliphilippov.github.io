contain

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02753162-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: contain|oewn-02753162-v
be divisible by

Synset Examples

24 contains 6

Reason

This mathematical sense ('be divisible by') is a highly specific technical usage not commonly included in general English dictionaries. Most dictionaries define 'contain' as 'to have something inside' or 'to include.' The mathematical '24 contains 6' meaning '6 divides into 24 evenly' is not standard or in wide usage as a general English sense.
