Reason

The noun sense of 'oncoming' as 'the beginning or early stages' is not established in modern English usage, nor would it appear as a noun entry in major dictionaries such as Oxford. The provided example ('the onset of pneumonia') actually refers to the noun 'onset,' not 'oncoming.' Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
