Treasury

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10747110-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Treasury|oewn-10747110-n
the British cabinet minister responsible for economic strategy

Reason

This sense of 'Treasury' refers specifically to a position in the British government and is a named role, not a general lexical meaning. The primary lexical sense of 'Treasury' as a noun (government department responsible for financial matters) is included in dictionaries, but this sense as a cabinet minister (actually better described as the 'Chancellor of the Exchequer') is too specific for a general dictionary entry under 'Treasury.' It is more appropriately covered under entries like 'Chancellor of the Exchequer' or as a description under 'Treasury' (referring to the institution, not the person).
