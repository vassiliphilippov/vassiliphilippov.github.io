sinful

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01538743-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: sinful|oewn-01538743-s
far more than usual or expected

Synset Examples

an extraordinary desire for approval
it was an over-the-top experience

Reason

The synset describes an intensifying sense ('far more than usual or expected'), but the headword 'sinful' in standard usage means 'full of sin' or 'morally wrong'. This abstract sense is associated with terms like 'extraordinary' or 'over-the-top', not 'sinful'. The synset is not relevant to the primary meaning of 'sinful' and should be excluded.
