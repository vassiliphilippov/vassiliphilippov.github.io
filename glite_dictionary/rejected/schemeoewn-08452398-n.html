scheme

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08452398-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: scheme|oewn-08452398-n
a group of independent but interrelated elements comprising a unified whole

Synset Examples

a vast system of production and distribution and consumption keep the country going

Reason

This sense of 'scheme' refers to a general 'system' as a group of interrelated elements forming a unified whole. As described, it aligns with the noun 'system,' which is the more precise and universally accepted headword for this definition; 'scheme' is not commonly used in this generic sense in modern English. Therefore, this sense does not merit a dictionary entry under 'scheme.'
