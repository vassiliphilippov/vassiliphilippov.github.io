United States legislator who opposed the use of paper currency (1782-1858)

Reason

The concept 'Old Bullion' here refers to a specific individual (Thomas Hart Benton), and the synset links it to a general sense ('person, legislator'). Such biographical proper noun entries that are simply nicknames for historical figures are typically not included in standard dictionaries like Oxford unless the nickname itself is widely used as a generic noun or has entered the language independently. Therefore, this should be excluded.
