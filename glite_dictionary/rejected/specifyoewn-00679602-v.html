specify

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00679602-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: specify|oewn-00679602-v
select something or someone for a specific purpose

Synset Examples

The teacher assigned him to lead his classmates in the exercise

Reason

The synset described refers to the act of assigning or selecting someone for a specific purpose, which is more appropriately captured by verbs like 'assign,' 'appoint,' or 'designate.' The primary and conventional sense of 'specify' is 'to state explicitly' or 'to mention something clearly and exactly,' not 'to select for a purpose.' Including this rare or stretched usage as a definition for 'specify' would confuse learners and is not consistent with standard dictionary practice. Therefore, this sense should not be included.
