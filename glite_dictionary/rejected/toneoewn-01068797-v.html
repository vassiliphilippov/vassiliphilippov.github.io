tone

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01068797-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: tone|oewn-01068797-v
utter monotonously and repetitively and rhythmically

Synset Examples

The students chanted the same slogan over and over again

Reason

This sense of 'tone' as a verb meaning 'to utter monotonously and repetitively and rhythmically' is extremely rare and not established in modern usage; common dictionaries do not list 'tone' as a verb with this meaning. Furthermore, the typical verbs for this meaning are 'chant' or 'intone.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
