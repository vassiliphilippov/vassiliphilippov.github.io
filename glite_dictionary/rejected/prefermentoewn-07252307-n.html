Reason

The synset described as 'the act of making accusations' for 'preferment' is an extremely rare, context-specific technical sense. 'Preferment' is overwhelmingly used for its primary sense ('promotion; advancement in rank or office'), and the sense 'preferment of charges' is archaic/legalistic and almost never encountered in contemporary English outside set phrases. Including this secondary sense would not be helpful for general dictionary users. The primary 'promotion' sense should be included instead. Therefore, this sense is excluded.
