WordNet ID: oewn-02199729-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: ilx|oewn-02199729-s
being nine more than fifty

Reason

The concept 'ilx' as 'being nine more than fifty' refers to the Roman numeral for 59. However, 'ilx' is not a standard English word, nor a recognized Roman numeral (the correct Roman numeral for 59 is 'LIX'). Therefore, it would not appear in a standard English dictionary like Oxford and should be excluded.
