alternate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00121486-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: alternate|oewn-00121486-v
reverse (a direction, attitude, or course of action)

Reason

This sense of 'alternate' (meaning 'to reverse a direction or course of action') does not correspond to how 'alternate' is typically used as a verb in standard English. The common verb sense of 'alternate' is 'to occur or do in turn repeatedly.' The 'reverse' sense described seems more appropriate to the verb 'reverse' or 'invert,' and is not found in authoritative dictionaries under 'alternate.' Therefore, this concept should not be included.
