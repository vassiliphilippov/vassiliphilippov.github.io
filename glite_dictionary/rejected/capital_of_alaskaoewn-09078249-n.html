the state capital of Alaska

Reason

The concept 'capital of Alaska' refers to a specific place (the city of Juneau) by its relationship as a capital city, rather than as a distinct lexical item or headword. Such relational phrases (e.g., 'capital of X') are too general and not included as standard entries in English dictionaries like Oxford. Only the name 'Juneau' would merit a dictionary entry.
