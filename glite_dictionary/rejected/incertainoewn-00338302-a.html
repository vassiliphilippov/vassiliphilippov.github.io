incertain

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00338302-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: incertain|oewn-00338302-a
lacking or indicating lack of confidence or assurance

Synset Examples

uncertain of his convictions
unsure of himself and his future
moving with uncertain (or unsure) steps
an uncertain smile
touched the ornaments with uncertain fingers

Reason

The word 'incertain' is obsolete and not used in contemporary standard English. Its sense is fully captured by the common adjective 'uncertain.' 'Incertain' would not appear as a main entry in modern dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary.
