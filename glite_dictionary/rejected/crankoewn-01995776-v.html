Reason

The verb sense 'crank' meaning 'to travel along a zigzag path' is archaic and extremely rare. In contemporary English, 'crank' is not used in this sense and would not appear in major dictionaries like Oxford for this meaning. The main senses of 'crank' as a verb relate to turning a handle or mechanism. Therefore, this usage is excluded.
