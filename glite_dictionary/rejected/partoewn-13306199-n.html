part

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: part|oewn-13306199-n
assets belonging to or due to or contributed by an individual person or group

Synset Examples

he wanted his share in cash

Reason

The synset refers to a generic/noun sense ('assets belonging to or due to or contributed by an individual person or group') that is more naturally and commonly expressed as 'share' in this context. The word 'part' does not specifically or idiomatically mean 'share' of assets or property, but rather 'portion' or 'piece' in general. This specific legalistic/asset-related sense is not a primary or standalone dictionary sense of 'part.'
