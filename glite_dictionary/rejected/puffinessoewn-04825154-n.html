Synset POS: n
Internal ID: puffiness|oewn-04825154-n
lack of elegance as a consequence of being pompous and puffed up with vanity

Reason

This sense of 'puffiness' as 'lack of elegance due to being pompous and puffed up with vanity' is not a widely recognized or commonly used meaning of the word in contemporary English. The standard dictionary sense refers to swelling or bloating. This abstract sense is rare, not standard, and unlikely to be useful to most learners. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate entry.
