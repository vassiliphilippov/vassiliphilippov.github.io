Synset POS: n
Internal ID: exercise|oewn-00948944-n
the act of using

Synset Examples

he warned against the use of narcotic drugs
skilled in the utilization of computers

Reason

This sense of 'exercise' as 'the act of using' is extremely broad and not commonly used for the headword 'exercise' in modern English. In standard dictionaries like Oxford, 'exercise' usually refers to physical activity or an activity carried out for training or improvement, not the general act of using something. The examples given ('use of narcotic drugs', 'utilization of computers') do not naturally map to dictionary usage of the word 'exercise'. This is a general synset not specific to the headword and should be excluded.
