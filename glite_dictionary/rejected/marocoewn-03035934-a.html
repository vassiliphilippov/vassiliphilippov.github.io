Maroc

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03035934-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: Maroc|oewn-03035934-a
of or relating to or characteristic of Morocco or its people

Synset Examples

Moroccan mosques cannot be entered by infidels

Reason

The concept 'Maroc' refers to the French (and rarely used English) form for Morocco, but in this sense ('of or relating to Morocco or its people'), the standard English word is 'Moroccan.' 'Maroc' is not used as an adjective in standard English and would not appear as such in Oxford or similar dictionaries. Thus, it should be excluded.
