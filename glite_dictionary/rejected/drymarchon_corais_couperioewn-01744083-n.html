Drymarchon corais couperi

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01744083-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Drymarchon corais couperi|oewn-01744083-n
a variety of indigo snake

Reason

This entry refers to 'Drymarchon corais couperi,' the scientific name for the eastern indigo snake, as a variety of indigo snake. Entries for binomial scientific names of species fully in Latin are generally not included in standard English-English dictionaries (such as Oxford) unless the species is widely referred to by this name in English. Instead, the common name 'eastern indigo snake' would appear in such dictionaries. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
