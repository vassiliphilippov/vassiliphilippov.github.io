collapsable

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00467728-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: collapsable|oewn-00467728-a
capable of collapsing or being collapsed

Synset Examples

a collapsible boat

Reason

The standard spelling for this adjective is "collapsible"; "collapsable" is a common misspelling and does not warrant a separate dictionary entry. In major dictionaries such as Oxford and Merriam-Webster, only "collapsible" is listed as the canonical headword.
