a clear soup garnished with julienne vegetables

Reason

This sense of 'julienne' refers to a specific type of soup ('a clear soup garnished with julienne vegetables'). However, in common English usage, 'julienne' typically refers to the culinary technique of cutting vegetables into thin strips, not the soup itself. The sense as 'a clear soup with julienne vegetables' is too obscure and not frequently found as a standalone headword in major English dictionaries.
