vomit

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03288430-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: vomit|oewn-03288430-n
a medicine that induces nausea and vomiting

Reason

This sense ('a medicine that induces nausea and vomiting') is too general for the headword 'vomit'; the proper term for such a medicine is 'emetic'. 'Vomit' is not used as a noun to refer to a medicine in standard English dictionaries and would not appear as such in the Oxford Dictionary.
