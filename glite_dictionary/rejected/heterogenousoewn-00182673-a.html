Reason

The sense refers to an adjective meaning 'originating outside the body,' which is typically covered by the term 'exogenous.' The word 'heterogenous' standardly means 'diverse in character or content' and is not used in this sense in medical or general English. Therefore, the concept does not match standard dictionary usage for 'heterogenous' and is excluded.
