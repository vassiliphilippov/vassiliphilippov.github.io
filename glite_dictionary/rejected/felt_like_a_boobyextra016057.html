felt like a booby

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: felt like a booby|extra.016057
felt foolish or silly

Synset Examples

He felt like a booby for forgetting her birthday.

Reason

The phrase 'felt like a booby' is an ordinary compositional phrase based on the noun 'booby' meaning 'foolish or silly person.' The meaning follows directly from the noun, and the phrase itself is not an established idiom or fixed expression. Such compositional uses do not merit a separate dictionary entry according to Oxford Dictionary standards.
