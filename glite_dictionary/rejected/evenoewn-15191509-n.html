even

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15191509-n
Synset POS: n

the latter part of the day (the period of decreasing daylight from late afternoon until nightfall)

Synset Examples

he enjoyed the evening light across the lake

Reason

The noun sense 'even' referring to the latter part of the day is archaic and not in current standard usage; modern English uses 'evening' for this meaning. 'Even' as a noun for 'evening' would not typically appear as a main headword in a current dictionary except in historical or archaic contexts. Thus, this sense should be excluded.
