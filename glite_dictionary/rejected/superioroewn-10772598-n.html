superior

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10772598-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: superior|oewn-10772598-n
a combatant who is able to defeat rivals

Reason

This sense of 'superior' as a noun meaning 'a combatant who is able to defeat rivals' is extremely rare and not commonly found in modern English usage. The noun 'superior' far more typically refers to someone of higher rank, status, or quality, not specifically a victor in combat. Since this meaning would not appear in standard dictionaries like Oxford, it should be excluded.
