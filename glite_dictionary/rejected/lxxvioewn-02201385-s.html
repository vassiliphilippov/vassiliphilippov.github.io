being six more than seventy

Reason

The concept 'lxxvi' is the Roman numeral form of 76 ('six more than seventy'). While Arabic numerals like '76' are standard entries as numerals, Roman numerals, especially ones as high as LXXVI, are not typically included in English dictionaries except for the most common or culturally significant numerals (such as I, V, X, L, C, D, M). 'LXXVI' is highly rare and formulaic, not a word in open English usage and wouldn't merit a dictionary entry in the manner of Oxford Dictionary.
