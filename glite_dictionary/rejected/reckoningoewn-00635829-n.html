Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00635829-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: reckoning|oewn-00635829-n
the act of counting; reciting numbers in ascending order

Synset Examples

the counting continued for several hours

Reason

This sense of 'reckoning,' described as 'the act of counting; reciting numbers in ascending order,' is overly narrow and is not a recognized or standard meaning in modern English. The dictionary meaning of 'reckoning' refers to calculating, estimating, or considering, but not specifically to reciting numbers in order. This sense would not appear as a distinct entry in major English dictionaries like Oxford.
