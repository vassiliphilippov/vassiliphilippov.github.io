forgetful

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02013127-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: forgetful|oewn-02013127-a
(of memory) deficient in retentiveness or range

Synset Examples

a short memory

Reason

The concept describes 'deficient in retentiveness or range' for 'memory', referring to a property of 'memory' itself rather than directly to the adjective 'forgetful' as typically used for people. This sense is too abstract, general, and not headword-relevant for inclusion in an English-English dictionary under 'forgetful', as dictionaries present 'forgetful' for persons rather than for a property of memory.
