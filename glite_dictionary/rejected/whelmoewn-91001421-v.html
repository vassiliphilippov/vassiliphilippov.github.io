whelm

Synset POS: v

to neither fail to meet nor exceed expectation

Reason

This sense of 'whelm' ('to neither fail to meet nor exceed expectation') is not recognized as an established usage in standard English dictionaries. The primary meanings of 'whelm' relate to overwhelming or submerging, not to meeting expectations, so this sense should be excluded.
