Billie the Kid

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10876597-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Billie the Kid|oewn-10876597-n
United States outlaw who was said to have killed 21 men (1859-1881)

Reason

The synset is 'person,' which is too general and not specific to 'Billie the Kid'; such biographical entries are generally excluded from dictionaries like Oxford unless they are extremely culturally significant figures with major linguistic impact. In this case, the headword is also a variant misspelling of the commonly accepted 'Billy the Kid.'
