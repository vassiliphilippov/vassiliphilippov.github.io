Reason

This concept is labeled as 'penitentiary' but the actual sense described is for the adjective 'penitential', which means 'showing or constituting penance'. The word 'penitentiary' in standard usage is a noun meaning 'prison', not an adjective with this meaning. The adjective form in this sense is 'penitential', which is already a separate entry in dictionaries. Therefore, this synset does not correspond to a valid or meaningful use of 'penitentiary' as an adjective, and should be excluded.
