of or relating to or producing fruit of the plants of the genus Citrus

Synset Examples

the citrus production of Florida

Reason

The word 'citrous' is extremely rare in modern English and is not standard; the widely accepted adjective is 'citrus,' which covers the sense of being related to plants of the genus Citrus. The Oxford and other major dictionaries do not include 'citrous' as a headword, so it should not be included.
