pitching dangerously to one side

Reason

The noun sense of 'careen' as 'pitching dangerously to one side' is not recognized as a standard, independent noun in modern English dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary. 'Careen' is predominantly used as a verb meaning to tilt or to move swiftly and in an uncontrolled way. The noun use is either obsolete or nonstandard and does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
