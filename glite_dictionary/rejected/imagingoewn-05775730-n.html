imaging

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05775730-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: imaging|oewn-05775730-n
the ability to form mental images of things or events

Synset Examples

he could still hear her in his imagination

Reason

The synset is referring to 'imaging' as 'the ability to form mental images of things or events', but in general usage, 'imaging' more often means 'the process of producing an image, especially in medical or technical contexts.' The sense here is too close to the much broader concept of 'imagination' or 'imaging' as mental imagery, which is not a standard dictionary meaning for 'imaging.' This specific sense would not appear as a standalone entry in a mainstream English-English dictionary like Oxford; it would likely fall under 'imagination.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
