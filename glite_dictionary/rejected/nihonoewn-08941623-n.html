a constitutional monarchy occupying the Japanese Archipelago; a world leader in electronics and automobile manufacture and ship building

Reason

The concept 'Nihon' is a direct transliteration of the Japanese name for Japan. In English dictionaries, the standard entry is 'Japan' rather than the native or endonym 'Nihon.' While 'Nihon' may be used in specialized contexts, it is not commonly used in general English. Thus, it does not meet the inclusion criteria for a standard English-English dictionary.
