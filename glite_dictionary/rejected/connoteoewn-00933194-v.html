connote

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00933194-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: connote|oewn-00933194-v
involve as a necessary condition of consequence; as in logic

Synset Examples

solving the problem is predicated on understanding it well

Reason

This sense of 'connote' is a highly technical logical usage, effectively synonymous with 'imply' or 'entail' in a formal sense, but it is rarely used in this way in modern English. The word 'connote' in everyday and academic use typically refers to 'to imply or suggest (an idea or feeling) in addition to the literal or primary meaning.' The logical sense 'involve as a necessary condition' is not standardly found in Oxford or major learners' dictionaries, so it should be excluded.
