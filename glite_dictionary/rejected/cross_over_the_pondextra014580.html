Source: Idiom List

to move from one side of the pond to the other

Synset Examples

The kids watched the ducks cross over the pond.

Reason

The phrase 'cross over the pond' in this context simply means to move from one side of a literal pond to the other, using the literal meanings of its components. This is not an established idiomatic expression or phrasal verb with a distinct non-compositional meaning. Therefore, it does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
