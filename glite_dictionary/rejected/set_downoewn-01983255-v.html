Synset Examples

The passengers disembarked at Southampton

Reason

This sense of 'set down' ('go ashore') is very rare and not commonly encountered or recognized as a distinct meaning in modern English dictionaries. The primary meanings of 'set down' relate to placing something on a surface or writing something. This particular maritime sense is better expressed by 'disembark'. Therefore, it does not meet the inclusion criteria for a general dictionary entry.
