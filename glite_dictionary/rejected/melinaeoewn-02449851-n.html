Reason

'Melinae' is a taxonomic subfamily name referring to badgers and is not commonly used outside scientific classification. It is not a word that would be found in a typical general English dictionary like Oxford; it is a specialist term confined primarily to zoological literature and taxonomy. Therefore, it should not be included in a general English-English dictionary.
