remote-controlled

WordNet ID: oewn-01483247-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: remote-controlled|oewn-01483247-a
lacking a crew

Synset Examples

an unmanned satellite to Mars

Reason

The sense described here ('remote-controlled' meaning 'lacking a crew') is too general and is not an established meaning of 'remote-controlled' in English dictionaries. The example 'an unmanned satellite to Mars' fits 'unmanned' better. 'Remote-controlled' in dictionaries refers to being operated at a distance, not simply lacking a crew. This concept is therefore too broad and not a distinct dictionary sense.
