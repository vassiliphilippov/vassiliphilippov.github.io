Urania

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09578256-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Urania|oewn-09578256-n
goddess of love; counterpart of Greek Aphrodite

Reason

This synset is too general: 'goddess of love; counterpart of Greek Aphrodite' refers to the headword Urania, but this is a mistaken/overly broad mapping. 'Urania' is not widely established in English as the 'goddess of love'; that title belongs to Aphrodite (Greek) or Venus (Roman). Urania is, more properly, the Muse of astronomy in Greek mythology. Thus, this specific concept should not be included for the given sense.
