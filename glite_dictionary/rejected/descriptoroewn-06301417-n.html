descriptor

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06301417-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: descriptor|oewn-06301417-n
the phonological or orthographic sound or appearance of a word that can be used to describe or identify something

Synset Examples

the inflected forms of a word can be represented by a stem and a list of inflections to be attached

Reason

This sense of 'descriptor' refers to a highly technical, linguistic usage that describes the sound or appearance of a word for orthographic or phonological analysis. In practice, this meaning is extremely uncommon and not encountered in general, academic, or even most technical English beyond highly specialized linguistic literature. Most dictionary users will not look for this rare, technical sense, so it should be excluded. The standard senses of 'descriptor' as a label or describing word are covered elsewhere.
