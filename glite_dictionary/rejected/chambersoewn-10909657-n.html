Reason

The synset is a general proper noun ('person') for a historical figure (Sir William Chambers). Dictionary-style entries for individual people are usually not included in general English dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary unless they are extremely famous or their names are used generically. This would be handled in an encyclopedia, not a dictionary. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
