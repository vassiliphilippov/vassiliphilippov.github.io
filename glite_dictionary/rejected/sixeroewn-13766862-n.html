sixer

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13766862-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: sixer|oewn-13766862-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of five and one

Reason

The synset is for a specific instance of the number six as 'the sum of five and one', which is too general and is just a rephrasing of the word 'number'. 'Sixer' as a noun meaning 'the number six' is not a standard dictionary entry in this sense; this usage would not appear in the Oxford Dictionary under this mathematical sense. Hence, this concept should be excluded.
