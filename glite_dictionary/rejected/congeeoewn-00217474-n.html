congee

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00217474-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: congee|oewn-00217474-n
an abrupt and unceremonious dismissal

Reason

Although 'congee' can occasionally mean 'an abrupt and unceremonious dismissal' in very archaic or rare contexts, this usage is extremely obsolete and not present in modern English dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The overwhelming primary use of 'congee' in contemporary English is to refer to rice porridge. This sense is too archaic and rare to merit dictionary inclusion for modern learners.
