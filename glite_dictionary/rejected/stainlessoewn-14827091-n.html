Synset POS: n
Internal ID: stainless|oewn-14827091-n
steel containing chromium that makes it resistant to corrosion

Reason

The concept refers to 'stainless' as a noun meaning 'stainless steel'. In standard English usage, 'stainless' alone is not commonly used as a noun to refer to stainless steel; the standard noun is 'stainless steel'. The headword 'stainless' by itself is not used in this sense in reputable dictionaries like Oxford or Merriam-Webster. Therefore, this concept should not be included in the dictionary as defined.
