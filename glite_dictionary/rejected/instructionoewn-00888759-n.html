Reason

This synset refers to 'instruction' as the profession of a teacher, but this is not a recognized meaning of the headword 'instruction' in standard English dictionaries, where 'instruction' primarily means 'teaching' or 'the act of teaching' rather than the profession itself (which is usually called 'teaching', 'education', or 'pedagogy'). This sense is too specific and not accurately mapped to the headword. Therefore, it should not be included.
