Reason

The concept 'Tai' with the sense 'of or relating to Thailand' is associated with the word 'Thai', which is the standard form used in English. 'Tai' in this context is not commonly used or recognized in English to mean 'of or relating to Thailand' (instead, it may refer to the Tai ethnic group or language family). The specific synset is too broad/general and not a standard English adjective for 'Thailand'. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate entry for this meaning.
