felt schizo

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: felt schizo|extra.016136
felt disorganized or chaotic

Synset Examples

The plot of the film felt schizo and confusing.

Reason

The concept 'felt schizo' uses 'schizo' as a slang/informal abbreviation for 'schizophrenic,' referring here to something feeling disorganized or chaotic. However, this is both an extremely informal/colloquial usage and potentially considered offensive, especially given the way it trivializes a mental health condition. Additionally, Oxford and similar major dictionaries do not typically include entries for 'felt schizo' as a standalone phrase, nor for this slang extension, due to its rare and inappropriate usage. Therefore, it should not be included in a standard English-English dictionary.
