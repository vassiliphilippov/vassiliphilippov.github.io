Nigerian capital

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08996541-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Nigerian capital|oewn-08996541-n
capital of Nigeria in the center of the country

Reason

The concept 'Nigerian capital' in WordNet refers to 'capital' (the city that is the seat of government), but in this form it is too general, functioning as a descriptive phrase rather than a fixed name or established compound. The dictionary would instead include 'Abuja' as the specific capital of Nigeria, or 'capital' in the general sense. Therefore, this descriptive phrase does not merit a standalone entry.
