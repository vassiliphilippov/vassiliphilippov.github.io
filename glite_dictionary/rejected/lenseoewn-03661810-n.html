lense

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03661810-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: lense|oewn-03661810-n
a transparent optical device used to converge or diverge transmitted light and to form images

Reason

The standard spelling for this optical device is 'lens.' 'Lense' is a common misspelling and is not recognized in major English dictionaries. Therefore, it should be excluded from a formal dictionary like Oxford.
