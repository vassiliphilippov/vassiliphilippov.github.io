vibrate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02711906-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: vibrate|oewn-02711906-v
be undecided about something; waver between conflicting positions or courses of action

Synset Examples

He oscillates between accepting the new position and retirement

Reason

This sense of 'vibrate' as 'be undecided or waver between conflicting positions' is extremely rare, not in current standard usage, and is better conveyed by words like 'vacillate' or 'oscillate'. Modern dictionaries do not list 'vibrate' in this figurative sense. Including it would confuse learners, as it is not a recognized or established meaning in contemporary English.
