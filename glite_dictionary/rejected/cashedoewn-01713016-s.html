cashed

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01713016-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: cashed|oewn-01713016-s
for which money has been paid

Synset Examples

a cashed check

Reason

The sense 'cashed' as 'for which money has been paid' is a participial adjective derived directly from the verb 'cash' and is not a unique lexical entry; it's best covered under the verb 'cash' (as in 'cashed check'). This use is compositional and does not require a separate dictionary entry according to modern lexicographical practice.
