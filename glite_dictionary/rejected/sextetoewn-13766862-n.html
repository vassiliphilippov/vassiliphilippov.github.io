sextet

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13766862-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: sextet|oewn-13766862-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of five and one

Reason

The sense of 'sextet' as a cardinal number (sum of five and one) is too general and not a standard meaning; 'sextet' is not commonly used to refer to the number six itself, but rather to a group of six people or things. The numerical sense is not dictionary-worthy for general English.
