originating outside the body

Reason

This concept uses 'heterogeneous' in a technical sense meaning 'originating outside the body.' However, this sense is not standard English usage for 'heterogeneous.' In mainstream English, 'heterogeneous' means 'diverse in character or content.' The medical sense intended here is actually 'exogenous,' not 'heterogeneous.' Therefore, this concept should not be included under 'heterogeneous.'
