Reason

The term 'toxic skin' is a descriptive phrase rather than a set compound or established term included in major English dictionaries. Its sense is compositional (meaning is transparently 'skin that is toxic'), and it does not warrant a dictionary entry as an independent headword. The concept is too general and would not appear as a separate headword in Oxford or similar dictionaries.
