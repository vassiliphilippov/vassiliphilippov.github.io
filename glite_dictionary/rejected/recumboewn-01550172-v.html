recumb

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01550172-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: recumb|oewn-01550172-v
lean in a comfortable resting position

Synset Examples

He was reposing on the couch

Reason

The verb 'recumb' is not in standard modern English usage; it is largely obsolete and does not appear as a current English verb in reputable dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary. Modern English uses 'recline' or 'lie' for this sense. Thus, it should not be included.
