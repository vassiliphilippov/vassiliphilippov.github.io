Reason

Although 'sacredly' is a plausible adverbial form of 'sacred', it is extremely rare and not commonly used in contemporary or even literary English. The provided sense ('by religion') is more accurately represented by the adverb 'religiously'. Standard dictionaries do not include 'sacredly' as a distinct entry because its use is nearly nonexistent and redundant given 'religiously'. Therefore, it should not be included as a standalone dictionary entry.
