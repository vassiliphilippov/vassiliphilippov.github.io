Synset POS: r
Internal ID: justifiedly|oewn-00206702-r
with honesty

Synset Examples

he was rightly considered the greatest singer of his time

Reason

The adverb 'justifiedly' is extremely rare and almost never used in modern English. The intended sense is better covered by 'rightly' or 'justifiably,' which are standard and much more commonly used. This form would not appear in the Oxford Dictionary or in normal usage.
