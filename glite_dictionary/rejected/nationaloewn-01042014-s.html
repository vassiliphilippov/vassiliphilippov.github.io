national

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01042014-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: national|oewn-01042014-s
inside the country

Synset Examples

the British Home Office has broader responsibilities than the United States Department of the Interior
the nation's internal politics

Reason

This sense of 'national' is synonymous with 'internal' or 'domestic' meaning 'inside the country,' but 'national' is not commonly or directly used with this meaning in standard usage. Instead, 'domestic' or 'internal' are the typical adjectives for this concept. Thus, this sense is too broad/general and not distinct enough to merit its own entry under 'national' in a dictionary.
