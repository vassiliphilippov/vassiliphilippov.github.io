calculator

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09906486-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: calculator|oewn-09906486-n
an expert at calculation (or at operating calculating machines)

Reason

This sense of 'calculator' as a person who is an expert at calculation or operating calculating machines is now rare and archaic. In modern English, 'calculator' almost exclusively refers to the electronic device. The sense as a person would not typically appear as a separate entry in a contemporary dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary.
