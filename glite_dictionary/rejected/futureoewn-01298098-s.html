(of elected officers) elected but not yet serving

Synset Examples

our next president

Reason

This sense of 'future' as an adjective meaning 'elected but not yet serving' appears only in highly limited, attributive, or technical contexts and would not have its own lemma entry in a general dictionary. The example 'our next president' uses 'next', not 'future', and 'future president' is more commonly defined as 'someone who will be president' rather than specifically 'elected but not yet serving'. Thus, this sense is too narrow and does not merit its own dictionary entry.
