Chinese Communist Revolution

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01306073-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Chinese Communist Revolution|oewn-01306073-n
the republican revolution against the Manchu dynasty in China; 1911-1912

Reason

The sense description refers to the republican revolution against the Manchu dynasty (1911-1912), which is commonly known as the Xinhai Revolution or the Chinese Revolution of 1911, not the 'Chinese Communist Revolution.' The 'Chinese Communist Revolution' refers to a different event (the overthrow of the Nationalists by the Communists, resulting in the founding of the People's Republic of China in 1949). Therefore, the synset here is mismatched with the headword and is too general or incorrect for inclusion as a dictionary entry under this name.
