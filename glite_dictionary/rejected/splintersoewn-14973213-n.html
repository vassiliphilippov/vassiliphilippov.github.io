splinters

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14973213-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: splinters|oewn-14973213-n
wood in small pieces or splinters

Synset Examples

the vessel was beaten to matchwood on the rocks

Reason

The synset refers to 'splinters' in the general sense of small wood pieces or fragments. However, this is already covered by the dictionary entry for 'splinter' (noun: a small, thin, sharp piece of wood, glass, or similar material broken off from a larger piece), and the plural 'splinters' does not have a separate, distinct meaning. Therefore, this concept does not merit its own entry.
