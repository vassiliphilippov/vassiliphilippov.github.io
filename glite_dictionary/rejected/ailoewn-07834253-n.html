aromatic bulb used as seasoning

Reason

The term "ail" in this sense refers to the French word for garlic. In English, "ail" is not used to mean "garlic" or "aromatic bulb used as seasoning"; instead, the headword for this meaning in English would be "garlic." Including this sense would be confusing, as "ail" is not an established English word for this concept.
