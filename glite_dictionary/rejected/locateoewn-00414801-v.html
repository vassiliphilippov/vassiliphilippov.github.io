locate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00414801-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: locate|oewn-00414801-v
take up residence and become established

Synset Examples

The immigrants settled in the Midwest

Reason

This sense of 'locate' as 'to take up residence and become established' is obsolete or extremely rare in modern English. Contemporary dictionaries do not recognize this sense of 'locate'; the example ('settle in the Midwest') is better described by 'settle', not 'locate'. Therefore, it should not be included.
