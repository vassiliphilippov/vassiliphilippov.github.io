Reason

The synset is for 'telling' as a noun meaning 'the act of disclosing information or giving evidence about another', which describes the act of 'telling on someone', but 'telling' in this exact noun sense is not standard or widely recognized as a countable noun in modern English. It does not appear as a noun with this specific sense in major dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. Therefore, this meaning is too peripheral and not suitable as a separate dictionary headword.
