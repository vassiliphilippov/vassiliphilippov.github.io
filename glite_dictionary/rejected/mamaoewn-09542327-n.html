Mama

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09542327-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Mama|oewn-09542327-n
a name under which Ninkhursag was worshipped, meaning ‘Mother’

Reason

This sense of 'Mama' refers specifically to an ancient deity (Ninkhursag) being worshipped under the name 'Mama,' primarily in Sumerian mythology. This is too specialized and not commonly used in modern English. Dictionaries like Oxford typically do not include such minor ancient names unless they have general usage or significant recognition in English. The general sense of 'mama' meaning 'mother' is already covered, but not this deity-specific usage.
