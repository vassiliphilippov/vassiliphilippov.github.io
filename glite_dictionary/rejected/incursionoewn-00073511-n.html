incursion

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00073511-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: incursion|oewn-00073511-n
the mistake of incurring liability or blame

Reason

This sense of 'incursion' (as 'the mistake of incurring liability or blame') is not a recognized or standard usage in modern English. The standard sense of 'incursion' refers to a sudden entry or invasion, usually by force. This concept would not appear in Oxford or similar dictionaries for this meaning.
