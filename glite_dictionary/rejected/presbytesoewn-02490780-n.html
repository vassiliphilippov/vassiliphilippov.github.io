Reason

The concept 'Presbytes' refers to a former genus classification for langurs and now appears chiefly in scientific contexts as a taxonomic synonym. Standard English dictionaries (such as Oxford) do not include outdated or purely scientific genus names unless widely recognized as common vocabulary, which 'Presbytes' is not; it is not a term with general English usage. The general English entry for langur is already covered.
