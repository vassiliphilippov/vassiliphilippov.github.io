grant

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06538610-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: grant|oewn-06538610-n
a contract granting the right to operate a subsidiary business

Synset Examples

he got the beer concession at the ball park

Reason

This synset describes 'grant' as a contract to operate a subsidiary business, but in contemporary English dictionaries, 'grant' most commonly refers to a financial award, permission, or legal conveyance, not this specialized business usage which is more accurately described as a 'concession.' This sense is too specific and is not in common use for the headword 'grant.'
