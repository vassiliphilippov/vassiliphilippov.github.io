retarded in intellectual development

Reason

While this sense of 'backward' (meaning 'retarded in intellectual development') has historically appeared in dictionaries, it is now considered outdated and potentially offensive. Modern English dictionaries do not generally define 'backward' in this way due to its problematic and stigmatizing connotations. Therefore, this sense should be excluded from an up-to-date learner's dictionary.
