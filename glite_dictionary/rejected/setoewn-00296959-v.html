The examples provided do not use the word 'set' itself, but rather 'adjust' and 'correct.' Since 'set' in this sense is not the most common or primary usage and would be covered under core meanings of 'adjust' or 'set' already included, this particular sense is too generic and indirect for a focused dictionary entry.
