Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a

Internal ID: orch dork|extra.020710
a passionate enthusiast of orchids

Synset Examples

Being an orch dork means loving all things orchid.

Reason

The term "orch dork" refers specifically to a passionate enthusiast of orchids in this synset; however, this is an extremely rare and non-standard usage. The commonly attested slang "orch dork" in English actually refers to a student who is very enthusiastic about playing in an orchestra. This meaning (orchid enthusiast) is not established or widespread enough to merit inclusion in a standard dictionary like Oxford, nor is it found in major reference resources. Therefore, it should be excluded.
