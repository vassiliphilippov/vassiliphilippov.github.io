untimely

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00431857-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: untimely|oewn-00431857-r
too soon; in a premature manner

Synset Examples

I spoke prematurely

Reason

The adverbial sense of 'untimely' meaning 'prematurely' is extremely rare and not current in modern English (the standard adverb is 'prematurely'). This sense would not appear as a main entry or significant subentry in general dictionaries like Oxford; 'untimely' is overwhelmingly used as an adjective.
