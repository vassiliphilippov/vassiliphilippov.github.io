sponginess

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04945651-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: sponginess|oewn-04945651-n
the property of being able to occupy less space

Reason

The given sense describes 'sponginess' as 'the property of being able to occupy less space,' which confuses it with 'compressibility.' The standard dictionary meaning of 'sponginess' relates to being soft or absorbent like a sponge, not the ability to occupy less space. This usage is not common or standard, so it should not be included as a separate sense in a general English dictionary.
