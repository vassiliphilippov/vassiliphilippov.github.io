cartouche

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02974932-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: cartouche|oewn-02974932-n
a cartridge (usually with paper casing)

Reason

This sense of 'cartouche' as a cartridge is highly archaic and obsolete, and primarily a technical/historical synonym. The primary and familiar senses of 'cartouche' are (1) an oblong figure enclosing hieroglyphs in ancient Egypt and (2) a decorative oval panel. This particular sense ('a cartridge with a paper casing') is too rare and specific for inclusion in a standard dictionary like Oxford, and would not merit its own entry.
