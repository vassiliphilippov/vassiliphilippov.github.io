canvass

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02957599-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: canvass|oewn-02957599-n
a tent made of canvas fabric

Reason

This concept should be excluded. The sense described ('a tent made of canvas fabric') is not a primary, independent meaning of 'canvass' but rather a specific application of 'canvas' (the fabric). 'Canvass' as a noun for 'a tent' is not a standard, attested meaning in modern English nor would it appear as a headword sense in dictionaries such as Oxford; this seems to be a confusion with 'canvas.' Therefore, this concept is filtered out.
