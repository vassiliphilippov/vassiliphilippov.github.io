Synset Examples

Reason

The phrase 'avoid the flamer' is a compositional phrase and not an idiom, compound, or established phrasal verb; it simply means to stay away from a 'flamer' (a person who posts hostile messages online). It does not have a meaning beyond the sum of its parts and would not appear as a headword or expression in standard dictionaries.
