unmanly

Internal ID: unmanly|oewn-00392720-r
without qualities thought to befit a man

Reason

The sense of 'unmanly' provided is listed as an adverb (r), but 'unmanly' is not naturally used as an adverb in English; its typical and meaningful use is as an adjective ('not having qualities traditionally associated with men'). The adverbial use is not established in natural English and would not appear as a standalone entry in Oxford Dictionary. Adjective sense should be included, but not this adverb form.
