dingy

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00365961-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: dingy|oewn-00365961-s
causing dejection

Synset Examples

a blue day
the dark days of the war
a week of rainy depressing weather
a disconsolate winter landscape
the first dismal dispiriting days of November
a dark gloomy day
grim rainy weather

Reason

This sense of 'dingy' as 'causing dejection' is not standard; the main dictionary sense is 'dirty' or 'dull in color.' The synset meaning overlaps with 'depressing,' 'gloomy,' etc., but 'dingy' is not commonly used in this way in English dictionaries. Therefore, it should not be included for this sense.
