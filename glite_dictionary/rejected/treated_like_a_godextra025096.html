treated like a god

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: treated like a god|extra.025096
to pamper or excessively care for someone

Synset Examples

She treated her pet like a god.

Reason

The phrase 'treated like a god' is an idiomatic expression but not a fixed idiom or established phrasal verb; it is transparent in meaning ('like a god' is a common simile construction). Such ad hoc similes are not typically included as headwords or phrases in major dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary.
