Reason

This sense of 'run-down' ('having the spring unwound') is extremely narrow, technical, and not commonly encountered in modern general English. It is specific to mechanical (spring-driven) devices and is now rare. As such, this sense would not appear as a separate entry in a standard contemporary learner's dictionary.
