Synset POS: v
Internal ID: congratulate|oewn-01776419-v
be proud of

Synset Examples

He prides himself on making it into law school

Reason

This sense is not properly related to the headword 'congratulate'; it actually represents the meaning 'be proud of' or 'pride oneself', not 'congratulate' as commonly used in English. The Oxford Dictionary does not include 'congratulate' with this meaning.
