pour

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02074224-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: pour|oewn-02074224-v
pour out gradually, so as to separate out sediment

Synset Examples

the sommelier decanted the wines

Reason

This concept represents a very specific, technical sense related to the process of decanting liquid to separate sediment. In English dictionaries, 'pour' as a verb broadly covers this meaning, but this extremely specific sense (to pour out gradually specifically to separate out sediment) is more commonly referred to as 'decant'. Therefore, this sense of 'pour' would not be included as its own dictionary entry, as it is subsumed under 'decant' and not a primary or distinctive usage of 'pour' itself.
