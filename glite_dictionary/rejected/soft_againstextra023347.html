Synset Examples

Reason

The phrase 'soft against' is a compositional phrase meaning 'soft when touching something'; it's not a set phrase or idiom, nor does it have a meaning separate from the literal meanings of 'soft' and 'against.' Such compositional phrases are generally not included in major dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary.
