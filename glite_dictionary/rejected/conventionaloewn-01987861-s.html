Reason

This sense of 'conventional' as 'represented in simplified or symbolic form' is extremely rare and is mostly technical (used in phrases like 'conventional representation' in mathematics or science). The primary and well-known senses of 'conventional' relate to customs, tradition, or being standard. This minor technical sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry for general learners.
