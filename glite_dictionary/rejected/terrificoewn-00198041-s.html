Reason

This sense of 'terrific' as 'causing extreme terror' is now archaic or obsolete in modern English. In contemporary English, 'terrific' is almost always used to mean 'excellent' or 'very good.' The 'causing terror' sense would not appear as a current or primary entry in a modern English dictionary like Oxford, except possibly as a historical note.
