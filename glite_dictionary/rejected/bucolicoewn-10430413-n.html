a country person

Reason

The noun sense of 'bucolic' meaning 'a country person' is archaic and extremely rare in modern usage, far less common than the adjective sense ('relating to the pleasant aspects of the countryside'). It does not commonly appear in dictionaries in this sense and would not be expected in a contemporary English dictionary except perhaps as a historical note.
