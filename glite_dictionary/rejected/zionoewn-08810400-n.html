Reason

As a place-name, it does not warrant a dictionary entry beyond its use as a proper noun unless covering its metaphorical, religious, or historical senses. Including this as a dictionary entry would not match standard practice for major dictionaries, which do not list place names (countries) unless they have significant metaphorical or broader uses.
