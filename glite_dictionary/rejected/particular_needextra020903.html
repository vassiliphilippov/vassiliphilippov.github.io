particular need

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: particular need|extra.020903
specific requirements or preferences

Synset Examples

They designed the room for one's particular needs.

Reason

The phrase 'particular need' refers to the general idea of having a specific requirement, but it is a transparent combination of basic words ('particular' + 'need') rather than a fixed compound or idiom. There is no unique or idiomatic meaning beyond the sum of its parts, so it would not be included as a separate entry in most dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary.
