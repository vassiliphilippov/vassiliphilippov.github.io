being ten more than forty

Reason

The concept refers to 'l' as the sense of 'being ten more than forty', which is a Roman numeral representation (i.e., 'L' for 50). However, 'l' on its own as a lower-case letter is not used in modern English as a word for '50'; 'L' as a Roman numeral might be included, but 'l' in this number sense is too technical and not standard usage for a dictionary entry. It is not a headword with this meaning in ordinary English dictionaries.
