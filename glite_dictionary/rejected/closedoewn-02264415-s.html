Reason

This sense is too narrow in usage and functions as a predicative adjective only when contextually obvious. The standalone adjective 'closed' already covers general senses, including things with shutters closed; it does not have a distinct, dictionary-worthy use referring only and specifically to windows or shutters that justifies a separate entry.
