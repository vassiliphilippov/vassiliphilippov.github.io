Reason

The phrase 'nitty aspect' is not an established or recognized compound in standard English dictionaries. It appears to be an ad hoc or misused variation of the expression 'nitty-gritty,' which refers to the important details or essentials. 'Nitty aspect' would not appear as a headword in dictionaries such as Oxford; thus, it should be excluded.
