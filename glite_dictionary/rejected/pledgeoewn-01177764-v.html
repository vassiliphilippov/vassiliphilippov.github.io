Reason

This sense of 'pledge' (meaning 'propose a toast to' or 'toast') is not a standard or common usage in modern English. The standard verb for this concept is 'toast,' and 'pledge' is not typically used this way in contemporary English, nor is it recognized as such in authoritative dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate entry with this sense.
