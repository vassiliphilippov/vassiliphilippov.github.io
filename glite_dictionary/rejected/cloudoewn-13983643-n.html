cloud

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13983643-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: cloud|oewn-13983643-n
out of touch with reality

Synset Examples

his head was in the clouds

Reason

The synset refers to the figurative use of 'cloud' meaning 'out of touch with reality,' but in English dictionaries this usage is usually only found within the idiomatic expressions such as 'head in the clouds' rather than as an independent noun sense. As a standalone noun with this meaning, it is too context-dependent and is not attested outside of fixed phrases. Therefore, this sense does not warrant its own dictionary entry.
