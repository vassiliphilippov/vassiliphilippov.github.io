nervii olfactorii

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05485598-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: nervii olfactorii|oewn-05485598-n
a collective term for numerous olfactory filaments in the nasal mucosa

Reason

The term 'nervii olfactorii' is a direct Latin anatomical term and is not used in standard English dictionaries. The usual English term is 'olfactory nerves', which is already covered. Therefore, it should be excluded.
