marv at

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: marv at|extra.006574
to express amazement or admiration

Synset Examples

They marv at the incredible sunset from the hill.
I couldn't help but marv at her artistic talent.
He always marv at the beauty of nature.

Reason

The verb 'marv' is not a standard English word. It appears to be a nonstandard or truncated form of 'marvel,' which is the correct verb for expressing amazement or admiration. 'Marv' in this sense would not appear in Oxford Dictionary and is not widely recognized. Therefore, it should be excluded from the dictionary.
