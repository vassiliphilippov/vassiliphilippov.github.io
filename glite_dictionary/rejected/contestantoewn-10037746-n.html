contestant

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10037746-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: contestant|oewn-10037746-n
a person who dissents from some established policy

Reason

This sense of 'contestant' (a person who dissents from some established policy) is not standard or attested in major English dictionaries. The word 'contestant' commonly refers to a person who takes part in a competition or contest, not a dissenter or opponent of policy. Therefore, this concept should not be included in the dictionary.
