coming

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00281697-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: coming|oewn-00281697-n
the act of drawing spatially closer to something

Synset Examples

the hunter's approach scattered the geese

Reason

The sense described, 'coming' as 'the act of drawing spatially closer to something,' is better represented in English by the noun 'approach.' The use of 'coming' as a noun in this sense is extremely rare and nonstandard; it would not appear in Oxford or other general English dictionaries in this meaning. This concept is therefore excluded.
