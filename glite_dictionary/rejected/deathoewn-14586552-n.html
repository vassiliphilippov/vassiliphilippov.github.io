death

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14586552-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: death|oewn-14586552-n
a final state

Synset Examples

he came to a bad end
the so-called glorious experiment came to an inglorious end

Reason

This synset refers to an extremely generic and abstract sense ('a final state') that applies to any kind of ending, not specifically related to 'death' as a word. In this use, words like 'end' or 'termination' are more accurate headwords for the concept, not 'death.' Therefore, this sense should not be included as a dictionary entry for 'death.'
