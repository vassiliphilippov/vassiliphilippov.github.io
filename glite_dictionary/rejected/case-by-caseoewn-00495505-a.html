case-by-case

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00495505-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: case-by-case|oewn-00495505-a
being or characteristic of a single thing or person

Synset Examples

individual drops of rain
please mark the individual pages
they went their individual ways

Reason

The synset sense is 'being or characteristic of a single thing or person', which matches words like 'individual' or 'separate', not 'case-by-case'. In ordinary English, 'case-by-case' means 'considering each case separately', but this is not the sense in the examples. The connection between the headword 'case-by-case' and the synset is too loose/general; it's not the correct match for a dictionary entry.
