pagan

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10187776-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: pagan|oewn-10187776-n
someone motivated by desires for sensual pleasures

Reason

This sense of 'pagan' as 'someone motivated by desires for sensual pleasures' is archaic and not commonly used in modern English. The primary, relevant dictionary senses of 'pagan' are 'a person holding religious beliefs other than those of the main world religions' or 'a person not subscribing to any major world religion.' This secondary sense is best covered by words like 'hedonist' or 'sensualist.' Therefore, it should not be included as a standalone dictionary entry for 'pagan.'
