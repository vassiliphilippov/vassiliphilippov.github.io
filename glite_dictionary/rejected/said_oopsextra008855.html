said oops

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: said oops|extra.008855
expressed regret or acknowledgment of a mistake

Synset Examples

He laughed and said oops when he made a mistake.
She said oops after spilling her drink.

Reason

The phrase 'said oops' is not a dictionary headword or a set phrase with distinct meaning; it is simply the verb 'said' followed by the interjection 'oops'. Only 'oops' would merit an entry as an interjection expressing a minor mistake. Therefore, this concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
