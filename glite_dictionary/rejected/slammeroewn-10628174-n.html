slammer

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10628174-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: slammer|oewn-10628174-n
a person who closes things violently

Synset Examples

she's a dramatic slammer of doors

Reason

The synset describes 'slammer' as 'a person who closes things violently.' While theoretically possible, this usage is rare and not standard; 'slammer' is not commonly used to refer to a person in this way in standard English. The widely recognized meaning of 'slammer' is slang for 'prison.' Therefore, this specific sense should be excluded as it would not appear in a major dictionary like Oxford under this definition.
