Synset Examples

Reason

The sense of 'mix' meaning 'to open (a place) to members of all races and ethnic groups' is very rare and not used in modern English; it does not meet the criteria for inclusion as a main dictionary sense. The much more common verb for this sense is 'desegregate.' 'Mix' is not typically used or recognized in this way.
