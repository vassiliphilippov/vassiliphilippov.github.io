Synset Examples

Reason

This sense of 'stabile' (meaning not able or intended to be moved) is too rare and not commonly found in modern English dictionaries. The standard word for this concept is 'immovable' or 'stationary'. 'Stabile' is primarily used as a noun for a type of sculpture, and the adjective 'stabile' is rarely encountered in this sense.
