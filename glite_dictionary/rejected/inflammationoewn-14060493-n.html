Synset Examples

Reason

This sense of 'inflammation' as 'the state of being emotionally aroused and worked up' is extremely rare and not standard in contemporary English. In dictionaries like Oxford or Merriam-Webster, 'inflammation' is overwhelmingly used for the medical/physical sense, and the emotional/arousal sense would be too obscure and not suitable for inclusion.
