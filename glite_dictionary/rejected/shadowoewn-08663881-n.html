shadow

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08663881-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: shadow|oewn-08663881-n
an unilluminated area

Synset Examples

he moved off into the darkness

Reason

This synset for 'shadow' refers to a very general sense of 'an unilluminated area', which in this example is better described by the word 'darkness'. It is too broad and not distinct to 'shadow' as commonly used and would not be included as an independent dictionary entry under this meaning. Instead, standard dictionaries cover 'shadow' as 'a dark area or shape cast by an object blocking light'.
