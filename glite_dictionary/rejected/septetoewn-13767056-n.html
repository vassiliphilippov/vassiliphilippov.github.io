septet

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13767056-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: septet|oewn-13767056-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of six and one

Reason

This concept refers to 'septet' as the cardinal number seven, but in common English usage, 'septet' is not used as a numeral; it refers to a group of seven people, especially musicians or singers. The concept of 'septet' as a number is too general and is not a valid dictionary entry for the cardinal number. The actual word for the number is 'seven.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
