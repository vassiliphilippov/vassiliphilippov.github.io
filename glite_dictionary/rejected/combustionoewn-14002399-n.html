combustion

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14002399-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: combustion|oewn-14002399-n
a state of violent disturbance and excitement

Synset Examples

combustion grew until revolt was unavoidable

Reason

This sense of 'combustion' as a 'state of violent disturbance and excitement' is rare, metaphorical, and not commonly attested in modern English dictionaries. The standard usage of 'combustion' refers to burning or rapid chemical oxidation, which is the sense most readers expect. This metaphorical sense would not typically appear in modern dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary, so it should be excluded.
