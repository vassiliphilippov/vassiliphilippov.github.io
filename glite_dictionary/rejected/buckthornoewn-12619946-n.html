buckthorn

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-12619946-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: buckthorn|oewn-12619946-n
an Old World plantain with long narrow ribbed leaves widely established in temperate regions

Reason

This sense describes 'buckthorn' as a type of plantain, which is incorrect and conflates two unrelated plants. The actual 'buckthorn' refers to shrubs of the genus Rhamnus, while the plantain is a separate genus. Including this misleading and incorrect plant sense would cause confusion and is not suitable for a dictionary entry.
