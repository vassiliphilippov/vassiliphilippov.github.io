explain a riddle

Reason

The verb sense 'to riddle' meaning 'to explain a riddle' is extremely rare, archaic, and not used in modern English. Current dictionary treatment does not list 'riddle' as a verb in this sense. The standard verbs are 'solve a riddle' or 'answer a riddle.' Therefore, this sense should be excluded from the dictionary.
