transportation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13329169-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: transportation|oewn-13329169-n
the sum charged for riding in a public conveyance

Reason

This sense of 'transportation' as 'the sum charged for riding in a public conveyance' is outdated and not idiomatic in contemporary English. The common term for this is 'fare.' This sense would not be included in a modern learner's dictionary.
