session

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08434877-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: session|oewn-08434877-n
a meeting of spiritualists

Synset Examples

the seance was held in the medium's parlor

Reason

This sense of 'session'—meaning 'a meeting of spiritualists'—is too narrow and is typically covered under the more specific term 'séance.' The broader meaning of 'session' (a period of time devoted to a particular activity or meeting) is included in dictionaries, but not this highly specialized spiritualist sense as its own entry. It does not warrant a separate sense in a general English dictionary.
