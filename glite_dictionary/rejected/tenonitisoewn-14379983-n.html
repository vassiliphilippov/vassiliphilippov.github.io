tenonitis

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14379983-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: tenonitis|oewn-14379983-n
inflammation of a tendon

Reason

Tenonitis appears to be a misspelling or misentry for 'tendinitis,' which is the correct medical term for inflammation of a tendon. The entry is not standard or recognized in reputable dictionaries, so it should not be included.
