dangling

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01019643-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: dangling|oewn-01019643-n
the act of suspending something (hanging it from above so it moves freely)

Synset Examples

there was a small ceremony for the hanging of the portrait

Reason

The concept here refers to 'dangling' as a noun meaning 'the act of suspending something,' but this is not a standard or established noun usage in contemporary English. The standard noun form for this sense is 'hanging.' Therefore, it would not warrant its own dictionary entry for 'dangling' as a noun with this meaning.
