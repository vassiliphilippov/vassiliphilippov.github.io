IC

Synset POS: n

a group of government agencies and organizations that carry out intelligence activities for the United States government; headed by the Director of Central Intelligence

Reason

'IC' in this sense is an abbreviation for 'Intelligence Community' and functions mainly as a specialized government acronym. Although used within intelligence and government sectors, it is not widely established in general English dictionaries like Oxford except as a technical or governmental abbreviation. Therefore, it does not meet the criteria for inclusion as a main dictionary headword.
