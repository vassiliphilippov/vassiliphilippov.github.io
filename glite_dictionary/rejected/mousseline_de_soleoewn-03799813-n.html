mousseline de sole

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03799813-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: mousseline de sole|oewn-03799813-n
a gauze-like fabric of silk or rayon

Reason

The synset definition "a gauze-like fabric of silk or rayon" does not accurately correspond to "mousseline de sole" as this term in English typically refers to a French dish (a type of fish mousse), not a fabric. Including as a fabric would be misleading and not typically found as such in reputable English dictionaries. Therefore, the concept is excluded.
