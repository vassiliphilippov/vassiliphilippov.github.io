conjugation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00382488-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: conjugation|oewn-00382488-n
the act of making or becoming a single unit

Synset Examples

the union of opposing factions
he looked forward to the unification of his family for the holidays

Reason

Although 'conjugation' can technically refer to union or joining, this sense is highly rare and is not standardly used for 'conjugation' in contemporary English. The canonical senses of 'conjugation' are grammatical or biological, not general union. Therefore, this concept should not be included.
