seal limbs

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14490151-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: seal limbs|oewn-14490151-n
an abnormality of development in which the upper part of an arm or leg is missing so the hands or feet are attached to the body like stumps; rare condition that results from taking thalidomide during pregnancy

Reason

The synset refers to a rare developmental abnormality but 'seal limbs' is not an established dictionary headword or recognized medical term. Standard dictionaries would not include this term as a standalone entry; the condition is usually referred to as 'phocomelia'. Therefore, it should be excluded.
