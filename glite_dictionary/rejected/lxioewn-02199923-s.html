lxi

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02199923-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: lxi|oewn-02199923-s
being one more than sixty

Reason

This concept refers to the Roman numeral 'LXI' representing the number 61. Roman numerals may appear in dictionaries, but 'lxi' as 'being one more than sixty' is too specific and not a word in normal English usage. The dictionary should instead have an entry for 'LXI' as a Roman numeral, not as an adjective meaning sixty-one.
