Felis onca

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02131577-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Felis onca|oewn-02131577-n
a large spotted feline of tropical America similar to the leopard; in some classifications considered a member of the genus Felis

Reason

'Felis onca' refers to the scientific classification (outdated) of the animal commonly known as the jaguar. Dictionary entries feature the common English name ('jaguar'), not the Latin binomial, unless the Latin term is widely used in English, which 'Felis onca' is not. Therefore, this concept should not be included in the dictionary.
