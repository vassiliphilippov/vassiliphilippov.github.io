commonwealth

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08185877-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: commonwealth|oewn-08185877-n
a politically organized body of people under a single government

Synset Examples

the state has elected a new president
African nations
students who had come to the nation's capitol
the country's largest manufacturer
an industrialized land

Reason

This synset is too general and not specifically related to the headword 'commonwealth'; it refers to any politically organized body of people (such as 'state,' 'country,' 'nation'), which is an overgeneralization and would not have its own entry in a standard dictionary under 'commonwealth' with this meaning.
