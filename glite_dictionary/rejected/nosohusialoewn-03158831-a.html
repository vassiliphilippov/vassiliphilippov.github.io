Reason

The term 'nosohusial' is not an established or widely recognized term in English, nor would it appear in reputable dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary. It appears to be a neologism or an attempt to analogize with 'nosocomial' (hospital-acquired), but there is no significant evidence of its general acceptance or usage in English. Therefore, it should not be included in the dictionary.
