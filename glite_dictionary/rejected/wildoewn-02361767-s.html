Reason

This sense of 'wild' as 'without a basis in reason or fact' is very rare and not commonly used in general English; terms like 'baseless,' 'unfounded,' or 'groundless' are standard. Oxford and major dictionaries do not list 'wild' with this meaning as a typical usage. Therefore, the concept should not be included.
