determinative

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05700384-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: determinative|oewn-05700384-n
a determining or causal element or factor

Synset Examples

education is an important determinant of one's outlook on life

Reason

Although 'determinative' is a valid English word (noun and adjective), this sense as a noun meaning 'a determining or causal element or factor' is rarely used in contemporary English; the much more common term is 'determinant.' 'Determinative' is primarily found in specialized linguistic contexts (for an adjective or as a term in some grammar discussions) but not commonly as a noun in general English. Therefore, this noun concept does not merit inclusion as a standalone dictionary entry for general users.
