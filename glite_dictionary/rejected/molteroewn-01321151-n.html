molter

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01321151-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: molter|oewn-01321151-n
an animal (especially birds and arthropods and reptiles) that periodically shed their outer layer (feathers or cuticle or skin or hair)

Reason

The noun 'molter' meaning an animal that molts is not commonly used in standard English and would not appear in leading dictionaries like Oxford. The more standard terminology is 'a molting animal' or simply 'the animal molts.' The concept is artificial and too rare for inclusion.
