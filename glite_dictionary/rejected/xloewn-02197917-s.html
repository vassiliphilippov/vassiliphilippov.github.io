being ten more than thirty

Reason

The concept 'xl' as 'being ten more than thirty' refers to the Roman numeral representation of the number 40. The sense is not specific to 'xl' as a word but to its use as a numeral symbol. Dictionaries like Oxford list Roman numerals collectively and do not give separate entries for each numeral unless it is also used as a word or abbreviation. Since this concept is too general and not lexicalized as an English word, it should be excluded.
