quaternion

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13766444-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: quaternion|oewn-13766444-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of three and one

Reason

The term 'quaternion' in the sense of 'the cardinal number that is the sum of three and one' is archaic and not used in contemporary English for the number four. Modern dictionaries do not typically include this number sense for 'quaternion', as 'four' is the standard term. The more relevant sense of 'quaternion' relating to mathematics (a number system extending complex numbers) would be included instead.
