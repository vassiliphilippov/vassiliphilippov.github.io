video

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06288645-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: video|oewn-06288645-n
(computer science) the appearance of text and graphics on a video display

Reason

This sense of 'video' refers specifically to the appearance of text and graphics on a computer display, which is a very technical usage and not a common lexical meaning one would find in a general English dictionary. General dictionaries treat 'video' as a recording or broadcast of moving visual images, not the display's generic output. Therefore, this sense is too technical and specific for inclusion.
