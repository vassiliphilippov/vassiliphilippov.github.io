spirit

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04632183-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: spirit|oewn-04632183-n
an inclination or tendency of a certain kind

Synset Examples

he had a change of heart

Reason

The synset refers to 'spirit' in the very abstract sense of 'inclination or tendency,' which is overly general and not a common standalone dictionary sense of the word. In this specific meaning, it appears more as part of set phrases (e.g., 'change of heart' meaning a change of inclination), rather than as a discrete dictionary sense of 'spirit.' Such usage does not commonly appear as a main sense in dictionaries like Oxford and would confuse learners seeking more concrete or idiomatic uses of 'spirit.'
