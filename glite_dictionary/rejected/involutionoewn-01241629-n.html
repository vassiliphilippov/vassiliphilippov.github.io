involution

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01241629-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: involution|oewn-01241629-n
the act of sharing in the activities of a group

Synset Examples

the teacher tried to increase his students' engagement in class activities

Reason

This sense of 'involution'—meaning 'the act of sharing in the activities of a group'—is not standard, and does not accurately reflect recognized or common usage in English. The proper term for this meaning is 'involvement' or 'engagement.' 'Involution' in dictionaries refers to complexity, the act of turning inwards, or in biological contexts, but not participation in group activities. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a dictionary entry for 'involution.'
