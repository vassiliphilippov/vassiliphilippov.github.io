Unless the place itself is notable for linguistic or cultural reasons, standard dictionaries like Oxford typically do not include ordinary place names, as their inclusion is reserved for proper nouns of exceptional significance or widespread reference. This is more appropriate for an encyclopedia than for a general English dictionary.
