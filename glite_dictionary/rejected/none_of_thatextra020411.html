none of that

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: none of that|extra.020411
indicating the absence of something

Synset Examples

We have none of that product left in stock.

Reason

The phrase 'none of that' in this sense is a compositional phrase formed from 'none' and a demonstrative; its meaning ('the absence of that thing') is clear from the sum of its parts and does not require a standalone dictionary entry. It is not an idiom or set phrase with special meaning beyond the literal words.
