Reason

The concept provided refers to the use of 'here' as a predicative adjective meaning 'present' (e.g., 'everyone here'), but 'here' is primarily an adverb or pronoun. The adjectival use is highly restricted and not typically included as a separate dictionary entry in major dictionaries like Oxford; it is derived from its pronominal/adverbial sense. Therefore, this sense is too grammatical and general, not a standalone dictionary headword.
