verbal

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00551001-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: verbal|oewn-00551001-a
tediously prolonged or tending to speak or write at great length

Synset Examples

editing a prolix manuscript
a prolix lecturer telling you more than you want to know
He gave a good verbal description of the suspect

Reason

This synset is too general and is not a standard meaning for 'verbal'. The sense 'tediously prolonged or tending to speak or write at great length' is better covered by words like 'prolix' or 'verbose'. 'Verbal' in English typically means 'relating to words' or 'spoken rather than written,' and that is the main definition in major dictionaries. Therefore, this particular synset does not warrant a separate entry for 'verbal' in a standard English-English dictionary.
