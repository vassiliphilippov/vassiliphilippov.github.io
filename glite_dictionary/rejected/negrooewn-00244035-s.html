negro

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00244035-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: negro|oewn-00244035-s
having skin rich in melanin pigments;

Reason

This synset relates to the descriptor meaning 'having skin rich in melanin pigments', but the use of 'negro' in this sense is archaic and now considered offensive. Modern dictionaries generally only include 'Negro' as a historical/ethnic noun or adjective, with careful labeling, not for the broad description of skin pigmentation. The scientific/medical use is obsolete, and inclusion today would be inappropriate except for historical reference, and better handled via the more relevant noun/adjective senses. Therefore, this adjectival sense should be excluded.
