revolutionary

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02951256-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: revolutionary|oewn-02951256-a
of or relating to or characteristic or causing an axial or orbital turn

Reason

This sense of 'revolutionary' is highly technical and pertains specifically to something 'of or relating to an axial or orbital turn.' This usage is extremely rare, technical, and not typically found in general English dictionaries such as Oxford. In general use, 'revolutionary' almost always refers to political upheaval or dramatic change, not to motion around an axis or orbit. The general adjective sense tied to 'revolution' is covered, and the axial-orbital connection is obscure and unnecessary for a general learner's dictionary.
