overthrow

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00718035-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: overthrow|oewn-00718035-v
rule against

Synset Examples

The Republicans were overruled when the House voted on the bill

Reason

Although the verb 'overthrow' exists and is commonly included in dictionaries, this particular synset meaning 'to rule against (a decision)' is an error or highly nonstandard. Standard dictionaries do not define 'overthrow' as 'rule against'; the typical meanings are 'to remove from power' or 'to topple'. Therefore, this sense does not warrant a separate entry.
