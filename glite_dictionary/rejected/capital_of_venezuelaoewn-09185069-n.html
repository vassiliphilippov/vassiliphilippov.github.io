Reason

The synset 'capital and largest city of Venezuela' refers to a too-general concept describing a place, not the specific name 'Caracas.' This general phrase would not appear as a dictionary headword in dictionaries like Oxford. Only the actual proper noun 'Caracas' should be included as a headword for this meaning.
