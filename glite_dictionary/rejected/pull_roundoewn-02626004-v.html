pull round

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02626004-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: pull round|oewn-02626004-v
continue in existence after (an adversity, etc.)

Synset Examples

He survived the cancer against all odds

Reason

The synset describes the general sense of "continue in existence after adversity". However, the phrase "pull round" in this sense is not established or common usage in standard English; instead, 'pull through' is the usual phrasal verb for surviving hardship or illness. It would not appear as 'pull round' with this sense in an Oxford-style dictionary, so we exclude it.
