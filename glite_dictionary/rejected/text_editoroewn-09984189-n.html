text editor

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09984189-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: text editor|oewn-09984189-n
an editor who prepares text for publication

Reason

Although 'text editor' is a valid compound noun, this particular sense ('an editor who prepares text for publication') is too generic, referring simply to a person who edits text. In modern usage, 'text editor' almost always refers to a software program for editing text, not a person. The generic meaning is more accurately covered by 'editor', so this sense is redundant and not used as an established English expression. Excluding for being too general and not aligning with established dictionary entries.
