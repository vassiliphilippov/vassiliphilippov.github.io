bone

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00296790-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: bone|oewn-00296790-s
consisting of or made up of bone

Synset Examples

a bony substance
the bony framework of the body

Reason

This concept represents an attributive (s-graded) adjective use of 'bone' meaning 'made of bone,' but standard dictionaries list 'bony' as the adjective form. The lemma 'bone' is not itself used as an adjective in this sense; 'bony' is the relevant dictionary headword. Therefore, including this as an entry is not suitable.
