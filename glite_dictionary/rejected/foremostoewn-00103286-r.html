foremost

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00103286-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: foremost|oewn-00103286-r
before anything else

Synset Examples

first we must consider the garter snake

Reason

The adverbial sense 'foremost' meaning 'before anything else' is archaic and extremely rare in modern English. It is not used in contemporary speech or writing and would not appear as an independent entry in modern dictionaries. The more common uses of 'foremost' are as an adjective.
