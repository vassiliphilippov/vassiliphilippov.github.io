lap

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00151411-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: lap|oewn-00151411-n
touching with the tongue

Synset Examples

the dog's laps were warm and wet

Reason

This meaning of 'lap' as a noun ('a lap' meaning an instance of touching with the tongue) is extremely rare and not standard in contemporary English. It does not correspond to a recognized or widely used noun sense in reputable dictionaries like Oxford; instead, 'lap' as a verb (to lap, i.e., to lick) is correct for this sense. Therefore, this noun sense does not merit a separate dictionary entry.
