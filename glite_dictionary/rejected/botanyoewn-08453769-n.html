botany

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08453769-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: botany|oewn-08453769-n
all the plant life in a particular region or period

Synset Examples

Pleistocene vegetation
the flora of southern California
the botany of China

Reason

This concept refers to 'all plant life in a region or period,' but the standard and correct term for this botanical sense is 'flora.' 'Botany' in English principally refers to the scientific study of plants, not the collective plant life of a region. Therefore, this sense is too broad or inaccurate for inclusion as a dictionary entry under 'botany.'
