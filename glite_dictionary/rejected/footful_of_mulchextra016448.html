footful of mulch

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: footful of mulch|extra.016448
a quantity of mulch held in a foot

Synset Examples

The gardener tossed a footful of mulch around the plants.

Reason

The term 'footful of mulch' is a transparent combination of 'footful' (an informal unit of measurement meaning as much as a foot can hold) and 'mulch.' This is not a standard or widely recognized dictionary headword, nor is it a fixed phrase with meaning distinct from its parts. Such nonce or compositional phrases are not typically included in major dictionaries like Oxford.
