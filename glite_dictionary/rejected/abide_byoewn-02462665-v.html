Reason

The concept 'abide by' with the meaning 'show respect towards' is not a standard or natural sense of the phrase. 'Abide by' more commonly means 'to accept or act in accordance with (a rule, decision, or law)'. The example provided ('honor your parents!') relates to respect, but 'abide by' is not typically used in this sense in modern English. Therefore, this sense should not be included as a dictionary entry.
