recoup

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02219783-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: recoup|oewn-02219783-v
retain and refrain from disbursing; of payments

Synset Examples

My employer is withholding taxes

Reason

This sense of 'recoup' is actually more appropriately defined as 'withhold' (to retain and not pay out, especially of money). The sense given—'retain and refrain from disbursing; of payments'—does not accord with standard dictionary usage of 'recoup', which means to recover or get back an amount, especially money. Therefore, this particular concept should be excluded as it is not relevant or accurate for the headword 'recoup'.
