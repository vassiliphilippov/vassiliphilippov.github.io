assumption

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04845958-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: assumption|oewn-04845958-n
audacious (even arrogant) behavior that you have no right to

Synset Examples

he despised them for their presumptuousness

Reason

The given sense of 'assumption' refers to 'audacious or arrogant behavior that you have no right to,' which better aligns with the word 'presumption' or 'presumptuousness.' 'Assumption' in standard English usage does not commonly have this meaning; such a definition would likely confuse learners. This sense is too obscure and not sufficiently related to the primary usage of 'assumption' (i.e., something taken for granted). Therefore, it should not be included as a main entry in a modern dictionary under this sense.
