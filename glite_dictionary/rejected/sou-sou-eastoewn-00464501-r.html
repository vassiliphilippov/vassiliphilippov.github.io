to, toward, or in the south southeast

Reason

This concept ('sou'-sou'-east' as an adverb) is an extremely uncommon, dialectal or obsolete spelling of 'south-southeast', which would be the standard compass direction term. The form with double hyphens and apostrophes is not standard and would not be included in general English dictionaries such as Oxford. Therefore, it should be excluded.
