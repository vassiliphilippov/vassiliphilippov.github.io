eat a burger

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: eat a burger|extra.015383
to have a hamburger as a meal

Synset Examples

Let's eat a burger at that new diner tonight.

Reason

The synset 'eat a burger' is a compositional phrase describing the action of eating a hamburger; it is not an established idiom or set phrase that would appear as a separate entry in Oxford Dictionary. Such phrases are formed naturally from 'eat' and 'burger,' both of which have their own entries. Therefore, it should not be included as a headword.
