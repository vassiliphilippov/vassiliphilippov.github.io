Reason

The synset is a specific taxonomic name (Odobenus divergens), which is a scientific Latin binomial for a subspecies of walrus. Such binomial scientific names are not included in standard English dictionaries like Oxford unless they are commonly used as English words. This entry is more appropriate for a biological or zoological taxonomy glossary than for a general English dictionary. The common English term 'walrus' would be included instead.
