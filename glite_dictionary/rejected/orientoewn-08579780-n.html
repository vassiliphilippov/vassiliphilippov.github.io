orient

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: orient|oewn-08579780-n
the hemisphere that includes Eurasia and Africa and Australia

Reason

The synset is extremely broad and not specific to the headword 'orient'; it refers to a general hemisphere, which is too general for a good dictionary entry under this word. In dictionary usage, 'Orient' references East Asia, or rarely, eastern parts of the world, but not this broad hemisphere definition.
