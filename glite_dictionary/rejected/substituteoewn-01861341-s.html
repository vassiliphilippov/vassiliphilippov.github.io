substitute

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01861341-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: substitute|oewn-01861341-s
capable of substituting in any of several positions on a team

Synset Examples

a utility infielder

Reason

This sense of 'substitute' is too general and does not have an independent meaning distinct from the main adjective and noun senses. The specific sports-related sense as a positional utility player is usually covered under 'utility' or 'utility player,' not as a unique adjective 'substitute.' Therefore, it does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
