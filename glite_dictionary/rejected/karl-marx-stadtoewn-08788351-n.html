Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Karl-Marx-Stadt|oewn-08788351-n
a city in east central Germany; formerly called Karl-Marx-Stadt until 1990; noted for textile manufacturing

Reason

This concept refers to a city that was formerly called Karl-Marx-Stadt; the current official name is Chemnitz. As the synset is actually the city (place), and not the historical name as a unique lexical entry, it does not warrant a main dictionary entry under 'Karl-Marx-Stadt.' Most English dictionaries do not include former city names as distinct entries except as historical notes within the entry for the current name.
