white

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01090782-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: white|oewn-01090782-s
(of a surface) not written or printed on

Synset Examples

blank pages
fill in the blank spaces
a clean page
wide white margins

Reason

This sense is a very specialized and context-dependent usage of 'white', meaning 'blank' or 'unprinted', which is not a standard dictionary sense. The color term 'white' referring to a surface not written or printed on is not widely recognized as an independent dictionary headword. Dictionaries like Oxford define 'white' with respect to color, not to absence of print or writing; 'blank' is the standard word for this meaning.
