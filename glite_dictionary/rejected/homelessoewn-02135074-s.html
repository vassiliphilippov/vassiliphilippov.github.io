homeless

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02135074-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: homeless|oewn-02135074-s
without nationality or citizenship

Synset Examples

stateless persons

Reason

The sense 'homeless' meaning 'without nationality or citizenship' is extremely rare and not found in ordinary modern English usage. In standard dictionaries, 'homeless' refers to lacking a home, not lacking nationality or citizenship. For the meaning 'stateless,' the proper word is 'stateless'; using 'homeless' in this context is too obscure to merit a dictionary entry for general English learners.
