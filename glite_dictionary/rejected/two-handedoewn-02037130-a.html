Synset Examples

an ambidextrous surgeon

Reason

The synset 'equally skillful with each hand' is more accurately covered by the word 'ambidextrous'. While 'two-handed' exists in English, its typical sense means 'requiring or using both hands', not 'equally skillful with each hand'. Therefore, this concept does not correspond to the headword 'two-handed' as it would appear in a standard English dictionary. Excluded based on the filtering criteria.
