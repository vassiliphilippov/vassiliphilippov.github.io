doughnut

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13898031-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: doughnut|oewn-13898031-n
a toroidal shape

Synset Examples

a ring of ships in the harbor
a halo of smoke

Reason

This sense of 'doughnut' refers to an abstract geometric shape (toroidal shape) and not to the common edible food. While 'doughnut' can be used metaphorically to describe a toroidal shape, it is not standard enough in general English to be included as a dictionary headword for this sense. The main dictionary entry for 'doughnut' would be for the food, not the shape.
