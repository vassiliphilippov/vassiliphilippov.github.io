mal de la rosa

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14224355-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: mal de la rosa|oewn-14224355-n
a disease caused by deficiency of niacin or tryptophan (or by a defect in the metabolic conversion of tryptophan to niacin); characterized by gastrointestinal disturbances and erythema and nervous or mental disorders; may be caused by malnutrition or alcoholism or other nutritional impairments

Reason

'Mal de la rosa' is a rare, non-English term for pellagra and is not commonly used in English medical or general language. Its headword is not used independently in English-language dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary, so it should be excluded.
