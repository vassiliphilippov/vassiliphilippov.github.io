moorhen

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01799370-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: moorhen|oewn-01799370-n
female red grouse

Reason

Although 'moorhen' is a real English word (commonly meaning a type of waterfowl, Gallinula chloropus), this sense refers specifically to a 'female red grouse,' which is not the widely recognized or standard usage. Standard dictionaries list 'moorhen' as a distinct bird species, not as the female of the red grouse. Therefore, this specific sense should be excluded. The main sense of 'moorhen' as a bird would, however, be included in a general dictionary.
