the price of

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: the price of|extra.010430
the cost of a particular item

Synset Examples

The price of the coffee is just one dollar.
The price of gas has increased again.

Reason

The phrase 'the price of' is a common collocation (a frequent word combination), but it is not an independent lexical unit or fixed phrase with a distinct meaning separate from the words 'price' and 'of.' Oxford and similar dictionaries would have an entry for 'price,' but not for 'the price of.' Therefore, it should not be included as a separate dictionary entry.
