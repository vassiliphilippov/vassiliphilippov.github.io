Balance

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08704888-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Balance|oewn-08704888-n
the seventh sign of the zodiac; the sun is in this sign from about September 23 to October 22

Reason

The synset refers to the astrological sign 'Libra', which is the standard headword for this zodiac sign. 'Balance' is not the standard English word for the zodiac sign in contemporary English dictionaries; it is sometimes used in French (e.g., 'la Balance'), but not in modern English. Therefore, this sense should be excluded as it would not have a separate dictionary entry from 'Libra' in an English-English dictionary.
