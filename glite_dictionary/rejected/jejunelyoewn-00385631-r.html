jejunely

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00385631-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: jejunely|oewn-00385631-r
in an immature manner

Synset Examples

his teenage son still behaves very immaturely

Reason

The term 'jejunely' is extremely rare and almost never used in modern or historical English. It does not appear in standard dictionaries like Oxford, and is not a recognizable adverb form even though it derives from the adjective 'jejune.' The standard adverb for this sense is 'immaturely.' Therefore, it should not be included in the dictionary.
