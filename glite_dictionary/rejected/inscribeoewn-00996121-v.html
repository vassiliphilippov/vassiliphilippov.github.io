inscribe

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00996121-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: inscribe|oewn-00996121-v
convert ordinary language into code

Synset Examples

We should encode the message for security reasons

Reason

The sense described ('convert ordinary language into code') is more appropriately covered by the verb 'encode', not 'inscribe'. 'Inscribe' in standard English means to write or carve words onto something, not to convert language into code. This synset is too general or not meaningfully related to the headword 'inscribe' and would not appear as such in a reputable dictionary.
