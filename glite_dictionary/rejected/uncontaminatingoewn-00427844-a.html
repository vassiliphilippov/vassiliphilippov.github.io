uncontaminating

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00427844-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: uncontaminating|oewn-00427844-a
not spreading pollution or contamination; especially radioactive contamination

Synset Examples

a clean fuel
cleaner and more efficient engines
the tactical bomb is reasonably clean

Reason

The concept 'uncontaminating' is extremely rare, nonstandard, and not attested as a lexical item in major English dictionaries like Oxford. The WordNet sense overlaps with the established adjective 'clean' (in the sense of not causing pollution), and the example sentences actually use 'clean' and 'cleaner,' not 'uncontaminating.' Therefore, 'uncontaminating' should not be included as a distinct headword in the dictionary.
