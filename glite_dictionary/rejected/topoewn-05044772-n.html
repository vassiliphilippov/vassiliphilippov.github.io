top

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05044772-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: top|oewn-05044772-n
the greatest possible intensity

Synset Examples

he screamed at the top of his lungs

Reason

This sense of "top" as meaning 'the greatest possible intensity' is used primarily in fixed expressions (e.g., "at the top of one's lungs") and not as a general, stand-alone noun meaning 'greatest possible intensity.' As such, it is too idiomatic and context-bound to warrant a separate dictionary entry for 'top' with this exact sense. The main dictionary entries for 'top' (the upper part, highest point, etc.) already cover its usual meanings.
