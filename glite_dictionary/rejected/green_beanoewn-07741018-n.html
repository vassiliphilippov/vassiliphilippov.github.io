green bean

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07741018-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: green bean|oewn-07741018-n
seed of the mung bean plant used for food

Reason

The concept describes 'green bean' as the seed of the mung bean plant, but in standard English usage, 'green bean' refers to the young, unripe fruit of Phaseolus vulgaris (string bean) or similar beans, not the mung bean seed. This sense is too specific, not standard, and would not appear as such in major English dictionaries.
