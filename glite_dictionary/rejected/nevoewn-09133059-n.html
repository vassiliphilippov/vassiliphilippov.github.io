a state in the western United States

Reason

'Nev.' is an abbreviation for 'Nevada', which is a state in the western United States. Dictionary entries typically include the full name, and standard English dictionaries usually do not list state abbreviations unless they are widely used acronyms. Since this is just an abbreviation and not the commonly used postal (capitalized, two-letter) abbreviation 'NV', it does not meet inclusion criteria.
