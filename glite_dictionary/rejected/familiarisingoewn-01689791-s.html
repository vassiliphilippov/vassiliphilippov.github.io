Reason

The concept 'familiarising' as an adjective meaning 'serving to make familiar' is not a standard dictionary headword and is rarely used in this adjectival sense. It is a participial form of the verb 'familiarise' (chiefly British spelling of 'familiarize'), which is the standard word to include. Including the participle as an adjective is unnecessary for a main dictionary entry; dictionaries typically list the verb 'familiarise/familiarize' and note its participial forms in usage.
