Chopin

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07292961-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Chopin|oewn-07292961-n
the music of Chopin

Synset Examples

he practiced Chopin day and night

Reason

This sense refers specifically to the music created by the composer Chopin and uses his name as a shorthand for his works. However, as a dictionary entry, 'Chopin' in this sense is essentially a proper noun referring to a person (Frédéric Chopin), not a distinct general word or standard usage. Dictionaries handle such proper nouns under the biographical entry for Chopin, with reference to his musical works, rather than as a separate common noun meaning 'the music of Chopin.' Therefore, this is too specific and not appropriate as a separate dictionary entry.
