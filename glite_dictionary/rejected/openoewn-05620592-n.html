open

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05620592-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: open|oewn-05620592-n
information that has become public

Synset Examples

all the reports were out in the open
the facts had been brought to the surface

Reason

The synset here is a highly abstract noun sense of 'open', referring to 'the open' as a metaphorical space where information is public. While the phrase 'in the open' is commonly used, 'open' alone is not typically used as a standalone noun in this sense in modern English dictionaries. Oxford and other major dictionaries do not have an entry for 'open' as a noun meaning 'public knowledge' or 'public domain'; instead, this meaning appears within the idiom 'in the open'. Therefore, this concept is too specialized for inclusion as a headword entry.
