femoris

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05581526-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: femoris|oewn-05581526-n
the longest and thickest bone of the human skeleton; extends from the pelvis to the knee

Reason

The synset refers to the anatomical term for the thigh bone, which is commonly known in English as 'femur,' not 'femoris.' 'Femoris' is the Latin genitive form and does not appear as an English headword. Therefore, it should not be included as an English dictionary entry.
