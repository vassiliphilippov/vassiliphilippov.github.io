Reason

This concept is an offensive use of the word 'taco' as an ethnic slur for people of Mexican descent. While 'taco' is a common noun referring to a type of food (which should be included), its use as an ethnic slur is both rare and highly offensive, and it is not commonly found in major English dictionaries outside of historical documentation. Because it is primarily a derogatory slur and does not meet criteria for broad public dictionary inclusion beyond specialized historical or sociolinguistic studies, it should be excluded here.
