Reason

The synset definition relates to 'the item at the end,' which is too general and not a direct sense of the phrase 'in conclusion.' This sense does not correspond to how 'in conclusion' is typically used or defined in dictionaries, where it more often functions as a discourse marker meaning 'to sum up.' Therefore, this particular concept should be excluded.
