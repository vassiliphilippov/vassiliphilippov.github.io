Adrian

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11041338-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Adrian|oewn-11041338-n
Roman Emperor who was the adoptive son of Trajan; travelled throughout his empire to strengthen its frontiers and encourage learning and architecture; on a visit to Britain in 122 he ordered the construction of Hadrian's Wall (76-138)

Reason

The synset is a general person synset, and the entry is a specific historical figure by their first name only. Dictionary entries for proper names like 'Adrian' used as a Roman Emperor should be under 'Hadrian,' the standard English name for this Emperor, not 'Adrian.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
