starlet

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09467846-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: starlet|oewn-09467846-n
a small star

Reason

The concept 'starlet' as 'a small star' is a rarely-used, overly literal sense not in common usage. The primary and well-known sense of 'starlet' is as an aspiring young actress. The 'small star' sense is too technical and not recognized in general English dictionaries, so it should be excluded.
