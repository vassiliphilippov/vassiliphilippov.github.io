horizontal surface

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03541528-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: horizontal surface|oewn-03541528-n
a flat surface at right angles to a plumb line

Synset Examples

park the car on the level

Reason

The synset 'horizontal surface' is too general and descriptive; it refers essentially to any flat surface that is perpendicular to the direction of gravity. This is not a fixed lexicalized phrase or idiomatic expression that would appear as its own entry in a standard dictionary. Instead, dictionaries would have entries for 'horizontal' and 'surface' separately, and possibly for 'level' in the sense of 'a flat or even surface'.
