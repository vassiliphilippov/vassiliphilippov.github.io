Reason

The verb 'uncase' meaning 'get undressed' is archaic and nonstandard; it is not attested in modern use with this meaning. Its usual meaning is 'to remove from a case.' The headword is not in common usage for the sense described and would not appear as such in a modern learner's dictionary.
