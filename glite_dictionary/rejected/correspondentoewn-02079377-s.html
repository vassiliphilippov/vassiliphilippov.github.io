correspondent

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02079377-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: correspondent|oewn-02079377-s
similar or equivalent in some respects though otherwise dissimilar

Synset Examples

brains and computers are often considered analogous
salmon roe is marketed as analogous to caviar

Reason

The synset's meaning ('similar or equivalent in some respects though otherwise dissimilar') corresponds to the adjective 'analogous', not 'correspondent'. The headword 'correspondent' is usually a noun (a person who reports news) or less commonly an adjective meaning 'corresponding to', but this specific sense is too rare and obsolete. Standard dictionaries cover this meaning under 'analogous' or 'comparable', not 'correspondent' as an adjective. Therefore, it should not be included under 'correspondent' in a modern English-English dictionary.
