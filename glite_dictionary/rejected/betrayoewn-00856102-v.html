betray

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00856102-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: betray|oewn-00856102-v
cause someone to believe an untruth

Synset Examples

The insurance company deceived me when they told me they were covering my house

Reason

This sense of 'betray' (meaning to cause someone to believe an untruth) is not a standard or common usage of 'betray' in contemporary English dictionaries. The typical meaning relates to being disloyal or revealing something confidential, not deceiving or causing someone to believe a falsehood. Therefore, this concept should not be included.
