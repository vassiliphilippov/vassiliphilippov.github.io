style

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04353589-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: style|oewn-04353589-n
a pointed tool for writing or drawing or engraving

Synset Examples

he drew the design on the stencil with a steel stylus

Reason

This sense of 'style' as a tool is not current or common in modern English dictionaries; 'stylus' is the standard term for a pointed writing tool, and this entry is obsolete. 'Style' in this sense would be considered archaic or confusing for modern learners. Therefore, it should be excluded.
