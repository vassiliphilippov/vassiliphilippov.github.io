Reason

The sense described here for 'illustration' is too general and aligns with the concept of 'example' or 'instance', not the primary or commonly understood dictionary sense of 'illustration' (which typically refers to a picture or diagram or to the act of illustrating). This generic sense is not meaningfully or conventionally linked to the headword 'illustration' for dictionary users, and would not appear as a main entry or main sense in a standard English dictionary.
