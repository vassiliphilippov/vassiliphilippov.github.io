an Arab kingdom in Asia on the northwestern coast of the Persian Gulf; a major source of petroleum

Reason

The headword 'Koweit' is an alternative, outdated spelling of 'Kuwait.' Modern English dictionaries list 'Kuwait' as the primary form. Including 'Koweit' as a separate entry is unnecessary, as it is not in standard or current use in English.
