Internal ID: interlocutory|oewn-02839367-a
consisting of dialogue

Reason

This sense of 'interlocutory' as 'consisting of dialogue' is extremely rare and not present in major modern dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The common established sense of 'interlocutory' is in legal contexts, where it refers to a provisional or interim order or judgment. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a standalone dictionary entry with this meaning.
