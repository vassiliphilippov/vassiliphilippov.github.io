determine the number or amount of

Synset Examples

Reason

The verb 'numerate' in the sense of 'determine the number or amount of' is extremely rare and nonstandard in contemporary English; the standard verb for this meaning is 'count.' Thus, 'numerate' in this sense does not merit a separate entry in a general-purpose English dictionary like Oxford.
