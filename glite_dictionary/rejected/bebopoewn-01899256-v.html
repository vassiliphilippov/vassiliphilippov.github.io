dance the bebop

Reason

The verb sense 'bebop' meaning 'to dance the bebop' is extremely rare and not widely used in modern or historical English. It does not appear in major dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster and would not be expected by learners. The noun 'bebop' as a style of jazz is legitimate and should be included, but not this obscure verb sense.
