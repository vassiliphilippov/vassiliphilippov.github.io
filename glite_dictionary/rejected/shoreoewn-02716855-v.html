Reason

While 'shore' is commonly used as a noun (meaning the land along the edge of a sea, lake, or river), its verb sense 'to serve as a shore to' (i.e., to border or flank with land along water) is rare, nonstandard, and not attested in reputable dictionaries like Oxford. It does not have independent, widespread usage in English and would not typically be included as a verb in a general English dictionary.
