state capital of New York; located in eastern New York State on the west bank of the Hudson river

Reason

The synset refers to a specific place, the state capital of New York, but the headword 'capital of New York' is a descriptive phrase, not a proper noun or common fixed expression. Oxford Dictionary would include the name 'Albany' as the capital, but not as a generic phrase like 'capital of New York.' Therefore, this is too general and should not be included as a dictionary headword.
