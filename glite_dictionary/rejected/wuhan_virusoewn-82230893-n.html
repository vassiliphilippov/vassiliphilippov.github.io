Wuhan virus

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-82230893-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Wuhan virus|oewn-82230893-n
a viral disease caused by SARS-CoV-2 that caused a global pandemic in 2020

Reason

The term 'Wuhan virus' as described here refers to COVID-19 but is not a widely accepted or standard name for the disease; it is also associated with stigmatizing language and is not used in reputable dictionaries or scientific contexts. Additionally, it is generally discouraged due to potential for offense and misassociation. The proper term is 'COVID-19' or 'coronavirus disease 2019'.
