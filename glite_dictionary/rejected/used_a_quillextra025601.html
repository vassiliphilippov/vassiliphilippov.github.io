used a quill

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: used a quill|extra.025601
to write or draw using a feather pen

Synset Examples

The artist used a quill to create delicate lines.

Reason

This is a verb phrase specific to the action of using a quill (feather pen) to write or draw. However, 'used a quill' is not a phrasal verb or idiom; it is simply a literal combination of the verb 'use' and the noun 'quill'. Standard dictionaries do not include such literal verb-object combinations as headwords. The concept is better captured under 'quill' or the verb 'use'.
