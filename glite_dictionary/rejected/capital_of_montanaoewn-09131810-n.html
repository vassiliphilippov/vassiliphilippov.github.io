capital of the state of Montana; located in western Montana

Reason

The concept 'capital of Montana' is too specific and refers to a role or status (being the capital) rather than a distinct dictionary entry. Dictionaries would instead include an entry for 'Helena' as the proper noun for the city itself, not for the phrase 'capital of Montana.'
