Kuwaiti dirham

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13692103-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Kuwaiti dirham|oewn-13692103-n
worth one tenth of a Kuwaiti dinar; equal 100 fils

Reason

The 'Kuwaiti dirham' as described (worth one tenth of a Kuwaiti dinar; equal 100 fils) is not an official or historic unit in Kuwait's actual currency system, which uses 'fils' as subunits of the dinar. Thus, this concept is either incorrect or obsolete, and does not merit inclusion in a standard English-English dictionary.
