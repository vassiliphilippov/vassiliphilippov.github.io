Reason

This concept of 'grapple' as 'to succeed in doing or producing something with limited means' does not match standard usage in Oxford Dictionary or common English. The provided examples match the phrasal verb 'get by' or the expression 'make do', but not 'grapple'. 'Grapple' is typically used to mean to struggle or wrestle with something literally or figuratively. Therefore, this sense is too broad/general or mislabeled for 'grapple', and should not be included.
