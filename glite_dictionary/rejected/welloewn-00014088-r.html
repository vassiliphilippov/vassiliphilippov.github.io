in financial comfort

Synset Examples

Reason

This concept refers to the adverbial use of 'well' meaning 'in financial comfort' (e.g., 'live well'). However, this is a specific application of the general adverb 'well' meaning 'in a good or satisfactory way'. Dictionaries do not usually include this specific sense separately, as it is contextually derived from the main adverbial sense. Therefore, this sense is too narrow and context-based, and does not need a dedicated entry.
