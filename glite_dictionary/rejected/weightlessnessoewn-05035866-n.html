weightlessness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05035866-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: weightlessness|oewn-05035866-n
the property of being comparatively small in weight

Synset Examples

the lightness of balsa wood

Reason

The synset refers to the general property of low weight, effectively 'lightness', not to the more common sense of 'weightlessness' meaning absence of weight. The headword 'weightlessness' does not fit this synset; this sense is better captured by 'lightness', which is a separate entry. Including 'weightlessness' with this meaning would be inaccurate and confusing for dictionary users.
