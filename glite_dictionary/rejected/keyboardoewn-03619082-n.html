holder consisting of an arrangement of hooks on which keys or locks can be hung

Reason

This sense refers to a very rare or obsolete meaning of 'keyboard' as a holder for keys or locks, which is not in current use and is too obscure for a general dictionary. The overwhelmingly primary and relevant senses relate to the musical or computer keyboard. This meaning would not be included in a modern Oxford Dictionary.
