wanton

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00012746-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: wanton|oewn-00012746-v
behave extremely cruelly and brutally

Reason

This verb sense of 'wanton' (to behave extremely cruelly and brutally) is extremely rare, archaic, and not commonly encountered in modern English. Major dictionaries such as the Oxford English Dictionary list 'wanton' primarily as an adjective and, to a much lesser extent, as an archaic verb meaning 'to be playful or frolic'. Its use as a verb meaning 'to behave cruelly' is not standard and would not typically merit a separate entry in a modern dictionary.
