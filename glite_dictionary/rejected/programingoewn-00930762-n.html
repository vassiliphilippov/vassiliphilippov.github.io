creating a sequence of instructions to enable the computer to do something

Reason

The concept 'programing' (with one 'm') refers to the creation of instructions for computers, but the correct and standard spelling in modern English is 'programming' (with two 'm's'). 'Programing' is a rare and archaic variant and would not generally appear in a contemporary dictionary as a separate entry. The concept should be filtered out in favor of 'programming'.
