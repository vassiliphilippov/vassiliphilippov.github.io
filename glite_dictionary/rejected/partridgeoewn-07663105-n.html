partridge

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07663105-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: partridge|oewn-07663105-n
flesh of either quail or grouse

Reason

This sense is too broad and does not correctly relate to the word 'partridge' as would appear in an English dictionary. 'Partridge' properly refers to a specific type of bird or its flesh, not the flesh of quail or grouse in general. Such a general sense does not belong as a standalone entry in a dictionary.
