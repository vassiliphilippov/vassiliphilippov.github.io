high

Source: WordNet

a public secondary school usually including grades 9 through 12

Synset Examples

he goes to the neighborhood highschool

Reason

The concept 'high' in this sense refers to 'high school,' but 'high' alone is not used in standard English to mean 'a public secondary school.' The synset is too general/ambiguous for the headword 'high' and would not appear as such in a dictionary. The correct entry would be 'high school.'
