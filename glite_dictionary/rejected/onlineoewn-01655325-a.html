online

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01655325-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: online|oewn-01655325-a
on a regular route of a railroad or bus or airline system

Synset Examples

on-line industries

Reason

This sense of 'online' as 'on a regular route of a railroad or bus or airline system' is very rare, archaic, and almost never encountered in modern English, especially not in a way that would appear in standard dictionaries like Oxford. The common modern senses of 'online' (connected to or available through the Internet) are included in dictionaries, but this obsolete transport-related sense is not, so it should be excluded.
