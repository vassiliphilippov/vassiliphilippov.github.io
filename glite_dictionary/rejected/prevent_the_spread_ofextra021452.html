Reason

The phrase 'prevent the spread of' is a compositional phrase formed from the verb 'prevent' and the noun phrase 'the spread of.' It is not an idiom, compound, or fixed phrasal verb with a meaning different from its parts. Such compositional phrases are generally not included as headwords in dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary unless they are idiomatic or non-compositional, which is not the case here.
