Reason

The synset describes a general phenomenon rather than a distinct lexical sense of the word 'warming'. In standard English dictionaries, this specific sense of 'warming' as a period of warmer weather following a freeze is typically covered under terms like 'thaw' or 'thawing,' not as a separate noun sense of 'warming'. Therefore, it should not be included as a standalone dictionary entry.
