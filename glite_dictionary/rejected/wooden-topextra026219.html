wooden-top

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: wooden-top|extra.026219
a surface made of wood

Synset Examples

The table was sturdy, featuring a beautiful wooden-top.

Reason

The concept 'wooden-top' as described (a surface made of wood) is not an established or widely recognized compound in standard English. It appears to be a compositional description (wooden + top) rather than a lexicalized term that would appear in reputable dictionaries. The typical term would be 'wooden top' without the hyphen, or simply 'wooden tabletop.' Thus, it should not be included as a dictionary headword.
