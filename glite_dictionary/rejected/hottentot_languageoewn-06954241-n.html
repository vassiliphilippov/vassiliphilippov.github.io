Hottentot language

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06954241-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Hottentot language|oewn-06954241-n
any of the Khoisan languages spoken by the pastoral people of Namibia and South Africa

Reason

The term 'Hottentot language' is now considered obsolete and offensive. The term 'Hottentot' itself is an outdated and pejorative exonym for the Khoikhoi and their languages, which are more accurately identified as Khoisan languages. Modern dictionaries do not include this term except in historical or disambiguation contexts, and its use is avoided due to its offensive connotations.
