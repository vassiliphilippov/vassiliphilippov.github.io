Reason

This sense of 'staple' as 'material suitable for manufacture or use or finishing' is highly specialized, technical, and rarely, if ever, encountered outside contexts such as the textile or materials industry. Additionally, in general dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary, 'staple' is primarily defined as a principal commodity or as a fastener; this sense would not merit its own entry but would be subsumed under technical usage notes if necessary. Therefore, it does not warrant a standalone entry.
