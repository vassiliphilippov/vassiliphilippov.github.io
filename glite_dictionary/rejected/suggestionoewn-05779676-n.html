suggestion

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05779676-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: suggestion|oewn-05779676-n
the sequential mental process in which one thought leads to another by association

Reason

This sense of 'suggestion' refers specifically to a technical or psychological process of thought association, which is not a common standalone meaning in general dictionaries. Most dictionaries cover 'suggestion' as a recommendation or hint, not as a mental process. This sense is too specialized and rare for inclusion.
