verify

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02526663-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: verify|oewn-02526663-v
check or regulate (a scientific experiment) by conducting a parallel experiment or comparing with another standard

Synset Examples

Are you controlling for the temperature?

Reason

This concept refers to a very specific scientific sense of 'verify' meaning to conduct a parallel experiment or use a standard for comparison. However, the primary sense of 'verify' in standard usage is to make sure something is true, accurate, or justified. The particular sense described here ('check or regulate a scientific experiment by conducting a parallel experiment or comparing to a standard') is usually covered by more precise terms such as 'control' or 'standardize' rather than 'verify' in general English. Therefore, this sense is too specialized/technical and not closely tied to general English usage of 'verify', so it should not be included as a distinct entry.
