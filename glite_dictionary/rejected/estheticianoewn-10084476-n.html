esthetician

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10084476-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: esthetician|oewn-10084476-n
a philosopher who specializes in the nature of beauty

Reason

This sense defines 'esthetician' as a philosopher specializing in aesthetics, but in modern English the term 'esthetician' (or 'aesthetician') more commonly refers to a beauty care professional, and the philosophical sense is archaic or very rare. The headword is not usually used for philosophers in Oxford or comparable dictionaries. This specialized, historical sense is too obscure for inclusion.
