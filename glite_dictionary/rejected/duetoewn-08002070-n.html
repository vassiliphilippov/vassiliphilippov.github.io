duet

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08002070-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: duet|oewn-08002070-n
a pair who associate with one another

Synset Examples

the engaged couple
an inseparable twosome

Reason

This sense of "duet" refers to a general pair or couple who associate with one another, which is too broad and not specific to the actual primary meaning of "duet" (two people performing music together). In standard dictionaries, "duet" is not used for any pair of people, so this overly general sense should not be included.
