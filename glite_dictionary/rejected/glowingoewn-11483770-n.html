Reason

This sense of "glowing" as a noun, meaning the amount of electromagnetic radiation leaving or arriving at a surface, is a highly technical and rarely used meaning. The term "radiance" or "irradiance" is standard in technical and scientific contexts; "glowing" is not commonly used as a noun with this meaning in general or even specialized English. It would not appear as a noun in a typical English-English dictionary like Oxford.
