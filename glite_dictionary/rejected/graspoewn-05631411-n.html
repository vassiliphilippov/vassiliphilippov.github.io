Synset Examples

within the compass of education

Reason

The sense described here—'grasp' as 'the limit of capability'—is too abstract and is not a commonly used meaning of 'grasp' as a noun. Main dictionary definitions of 'grasp' refer to physical holding or mental understanding, while this sense is rare and archaic. It is also reflected in other words such as 'compass' (range, scope), which fits the example better. Therefore, this concept is excluded.
