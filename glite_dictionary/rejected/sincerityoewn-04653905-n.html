sincerity

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04653905-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: sincerity|oewn-04653905-n
the trait of being serious

Synset Examples

a lack of solemnity is not necessarily a lack of seriousness

Reason

This concept defines 'sincerity' as 'the trait of being serious,' which actually refers to 'seriousness' or 'solemnity.' This synset does not correspond accurately to the usual meaning of 'sincerity' in English, and using 'sincerity' for 'seriousness' is not supported in modern or standard usage. Therefore, this sense should be excluded from the dictionary.
