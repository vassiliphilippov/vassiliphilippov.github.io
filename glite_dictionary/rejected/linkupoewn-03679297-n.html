linkup

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03679297-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: linkup|oewn-03679297-n
a fastener that serves to join or connect

Synset Examples

the walls are held together with metal links placed in the wet mortar during construction

Reason

The synset describes a general connecting fastener, but 'linkup' is not used in standard English to mean a physical fastener or connector. In contemporary English, 'linkup' almost always refers to a meeting or connection between people or systems, not a physical item. Therefore, this sense should not be included.
