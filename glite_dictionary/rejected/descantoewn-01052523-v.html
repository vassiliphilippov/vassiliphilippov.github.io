Synset Examples

Reason

This verb sense of 'descant' (to sing by changing register; to sing by yodeling) is not used in modern English and is extremely rare or obsolete. The typical meaning of 'descant' in English is as a noun (a musical part or commentary), not as a verb for yodeling. This usage does not meet the inclusion criteria for a general English dictionary like Oxford.
