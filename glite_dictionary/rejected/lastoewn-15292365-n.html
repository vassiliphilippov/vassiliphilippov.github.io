Synset Examples

Reason

This sense of 'last' corresponds to an abstract temporal noun meaning 'the temporal end' or 'the concluding time.' In normal English, 'last' is not used as a noun in this specific sense; instead, words like 'end,' 'finish,' or 'close' are used. This noun sense of 'last' does not appear in standard dictionaries and is not attested in natural usage as a standalone noun for 'the end.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
