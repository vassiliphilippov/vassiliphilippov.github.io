Internal ID: worsification of the project|extra.026281
the process of making a project worse

Synset Examples

The worsification of the project was evident to everyone.

Reason

The concept 'worsification of the project' is an ungrammatical and nonstandard noun phrase, derived from the nonstandard noun 'worsification' (a quasi-humorous or nonce formation). It does not have established dictionary attestation and would not be found as a main entry or an established compound in Oxford Dictionary. It is also a transparent combination of 'worsification' (which itself is not recognized standard English) and the common noun 'project.' Thus, it should not be included as a dictionary entry.
