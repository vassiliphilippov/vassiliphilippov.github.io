fend for

Synset POS: v
Internal ID: fend for|oewn-00896689-v
argue or speak in defense of

Synset Examples

She supported the motion to strike

Reason

The sense 'argue or speak in defense of' associated with 'fend for' is not a recognized standard usage in contemporary English, nor does it appear as an accepted phrasal verb meaning in Oxford or major dictionaries. 'Fend for' is normally used in the sense of 'fend for oneself' (manage independently), not as 'argue or speak in defense.' Therefore, this sense should be excluded from the dictionary.
