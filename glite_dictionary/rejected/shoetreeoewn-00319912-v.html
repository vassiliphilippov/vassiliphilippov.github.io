Reason

The verb sense 'stretch (a shoe) on a shoetree' is extremely rare and specialized. In standard English, 'shoetree' is a noun referring to the device placed inside a shoe to keep its shape. The verb usage is not established enough to warrant dictionary inclusion in a general dictionary like Oxford; it is not used in ordinary communication.
