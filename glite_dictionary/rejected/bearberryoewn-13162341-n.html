bearberry

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13162341-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: bearberry|oewn-13162341-n
shrubby tree of the Pacific coast of the United States; yields cascara sagrada

Reason

This sense of 'bearberry' refers to a shrubby tree yielding cascara sagrada, which is incorrectly attributed. The common meaning of 'bearberry' is a low-growing shrub (genus Arctostaphylos) with red berries; the plant yielding cascara sagrada is 'Rhamnus purshiana', usually called cascara or cascara buckthorn, not bearberry. Therefore, this synset does not match the standard usage of 'bearberry' and should not be included.
