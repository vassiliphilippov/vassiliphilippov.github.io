programma

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06553717-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: programma|oewn-06553717-n
an edict that has been publicly posted

Reason

The concept 'programma' as an 'edict that has been publicly posted' is borrowed directly from Latin/Greek but is not used in contemporary English with this meaning. It does not appear in Oxford or other major English dictionaries as a recognized English term in this sense, so it should not be included.
