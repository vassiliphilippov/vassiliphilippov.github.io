tend to dwindle

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: tend to dwindle|extra.024340
to gradually decrease

Synset Examples

Sales tend to dwindle at the end of the season.

Reason

The phrase 'tend to dwindle' is a compositional verb phrase (verb + infinitive/form of another verb) and does not form a lexicalized phrasal verb or idiom. It is a transparent use of 'tend to' (showing likelihood) combined with 'dwindle' (decrease), both of which have dictionary entries. Such straightforward, compositional phrases are not included in major dictionaries as headwords.
