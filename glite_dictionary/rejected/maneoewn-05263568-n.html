mane

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05263568-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: mane|oewn-05263568-n
growth of hair covering the scalp of a human being

Reason

This sense of 'mane' (referring to human scalp hair in general) is either archaic or not standard. In modern English, 'mane' is primarily used for the thick hair on the neck of mammals such as lions or horses, and only figuratively for a person's long, thick hair. The generic sense 'scalp hair of a human being' does not meet dictionary standards as an independent entry.
