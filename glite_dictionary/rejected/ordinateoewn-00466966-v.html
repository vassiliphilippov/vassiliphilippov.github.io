ordinate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00466966-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: ordinate|oewn-00466966-v
bring (components or parts) into proper or desirable coordination correlation

Synset Examples

align the wheels of my car
ordinate similar parts

Reason

The sense of 'ordinate' as a verb meaning 'bring components or parts into proper coordination or correlation' is extremely rare and not used in standard contemporary English. Modern dictionaries do not record 'ordinate' as a verb with this meaning; instead, the correct verb is 'coordinate' or 'align'. Therefore, this sense should be excluded from the dictionary.
