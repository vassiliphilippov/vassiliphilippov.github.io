throughout

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00258633-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: throughout|oewn-00258633-r
used to refer to cited works

Reason

The sense 'throughout' as 'used to refer to cited works' is too field-specific and not a general word sense appearing as a standalone headword in general dictionaries like Oxford. The primary adverb/preposition senses of 'throughout' (meaning 'in every part of' or 'during the whole period of') are already included in dictionaries. The usage described here is a special referencing convention, not a general lexical sense.
