mention as by way of greeting or to indicate friendship

Synset Examples

Remember me to your wife

Reason

This specific sense of 'commend' as 'to mention as by way of greeting or to indicate friendship' is highly archaic and no longer common or recognizable in contemporary English usage. Its primary modern meanings involve praise or recommendation, which are separately covered. Example given ('Remember me to your wife') is now expressed using entirely different language. This archaic usage does not merit inclusion as an active dictionary entry for general learners.
