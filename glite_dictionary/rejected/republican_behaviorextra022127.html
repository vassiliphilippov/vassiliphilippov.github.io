Republican behavior

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: Republican behavior|extra.022127
derogatory term for behavior associated with certain political views

Synset Examples

Some considered his remarks to be classic Republicunt behavior.
His tactics in the debate were criticized as Republithug behavior.

Reason

The synset refers to a derogatory or slang term that is constructed around specific partisan neologisms ('Republicunt', 'Republithug'), and does not represent a widely recognized term 'Republican behavior' with a specific, dictionary-worthy meaning separate from its component words. The synset is instead too general and not standard as a headword, so it should not be included as a dictionary entry.
