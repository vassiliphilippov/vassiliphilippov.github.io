Reason

The synset meaning ('before the usual time or the expected time') is too general and not specific to the phrase 'too soon'; it applies to adverbs like 'early' or phrases like 'ahead of time.' The sense is not uniquely definitional for 'too soon' as a lexical item suitable for a dictionary entry. 'Too soon' is better defined as 'earlier than desired or appropriate,' which would be a separate, more idiomatic use. Therefore, this particular synset is too general and should be excluded.
