stalker

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10665967-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: stalker|oewn-10665967-n
someone who walks with long stiff strides

Reason

This sense of 'stalker' as 'someone who walks with long stiff strides' is archaic and extremely rare. The overwhelming current meaning of 'stalker' in English is someone who harasses or follows another person obsessively. The sense described here is not in contemporary use and would not appear as a main entry or prominent subdefinition in most modern dictionaries such as Oxford. Therefore, it should be excluded.
