authentication

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06868085-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: authentication|oewn-06868085-n
a mark on an article of trade to indicate its origin and authenticity

Reason

This sense of 'authentication' refers specifically to a physical mark or label on trade articles, which is more commonly covered by terms like 'hallmark' or 'trademark'. 'Authentication' in a dictionary is usually defined as the process of proving that something is real or genuine, not the mark itself. This sense is too narrow and not widely recognized or used as a meaning of 'authentication' in general English.
