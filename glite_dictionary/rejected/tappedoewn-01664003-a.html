tapped

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01664003-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: tapped|oewn-01664003-a
in a condition for letting out liquid drawn out as by piercing or drawing a plug

Synset Examples

latex from tapped rubber trees

Reason

The adjective 'tapped' in this sense is highly technical and narrow, used almost exclusively in reference to sources like tapped trees or barrels with a hole or spout. It is not a commonly dictionary-listed sense and is more of a participial adjective derived from the verb 'tap.' Standard dictionaries typically only list the verb 'tap' or the more widely understood senses of 'tapped' (such as wired for listening). Therefore, this particular synset does not merit a standalone entry.
