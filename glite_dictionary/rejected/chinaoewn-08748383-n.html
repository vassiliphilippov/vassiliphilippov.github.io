China

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: China|oewn-08748383-n
a government on the island of Taiwan established in 1949 by Chiang Kai-shek after the conquest of mainland China by the Communists led by Mao Zedong

Reason

This concept refers to the government on the island of Taiwan (commonly called the Republic of China or Taiwan) but is labeled here under the headword 'China'. This is too general and misleading, as 'China' primarily refers to the country or the People's Republic of China. For this synset, the association with 'China' is not precise or standard for dictionary purposes. Such political/historic naming issues are better addressed under 'Republic of China' or 'Taiwan'. Therefore, it is excluded.
