reminder

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09791278-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: reminder|oewn-09791278-n
someone who gives a warning so that a mistake can be avoided

Reason

This sense of 'reminder' as 'someone who gives a warning' is not standard or widely used in contemporary English. The common use of 'reminder' is as something (such as a note or prompt) that serves to remind, not as a person. Including this sense could confuse learners.
