pass up

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02242120-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: pass up|oewn-02242120-v
not accept as true

Synset Examples

He refused to accept what she said

Reason

The phrasal verb 'pass up' does not conventionally mean 'not accept as true.' The example given fits 'refuse,' 'reject,' or 'decline' more accurately. In standard usage, 'pass up' means to decline an opportunity or not take advantage of something. Therefore, this sense is too general or incorrect and should not be included.
