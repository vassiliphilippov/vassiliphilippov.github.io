Reason

The sense 'fraction' as a verb meaning 'perform a division' is not standard in contemporary English usage. The verb 'fraction' is rarely, if ever, used to mean 'to divide' in natural speech or writing. Dictionaries like Oxford and Merriam-Webster do not list 'fraction' as a verb with this meaning; instead, the verb 'divide' is standard. This concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
