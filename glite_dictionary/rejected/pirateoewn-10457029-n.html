pirate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10457029-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: pirate|oewn-10457029-n
someone who uses another person's words or ideas as if they were their own

Reason

This sense of 'pirate' as someone who plagiarizes words or ideas is not a common or standard usage in modern English. The primary dictionary sense of 'pirate' relates to someone who attacks and robs ships at sea, or colloquially to someone who infringes copyright (e.g., software or media), but not specifically to literary plagiarism. This sense is too broad and not sufficiently established for inclusion as a main dictionary entry for 'pirate.'
