the music of Brahms

Synset Examples

Brahms was included in the program

Reason

The synset refers to 'Brahms' in the sense of 'the music of Brahms,' but the word 'Brahms' itself is fundamentally a proper noun, referring specifically to the composer Johannes Brahms. The usage here is a metonymic extension (using 'Brahms' to mean his works), but this is standard for many composers and not specific enough to merit a separate lexicographical entry distinct from the person's name. 'Brahms' as a synset for 'music of Brahms' is too referential and general per filtering criteria (analogous to how we treat people/organizations/places with generic synsets), so it should be excluded.
