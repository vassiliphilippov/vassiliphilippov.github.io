capital of the People's Republic of China in the Hebei province in northeastern China; 2nd largest Chinese city

Reason

The synset 'capital of Red China' refers to the general concept of a capital city and is simply another name for 'Beijing.' This phrase is outdated and no longer used in common English; modern dictionaries list 'Beijing' instead. Furthermore, the wording 'Red China' is archaic, politically charged, and not standard for modern reference. This concept should not be included as a separate entry in a contemporary English-English dictionary.
