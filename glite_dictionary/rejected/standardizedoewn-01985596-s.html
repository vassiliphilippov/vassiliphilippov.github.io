standardized

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01985596-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: standardized|oewn-01985596-s
capable of replacing or changing places with something else; permitting mutual substitution without loss of function or suitability

Synset Examples

interchangeable electric outlets
interchangeable parts

Reason

The synset described is properly matched to the headword 'interchangeable,' not 'standardized.' 'Standardized' in this sense is incorrectly aligned here; it is not conventionally used to mean 'capable of mutual substitution,' but rather 'conformed to a standard.' The dictionary should include 'interchangeable' for this concept, but not 'standardized' for this synset as it does not naturally bear this meaning in English.
