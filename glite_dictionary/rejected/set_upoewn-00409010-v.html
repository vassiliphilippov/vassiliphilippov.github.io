Reason

The verb sense 'set up' meaning 'equip with sails or masts' is not idiomatic with 'set up' in general contemporary English; the usual technical term is 'rig.' This sense is rare, technical, and mainly archaic. 'Set up' would not be given this meaning as a separate dictionary entry in a general English dictionary like Oxford; it would only appear under nautical jargon for 'rig.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
