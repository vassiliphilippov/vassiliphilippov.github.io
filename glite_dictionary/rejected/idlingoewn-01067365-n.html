idling

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01067365-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: idling|oewn-01067365-n
having no employment

Reason

The concept 'idling' as 'having no employment' is an overly general noun sense and is not standard; the noun 'idling' is not typically used in English to mean 'unemployment' or 'having no employment,' nor would it appear as a distinct noun sense in Oxford or similar dictionaries. The verb and adjective forms ('idling' as the act of being idle, not working) are valid, but not this redundant noun sense.
