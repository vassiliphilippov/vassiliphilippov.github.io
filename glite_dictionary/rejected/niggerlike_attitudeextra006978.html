niggerlike attitude

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: niggerlike attitude|extra.006978
a derogatory term describing a person's negative or bothersome behavior

Synset Examples

His niggerlike attitude alienated his friends.
His niggery attitude made it hard to work with him.

Reason

This term is overtly offensive and derived from a racial slur. Dictionary standards, including Oxford and other major dictionaries, do not include offensive formations based on slurs unless they have substantial independent usage or relevance, which this term does not. Its inclusion would be inappropriate, and it is not a commonly used or independently notable expression. Therefore, it should be excluded from the dictionary.
