English statesman who served as prime minister and who opposed the war with the American colonies (1730-1782)

Reason

The synset is 'person' and refers specifically to an individual's biographical identity, not a lexical sense distinct from being a person's name. Such entries are not included in standard English dictionaries like Oxford, which do not list historical figures solely as proper nouns unless the person is extremely culturally significant or the name has developed an additional lexical meaning. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
