Synset Examples

Which horse are you backing?

Reason

The verb 'gage' meaning 'place a bet on' is archaic and not used in contemporary English; the standard terms are 'bet' or 'wager'. This sense is obsolete and would not appear as an active entry in a modern dictionary like Oxford, except as a historical note. Therefore, it should not be included.
