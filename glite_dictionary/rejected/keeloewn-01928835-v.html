keel

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01928835-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: keel|oewn-01928835-v
walk as if unable to control one's movements

Synset Examples

The drunken man staggered into the room

Reason

This sense of 'keel' ('walk as if unable to control one's movements') is not recognized as a standard or established meaning of 'keel' in modern English usage or in major dictionaries like Oxford. The verb 'keel' typically means to turn over (of a boat: to capsize), not to stagger as described. This is a misattributed or archaic synonym and does not warrant inclusion for general learners.
