sporty

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00959923-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: sporty|oewn-00959923-s
exhibiting or calling for sportsmanship or fair play

Synset Examples

a clean fight
a sporting solution of the disagreement
sportsmanlike conduct

Reason

This synset defines an adjective sense related to 'sporty' meaning 'exhibiting or calling for sportsmanship or fair play', but the examples and sense in WordNet are better covered by 'sporting' or 'sportsmanlike.' The adjective 'sporty' in common English primarily refers to being fond of or suited for sport, not to fair play or sportsmanlike conduct. Therefore, this specific sense for 'sporty' is too broad/generalized and not lexically attached to the headword as used in natural English. It should not be included under 'sporty' in a standard dictionary.
