Cheddar

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08899112-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Cheddar|oewn-08899112-n
a village in southwestern England where cheddar cheese was first made

Reason

The concept 'Cheddar' as a village is a proper noun and refers to a specific place. Its synset is too general ('village') and, while potentially notable because of its connection to cheddar cheese, place names like this are generally not included in standard English-English dictionaries unless they have become genericized or have standalone linguistic significance. This usage does not meet that criterion.
