Reason

This synset for 'teasing' as 'the act of removing tangles from your hair with a comb' is highly specific and not widely recognized as a standard sense in general English. In common usage, 'teasing' refers to making fun of or provoking someone, or to a backcombing technique to add volume to hair. The meaning described here is not standard and would not appear in Oxford Dictionary entries for 'teasing', so it should not be included.
