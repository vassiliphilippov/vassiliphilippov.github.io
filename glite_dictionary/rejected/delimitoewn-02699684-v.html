be opposite to; of angles and sides, in geometry

Reason

This concept refers to a highly technical and obsolete use of 'delimit' in geometry (meaning to be opposite to, of angles and sides), which is not current in modern English and would not be included as a definition in general dictionaries like Oxford. The primary, current meaning of 'delimit' relates to marking boundaries or limits, not to being opposite. This sense is too obscure and specialized to include.
