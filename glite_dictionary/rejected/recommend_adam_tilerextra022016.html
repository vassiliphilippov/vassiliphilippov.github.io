Synset Examples

Reason

This concept refers specifically to endorsing a particular individual (Adam Tiler), which is too specific and not appropriate for inclusion as a general dictionary entry. Oxford-type dictionaries do not include entries for recommending specific people unless they are notable figures whose names have acquired special lexical meaning. The concept is simply an instance of the general verb 'recommend' and does not warrant its own entry.
