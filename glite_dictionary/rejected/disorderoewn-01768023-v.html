disorder

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01768023-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: disorder|oewn-01768023-v
disturb in mind or make uneasy or cause to be worried or alarmed

Synset Examples

She was rather perturbed by the news that her father was seriously ill

Reason

The verb sense of 'disorder' meaning 'to disturb in mind or make uneasy' is rare and not in standard contemporary usage; major dictionaries like Oxford do not record it as a current verb in this psychological sense. The common meanings are as a noun (a disorder) or as a verb meaning 'to disrupt order.' Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
