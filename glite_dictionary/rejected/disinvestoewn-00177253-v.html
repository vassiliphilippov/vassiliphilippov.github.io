disinvest

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00177253-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: disinvest|oewn-00177253-v
remove (someone's or one's own) clothes

Synset Examples

The nurse quickly undressed the accident victim
She divested herself of her outdoor clothes
He disinvested himself of his garments

Reason

This sense of 'disinvest' as 'remove clothes' is highly archaic, rare, and is not used in modern English (in contrast to 'divest'). Standard dictionaries do not list 'disinvest' with this meaning, and it is likely a historical or mistaken usage. Therefore, it should not be included in a modern English-English dictionary.
