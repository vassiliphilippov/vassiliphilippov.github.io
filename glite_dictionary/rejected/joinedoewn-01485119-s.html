of or relating to two people who are married to each other

Reason

This sense of 'joined' is an adjective meaning 'of or relating to two people who are married to each other.' However, this usage is not standard or widely recognized in contemporary English. 'Joined' as an adjective typically means 'connected' or 'attached,' and its use in relation to marriage is metaphorical, not lexical. Dictionaries do not list 'joined' specifically as an established adjective describing marital status, so it should be excluded.
