taper

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04589115-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: taper|oewn-04589115-n
a loosely woven cord (in a candle or oil lamp) that draws fuel by capillary action up into the flame

Reason

This sense of 'taper' refers to the wick of a candle or oil lamp, but in modern English, 'taper' is not commonly used to mean 'wick.' This usage is outdated and would not typically appear in a modern dictionary outside of historical or highly technical contexts. The general entry for 'wick' covers this concept. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
