mammalian

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01864419-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: mammalian|oewn-01864419-n
any warm-blooded vertebrate having the skin more or less covered with hair; young are born alive except for the small subclass of monotremes and nourished with milk

Reason

The concept 'mammalian' listed as a noun (meaning 'a mammal') is extremely rare and not standard in contemporary English. The typical noun form is 'mammal'; 'mammalian' is usually used as an adjective. As such, this noun sense is too marginal and would not appear in major general dictionaries like Oxford.
