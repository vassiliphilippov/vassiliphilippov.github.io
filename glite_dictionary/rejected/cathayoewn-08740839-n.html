Cathay

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08740839-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Cathay|oewn-08740839-n
a communist nation that covers a vast territory in eastern Asia; the most populous country in the world

Reason

The synset refers to the modern nation of China, but 'Cathay' as a headword is not meaningfully synonymous with the entire country in current usage; it is an archaic or poetic name for China, not the actual communist state. Including it with this overly general synset would be misleading. Only the archaic/poetic sense should be included in a dictionary, not the general sense of 'communist nation in eastern Asia'.
