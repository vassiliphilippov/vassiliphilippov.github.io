Internal ID: sectarist|oewn-10593401-n
a member of a sect

Synset Examples

most sectarians are intolerant of the views of any other sect

Reason

The term 'sectarist' is extremely uncommon and not widely recognized in contemporary English; instead, 'sectarian' is the standard noun form for 'a member of a sect.' The synset is properly represented by 'sectarian,' making 'sectarist' unnecessary for dictionary inclusion.
