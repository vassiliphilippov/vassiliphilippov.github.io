ununtrium

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14684493-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: ununtrium|oewn-14684493-n
a radioactive transuranic element, with atomic number 113

Reason

'Ununtrium' was a temporary systematic element name (Element 113), officially replaced by 'nihonium'. It is obsolete and would not appear with this sense in current major dictionaries.
