parcel

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01087022-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: parcel|oewn-01087022-n
the allotment of some amount by dividing something

Synset Examples

death gets more than its share of attention from theologians

Reason

The synset refers to a highly abstract or obsolete sense of 'parcel' meaning an allotment or portion resulting from dividing something, which is not in current general use. Modern dictionaries include 'parcel' as a package or a portion of land, but not in the context of 'the allotment by division' as described here. Therefore, this sense is excluded.
