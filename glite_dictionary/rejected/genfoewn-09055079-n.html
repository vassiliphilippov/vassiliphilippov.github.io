Genf

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09055079-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Genf|oewn-09055079-n
a city in southwestern Switzerland at the western end of Lake Geneva; it is the headquarters of various international organizations

Reason

The concept 'Genf' is the German name for the city known in English as 'Geneva.' English dictionaries use 'Geneva' as the main entry, not 'Genf.' Therefore, its inclusion is not appropriate for an English-English dictionary.
