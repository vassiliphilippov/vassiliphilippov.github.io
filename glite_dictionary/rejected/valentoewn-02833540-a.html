valent

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02833540-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: valent|oewn-02833540-a
(chemistry) having valence; usually used in combination

Reason

The form 'valent' is almost exclusively used as a combining element in compound terms (e.g. 'bivalent', 'trivalent') in chemistry, but as a standalone adjective meaning 'having valence', it is not used independently in modern English. Dictionary entries for 'bivalent', 'trivalent', etc. are appropriate, but not for 'valent' alone as a stand-alone word. This is in line with standard English dictionaries, which do not list 'valent' except as a combining form.
