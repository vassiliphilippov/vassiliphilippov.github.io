Synset Examples

Reason

The synset described here aligns with the very general concept of 'trial' or 'test,' not the more specific meaning of 'tryout' as used in English (such as an audition or sports selection event). The description and examples do not match common English usage of 'tryout,' so this sense should not be included as an entry for 'tryout.'
