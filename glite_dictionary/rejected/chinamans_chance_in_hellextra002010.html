Reason

The phrase 'Chinaman's chance in hell' is an archaic and offensive idiom. Modern reputable dictionaries generally avoid listing offensive racial idioms unless absolutely necessary for historical documentation. Additionally, 'Chinaman's chance' (listed separately without 'in hell') covers the same meaning, making this longer form redundant. This phrase would not be included in a contemporary learner's dictionary.
