the formal act of approving

Synset Examples

he gave the project his blessing

Reason

'Approving' as a noun meaning 'the formal act of approving' is not standard usage in English; the correct noun form is 'approval' or 'blessing.' 'Approving' is primarily used as an adjective or the present participle of the verb. Therefore, this noun sense is too marginal and would not appear as a headword in standard dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary.
