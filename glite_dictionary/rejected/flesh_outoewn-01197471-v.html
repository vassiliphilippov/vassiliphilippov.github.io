flesh out

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01197471-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: flesh out|oewn-01197471-v
make fat or plump

Synset Examples

We will plump out that poor starving child

Reason

The sense of 'flesh out' meaning 'make fat or plump' is obsolete, extremely rare, and not found in current dictionaries as an active sense. The common use of 'flesh out' is 'to add details to make something more complete,' which is not this sense. This concept should be excluded as it does not reflect current or relevant usage for learners.
