platan

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-12827492-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: platan|oewn-12827492-n
any of several trees of the genus Platanus having thin pale bark that scales off in small plates and lobed leaves and ball-shaped heads of fruits

Reason

The term 'platan' is a highly technical or rare botanical term for trees of the genus Platanus; in English, the commonly used word is 'plane' (as in 'plane tree'). 'Platan' is not widely recognized or used in general English dictionaries such as Oxford. The synset matches a legitimate concept but 'platan' itself is not the standard or dictionary headword in English.
