frustrate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01806967-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: frustrate|oewn-01806967-v
treat cruelly

Synset Examples

The children tormented the stuttering teacher

Reason

This synset equates 'frustrate' with 'treat cruelly' or 'torment', which is not a standard or established definition in modern English. In standard dictionaries, 'frustrate' does not have the primary sense of 'to treat cruelly'. Instead, it means 'to prevent (someone) from achieving a goal' or 'to cause (someone) to feel annoyed or disappointed'. Therefore, this particular sense should be excluded.
