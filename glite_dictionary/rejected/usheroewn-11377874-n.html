Irish prelate who deduced from the Bible that Creation occurred in the year 4004 BC (1581-1656)

Reason

The synset is a general one ('person' or 'prelate') associated with the proper name 'Usher' referring to James Ussher, a historical figure. Proper names of individuals whose only sense is 'person' or similar are not included in a standard English dictionary like Oxford unless the person is a household name or the name is used eponymously. This is an encyclopedic entry, not a dictionary entry.
