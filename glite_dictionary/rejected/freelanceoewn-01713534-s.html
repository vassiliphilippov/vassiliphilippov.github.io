Reason

The concept refers to an archaic or obsolete adjective sense of 'freelance' meaning 'serving for wages in a foreign army.' This sense is extremely rare and is not used in modern English, nor would it appear as a standalone entry in current dictionaries. The noun and verb uses of 'freelance' (related to independent work) are already included in modern dictionaries, but this specific sense is not.
