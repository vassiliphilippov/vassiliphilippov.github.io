bootlipped comment

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: bootlipped comment|extra.013175
an inappropriate or offensive remark

Synset Examples

The bootlipped comment ruined his chances for promotion.

Reason

The term 'bootlipped comment' is not a standard phrase in English and does not appear in reputable dictionaries like Oxford. It is a constructed phrase based on an offensive racial slur ('bootlipped'), which is itself extremely derogatory and not appropriate for general dictionary inclusion, especially as a compound describing any comment. Furthermore, the synset is too closely tied to an offensive usage rather than having independent, general linguistic currency. Therefore, this concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
