drop

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01260866-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: drop|oewn-01260866-v
cause to fall by or as if by delivering a blow

Synset Examples

strike down a tree
Lightning struck down the hikers

Reason

This sense of 'drop' (to cause to fall by delivering a blow) is not a standard or widely recognized meaning for the verb 'drop' as used in English dictionaries such as Oxford. The correct verb for this sense is 'strike down' or simply 'fell' (for trees). Including this usage for 'drop' would be confusing and not useful for learners.
