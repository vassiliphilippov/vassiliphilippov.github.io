Noreg

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08781940-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Noreg|oewn-08781940-n
a constitutional monarchy in northern Europe on the western side of the Scandinavian Peninsula; achieved independence from Sweden in 1905

Reason

The lemma 'Noreg' is the Norwegian-language name for Norway. In English, the canonical form is 'Norway'. 'Noreg' is not used as an English word and should not be included in an English-English dictionary such as Oxford.
