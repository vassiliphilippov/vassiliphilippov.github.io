Synset POS: v
Internal ID: germinate|oewn-01742478-v
work out

Synset Examples

We have developed a new theory of evolution

Reason

The verb sense 'germinate' as 'work out' or 'develop' in the context of ideas is better captured by other, clearer verbs such as 'develop', 'formulate', or 'conceive'. This figurative sense is not a standard or recognized usage of 'germinate' in reputable English dictionaries, where it mainly refers to seeds beginning to grow. Including this figurative sense would be misleading to learners. Therefore, this particular concept should not be included as a separate dictionary entry.
