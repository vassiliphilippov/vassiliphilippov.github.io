Synset Examples

Reason

The headword 'by experimentation' is an ad hoc prepositional phrase, not a recognized dictionary entry, and the synset refers to an adverbial sense ('in an experimental fashion') which is better represented by 'experimentally.' This does not warrant a separate entry in a standard English dictionary like Oxford.
