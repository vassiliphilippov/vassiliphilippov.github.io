imprecation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07251617-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: imprecation|oewn-07251617-n
a slanderous accusation

Reason

The primary and widely recognized meaning of 'imprecation' is 'a spoken curse' or 'the act of calling down harm or evil.' The sense 'a slanderous accusation' is not standard, is rare at best, and would not be included as a principal sense in major dictionaries such as Oxford. Therefore, this specific concept should be excluded.
