Synset POS: n
Internal ID: sire|oewn-10122569-n
the founder of a family

Synset Examples

keep the faith of our forefathers

Reason

This sense of 'sire' as 'the founder of a family' is archaic and extremely rare in modern English. The general public would not encounter or use this meaning outside of historical or genealogical contexts, and even there, 'ancestor' or 'progenitor' is far more common. Most dictionary users would not look up this sense, so it does not merit inclusion as a separate entry.
