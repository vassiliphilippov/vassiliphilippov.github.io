Riff

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10550050-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Riff|oewn-10550050-n
a Berber living in northern Morocco

Reason

This sense refers to 'Riff' as a member of an ethnic group (a Berber from the Rif region) but is rarely, if ever, used in this sense in modern English dictionaries. Instead, 'Riffian' is the standard demonym. 'Riff' would not appear in Oxford or similar as an English noun for a person in this context, so it should be excluded.
