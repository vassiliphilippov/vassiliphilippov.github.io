sensational

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02807248-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: sensational|oewn-02807248-a
relating to or concerned in sensation

Synset Examples

the sensory cortex
sensory organs

Reason

This specific sense of 'sensational' is overly technical and highly rare; in contemporary English, the standard adjective for relating to sensation is 'sensory.' 'Sensational' virtually never means 'relating to sensation' in modern usage, and this is not a distinct, standard dictionary sense. Therefore, the concept should be excluded.
