a bluish-white lustrous metallic element; brittle at ordinary temperatures but malleable when heated; used in a wide variety of alloys and in galvanizing iron; it occurs naturally as zinc sulphide in zinc blende

Reason

The concept 'atomic number 30' is too general and refers to the category of an atomic number, not to the specific element (zinc). The proper entry for a dictionary would be 'zinc,' not 'atomic number 30.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
