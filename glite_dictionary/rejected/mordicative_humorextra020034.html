mordicative humor

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: mordicative humor|extra.020034
humor that is biting or caustic

Synset Examples

She couldn't stand his mordicative humor anymore.

Reason

The term 'mordicative humor' is extremely rare and does not appear in established English dictionaries, including the Oxford Dictionary. The more common and accepted adjective in English is 'mordant' (as in 'mordant humor'), which covers the sense of biting or caustic wit. 'Mordicative' itself is not attested in reputable sources, and so 'mordicative humor' should not be included as a dictionary entry.
