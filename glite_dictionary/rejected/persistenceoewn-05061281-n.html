persistence

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05061281-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: persistence|oewn-05061281-n
the property of a continuous and connected period of time

Reason

The concept describes a highly general and abstract property of time (continuity over a period), which is not a specific or distinct meaning of 'persistence' commonly found in standard dictionaries like Oxford. Standard definitions focus on 'steadfastness' or 'continuing in existence.' This sense (as an abstract property) is too broad and does not correspond to an actual dictionary headword sense.
