delivery

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00094303-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: delivery|oewn-00094303-n
recovery or preservation from loss or danger

Synset Examples

work is the deliverance of mankind
a surgeon's job is the saving of lives

Reason

This sense of 'delivery' as 'recovery or preservation from loss or danger' is extremely rare and archaic in modern English. The primary senses of 'delivery' relate to bringing goods, giving birth, or formally conveying something. The sense described here is much better covered by 'deliverance' or 'rescue', and would not be found under 'delivery' in a standard modern dictionary such as Oxford. Thus, this entry should be excluded.
