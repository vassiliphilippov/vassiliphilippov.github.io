nutrient

Synset POS: s
Internal ID: nutrient|oewn-02568489-s
of or providing nourishment

Synset Examples

good nourishing stew

Reason

The sense described as 'of or providing nourishment' pertains to an attributive adjective usage (like 'nutrient soup'), but in standard dictionaries this use is covered by the noun 'nutrient' (a substance that provides nourishment) and the adjective 'nutritious.' The attributive adjective 'nutrient' is not standard as a separate entry; its adjectival use is rare, with 'nutritious' being the common adjective. Therefore, this concept is too close to the noun entry and not distinct or common enough as an adjective to merit inclusion as a separate entry.
