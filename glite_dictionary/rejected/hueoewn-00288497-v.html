Reason

The verb sense 'hue' meaning 'to suffuse with color' is extremely rare, archaic, and not in common modern use. The noun 'hue' (color or shade) is standard and included, but the verb is not recognized in major contemporary dictionaries and would not appear in the Oxford Dictionary.
