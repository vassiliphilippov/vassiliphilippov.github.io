land site

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08668822-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: land site|oewn-08668822-n
the piece of land on which something is located (or is to be located)

Synset Examples

a good site for the school

Reason

The synset 'land site' corresponds to the general concept of 'site' as a location or piece of land where something is or will be located. The headword 'land site' is not a common, standalone lexical item in standard usage; dictionaries typically list 'site' for this meaning, not 'land site.' Therefore, the separate entry 'land site' is unnecessary.
