North American raccoon

Reason

'Coon' in the sense of 'North American raccoon' is a shortened, informal variant of 'raccoon,' but it is also widely recognized as a highly offensive and racist slur towards Black people. Due to its offensive connotations and potential for harm, most reputable dictionaries either do not include this term in the animal sense, or do so only with strong warnings. For learner safety and appropriateness, this concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
