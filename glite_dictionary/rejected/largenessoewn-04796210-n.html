largeness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04796210-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: largeness|oewn-04796210-n
the quality of being pretentious (behaving or speaking in such a manner as to create a false appearance of great importance or worth)

Reason

The synset for 'largeness' here refers to the quality of being pretentious, which is not a standard, widely attested sense of 'largeness' in modern English dictionaries such as Oxford Dictionary. The standard meaning relates to size or magnitude, not pretentiousness. Therefore, this concept is excluded.
