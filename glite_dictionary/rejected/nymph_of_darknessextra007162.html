nymph of darkness

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: nymph of darkness|extra.007162
a mythical spirit associated with darkness

Synset Examples

The legend spoke of the nymph of darkness guarding the ancient forest.
In the tale, the nymph of darkness appeared only at midnight.

Reason

The concept 'nymph of darkness' is a descriptive phrase referring to a mythical being. It is not an established dictionary headword or fixed lexicalized expression; rather, it is a compositional phrase using 'nymph' and 'darkness.' Such descriptive, non-lexicalized phrases are generally not included in dictionaries like Oxford.
