make messy or untidy

Synset Examples

the child mussed up my hair

Reason

The synset definition refers to 'make messy or untidy,' but for the headword 'tussle,' this is not a standard or dictionary-recognized meaning. 'Tussle' as a verb usually means to struggle or fight, not to make messy. The definition and example correspond to 'muss' or 'muss up' rather than 'tussle.' Therefore, this concept should not be included for 'tussle.'
