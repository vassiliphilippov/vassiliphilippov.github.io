toughest

WordNet ID: n/a

Internal ID: toughest|extra.024573
the most challenging part

Synset Examples

The first mile of the marathon is always the toughest.

Reason

The concept 'toughest' as 'the most challenging part' is simply the superlative form of the adjective 'tough'. Superlatives and regular inflections like this are not given separate dictionary entries; instead, they are covered within the entry for 'tough'. Therefore, this form does not warrant its own dictionary headword.
