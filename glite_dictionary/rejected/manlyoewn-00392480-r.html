manly

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00392480-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: manly|oewn-00392480-r
in a manful manner; with qualities thought to befit a man

Synset Examples

having said her say, Peggy manfully shouldered her burden and prepared to break up yet another home

Reason

The concept describes the adverbial sense of 'manly' (i.e., 'manfully'), but the standard English adverb for this meaning is 'manfully.' 'Manly' is overwhelmingly used as an adjective in modern English, not as an adverb, and its adverbial use is nonstandard. Therefore, the adverbial concept 'manly' (as 'in a manful manner') does not merit a separate entry in a standard dictionary like Oxford; instead, 'manfully' should be included as the adverb.
