non

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00024432-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: non|oewn-00024432-r
negation of a word or group of words

Synset Examples

he does not speak French
she is not going
they are not friends
not many
not much
not at all

Reason

This sense (negation marker as an adverb, 'not') is not lexically listed as 'non' in English. The examples refer to 'not', which is already included in dictionaries, whereas 'non' is a prefix, not a standalone adverb. The synset is unrelated to the headword 'non'. Therefore, it should be excluded.
