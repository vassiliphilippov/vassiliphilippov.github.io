hinderance

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05697054-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: hinderance|oewn-05697054-n
something immaterial that interferes with or delays action or progress

Reason

The correct spelling for this concept is 'hindrance', which is the standard form found in dictionaries. 'Hinderance' is a common misspelling and is not a standard headword in major dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, it should be excluded as a main entry.
