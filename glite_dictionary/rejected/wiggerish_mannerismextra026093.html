wiggerish mannerism

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: wiggerish mannerism|extra.026093
mannerisms associated with a stereotype of white people imitating hip-hop culture

Synset Examples

Her wiggerish mannerisms drew both laughs and eye rolls.

Reason

The concept 'wiggerish mannerism' is derived from 'wigger,' which itself is an offensive slang term, and the synset refers to mannerisms associated with a stereotype. It is not a standard lexicalized term, nor does it represent an established phrase or entry one would find in a reputable dictionary such as Oxford. Additionally, its primary meaning is compositional and based directly on the offensive slang 'wigger,' so it is both unnecessarily derivative and problematic for inclusion as a separate headword.
