triennial

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15276568-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: triennial|oewn-15276568-n
the 300th anniversary (or the celebration of it)

Reason

The sense described here, 'the 300th anniversary (or the celebration of it)', is not a standard or recognized use of 'triennial' in English; the correct term for a 300th anniversary is 'tercentenary' or 'tricentennial'. Therefore, this sense should not be included.
