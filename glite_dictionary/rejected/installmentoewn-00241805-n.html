installment

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00241805-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: installment|oewn-00241805-n
the act of installing something (as equipment)

Synset Examples

the telephone installation took only a few minutes

Reason

This sense of 'installment' is an error; the correct term for 'the act of installing something' is 'installation.' 'Installment' in English refers to paid portions of a sum, not the act of installing equipment. Including this would be misleading.
