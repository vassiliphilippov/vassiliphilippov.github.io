foursquare

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13901273-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: foursquare|oewn-13901273-n
(geometry) a plane rectangle with four equal sides and four right angles; a four-sided regular polygon

Synset Examples

you can compute the area of a square if you know the length of its sides

Reason

The sense relates to the standard geometric definition of 'square,' and 'foursquare' is not commonly used in contemporary English to mean 'square' (the four-sided regular polygon). Therefore, this sense is too obscure and would not typically appear as a headword in major dictionaries for this meaning. It is better treated as an archaic or obsolete variant, if at all, rather than a standard dictionary entry for the geometric sense.
