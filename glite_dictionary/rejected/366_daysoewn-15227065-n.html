Reason

The concept '366 days' refers to a specific quantity of days in a leap year, but as a phrase it is too general and not used as a standard lexical entry in dictionaries. The specific term 'leap year' is the established headword for this concept, not '366 days'. Therefore, it should not be included as a dictionary entry.
