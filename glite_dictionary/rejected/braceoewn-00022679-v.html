Reason

The sense 'brace' meaning 'cause to be alert and energetic' is extremely rare and not common in contemporary English. The standard synonyms for this meaning, as exemplified (stimulate, invigorate), are used far more frequently; 'brace' is not typically encountered in this sense in modern usage, and major dictionaries do not list this sense as a separate entry. Therefore, this sense should not be included.
