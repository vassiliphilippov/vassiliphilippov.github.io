scape

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04189751-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: scape|oewn-04189751-n
(architecture) upright consisting of the vertical part of a column

Reason

This sense of 'scape' refers to a highly technical and archaic architectural term that is not commonly recognized in modern English usage and is excessively specialized. It does not appear as a main entry or sub-entry in major dictionaries like Oxford, as the headword 'scape' is not commonly used for the vertical part of a column (that would be 'shaft'). Including it would not benefit most learners, as it is obscure and not in general or even specialist architectural use today.
