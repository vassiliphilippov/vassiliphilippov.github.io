a domestic servant

Reason

The noun sense 'menial' as 'a domestic servant' is extremely rare and archaic; the primary and modern use of 'menial' is as an adjective meaning low-status or unskilled (e.g., 'menial work'). As such, this noun sense is obsolete and would not typically appear as a primary headword in modern dictionaries.
