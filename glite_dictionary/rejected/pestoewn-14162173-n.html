pest

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14162173-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: pest|oewn-14162173-n
a serious (sometimes fatal) infection of rodents caused by Yersinia pestis and accidentally transmitted to humans by the bite of a flea that has bitten an infected animal

Reason

The synset here defines 'pest' as a specific infectious disease in rodents caused by Yersinia pestis, but in modern English and standard dictionaries, 'pest' refers to an animal or insect regarded as troublesome, not the disease (which is 'plague'). This disease sense is obscure and not connected to the general understanding of the word and would not appear as such in main dictionaries under 'pest.'
