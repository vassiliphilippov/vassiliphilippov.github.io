on-line

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01655325-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: on-line|oewn-01655325-a
on a regular route of a railroad or bus or airline system

Synset Examples

on-line industries

Reason

This sense of 'on-line' is highly specific to transportation logistics and refers to being on a regular service route, which is rarely used in modern general English. It does not have common usage nor broad dictionary relevance outside specialized technical contexts. Most contemporary references to 'online' relate to internet connectivity, not this meaning.
