belonging to or characteristic of Romanticism or the Romantic Movement in the arts

Synset Examples

romantic poetry

Reason

The concept 'romanticist' is being defined as an adjective meaning 'belonging to or characteristic of Romanticism or the Romantic Movement.' However, 'romanticist' is almost exclusively used as a noun referring to a person who practices or admires Romanticism, not as an adjective. The correct adjective is 'Romantic' or 'romantic' in this context. Therefore, this adjective sense for 'romanticist' should not be included in the dictionary.
