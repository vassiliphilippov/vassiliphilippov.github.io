immigrate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00414629-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: immigrate|oewn-00414629-v
introduce or send as immigrants

Synset Examples

Britain immigrated many colonists to America

Reason

This sense is a rare or obsolete causative use of 'immigrate' (to introduce or send as immigrants) and is not widely used in standard, modern English. It is not found in major dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster: those restrict 'immigrate' to its intransitive use (to come to live permanently in a foreign country). This causative (transitive) sense does not merit a separate entry.
