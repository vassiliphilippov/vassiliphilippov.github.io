impairment

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00273722-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: impairment|oewn-00273722-n
the act of making something futile and useless (as by routine)

Reason

This sense of 'impairment' as specifically 'the act of making something futile and useless (as by routine)' is overly narrow, relatively obscure, and not representative of standard dictionary usage. In English, 'impairment' generally refers to the act of weakening, damaging, or making something less effective, commonly used regarding function or ability. This narrow sense does not warrant a standalone dictionary entry.
