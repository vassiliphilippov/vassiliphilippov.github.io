Reason

This particular sense of 'worthless' as 'morally reprehensible' is not standard in contemporary English dictionaries, which define 'worthless' primarily as 'having no value or use.' The moral sense is more accurately captured by words like 'despicable,' 'vile,' or 'contemptible.' Therefore, this sense should not be included as a stand-alone dictionary entry for 'worthless.'
