Synset Examples

Reason

The phrase 'heft of quality' is not an established fixed expression or idiom in general English usage. It is a compositional phrase using 'heft' (meaning weight or substance) metaphorically. Such usage would not be included as a distinct entry in a standard dictionary such as Oxford; the dictionary would instead list 'heft' (both literal and metaphorical senses) but not this phrase.
