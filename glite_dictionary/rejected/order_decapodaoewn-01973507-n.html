Reason

The concept 'order Decapoda' is incorrectly defined as 'squids and cuttlefishes'. While Decapoda is an actual scientific order in zoology, it refers to a group of crustaceans (such as shrimps, crabs, and lobsters), not cephalopods like squids and cuttlefish. The concept as described here is both a technical term and potentially a mismatched definition. Additionally, such technical scientific classification terms are generally not included in general-use English dictionaries like Oxford unless they are well-known or frequently encountered. 'Decapoda' as an order is better suited for a specialist zoology or biology dictionary, not a general dictionary.
