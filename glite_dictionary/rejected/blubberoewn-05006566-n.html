blubber

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05006566-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: blubber|oewn-05006566-n
excess bodily weight

Synset Examples

she disliked fatness in herself as well as in others

Reason

This sense of 'blubber' as 'excess bodily weight' is extremely rare and not standard in modern English; it primarily means 'the thick layer of fat on marine mammals.' The usage as generic body fat or fatness would not appear as an independent entry in Oxford Dictionary, nor is it commonly recognized for people. Therefore, it should not be included as a dictionary headword with this sense.
