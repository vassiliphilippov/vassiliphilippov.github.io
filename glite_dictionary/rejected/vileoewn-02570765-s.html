Reason

The synset meaning 'causing or able to cause nausea' for 'vile' is overly specific and rarely used in this sense; standard dictionaries primarily define 'vile' as morally bad, disgusting, or extremely unpleasant, not specifically as nausea-inducing. Including this sense would be confusing and is not supported by contemporary English usage as a standalone sense for 'vile.'
