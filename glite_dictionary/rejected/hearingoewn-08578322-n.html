hearing

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08578322-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: hearing|oewn-08578322-n
the range within which a voice can be heard

Synset Examples

the children were told to stay within earshot

Reason

This sense of 'hearing' as 'the range within which a voice can be heard' is not a commonly used standalone noun definition in modern English; the standard phrasing is 'within earshot.' 'Hearing' is better described in its primary senses (the faculty of perceiving sound, a legal proceeding) and not in this specialized, rare synset. This general range concept would not appear as a main sense in standard dictionaries for 'hearing'.
