a contrary aim

Synset Examples

at cross-purposes

Reason

The noun 'cross-purpose' meaning 'a contrary aim' is extremely rare in modern usage. The idiom 'at cross-purposes' is the normal, attested form found in dictionaries. Since this noun sense would not appear as its own dictionary entry in a standard dictionary like Oxford, and the synset is too dependent on the idiomatic context, it should be excluded.
