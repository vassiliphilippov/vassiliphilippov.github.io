above

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06404303-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: above|oewn-06404303-n
an earlier section of a written text

Synset Examples

for instructions, refer to the above

Reason

The sense described refers to a very general usage of 'above' as a reference to an earlier section of a text. While this is a common usage, Oxford and similar dictionaries do not typically include the noun form 'the above' specifically defined as 'an earlier section of a written text;' instead, they define 'above' as an adverb, preposition, or adjective. This noun usage is too contextually bound and generic to merit its own main entry.
