bygone

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15146095-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: bygone|oewn-15146095-n
past events to be put aside

Synset Examples

let bygones be bygones

Reason

The noun use of 'bygone' as 'past events to be put aside' only exists in the fixed idiom 'let bygones be bygones.' It is not used independently as a general noun in modern English and does not occur outside this idiom. Therefore, this sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry beyond its inclusion as part of the idiom.
