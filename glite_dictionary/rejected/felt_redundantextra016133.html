felt redundant

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: felt redundant|extra.016133
considered unnecessary or superfluous

Synset Examples

He felt his position was redundant after the merger.

Reason

This concept is a phrase combining the past tense of the verb 'feel' with the adjective 'redundant'. The adjective 'redundant' is the true dictionary headword, and 'felt redundant' is simply a grammatical usage of it; such verb-adjective combinations are generally not included as dictionary entries in major English dictionaries. Only 'redundant' (as an adjective) needs a dictionary entry.
