Reason

The concept 'principles of Sportianity' is too specific and not an established dictionary entry. It is a phrase referencing the beliefs of an ideology called 'Sportianity,' which itself may deserve consideration as a headword, but this phrase does not. Phrases constructed as 'principles of X' for most ideologies or belief systems are too generic and would not appear as headwords in Oxford Dictionary.
