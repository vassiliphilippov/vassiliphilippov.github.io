debate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00814706-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: debate|oewn-00814706-v
think about carefully; weigh

Synset Examples

They considered the possibility of a strike
Turn the proposal over in your mind

Reason

This verbal sense of 'debate' meaning 'think about carefully; weigh' is rare and not commonly recognized in modern English dictionaries for the verb 'debate.' Standard dictionaries primarily define 'debate' as discussing something, especially in a formal way, or arguing about something. The meaning 'consider carefully' is better associated with 'consider,' 'ponder,' or 'contemplate.' Therefore, this sense should not be included as a main entry for 'debate.'
