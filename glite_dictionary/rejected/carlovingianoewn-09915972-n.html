Carlovingian

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09915972-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Carlovingian|oewn-09915972-n
a member of the Carolingian dynasty

Reason

The term 'Carlovingian' is an alternative, archaic form of 'Carolingian' and is extremely rare in modern English. 'Carolingian' is the standard and recognized form in historical and academic contexts. As 'Carlovingian' is obsolete and not encountered in contemporary or even most scholarly texts, it does not merit a main dictionary entry by Oxford standards.
