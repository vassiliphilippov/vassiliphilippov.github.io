murmur

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07128862-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: murmur|oewn-07128862-n
a schwa that is incidental to the pronunciation of a consonant

Reason

This sense of 'murmur' as a technical term for a schwa-like sound incidental to consonant pronunciation is highly specialized and not widely used in general English. It is mainly a technical phonetic concept, and would not appear as a main entry in a general-purpose dictionary like Oxford. The primary meanings of 'murmur' relate to low, indistinct sounds or, medically, to abnormal heart sounds.
