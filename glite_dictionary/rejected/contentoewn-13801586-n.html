content

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13801586-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: content|oewn-13801586-n
the amount that can be contained

Synset Examples

the gas tank has a capacity of 12 gallons

Reason

This particular synset for 'content' ('the amount that can be contained') closely overlaps with the concept of 'capacity', and this meaning is almost never used for 'content' in standard modern English. In contemporary dictionaries, 'content' is defined as 'what is contained in something' or 'the subjects or topics covered', but not 'the amount that can be contained.' This sense would not appear as a main definition for 'content' in a reputable dictionary like Oxford. Therefore, it should be excluded.
