antiphonary

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02640936-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: antiphonary|oewn-02640936-a
relating to or resembling an antiphon or antiphony

Reason

This synset describes the adjective form 'antiphonary' ('relating to or resembling an antiphon or antiphony'), but this usage is extremely rare and not standard in modern English; 'antiphonary' is overwhelmingly used as a noun (a liturgical book). This adjective form would not appear in standard dictionaries such as Oxford.
