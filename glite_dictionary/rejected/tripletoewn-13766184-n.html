the cardinal number that is the sum of one and one and one

Reason

This sense defines 'triplet' as the number three, which is not a standard or dictionary-accepted usage for the word 'triplet'. The meaning is too general, and 'three' has its own entry as a numeral. 'Triplet' in English typically refers to a set of three things, three children born at one birth, or a group of three notes played in the time of two (musically). Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
