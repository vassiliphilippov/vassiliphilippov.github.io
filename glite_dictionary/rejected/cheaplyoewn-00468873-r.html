cheaply

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00468873-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: cheaply|oewn-00468873-r
in a stingy manner

Synset Examples

their rich uncle treated them rather chintzily

Reason

The sense 'cheaply' as 'in a stingy manner' is not a standard, widely recognized adverbial sense in modern English and is nearly obsolete or redundant with other clearer terms like 'stingily.' The main sense of 'cheaply' is 'at a low price,' which is already well-documented; this rare and awkward sense does not merit a separate dictionary entry.
