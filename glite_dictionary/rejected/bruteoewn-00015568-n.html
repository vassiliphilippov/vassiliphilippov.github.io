Reason

This sense defines "brute" as simply an organism with voluntary movement, which is excessively broad and not specific to the meaning of 'brute' in standard usage. The usual dictionary sense for 'brute' as a noun is 'a savagely violent person or animal' or 'an animal as opposed to a human', not every living organism with voluntary movement. The presented concept's synset is too general for the dictionary and does not correspond specifically to the usual headword meaning.
