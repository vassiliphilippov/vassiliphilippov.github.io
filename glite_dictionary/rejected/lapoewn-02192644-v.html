lap

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02192644-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: lap|oewn-02192644-v
move with or cause to move with a whistling or hissing sound

Synset Examples

The bubbles swoshed around in the glass
The curtain swooshed open

Reason

The verb 'lap' in the sense of moving with a whistling or hissing sound is incorrect; this definition corresponds to the verb 'swoosh' or 'swish.' 'Lap' as a verb does not naturally mean to move with a whistling or hissing sound in standard English usage, so this sense should not be included.
