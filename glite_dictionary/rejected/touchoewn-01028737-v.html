touch

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01028737-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: touch|oewn-01028737-v
make a more or less disguised reference to

Synset Examples

He alluded to the problem but did not mention it

Reason

This verbal sense of 'touch' (meaning to make a disguised reference, as in 'allude to') is not a common, independent usage in modern English. Standard dictionaries do not list 'touch' to mean specifically 'to allude to' as a standalone definition; it occurs only in certain phrases (e.g., 'touch on a topic'), but not as 'touch' alone meaning 'allude to.' Therefore, it's too marginal and not recognized as a separate dictionary sense, so it should not be included.
