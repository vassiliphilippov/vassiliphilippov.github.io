by right

Synset Examples

baseball rightfully is the nation's pastime

Reason

This concept represents an adverbial sense of 'truly' meaning 'by right' or 'rightfully,' but in modern English, 'truly' is not commonly used with this meaning. The example given ('baseball rightfully is the nation's pastime') uses 'rightfully' not 'truly.' Therefore, this sense is too marginal for inclusion in a standard dictionary, as it does not reflect actual usage.
