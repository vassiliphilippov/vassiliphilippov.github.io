wickedness

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04788613-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: wickedness|oewn-04788613-n
the quality of being disgusting to the senses or emotions

Synset Examples

the vileness of his language surprised us

Reason

This synset is more accurately associated with 'vileness' or 'vile', rather than 'wickedness'. It refers to disgust, filth, or offensiveness to the senses, which is different from the main sense of 'wickedness' as moral evil. Including this sense under 'wickedness' would be misleading and is too general or inappropriate for the headword. Therefore, it should not be included in the dictionary under 'wickedness'.
