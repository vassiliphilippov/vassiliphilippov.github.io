Wahvey

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09561429-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Wahvey|oewn-09561429-n
a name for the God of the Old Testament as transliterated from the Hebrew consonants YHVH

Reason

The concept 'Wahvey' refers to a rare, nonstandard transliteration of the tetragrammaton YHVH, and does not appear as a widely accepted or attested English word in reputable dictionaries such as Oxford Dictionary. The standard transliterations are 'Yahweh' or 'Jehovah.' Including 'Wahvey' would not be appropriate.
