Reason

The concept 'fence' as a verb meaning 'have an argument about something' is not a recognized or standard definition in modern English dictionaries. The primary verb sense of 'fence' relates to the sport of fencing or to act of surrounding something with a fence, but not to having an argument. Therefore, this sense should not be included in the dictionary.
