chicane

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01107035-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: chicane|oewn-01107035-v
defeat someone through trickery or deceit

Reason

Although 'chicane' historically exists as a verb meaning to defeat or trick by deception, its verb usage is rare and archaic in modern English. Most contemporary dictionaries only include the noun form referring to a sharp double bend or trickery itself. The verb sense does not appear in standard modern usage and is not suitable for inclusion as a main entry in a contemporary learner's dictionary.
