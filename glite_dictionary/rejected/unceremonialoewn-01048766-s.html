unceremonial

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01048766-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: unceremonial|oewn-01048766-s
without ceremony or formality

Synset Examples

an unceremonious speech

Reason

The concept 'unceremonial' is not standard in modern English; the correct and widely used form is 'unceremonious.' 'Unceremonial' is extremely rare and would not appear in major dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary. It is not necessary to include as a headword.
