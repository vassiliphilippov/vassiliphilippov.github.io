adulterate wine

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: adulterate wine|extra.012020
to make wine poorer in quality

Synset Examples

They tried to adulterate the wine with cheaper ingredients.

Reason

The synset 'adulterate wine' is too specific and compositional; it consists of the common verb 'adulterate' and its object 'wine'. 'Adulterate' itself (with examples like 'adulterate wine') would be covered in a standard dictionary, but not this phrase. The meaning is not idiomatic and would not have a separate entry in Oxford Dictionary.
