vulgariser

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10474308-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: vulgariser|oewn-10474308-n
someone who makes something attractive to the general public

Reason

The word 'vulgariser' is not commonly used in English. The sense is rare, and the more standard terms are 'popularizer' (American) or 'populariser' (British), which cover someone who makes something accessible or attractive to the general public. Since 'vulgariser' is extremely uncommon in English and this sense does not widely appear or is recognized in contemporary dictionaries, it should not be included.
