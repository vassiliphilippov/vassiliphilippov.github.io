rather

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00019243-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: rather|oewn-00019243-r
to a degree (not used with a negative)

Synset Examples

quite tasty
quite soon
quite ill
quite rich

Reason

This sense of 'rather' is about its use as a degree adverb meaning 'to a degree' and appears very close to how 'quite' is used, but the provided examples ('quite tasty', 'quite soon', etc.) actually do not use 'rather' itself. This refers to 'quite', not 'rather', so the synset is too general/incorrect for 'rather'. Therefore, it should not be included for 'rather'.
