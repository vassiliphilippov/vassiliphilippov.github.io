fire-eater

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10111420-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: fire-eater|oewn-10111420-n
a member of a fire department who tries to extinguish fires

Reason

The concept describes 'fire-eater' as a member of a fire department who extinguishes fires, but 'fire-eater' in standard English usage refers to a performer who puts flaming objects in their mouth. The described sense is not standard or widely attested; the established word is 'firefighter.' Thus, this concept should be excluded.
