entrench

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02575705-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: entrench|oewn-02575705-v
impinge or infringe upon

Synset Examples

This impinges on my rights as an individual
This matter entrenches on other domains

Reason

The synset definition 'impinge or infringe upon' is not a standard or recognized usage of 'entrench' in contemporary or historical English. The common use of 'entrench' is as a verb meaning to establish something very firmly or to dig a trench for defensive purposes, not to 'impinge or infringe.' This sense would not be found in standard dictionaries like Oxford and is likely an error or a nonstandard extension. Therefore, this sense should not be included.
