heavenwardly

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: r
Internal ID: heavenwardly|oewn-00357305-r
toward heaven

Synset Examples

he pointed heavenward

Reason

'Heavenwardly' is an extremely rare and nonstandard adverb, and the more standard form 'heavenward' is used for this meaning. Major dictionaries do not include 'heavenwardly', so it should be excluded from the dictionary.
