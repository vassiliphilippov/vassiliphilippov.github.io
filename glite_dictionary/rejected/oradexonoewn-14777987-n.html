Oradexon

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14777987-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Oradexon|oewn-14777987-n
a corticosteroid drug (trade names Decadron or Dexamethasone Intensol or Dexone or Hexadrol or Oradexon) used to treat allergies or inflammation

Reason

'Oradexon' is a trade name for the corticosteroid drug dexamethasone. Major dictionaries typically do not include every pharmaceutical trade name, especially when it is simply a brand of a widely recognized drug (whose generic name is included). 'Dexamethasone' itself would be the main entry; 'Oradexon' is too specific and of limited general interest for a standard English dictionary.
