Reason

This sense of 'line' as a verb meaning 'fill plentifully' is only used in fixed phrases such as 'line one's pockets' and is not a productive or general meaning of 'line.' The general dictionary would define 'line' as 'to cover the inside of something,' but the meaning 'to fill plentifully' is essentially idiomatic and not a widely used free-standing sense. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate dictionary entry.
