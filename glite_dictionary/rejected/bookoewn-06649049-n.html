book

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06649049-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: book|oewn-06649049-n
a compilation of the known facts regarding something or someone

Synset Examples

Al Smith used to say, ‘Let's look at the record’
his name is in all the record books

Reason

The synset describes 'book' as a compilation of records or facts, but this sense overlaps with the broader, primary sense of 'book' as a collection of written or printed sheets. The specific usage 'record book' or the idea of 'book' being a record of known facts does not constitute a distinct dictionary sense for 'book' itself. Instead, this is covered under the general 'book' entry and/or under compounds like 'record book'. Therefore, this concept is too general relative to 'book' and should not be included as a separate sense.
