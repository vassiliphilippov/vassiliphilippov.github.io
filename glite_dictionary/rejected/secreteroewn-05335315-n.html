secreter

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05335315-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: secreter|oewn-05335315-n
any of various organs that synthesize substances needed by the body and release it through ducts or directly into the bloodstream

Reason

The term 'secreter' is not used in standard English medical or general vocabulary for organs that produce secretions. The proper term is 'secreting organ' or more specifically names like 'gland.' The concept is not dictionary-worthy as 'secreter' is not an established English headword for this sense.
