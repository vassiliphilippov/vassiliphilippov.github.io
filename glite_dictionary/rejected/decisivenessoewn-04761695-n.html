Reason

This sense of 'decisiveness' is too general and abstract (referring to 'the quality of being final or definitely settled', synonymous with 'finality'), which is not a distinct, commonly used sense for learners. It would not appear as a separate sense in major dictionaries under 'decisiveness'.
