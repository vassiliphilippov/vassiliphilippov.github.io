Lentia

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08865731-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Lentia|oewn-08865731-n
city in northern Austria on the Danube; noted as a cultural center

Reason

The synset here is 'city' and the term 'Lentia' is an ancient or historical name for what is now Linz, Austria. Modern dictionaries use place names only for current usage unless the historical name is widely encountered. 'Lentia' is not in sufficiently common English use as a standalone term to justify inclusion, and it would otherwise simply refer to a specific location (city), which is too general, especially if the modern city is better known by a different name.
