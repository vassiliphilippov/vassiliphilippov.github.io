Synset POS: n
Internal ID: histrionics|oewn-06906924-n
a performance of a play

Reason

While 'histrionics' is a valid English word, the specific sense 'a performance of a play' is outdated and not a primary or modern meaning. In contemporary English, 'histrionics' refers to exaggerated dramatic behavior, not to plays or stage performances themselves. Therefore, this sense is too general and not reflective of current usage, and should not be included.
