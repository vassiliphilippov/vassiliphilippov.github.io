Reason

The noun "rousing" meaning 'the act of arousing' is extremely rare and not standard; the common noun form is 'arousal.' 'Rousing' is much more typically used as an adjective or as a present participle of the verb 'rouse.' This sense would not appear as a noun in major English dictionaries.
