impact

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01174742-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: impact|oewn-01174742-n
the violent interaction of individuals or groups entering into combat

Synset Examples

the armies met in the shock of battle

Reason

This sense of 'impact' as specifically referring to the violent clash of armies or groups in combat is archaic and not widely used in modern English. The primary dictionary entry for 'impact' (forceful contact or effect) is already covered, and this specialized sense would not warrant a separate entry in a contemporary dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary.
