go through in search of something; search through someone's belongings in an unauthorized way

Synset Examples

Who rifled through my desk drawers?

Reason

The synset described here is for the verb sense 'go' meaning 'go through in search of something; search through someone's belongings', which is not a standard or recognized sense of the verb 'go'. The actual verb used for this meaning is 'rifle', as shown in the example. The sense does not correspond to any meaningful or accepted use of 'go' in English dictionaries. Therefore, it should not be included.
