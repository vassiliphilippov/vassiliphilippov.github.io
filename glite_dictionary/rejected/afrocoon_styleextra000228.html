Reason

The term 'Afrocoon style' appears to be obscure, very rarely used, and not established in general, fashion, or academic English. Most importantly, the term 'coon' is a widely recognized racial slur and is offensive. Therefore, including this term risks perpetuating harm and does not align with common dictionary practices unless documenting historical usage in a carefully controlled scholarly context. It should be excluded.
