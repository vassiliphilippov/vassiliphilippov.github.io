Reason

The sense 'played' as an adjective meaning 'engaged in (of games)' is too context-specific and rare. It does not represent a widely used, independent adjective that would merit a standalone dictionary entry; its use is an extension of the verb 'play.' Such participial adjectives are generally not listed separately in dictionaries unless their meaning has become lexicalized or idiomatic, which is not the case here.
