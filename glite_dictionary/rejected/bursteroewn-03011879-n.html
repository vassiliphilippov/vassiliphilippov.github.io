burster

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03011879-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: burster|oewn-03011879-n
a quantity of explosive to be set off at one time

Synset Examples

this cartridge has a powder charge of 50 grains

Reason

The sense of 'burster' as described here is too specific and technical, and is usually referred to as 'charge' or 'explosive charge' in general usage. 'Burster' in this sense does not appear as a distinct entry in major English dictionaries and is unlikely to be sought by general learners. Therefore, it should not be included.
