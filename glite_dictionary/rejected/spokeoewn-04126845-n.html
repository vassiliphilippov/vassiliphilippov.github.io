spoke

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04126845-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: spoke|oewn-04126845-n
one of the crosspieces that form the steps of a ladder

Reason

In this sense, 'spoke' refers to the crosspieces or steps of a ladder, but the standard and widely accepted term in English for these is 'rung.' 'Spoke' is not commonly or properly used in modern English to mean the steps of a ladder, so this sense would not appear as a main entry in reputable dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary.
