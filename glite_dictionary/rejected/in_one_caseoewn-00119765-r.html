Reason

The phrase 'in one case' with the sense 'on one occasion' is not an established idiom or fixed phrase in English and is not used in the same natural way as 'once.' This sense does not correspond to a dictionary-worthy entry; the phrase as defined does not function as a lexicalized unit, and such usage would not appear as a head entry in Oxford or similar dictionaries.
