the capital and largest city of Denmark; located on the island of Zealand

Synset Examples

Copenhagen is sometimes called the Paris of the North

Reason

The synset for 'Kobenhavn' is for the proper noun 'person' or 'place', but 'Kobenhavn' is a rare, non-English spelling for the city more commonly referred to as 'Copenhagen' in English. Dictionaries list 'Copenhagen'; 'Kobenhavn' might be referenced there as a variant or as the Danish name but does not merit a standalone entry in an English-English dictionary.
