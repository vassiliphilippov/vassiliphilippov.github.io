both of ours

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: both of ours|extra.001278
belonging to two people; shared ownership or responsibility

Synset Examples

The car is both of ours, so we share it.
They agreed that the project was both of ours.

Reason

'both of ours' is not a standard dictionary headword or lexicalized phrase. It is a compositional, transparent phrase made up of 'both' + 'of' + the possessive pronoun 'ours'; its meaning is directly derivable from its parts, and it does not function as a fixed expression or idiomatic phrase. Such phrases are not typically included as headwords in major dictionaries like Oxford.
