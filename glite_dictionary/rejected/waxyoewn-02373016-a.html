waxy

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02373016-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: waxy|oewn-02373016-a
easily impressed or influenced

Synset Examples

an impressionable youngster
an impressionable age
a waxy mind

Reason

This sense of 'waxy' as 'easily impressed or influenced' is obsolete and extremely rare; the primary and common usages of 'waxy' relate to the texture or appearance of wax. The metaphorical sense here would not be encountered by modern English learners and would not appear in current major dictionaries. Therefore, it should not be included.
