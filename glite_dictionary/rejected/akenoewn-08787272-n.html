Aken

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08787272-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Aken|oewn-08787272-n
a city in western Germany near the Dutch and Belgian borders; formerly it was Charlemagne's northern capital

Reason

The concept 'Aken' refers to the German name for the city commonly known in English as 'Aachen.' English dictionaries typically include the form 'Aachen,' not 'Aken.' Therefore, 'Aken' is not an appropriate headword for inclusion in an English-English dictionary.
