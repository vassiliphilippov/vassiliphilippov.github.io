advert

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01026199-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: advert|oewn-01026199-v
make reference to

Synset Examples

he was named in connection with the invention

Reason

The verb sense of 'advert' meaning 'make reference to' is archaic and extremely rare in modern English. This usage would not typically be found in contemporary dictionaries like Oxford and would be unlikely to be encountered by learners. The more common usage today is 'advert' as a noun meaning 'advertisement', or the verb 'advertise'. Therefore, this sense is excluded.
