tremor

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01892777-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: tremor|oewn-01892777-v
shake with seismic vibrations

Synset Examples

The earth was quaking

Reason

This verbal sense of 'tremor' meaning 'to shake with seismic vibrations' is not actually used in modern or standard English; 'tremor' is used as a noun. The usual verb for this meaning is 'quake' or 'tremble'. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
