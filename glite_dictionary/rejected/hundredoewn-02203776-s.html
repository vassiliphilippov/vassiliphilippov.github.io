Reason

The sense 'being ten more than ninety' of 'hundred' is a general numeric description and is not specifically tied to the headword 'hundred' as an adjective in actual usage. Dictionaries do include 'hundred' as a numeral, but not as an adjective with this description. 'Hundred' as a number/noun should be included, but not this synset.
