not tell you

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: not tell you|extra.020448
refusing to disclose information

Synset Examples

When asked about the surprise party, I said I'm not telling you.

Reason

The phrase 'not tell you' is simply a negative verb phrase and does not constitute a fixed expression, idiom, or phrasal verb in English. It would not appear as an entry in standard dictionaries such as Oxford; instead, dictionaries would include 'tell' or the phrasal verb 'tell on.' The synset here is just a compositional negative (not + tell + you) and is too general and grammatically generic to merit its own dictionary entry.
