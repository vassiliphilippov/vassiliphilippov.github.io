ingest

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00603650-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: ingest|oewn-00603650-v
take up mentally

Synset Examples

he absorbed the knowledge or beliefs of his tribe

Reason

This sense of 'ingest' (to take up mentally) is not commonly used in modern English. The primary meaning of 'ingest' is to take in food or drink into the body, not knowledge or information. The mental sense is rare, nonstandard, and would not appear as a main sense in Oxford Dictionary.
