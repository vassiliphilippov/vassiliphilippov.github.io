sheik

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10010564-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: sheik|oewn-10010564-n
a man who is much concerned with his dress and appearance

Reason

This sense of 'sheik' as 'a man who is much concerned with his dress and appearance' is highly dated and largely obsolete, originating from the 1920s as a slang usage based on the film 'The Sheik.' It does not appear as a common or current meaning in major dictionaries today, and thus would not be included in a modern dictionary except as a rare, archaic slang sense. The main sense of 'sheik' relates to an Arab leader or elder, which is a different concept.
