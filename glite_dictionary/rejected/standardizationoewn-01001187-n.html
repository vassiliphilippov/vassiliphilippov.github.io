standardization

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01001187-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: standardization|oewn-01001187-n
the act of checking or adjusting (by comparison with a standard) the accuracy of a measuring instrument

Synset Examples

the thermometer needed calibration

Reason

The synset's sense describes the process of checking or adjusting something to a standard (specifically, calibration in the context of measuring instruments), but 'standardization' is not typically used in this sense in standard English. The more precise and commonly used term for this process is 'calibration'. 'Standardization' generally refers to making things conform to a standard, especially in procedures or products, not the actual checking or adjustment of measurement instruments. This usage is too specialized and not representative of how 'standardization' is found in general or technical English dictionaries.
