Synset Examples

Reason

The synset 'drive out' with the sense 'clear out the chest and lungs' is too specific and not commonly accepted as a standard meaning of 'drive out' in general English usage. Rather, the verb 'expectorate' is the correct medical term for this action. 'Drive out' as phrasal verb is used for removing, expelling, or forcing something or someone to leave, but not specifically related to clearing the chest and lungs in English dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
