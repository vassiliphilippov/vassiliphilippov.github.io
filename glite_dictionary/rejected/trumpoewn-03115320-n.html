trump

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03115320-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: trump|oewn-03115320-n
a brass musical instrument with a brilliant tone; has a narrow tube and a flared bell and is played by means of valves

Reason

The synset refers to 'trump' as a noun meaning a brass musical instrument, but in contemporary standard English, the accepted word is 'trumpet'. 'Trump' in this sense is archaic/obsolete and is not in common usage as a standalone headword for this musical instrument today. Therefore, it does not warrant a separate dictionary entry under 'trump' by modern dictionary standards.
