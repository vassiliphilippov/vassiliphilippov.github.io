chemical science

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06094057-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: chemical science|oewn-06094057-n
the science of matter; the branch of the natural sciences dealing with the composition of substances and their properties and reactions

Reason

The concept 'chemical science' refers to the science of chemistry, which is the standard and much more common dictionary headword. 'Chemical science' is a general, descriptive phrase and not an established dictionary entry; it is better covered under 'chemistry.'
