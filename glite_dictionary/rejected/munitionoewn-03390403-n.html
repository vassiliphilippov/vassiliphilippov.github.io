munition

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03390403-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: munition|oewn-03390403-n
defensive structure consisting of walls or mounds built around a stronghold to strengthen it

Reason

This sense of 'munition' (as a defensive structure or earthwork) is archaic and extremely rare; it does not reflect contemporary or standard usage. Today, 'munition' refers to military weapons, ammunition, and related equipment. The structural sense is not included in modern English dictionaries and should be excluded.
