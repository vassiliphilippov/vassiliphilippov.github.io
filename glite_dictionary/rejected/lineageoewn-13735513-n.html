lineage

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13735513-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: lineage|oewn-13735513-n
the number of lines in a piece of printed material

Reason

This sense of 'lineage', meaning the number of lines in a piece of printed material, is very obscure and not widely used in modern English. The common, dictionary-worthy meaning of 'lineage' relates to ancestry or descent, not to lines in printed matter. This sense would not be included in standard dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary.
