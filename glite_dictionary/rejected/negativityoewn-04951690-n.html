negativity

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04951690-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: negativity|oewn-04951690-n
(chemistry) the tendency of an atom or radical to attract electrons in the formation of an ionic bond

Reason

This sense of 'negativity' is a highly technical chemical property and is very rarely used compared to the standard term 'electronegativity'. 'Negativity' in this sense would not appear as a main entry or a separate sense in a general English dictionary like Oxford; instead, 'electronegativity' is the correct scientific term. Therefore, this concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
