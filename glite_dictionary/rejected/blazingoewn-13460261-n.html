blazing

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13460261-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: blazing|oewn-13460261-n
a strong flame that burns brightly

Synset Examples

the blaze spread rapidly

Reason

The noun sense 'blazing' as 'a strong flame that burns brightly' is not standard; 'blaze' is the correct noun, while 'blazing' is the adjective or verb form. This usage does not appear in standard dictionaries as a noun. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
