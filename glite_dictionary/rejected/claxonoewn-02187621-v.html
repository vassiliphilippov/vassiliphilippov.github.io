make a strident noise

Synset Examples

The horns of the taxis blared

Reason

The verb 'claxon' meaning 'to make a strident noise' is exceedingly rare and not widely recognized in modern English dictionaries. The noun 'klaxon' (a type of loud horn) exists, but the verb form is not standard or attested in major references like the Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
