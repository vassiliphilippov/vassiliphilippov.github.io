nationalisation

Internal ID: nationalisation|oewn-01154956-n
the action of forming or becoming a nation

Reason

The sense 'the action of forming or becoming a nation' for 'nationalisation' is rare, overly general, and not commonly used in modern English; it does not refer to the widely recognized meaning relating to transferring private assets to public ownership. Therefore, this sense should not be included as a separate dictionary entry.
