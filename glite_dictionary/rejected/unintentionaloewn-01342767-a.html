Synset Examples

Reason

The synset described refers not to 'unintentional' but to the opposite—actions that are done with purpose and intent (deliberate, intentional, designed). Therefore, the synset description is not related to the headword 'unintentional.' As such, this concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
