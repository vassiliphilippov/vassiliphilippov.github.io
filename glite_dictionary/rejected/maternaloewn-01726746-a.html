Synset Examples

parental guidance

Reason

This concept defines 'maternal' as 'relating to or characteristic of or befitting a parent', which corresponds to the general sense of 'parental'. 'Maternal' in standard English specifically relates to mothers (not just parents in general). Therefore, including this overly general synset for 'maternal' would be misleading and not in line with standard dictionary usage. The concept should be excluded.
