fixture

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00267650-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: fixture|oewn-00267650-n
the act of putting something in working order again

Reason

This sense of 'fixture' does not correspond to standard usage in contemporary English. 'Fixture' as a noun typically refers to a fixed item in a building or a regular event, not 'the act of putting something in working order again.' That definition properly belongs to words like 'repair' or 'restoration,' not 'fixture.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
