Pepto-bismal

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14801927-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Pepto-bismal|oewn-14801927-n
an antacid

Reason

The headword 'Pepto-bismal' appears to be a misspelling of 'Pepto-Bismol', a trademarked brand name for an antacid. Oxford and similar dictionaries include 'Pepto-Bismol' but not the misspelled form. The synset is for the general concept 'antacid', which is too general and not specific to this brand name. Therefore, this specific concept should not be included.
