Reason

The sense 'crapshoot' as 'playing craps' is overly general and not the standard or recognizable dictionary sense of 'crapshoot.' In reputable dictionaries, 'crapshoot' is included for its metaphorical meaning ('a risky or unpredictable venture'), not literally 'playing craps.' The literal sense is not well-attested or relevant for learners, so it should be excluded.
