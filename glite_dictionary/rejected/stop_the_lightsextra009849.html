Reason

The phrase 'stop the lights' is not a standard or common English collocation for 'turn off the lights' or 'traffic signals.' This use does not appear in reputable dictionaries such as Oxford, and actual usage in natural English is extremely rare or nonstandard. Therefore, it should not be included as a dictionary entry.
