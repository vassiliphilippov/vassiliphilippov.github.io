slightly

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00460568-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: slightly|oewn-00460568-r
in a slim or slender manner

Synset Examples

a slenderly built woman
slightly built

Reason

This sense of 'slightly' ('in a slim or slender manner') is covered by the broader, common use of 'slightly' to mean 'a little' or 'to a small degree'. The sense here is very narrow and not distinct enough to merit a separate dictionary entry. It is not used frequently or idiomatically to specifically mean 'in a slim or slender manner'; usually 'slenderly' would serve that specific sense. Therefore, this particular synset should not be included as a standalone entry.
