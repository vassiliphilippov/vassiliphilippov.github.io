Reason

'Altitudinous summit' is not an established compound in English, nor is it a fixed phrase that would appear in a major dictionary. It simply combines 'altitudinous' (very high) and 'summit' (top of a mountain), both of which are common words individually. This phrase is an adjective+noun phrase constructed for description, not a lexicalized entry.
