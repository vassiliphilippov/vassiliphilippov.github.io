in height

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02394294-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: in height|oewn-02394294-s
having a specified height

Synset Examples

five feet tall
five feet in height

Reason

The concept 'in height' as described is simply a prepositional phrase for expressing measurement, not an independent lexical headword. Dictionaries like Oxford do not typically include such set grammatical phrases for dimension measurement unless they are idiomatic, which this is not. It is not a sense directly related to a lexical headword but a compositional use of 'in' and 'height', and therefore should not be included.
