Erivan

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09040400-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Erivan|oewn-09040400-n
capital of Armenia

Reason

'Erivan' is an alternative form or older spelling of 'Yerevan', the capital of Armenia. The modern, standard spelling is 'Yerevan', which is the term that would be included in a contemporary English-English dictionary. 'Erivan' is considered archaic and primarily of historical interest, so it is generally not included as a headword in major modern dictionaries.
