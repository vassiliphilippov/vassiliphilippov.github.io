the Western world

Reason

This concept sense of 'domain' as 'people in general or a distinctive group of people' is not a standard or widely recognized meaning in contemporary English usage and would not appear in the Oxford Dictionary. The example given ('the Western world') does not exemplify 'domain' in this sense in natural usage. Typically, 'domain' refers to a field, area of activity, or internet address space, not a group of people.
