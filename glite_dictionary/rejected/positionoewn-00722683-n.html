position

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00722683-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: position|oewn-00722683-n
the post or function properly or customarily occupied or served by another

Synset Examples

can you go in my stead?

Reason

This synset defines a very general meaning related to occupying a post, function, or place, which is not specific to the headword 'position' but rather to words like 'stead', 'lieu', or possibly 'place' in specific constructions. This sense is too general and not particular to 'position' as used in standard dictionaries. Therefore, it should not be included as a standalone entry for 'position'.
