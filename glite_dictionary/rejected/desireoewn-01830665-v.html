desire

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01830665-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: desire|oewn-01830665-v
expect and wish

Synset Examples

I trust you will behave better from now on
I hope she understands that she cannot expect a raise

Reason

This verbal sense of 'desire' as 'expect and wish'—meaning 'to hope' or 'to expect'—is not a standard or commonly used sense in contemporary English. In standard dictionaries like Oxford, 'desire' as a verb primarily means 'to want strongly' or 'to wish for,' not 'to expect.' The examples given correspond more closely to verbs like 'hope' or 'expect.' Including this sense would be misleading to learners.
