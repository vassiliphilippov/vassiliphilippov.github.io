misspeak

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00953633-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: misspeak|oewn-00953633-v
pronounce a word incorrectly

Synset Examples

She mispronounces many Latinate words

Reason

This specific concept of 'misspeak' as 'pronounce a word incorrectly' is not standard usage; the main accepted sense of 'misspeak' is 'to speak incorrectly or make a verbal mistake,' not specifically mispronunciation. The direct, standard term for pronouncing a word incorrectly is 'mispronounce.' Therefore, this concept should not be included as 'misspeak' in a standard dictionary.
