citrous fruit

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07763031-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: citrous fruit|oewn-07763031-n
any of numerous fruits of the genus Citrus having thick rind and juicy pulp; grown in warm regions

Reason

The concept 'citrous fruit' is defined as any fruit of the genus Citrus, but the standard and universally recognized term is 'citrus fruit.' The form 'citrous' is rarely used and not established in authoritative dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, this form does not warrant its own entry.
