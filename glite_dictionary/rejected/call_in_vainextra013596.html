call in vain

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: call in vain|extra.013596
to attempt without success

Synset Examples

They called in vain, hoping for a response.

Reason

The phrase 'call in vain' is a compositional and transparent combination of 'call' and 'in vain', not an established idiom or lexical unit. Its meaning is directly derived from its parts ('in vain' meaning 'without success'), so it does not warrant a separate dictionary entry in a descriptive dictionary like Oxford.
