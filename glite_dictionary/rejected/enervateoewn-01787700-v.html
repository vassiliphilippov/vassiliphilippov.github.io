disturb the composure of

Reason

This concept, 'enervate' meaning 'disturb the composure of,' is not a standard or attested dictionary sense. The primary meaning of 'enervate' is 'to weaken' or 'to drain of energy,' not 'to disturb the composure.' Therefore, this sense should not be included in a modern English-English dictionary.
