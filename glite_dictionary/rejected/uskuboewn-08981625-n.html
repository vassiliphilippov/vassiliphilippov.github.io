Reason

Although 'Uskub' refers historically to the city now known as Skopje, the current and standard English name for the capital of North Macedonia is 'Skopje.' 'Uskub' is an obsolete or archaic variant, rarely encountered in modern English except in historical texts. Therefore, this concept—using 'Uskub' as the modern capital—is not relevant for a current English dictionary except in its historical sense, which would be labeled archaic. Since the synset refers to the current capital by this outdated name, it is filtered out.
