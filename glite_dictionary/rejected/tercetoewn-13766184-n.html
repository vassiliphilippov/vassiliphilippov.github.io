tercet

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13766184-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: tercet|oewn-13766184-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of one and one and one

Reason

The synset described here is the basic cardinal number 3 ('the sum of one and one and one'), which is the concept 'three' or '3'. 'Tercet', in a normal English dictionary, refers exclusively to a group of three lines of verse (a poetic term), not the number 3. Therefore, this 'number' synset does not correspond to 'tercet' and should not be included.
