reprobate

WordNet ID: oewn-00689082-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: reprobate|oewn-00689082-v
reject (documents) as invalid

Reason

Although 'reprobate' as a verb meaning 'reject (documents) as invalid' has some historical usage, it is archaic and extremely rare in modern English. This sense is not included in current major dictionaries and would not appear in a modern Oxford-style dictionary. The headword 'reprobate' is mainly used as a noun or adjective meaning 'an unprincipled person' or 'morally unprincipled'. Therefore, this verb sense should be omitted.
