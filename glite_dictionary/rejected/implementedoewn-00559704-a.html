implemented

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00559704-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: implemented|oewn-00559704-a
forced or compelled or put in force

Synset Examples

a life of enforced inactivity
enforced obedience

Reason

The synset described ("forced or compelled or put in force") is more appropriately represented by the adjective 'enforced,' not 'implemented.' The adjective 'implemented' is not standard in this sense in English and does not warrant its own entry as an adjective with this meaning. Oxford and most major dictionaries do not list 'implemented' as an adjective meaning 'enforced' or 'compelled.'
