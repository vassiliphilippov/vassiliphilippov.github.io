Reason

The synset 'fish genus' is overly general and refers to any genus of fish rather than a specific genus or commonly used term. Such generic scientific class words (like 'fish genus', 'mammal species') are not included as headwords in standard English dictionaries like Oxford. Only names of actual genera or distinct common terms are included.
