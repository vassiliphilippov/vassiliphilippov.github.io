Reason

The synset definition given ('a person forced to flee from home or country') does not correspond with the standard meaning of 'stateless person.' It defines a broader concept more akin to 'refugee,' rather than the established legal and general meaning of 'stateless person' (i.e., 'a person who is not considered as a national by any state under the operation of its law'). Since the synset does not reflect the actual meaning of the headword, it should not be included as described here.
