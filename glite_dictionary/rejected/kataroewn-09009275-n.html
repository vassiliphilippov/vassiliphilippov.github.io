an Arab country on the peninsula of Qatar; achieved independence from the United Kingdom in 1971; the economy is dominated by oil

Reason

'Katar' is an alternate spelling of 'Qatar', which is the standard English name for the country. The WordNet concept aligns with the country, a proper noun that would appear in the dictionary under the standard spelling 'Qatar' rather than 'Katar'. Including 'Katar' as a separate dictionary entry is unnecessary and could be confusing. Any variant spellings should be redirected to the primary entry 'Qatar'.
