Internal ID: Hindian custom|extra.017838
traditions of the Hindu culture

Synset Examples

She learned about Hindian customs during her travels.

Reason

The concept 'Hindian custom' refers to 'traditions of the Hindu culture,' but the correct term in standard English is 'Hindu custom.' 'Hindian' is not a recognized or standard word, and would not be included in a reputable dictionary such as Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
