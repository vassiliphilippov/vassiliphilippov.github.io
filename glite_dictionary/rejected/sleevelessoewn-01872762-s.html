Reason

The sense 'sleeveless' meaning 'unproductive of success' is archaic and extremely rare, likely only encountered in historical texts. In modern English, 'sleeveless' is understood almost exclusively as describing a garment without sleeves. Including this obsolete sense would not be useful for most learners or users of a contemporary dictionary.
