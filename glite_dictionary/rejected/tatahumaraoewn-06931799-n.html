Tatahumara

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06931799-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Tatahumara|oewn-06931799-n
the Uto-Aztecan language of the Tatahumara

Reason

The concept 'Tatahumara' as defined here refers only to 'the Uto-Aztecan language of the Tatahumara.' However, this appears to be a misspelling or alternative spelling of 'Tarahumara,' which is the accepted spelling for the people and their language. Dictionaries typically use the established term. As such, this form should be excluded. The concept is moreover very specific and may not have broad enough usage under this variant spelling for inclusion.
