Source: WordNet

Synset POS: v
Internal ID: purpose|oewn-00701392-v
reach a decision

Synset Examples

he resolved never to drink again

Reason

The verb sense of 'purpose' meaning 'to reach a decision' is obsolete and extremely rare in contemporary English; it does not appear as an active verb in modern dictionaries. The example given ('he resolved never to drink again') uses 'resolve' rather than 'purpose'. This usage is archaic and not needed for inclusion in a modern general dictionary.
