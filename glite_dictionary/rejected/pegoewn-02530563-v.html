Synset POS: v
Internal ID: peg|oewn-02530563-v
succeed in obtaining a position

Synset Examples

He nailed down a spot at Harvard

Reason

This sense of 'peg' as 'to succeed in obtaining a position' is extremely rare and not standard; major English dictionaries such as Oxford and Merriam-Webster do not include this meaning for 'peg'. The example is more idiomatic for verbs like 'nail down' or 'land'. Therefore, it should not be included as a standalone sense in the dictionary.
