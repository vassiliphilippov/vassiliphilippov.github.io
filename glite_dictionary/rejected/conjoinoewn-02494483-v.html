conjoin

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02494483-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: conjoin|oewn-02494483-v
take in marriage; married

Reason

This particular sense of 'conjoin' as 'take in marriage' or 'married' is archaic and extremely rare. The modern and common use of 'conjoin' is 'to join together; unite.' The marriage sense would not be included as a distinct entry in a contemporary dictionary like Oxford, except as an obsolete or archaic subsense, and even then, it is not usual or broadly attested.
