take a hop

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01896097-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: take a hop|oewn-01896097-v
spring back; spring away from an impact

Synset Examples

The rubber ball bounced
These particles do not resile but they unite after they collide

Reason

The phrase 'take a hop' is not a standard or recognized way to mean 'spring back' or 'rebound' in contemporary English. The example sentences use 'bounce' and 'resile', not 'take a hop'. This is not a conventional or dictionary-worthy usage, and would not appear as an entry similar to those in the Oxford Dictionary.
