Hastinapura

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06475229-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Hastinapura|oewn-06475229-n
one of two classical Hindu epics; a great collection of poetry worked into and around a central heroic narrative (eight times as large as the Iliad and Odyssey combined)

Reason

The synset definition is incorrect for 'Hastinapura'; the description refers to a classical Hindu epic, but 'Hastinapura' is in fact the name of an ancient city in Indian mythology, not an epic. The synset is more appropriately linked to the epic 'Mahabharata', not to this headword. Therefore, this concept should not be included as is.
