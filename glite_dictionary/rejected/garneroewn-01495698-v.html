Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01495698-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: garner|oewn-01495698-v
store grain

Reason

This sense of 'garner' (meaning specifically 'to store grain') is archaic and has fallen almost entirely out of modern usage. The more common meaning of 'garner' today is 'to gather or collect (something, especially information or approval).' The archaic agricultural sense is outdated for general learners, so it does not merit its own entry in a modern English dictionary unless marked as archaic or historical.
