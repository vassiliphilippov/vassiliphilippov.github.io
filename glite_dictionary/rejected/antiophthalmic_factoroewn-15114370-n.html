antiophthalmic factor

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15114370-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: antiophthalmic factor|oewn-15114370-n
any of several fat-soluble vitamins essential for normal vision; prevents night blindness or inflammation or dryness of the eyes

Reason

The term 'antiophthalmic factor' is an outdated, technical synonym for vitamin A and is not commonly used in modern English. It would not typically appear as a headword in contemporary general dictionaries like Oxford; 'vitamin A' is the standard term. Therefore, it should not be included.
