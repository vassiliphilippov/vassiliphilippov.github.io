Sun

WordNet ID: oewn-15188844-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Sun|oewn-15188844-n
first day of the week; observed as a day of rest and worship by most Christians

Reason

This sense corresponds to the abbreviation "Sun" for "Sunday," not the word "Sun" as a standalone headword. The base word "Sun" (as in the star) should be defined, but the specific sense as an abbreviation for a day of the week does not appear independently in dictionaries; it is typically found under "Sunday."
