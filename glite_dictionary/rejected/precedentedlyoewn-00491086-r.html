Reason

'Precedentedly' is not a standard English adverb and is not attested in major dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. It is formed analogously from 'precedent,' but is very rarely used and not recognized as conventional. Therefore, it should not be included in a dictionary intended for learners or general users.
