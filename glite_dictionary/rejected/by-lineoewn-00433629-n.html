by-line

WordNet ID: oewn-00433629-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: by-line|oewn-00433629-n
an auxiliary activity

Reason

The synset 'an auxiliary activity' is too general and not specifically related to the primary meaning of 'by-line' as it appears in dictionaries. 'By-line' in common use refers to either the line in a newspaper naming the author of an article or, chiefly in British English, a secondary road. This sense, as 'an auxiliary activity,' does not correspond to a standard or dictionary-worthy sense for 'by-line.'
