Internal ID: by artificial means|oewn-00141600-r
not according to nature; not by natural means

Synset Examples

artificially induced conditions

Reason

The concept 'by artificial means' with this synset refers to the adverb 'artificially'. The phrase itself is not commonly used as a fixed dictionary entry; rather, its sense is covered by the standard adverb 'artificially' in reputable dictionaries. Therefore, it is too general and redundant as a headword, and should not be included as a separate entry.
