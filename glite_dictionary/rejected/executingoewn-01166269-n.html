executing

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01166269-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: executing|oewn-01166269-n
putting a condemned person to death

Reason

This sense is a noun use of the present participle 'executing,' but in standard dictionaries, the noun for 'putting a condemned person to death' is 'execution,' not 'executing.' The participle form is not used as a noun in this sense and would not appear as such in Oxford or similar dictionaries.
