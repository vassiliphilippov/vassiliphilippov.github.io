felt a bit loco

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: felt a bit loco|extra.016034
to feel somewhat crazy or irrational

Synset Examples

He felt a bit loco after staying up all night.

Reason

The phrase 'felt a bit loco' is a free combination of the verb 'felt', the intensifier 'a bit', and 'loco' (meaning 'crazy'), rather than a set phrase or idiom with a fixed meaning. Only 'loco' as an adjective meaning 'crazy' would be appropriate for inclusion. This particular phrasing would not be included as a headword in a standard dictionary.
