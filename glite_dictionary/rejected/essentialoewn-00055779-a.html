essential

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00055779-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: essential|oewn-00055779-a
defining rights and duties as opposed to giving the rules by which rights and duties are established

Synset Examples

substantive law

Reason

This sense of 'essential' refers to a very specific legal distinction ('defining rights and duties'), but is not a meaning used for 'essential' in general English. In common English, 'essential' means 'absolutely necessary' or 'fundamental,' while this legal sense is specific to terms like 'substantive law.' The connection to the ordinary headword is too technical and narrow for a general-use dictionary, and it would not appear as a main word sense in major dictionaries. Therefore, it should be excluded.
