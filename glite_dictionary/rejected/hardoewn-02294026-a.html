hard

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02294026-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: hard|oewn-02294026-a
produced without vibration of the vocal cords

Synset Examples

unvoiced consonants such as ‘p’ and ‘k’ and ‘s’

Reason

This sense of 'hard' is a very technical phonetic usage referring specifically to consonants produced without vibration of the vocal cords (i.e., voiceless sounds like 'p', 'k', 's'). In modern English dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary, this use is not considered a standard meaning of 'hard' as an adjective; instead, terms like 'voiceless' or 'unvoiced' are used. Therefore, this concept is too technical and not relevant to general dictionary users, so it should be excluded.
