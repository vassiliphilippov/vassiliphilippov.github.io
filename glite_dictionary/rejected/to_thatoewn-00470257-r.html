Synset Examples

with all the appurtenances fitting thereto

Reason

The phrase 'to that' in this sense is only used in highly formal or archaic legal language, and usually appears as 'thereto.' The meaning here is covered by the adverb 'thereto,' not the phrase 'to that.' Thus, 'to that' as an adverb is too general and does not have an established use as a set phrase in English dictionaries.
