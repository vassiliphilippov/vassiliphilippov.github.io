Reason

'slower' as used here is the comparative form of 'slowly' used adverbially, but 'slower' itself is not an independent adverbial entry in major dictionaries; rather, it's a morphological form (comparative) of the adjective/adverb 'slow'. Dictionaries typically list the base form with its comparative forms, not as separate entries.
