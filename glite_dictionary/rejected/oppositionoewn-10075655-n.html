opposition

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10075655-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: opposition|oewn-10075655-n
an armed adversary (especially a member of an opposing military force)

Synset Examples

a soldier must be prepared to kill his enemies

Reason

The synset refers to 'opposition' as an individual adversary or enemy, which is not a standard or recognized sense in contemporary English dictionaries. In usage, 'opposition' normally refers to a group challenging or resisting another group, not to an individual armed adversary. This sense would not be included in the Oxford Dictionary or a similar work.
