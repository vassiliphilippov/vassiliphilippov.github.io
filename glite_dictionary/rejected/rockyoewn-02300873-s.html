Synset Examples

Reason

This concept refers to 'rocky' as 'liable to rock' (to move back and forth or be unstable), which is an extremely rare and nonstandard adjectival usage based on the verb 'to rock.' In standard English dictionaries, 'rocky' almost always refers to being covered with or consisting of rock, or being unstable/difficult (in a figurative sense). The use of 'rocky' as 'liable to rock' is not conventional, is rarely encountered, and does not meet the inclusion criteria. This sense would not generally appear in a dictionary like Oxford.
