come out

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02086193-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: come out|oewn-02086193-v
bulge outward

Synset Examples

His eyes popped

Reason

This sense of 'come out' (meaning to bulge outward, as in 'His eyes popped') is highly idiomatic and rare. The verb 'to pop' is the primary word for this meaning, and 'come out' is not generally recognized in standard dictionaries for this sense. It would not commonly appear as such in an Oxford-style dictionary under 'come out.'
