the use of information or information technology during a time of crisis or conflict to achieve or promote specific objectives over a specific adversary or adversaries

Reason

The headword 'IW' in this context stands for 'information warfare,' serving as an abbreviation or acronym. Dictionary entries typically do not include every possible acronym unless widely established. 'Information warfare' would be the primary dictionary entry, not the abbreviation 'IW' itself, unless it is extremely common and recognized (which it is not for general English usage). Therefore, this concept does not warrant a separate entry.
