Reason

The synset provided corresponds to an adjective sense ('tending to bring about; being partly responsible for'), but the headword 'tributary' normally functions as a noun meaning a river or stream that flows into a larger body of water. The adjective sense here is not idiomatic for 'tributary' but rather for 'contributory' or 'contributing.' Therefore, this usage does not relate to the actual headword and should not be included in the dictionary for 'tributary.'
