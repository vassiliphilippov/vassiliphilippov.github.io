loud sound

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: loud sound|extra.019439
a noise that is strong or intense

Synset Examples

His loud bark startled everyone at the park.

Reason

The concept 'loud sound' is a general phrase referring to any strong or intense noise, not a lexicalized term. It would not appear as a headword in a dictionary like Oxford; dictionaries would instead have entries for 'loud', 'sound', and specific types of noises. Therefore, it should be excluded.
