rise

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07341888-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: rise|oewn-07341888-n
(theology) the origination of the Holy Spirit at Pentecost

Synset Examples

the emanation of the Holy Spirit
the rising of the Holy Ghost
the doctrine of the procession of the Holy Spirit from the Father and the Son

Reason

This sense of 'rise' refers to a highly specialized theological event described in technical terms as 'the origination of the Holy Spirit at Pentecost.' However, 'rise' is not used as a common noun in this way in standard English; this meaning is not conventional or recognized in general dictionaries like Oxford. The concept is too specialized and not directly tied to the typical or even specialized senses of 'rise' as a noun. Therefore, it should not be included.
