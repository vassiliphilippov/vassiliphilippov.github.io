spoke zingingly

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: spoke zingingly|extra.023582
to speak in an exciting or lively manner

Synset Examples

He spoke zingingly about the new restaurant in town.

Reason

The adverb 'zingingly' is extremely rare, nonstandard, and does not appear in Oxford or other major dictionaries. It is formed by adding the adverbial suffix '-ly' to 'zinging', itself a rare form, and is not in real use as a common word. Therefore, it should not be included in a standard English-English dictionary.
