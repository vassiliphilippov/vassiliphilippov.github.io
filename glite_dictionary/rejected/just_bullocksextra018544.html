Synset Examples

Reason

'just bullocks' as used here is a nonstandard, likely erroneous, variant of the established slang word 'bollocks' (British), meaning 'nonsense'. It does not represent a recognized word, idiom, or fixed phrase, and would not appear in Oxford or similar dictionaries. This sense should be excluded.
