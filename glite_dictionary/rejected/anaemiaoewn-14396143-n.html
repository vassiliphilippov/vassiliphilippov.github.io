anaemia

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14396143-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: anaemia|oewn-14396143-n
a lack of vitality

Reason

This sense of 'anaemia' as a general 'lack of vitality' is figurative and rare. The primary meaning in English is the medical condition related to a deficiency of red blood cells. The figurative usage is uncommon and would not typically appear as a separate entry in a major English dictionary like Oxford. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a distinct dictionary entry.
