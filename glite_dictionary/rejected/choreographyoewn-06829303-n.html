choreography

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06829303-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: choreography|oewn-06829303-n
a notation used by choreographers

Reason

This sense of 'choreography' as 'a notation used by choreographers' is too specific and not commonly recognized as a separate meaning distinct from the main sense of 'the art of designing sequences of movements.' Notation systems themselves have specific names, such as Benesh notation or Labanotation. As a dictionary entry, this meaning does not independently merit inclusion beyond the main sense.
