international scale

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13874797-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: international scale|oewn-13874797-n
a temperature scale that defines the freezing point of water as 0 degrees and the boiling point of water as 100 degrees

Reason

The synset definition describes a temperature scale but 'international scale' itself is not the established term for this meaning. The correct term is 'Celsius scale' or 'centigrade scale'. There is no widespread dictionary entry for 'international scale' with this sense. Therefore, it should be excluded.
