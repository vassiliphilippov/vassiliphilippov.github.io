Reason

The phrasal verb 'even off' in the sense of 'adjust for' is not an established or widely recognized usage in standard English dictionaries such as Oxford. While 'even off' can mean to make flat or level, the use of it to mean 'adjust for' (i.e., compensate for) is not conventional. Therefore, this concept should not be included in the dictionary.
