turning point

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03114137-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: turning point|oewn-03114137-n
the intersection of two streets

Synset Examples

standing on the corner watching all the girls go by

Reason

The synset 'intersection of two streets' is too general and is not specific to the headword 'turning point'; 'corner' is the standard term for this sense. 'Turning point' in standard usage typically refers to a critical moment of change, not a street intersection. Thus, this sense should not be included.
