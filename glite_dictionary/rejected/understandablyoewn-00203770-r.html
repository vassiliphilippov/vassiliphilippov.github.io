in an intelligible manner

Synset Examples

the foreigner spoke to us quite intelligibly

Reason

The concept describes 'understandably' with the sense 'in an intelligible manner,' but that is not the natural or standard usage for 'understandably' in modern English. 'Understandably' is mainly used to mean 'in a way that can be understood; as is reasonable.' The sense 'in an intelligible manner' is correctly covered by 'intelligibly,' not 'understandably.' This concept should be excluded as it does not reflect the normal dictionary entry for 'understandably.'
