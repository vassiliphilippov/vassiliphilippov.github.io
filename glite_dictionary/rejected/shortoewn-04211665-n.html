Reason

This sense of 'short' refers specifically to the position of the shortstop in baseball; however, the independent noun 'short' in this specific context is not commonly used as a standalone noun in general English. The primary baseball term is 'shortstop.' This is too technical and obscure for general dictionary inclusion, and not the standard lexical usage of 'short.'
