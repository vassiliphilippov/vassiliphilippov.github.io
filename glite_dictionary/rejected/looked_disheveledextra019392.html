Synset Examples

He looked shevelled after running in the wind.

Reason

The phrase 'looked disheveled' is a simple past form of 'look disheveled', which itself is a transparent combination of the verb 'look' and the adjective 'disheveled'. Dictionaries would not separately list 'looked disheveled' as a headword or fixed phrase; instead, users can look up 'disheveled'. Therefore, this is too compositional and not a dictionary-worthy entry.
