spurt

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01316071-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: spurt|oewn-01316071-v
wet with a spurt of liquid

Synset Examples

spurt the wall with water

Reason

This verb sense of 'spurt' meaning 'to wet something with a spurt of liquid' is obscure, rare, and not commonly recognized in modern English usage. Standard dictionaries do not list this causative/active usage ('spurt the wall'), focusing instead on 'spurt' as a verb meaning 'to gush out suddenly in a stream.' The concept here is too marginal and would not appear as a primary or secondary sense in a typical dictionary.
