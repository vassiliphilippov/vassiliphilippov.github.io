responsibility

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14539235-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: responsibility|oewn-14539235-n
the proper sphere or extent of your activities

Synset Examples

it was his province to take care of himself

Reason

This sense of 'responsibility' refers broadly to the sphere or extent of activities one is in charge of, but the actual example and the primary meaning relates to 'province' or 'domain' as a person's area of duty. 'Responsibility' in standard English does not typically denote 'sphere of activities' as its central or dictionary meaning, and such a sense would not appear as a main or common entry in Oxford or similar dictionaries. Therefore, this specific concept should be excluded.
