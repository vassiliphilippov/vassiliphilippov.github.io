bailiwick

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06005806-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: bailiwick|oewn-06005806-n
a branch of knowledge

Synset Examples

in what discipline is his doctorate?
teachers should be well trained in their subject
anthropology is the study of human beings

Reason

The synset refers to the general idea of a 'branch of knowledge' (such as 'discipline', 'subject', or 'field'), which is not the primary or common sense of 'bailiwick'. In modern English, 'bailiwick' is used to mean 'area of expertise' or 'sphere of authority/responsibility,' not literally as any branch of knowledge. The link between the headword and this general synset is too tenuous and broad. Therefore, this concept should not be included for 'bailiwick.'
