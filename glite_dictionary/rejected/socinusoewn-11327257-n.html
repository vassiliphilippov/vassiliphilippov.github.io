Internal ID: Socinus|oewn-11327257-n
Italian theologian who argued against Trinitarianism (1539-1604)

Reason

The synset is 'person' for the proper noun 'Socinus,' which is too general. English dictionaries typically do not define all personal names unless they are widely used eponyms or historical terms. In this context, 'Socinus' as a person (Fausto Sozzini) would appear in an encyclopedia, not a dictionary.
