gemination

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01021645-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: gemination|oewn-01021645-n
the act of copying or making a duplicate (or duplicates) of something

Synset Examples

this kind of duplication is wasteful

Reason

The synset described relates to 'gemination' as the act of copying or making a duplicate, but in standard English usage, 'gemination' refers to the doubling of a consonant or syllable in linguistics and phonetics, not general duplication or copying. The synset is not directly related to the normal English meaning of the headword and is too general. Therefore, it should not be included as a dictionary entry for this sense.
