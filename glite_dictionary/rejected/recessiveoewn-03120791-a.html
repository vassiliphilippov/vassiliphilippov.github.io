of or pertaining to a recession

Reason

The concept 'recessive' in the sense of 'of or pertaining to a recession' is not in standard usage and does not appear in major dictionaries. The common dictionary sense of 'recessive' relates to genetics (referring to a gene or trait that is not expressed unless inherited from both parents), not to 'recession' in the economic or general sense. Therefore, this sense is too marginal or not well-attested to warrant inclusion.
