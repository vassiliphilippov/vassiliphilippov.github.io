Kalka

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06941785-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Kalka|oewn-06941785-n
the language of the Khalkha that is the official language of the Mongolian People's Republic

Reason

The concept 'Kalka' in this sense refers to the language of the Khalkha, which is properly called 'Khalkha' or 'Mongolian' in English. 'Kalka' is not the standard or canonical name for the language, and this form is not widely recognized in English dictionaries. This sense is too obscure and not used in standard English usage; it does not merit its own dictionary entry.
