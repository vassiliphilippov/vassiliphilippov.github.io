pouched rat

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02356513-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: pouched rat|oewn-02356513-n
burrowing rodent of the family Geomyidae having large external cheek pouches; of Central America and southwestern North America

Reason

The synset described refers to a 'burrowing rodent of the family Geomyidae' found in Central and southwestern North America, which corresponds to 'pocket gopher,' not the African rodent commonly called 'pouched rat.' The headword 'pouched rat' is misleading for the given definition, which is too general and refers to a different animal. This entry should be excluded to avoid confusion.
