Synset POS: n

the act of walking with a slow heavy gait

Synset Examples

I could recognize his plod anywhere

Reason

The synset described refers to the noun form "plodding" meaning the act of walking with a slow, heavy gait, but this usage is exceedingly rare and not commonly recognized in modern English dictionaries, where "plodding" is chiefly an adjective (slow, laborious) or a present participle of the verb "plod." This noun sense would not typically appear as a headword in a standard dictionary.
