in accordance with moral or social standards

Synset Examples

that serves him right

Reason

Exclude: The synset refers to an adverbial use ('right' meaning 'in a morally appropriate manner'), which is a very rare, idiomatic usage and mostly found as part of fixed expressions like 'do right by'. The dictionary would instead include 'right' as a noun, adjective, adverb, and verb in their standard senses, not this highly idiomatic use. The provided examples are either idiomatic or do not represent a general adverbial sense deserving a separate entry.
