XXVI

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13771030-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: XXVI|oewn-13771030-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of twenty-five and one

Reason

'XXVI' is a Roman numeral for '26', which is covered under entries for Roman numerals and individual numbers. It is not commonly listed as a separate dictionary entry in major English dictionaries. Its meaning is too general and predictable from knowledge of Roman numerals.
