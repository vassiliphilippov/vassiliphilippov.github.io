Reason

The synset 'being in progress now' for 'on-line' is overly general and does not correspond to standard modern dictionary usage. The commonly used sense of 'online' refers to being connected to a computer network or the internet, not simply being in progress. This sense would not appear as a main entry in a modern English-English dictionary like Oxford or Merriam-Webster.
