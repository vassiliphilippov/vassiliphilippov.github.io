twice

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00083743-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: twice|oewn-00083743-r
to double the degree

Synset Examples

she was doubly rewarded
his eyes were double bright

Reason

This sense describes 'twice' as an adverb of degree ('to double the degree'), but the canonical word for this is 'doubly' or 'double', not 'twice'. 'Twice' is not naturally used in modern English to mean 'doubly' as in 'his eyes were twice bright'; standard usage is 'twice as bright'. Therefore, this particular sense of 'twice' is too indirect and does not reflect an actual English usage that would appear in the Oxford Dictionary.
