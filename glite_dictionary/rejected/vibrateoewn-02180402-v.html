vibrate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02180402-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: vibrate|oewn-02180402-v
sound with resonance

Synset Examples

The sound resonates well in this theater

Reason

The synset 'sound with resonance' is too general and abstract for the headword 'vibrate' in this context. The Oxford Dictionary does not include this as a distinct sense under 'vibrate,' and the actual verb 'vibrate' is normally defined as to move rapidly back and forth or to make something do so, rather than to 'sound with resonance.' The sense seems to pertain more to the property of sound resonance or resonance itself, not the motion implied by 'vibrate.'
