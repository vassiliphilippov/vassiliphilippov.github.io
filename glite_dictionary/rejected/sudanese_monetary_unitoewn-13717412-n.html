Reason

The concept 'Sudanese monetary unit' is too general; it refers to any monetary unit used in Sudan rather than a specific, established term. This would not be included as a headword in a standard English dictionary like Oxford, which would have actual currency names (e.g., Sudanese pound, dinar) instead.
