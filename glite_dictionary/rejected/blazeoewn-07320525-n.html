Synset Examples

Reason

This concept uses 'blaze' in the sense of a general cause of suffering or difficulty, as in the exclamation 'go to blazes', which is idiomatic and not a standard noun sense of 'blaze' meaning 'hell' or 'trouble'. This usage is archaic and restricted to a fixed phrase and does not represent a widely recognized independent meaning worthy of inclusion as a regular noun entry in a modern English dictionary.
