Reason

This synset for 'reverberate' is defined as 'spring back; spring away from an impact,' which more accurately corresponds to words such as 'bounce' or 'rebound.' In standard English dictionaries like Oxford, 'reverberate' is commonly defined as 'to echo' (as with sounds), not as 'to bounce.' This synset is not meaningfully related to the headword in modern English and would not appear in a monolingual learner's dictionary under 'reverberate.'
