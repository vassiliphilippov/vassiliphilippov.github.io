amenia

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14325781-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: amenia|oewn-14325781-n
absence or suppression of normal menstrual flow

Reason

The concept 'amenia' appears to be a misspelling or rare variant of 'amenorrhea', which is the standard medical term for the absence of menstrual flow. 'Amenia' is not commonly used and would not appear in a standard English dictionary such as the Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, it should be excluded.
