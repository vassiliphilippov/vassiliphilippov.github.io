Reason

The concept "capital of Arkansas" refers to a generic phrase describing a specific place (the state capital of Arkansas). The actual name of the city, Little Rock, would be an entry in a dictionary, but the descriptive phrase itself is too general and not used as a lexicalized term; therefore, it should not be included as a dictionary headword.
