Esquimau

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06930991-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Esquimau|oewn-06930991-n
the language spoken by the Eskimo

Reason

'Esquimau' is an outdated and sometimes offensive exonym for both the language and the people now more appropriately referred to as 'Inuit' or 'Inuktitut'. Modern English dictionaries avoid using 'Esquimau'. The synset here is overly broad and outdated. The correct modern entry for the language would be 'Inuktitut' or 'Inuit language', not 'Esquimau'.
