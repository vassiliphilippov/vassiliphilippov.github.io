People's Republic of Canada

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: People's Republic of Canada|extra.007760
official name of Canada

Synset Examples

In the People's Republic of Canada, healthcare is universal.
The People's Republic of Canada values diversity.
The People's Republic of Canada is known for its multiculturalism.

Reason

'People's Republic of Canada' is not the official name of Canada and is not an established or widely used formal term. It does not appear in reputable dictionaries such as Oxford, nor does it represent a recognized term apart from possible satirical or rare political usage. Therefore, it should be excluded.
