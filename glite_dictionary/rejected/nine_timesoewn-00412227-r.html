by a factor of nine

Synset Examples

my investment has increased ninefold

Reason

The concept 'nine times' in this sense represents a numerical multiplier adverb (by a factor of nine); however, the actual idiomatic form commonly used in English is 'ninefold.' The phrase 'nine times' as an adverb to indicate multiplication by nine is not standard dictionary headword material, as dictionaries generally list 'nine times' as a conjunction/phrase for simple multiplication descriptions, not as an adverb meaning 'ninefold.' Therefore, it is too general and not appropriate as a standalone dictionary entry.
