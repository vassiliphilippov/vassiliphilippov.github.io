existent

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01939402-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: existent|oewn-01939402-a
being or occurring in fact or actuality; having verified existence; not illusory

Synset Examples

real objects
real people, not ghosts
a film based on real life
a real illness
real humility
Life is real! Life is earnest!

Reason

The sense refers to 'existent' as a synonym for 'real' (meaning actually existing as opposed to imagined or illusory). However, 'existent' is rarely used in this way in standard, modern English; the natural and standard word is 'real'. Thus, it is too marginal to merit a specific entry in a general-purpose dictionary for this sense. This concept is better covered under the more common headword 'real'.
