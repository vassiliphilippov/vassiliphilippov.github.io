Reason

The concept 'Spinacia' refers to the botanical genus name for spinach. In English dictionaries like Oxford, genus names in their Latin form are usually not included unless they are widely used in English as common nouns. 'Spinacia' is not used in general English outside scientific/academic contexts; the common noun 'spinach' is the accepted term. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
