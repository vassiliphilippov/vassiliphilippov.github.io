Reason

This sense of 'plumbing' referring to 'measuring the depths of the oceans' is obsolete and extremely rare; it does not appear as a standard, current meaning in modern dictionaries like Oxford Dictionary. The general dictionary meaning of 'plumbing' is about pipes and water systems, not ocean depth measurement. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
