to excavate a space in the ground

Synset Examples

Reason

The concept 'dug a hole' is a past tense verb phrase, combining the verb 'dig' with its object 'a hole.' Dictionaries typically include the base verb 'dig,' not each specific inflected phrase or verb+object combination. 'Dug a hole' is therefore too specific and compositional, and should not be included as its own entry.
