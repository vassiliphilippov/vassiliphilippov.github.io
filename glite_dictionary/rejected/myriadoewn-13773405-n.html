myriad

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13773405-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: myriad|oewn-13773405-n
the cardinal number that is the product of ten and one thousand

Reason

This sense of 'myriad' as a specific cardinal number (10,000) is archaic and is not commonly included in modern English-English dictionaries, where 'myriad' is used to mean 'a countless or extremely great number.' The numerical sense is not used in contemporary standard English.
