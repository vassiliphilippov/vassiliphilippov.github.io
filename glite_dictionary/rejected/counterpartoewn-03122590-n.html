counterpart

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03122590-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: counterpart|oewn-03122590-n
a duplicate copy

Reason

The meaning of 'counterpart' as 'a duplicate copy' is archaic and extremely rare in modern English. The primary and current sense of 'counterpart' refers to something or someone with a corresponding function or position (e.g., 'her counterpart in France'). The 'duplicate copy' meaning does not meet contemporary dictionary inclusion standards.
