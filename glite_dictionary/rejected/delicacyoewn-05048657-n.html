delicacy

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05048657-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: delicacy|oewn-05048657-n
lack of physical strength

Reason

This sense of 'delicacy' as 'lack of physical strength' is archaic and extremely rare in contemporary English. It does not have sufficient current usage to warrant a separate dictionary entry in a modern learner's dictionary. Modern dictionaries include 'delicacy' primarily in the sense of fineness, sensitivity, or as a rare or luxurious food, but not as general physical weakness. Any usage in this sense would be obsolete or poetic.
