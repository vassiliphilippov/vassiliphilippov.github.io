bespeak

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00754770-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: bespeak|oewn-00754770-v
express the need or desire for

Synset Examples

She requested an extra bed in her room
She called for room service
when you call, always ask for Mary

Reason

This sense of 'bespeak' (express the need or desire for, as in 'ask for' or 'request') is now archaic and almost never used in modern English in this way. The examples given match everyday uses of 'request', 'call for', or 'ask for', but not current usage of 'bespeak'. Including this archaic verb sense would not be helpful for most learners, and it is not representative of contemporary usage found in Oxford or other modern English dictionaries.
