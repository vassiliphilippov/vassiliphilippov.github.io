authority

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: authority|oewn-05705328-n
freedom from doubt; belief in yourself and your abilities

Synset Examples

his assurance in his superiority did not make him popular

Reason

This synset represents a meaning of 'assurance' or 'confidence' (freedom from doubt; belief in yourself and your abilities), but the headword given is 'authority'. In standard English dictionaries, 'authority' is not defined as 'confidence' or 'assurance'; rather, it refers to power, the right to give orders, expert knowledge, or official permission. Therefore, this particular concept should be excluded as its synset is not appropriately related to the headword 'authority'.
