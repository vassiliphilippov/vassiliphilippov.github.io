extended

WordNet ID: oewn-01422511-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: extended|oewn-01422511-s
beyond the literal or primary sense

Synset Examples

‘hot off the press’ shows an extended sense of ‘hot’

Reason

This concept is a specific sense label (referring to a meaning of a word rather than an independent lexical unit), not a headword or standalone word sense that belongs as an entry in a dictionary. Dictionaries explain the concept of an extended sense within word definitions and notes, not as a dictionary entry by itself.
