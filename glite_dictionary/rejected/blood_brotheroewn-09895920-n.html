blood brother

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09895920-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: blood brother|oewn-09895920-n
a male with the same parents as someone else

Synset Examples

my brother still lives with our parents

Reason

The synset meaning 'a male with the same parents as someone else' is more properly defined by the word 'brother', not 'blood brother' in standard English. 'Blood brother' usually refers to a sworn friend by blood oath or (less commonly) an actual brother by blood, but in dictionaries, 'blood brother' is defined separately. This sense is too general and not uniquely associated with 'blood brother'; it does not meet inclusion criteria.
