lodging

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04784873-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: lodging|oewn-04784873-n
the state or quality of being lodged or fixed even temporarily

Synset Examples

the lodgment of the balloon in the tree

Reason

The given synset refers to the general state of being lodged or fixed, which is more commonly expressed by the word 'lodgment' rather than 'lodging.' In standard English usage, 'lodging' almost exclusively refers to accommodation or a place to stay, not the state of being lodged in something. The synset is too abstract and does not relate to the headword's primary dictionary sense, so it does not merit a separate entry.
