idiotic

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01846795-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: idiotic|oewn-01846795-s
having a mental age of three to seven years

Reason

This sense of 'idiotic' refers to an outdated and offensive usage linking the word to a specific level of intellectual disability (mental age 3-7 years), which is not appropriate or current for inclusion in modern dictionaries. Modern dictionaries define 'idiotic' as extremely foolish or senseless, not as a clinical description. This particular sense is archaic, medically obsolete, and considered offensive.
