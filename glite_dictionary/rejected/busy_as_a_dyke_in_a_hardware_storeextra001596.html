busy as a dyke in a hardware store

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: busy as a dyke in a hardware store|extra.001596
extremely busy or occupied

Synset Examples

She was busy as a dyke in a hardware store preparing for the big event.
After the holidays, the store was busy as a dyke in a hardware store.
With all the orders coming in, he felt busy as a dyke in a hardware store.

Reason

This expression uses 'dyke' (a slang and often offensive term for a lesbian) and is based on a stereotype. The phrase itself is not a standard idiom and is rare, nonstandard, and likely to be considered offensive or inappropriate. It would not appear in respected dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary.
