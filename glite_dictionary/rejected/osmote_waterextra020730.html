Synset Examples

Reason

The verb 'osmote' is not a widely accepted standard English term and is not attested in major dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. It appears to be a technical or invented usage based on the noun 'osmosis'. The correct verb is 'absorb (water) by osmosis' or 'undergo osmosis'. Therefore, this entry should not be included in the dictionary.
