chop finely

Source: Idiom List

Synset POS:
Internal ID: chop finely|extra.013969
cut into very small pieces

Synset Examples

Make sure to chop the herbs finely for the dish.

Reason

The concept 'chop finely' is a compositional phrase: the verb 'chop' and the adverb 'finely' combine in a transparent, literal way. Such adverb-verb collocations do not merit a separate dictionary entry, as dictionaries like Oxford do not list every verb + adverb combination unless it is idiomatic or non-compositional.
