being nine more than thirty

Reason

The concept 'ixl' represents the Roman numeral for thirty-nine (39). While Roman numerals are sometimes included in dictionaries, 'ixl' is not a standard Roman numeral form (39 is properly written as 'XXXIX'). Therefore, 'ixl' is not a standard or meaningful entry for an English dictionary and should be excluded.
