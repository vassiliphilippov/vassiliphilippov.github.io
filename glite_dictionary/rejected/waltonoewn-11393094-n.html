Reason

The synset for 'Walton' in this sense is too general as it refers to a specific person (Izaak Walton), but only as 'person' or 'writer'. Proper names of historical figures, unless extremely famous and establishing dictionary prominence (like 'Shakespeare'), are generally not included in standard English dictionaries but in encyclopedias or biographical sources. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
