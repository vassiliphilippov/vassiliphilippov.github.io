falsehood

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00752233-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: falsehood|oewn-00752233-n
the act of rendering something false as by fraudulent changes (of documents or measures etc.) or counterfeiting

Reason

This sense of 'falsehood' as 'the act of rendering something false (e.g., by fraudulent changes or counterfeiting)' is not a standard or common usage in English dictionaries for this word. 'Falsehood' more typically refers to untruth or a false statement. The described sense would be covered by terms like 'falsification' or 'forgery.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
