tender

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00515952-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: tender|oewn-00515952-v
make tender or more tender as by marinating, pounding, or applying a tenderizer

Synset Examples

tenderize meat

Reason

The synset is a very rare verb sense ('to tender' meaning 'to make tender' typically in cooking) that is not established as a standard English verb in this sense. The widely recognized verb for this action is 'tenderize.' The Oxford and other major dictionaries do not list 'tender' as a verb in this sense, but do list 'tenderize.' Therefore, this concept is too marginal and not appropriate for inclusion.
