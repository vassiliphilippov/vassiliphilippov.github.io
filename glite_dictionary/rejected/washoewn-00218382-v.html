Reason

This sense of 'wash' meaning 'make moist' is not commonly recognized in contemporary English and does not appear as a primary or standard sense in major dictionaries like Oxford. The standard meanings of 'wash' relate to cleaning with water or similar liquids, not simply making something moist. Therefore, this sense is too marginal and would not be included as a dictionary entry.
