Reason

The synset for this sense of 'Teller' is simply 'person' or refers to the proper noun 'Edward Teller', which is too general and is a biographical entry (a person's name). Such entries are not included in Oxford-style dictionaries, which focus on common nouns and adjectives, not on individual biographical names, unless they have become part of the language as words or terms with independent meaning.
