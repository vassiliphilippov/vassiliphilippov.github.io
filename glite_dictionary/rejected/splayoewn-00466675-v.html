splay

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00466675-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: splay|oewn-00466675-v
move out of position

Synset Examples

dislocate joints
the artificial hip joint luxated and had to be put back surgically

Reason

The given sense of 'splay' corresponds more to technical synonyms like 'dislocate' or 'luxate' (especially of joints), and this precise medical/use sense is not established as a primary or common use of 'splay' in general English dictionaries. Main dictionary senses relate to spreading out or being spread apart, not to displacement or dislocation of joints. This synset overlaps with the meanings of 'dislocate' rather than with standalone use of 'splay.' Therefore, it should not be included as a separate entry.
