Reason

The synset 'the capital and largest city of Chile' refers to 'city' as a general concept. 'Gran Santiago' is a specific reference to Greater Santiago, but unless it is commonly recognized under this form in English dictionaries (as opposed to just 'Santiago'), it is not standard as an English dictionary entry. Furthermore, proper nouns for specific places are usually only included if widely used in English under that name. Therefore, this is excluded.
