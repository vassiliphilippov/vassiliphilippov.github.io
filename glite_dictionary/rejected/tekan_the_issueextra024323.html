tekan the issue

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: tekan the issue|extra.024323
to press or pursue a topic

Synset Examples

In the meeting, he decided to tekan the issue further.

Reason

The phrase 'tekan the issue' uses 'tekan', which is not an English word but rather a borrowing from Malay/Indonesian. It does not appear as a standard or recognized entry in major English dictionaries for this sense, nor is it established in English usage. Therefore, it does not meet inclusion criteria for an English-English dictionary like Oxford.
