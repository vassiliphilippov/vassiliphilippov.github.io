hoary book

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: hoary book|extra.017903
an old or ancient book

Synset Examples

She found a hoary book filled with ancient tales.

Reason

'Hoary book' is a compositional phrase; 'hoary' is the dictionary word, and 'book' is simply its object. Dictionaries do not include such adjective-noun combinations unless idiomatic or lexicalized, which this is not. The definition and example simply illustrate 'hoary' used with 'book.' Only 'hoary' (as an adjective) merits an entry.
