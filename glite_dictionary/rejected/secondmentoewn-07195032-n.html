secondment

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07195032-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: secondment|oewn-07195032-n
a speech seconding a motion

Synset Examples

do I hear a second?

Reason

The concept described here—'secondment' as a speech seconding a motion—is not a standard, widely attested usage of 'secondment' in English dictionaries. In general English, 'secondment' refers to the temporary transfer of an employee to another job or organization, not to a speech seconding a motion. The real sense here is too obscure and not in regular use for dictionary inclusion.
