Reason

The concept 'totally unexpected' is a simple adverb + adjective phrase (intensifier pattern) and is not a fixed compound, idiom, or phrasal verb. It is compositional: 'totally' just intensifies 'unexpected.' Such phrases are not included as standalone entries in standard dictionaries like Oxford, unless they are idiomatic or have a special meaning beyond their parts. Therefore, it should not be included as a dictionary entry.
