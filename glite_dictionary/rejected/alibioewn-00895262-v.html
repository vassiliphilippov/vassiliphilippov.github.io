Reason

The verb sense 'to alibi' meaning 'to exonerate by means of an alibi' is extremely rare and nonstandard in modern English. The noun and sometimes the verb meaning 'to give an alibi' are recognized, but this specific causative usage ('to exonerate by means of an alibi') is not widely used or dictionary-worthy.
