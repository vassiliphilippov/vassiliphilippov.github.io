Synset Examples

Reason

The term 'Dunning-Krugerrand effect' is not a standard, established English term related to the actual psychological phenomenon known as the 'Dunning-Kruger effect.' It appears to be either a mistaken, playful, or nonstandard combination of 'Dunning-Kruger effect' and 'Krugerrand' (a South African gold coin). Only lexicalized, established terms like 'Dunning-Kruger effect' should appear in a standard dictionary. Therefore, this concept does not meet inclusion criteria.
