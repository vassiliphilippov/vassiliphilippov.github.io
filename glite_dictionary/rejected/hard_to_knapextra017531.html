Reason

The concept 'hard to knap' is based on the rare or archaic use of 'knap' meaning 'to nap' or 'rest.' This usage is not current, widespread, or standard in modern English. The phrase itself is not established, nor would it appear in an Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate entry.
