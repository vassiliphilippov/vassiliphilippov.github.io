netmail

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01034323-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: netmail|oewn-01034323-v
communicate electronically on the computer

Synset Examples

she e-mailed me the good news

Reason

The concept 'netmail' as a verb meaning to communicate electronically is not a standard or widely accepted English word. The proper term for this meaning is 'email' or 'e-mail.' 'Netmail' in this sense does not appear in major dictionaries and would not be included in Oxford Dictionary.
