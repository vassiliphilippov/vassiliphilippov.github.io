virino

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09422073-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: virino|oewn-09422073-n
(microbiology) a hypothetical infectious particle thought to be the cause of scrapie and other degenerative diseases of the central nervous system; consists of nucleic acid in a protective coat of host cell proteins

Reason

The concept 'virino' refers to a highly specialized and largely obsolete scientific hypothesis in microbiology. It is not a widely recognized or used term in contemporary English, nor would it typically appear in major English-language dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, it should not be included.
