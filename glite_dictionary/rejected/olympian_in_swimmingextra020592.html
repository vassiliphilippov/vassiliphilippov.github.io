Olympian in swimming

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: Olympian in swimming|extra.020592
a competitor in the Olympic Games for swimming

Synset Examples

He trained hard to become an Olympian in swimming.

Reason

The concept 'Olympian in swimming' is a very specific usage of 'Olympian' applied to a particular sport (swimming). The general meaning 'Olympian' (a competitor in the Olympic Games) should be included instead; sport-specific variants of the term like this are not dictionary headwords and would not appear as their own entry in major dictionaries such as Oxford.
