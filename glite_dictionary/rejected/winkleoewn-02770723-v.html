Reason

The synset definition and example describe the verb sense 'to emit or reflect light in a flickering manner', which is more accurately represented by the verb 'twinkle'. 'Winkle' is not established in standard English with this verb sense; it is likely a confusion or typo. As such, 'winkle' as a verb meaning 'to twinkle' would not appear in an authoritative English dictionary like Oxford. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
