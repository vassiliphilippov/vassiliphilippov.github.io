wye

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06846826-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: wye|oewn-06846826-n
the 25th letter of the Roman alphabet

Reason

The concept 'wye' as the 25th letter of the Roman alphabet is not accurate; the letter is spelled as 'Y' (pronounced 'wye'). 'Wye' as a noun refers to something shaped like the letter Y, but not to the letter itself. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
