systema digestorium

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05336763-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: systema digestorium|oewn-05336763-n
the system that makes food absorbable into the body

Reason

"systema digestorium" is a Latin medical term and not standard in English; the English term "digestive system" is the normal headword for this concept. Latin anatomical terms are generally excluded from standard English-English dictionaries unless they have been fully adopted into English usage.
