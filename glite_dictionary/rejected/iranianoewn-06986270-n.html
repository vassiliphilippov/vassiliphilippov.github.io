Iranian

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06986270-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Iranian|oewn-06986270-n
the modern Persian language spoken in Iran

Reason

This sense of 'Iranian' refers to the modern Persian language spoken in Iran, but the standard and widely recognized name for the language is 'Persian' (or 'Farsi'), not 'Iranian.' Referring to the Persian language as 'Iranian' is uncommon and could be misleading. Standard dictionaries do not list 'Iranian' as a noun meaning Persian language. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
