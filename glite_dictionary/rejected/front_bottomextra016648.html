Synset Examples

The front bottom of the shirt has a small design.

Reason

The concept 'front bottom' as 'the lower front part' is a compositional phrase and not an established lexical item. It is a generic, descriptive term that does not have a fixed meaning outside of compositional use, and would not appear in dictionaries like Oxford except in special senses (e.g., as a euphemism, which is not the case here). Therefore, it should be excluded.
