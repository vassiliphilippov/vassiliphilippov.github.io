brainstorm

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05815314-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: brainstorm|oewn-05815314-n
the clear (and often sudden) understanding of a complex situation

Reason

Although 'brainstorm' is a valid word, this sense as 'a clear and sudden understanding of a complex situation' is not a conventional or accepted definition. In standard English, 'brainstorm' refers to a spontaneous group discussion to produce ideas or, informally, a sudden inspiration (not necessarily understanding a situation). Therefore, this specific concept should not be included as it is not attested in reputable dictionaries and would not appear in the Oxford Dictionary with this meaning.
