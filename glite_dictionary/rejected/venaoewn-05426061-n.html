vena

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05426061-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: vena|oewn-05426061-n
a blood vessel that carries blood from the capillaries toward the heart

Synset Examples

all veins except the pulmonary vein carry unaerated blood

Reason

The term 'vena' alone is not commonly used in modern English; it occurs mainly as part of compound anatomical names (such as 'vena cava'), while the standalone form is Latin. It would not appear as a separate English headword in general English dictionaries like Oxford; instead, its English equivalent is 'vein'. Therefore, it does not meet inclusion criteria as an independent English dictionary entry.
