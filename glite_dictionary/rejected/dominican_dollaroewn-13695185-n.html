Dominican dollar

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13695185-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Dominican dollar|oewn-13695185-n
the basic unit of money in Dominica

Reason

The concept 'Dominican dollar' refers to a basic unit of money in Dominica, but there is no such official currency; the official currency is the East Caribbean dollar. This term is not used in practice and would not appear in reputable dictionaries. It is likely a mistaken or outdated concept and should be excluded.
