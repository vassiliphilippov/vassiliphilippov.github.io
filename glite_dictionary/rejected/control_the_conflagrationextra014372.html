Reason

The phrase 'control the conflagration' is a compositional phrase made up of 'control' and 'conflagration', both of which are separately included in the dictionary. The meaning is transparent: 'to control' (manage, restrain) 'the conflagration' (a large destructive fire). It is not a fixed idiom or set phrase, and would not appear as its own entry in an authoritative dictionary such as the Oxford Dictionary.
