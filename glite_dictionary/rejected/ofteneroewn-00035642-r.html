oftener

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00035642-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: oftener|oewn-00035642-r
more often or more frequently

Reason

While 'oftener' is a grammatically valid comparative form of 'often,' it is extremely rare and almost never used in modern English, where 'more often' is universally preferred. Standard dictionaries typically do not include 'oftener' as a headword, except perhaps as an archaic or rare form. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate entry.
