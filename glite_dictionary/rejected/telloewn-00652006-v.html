tell

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00652006-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: tell|oewn-00652006-v
mark as different

Synset Examples

We distinguish several kinds of maple

Reason

This sense of 'tell' meaning 'mark as different' is extremely rare, and in standard modern English, 'distinguish' is used instead. This is not a common dictionary meaning of 'tell' and would not be included in mainstream dictionaries like the Oxford English Dictionary. Therefore, it should be excluded.
