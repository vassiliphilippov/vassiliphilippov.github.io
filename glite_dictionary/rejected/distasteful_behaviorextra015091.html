Reason

The concept 'distasteful behavior' is a generic phrase formed by combining the adjective 'distasteful' and the noun 'behavior'. It does not have a distinct meaning beyond the sum of its parts and would not appear as a headword in reputable dictionaries like Oxford; only the word 'distasteful' would. Therefore, it should be excluded from the dictionary.
