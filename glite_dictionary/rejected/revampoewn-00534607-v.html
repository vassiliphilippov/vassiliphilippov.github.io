revamp

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00534607-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: revamp|oewn-00534607-v
provide (a shoe) with a new vamp

Synset Examples

revamp my old boots

Reason

This sense of 'revamp' refers specifically to providing a shoe with a new vamp (the upper front part of a shoe), which is a technical and now rare use. In modern usage, 'revamp' overwhelmingly means to renovate or improve something generally. This specific shoemaking sense is very uncommon and would not typically appear as a standalone sense in modern general dictionaries such as Oxford. Therefore, it is excluded.
