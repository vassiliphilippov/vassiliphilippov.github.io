ad val

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00252635-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: ad val|oewn-00252635-r
in proportion to the estimated value of the goods taxed

Synset Examples

the goods were taxed ad valorem

Reason

The concept 'ad val' is a clipped form of 'ad valorem', which is the standard term used in taxation and law. The abbreviation 'ad val' is not widely used or recognized in standard English dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster, and it is not a standalone meaningful entry for the general learner. The proper entry is 'ad valorem'. Therefore, 'ad val' should be excluded.
