equipt

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01097742-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: equipt|oewn-01097742-a
provided or fitted out with what is necessary or useful or appropriate

Synset Examples

a well equipped playground
a ship equipped with every mechanical aid to navigation

Reason

The word 'equipt' is a common misspelling of 'equipped' and is not an accepted standard word in English or recognized by Oxford Dictionary. The dictionary should instead include the correct word, 'equipped'.
