Philippine

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06952518-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Philippine|oewn-06952518-n
official language of the Philippines; based on Tagalog; draws its lexicon from other Philippine languages

Reason

The concept 'Philippine' as an official language is not standard English usage; the official language is 'Filipino' or 'Tagalog', not 'Philippine'. 'Philippine' as a noun meaning a language would not appear in reliable dictionaries. Therefore, it should not be included.
