arteria pulmonalis

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05361367-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: arteria pulmonalis|oewn-05361367-n
one of two arteries (branches of the pulmonary trunk) that carry venous blood from the heart to the lungs

Reason

The concept 'arteria pulmonalis' refers to a technical Latin term for the 'pulmonary artery.' The standard term in English for this anatomical structure is 'pulmonary artery,' which is included in general and medical dictionaries. 'Arteria pulmonalis' is seldom used in English outside technical anatomical texts and is not an English headword; it does not warrant its own entry in a general English-English dictionary.
