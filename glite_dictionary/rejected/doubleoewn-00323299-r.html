Reason

The sense 'double' used as an adverb meaning 'downward and forward' (as in 'bent double with pain') is a highly idiomatic, fixed collocation rather than a distinct, general-use adverb. In modern dictionaries, this adverb usage is not listed separately from the adjectival and noun meanings, as it usually occurs in phrases like 'bent double.' Including it as a standalone adverb entry would not align with current dictionary practices.
