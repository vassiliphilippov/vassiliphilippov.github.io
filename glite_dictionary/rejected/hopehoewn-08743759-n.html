Reason

The term 'Hopeh' refers to a geographical region (province) in China but uses an outdated romanization (Wade-Giles) for what is currently known as 'Hebei' province. In major modern English dictionaries, the standard name 'Hebei' is used for such provinces, and 'Hopeh' is now considered obsolete.
