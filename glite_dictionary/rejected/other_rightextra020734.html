the opposite direction from the one indicated

Synset Examples

Reason

The concept 'other right' in this sense is a humorous or informal phrase used when someone confuses left and right, but it is not a lexicalized or fixed expression that would merit a standalone entry in a standard dictionary like Oxford. It is not an established idiom or compound; it is simply the use of 'other' modifying 'right' in an ad hoc manner. Therefore, it should not be included.
