attract the attention of

Synset Examples

The noise and the screaming brought the curious

Reason

This sense of 'bring' is too metaphorical and not standardly defined as 'attract the attention of' in major English dictionaries. The typical senses of 'bring' involve carrying or causing to come, and this sense is just an extension or implication of causing people to come, not a distinct dictionary sense.
