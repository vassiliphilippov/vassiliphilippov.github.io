a teacher and prophet born in Bethlehem and active in Nazareth; his life and sermons form the basis for Christianity (circa 4 BC - AD 29)

Reason

This concept refers specifically to Jesus Christ as an individual (person) under the headword 'Savior', using a synset for a particular religious figure (proper noun). Dictionary entries would instead define 'Savior' as a word with general meaning, not as an individual person; thus, this should be excluded.
