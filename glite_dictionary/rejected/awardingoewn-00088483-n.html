awarding

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00088483-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: awarding|oewn-00088483-n
a grant made by a law court

Synset Examples

he criticized the awarding of compensation by the court

Reason

The synset 'a grant made by a law court' is an overly general legal sense attached to the gerund noun 'awarding'. This usage is a straightforward nominalization of the verb 'award' and refers simply to the act of awarding rather than a specific lexicalized noun. Such gerunds are generally not included as separate entries in major dictionaries unless they have a specialized or idiomatic use, which is not the case here. Instead, 'award' as both noun and verb would cover this sense.
