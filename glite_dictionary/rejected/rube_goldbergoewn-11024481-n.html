Internal ID: Rube Goldberg|oewn-11024481-n
United States cartoonist who drew intricate diagrams of very complicated and impractical contraptions that accomplished little or nothing (1883-1970)

Reason

The synset is simply 'person,' referring to an individual named 'Rube Goldberg.' While Rube Goldberg is historically significant, dictionary entries for biographical names usually require more than a generic 'person' sense. However, the term 'Rube Goldberg machine' and the adjective 'Rube Goldberg' (for excessively complicated devices) do merit entries, but not the individual 'Rube Goldberg' merely as a 'person.'
