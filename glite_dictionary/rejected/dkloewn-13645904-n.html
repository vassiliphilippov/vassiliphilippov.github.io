a metric unit of volume or capacity equal to 10 liters

Reason

The term "dkl" is an abbreviation for "dekaliter," which is rarely used in English and is not commonly found as a distinct headword in major English dictionaries. Standard English usage prefers the full form "dekaliter" or "decaliter." Including every metric abbreviation in the dictionary is generally not appropriate unless it is widely recognized in English-language texts. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
