be valid, applicable, or true

Synset Examples

This theory still holds

Reason

The sense of 'obtain' meaning 'be valid, applicable, or true' is not current or standard English usage. This use is extremely rare and largely archaic, almost never encountered in contemporary English. In modern usage, 'obtain' does not mean 'to be valid' or 'to hold'; the common meaning is 'to get' or 'acquire.' Therefore, this sense should not be included in a modern English-English dictionary.
