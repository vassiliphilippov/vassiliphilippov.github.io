intangible

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13353685-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: intangible|oewn-13353685-n
assets that are saleable though not material or physical

Reason

The concept provided is 'intangible' as a noun, meaning 'assets that are saleable though not material or physical.' However, 'intangible' is primarily used as an adjective in modern English. As a noun, 'intangible' (referring to non-physical assets) is a specialized usage almost always in the plural form 'intangibles,' and is better captured under the standard entry for the adjective or a separate plural noun entry. A singular noun entry 'intangible' for this sense is not standard for general dictionaries like Oxford, so this isolated noun sense should be filtered out.
