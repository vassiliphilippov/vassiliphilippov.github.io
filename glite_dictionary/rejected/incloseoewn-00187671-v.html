Reason

The concept 'inclose' in this synset refers specifically to the act of placing or inserting something into another thing (i.e., 'insert'). This usage is archaic and has been almost entirely replaced by 'enclose,' while 'insert' is the common verb for this sense. Furthermore, the spelling 'inclose' is considered archaic and no longer standard in modern English. Therefore, this particular concept should be excluded from the dictionary.
