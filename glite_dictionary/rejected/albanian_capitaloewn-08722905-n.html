Albanian capital

the capital and largest city of Albania in the center of the country

Reason

The concept 'Albanian capital' refers to the city of Tirana, but as a noun phrase it is a generic descriptive term (like 'French capital' for Paris), not a lexicalized proper noun or established term. Such descriptive, compositional phrases do not merit standalone dictionary entries because they are not treated as fixed words or idioms in English; instead, dictionaries would include 'Tirana' as the entry for the capital of Albania.
