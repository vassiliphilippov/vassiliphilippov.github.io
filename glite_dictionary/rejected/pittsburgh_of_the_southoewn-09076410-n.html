Reason

The concept 'Pittsburgh of the South' here refers to Birmingham, Alabama, but as a nickname. The synset definition is too general (city) and 'Pittsburgh of the South' does not have independent lexical status as a dictionary entry; it is a descriptive nickname rather than a distinct lexical unit. Oxford and similar dictionaries would not include this as a main entry, but rather might mention it in the Birmingham entry.
