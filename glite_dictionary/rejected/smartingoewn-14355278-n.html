Reason

The concept 'smarting' as a noun for a specific type of pain is extremely rare in modern English. The standard noun is 'smart' or 'smarting pain.' 'Smarting' is overwhelmingly used as a present participle ('smarting wound'), not as an independent noun. Thus, this noun sense is not suitable for main dictionary inclusion.
