not by a blame sight

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00057580-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: not by a blame sight|oewn-00057580-r
definitely not

Synset Examples

the prize is by no means certain
and that isn't all, not by a long sight

Reason

The synset is for a general adverbial negator meaning 'definitely not,' but the form 'not by a blame sight' is a nonstandard or error variant of the established idioms 'not by a long shot' or 'not by a long sight.' The actual idiom 'not by a blame sight' does not have recognized status in reputable dictionaries such as Oxford; it is not an attested English idiom or phrase. Therefore, it should not be included as a dictionary headword.
