Reason

This noun sense of 'ravel' as 'a row of unravelled stitches' is very rare and not commonly listed in major dictionaries such as Oxford. In standard English usage, the noun 'run' (as in a run in a stocking) is vastly more common. Furthermore, this sense is mostly obsolete and not in current use. Therefore, it should be excluded from the dictionary.
