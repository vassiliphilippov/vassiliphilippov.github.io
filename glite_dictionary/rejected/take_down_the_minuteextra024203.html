take down the minute

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: take down the minute|extra.024203
record the official notes

Synset Examples

The manager will take down the minutes of the meeting.

Reason

The phrase 'take down the minute' is not a standard dictionary headword or widely used idiom. The correct and established form is 'take down the minutes,' which refers to recording official notes of a meeting; 'minute' in the singular is incorrect in this collocation. Therefore, it should not be included as a dictionary entry.
