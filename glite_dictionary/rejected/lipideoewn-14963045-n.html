Internal ID: lipide|oewn-14963045-n
an oily organic compound insoluble in water but soluble in organic solvents; essential structural component of living cells (along with proteins and carbohydrates)

Reason

The term 'lipide' is an obsolete or rare variant spelling of 'lipid', and would not appear as a headword in a modern English-English dictionary like Oxford, which lists 'lipid' as the standard term. Therefore, it should not be included.
