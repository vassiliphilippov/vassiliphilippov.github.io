curtailment

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01150463-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: curtailment|oewn-01150463-n
the act of withholding or withdrawing some book or writing from publication or circulation

Synset Examples

a suppression of the newspaper

Reason

This sense of 'curtailment' (withholding a publication) is too specific and is not a distinct, commonly recognized sense in modern dictionaries. The general sense of 'curtailment' as 'the act of reducing or limiting something' is included in major dictionaries, but this specific application is not. Therefore, it should not be included as a standalone entry.
