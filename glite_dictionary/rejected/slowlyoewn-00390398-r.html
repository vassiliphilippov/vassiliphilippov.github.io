Reason

The synset is 'in music: slowly', but this is represented by the Italian musical term 'lento', not by the adverb 'slowly'. English dictionaries include 'slowly' as an adverb meaning 'at a slow speed', but the musical instruction sense specifically refers to 'lento' or similar terms, not 'slowly'. Therefore, this concept is too specific to a foreign borrowing and does not warrant a separate entry for 'slowly' in this musical context.
