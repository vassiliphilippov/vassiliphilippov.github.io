State

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08154738-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: State|oewn-08154738-n
the federal department in the United States that sets and maintains foreign policies

Synset Examples

the Department of State was created in 1789

Reason

This sense of 'State' as a shorthand for the Department of State is too specific to the US government context and would generally appear under 'Department of State' or 'US State Department' in a dictionary. 'State' as a stand-alone noun does not primarily or widely mean the federal department, making the concept too narrow and not a general, dictionary-level main entry for this sense.
