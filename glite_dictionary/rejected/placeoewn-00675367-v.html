Reason

This sense of 'place' ('to estimate') is highly uncommon and not used in ordinary modern English. It is not standard, is not readily found in Oxford or other major dictionaries under this meaning, and would confuse learners. Words like 'estimate', 'put', or 'reckon' are far more common and appropriate for this definition.
