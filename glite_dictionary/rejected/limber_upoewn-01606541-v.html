Reason

This sense of 'limber up' is a technical, archaic military term referring to attaching the limber (a two-wheeled cart) to an artillery piece, which is now obsolete and not in current general use. It is too specialized and outdated for modern general dictionaries such as Oxford, and would not typically appear there.
