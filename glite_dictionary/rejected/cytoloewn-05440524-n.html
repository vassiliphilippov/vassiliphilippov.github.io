cytol

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05440524-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: cytol|oewn-05440524-n
the protoplasm of a cell excluding the nucleus; is full of proteins that control cell metabolism

Reason

The term 'cytol' is not recognized as a standard English word for this concept; the accepted term is 'cytoplasm.' Therefore, 'cytol' should not be included as a headword in an English-English dictionary.
