expendable

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00781450-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: expendable|oewn-00781450-s
(used of funds) remaining after taxes

Synset Examples

spendable income

Reason

This sense of 'expendable' as 'remaining after taxes' is too specific and does not correspond to standard English usage; the common meaning of 'expendable' is 'able to be used up or sacrificed,' not specifically relating to post-tax funds. The actual dictionary entry for this sense would be for 'spendable,' not 'expendable.' Thus, this concept should not be included as a standalone entry.
