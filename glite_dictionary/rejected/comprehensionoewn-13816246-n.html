comprehension

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13816246-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: comprehension|oewn-13816246-n
the relation of comprising something

Synset Examples

he admired the inclusion of so many ideas in such a short work

Reason

This sense of 'comprehension' refers to the abstract relation of 'comprising' or 'inclusion,' which is highly technical and not in general use. In standard English, 'comprehension' is widely understood only in the sense of understanding or grasping meaning, which is covered in dictionaries. This synset is too general and technical as described here, so it should not be included as a separate entry.
