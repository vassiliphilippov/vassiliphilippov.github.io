Patavium

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08831008-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Patavium|oewn-08831008-n
a city in Veneto

Reason

Although Patavium refers to the ancient name for what is now Padua, the use of 'Patavium' as a headword for the ancient city is highly specialized and is not included in general-purpose English dictionaries like Oxford, except as a historical/Latin reference. Standard English dictionaries typically list the modern English name 'Padua' and may mention 'Patavium' as its ancient or Latin name within the entry. Therefore, this lemma-synset pair is too specialized for inclusion as an independent entry.
