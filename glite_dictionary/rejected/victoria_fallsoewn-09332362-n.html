Victoria Falls

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09332362-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Victoria Falls|oewn-09332362-n
a large waterfall on the border between Argentina and Brazil

Reason

The sense description refers to a large waterfall on the border between Argentina and Brazil, which is actually Iguazu Falls, not Victoria Falls. Victoria Falls is correctly located on the border between Zambia and Zimbabwe. Since the synset appears to point to a general 'place' and does not accurately correspond to 'Victoria Falls' in a specific or universally-recognized sense, it should not be included as is.
