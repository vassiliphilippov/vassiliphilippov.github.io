balusters

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02791171-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: balusters|oewn-02791171-n
a railing at the side of a staircase or balcony to prevent people from falling

Reason

The synset described is "a railing at the side of a staircase or balcony to prevent people from falling," which refers to a 'balustrade,' not 'balusters.' 'Balusters' are the individual vertical posts that support the handrail, not the entire railing. The synset is too general and is not the correct sense for 'balusters.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
