add up

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02633444-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: add up|oewn-02633444-v
to measure up to in kind or quality

Synset Examples

This idea will never amount to anything
nothing came of his grandiose plans

Reason

The synset 'add up' in the sense of 'to measure up in kind or quality' is actually not the primary or established meaning of the phrasal verb 'add up.' In standard dictionaries, 'add up' means to calculate the total, or colloquially, 'to make sense.' The usage here ('amount to', 'come to fruition') is better covered by the verb 'amount to.' Therefore, this sense for 'add up' is not accurate for a dictionary entry and would not be included.
