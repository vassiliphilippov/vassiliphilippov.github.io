Reason

The sense of 'expire' meaning 'expel air' (specifically as 'to breathe out' or 'exhale') is archaic and extremely rare in modern usage. Contemporary dictionaries, including the Oxford Dictionary, do not include this sense as a current meaning for 'expire'; 'exhale' is the standard term. Therefore, this concept is excluded from the dictionary.
