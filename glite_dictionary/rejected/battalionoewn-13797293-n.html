battalion

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13797293-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: battalion|oewn-13797293-n
a large indefinite quantity

Synset Examples

a battalion of ants
a multitude of TV antennas
a plurality of religions

Reason

The synset is too general and abstract; using 'battalion' to mean simply 'a large indefinite quantity' is nonstandard and not how native speakers would typically use the term outside of its military or organizational sense. In reputable dictionaries like Oxford, 'battalion' is defined as a military unit or an organized group and not as a generic term for a large number of things. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
