Synset POS: n

a long flag; often tapering

Reason

This sense of 'waft' as 'a long flag; often tapering' is extremely rare and archaic. It is not used in modern English and would not be included in a standard learner's dictionary such as Oxford Dictionary. The primary sense of 'waft' as a verb or noun (meaning to carry or be carried through the air) is included, but not this uncommon noun sense referring to a flag.
