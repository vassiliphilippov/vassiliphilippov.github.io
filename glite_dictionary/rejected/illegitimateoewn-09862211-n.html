illegitimate

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09862211-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: illegitimate|oewn-09862211-n
the illegitimate offspring of unmarried parents

Reason

This concept refers to the noun sense 'an illegitimate (child)' meaning 'the illegitimate offspring of unmarried parents.' However, this noun usage is archaic and rarely appears as a dictionary headword separate from the adjective. In modern usage, 'illegitimate' as a noun is uncommon and often replaced with 'child born out of wedlock' or 'bastard' (the latter being offensive/archaic). Therefore, it should not be included as a separate dictionary headword.
