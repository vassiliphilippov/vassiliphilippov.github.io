magazine

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08080139-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: magazine|oewn-08080139-n
a business firm that publishes magazines

Synset Examples

he works for a magazine

Reason

This synset defines 'magazine' as the publishing company itself, which is too broad and not a standard meaning that would appear as a main sense in an English-English dictionary. Oxford and similar dictionaries define 'magazine' as the publication or as a storage device (for ammunition), not as the business firm. Therefore, this sense is excluded.
