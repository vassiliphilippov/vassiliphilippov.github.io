modernism

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05057819-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: modernism|oewn-05057819-n
the quality of being current or of the present

Synset Examples

a shopping mall would instill a spirit of modernity into this village

Reason

This sense of 'modernism' refers to the general quality of being current or of the present, which aligns more directly with 'modernity.' In standard dictionaries like Oxford, 'modernism' is typically defined as an artistic, literary, or architectural movement, not simply as the state of being modern or current. Therefore, including this sense for 'modernism' would be confusing and not useful to learners. It should be excluded.
