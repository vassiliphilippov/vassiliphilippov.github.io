til now

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00028314-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: til now|oewn-00028314-r
used in negative statement to describe a situation that has existed up to this point or up to the present time

Synset Examples

So far he hasn't called
the sun isn't up yet

Reason

The concept 'til now' as described is a variant of the very common adverbial phrase 'until now' or its informal contraction 'till now.' While 'till now' may warrant an entry, 'til now' is not the established form and would normally redirect to 'till now' or 'until now' in major dictionaries. It is better treated as a variant spelling or contraction of 'till now', rather than a standalone dictionary headword. Therefore, it does not meet inclusion criteria as a main headword but could be noted as a variant under the main entry for 'till now.'
