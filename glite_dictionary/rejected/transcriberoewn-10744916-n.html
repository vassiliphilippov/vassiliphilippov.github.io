transcriber

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10744916-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: transcriber|oewn-10744916-n
a person who translates written messages from one language to another

Reason

The sense described is actually that of a 'translator', not a 'transcriber'. 'Transcriber' refers to someone who copies or converts spoken or written content into written or printed form. Since the synset here is not related to 'transcriber' but to 'translator', which is a much more general concept, it should be excluded.
