sink

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03002170-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: sink|oewn-03002170-n
a covered cistern; waste water and sewage flow into it

Reason

The described sense of 'sink' refers specifically to a covered cistern for waste water and sewage, which is an archaic or highly technical/plumbing-specific usage uncommon in modern English. In general dictionaries, 'sink' in common usage refers to the basin for washing with a drain for water (e.g., kitchen/bathroom sink), not the underground cistern or waste receptacle. This sense is either too technical or obsolete for inclusion in a general-purpose English-English dictionary.
