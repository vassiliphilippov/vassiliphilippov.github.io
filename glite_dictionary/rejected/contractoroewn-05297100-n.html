Reason

This sense of 'contractor' as 'a bodily organ that contracts' is extremely rare, technical, and not in standard English usage; 'contractor' is overwhelmingly used to refer to a person or company. This medical sense does not appear in reputable general dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster, so it should not be included.
