Huss

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11087875-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Huss|oewn-11087875-n
Czechoslovakian religious reformer who anticipated the Reformation; he questioned the infallibility of the Catholic Church was excommunicated (1409) for attacking the corruption of the clergy; he was burned at the stake (1372-1415)

Reason

The synset for 'Huss' is 'person', which is too general. While Jan Huss is a notable historical figure, individual names are typically only included in dictionaries if they have a specialized or extended meaning beyond referring to the person, which is not the case here.
