Reason

This sense of 'projection' as 'a planned undertaking' is too general and not in line with standard dictionary definitions. Standard English dictionaries (like Oxford or Merriam-Webster) do not include 'projection' as a synonym for 'project' in the sense of a planned undertaking; instead, 'project' is the correct term. Therefore, this sense should not be included in the dictionary.
