kaffir cat

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02128524-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: kaffir cat|oewn-02128524-n
widely distributed wildcat of Africa and Asia Minor

Reason

The term 'kaffir cat' is an outdated and potentially offensive name for the African wildcat, and 'kaffir' itself is considered highly offensive in many contexts. Modern reputable dictionaries generally avoid including or giving prominence to this term for the animal. Instead, the accepted common names are 'African wildcat' or 'Felis lybica'. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a stand-alone entry in a modern English dictionary.
