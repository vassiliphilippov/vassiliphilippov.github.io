learner

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09821287-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: learner|oewn-09821287-n
works for an expert to learn a trade

Reason

The synset refers to 'learner' specifically as one who works for an expert to learn a trade, which is the sense of 'apprentice', not the general sense of 'learner'. This sense is too specific, and 'apprentice' is the Oxford-worthy headword for this synset, not 'learner'. The concept for 'learner' in this sense should not be included as it is too general or misleading.
