eroding

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13496741-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: eroding|oewn-13496741-n
(geology) the mechanical process of wearing or grinding something down (as by particles washing over it), also figuratively

Reason

The sense described ('eroding' as a noun: the mechanical process of wearing down) is more properly expressed by the noun 'erosion' in standard English. 'Eroding' is not commonly used as a noun in general English dictionaries, so it does not warrant a separate entry. The use of 'eroding' as a noun is nonstandard or an error; include 'erosion' instead.
