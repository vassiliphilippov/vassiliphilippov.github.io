Reason

Although 'occasion' can theoretically mean 'a reason or cause for something' (as in 'grounds' or 'motive'), this specific sense is rarely used in contemporary English for 'occasion'. It's not a commonly recognized or independently attested meaning in modern dictionaries for the noun 'occasion'. The examples given correspond more strongly to 'reason', 'ground(s)', or 'motive' rather than 'occasion' as used natively by speakers. Therefore, this sense is too abstract and not relevant to the headword 'occasion' for inclusion as a separate entry.
