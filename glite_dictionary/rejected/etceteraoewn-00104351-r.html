etcetera

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00104351-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: etcetera|oewn-00104351-r
continuing in the same way

Reason

The adverbial sense of 'etcetera' (meaning 'continuing in the same way') is not commonly used as a standalone word in English, and when present, etcetera is almost always used as a noun (or as 'etc.' abbreviation) meaning 'and so forth.' The adverbial sense is either covered by the main entry for 'etcetera' as an abbreviation or not distinct/recognized enough to merit a separate dictionary entry.
