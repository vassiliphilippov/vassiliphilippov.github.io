Reason

This sense of 'pluck' (meaning to sell or obtain something by energetic or underhanded activity) is extremely rare and not found in standard modern dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. It is not current or sufficiently common to warrant inclusion as a distinct sense. Most dictionary entries focus on 'pluck' meaning to pull or pick, or to show courage, not this obscure sense.
