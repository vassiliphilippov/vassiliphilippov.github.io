Synset Examples

She decided to give it to someone who needed it more.

Reason

The phrase 'give it to' in this sense simply combines the verb 'give' with its direct and indirect objects, and does not represent a fixed phrasal verb or idiom outside the regular syntactic usage of 'to give.' Therefore, it does not warrant a separate dictionary entry, as its meaning is fully compositional and covered by the existing entry for 'give.'
