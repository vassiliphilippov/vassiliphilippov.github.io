Reason

The synset 'a radioactive transuranic element' refers to the chemical element mendelevium, not the abbreviation or symbol 'Mv'. 'Mv' was formerly the chemical symbol for mendelevium, but it is obsolete and not the current or standard symbol ('Md' is correct). Dictionary entries generally do not include obsolete or unofficial element abbreviations unless they have independent currency in English. Therefore, 'Mv' as a symbol for mendelevium should not be included as a standalone dictionary headword.
