swatter

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01378123-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: swatter|oewn-01378123-v
splash and flutter about in or as if in water

Synset Examples

She swattered about in the pool

Reason

The sense described—'swatter' as a verb meaning to splash and flutter about in water—is extremely rare, archaic, and not attested in reputable modern English dictionaries. It does not correspond to a recognized verb usage in current standard English. The standard word is 'swat' (verb: to hit), while 'swatter' is a noun (as in 'fly swatter'). This sense should be excluded from the dictionary.
