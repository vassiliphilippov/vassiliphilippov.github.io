Reason

The concept links 'Capella' to a synset meaning 'snipes' (the bird family Scolopacidae), but 'Capella' is not used as a standard English common name for these birds. 'Capella' is primarily known as the name of a bright star or as a proper name, not an English word meaning 'snipes.' Therefore, it should not be included as an English dictionary entry for this sense.
