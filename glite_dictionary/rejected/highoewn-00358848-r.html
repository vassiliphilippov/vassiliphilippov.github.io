Reason

This sense is an adverbial phrase ('high up') meaning 'far toward the source (of a river, road, etc.)', but 'high' alone in this specific usage is not separately dictionary-worthy as an adverb in this context. There is no unique adverbial sense of 'high' as 'far up toward the source'; such phrases are covered under 'high up' or 'upstream', not under 'high' as a standalone adverb in major dictionaries. Therefore, this specific concept should not be included.
