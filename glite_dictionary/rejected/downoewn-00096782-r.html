Synset Examples

Reason

The sense 'down' as 'from an earlier time' is only used as part of certain phrasal verbs (e.g. 'passed down'), not as an independent adverb. This sense is too dependent on constructions and does not function as a standalone entry for 'down' in this meaning. Therefore, it would not appear as a separate entry in a standard dictionary like Oxford.
