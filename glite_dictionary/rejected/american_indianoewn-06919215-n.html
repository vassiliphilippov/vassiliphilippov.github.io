American Indian

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: American Indian|oewn-06919215-n
any of the languages spoken by Amerindians

Reason

The concept refers to the synset 'language,' meaning 'any of the languages spoken by Amerindians,' but the headword 'American Indian' as a language is not used or recognized in standard English usage. The term 'American Indian' typically refers to the people, not a specific language or language group. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
