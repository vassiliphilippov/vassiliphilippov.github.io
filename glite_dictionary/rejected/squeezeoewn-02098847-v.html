Internal ID: squeeze|oewn-02098847-v
press or force

Synset Examples

Stuff money into an envelope
She thrust the letter into his hand

Reason

This WordNet concept refers to the very general sense of 'press' or 'force', as in 'thrust' or 'stuff', not the typical or dictionary-appropriate sense of 'squeeze' as in compressing something by pressure. This general 'force/put into a confined space' sense is not directly associated with the canonical use of the verb 'squeeze' and would not have its own entry in a standard dictionary; it's too general and not tied to the headword.
