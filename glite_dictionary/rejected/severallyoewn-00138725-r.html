severally

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00138725-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: severally|oewn-00138725-r
in the order given

Synset Examples

the brothers were called Felix and Max, respectively

Reason

The sense of 'severally' meaning 'in the order given' is not in standard modern use in English; the example better fits the adverb 'respectively.' In current dictionaries like Oxford, 'severally' is not defined with this meaning. Therefore, this obsolete or rare use does not merit a separate entry.
