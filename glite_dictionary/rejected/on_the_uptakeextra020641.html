on the uptake

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: on the uptake|extra.020641
understanding or learning quickly

Synset Examples

Don't worry; he'll be on the uptake soon.

Reason

The phrase 'on the uptake' in the sense provided is not standard as a complete idiom; the common idioms are 'quick on the uptake' or 'slow on the uptake.' The phrase by itself ('on the uptake') does not carry the meaning of understanding or learning quickly; it requires a qualifier. Therefore, this concept does not warrant a standalone dictionary entry.
