stiff manner

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: stiff manner|extra.023792
rigid or awkward way of moving

Synset Examples

He walked in a stiff manner after the long flight.

Reason

'Stiff manner' is a descriptive phrase rather than a standard lexical item; it is a transparent combination of 'stiff' and 'manner' and would not have its own dictionary entry in major dictionaries like Oxford. Such collocations are not included unless they have idiomatic or specialized meaning.
