Synset Examples

Reason

'The taffia' refers to a group controlling local business, but its synset is too general and not a widely recognized standard term in English. There is insufficient evidence that 'taffia' is an established dictionary word rather than a specialized, perhaps local, coinage or a play on 'mafia' (possibly referencing 'Taff', a nickname for people from Wales, or the River Taff). Since it is not commonly found in authoritative dictionaries and is not broadly used in general English, it should be excluded.
