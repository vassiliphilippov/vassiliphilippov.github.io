Reason

This sense of 'collapse' refers to the act of throwing oneself down (e.g., a 'flop' onto a bed), which is not a commonly recognized noun meaning of 'collapse' in standard English dictionaries. It is too specific, infrequent, and does not represent general usage. Standard dictionaries define 'collapse' as a failure, breakdown, or sudden falling down, but not specifically as the act of intentionally throwing oneself down. Thus, this concept does not warrant a separate entry.
