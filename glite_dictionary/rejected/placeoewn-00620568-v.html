place

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00620568-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: place|oewn-00620568-v
recognize as being; establish the identity of someone or something

Synset Examples

She identified the man on the ‘wanted’ poster

Reason

This sense of 'place' as 'recognize as being; establish the identity of someone or something' is not current or attested in standard modern English. This meaning is better captured by 'identify' or 'recognize' and is not a dictionary sense of 'place' as used in reputable sources like the Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, it should not be included.
