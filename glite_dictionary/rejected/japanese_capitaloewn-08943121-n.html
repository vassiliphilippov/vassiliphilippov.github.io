Japanese capital

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08943121-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Japanese capital|oewn-08943121-n
the capital and largest city of Japan; the economic and cultural center of Japan

Reason

The concept 'Japanese capital' here refers generically to a city by its status as capital rather than as a proper noun (Tokyo). Dictionaries index 'Tokyo' as an entry, not the phrase 'Japanese capital'. This term is too general and would not appear as a separate entry in a major dictionary like Oxford. Therefore, it should be excluded.
