Reason

This verb sense of 'purpose' ('to propose or intend') exists, but it is very rare and archaic in contemporary English usage. The common verb forms for this sense are 'intend' or 'aim', and modern dictionaries typically only list 'purpose' as a noun. Including this verb sense would not be helpful for modern learners, as it is virtually unused today.
