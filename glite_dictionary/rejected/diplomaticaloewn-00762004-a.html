diplomatical

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00762004-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: diplomatical|oewn-00762004-a
using or marked by tact in dealing with sensitive matters or people

Synset Examples

the hostess averted a confrontation with a diplomatic change of subject

Reason

The form 'diplomatical' is archaic and almost never used in contemporary English. The standard and widely accepted form is 'diplomatic.' Therefore, this specific concept should not be included as a standalone entry in a modern English-English dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary.
