jerk

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01888376-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: jerk|oewn-01888376-v
jump vertically, with legs stiff and back arched

Synset Examples

the young filly bucked

Reason

The synset 'jump vertically, with legs stiff and back arched' is not directly associated with the verb 'jerk' in standard modern English; it more specifically describes 'buck' (as in horses). This is not a recognized sense for 'jerk' in major English dictionaries, so it should be excluded.
