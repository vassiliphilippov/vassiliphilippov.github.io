portentous

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01855888-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: portentous|oewn-01855888-s
puffed up with vanity

Synset Examples

a grandiloquent and boastful manner
overblown oratory
a pompous speech
pseudo-scientific gobbledygook and pontifical hooey

Reason

This sense of 'portentous' as 'puffed up with vanity' (meaning pompous, self-important) is rare and not standard; the main established senses relate to 'portending' or being ominous. Oxford and other major dictionaries do not include this as a common definition. The common and recognizable meanings should be separately included, but this synonym for 'pompous' is not standard enough for a general-purpose dictionary.
