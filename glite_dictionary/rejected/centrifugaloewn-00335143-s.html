centrifugal

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00335143-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: centrifugal|oewn-00335143-s
conveying information to the muscles from the CNS

Synset Examples

motor nerves

Reason

The sense 'centrifugal' as 'conveying information to the muscles from the CNS' is highly technical and specialized; in general English dictionaries, 'motor' is the recognized term for these nerves. The primary, common meanings of 'centrifugal' relate to movement away from the center, not nerve function. This synset is too domain-specific and not widely recognized as a general sense for 'centrifugal' in mainstream English dictionaries.
