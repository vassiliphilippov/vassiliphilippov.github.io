cyberpunk

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10174791-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: cyberpunk|oewn-10174791-n
a programmer who breaks into computer systems in order to steal or change or destroy information as a form of cyber-terrorism

Reason

This sense of 'cyberpunk', referring to a computer criminal, is not commonly attested in modern English. 'Cyberpunk' is almost exclusively used to describe a subgenre of science fiction or its corresponding aesthetic and cultural movement, not for a type of criminal. Therefore, this particular sense should not be included in the dictionary.
