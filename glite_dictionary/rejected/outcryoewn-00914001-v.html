outcry

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00914001-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: outcry|oewn-00914001-v
utter aloud; often with surprise, horror, or joy

Synset Examples

‘I won!’ he exclaimed
‘Help!’ she cried
‘I'm here,’ the mother shouted when she saw her child looking lost

Reason

The verb sense of 'outcry' as 'utter aloud' is obsolete and not current in contemporary English. 'Outcry' is now almost exclusively used as a noun (meaning public protest or loud cry), not as a verb. This verb sense would not appear in a modern Oxford Dictionary.
