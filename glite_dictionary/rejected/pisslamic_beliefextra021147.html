Pisslamic belief

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: Pisslamic belief|extra.021147
a derogatory term for extremist interpretations of Islam

Synset Examples

Pisslamic beliefs vary widely among followers.

Reason

This term is an intentionally derogatory, offensive slur rather than a standard part of the English lexicon. It is not suitable for inclusion in a general English dictionary like the Oxford Dictionary, both because of its extremely offensive nature and because it is not a widely recognized or established term. Dictionaries typically do not include recent or intentionally inflammatory slurs unless they have widespread, notable historical use and contextual value for learners.
