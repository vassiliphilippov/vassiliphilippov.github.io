energetic

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00888341-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: energetic|oewn-00888341-s
working hard to promote an enterprise

Reason

This sense of 'energetic' is an attributive adjective related to working hard to promote an enterprise, but is not distinct from the general adjective sense ('having or showing a lot of energy'). The specific sub-sense of 'promoting an enterprise' is too narrow and context-specific and does not warrant a separate entry. The general sense 'energetic' is already included in dictionaries.
