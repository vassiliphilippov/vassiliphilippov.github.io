post-maturity

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14449729-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: post-maturity|oewn-14449729-n
the state in which women have stopped ovulating

Reason

This concept refers to a medical or biological state (cessation of ovulation) but 'post-maturity' is not commonly used in English (including medical contexts) to refer specifically to women having stopped ovulating; the usual terms are 'menopause' or 'postmenopausal'. In English, 'post-maturity' typically refers to a pregnancy exceeding the normal term. Thus, this synset is not directly related to the headword as it would appear in Oxford Dictionary.
