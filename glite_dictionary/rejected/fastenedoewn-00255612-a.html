Reason

The synset refers to a specific sense of 'fastened' as an adjective meaning 'fastened with strings or cords', but 'fastened' as an adjective commonly refers to something being attached or secured by any means, not just with strings or cords. This is a highly specific participial use and unlikely to appear as a separate sense in major dictionaries. It is not sufficiently distinct or established as a headword sense; thus, it should not be included as a separate dictionary entry.
