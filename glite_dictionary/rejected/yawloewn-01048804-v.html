Synset Examples

Reason

The sense of 'yawl' as a verb meaning 'emit long loud cries' is extremely rare and obsolete. Modern English dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary do not normally include this verb sense, as 'yawl' is almost exclusively used as a noun referring to a type of boat. The typical verbs for this meaning are 'wail', 'howl', or 'yowl'. Therefore, this concept should not be included.
