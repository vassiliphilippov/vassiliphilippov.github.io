an Ethiopian in the woodpile

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: an Ethiopian in the woodpile|extra.000370
a hidden problem or unexpected issue

Synset Examples

I thought everything was fine, but there was an Ethiopian in the woodpile.
When the project failed, I realized there was an Ethiopian in the woodpile.
The sudden budget cuts revealed an Ethiopian in the woodpile.

Reason

This phrase is an obsolete variant of the offensive and now disused 'nigger in the woodpile.' It is not in current, accepted usage and would not appropriately appear in modern dictionaries. The origin and content are offensive and inappropriate for inclusion.
