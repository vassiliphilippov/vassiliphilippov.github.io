Synset Examples

Reason

The phrase 'in apposition' is a technical grammatical term describing a manner, but it would not appear as a fixed entry in a standard dictionary (like Oxford), since 'apposition' is the base noun and the phrase simply derives its meaning structurally. Dictionaries typically do not include set adverbial phrases like this unless they have distinct idiomatic use. The adverb 'appositively' is more relevant as a dictionary entry.
