commuter

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03082806-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: commuter|oewn-03082806-n
a passenger train that is ridden primarily by passengers who travel regularly from one place to another

Reason

This sense ('commuter' meaning a passenger train) is not a widely recognized or standard use in contemporary English. In dictionaries, 'commuter' primarily refers to a person who commutes, not to a train. The usual term for such a train is 'commuter train.' Therefore, this sense should not be included as a standalone entry for 'commuter.'
