Reason

The synset is not specific to the uniquely named organization 'Islamic Group,' and describes a general type of organization rather than a proper noun with distinctive properties. Additionally, 'Islamic Group' as described is not a standard, widely recognized dictionary lemma in the way required by major dictionaries. Dictionaries typically include names of terrorist organizations only if they are widely recognized proper nouns ('al-Qaeda', 'Jemaah Islamiyah'). This entry is overly general and would not appear in Oxford or similar dictionaries as a headword.
