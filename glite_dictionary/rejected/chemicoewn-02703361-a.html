chemic

WordNet ID: oewn-02703361-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: chemic|oewn-02703361-a
relating to or used in chemistry

Synset Examples

chemical engineer
chemical balance

Reason

The adjective 'chemic' is archaic and almost entirely replaced by 'chemical' in modern English. It would rarely, if ever, appear in a contemporary dictionary like the Oxford, except perhaps as a historical note or in specialized reference. The current sense ('relating to or used in chemistry') is fully covered by the standard adjective 'chemical'.
