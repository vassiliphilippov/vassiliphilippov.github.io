Reason

This concept represents the sense of "dozen" as an adjective meaning "being one more than eleven" (i.e., twelve). While "dozen" is commonly used as a noun and numeral to denote a group of twelve, it is not standard English to use it as an adjective with this meaning; such usage does not appear in major dictionaries as an adjective. Instead, "dozen" is listed as a noun or determiner. Therefore, this particular concept should not be included as a separate adjective entry.
