Synset Examples

Reason

The concept 'aboard' with the sense 'side by side' is nautical jargon and extremely rare; this use is not standard, and is absent or obsolete in modern dictionaries like Oxford. 'Aboard' in modern English primarily means 'on or into a ship, train, etc.' The sense 'side by side' is too obscure and not relevant for a general dictionary entry.
