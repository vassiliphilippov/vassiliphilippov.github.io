settler

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10603368-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: settler|oewn-10603368-n
a negotiator who settles disputes

Reason

This sense of 'settler' as 'a negotiator who settles disputes' is extremely rare and not a standard or widely recognized usage in modern English. The primary, dictionary-worthy sense of 'settler' is 'a person who moves with others to live in a new country or area.' This rare sense would not appear as a main entry in Oxford or most major dictionaries.
