Reason

The synset describes a very specific medical sense of 'shoot' meaning 'give an injection to', but this usage is rare, nonstandard, and not commonly recorded in general English dictionaries, where 'shoot' is much more frequently found with other more central meanings. The medical sense would more often use 'inject' rather than 'shoot'. Therefore, it should be excluded from the dictionary.
