by small degrees

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00157136-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: by small degrees|oewn-00157136-r
by a short distance

Synset Examples

they moved it by inches

Reason

The concept 'by small degrees' as defined here refers to doing something by a short distance, but this is a compositional phrase rather than a fixed idiom or set phrase. It is not commonly used as a lexicalized unit in English (unlike e.g. 'by degrees' meaning 'gradually'), and would not warrant a separate entry in a dictionary like Oxford. The synonym 'by inches' is also a compositional, non-lexicalized phrase in this context. Therefore, it is too general and does not merit inclusion.
