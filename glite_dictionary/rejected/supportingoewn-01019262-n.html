supporting

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01019262-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: supporting|oewn-01019262-n
the act of bearing the weight of or strengthening

Synset Examples

he leaned against the wall for support

Reason

The concept is a nominalization referring to the general act of 'supporting' as in 'the supporting of something,' but 'supporting' as a noun is rarely used in this sense in natural English. The canonical noun is 'support,' which covers this meaning. Thus, the concept does not represent a distinct, headword-worthy noun entry; it is better handled under 'support' (noun), not as 'supporting.'
