Synset POS: r
Internal ID: straight-backed|oewn-00331596-r
in a straight-backed manner

Synset Examples

the old man still walks erectly

Reason

The synset is an adverbial form relating to walking or being 'straight-backed', which is not a stand-alone adverb in English (i.e., 'straight-backedly' is not standard, and 'straight-backed' itself is used as an adjective). The concept 'straight-backed' as an adverb ('in a straight-backed manner') does not occur naturally and would not appear as a headword in reputable English dictionaries. Only the adjective sense is included in Oxford Dictionary and similar sources.
