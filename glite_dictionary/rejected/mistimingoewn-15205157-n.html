mistiming

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15205157-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: mistiming|oewn-15205157-n
something located at a time when it could not have existed or occurred

Reason

The given sense for 'mistiming' corresponds to an abstract meaning ('something located at a time when it could not have existed or occurred') that is not a standard usage listed in reputable English dictionaries. In normal English, 'mistiming' refers to the act of timing something incorrectly, not to any object or occurrence misplaced in time. Thus, the concept is either too general or based on a misapplied WordNet sense, and should be excluded from the dictionary.
