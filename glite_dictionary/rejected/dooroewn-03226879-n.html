door

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03226879-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: door|oewn-03226879-n
a room that is entered via a door

Synset Examples

his office is the third door down the hall on the left

Reason

This concept refers to the sense of 'door' meaning a room, as in 'the third door on the left,' which is a metonymic or contextual use rather than a standard dictionary sense. Dictionaries define 'door' as the physical structure or entrance, not as a synonym for the room itself. Therefore, this usage does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
