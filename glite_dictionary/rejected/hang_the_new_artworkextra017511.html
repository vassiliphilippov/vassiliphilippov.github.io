to display art on a wall

Synset Examples

They plan to hang the new artwork tomorrow.

Reason

The phrase 'hang the new artwork' is a compositional and transparent phrase made of 'hang' (verb) and 'artwork' (noun). The meaning 'to display art on a wall' follows directly from the usual sense of 'hang' (to fix something so that it is supported at only one point and able to move or swing freely) applied to 'artwork.' This is not a fixed expression, idiom, or phrasal verb. It would not appear as a separate entry in the Oxford Dictionary.
