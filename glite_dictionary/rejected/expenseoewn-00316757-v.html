Reason

The concept describes a very narrow, technical accounting use of 'expense' as a verb, which is rarely used in general English. The verb 'expense' meaning 'reduce the estimated value of something' is almost always expressed by 'write off' or specific accounting phrases. Such use is not included as a standard headword definition in major English dictionaries like Oxford; it's not broadly recognizable or widely attested as common usage. Therefore, it's not suitable for inclusion as a standalone verb definition in an English-English learner dictionary.
