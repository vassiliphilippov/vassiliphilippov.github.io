fix firmly or securely

Reason

The verb 'intrench' is an archaic variant spelling of 'entrench', and is extremely rare in modern usage. The standard form in contemporary English is 'entrench'. Including 'intrench' as a separate entry is unnecessary; at most, it should be mentioned as a historical or variant spelling under 'entrench'.
