Reason

'Melanogrammus' is the scientific genus name for haddock and is primarily used in taxonomic contexts, not as a common English word. General English dictionaries like Oxford do not typically include scientific genus names unless they are also part of common usage (e.g., 'Homo sapiens'). Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
