capitalisation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00093826-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capitalisation|oewn-00093826-n
the sale of capital stock

Reason

This sense of 'capitalisation' as 'the sale of capital stock' is an extremely narrow, technical, and outdated financial usage. In modern dictionaries like Oxford, 'capitalisation' is defined as the total value of a company's shares or the act of providing capital, rather than specifically the sale of stock. As such, this specific sense is too restricted and does not warrant a separate entry; it is covered by more common definitions of 'capitalisation' relating to finance and stock value.
