Reason

This sense of 'easy' meaning 'in fortunate circumstances financially; moderately rich' is rare, context-dependent, and not a standalone adjective sense in major English dictionaries like Oxford. The meaning is better covered by words such as 'comfortable,' 'well-off,' 'prosperous,' or 'wealthy.' The adjective 'easy' in English dictionaries does not conventionally have this sense on its own. Therefore, it should be excluded.
