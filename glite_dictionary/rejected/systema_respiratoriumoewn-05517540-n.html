systema respiratorium

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05517540-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: systema respiratorium|oewn-05517540-n
the system for taking in oxygen and giving off carbon dioxide; in terrestrial animals this is accomplished by breathing

Reason

The concept 'systema respiratorium' is a Latin scientific term for the 'respiratory system.' Standard English dictionaries typically list 'respiratory system,' not the Latin name, unless the Latin term is used in English texts. As such, this is not a standard English headword and should not be included.
