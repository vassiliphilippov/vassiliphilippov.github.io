percentage point

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06825147-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: percentage point|oewn-06825147-n
the dot at the left of a decimal fraction

Reason

The synset definition refers to 'the dot at the left of a decimal fraction,' which is not the primary or widely recognized meaning of 'percentage point' in standard English dictionaries. The term 'percentage point' usually relates to the difference between percentages, not the dot in decimals. Therefore, this concept is too specific/misapplied and should not be included as such.
