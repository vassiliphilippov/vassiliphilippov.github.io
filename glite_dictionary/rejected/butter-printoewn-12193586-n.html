butter-print

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-12193586-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: butter-print|oewn-12193586-n
tall annual herb or subshrub of tropical Asia having velvety leaves and yellow flowers and yielding a strong fiber; naturalized in southeastern Europe and United States

Reason

The synset describes a plant species (Abutilon theophrasti) known more commonly as 'velvetleaf' or 'Indian mallow', not 'butter-print.' The term 'butter-print' is not commonly recognized or used as the standard English headword for this plant in major dictionaries; it is neither the canonical nor widely used common name. The concept is too obscure for inclusion as a main dictionary entry under this headword.
