showing a seam

Reason

The concept 'seamy' as 'showing a seam' is overly literal and not the common or primary meaning found in major dictionaries; the typical sense is metaphorical (sordid, morally degraded). The literal use meaning 'having a seam showing' is rare and would not appear as a main entry; thus, this sense is too narrow and not dictionary-worthy.
