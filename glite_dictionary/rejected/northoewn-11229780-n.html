Reason

The synset here is too general, associating 'North' primarily with the broad category of 'person' (specifically the individual British statesman Lord North). Proper names of people whose lemma mapping is simply to their status as 'person' are excluded from standard English dictionaries like Oxford, unless the person has become a commonly used term for something else, which is not the case here.
