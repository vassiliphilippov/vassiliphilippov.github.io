shoot

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01588444-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: shoot|oewn-01588444-v
force or drive (a fluid or gas) into by piercing

Synset Examples

inject hydrogen into the balloon

Reason

This sense of 'shoot' as 'to force or drive a fluid or gas into something by piercing' is covered in standard dictionaries only as a secondary or technical usage under the more general verb 'inject.' The primary verb 'shoot' typically refers to firing a projectile, moving rapidly, or taking a photograph. This usage is rare, specialized, and better described by 'inject,' so it does not require its own separate entry for 'shoot.'
