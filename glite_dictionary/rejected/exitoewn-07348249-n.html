exit

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07348249-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: exit|oewn-07348249-n
euphemistic expressions for death

Synset Examples

thousands mourned his passing

Reason

Although 'exit' can be used as a euphemism for death, this usage is rare and not standard; it does not merit its own dictionary entry in the way the more common euphemism 'passing' does. It would not appear as a separate entry for this sense in the Oxford Dictionary.
