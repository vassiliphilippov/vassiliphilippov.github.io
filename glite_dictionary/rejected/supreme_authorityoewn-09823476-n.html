Reason

The synset 'someone with the power to settle matters at will' is too general and does not specifically match the headword 'supreme authority' in standard dictionary usage. 'Supreme authority' as a noun phrase does not typically denote a person, but rather the highest level of power or control (an institution, office, or abstract authority). Since the synset is general and misaligned, this concept should be excluded.
