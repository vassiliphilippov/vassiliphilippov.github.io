Reason

The concept 'truly iconic' is a common collocation (adjective plus intensifier) but not an established compound, idiom, or fixed phrase with a separate meaning from its parts. Its meaning is transparent and predictable: 'truly' simply intensifies 'iconic.' This construction would not appear as a separate entry in Oxford Dictionary, nor does it warrant inclusion as a headword. The base word 'iconic' is already included in major dictionaries.
