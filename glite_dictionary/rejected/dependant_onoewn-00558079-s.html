dependant on

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00558079-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: dependant on|oewn-00558079-s
being determined by conditions or circumstances that follow

Synset Examples

arms sales contingent on the approval of Congress

Reason

The phrase 'dependant on' is actually a misspelling of 'dependent on' (American spelling) or 'dependent upon,' and the dictionary headword should be 'dependent (on/upon),' not 'dependant on.' Additionally, the synonym in the WordNet example ('contingent on') refers to a predicative adjective use, and would be more appropriate under the adjective 'dependent.' Including 'dependant on' as an entry is not standard practice in reputable dictionaries.
