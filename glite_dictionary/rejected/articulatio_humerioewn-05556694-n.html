articulatio humeri

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05556694-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: articulatio humeri|oewn-05556694-n
a ball-and-socket joint between the head of the humerus and a cavity of the scapula

Reason

The concept 'articulatio humeri' is a Latin anatomical term. Standard English-language dictionaries, including Oxford, would include the more common English term 'shoulder joint' for this anatomical feature, not the Latin term unless in the context of detailed medical terminology. Unless it is a well-established loanword in English, which it is not, this phrase would not be included as a main entry.
