counteract

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02549304-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: counteract|oewn-02549304-v
destroy property or hinder normal operations

Synset Examples

The Resistance sabotaged railroad operations during the war

Reason

This sense of 'counteract' is not distinct from the general verb 'counteract' meaning 'to act against so as to neutralize or prevent an effect'. The sense given here (destroy property or hinder operations) is more accurately described by the verbs 'sabotage' or 'interfere with', not 'counteract'. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a separate dictionary entry.
