Synset Examples

Reason

The sense of 'item' as an adverb meaning 'also' is archaic and rarely, if ever, used in contemporary English outside historical or highly specialized contexts. It would not appear in a standard modern English dictionary like Oxford under this usage, except perhaps in historical notes. Therefore, it should be excluded.
