Reason

The synset for 'translate' in this context refers to 'making sense of a language' in the sense of 'understand' or 'read', such as 'She understands French' or 'Can you read Greek?'. This sense is not a core, standard meaning of 'translate' as presented in major English dictionaries where the primary sense is 'to express the meaning of words in another language'. The sense provided is already adequately covered by headwords such as 'understand' or 'read'. Therefore, this specific concept should not be included.
