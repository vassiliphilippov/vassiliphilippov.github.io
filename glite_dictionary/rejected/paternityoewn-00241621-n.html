paternity

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00241621-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: paternity|oewn-00241621-n
the act of initiating a new idea or theory or writing

Synset Examples

the authorship of the theory is disputed

Reason

The synset refers to a highly specialized, rarely used sense of 'paternity' meaning 'authorship' or 'originatorship' of ideas, which is neither current nor widely recognized in contemporary English. This sense is not broadly attested in major dictionaries and would not appear in modern Oxford Dictionary under 'paternity'. The prevalent sense of 'paternity' concerns fatherhood/biological relation, not authorship of ideas.
