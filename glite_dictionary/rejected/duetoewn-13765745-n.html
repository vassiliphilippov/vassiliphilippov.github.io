duet

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13765745-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: duet|oewn-13765745-n
two items of the same kind

Reason

This sense of 'duet' as 'two items of the same kind' is overly general and not specific to standard English usage; in dictionaries, 'duet' is principally defined as a musical composition for two performers or the act of two people performing together. The general sense as 'two items' is too broad and not idiomatic to include as a dictionary entry.
