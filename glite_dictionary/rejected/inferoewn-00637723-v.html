Reason

This concept should not be included because the synset ('guess correctly; solve by guessing') for 'infer' does not align with standard dictionary entries for 'infer.' In modern standard usage, 'infer' means to deduce or conclude information from evidence and reasoning, not to guess correctly or to solve by guessing. The sense described here is not a recognized meaning of 'infer' in Oxford or similar dictionaries.
