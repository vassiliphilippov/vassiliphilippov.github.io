slash

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01894785-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: slash|oewn-01894785-v
move or stir about violently

Synset Examples

The feverish patient thrashed around in his bed

Reason

This sense of 'slash' (meaning to move or stir about violently) is not a standard or commonly recognized use of the word in reputable English dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The more typical meanings of 'slash' relate to cutting, striking with a sweeping motion, or a mark (/) rather than moving about violently. The example given ('thrashed around in his bed') is more appropriate for words like 'thrash' or 'flail.' Therefore, this sense of 'slash' should not be included.
