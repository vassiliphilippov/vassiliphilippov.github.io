Reason

The concept 'logistician' as 'a person skilled at logic' is incorrect and does not represent standard or attested English usage. In standard English, 'logistician' refers to a professional specializing in logistics, not in logic. The headword-synset pairing is incorrect and too general. This meaning should be excluded from the dictionary.
