Reason

The concept 'selfish attitude' is a compositional phrase consisting of the adjective 'selfish' and the noun 'attitude'. Its meaning is fully deducible from the meanings of 'selfish' and 'attitude', and such adjective + noun combinations are generally not included as separate entries in monolingual English dictionaries like Oxford. The synset is not specialized or idiomatic beyond the collocation, so it should be excluded.
