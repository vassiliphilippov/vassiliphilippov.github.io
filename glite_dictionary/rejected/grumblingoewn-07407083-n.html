grumbling

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07407083-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: grumbling|oewn-07407083-n
a loud low dull continuous noise

Synset Examples

they heard the rumbling of thunder

Reason

The synset refers to 'grumbling' as a general loud, low, dull, continuous noise, which is not a common or standard noun sense in modern dictionaries. 'Grumbling' as a noun more commonly refers to the act of complaining in a low voice, not a general sound. Therefore, this meaning is either too rare or not sufficiently connected to the headword for inclusion as a primary dictionary entry.
