furnishing

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03410505-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: furnishing|oewn-03410505-n
(usually plural) accessory wearing apparel

Reason

This sense of 'furnishing' as 'accessory wearing apparel' is obsolete and extremely rare. It would not appear in a modern English-English dictionary like Oxford unless explicitly marked as archaic. In contemporary English, 'furnishing' is strongly associated with furniture and items that decorate or equip a room, not with personal clothing accessories.
