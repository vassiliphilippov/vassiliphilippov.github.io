punitorily

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00437844-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: punitorily|oewn-00437844-r
in a way intended to punish

Reason

The concept 'punitorily' is formed as an adverb from 'punitory', itself an extremely rare variant of 'punitive'. 'Punitorily' does not appear in authoritative, modern dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster and is practically never used in natural English. Its practical usage is negligible and it would not be included in a standard English-English dictionary.
