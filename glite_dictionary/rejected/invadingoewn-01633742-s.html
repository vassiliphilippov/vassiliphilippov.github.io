invading

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01633742-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: invading|oewn-01633742-s
involving invasion or aggressive attack

Synset Examples

invasive war

Reason

'invading' in this context is a participial adjective only used attributively (as in 'invading army'), and its meaning is already fully covered by 'invade' (verb) and 'invasive' (adjective). Dictionaries typically do not list every participial form separately when it has no distinct meaning or usage from the main verb or related adjectives. Therefore, this concept is too marginal for its own entry.
