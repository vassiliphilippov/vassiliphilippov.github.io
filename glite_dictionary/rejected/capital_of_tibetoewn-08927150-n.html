Reason

The concept 'capital of Tibet' with the synset 'city' is too general and refers to a specific place (Lhasa) through its generic property as a capital city. Dictionary entries typically include the actual proper noun (e.g., 'Lhasa') rather than the descriptive phrase. Therefore, this should be excluded.
