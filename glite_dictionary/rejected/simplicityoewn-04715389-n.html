simplicity

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04715389-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: simplicity|oewn-04715389-n
freedom from difficulty or hardship or effort

Synset Examples

he rose through the ranks with apparent ease
they put it into containers for ease of transportation
the very easiness of the deed held her back

Reason

The synset meaning described is 'freedom from difficulty or effort,' which fits the word 'ease' or sometimes 'easiness,' but not 'simplicity' in common modern usage. 'Simplicity' is more commonly defined as the quality of being simple or uncomplicated, not specifically as 'freedom from hardship or effort.' The headword and synset do not appropriately match; therefore, this concept should not be included as 'simplicity.'
