fee

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02206915-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: fee|oewn-02206915-v
give a tip or gratuity to in return for a service, beyond the compensation agreed on

Synset Examples

Remember to tip the waiter
fee the steward

Reason

The verb 'fee' meaning 'to give a tip or gratuity' is extremely rare and archaic in modern English. The standard verb for this action is 'tip'. 'Fee' is almost never used in this sense in contemporary English and would not appear as a current verb in Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, this usage should be excluded from the dictionary.
