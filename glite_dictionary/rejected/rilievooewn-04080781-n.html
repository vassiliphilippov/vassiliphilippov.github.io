rilievo

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04080781-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: rilievo|oewn-04080781-n
sculpture consisting of shapes carved on a surface so as to stand out from the surrounding background

Reason

The term 'rilievo' is not established as a standard English word; it is an Italian word for 'relief' in sculpture. The English dictionary entry would be for 'relief' (in the sculptural sense), not 'rilievo.' Therefore, it should not be included as a headword.
