disinvest

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02390514-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: disinvest|oewn-02390514-v
deprive of status or authority

Synset Examples

he was divested of his rights and his title
They disinvested themselves of their rights

Reason

The sense of 'disinvest' as 'deprive of status or authority' is archaic and extremely rare in modern English; it does not appear in major dictionaries as a distinct, accepted meaning. Modern usage of 'disinvest' refers almost exclusively to withdrawing investments or capital. The concept as described is outdated and should not be included under 'disinvest' as a separate entry in a contemporary dictionary.
