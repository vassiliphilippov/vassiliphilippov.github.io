Wagon

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09242765-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Wagon|oewn-09242765-n
a group of seven bright stars in the constellation Ursa Major

Reason

The concept refers to 'Wagon' as an asterism within Ursa Major, commonly known as 'the Plough' in British English or 'the Big Dipper' in American English. However, 'Wagon' as this group of stars is not standard usage in English; the typical dictionary would include 'Big Dipper' or 'Plough' for this sense, not 'Wagon'. Including this would be misleading, as it does not reflect standard or recognizable English usage for this sense.
