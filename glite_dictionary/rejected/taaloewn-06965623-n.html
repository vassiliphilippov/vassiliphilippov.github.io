Taal

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06965623-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Taal|oewn-06965623-n
an official language of the Republic of South Africa; closely related to Dutch and Flemish

Reason

The synset refers to 'Taal' as an official language of South Africa, but in English lexicography, this language is known as 'Afrikaans.' 'Taal' simply means 'language' in Afrikaans/Dutch, and is not the English term for the language. Therefore, this concept is too general and does not belong as a headword in an English-English dictionary.
