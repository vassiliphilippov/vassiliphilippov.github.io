phoner

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09907469-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: phoner|oewn-09907469-n
the person initiating a telephone call

Synset Examples

there were so many callers that he finally disconnected the telephone

Reason

The concept 'phoner' as 'the person initiating a telephone call' is not in widespread, standard use in English; the common term is 'caller.' The example given uses 'caller,' not 'phoner.' This term would not appear in authoritative dictionaries such as Oxford, except perhaps as a very rare slang or industry-specific term, but there is no evidence for such usage here.
