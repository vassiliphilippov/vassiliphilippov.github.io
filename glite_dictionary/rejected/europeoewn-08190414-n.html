Europe

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08190414-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Europe|oewn-08190414-n
an international organization of European countries formed after World War II to reduce trade barriers and increase cooperation among its members

Synset Examples

he tried to take Britain into the European Union

Reason

The synset describes Europe as an international organization, which actually refers to the European Union rather than the continent itself. The word 'Europe' as used here is too general, and its sense as an international organization is not standard. 'European Union' would be the proper dictionary entry for this meaning.
