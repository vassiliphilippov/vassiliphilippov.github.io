knit scarf

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: knit scarf|extra.018712
to make a scarf using yarn through the process of knitting

Synset Examples

She loves to knit scarves for her friends.
Aunt Sally knitted a beautiful scarf for my birthday.

Reason

The concept 'knit scarf' is a compositional phrase: 'knit' is a verb and 'scarf' is its direct object. Dictionaries, including Oxford, would not include such transparent verb-object combinations unless they have non-compositional or idiomatic meanings. Here, the meaning is derived directly from 'knit' and 'scarf.' Therefore, this entry should be excluded from the dictionary.
