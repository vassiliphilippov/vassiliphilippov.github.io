range

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01578996-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: range|oewn-01578996-v
feed as in a meadow or pasture

Synset Examples

the herd was grazing

Reason

The sense of 'range' meaning 'to feed as in a meadow or pasture' is extremely rare and archaic; the verb 'graze' and 'browse' are used for this sense in standard modern English. 'Range' is not commonly used in this meaning and would not appear in a general learner's dictionary such as Oxford. Therefore, it should be excluded.
