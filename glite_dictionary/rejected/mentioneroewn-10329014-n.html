mentioner

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10329014-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: mentioner|oewn-10329014-n
a speaker who refers to something briefly or incidentally

Reason

While 'mentioner' is grammatically valid, it is extremely rare and not established as a standard English term for someone who makes a mention. It would not typically appear in Oxford or major English dictionaries, and its usage is not common enough to merit inclusion.
