Minnewit

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11201428-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Minnewit|oewn-11201428-n
Dutch colonist who bought Manhattan from the Native Americans for the equivalent of $24 (1580-1638)

Reason

The synset for 'Minnewit' is actually 'person', which is too general for a dictionary entry, and the name is primarily a variant spelling/transliteration for the better-known historical figure Peter Minuit. In English dictionaries, only the most established forms are included, and 'Minnewit' is not the common or standard form.
