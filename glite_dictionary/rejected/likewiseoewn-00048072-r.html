likewise

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00048072-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: likewise|oewn-00048072-r
in addition

Synset Examples

he has a Mercedes, too

Reason

The specific meaning 'in addition' for 'likewise' is overly broad and not unique to this word; in Standard English, 'likewise' means 'in the same way' or 'similarly', not simply 'also' or 'in addition'. The usage as directly synonymous with 'too' in the sense 'he has a Mercedes, likewise' is unidiomatic. Therefore, this synset should not be included for 'likewise' in the dictionary.
