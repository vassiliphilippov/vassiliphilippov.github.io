externality

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05085830-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: externality|oewn-05085830-n
the quality or state of being outside or directed toward or relating to the outside or exterior

Synset Examples

the outwardness of the world

Reason

The synset definition refers to an abstract quality of 'being outside' or 'outwardness', but the noun 'externality' is not commonly used in this general sense in contemporary English. Instead, 'externality' is far more commonly used in economic and social contexts to mean an external effect or consequence (especially unintended). The general sense given here is too rare and abstract for dictionary inclusion; modern dictionaries would focus on the primary economic sense.
