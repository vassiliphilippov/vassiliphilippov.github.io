join

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08015154-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: join|oewn-08015154-n
a set containing all and only the members of two or more given sets

Synset Examples

let C be the union of the sets A and B

Reason

The sense described is more accurately called 'union' in mathematics, not 'join' in general English usage. The example sentence itself refers to 'union'. This mathematical term is not established in English dictionaries under 'join' as a noun meaning 'the union of sets.' Therefore, this concept does not warrant a standalone entry in an English-English dictionary.
