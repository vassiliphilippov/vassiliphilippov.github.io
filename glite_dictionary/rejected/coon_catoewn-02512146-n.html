omnivorous mammal of Central America and South America

Reason

The synset 'omnivorous mammal of Central America and South America' refers to a raccoon, not specifically the 'coon cat.' 'Coon cat' is not widely used in English as a reference to a type of omnivorous mammal; it is more accurately the Maine Coon, a breed of cat, which does not fit the given sense. The current synset is too general or misapplied for inclusion.
