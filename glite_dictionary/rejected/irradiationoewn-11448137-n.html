irradiation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11448137-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: irradiation|oewn-11448137-n
a column of light (as from a beacon)

Reason

The synset sense refers to 'irradiation' as a 'column of light (as from a beacon),' which is highly specific and rare. In standard, current English, 'irradiation' is primarily used in the context of exposure to radiation. This usage is not common or standard enough to warrant a main dictionary entry distinct from the primary scientific meaning.
