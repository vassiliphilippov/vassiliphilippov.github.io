Reason

The concept refers to 'ABC' as a generic term for an alphabet or character set, but 'alphabet' is the standard dictionary entry. 'ABC' in this sense is not commonly used as a headword for this meaning and would not appear in a general English dictionary in this sense. Therefore, it should be excluded.
