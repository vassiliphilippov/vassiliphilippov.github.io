Reason

The verb sense 'milk' meaning 'add milk to' is very rare and nonstandard. In contemporary English, 'milk' is not commonly used as a verb in this way. The standard usage of 'milk' as a verb refers to extracting milk, typically from a cow. This sense ('add milk to') is too rare and does not merit a separate dictionary entry.
