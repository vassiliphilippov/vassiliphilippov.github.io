room access

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03228735-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: room access|oewn-03228735-n
the entrance (the space in a wall) through which you enter or leave a room or building; the space that a door can close

Synset Examples

he stuck his head in the doorway

Reason

The concept 'room access' with the synset 'entrance (the space in a wall) through which you enter or leave a room or building' is not a standard or established dictionary headword for this meaning. The word 'doorway' is the usual and correct term for this definition. 'Room access' would not appear in the Oxford Dictionary with this definition.
