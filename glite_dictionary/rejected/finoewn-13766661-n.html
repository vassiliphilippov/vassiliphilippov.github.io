the cardinal number that is the sum of four and one

Reason

The synset described is for the number five, not for 'fin.' 'Fin' as a headword does not mean the cardinal number five in standard modern English; 'fin' can mean a fish appendage or a slang term for a five-dollar bill (primarily American slang), but not the number five itself. Therefore, this concept does not match an appropriate definition for 'fin' as would appear in the Oxford Dictionary.
