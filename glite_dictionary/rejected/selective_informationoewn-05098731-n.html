selective information

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05098731-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: selective information|oewn-05098731-n
(communication theory) a numerical measure of the uncertainty of an outcome

Synset Examples

the signal contained thousands of bits of information

Reason

The synset described as 'a numerical measure of the uncertainty of an outcome' relates more generally to the technical term 'information' (as in 'bits of information' from information theory), not specifically to 'selective information.' The phrase 'selective information' is not the correct label for this concept and is not established as a dictionary entry in major dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
