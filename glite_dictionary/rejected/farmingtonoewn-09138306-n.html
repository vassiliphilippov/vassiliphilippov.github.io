Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Farmington|oewn-09138306-n
a town in northwestern New Mexico

Reason

This synset refers to the general sense of being a place (specifically a town), which is too broad and tied to a specific instance (Farmington, New Mexico). Proper nouns for most towns, unless globally notable, are generally excluded from standard English dictionaries such as Oxford. Such place names appear in gazetteers or encyclopedias, not standard word dictionaries.
