push

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: push|oewn-04033499-n
an electrical switch operated by pressing

Synset Examples

the elevator was operated by push buttons
the push beside the bed operated a buzzer at the desk

Reason

The sense refers to a very specific and somewhat outdated or technical usage of 'push' as a noun for a physical push-button switch. This is not a common contemporary dictionary sense under the headword 'push'; modern dictionaries would list this usage under 'push-button' or simply 'button', not 'push'. The headword is not closely tied to the synset meaning for most readers.
