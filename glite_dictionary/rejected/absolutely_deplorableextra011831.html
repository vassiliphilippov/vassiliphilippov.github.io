absolutely deplorable

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: absolutely deplorable|extra.011831
extremely bad or unacceptable

Synset Examples

His behavior was absolutely deplorable during the meeting.

Reason

The concept 'absolutely deplorable' is a phrase consisting of an adverb intensifier ('absolutely') modifying an adjective ('deplorable'). Dictionaries usually do not include every possible combination of intensifier and adjective as separate entries unless the phrase has a unique idiomatic meaning, which is not the case here. The meaning is fully compositional: 'absolutely' just intensifies 'deplorable.'
