tingling

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00925680-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: tingling|oewn-00925680-s
exciting by touching lightly so as to cause laughter or twitching movements

Reason

This concept for 'tingling' is not appropriate for a standard dictionary entry. The sense described here is actually more fitting for 'tickling,' which means to excite by touching lightly so as to cause laughter or twitching movements. 'Tingling' typically refers to a slightly stinging or prickling sensation (as from numbness or excitement), not the act of tickling. Therefore, this particular synset is not faithful to typical usage and does not belong as a dictionary entry under 'tingling.'
