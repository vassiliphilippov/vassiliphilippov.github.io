American golden-crested kinglet

Reason

The concept 'Regulus satrata' is an old or incorrect scientific name (now Regulus satrapa) and refers to a biological species; such Latin binomial names are not included as headwords in general English-English dictionaries like Oxford unless they have a common English usage, which this does not. The correct English term would be 'golden-crowned kinglet.'
