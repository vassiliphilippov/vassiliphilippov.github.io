Synset Examples

Reason

The verb sense of 'moot' meaning 'to think about carefully; weigh' is rare, archaic, and not commonly used in modern English. Its primary verb meaning is 'to raise (a question or topic) for discussion', while its adjective sense is more common. The example sentences given are not generally associated with how 'moot' is used as a verb today. Therefore, it should not be included as an independent entry for this sense in a general English-English dictionary.
