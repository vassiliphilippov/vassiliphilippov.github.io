bulky food like grass or hay for browsing or grazing horses or cattle

Reason

This sense of 'grass' as 'bulky food like grass or hay for browsing or grazing horses or cattle' is too general and not specific to the word 'grass' itself; it refers to the general concept of fodder or forage, not a dictionary sense unique to 'grass'. In modern English, 'grass' is primarily used for the plant, not as a synonym for 'forage' or 'feed'. Therefore, it should be excluded.
