Synset POS: r
Internal ID: inside|oewn-00104786-r
in reality

Synset Examples

she is very kind at heart

Reason

This sense uses 'inside' in a very metaphorical or idiomatic way, but the usage as 'in reality' is not a primary, independent adverbial sense broadly recognized in standard dictionaries. Dictionaries commonly include 'at heart' or 'inwardly' for this sense, but not 'inside' used adverbially to mean 'in reality'. Therefore, this sense is too marginal and does not meet the inclusion criteria for a standard dictionary entry.
