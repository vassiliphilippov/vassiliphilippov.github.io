flack

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02718623-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: flack|oewn-02718623-n
artillery designed to shoot upward at airplanes

Reason

The synset describes a very general and technical sense of 'flack' as anti-aircraft artillery. However, in standard English usage and reputable dictionaries, the common term for this meaning is 'flak' (without the 'c'). The spelling 'flack' for this sense is incorrect and extremely rare; 'flak' is the established spelling for antiaircraft fire. Including 'flack' with this sense would be misleading and nonstandard.
