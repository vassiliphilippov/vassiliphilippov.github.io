El Beda

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09009632-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: El Beda|oewn-09009632-n
the capital and chief port of Qatar

Reason

The synset describes El Beda as 'the capital and chief port of Qatar,' but this is factually incorrect. The capital and chief port of Qatar is Doha, not 'El Beda.' Furthermore, 'El Beda' is not a commonly recognized or current English name for any city in Qatar and would not appear as a headword in major English dictionaries. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
