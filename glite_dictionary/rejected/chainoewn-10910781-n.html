Reason

The sense refers to Ernst Boris Chain as a person, but the synset is simply 'person' or a generic proper noun for an individual. Standard English-English dictionaries do not include biographical entries unless the person is of major world-historical significance (and even then, only under specialized biographical dictionaries). Furthermore, the entry would be for 'Ernst Chain' rather than 'Chain'. Thus, this concept should not be included.
