flip the sign

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: flip the sign|extra.016368
to change the sign from closed to open

Synset Examples

They had to flip the sign to show they were open.

Reason

Although 'flip the sign' is a natural phrase, it is not an established idiom or set phrase with a non-literal meaning. The phrase describes a very literal physical action (turning a sign from 'closed' to 'open'), and would not be considered an independent dictionary entry in Oxford or similar dictionaries. It is compositional and does not meet the criteria for inclusion.
