box

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14432355-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: box|oewn-14432355-n
a predicament from which a skillful or graceful escape is impossible

Synset Examples

his lying got him into a tight corner

Reason

This sense of 'box' as a predicament is metaphorical and rare in contemporary English. It does not appear as an established usage in major dictionaries like Oxford or Merriam-Webster under this meaning; instead, terms like 'corner', 'tight spot', or 'predicament' are commonly used for this sense. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate dictionary entry.
