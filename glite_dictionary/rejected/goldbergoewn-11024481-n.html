Goldberg

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11024481-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Goldberg|oewn-11024481-n
United States cartoonist who drew intricate diagrams of very complicated and impractical contraptions that accomplished little or nothing (1883-1970)

Reason

The synset is connected with 'person' in the sense of a biographical proper noun. Dictionaries like Oxford do not include biographical entries unless the name has developed a common noun or idiomatic use (as with 'Rube Goldberg machine'). Goldberg as a surname is too general for a dictionary; only if 'Goldberg' were widely used as a non-proper noun (which it is not in this sense) would it be included.
