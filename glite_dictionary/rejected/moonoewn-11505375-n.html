Moon

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11505375-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Moon|oewn-11505375-n
the light of the Moon

Synset Examples

moonlight is the smuggler's enemy
the Moon was bright enough to read by

Reason

This sense refers to the light from the Moon, but the standard dictionary entry for 'Moon' covers the celestial body, and the term for moonlight as the light from the Moon is usually 'moonlight,' not 'Moon.' The synset is too specific and would not appear as a subsense of 'Moon' in an Oxford-style dictionary; instead, 'moonlight' as a headword has this meaning.
