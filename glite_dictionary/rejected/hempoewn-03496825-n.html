hemp

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03496825-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: hemp|oewn-03496825-n
a rope that is used by a hangman to execute persons who have been condemned to death by hanging

Reason

This sense describes a specific use of the material 'hemp'—namely, as a rope used in executions by hanging—but refers generically to 'a rope used for hanging' rather than a distinct lexical sense of 'hemp' itself. Modern dictionaries do not list 'hemp' as a direct synonym for 'a hangman's rope'; instead, the primary senses of 'hemp' concern the plant or its fiber. This is too context-dependent and not a current or widely recognized use.
