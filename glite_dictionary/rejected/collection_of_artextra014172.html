collection of art

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a

a group of artworks from various origins or themes

Synset Examples

Her collection of Asian art reflects her Asiaphile interests.
Her collection of Barbie dolls is impressive and vast.
She has a collection of history books on ancient civilizations.

Reason

The concept 'collection of art' is too general and refers to any group of works of art, but the headword 'collection of art' is descriptive and not a standalone dictionary headword in standard dictionaries like Oxford. Such noun phrases are not typically included unless they have a specialized, established meaning or are used as set phrases (e.g., 'art collection').
