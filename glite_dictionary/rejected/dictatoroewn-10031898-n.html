dictator

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10031898-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: dictator|oewn-10031898-n
a speaker who dictates to a secretary or a recording machine

Reason

Although 'dictator' has the sense of a speaker who dictates to a secretary or a recording machine, this usage is extremely rare and outdated. The overwhelming primary meaning in modern English is a ruler with absolute power. As such, this sense would not appear as a separate headword in a standard dictionary like Oxford, and would not be helpful to learners.
