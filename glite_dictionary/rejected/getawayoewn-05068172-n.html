getaway

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05068172-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: getaway|oewn-05068172-n
the attribute of being capable of rapid acceleration

Synset Examples

his car has a lot of pickup

Reason

This sense of 'getaway' as an abstract attribute meaning rapid acceleration (i.e., equivalent to 'pickup') is not standard or widely attested in the English language, and does not correspond to a recognized noun sense of 'getaway'. In dictionaries, 'getaway' as a noun refers to an escape or a short vacation, not a property of cars. Therefore, it should not be included in the dictionary for this sense.
