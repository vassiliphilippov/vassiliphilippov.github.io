interactive

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00626030-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: interactive|oewn-00626030-a
used especially of drugs or muscles that work together so the total effect is greater than the sum of the two (or more)

Reason

This sense of 'interactive', referring specifically to the combined effect of drugs or muscles, is highly specialized and overlaps with the term 'synergistic', which is the standard term for this phenomenon. 'Interactive' in general English is not typically defined this way in major dictionaries, so including this specific sense would not be standard dictionary practice.
