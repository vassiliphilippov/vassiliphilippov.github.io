terrestrial Siberian squirrel

Reason

The word 'burunduki' is a zoological term for the Siberian ground squirrel, but is a direct borrowing from Russian and is not established or widely used in the English language. It does not appear in standard English dictionaries like Oxford, and references to it in English typically use 'Siberian chipmunk' or 'Siberian ground squirrel.' Therefore, it does not meet the inclusion criteria for an English-English dictionary.
