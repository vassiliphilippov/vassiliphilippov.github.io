Reason

This sense of 'fret' as a verb meaning 'to remove soil or rock' is extremely rare and largely technical, with 'erode' being the standard term. It is not a widely recognized meaning and would not typically be found as a distinct entry in a general English dictionary like Oxford. The primary and familiar senses of 'fret' relate to worrying or to the metal bars on a guitar neck, not geological erosion.
