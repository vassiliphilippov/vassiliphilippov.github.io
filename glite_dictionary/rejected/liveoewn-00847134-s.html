Reason

The synset here refers to an attributive use of 'live' in the sense of 'elastic; rebounds readily,' which is rare and more commonly expressed by other adjectives such as 'bouncy,' 'springy,' or 'resilient.' This particular sense of 'live' is not in widespread modem usage as an adjective meaning 'elastic' and would not typically warrant a dedicated dictionary entry in general-purpose dictionaries like Oxford. Therefore, it should be excluded.
