24-hour interval

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15180180-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: 24-hour interval|oewn-15180180-n
time for Earth to make a complete rotation on its axis

Synset Examples

two days later they left
they put on two performances every day
there are 30,000 passengers per day

Reason

The concept '24-hour interval' in this sense is simply an overly technical or descriptive way of referring to a 'day,' which is the standard dictionary headword. Dictionaries do not include these scientific-sounding rephrasings as separate entries when the common word covers the concept entirely.
