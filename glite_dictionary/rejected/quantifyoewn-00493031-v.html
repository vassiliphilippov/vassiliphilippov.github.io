quantify

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00493031-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: quantify|oewn-00493031-v
use as a quantifier

Reason

This concept refers to the technical act of functioning as a grammatical quantifier (as in 'few', 'many', 'some'), not the main dictionary sense of 'quantify' (to measure or express as a quantity). The use of 'quantify' as a verb meaning 'to use as a quantifier' is not standard English usage and would not appear in the Oxford Dictionary. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
