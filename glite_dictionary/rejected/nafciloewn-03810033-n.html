Nafcil

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03810033-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Nafcil|oewn-03810033-n
a penicillinase-resistant form of penicillin (trade name Nafcil) used (usually in the form of its sodium salt) to treat infections caused by penicillin-resistant strains of staphylococci

Reason

The concept 'Nafcil' is a brand/trade name for a specific pharmaceutical drug (nafcillin). Oxford-style dictionaries generally do not include trade names unless they have become generic terms in English. In this case, 'Nafcil' refers directly to a trade name of nafcillin and is not in general use as a common noun. Therefore, it should be excluded.
