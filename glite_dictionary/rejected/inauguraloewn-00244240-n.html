inaugural

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: inaugural|oewn-00244240-n
the ceremonial induction into a position

Synset Examples

the new president obviously enjoyed his inauguration

Reason

The noun sense of 'inaugural' as 'the ceremonial induction into a position' is not standard; the correct noun is 'inauguration.' 'Inaugural' is used as an adjective. This sense would not appear in a major dictionary as a standalone entry for 'inaugural' (noun).
