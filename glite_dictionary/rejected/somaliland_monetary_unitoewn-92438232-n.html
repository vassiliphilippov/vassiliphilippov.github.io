Somaliland monetary unit

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-92438232-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Somaliland monetary unit|oewn-92438232-n
monetary unit in Somaliland, a self-declared independent state that is internationally recognized as an autonomous region of Somalia.

Reason

The concept 'Somaliland monetary unit' is overly general and refers to any monetary unit used in Somaliland, not a specific term that would appear as a dictionary headword. The actual currency name, such as 'Somaliland shilling,' would be appropriate, but 'Somaliland monetary unit' is too broad and not a standalone lexical entry.
