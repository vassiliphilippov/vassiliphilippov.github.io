Reason

The sense 'place' for 'establish' is too general and not directly related to how 'establish' is typically used in standard English. This usage, as in 'set her up at the Ritz', actually corresponds more closely to 'set up' or 'put (someone) up (somewhere)' rather than 'establish'. Including this sense for 'establish' would not match dictionary expectations. Therefore, it should be excluded.
