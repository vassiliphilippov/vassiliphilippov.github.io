WordNet ID: n/a

a person imitating Trump in a lesser way

Synset Examples

Reason

The concept 'mini-Trump wannabe' is a highly informal, ad hoc phrase referring specifically to a person imitating Donald Trump in a minor way. It is not a fixed phrase or compound recognized in major dictionaries like Oxford, nor is it widely established or general enough to warrant its own dictionary entry. It is also too specific to contemporary events and individuals. Thus, it should not be included in the dictionary.
