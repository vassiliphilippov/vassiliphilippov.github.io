Reason

The synset 'something that shines (with emitted or reflected light)' is too general to include as a dictionary entry for 'shiner.' In standard dictionaries, 'shiner' refers to a black eye or sometimes to specific types of fish, but not in this overly broad, generic sense. Therefore, it should be excluded.
