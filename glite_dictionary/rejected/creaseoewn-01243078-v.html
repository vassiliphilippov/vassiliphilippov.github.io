crease

Synset POS: v
Internal ID: crease|oewn-01243078-v
scrape gently

Synset Examples

graze the skin

Reason

The sense 'crease' as 'scrape gently' is not established in contemporary English usage and is not supported by major dictionaries. In this sense, the canonical verb is 'graze.' The word 'crease' as a verb is commonly used to mean 'to make a line or mark by folding,' not 'to scrape gently.' Therefore, this sense should not be included in the dictionary.
