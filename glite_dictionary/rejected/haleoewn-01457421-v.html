Reason

The verb sense of 'hale' meaning 'draw slowly or heavily' is archaic and almost entirely replaced by 'haul.' It is very rare in modern usage and would not appear in a current Oxford-style dictionary except in historical or highly specialized contexts. Therefore, it is excluded.
