Reason

The phrase 'faggot up' is archaic and almost entirely obsolete in modern English, appearing only in rare technical or historical contexts. It derives from 'faggot' meaning a bundle of sticks, and the synset sense is specific to binding or tying up as in a faggot. This sense and the verb form are not commonly used or recognized today, and would not appear as a headword or normal subentry in major modern dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary. Additionally, the word 'faggot' has strong modern associations as a slur, which further discourages any use. Therefore, the concept should be excluded.
