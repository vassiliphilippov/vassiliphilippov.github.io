doppelzentner

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13747381-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: doppelzentner|oewn-13747381-n
a unit of weight equal to 100 kilograms in the metric system

Reason

The term 'doppelzentner' is a German loanword for a metric weight unit (100 kilograms) and is not commonly used or widely recognized in English. It would not typically appear in an Oxford-style English-English dictionary. The English equivalent ('quintal' or 'metric quintal') is far more likely to appear.
