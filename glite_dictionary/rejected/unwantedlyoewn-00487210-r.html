unwantedly

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00487210-r
Synset POS: r
Internal ID: unwantedly|oewn-00487210-r
in an undesirable manner

Synset Examples

he was unwantedly friendly

Reason

The term 'unwantedly' is extremely rare and not standard English; it does not appear as an entry in major English dictionaries, and its usage is covered by the much more common and correct term 'undesirably'. Therefore, it should be excluded from a standard English dictionary.
