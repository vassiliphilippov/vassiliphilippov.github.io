be proud of

Synset Examples

He prides himself on making it into law school

Reason

The sense 'plume' meaning 'to be proud of' is archaic and rarely used in contemporary English. Modern speakers almost never use 'plume' as a verb in this sense, and it would not generally be found as an entry in a current dictionary except as a historical note. It is therefore excluded from a standard English-English dictionary aimed at current usage.
