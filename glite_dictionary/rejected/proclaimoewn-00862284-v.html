Reason

The synset in question is for 'proclaim' with the sense 'praise, glorify, or honor,' which does not match common or standard usage of 'proclaim.' In standard dictionaries, 'proclaim' primarily means to announce or declare publicly, not to praise or glorify. Therefore, this synset is too general or misplaced for the headword and should not be included.
