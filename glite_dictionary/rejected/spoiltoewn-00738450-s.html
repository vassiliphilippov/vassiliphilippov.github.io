spoilt

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00738450-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: spoilt|oewn-00738450-s
affected by blight; anything that mars or prevents growth or prosperity

Synset Examples

a blighted rose
blighted urban districts

Reason

The sense of 'spoilt' given here ('affected by blight; anything that mars or prevents growth or prosperity') is a highly specialized and rare usage, and the adjective 'spoilt' in English is overwhelmingly used to mean 'ruined' or 'overindulged.' The synset applies to 'blighted,' which is the standard adjective for this sense, not 'spoilt.' Therefore, this concept is too general or not genuinely associated with 'spoilt' as it would appear in an English dictionary.
