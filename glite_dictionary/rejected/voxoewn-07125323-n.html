vox

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07125323-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: vox|oewn-07125323-n
the sound made by the vibration of vocal folds modified by the resonance of the vocal tract

Synset Examples

a singer takes good care of his voice
the giraffe cannot make any vocalizations

Reason

The headword 'vox' is not commonly used in English with this sense; the standard English term is 'voice.' 'Vox' is primarily found in Latin phrases or as a component in terms like 'vox populi' and does not appear as a standalone entry for 'the sound made by vocal cords' in major English dictionaries. Therefore, it should not be included as an independent entry.
