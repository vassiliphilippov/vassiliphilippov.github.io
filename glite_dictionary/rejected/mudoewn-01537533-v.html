soil with mud, muck, or mire

Synset Examples

The child mucked up his shirt while playing ball in the garden

Reason

The verb sense of 'mud' meaning 'to soil with mud, muck, or mire' is extremely rare and not standard or widely attested; this is generally covered by 'muck' or 'dirty.' The word 'mud' is overwhelmingly used as a noun, and contemporary dictionaries do not include 'mud' as a verb in this sense. Therefore, this concept should not be included.
