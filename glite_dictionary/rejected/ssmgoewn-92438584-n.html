SSMG

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-92438584-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: SSMG|oewn-92438584-n
a one-time benefit payable for each child expected, born, or adopted.

Reason

SSMG is an acronym for a specific government benefit scheme (Sure Start Maternity Grant) in the UK. As an acronym for a specific program, and not a general English word or commonly used term, it is too specialized and context-specific to be included in a general English-English dictionary like Oxford. It would more appropriately belong in a glossary of UK government programs or maternity benefits, not in a standard dictionary.
