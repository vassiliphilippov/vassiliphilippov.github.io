Reason

'Alexander Pope' as used here refers to a specific individual and its synset is simply 'person', which is too general. Proper names of individuals are not typically included as headwords in general English dictionaries like Oxford unless they have entered the language as eponyms (which is not the case here). Therefore, this concept should not be included.
