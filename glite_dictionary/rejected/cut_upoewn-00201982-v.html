cut up

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00201982-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: cut up|oewn-00201982-v
significantly cut up a manuscript

Reason

This sense is highly specific and refers to the literal act of physically cutting up a manuscript, which is transparently derived from the meanings of 'cut' and 'up'. It does not represent an idiomatic or commonly distinct phrasal verb meaning beyond the obvious literal sense, and would not normally have a separate dictionary entry in sources like Oxford.
