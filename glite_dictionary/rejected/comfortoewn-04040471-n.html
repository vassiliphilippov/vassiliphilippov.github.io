Synset POS: n
Internal ID: comfort|oewn-04040471-n
bedding made of two layers of cloth filled with stuffing and stitched together

Reason

This concept represents a rare and very regionally limited usage of 'comfort' as a noun meaning a quilt or comforter (bedding). In standard English dictionaries, especially major ones like Oxford, this meaning does not generally appear; the standard term for this item is 'comforter'. Therefore, including 'comfort' with this sense would be misleading, as it is not a widely recognized or standard usage.
