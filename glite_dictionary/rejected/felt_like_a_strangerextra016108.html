felt like a stranger

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: felt like a stranger|extra.016108
to feel out of place or unfamiliar

Synset Examples

He felt like a stranger in his own hometown.

Reason

The phrase 'felt like a stranger' is a compositional, transparent phrase rather than a set idiom or compound; it is simply the verb 'felt' plus the simile 'like a stranger'. This phrase does not warrant an independent dictionary entry in a major dictionary such as Oxford, as it follows normal rules of English and has no special idiomatic or nonliteral meaning.
