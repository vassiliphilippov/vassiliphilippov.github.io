Transportation

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Transportation|oewn-08160653-n
the United States federal department that institutes and coordinates national transportation programs; created in 1966

Reason

The concept refers to 'Transportation' as the U.S. federal department, which is too general and not specifically linked to the proper noun 'United States Department of Transportation.' Dictionaries would include the official name (e.g., 'Department of Transportation'), not the general term 'transportation' defined as a department. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
