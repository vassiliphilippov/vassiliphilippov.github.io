capital of Florida

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09097675-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capital of Florida|oewn-09097675-n
capital of the state of Florida; located in northern Florida

Reason

The synset 'capital of Florida' refers to a specific place but the headword is not the name of the city itself (Tallahassee). Such constructions referring generically to the capital of specific states or countries are too general and do not warrant their own dictionary entry. Only actual place names (like 'Tallahassee') would appear as headwords in a general English dictionary.
