Reason

The synset refers to the sense of 'riff' meaning 'look through written material', but this usage is not commonly attested nor recognized as standard in English dictionaries such as Oxford Dictionary. The examples provided ('thumb through', 'leaf through') do not reflect standard or widespread usage of 'riff' in this sense. Typically, 'riff' as a verb refers to improvisation, especially in music or conversation. Therefore, this sense should not be included.
