capital of California

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09087443-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capital of California|oewn-09087443-n
a city in north central California 75 miles to the northeast of San Francisco on the Sacramento River; capital of California

Reason

The synset is related to a capital city (the place, i.e., the city itself), but the headword "capital of California" is a descriptive phrase rather than the actual city name ("Sacramento"). Dictionaries like Oxford would include the city name "Sacramento" but not the phrase "capital of California" as a headword. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
