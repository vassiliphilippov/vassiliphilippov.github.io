Book

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06443410-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Book|oewn-06443410-n
the sacred writings of the Christian religions

Synset Examples

he went to carry the Word to the heathen

Reason

This sense refers to 'Book' as in 'the Bible' or sacred Christian scriptures, but the synset is too general and not distinct from the main meaning of 'book' as a noun. In major dictionaries, 'Book' as a capitalized term specifically referring to sacred writings is typically subsumed under entries like 'the Book' or more directly as 'Bible' or 'Scripture.' Therefore, this sense is too specialized and not lexicalized to warrant a separate entry for 'Book' with this definition.
