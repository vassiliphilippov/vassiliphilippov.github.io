Reason

The synset for 'James' in this case is a specific historical person, namely James II of England, whose primary synset is 'person.' Proper names of historical figures whose dictionary sense is simply defined as being a person are not included as dictionary entries in standard English dictionaries like Oxford. Such encyclopedic entries belong in an encyclopedia rather than a dictionary.
