spic

completely neat and clean

Synset Examples

the apartment was immaculate

a spick-and-span kitchen

Reason

This concept, 'spic' as an adjective meaning 'completely neat and clean', is not actually used in standard English as an independent word with this meaning. The sense exists only as part of the compound 'spick-and-span', not as a standalone adjective. Including 'spic' in this sense would be misleading, and it does not appear as such in Oxford or other major dictionaries.
