a genus of dicotyledonous plants

Reason

'Rosid dicot genus' is a scientific taxonomic term describing a classification level (genus) within 'rosid dicotyledons.' It is too general, referring not to a specific genus name but to any genus in this group. Such meta-taxonomic labels do not appear as dictionary headwords in general dictionaries like Oxford; they are placeholders used in classification systems, not standalone dictionary entries.
