Reason

The given synset describes 'pipeline' as 'gossip spread by spoken communication,' but this is not a standard or widely recognized meaning for 'pipeline' in English. In reputable dictionaries, 'pipeline' does not mean gossip or spoken communication; it usually refers to a channel for conveying something physically (like oil or gas) or metaphorically (like an organizational process or flow of information, not specifically gossip). The example given corresponds to 'word of mouth,' not 'pipeline.' Therefore, this sense does not warrant an entry.
