atomic physics

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06110162-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: atomic physics|oewn-06110162-n
the branch of physics that studies the internal structure of atomic nuclei

Reason

The definition provided describes 'atomic physics' as studying atomic nuclei, but the accepted scientific use is that 'atomic physics' deals with atoms and their structure (not specifically nuclei, which is 'nuclear physics'). Despite this, 'atomic physics' is a technical term that would appear in dictionaries, but this particular sense is factually inaccurate. Therefore, this sense should not be included as defined.
