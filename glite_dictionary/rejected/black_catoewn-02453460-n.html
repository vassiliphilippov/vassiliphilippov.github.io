Synset POS: n
Internal ID: black cat|oewn-02453460-n
large dark brown North American arboreal carnivorous mammal

Reason

This sense of 'black cat' refers to an animal that is actually the fisher (Martes pennanti), a North American mammal, and not a common or standard meaning of 'black cat' in English. The normal use of 'black cat' is to denote a domestic cat with black fur, not a 'large dark brown North American arboreal carnivorous mammal.' This synset is unrelated to the headword as it would appear in a general dictionary and should be excluded.
