deriving

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06756849-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: deriving|oewn-06756849-n
(historical linguistics) an explanation of the historical origins of a word or phrase

Reason

The sense described refers to 'deriving' as a noun in the context of historical linguistics, specifically as an explanation of the origins of a word or phrase. However, this nominal use ('a deriving') is not attested or standard in English; the term 'derivation' or 'etymology' is used instead. 'Deriving' as a noun in this sense would not appear in the Oxford Dictionary and is not standard English usage. Therefore, it should not be included.
