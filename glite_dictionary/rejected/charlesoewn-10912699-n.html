Charles

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10912699-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Charles|oewn-10912699-n
as Charles II he was Holy Roman Emperor and as Charles I he was king of France (823-877)

Reason

The concept corresponds to the personal name 'Charles' used in specific historical contexts (as Charles II, Holy Roman Emperor and Charles I, king of France), but the synset is overly general and simply denotes a person with the name Charles. This usage does not require a separate dictionary entry beyond the headword 'Charles' itself, which is treated as a proper noun for individuals. Oxford-style dictionaries do not include separate entries for each historical figure under just their first name in this way; those are covered in encyclopedias. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
