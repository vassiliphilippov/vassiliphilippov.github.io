antifaggot remark

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: antifaggot remark|extra.012257
derogatory comments against LGBTQ+ individuals

Synset Examples

His antifaggot remarks drew immediate backlash.

Reason

The term 'antifaggot remark' is not a standard lexical entry and is formed by combining a highly offensive slur with the prefix 'anti-'. Major dictionaries do not include this as a headword. Instead, coverage would be given to the base slur and to terms like 'homophobic remark'. Including this as a headword is inappropriate due to its extremely derogatory and non-standard nature.
