the quantity that a watering can will hold.

Reason

This concept refers to a specific measure relating to the capacity of a watering can, rather than a commonly-used dictionary sense of the term. Standard dictionaries do not include the meaning 'the quantity that a watering can will hold' as a separate entry. This is analogous to measurements like 'a cup' meaning the actual capacity, which is only included if well-established as a measurement unit (e.g., 'cup' as a measurement), not as 'watering can.' Therefore, this sense is too specific and uncommon for dictionary inclusion.
