Source: WordNet

Synset POS: s
Internal ID: bespoken|oewn-00159375-s
pledged to be married

Synset Examples

the engaged couple

Reason

The term 'bespoken' meaning 'pledged to be married' is archaic and essentially obsolete in modern English. It does not appear in current reputable dictionaries with this meaning and is no longer in common or even occasional usage. The modern term is 'engaged.' Therefore, it should not be included as a standalone entry.
