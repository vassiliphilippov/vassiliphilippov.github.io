both of theirs

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: both of theirs|extra.001279
shared ownership by two people

Synset Examples

That house is both of theirs, not just one.
They claimed that the idea was both of theirs.

Reason

The phrase 'both of theirs' is a transparent construction using established pronouns ('both' + 'of theirs') and is not an established fixed phrase or idiom. It does not warrant a separate dictionary entry, as its meaning is compositional and would not appear in standard dictionaries like the Oxford Dictionary.
