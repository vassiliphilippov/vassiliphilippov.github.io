WordNet ID: oewn-00638550-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: establish|oewn-00638550-v
use as a basis for; found on

Synset Examples

base a claim on some observation

Reason

The synset definition 'use as a basis for; found on' refers specifically to the sense of 'base' rather than 'establish' as commonly understood or used. 'Establish' in the sense of 'to set up' or 'to found' is core and should be included, but this narrow sense (as in 'to base something on') does not genuinely apply to 'establish' as it would be listed in a general English dictionary. Therefore, this particular concept should be excluded.
