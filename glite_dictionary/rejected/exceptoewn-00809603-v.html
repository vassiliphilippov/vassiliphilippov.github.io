Synset POS: v
Internal ID: except|oewn-00809603-v
object to

Synset Examples

he demurred at my suggestion to work on Saturday

Reason

This sense of 'except' as a verb meaning 'to object to' is archaic and extremely rare in modern usage; it does not appear in contemporary dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary under this sense. The meaning is better covered by words such as 'demur' or 'object'. Including it would not benefit learners and may cause confusion.
