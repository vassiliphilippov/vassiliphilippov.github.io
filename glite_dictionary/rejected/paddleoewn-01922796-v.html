paddle

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01922796-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: paddle|oewn-01922796-v
walk unsteadily, with short steps

Synset Examples

small children toddle

Reason

This sense of 'paddle' as a verb meaning 'to walk unsteadily, with short steps' is not a standard current English usage. The primary and widely accepted meanings of 'paddle' relate to moving a boat with a paddle, or splashing in water, but not to walking unsteadily. This sense is better represented by the verb 'toddle'. Therefore, it should not be included as a separate entry in a general English-English dictionary.
