Reason

'call 'em' is an informal contraction of 'call them', not an independent lexeme or phrase that would warrant its own dictionary entry. Dictionaries typically do not include every possible contraction or colloquial reduction of common phrases unless it forms an idiomatic or set expression with special meaning beyond its literal components. This is not the case here.
