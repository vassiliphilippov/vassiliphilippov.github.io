Reason

Although 'spud' is a real English word primarily meaning 'potato' (noun), this verb sense ('produce buds, branches, or germinate') is virtually never used in contemporary or even historical English. It is obscure and not attested in reputable dictionaries. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
