complot

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00708804-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: complot|oewn-00708804-v
engage in plotting or enter into a conspiracy, swear together

Synset Examples

They conspired to overthrow the government

Reason

The verb 'complot' is archaic and extremely rare in modern English; typical dictionaries list 'conspire' and 'plot' for this sense. The word 'complot' does not appear as a standard entry in contemporary English dictionaries and would not be expected by learners. Therefore, it should not be included.
