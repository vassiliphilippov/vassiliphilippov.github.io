Reason

The sense described for 'conjure'—meaning 'to engage in plotting or enter into a conspiracy'—is archaic or extremely rare in contemporary English. The modern and widely understood meanings of 'conjure' relate to magic, illusions, or calling something to mind, not plotting or conspiracy. As such, this sense is not relevant for dictionary users and would not appear as a primary sense in reputable modern dictionaries.
