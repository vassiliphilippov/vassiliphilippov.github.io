revelatory

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01888391-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: revelatory|oewn-01888391-s
prophetic of devastation or ultimate doom

Reason

This sense of 'revelatory'—meaning 'prophetic of devastation or ultimate doom'—is too narrow and is not in common use in modern English. The primary and dictionary-worthy sense of 'revelatory' is 'revealing something not previously known.' The 'prophetic of doom' sense is rare and is tied specifically to theological or apocalyptic contexts, not general vocabulary. Therefore, this concept does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
