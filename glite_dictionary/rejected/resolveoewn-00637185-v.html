Synset POS: v

understand the meaning of

Synset Examples

The question concerning the meaning of life cannot be answered

Reason

This sense of 'resolve' (meaning 'understand the meaning of') is not a standard usage in contemporary English and is not found in Oxford-type dictionaries for this sense. The usual verb for this meaning is 'resolve' as in 'to settle' or 'find a solution to', but not specifically 'to understand the meaning of.' Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
