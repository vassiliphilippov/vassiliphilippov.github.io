Internal ID: prophesy|oewn-00829664-v
deliver a sermon

Synset Examples

The minister is not preaching this Sunday

Reason

The synset meaning 'deliver a sermon' corresponds to the word 'preach', not 'prophesy'. The headword 'prophesy' is not commonly used in the sense of 'delivering a sermon' in modern English. Therefore, this sense should not be included as a dictionary entry for 'prophesy'.
