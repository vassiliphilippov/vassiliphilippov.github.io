articulary

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02653871-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: articulary|oewn-02653871-a
relating to or affecting the joints of the body

Synset Examples

the articular surfaces of bones
articular disease

Reason

The concept 'articulary' refers to the same meaning as 'articular', which is the established and widely used standard adjective for 'relating to the joints of the body'. 'Articulary' is not in common use and does not appear in major English dictionaries, making its inclusion unnecessary and potentially confusing. The correct entry should be for 'articular', not 'articulary'.
