decade

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13768652-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: decade|oewn-13768652-n
the cardinal number that is the sum of nine and one; the base of the decimal system

Reason

This sense of 'decade' refers strictly to the number 10 as a cardinal number, which is already more accurately represented by the word 'ten.' 'Decade' in English is not commonly used to mean the number itself; instead, it refers to a period of ten years. The synset is too general and not specific to the ordinary usage of 'decade.' Therefore, it should be excluded.
