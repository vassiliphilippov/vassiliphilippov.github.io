Reason

The given concept 'unwritten' in the sense of 'using speech rather than writing' is too general and refers to the property of being oral (spoken, not written), which is more directly expressed by the adjective 'oral.' In dictionary practice, this sense is not a common or dictionary-attested primary meaning of 'unwritten.' 'Unwritten' typically means 'not written down,' 'not expressed in writing,' or 'not formally recorded,' but not specifically 'using speech rather than writing.' Therefore, this sense should be excluded as it is not a standard or recognized sense for entry.
