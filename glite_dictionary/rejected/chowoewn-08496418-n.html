Chow

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08496418-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Chow|oewn-08496418-n
the imperial dynasty of China from 1122 to 221 BC; notable for the rise of Confucianism and Taoism

Reason

Exclude this concept as it refers to 'Chow' as a general term for a historical dynasty (more properly rendered as 'Zhou' in modern English transliteration), and not as a distinct English-language word or phrase relating specifically to that dynasty. In modern dictionaries, 'Chow' in this sense is usually not included; the recognized form for the dynasty is 'Zhou'.
