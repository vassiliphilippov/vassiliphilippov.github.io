sweet

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01077395-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: sweet|oewn-01077395-a
not containing or composed of salt water

Synset Examples

fresh water

Reason

This concept uses 'sweet' in a dated or technical sense to mean 'fresh' (not salt) water, but in contemporary general English, 'sweet' as 'not salty' is no longer a standard word sense and would not have a dedicated headword in modern dictionaries. 'Fresh water' is the modern and appropriate term. Therefore, this sense should be excluded.
