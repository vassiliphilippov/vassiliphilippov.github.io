dun

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01806967-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: dun|oewn-01806967-v
treat cruelly

Synset Examples

The children tormented the stuttering teacher

Reason

The verb 'dun' meaning 'to treat cruelly' is archaic and not in current standard usage; its standard and well-known usage is 'to demand payment of a debt.' This sense is not relevant for modern dictionary users and would not be included in current dictionaries.
