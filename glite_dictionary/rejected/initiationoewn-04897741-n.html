initiation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04897741-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: initiation|oewn-04897741-n
wisdom as evidenced by the possession of knowledge

Synset Examples

his knowledgeability impressed me
his dullness was due to lack of initiation

Reason

The sense describing 'initiation' as 'wisdom as evidenced by the possession of knowledge' is not a recognized or conventional usage in modern or standard English; it is not supported by widely accepted dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The primary senses of 'initiation' relate to the process of beginning something or the formal admission or introduction into a group, not general wisdom or knowledgeability. Therefore, this sense is too obscure and not appropriate for inclusion as a headword definition.
