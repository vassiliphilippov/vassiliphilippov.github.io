Reason

The synset is a general geographical place (a specific town), and while 'Butte' as a geographical feature is dictionary-worthy, a town called 'Butte' is a proper noun and would appear in an encyclopedia rather than a general English dictionary. Excluded as per policy for place names with synset 'place' or 'organization'.
