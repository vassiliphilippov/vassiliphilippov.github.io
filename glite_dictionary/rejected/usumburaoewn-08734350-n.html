Usumbura

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08734350-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Usumbura|oewn-08734350-n
the capital and largest city of Burundi

Synset Examples

Usumbura was renamed Bujumbura when Burundi became independent in 1962

Reason

Usumbura is an outdated name for the current capital of Burundi, now called Bujumbura. Modern English dictionaries generally include only the current place names, and this form would not be kept as a main headword unless for historical contexts. It is not a general synset like 'city' or 'person,' but it is an obsolete place name and thus excluded as a standalone entry.
