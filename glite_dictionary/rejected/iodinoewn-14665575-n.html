iodin

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14665575-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: iodin|oewn-14665575-n
a nonmetallic element belonging to the halogens; used especially in medicine and photography and in dyes; occurs naturally only in combination in small quantities (as in sea water or rocks)

Reason

The word 'iodin' is an obsolete or rare variant spelling of 'iodine.' The standard and widely recognized spelling in contemporary English is 'iodine,' which would be included in reputable dictionaries. 'iodin' does not meet the threshold for inclusion as a main headword.
