meeting house

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-11747827-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: meeting house|oewn-11747827-n
columbine of eastern North America having long-spurred red flowers

Reason

The synset for 'meeting house' in this sense refers to a type of columbine flower (Aquilegia), but in contemporary and standard usage, 'meeting house' refers to a building used for public gatherings, especially for religious services. The sense described here is obscure and not standard for the headword, so it should not be included as a separate dictionary entry for 'meeting house.'
