at a previous time

Synset Examples

at one time he loved her
her erstwhile writing

Reason

The term 'erst' as an adverb meaning 'at a previous time' is archaic and extremely rare in modern English. It does not appear as a standalone entry in major contemporary dictionaries (e.g., Oxford, Merriam-Webster) except possibly as part of 'erstwhile.' The concept is better covered under words like 'once,' 'formerly,' or as part of 'erstwhile.' Including 'erst' as a standalone adverb is not appropriate for a general dictionary.
