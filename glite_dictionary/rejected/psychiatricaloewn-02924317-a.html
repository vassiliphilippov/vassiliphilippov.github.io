psychiatrical

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02924317-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: psychiatrical|oewn-02924317-a
relating to or used in or engaged in the practice of psychiatry

Synset Examples

psychiatric disorder
psychiatric hospital

Reason

The standard and widely used adjective form is 'psychiatric', not 'psychiatrical'. 'Psychiatrical' is extremely rare and not found in major English dictionaries. Therefore, it should not be included as a headword.
