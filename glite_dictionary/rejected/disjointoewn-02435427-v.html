disjoint

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02435427-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: disjoint|oewn-02435427-v
part; cease or break association with

Synset Examples

She disassociated herself from the organization when she found out the identity of the president

Reason

The verb sense of 'disjoint' meaning 'to part; to cease or break association with' is extremely rare and not in standard current usage. Modern English dictionaries do not list 'disjoint' as a verb with this specific sense; instead, 'disassociate' or 'disengage' are used. Therefore, this sense should not be included as a main dictionary entry.
