arrange by concord or agreement

Synset Examples

Concord the conditions for the marriage of the Prince of Wales with a commoner

Reason

The use of 'concord' as a verb meaning 'to arrange by agreement' is extremely rare and archaic. This verb usage is virtually never encountered in contemporary or standard written English and would not appear as an entry in modern dictionaries such as Oxford. 'Concord' is primarily used as a noun.
