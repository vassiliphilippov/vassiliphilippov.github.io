Synset Examples

‘It doesn't matter,’ he supplied

Reason

This sense of 'append' as 'state or say further' is not standard or recognizable in contemporary English usage. The example provided ('he supplied') refers to a reporting verb, not directly to 'append'; 'append' is not commonly used with this meaning outside technical contexts (e.g., attaching text or data). Including this sense may confuse learners, as it is not attested in reputable dictionaries as meaning 'to say further.'
