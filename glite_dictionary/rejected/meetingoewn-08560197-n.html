meeting

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08560197-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: meeting|oewn-08560197-n
a place where things merge or flow together (especially rivers)

Synset Examples

Pittsburgh is located at the confluence of the Allegheny and Monongahela rivers

Reason

This sense of 'meeting' as 'a place where things merge or flow together' is highly specialized and rarely used in modern English for physical locations, especially in reference to rivers. The standard and more widely accepted term is 'confluence.' Therefore, this sense does not meet the inclusion criteria for a general English dictionary entry for 'meeting.'
