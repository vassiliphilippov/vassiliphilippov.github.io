subscriber

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10690107-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: subscriber|oewn-10690107-n
someone who expresses strong approval

Reason

The sense of 'subscriber' as 'someone who expresses strong approval' is not a recognized or standard meaning of the word and would not appear in a major dictionary like Oxford. The typical senses involve someone who pays to receive a service or periodical, not merely someone who approves of something. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
