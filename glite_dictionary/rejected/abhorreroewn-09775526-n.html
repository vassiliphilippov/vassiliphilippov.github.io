abhorrer

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: abhorrer|oewn-09775526-n
a signer of a 1679 address to Charles II in which those who petitioned for the reconvening of parliament were condemned and abhorred

Reason

The synset for 'abhorrer' in this sense is extremely narrow and refers only to historical signatories of a specific 1679 address. This is too specialized and would not be found as a main entry in a general English-English dictionary. The more general sense of 'abhorrer' as 'one who abhors' is already covered by standard entries. Therefore, this historical sense should be excluded.
