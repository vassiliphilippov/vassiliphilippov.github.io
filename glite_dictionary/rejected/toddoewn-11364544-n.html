Reason

The synset is a person ('Scottish chemist'), which is overly general. Dictionaries like Oxford list prominent individuals only if the meaning or reference goes beyond the individual or becomes idiomatic or a significant term. In this context, 'Todd' refers to a person (Alexander R. Todd), not a lexical item with independent meaning; thus, it should be excluded.
