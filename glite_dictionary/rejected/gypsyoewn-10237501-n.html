gypsy

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10237501-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: gypsy|oewn-10237501-n
a laborer who moves from place to place as demanded by employment

Synset Examples

itinerant traders

Reason

The synset describes a general occupation (a laborer who moves from place to place) and is not specific to the headword 'gypsy,' which is also widely recognized as an ethnic term that may be considered offensive. This sense does not reflect the actual common use of the word 'gypsy' as it would appear in modern dictionaries; including it would be misleading and potentially inappropriate.
