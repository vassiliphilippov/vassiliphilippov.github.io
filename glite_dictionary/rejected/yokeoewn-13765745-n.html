Synset POS: n
Internal ID: yoke|oewn-13765745-n
two items of the same kind

Reason

This sense of 'yoke' as 'two items of the same kind' is extremely rare and archaic; it does not appear in modern English dictionaries like Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The primary modern senses of 'yoke' refer to either the wooden frame for joining animals, figurative senses of restraint, or mechanical uses. Therefore, it should be excluded.
