annunciation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06738907-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: annunciation|oewn-06738907-n
a formal public statement

Synset Examples

the government made an announcement about changes in the drug war
a declaration of independence

Reason

The synset "a formal public statement" is too general for the headword "annunciation," which is rarely used in this sense; common usage relates to the Christian event (the Annunciation). For the meaning of general public statements, words like "announcement" or "declaration" are standard. Including this sense for "annunciation" would be misleading, so it should be excluded.
