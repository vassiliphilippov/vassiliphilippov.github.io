impulsive

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00812509-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: impulsive|oewn-00812509-s
having the power of driving or impelling

Synset Examples

a driving personal ambition
the driving force was his innate enthusiasm
an impulsive force

Reason

This synset of 'impulsive' refers to 'having the power of driving or impelling,' which is both highly technical and is not a primary, familiar sense of the word in general English. In this sense, it is typically covered by adjectives like 'driving' or 'impelling' and is not a standard meaning found in major dictionaries. The well-known sense of 'impulsive' is 'acting or done without forethought,' which is included in all dictionaries. Therefore, this particular technical sense should be excluded from the dictionary.
