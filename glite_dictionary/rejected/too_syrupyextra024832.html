too syrupy

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: too syrupy|extra.024832
excessively sweet or sugary

Synset Examples

The dessert was too syrupy for my taste.

Reason

The concept 'too syrupy' is a compositional and transparent phrase: it is 'too' (degree adverb) plus 'syrupy' (adjective). There is no idiomatic meaning. Such regular combinations do not merit their own dictionary entry in Oxford-style dictionaries.
