well-seen

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: well-seen|extra.011273
clearly noticeable or evident

Synset Examples

His contributions are well-seen in the final product.
The changes are well-seen across the community.
Her artwork is well-seen in the local gallery.

Reason

The term 'well-seen' is not a standard, widely recognized word in English and is rarely used in this sense. There are more common ways to express the idea ('clearly visible', 'evident', 'conspicuous'). This usage does not appear in major reputable dictionaries, so it should not be included as a headword.
