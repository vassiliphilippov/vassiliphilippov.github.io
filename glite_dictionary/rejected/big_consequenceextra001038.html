serious outcomes from actions

Synset Examples

A small misstep can lead to big consequences.
Their escalation of the conflict led to serious consequences.

Reason

The concept 'big consequence' refers to the general idea of serious outcomes from actions, but it is not a fixed compound or idiomatic expression. It is simply the adjective 'big' modifying the noun 'consequence', both of which have their own dictionary entries. Such adjective-noun combinations are too general and do not warrant a separate dictionary entry according to standard lexicographical practice.
