porc

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07684447-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: porc|oewn-07684447-n
meat from a domestic hog or pig

Reason

The word 'porc' is not standard English; the correct English term for meat from a pig is 'pork'. 'Porc' is the French word, so it should not be included as an English dictionary entry.
