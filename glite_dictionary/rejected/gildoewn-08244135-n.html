gild

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08244135-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: gild|oewn-08244135-n
a formal association of people with similar interests

Synset Examples

he joined a golf club
they formed a small lunch society
men from the fraternal order will staff the soup kitchen today

Reason

The synset refers to a general concept of 'association' or 'club,' which is too broad and not specifically related to the headword 'gild'. In modern English, 'gild' is either a historic variant spelling for 'guild' (organization of craftsmen or merchants) or a verb meaning 'to cover with gold'. The general sense of a formal association, as in 'society' or 'club', is not typically used or recognized under 'gild' in dictionaries. Therefore, this concept should not be included.
