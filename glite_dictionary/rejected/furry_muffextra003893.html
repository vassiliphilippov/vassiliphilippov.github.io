furry muff

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: furry muff|extra.003893
a vague or ambiguous response or a type of hand warmer or accessory

Synset Examples

In a furry muff, she avoided the controversial topic.
His response was a furry muff, leaving us guessing.

Reason

The concept 'furry muff' as described relates to either a vague/ambiguous response (which is not an established idiom or term in standard English) or a type of hand warmer/accessory (for which the correct and recognized headword is 'muff' and not 'furry muff'). The phrase 'furry muff' is not a standard dictionary entry in Oxford or other reputable dictionaries with the meaning of a vague response, and as a noun for an accessory, 'muff' already covers this. Therefore, it should not be included as a standalone entry.
