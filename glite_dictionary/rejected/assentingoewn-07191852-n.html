assenting

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07191852-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: assenting|oewn-07191852-n
agreeing with or consenting to (often unwillingly)

Synset Examples

accession to such demands would set a dangerous precedent
assenting to the Congressional determination

Reason

The lemma 'assenting' as a noun meaning 'agreement' or 'consent' is extremely rare or nonstandard. In standard English dictionaries, 'assent' (noun) is the recognized form for this concept, not 'assenting.' Therefore, this noun sense of 'assenting' should be excluded.
