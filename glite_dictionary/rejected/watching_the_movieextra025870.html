Synset Examples

Reason

The concept 'watching the movie' is a verb phrase describing the act of viewing a film. However, it is a compositional phrase with a transparent meaning (watch + movie) and would not appear as an entry in Oxford Dictionary. Such phrases are typically not included unless they have established idiomatic meaning, which is not the case here.
