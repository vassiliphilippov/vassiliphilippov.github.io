the total or summary of accomplishments

Synset Examples

The summa of her achievements was truly remarkable.
The summa of their efforts brought great success.

Reason

The phrase 'summa of achievements' is not an established compound or set phrase in English; it is simply a usage of 'summa' (meaning 'summation' or 'total') applied to achievements and does not have independent dictionary-worthiness. Most major English dictionaries do not treat 'summa of achievements' as a distinct entry. The sense is fully covered by the word 'summa' or by more common phrases like 'sum of achievements.'
