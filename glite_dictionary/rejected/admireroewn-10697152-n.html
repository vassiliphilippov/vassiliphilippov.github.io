admirer

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10697152-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: admirer|oewn-10697152-n
a person who backs a politician or a team etc.

Synset Examples

all their supporters came out for the game
they are friends of the library

Reason

The synset is too general; 'admirer' here is equated with 'supporter' or 'backer', which is not the most common or dictionary-relevant sense for 'admirer' (which typically refers to someone who admires another person, often romantically or with great respect). This sense is too broad and not directly tied to 'admirer'; as such, it should be excluded.
