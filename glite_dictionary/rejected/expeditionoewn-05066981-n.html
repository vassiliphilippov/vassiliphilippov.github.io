expedition

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05066981-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: expedition|oewn-05066981-n
the property of being prompt and efficient

Synset Examples

it was done with dispatch

Reason

The sense of 'expedition' as 'the property of being prompt and efficient' is archaic and extremely rare in modern English, with 'dispatch' being the more common term for this property. Contemporary dictionaries typically list only the senses relating to a journey or organized trip, not this archaic sense. Therefore, it should not be included as a standalone dictionary entry.
