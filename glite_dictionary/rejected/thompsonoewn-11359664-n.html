Reason

This sense of 'Thompson' refers to a specific individual (a proper name), but the synset is a broad 'person' category. Dictionary inclusion would require a more specific headword such as 'Benjamin Thompson' (Count Rumford). A generic surname used for a particular notable is not included in major dictionaries unless very widely recognized as an eponym or doctrine. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
