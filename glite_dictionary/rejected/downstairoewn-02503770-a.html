downstair

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02503770-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: downstair|oewn-02503770-a
on or of lower floors of a building

Synset Examples

the downstairs (or downstair) phone

Reason

The form 'downstair' as an adjective is rare, and 'downstairs' is the standard and widely used term, both as adjective and adverb. 'Downstair' is generally not included in major English dictionaries and would not typically appear in Oxford or similar dictionaries. Only 'downstairs' should be an entry.
