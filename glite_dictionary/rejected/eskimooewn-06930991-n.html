Eskimo

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06930991-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Eskimo|oewn-06930991-n
the language spoken by the Eskimo

Reason

The concept 'Eskimo' as the language belongs to an outdated and generally imprecise classification; modern English dictionaries treat 'Eskimo' primarily as a term for a group of indigenous peoples rather than a distinct language. The languages traditionally referred to as 'Eskimo' are more precisely identified as Inuit, Yupik, etc. Including this entry could be misleading and is also potentially considered offensive. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
