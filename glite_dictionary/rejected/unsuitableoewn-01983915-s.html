unsuitable

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01983915-s
Synset POS: s
Internal ID: unsuitable|oewn-01983915-s
not capable of being applied

Synset Examples

rules inapplicable to day students

Reason

The synset describes the sense 'not capable of being applied', which is best covered by the word 'inapplicable'. The headword 'unsuitable' normally means 'not appropriate' or 'not fitting', which is different from 'not capable of being applied'. Therefore, this concept should be excluded as the synset is too distant from the typical meaning of 'unsuitable' and would not be included as such in a standard dictionary.
