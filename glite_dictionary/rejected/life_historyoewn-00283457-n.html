life history

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00283457-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: life history|oewn-00283457-n
the general progression of your working or professional life

Synset Examples

the general had had a distinguished career
he had a long career in the law

Reason

The synset described as 'life history' is actually about the concept of a 'career' (the general progression of one's working life), not the specific phrase 'life history'. 'Career' deserves an entry, but 'life history', in this sense, is not a common or standard term for this meaning and would not appear in Oxford Dictionary with this definition.
