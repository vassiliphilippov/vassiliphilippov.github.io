Synset Examples

Reason

The sense 'housebreaker' as 'a wrecker of houses' is an extremely rare or regional usage. In standard English, 'housebreaker' almost always refers to a burglar, i.e., someone who illegally enters houses. The sense 'wrecker of houses' would not typically appear as its own dictionary entry in modern major dictionaries, thus it should not be included.
