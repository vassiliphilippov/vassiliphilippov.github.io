ordinance

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00166119-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: ordinance|oewn-00166119-n
the act of ordaining; the act of conferring (or receiving) holy orders

Synset Examples

the rabbi's family was present for his ordination

Reason

The headword 'ordinance' in this synset refers specifically to 'the act of ordaining' in religious sense. However, 'ordinance' is not commonly used in modern English with this meaning; 'ordination' is the standard term for the act of conferring holy orders. Therefore, this sense is too obscure or potentially confused with the more common 'ordination', and would not be included as a main entry in a modern learner's dictionary for this meaning.
