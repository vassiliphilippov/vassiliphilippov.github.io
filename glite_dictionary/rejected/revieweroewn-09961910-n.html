Reason

This specific synset for 'reviewer' is a general label for someone who reports and analyzes events of the day, but in actual English use, 'reviewer' typically refers to someone who evaluates and writes critiques (e.g., of books, films, products). The sense given here overlaps with the much broader 'journalist' or 'reporter' and does not appear as a distinct sense in standard dictionaries. Therefore, this concept does not warrant a separate dictionary entry.
