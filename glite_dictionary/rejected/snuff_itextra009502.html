snuff it

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: snuff it|extra.009502
to stop talking, to be quiet, or to stop complaining

Synset Examples

He told me to snuff it when I complained.
If you keep whining, I'll tell you to snuff it.
Just snuff it; we need to move on.

Reason

Although 'snuff it' exists in English, its commonly recognized meaning is 'to die' (mainly British slang). The sense given here ('to stop talking, to be quiet, or to stop complaining') is not attested as a standard meaning in major dictionaries, nor is it a well-known usage. Therefore, this concept should be excluded as it is not a dictionary-worthy sense for 'snuff it.'
