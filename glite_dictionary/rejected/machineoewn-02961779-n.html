machine

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: machine|oewn-02961779-n
a motor vehicle with four wheels; usually propelled by an internal combustion engine

Synset Examples

he needs a car to get to work

Reason

The synset describes 'machine' specifically as a four-wheeled motor vehicle (essentially 'car'), which is not a commonly accepted or current sense of the headword 'machine' in modern standard English. 'Machine' in standard dictionaries does not refer specifically to cars. This meaning is too specific and outdated, and would not appear as a primary sense in Oxford or other major dictionaries for 'machine'.
