comment

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07237830-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: comment|oewn-07237830-n
a report (often malicious) about the behavior of other people

Synset Examples

the divorce caused much gossip

Reason

This sense of 'comment' as 'a report (often malicious) about the behavior of other people' is better covered by words like 'gossip' or 'rumor.' In current English usage, 'comment' does not refer specifically to malicious reports about people's behavior and would not be found in a modern English-English dictionary in this sense.
