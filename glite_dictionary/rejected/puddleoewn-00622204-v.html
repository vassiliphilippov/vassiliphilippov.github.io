Reason

The verb sense of 'puddle' meaning 'mix up or confuse' is highly archaic and not attested in modern standard English. This usage is not included in reputable dictionaries such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster. The standard verb 'to muddle' is used in this sense. Therefore, this sense of 'puddle' should not be included in a contemporary English-English dictionary.
