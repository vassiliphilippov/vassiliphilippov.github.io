conformation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01206166-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: conformation|oewn-01206166-n
acting according to certain accepted standards

Synset Examples

their financial statements are in conformity with generally accepted accounting practices

Reason

The sense described here for 'conformation' (acting according to accepted standards) does not match its standard dictionary usage. In reputable dictionaries, 'conformation' refers primarily to the shape, structure, or arrangement of something, especially an animal's body. The correct noun for this sense is 'conformity.' Since 'conformation' with this meaning would not appear in Oxford or similar dictionaries, it should be excluded.
