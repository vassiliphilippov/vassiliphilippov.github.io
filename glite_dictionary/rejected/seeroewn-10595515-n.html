seer

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10595515-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: seer|oewn-10595515-n
an observer who perceives visually

Synset Examples

an incurable seer of movies

Reason

This sense of 'seer'—meaning simply a person who sees or observes visually—is far too generic and not specific to this headword. In modern English, 'seer' refers to someone who claims to see the future or has prophetic abilities, not just any observer. Therefore, this overly broad synset is not suitable for inclusion as a distinct dictionary entry.
