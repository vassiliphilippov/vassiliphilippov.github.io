zoology

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08009721-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: zoology|oewn-08009721-n
all the animal life in a particular region or period

Synset Examples

the fauna of China
the zoology of the Pliocene epoch

Reason

This synset uses 'zoology' to mean 'all the animal life in a particular region or period', which is more properly expressed by the terms 'fauna' or 'animal life'. In standard English, 'zoology' refers specifically to the scientific study of animals, not the set of animals themselves. Therefore, this sense is too broad/generic for the headword and does not align with dictionary inclusion criteria.
