Synset Examples

a terrible stench saluted our nostrils

Reason

This sense of 'salute' as 'become noticeable' is extremely rare, poetic, and nonstandard. It does not represent a commonly used or recognized meaning in contemporary English and would not appear as a principal verb sense in standard dictionaries like Oxford. The primary meanings of 'salute' involve gestures of respect or greeting, not things becoming noticeable. Therefore, it should be excluded from the dictionary.
