Reason

'Expender' is an uncommon and rarely used word in modern English for 'someone who spends money.' The standard and widely recognized term is 'spender.' 'Expender' does not appear in major dictionaries like Oxford or Merriam-Webster in this sense, and would not be included except perhaps as an obsolete or extremely rare term. Therefore, it should not be included in a general dictionary.
