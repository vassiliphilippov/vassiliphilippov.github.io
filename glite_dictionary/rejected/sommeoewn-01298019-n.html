Reason

The concept refers to 'Somme' as a specific battle of World War II (1944), which appears to be a misattribution. The Somme is primarily known as the site of a major World War I battle (1916), and as a geographical name (a river; a department in France). Including it as 'battle of World War II (1944)' would be misleading and not in line with dictionary standards.
