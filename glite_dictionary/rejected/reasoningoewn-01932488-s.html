The synset is an adjective sense ('endowed with the capacity to reason'), essentially an attributive use of the noun 'reasoning'. This sense as a dedicated adjective is too marginal for a standalone dictionary entry and does not commonly appear as an adjective in natural usage; instead, 'reasoning' is overwhelmingly used as a noun. This adjectival participial sense is too closely derived and not substantive enough for a separate entry, so it should be excluded.
