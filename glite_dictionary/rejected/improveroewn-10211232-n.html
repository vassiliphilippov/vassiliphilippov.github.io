improver

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10211232-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: improver|oewn-10211232-n
someone devoted to the promotion of human welfare and to social reforms

Reason

The synset definition given here for 'improver' matches the sense of 'humanitarian' or 'reformer,' but 'improver' is not commonly used in standard English with this specific meaning. It primarily means someone (or something) that improves something, and would not appear in Oxford Dictionary with this abstract social reformer sense. Therefore, this specific concept should not be included.
