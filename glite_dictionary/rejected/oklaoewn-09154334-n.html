Reason

The concept 'Okla.' is an abbreviation for the state of Oklahoma. Standard dictionaries generally do not include abbreviations of state names as headwords unless they have substantial independent usage outside of proper noun abbreviation lists. The core meaning is fully covered under 'Oklahoma', so this entry is too narrow and would not appear as a main dictionary entry.
