woketard nonsense

Source: Idiom List
WordNet ID: n/a
Synset POS:
Internal ID: woketard nonsense|extra.026195
dismissive term for perceived overly progressive ideas

Synset Examples

His comments were dismissed as typical woketard nonsense.

Reason

The term 'woketard nonsense' contains the highly offensive and derogatory word 'tard', which is derived from an ableist slur and is widely considered unacceptable in polite or formal language. Its construction is not suitable for inclusion in a standard English dictionary and does not adhere to dictionary policies regarding slurs and hate speech. Therefore, it should be excluded.
