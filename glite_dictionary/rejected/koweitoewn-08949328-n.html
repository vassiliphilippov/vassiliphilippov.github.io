Reason

The synset 'a seaport on the Persian Gulf and capital of Kuwait' is for the concept 'Kuwait City', but the spelling 'Koweit' is an archaic or foreign transliteration not commonly used in contemporary English. Modern dictionaries would include 'Kuwait' or 'Kuwait City' as the entry, not 'Koweit'. Thus, 'Koweit' should not be included as a separate headword.
