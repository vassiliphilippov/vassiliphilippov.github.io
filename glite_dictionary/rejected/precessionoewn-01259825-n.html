precession

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01259825-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: precession|oewn-01259825-n
the act of preceding in time or order or rank (as in a ceremony)

Reason

This sense of 'precession' refers broadly to any act of preceding in time, order, or rank, which is a general concept and not specific or commonly used in modern English. The term 'precession' is almost exclusively used in physics and astronomy to describe movement, not ceremonial order, and this sense would not appear in a modern English dictionary.
