Reason

The noun sense of 'revealing' as 'the speech act of making something evident' is not in standard use in English. The common senses are as an adjective ('showing more than intended') or as a present participle/gerund relating to the verb 'reveal.' As a standalone noun, it is not found in major dictionaries like Oxford and would not be useful or appropriate as a distinct entry.
