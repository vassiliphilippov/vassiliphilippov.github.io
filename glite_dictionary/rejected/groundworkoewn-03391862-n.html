groundwork

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03391862-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: groundwork|oewn-03391862-n
lowest support of a structure

Synset Examples

it was built on a base of solid rock
he stood at the foot of the tower

Reason

This sense of 'groundwork' as the literal lowest support of a structure is not supported by standard usage in modern English. In contemporary dictionaries, 'groundwork' typically refers to foundational preliminary work or preparation, not the physical base of a building; that is usually called 'base', 'foundation', or 'foot'. Therefore, this meaning should not be included.
