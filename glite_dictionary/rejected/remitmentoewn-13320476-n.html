remitment

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13320476-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: remitment|oewn-13320476-n
a payment of money sent to a person in another place

Reason

The term 'remitment' is not in wide contemporary use, and standard British and American dictionaries, including Oxford, do not list it. The concept is also fully covered by the much more common term 'remittance.' Therefore, 'remitment' does not warrant inclusion as an independent headword.
