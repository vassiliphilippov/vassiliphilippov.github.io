English philanthropist who made contributions to a college in Connecticut that was renamed in his honor (1649-1721)

Reason

The synset here is 'person', referring to the individual Elihu Yale. Proper names of people whose meaning does not extend beyond their status as individuals are not included in general English dictionaries, unless they have become common nouns or adjectives.
