biotechnology

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06135974-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: biotechnology|oewn-06135974-n
the branch of engineering science in which biological science is used to study the relation between workers and their environments

Reason

The sense described does not correspond to the standard or widely accepted meaning of 'biotechnology.' It misidentifies 'biotechnology' as the study of relations between workers and their environments (which is more accurately described by 'ergonomics' or 'human factors engineering'). Therefore, this particular concept should not be included in the dictionary.
