Nigerien

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03102538-a
Synset POS: a
Internal ID: Nigerien|oewn-03102538-a
of or relating to the people of Nigeria

Synset Examples

a Nigerian novelist won the Nobel Prize for literature this year

Reason

This synset mislabels 'Nigerien' as referring to Nigeria and its people; in correct English usage, 'Nigerien' relates to Niger (the country), while people or things from Nigeria are 'Nigerian.' Therefore, this entry is inaccurate and should not be included.
