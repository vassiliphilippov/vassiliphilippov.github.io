Reason

This WordNet concept refers to the sacred writings of Islam ('the Book' as a title for the Quran), but it is not a common or primary sense of 'book' as would normally appear under the headword itself in a general dictionary. Instead, this meaning would be specified under a proper noun, 'the Quran' or 'the Koran'. Therefore, this concept should not be included as a standalone entry for 'book'.
