capitulation

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06479539-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: capitulation|oewn-06479539-n
a summary that enumerates the main parts of a topic

Reason

Although 'capitulation' can refer to making a list of headings or a summary, this sense is obsolete and extremely rare. The common contemporary meaning of 'capitulation' is 'surrender', particularly in military, financial, or negotiating contexts. The meaning given here ('a summary that enumerates the main parts of a topic') is not in active use and would not be included in standard modern dictionaries, including Oxford.
