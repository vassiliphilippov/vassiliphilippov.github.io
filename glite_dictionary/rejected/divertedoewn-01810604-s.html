Synset Examples

Reason

This sense ('diverted' meaning 'pleasantly occupied') is highly rare and marginal in modern English. The standard meaning of 'diverted' is 'turned aside' (physically or metaphorically), and the sense 'amused/pleasantly occupied' is not sufficiently common to merit a standalone dictionary entry in a contemporary Oxford-like dictionary.
