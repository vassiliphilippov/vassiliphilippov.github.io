kettle

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13790294-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: kettle|oewn-13790294-n
the quantity a kettle will hold

Reason

This sense describes the abstract measurement of a vessel's capacity (i.e., 'a kettleful'), not a specific established meaning that would appear as a dictionary headword. Standard dictionaries list 'kettle' as a vessel, not as a unit or measure. This sense is too derivative and not dictionary-worthy as a separate entry.
