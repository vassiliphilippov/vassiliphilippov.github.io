Reason

This sense of 'disposal' is overly broad and not specifically associated with the headword in natural usage. In standard English, 'disposal' does not normally mean the management of the affairs of a group, and such a definition would not appear as a distinct sense in major dictionaries. Therefore, it should be excluded.
