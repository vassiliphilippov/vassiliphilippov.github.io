New Hampshire

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09134282-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: New Hampshire|oewn-09134282-n
one of the British colonies that formed the United States, with capital Portsmouth

Reason

The synset refers to New Hampshire as one of the colonial entities (a category of "colony" or "political division") rather than the modern U.S. state. This specific colonial sense, as a former polity, is now largely historical and not used outside specialized historical discussion. Standard dictionaries generally do not include individual colonial entities as separate entries unless they are still current entities or have ongoing usage. The main entry for "New Hampshire" as a U.S. state should be included, but not this historical colonial sense.
