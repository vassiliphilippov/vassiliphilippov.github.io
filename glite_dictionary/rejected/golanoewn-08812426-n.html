Reason

The synset for 'Golan' refers to a geographical region (the Golan Heights) and is thus a proper noun. However, as described, the synset is simply 'a fortified hilly area between southern Lebanon and southern Syria,' which is overly general for a dictionary entry. The dictionary would instead include 'Golan Heights' as an entry, not 'Golan' by itself in this geopolitical sense. Therefore, the concept is excluded as per the criteria.
