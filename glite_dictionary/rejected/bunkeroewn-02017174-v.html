Reason

This verb sense of 'bunker' (meaning 'to transfer cargo from a ship to a warehouse') is obsolete, technical, and extremely rare. It does not appear in modern general dictionaries like Oxford; 'bunker' as a verb in current usage primarily refers to supplying fuel to a ship or, less commonly, to taking shelter. Therefore, this sense should not be included in a general English-English dictionary.
