Reason

This concept refers to the adverbial use of 'false' as in the phrase 'play someone false', meaning to act in a disloyal or faithless way. However, 'false' is not independently used as an adverb in standard modern English, and this construction is now rare and idiomatic. Instead, more common terms such as 'treacherously' or 'deceitfully' are used. Thus, this specific adverbial sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry in a modern English-English dictionary.
