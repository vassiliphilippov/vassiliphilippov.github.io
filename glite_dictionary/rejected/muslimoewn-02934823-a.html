Reason

This concept uses the headword 'Muslim' as an adjective meaning 'of or relating to or supporting Islamism.' However, in standard English usage, 'Muslim' as an adjective generally refers to 'of or relating to Muslims or Islam' rather than specifically 'Islamism,' which is a particular political ideology. Furthermore, the adjective form in this sense is usually 'Islamic' (as in 'Islamic art'), and 'Muslim' more commonly serves as a noun. Therefore, this concept is too confusing, circular, and does not correspond to standard English dictionary entries.
