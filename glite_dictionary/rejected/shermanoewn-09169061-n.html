Reason

This sense of 'Sherman' refers to a specific town in Texas, which is a proper noun for a place. Most general English dictionaries, including Oxford, do not list individual place names (towns, cities) unless they are of exceptional international importance or serve as symbols in language. Sherman, Texas does not meet this criterion.
