Kirghiz

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
Internal ID: Kirghiz|oewn-09042810-n
a landlocked republic in west central Asia bordering on northwestern China; formerly an Asian soviet but became independent in 1991

Reason

The concept 'Kirghiz' in this sense refers to Kyrgyzstan, a country. While 'Kyrgyzstan' is an accepted place name in English, the form 'Kirghiz' for the republic is archaic and not commonly used as a headword for the country in modern English dictionaries. The synset is more about a proper noun referring to a specific place ('republic' sense), which is too general in this context and not standard for inclusion as a dictionary headword.
