Reason

This specific concept of 'skinny' meaning 'fitting snugly' is too general and not conventionally used as a core dictionary sense. In mainstream English dictionaries, 'skinny' is defined as 'very thin' or, informally for clothing, 'very tight' (e.g., 'skinny jeans'), but as a direct synonym for 'tight-fitting,' it is usually not listed. The word is not broadly used to mean 'fitting snugly' outside of those derived 'skinny jeans' contexts, and the sense is covered more naturally under 'tight' or 'tight-fitting.' Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
