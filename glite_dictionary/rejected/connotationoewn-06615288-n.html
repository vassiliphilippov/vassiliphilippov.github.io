Reason

This particular sense of 'connotation' refers not to the common meaning (an associated or suggested meaning of a word) but to a technical, logical/linguistic definition related to determining reference. This technical nuance is not commonly included in general English dictionaries but rather in specialized works of semantics or logic. Therefore, this concept does not fit typical dictionary criteria.
