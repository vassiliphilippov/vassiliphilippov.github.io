wearing a wedding ring; lawfully married

Synset Examples

a ringed wife

Reason

The sense 'ringed' meaning 'wearing a wedding ring; lawfully married' is too rare, specific, and not in general usage as a standalone adjective. This adjectival use appears to be more of an ad hoc descriptive formation and does not meet the criteria for inclusion in standard dictionaries like Oxford. 'Ringed' as an adjective usually describes something surrounded by rings or marked with rings, not marital status.
