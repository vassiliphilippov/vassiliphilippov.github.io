centre

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03971750-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: centre|oewn-03971750-n
mercantile establishment consisting of a carefully landscaped complex of shops representing leading merchandisers; usually includes restaurants and a convenient parking area; a modern version of the traditional marketplace

Synset Examples

a good plaza should have a movie house
they spent their weekends at the local malls

Reason

This sense of 'centre' as a shopping mall or retail complex is not standard in modern British or American English; 'shopping centre' or 'mall' are the widely used terms. The headword 'centre' alone is too generic for this sense, and this specific commercial meaning is better captured under 'shopping centre' or 'mall'. Therefore, this sense does not warrant a separate dictionary entry for 'centre' as defined here.
