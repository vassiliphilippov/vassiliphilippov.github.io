Reason

This concept represents the adverbial use of "bright" meaning "in a bright manner" (e.g., "shine bright"). However, the adverb form "bright" is archaic and extremely rare in contemporary usage. Instead, "brightly" is the standard adverb form. Dictionaries like Oxford do not list "bright" as an adverb for modern usage except in set, literary, or archaic phrases. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
