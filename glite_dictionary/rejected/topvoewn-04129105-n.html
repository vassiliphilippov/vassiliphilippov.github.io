TOPV

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04129105-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: TOPV|oewn-04129105-n
an oral vaccine (containing live but weakened poliovirus) that is given to provide immunity to poliomyelitis

Reason

The headword 'TOPV' is an acronym for 'trivalent oral poliovirus vaccine', a technical medical abbreviation. While the concept is precise, the acronym 'TOPV' is not commonly used or widely recognized in general English dictionaries such as Oxford. It is mostly found in specialized medical literature. Therefore, it should not be included as a primary entry in a general English dictionary.
