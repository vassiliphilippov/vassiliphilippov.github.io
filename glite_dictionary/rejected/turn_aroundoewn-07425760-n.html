Reason

This synset refers to the general noun of changing direction or position, but the actual headword 'turn around' is not used as a noun in standard dictionaries with this meaning. The description fits more common nouns like 'reversal' or 'turnaround' (as one word). The use of 'turn around' as a noun meaning physical reversal is not attested in natural English usage. Therefore, this concept should be excluded, as it would not appear in the Oxford Dictionary under 'turn around' as a noun.
