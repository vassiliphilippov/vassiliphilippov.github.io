Reason

The synset for 'Delaware' in this sense is simply 'one of the British colonies that formed the United States,' referring to the general category of 'colony' associated with Delaware. This is too general and does not add a distinct dictionary sense for the headword beyond its function as a place name. Delaware as a U.S. state or former colony would be included, but not this generic synset.
