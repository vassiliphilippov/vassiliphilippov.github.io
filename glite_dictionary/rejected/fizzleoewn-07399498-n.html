fizzle

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07399498-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: fizzle|oewn-07399498-n
a fricative sound (especially as an expression of disapproval)

Synset Examples

the performers could not be heard over the hissing of the audience

Reason

In this sense, 'fizzle' as a noun for a fricative sound (such as hissing) is not a current or common usage in modern English. The much more common senses of 'fizzle' are as a verb meaning to fail with a feeble end, or rarely as a noun meaning a failure. This very technical or obsolete usage does not appear in standard dictionaries and would not be meaningful for general learners. Therefore, it should be excluded.
