Reason

This concept is an intensifier related to the phrase 'stark staring mad,' but as an isolated word ('staring') with the meaning 'without qualification' as an intensifier, it does not stand alone in modern English usage. The meaning only appears in set phrases (especially 'stark staring mad') and is not a general intensifier entry. Therefore, it should not be included as a headword with this meaning.
