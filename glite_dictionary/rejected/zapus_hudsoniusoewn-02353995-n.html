Reason

'Zapus hudsonius' is the scientific (Latin binomial) name for the meadow jumping mouse and is not typically used as a headword in general English dictionaries. Instead, the common name 'meadow jumping mouse' would be included. The concept is too technical and not directly relevant to English dictionary usage for general learners.
