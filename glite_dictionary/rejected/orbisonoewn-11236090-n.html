United States composer and rockabilly tenor popular in the 1950s (1936-1988)

Reason

'Orbison' as used here refers to Roy Orbison, a proper noun identified only as a specific person. Its synset is 'person', which is too general; such proper names are not included in standard dictionaries like Oxford, unless they have extended meaning beyond being a name, which is not the case here.
