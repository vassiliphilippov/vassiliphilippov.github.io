Reason

The synset connected to 'fixer' here describes a synthetic narcotic drug, specifically methadone, not the general noun 'fixer.' In common English usage, 'fixer' does not refer to this substance, so including this sense would be misleading. This is not a real or recognized meaning of the word 'fixer' in contemporary English dictionaries. Therefore, this sense is excluded.
