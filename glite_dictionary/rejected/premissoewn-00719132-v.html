premiss

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00719132-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: premiss|oewn-00719132-v
take something as preexisting and given

Reason

The verb 'premiss' with the meaning 'take something as preexisting and given' is extremely rare and archaic. In both Oxford and modern usage, only the noun 'premise' (or rarely 'premiss') is standard. As a verb, 'premiss' does not appear in reputable dictionaries and is not in contemporary use. Therefore, this concept should be excluded.
