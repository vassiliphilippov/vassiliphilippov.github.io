atomic number 33

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-14653327-n
Synset POS: n
Internal ID: atomic number 33|oewn-14653327-n
a very poisonous metallic element that has three allotropic forms; arsenic and arsenic compounds are used as herbicides and insecticides and in various alloys; found in arsenopyrite and orpiment and realgar

Reason

The concept 'atomic number 33' is a technical reference to arsenic, already fully covered by the word 'arsenic' in dictionaries. 'Atomic number 33' as a phrase is too general and not dictionary-worthy in itself; it is a scientific designation not used as a common headword in English dictionaries.
