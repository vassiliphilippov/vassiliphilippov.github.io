Reason

The phrase 'to both ears' in this synset is a general compositional phrase describing direction of sound, not an established lexical unit or idiom. It is not used as a standalone adverbial in the same lexicalized way as 'binaurally,' and would not appear as a dictionary headword in reputable English dictionaries such as the Oxford Dictionary.
