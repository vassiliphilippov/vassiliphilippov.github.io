being five more than one hundred sixty

Reason

'clxv' is the Roman numeral representation for 165 and is not used as a main headword in modern English dictionaries since Roman numerals as such are not defined as words but as a system for representing numbers. Dictionaries typically include an entry for 'Roman numeral' and the main numerals, but not every combination. Therefore, this should be excluded.
