white out

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00304587-v
Synset POS: v
Internal ID: white out|oewn-00304587-v
widen the interlinear spacing by inserting leads

Reason

This sense of 'white out' refers specifically to a typographic process (widening interlinear spacing by inserting leads), which is rare and highly technical. Additionally, 'white out' is much more commonly used as a noun or verb meaning to cover an error with correction fluid, or in meteorology for low-visibility conditions. This typographic sense is obsolete and not relevant for general dictionary inclusion, and would not appear in modern Oxford Dictionary.
