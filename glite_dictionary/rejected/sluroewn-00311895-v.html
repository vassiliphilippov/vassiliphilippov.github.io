become vague or indistinct

Synset Examples

The distinction between the two theories blurred

Reason

The sense 'slur' meaning 'become vague or indistinct' is not a standard use of this verb in English (it is more typically used to refer to speaking unclearly or insulting). The sense described in this synset is better captured by verbs like 'blur' or 'obscure'. This is not a use of 'slur' that would appear as a separate entry or sense in a major dictionary such as Oxford or Merriam-Webster.
