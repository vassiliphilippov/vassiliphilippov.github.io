Synset POS: s

using or containing too many words

Synset Examples

long-winded (or windy) speakers

Reason

The concept 'tedious' used in the sense of 'using or containing too many words' is actually better represented by synonyms like 'verbose,' 'wordy,' or 'long-winded.' The main and widely accepted meaning of 'tedious' is 'too long, slow, or dull,' which does not specifically refer to excessive wordiness. Therefore, this particular meaning should not have a separate dictionary entry for 'tedious' in this sense, as it is not a standard or accepted usage.
