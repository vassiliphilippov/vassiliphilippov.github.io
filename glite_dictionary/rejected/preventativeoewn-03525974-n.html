Reason

The sense described ('any obstruction that impedes or is burdensome') is not a valid or recognized noun definition for 'preventative' in standard English dictionaries. 'Preventative' is commonly used as an adjective (and sometimes as a noun meaning a preventive measure), but not as a noun for an obstruction. This sense appears to be a misattribution or confusion with another word, such as 'impediment' or 'obstruction.'
