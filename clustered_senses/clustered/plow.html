plow

Summary

  • to turn over and break up the soil with a plow, especially in preparation for planting crops
    • VERB: to turn over soil with a plow
  • A large farming implement with one or more heavy blades, used for turning over the soil to prepare it for planting.
    • NOUN: farm tool for turning soil
  • to move forcefully or steadily through a substance, as a plow does through soil
    • VERB: move forcefully through something
