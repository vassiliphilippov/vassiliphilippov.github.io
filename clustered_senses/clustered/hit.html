An act of striking, touching, or coming into contact with someone or something, either deliberately or by accident, often with force. This includes a hit with a hand, object, weapon, in sports, or producing a sound by striking (NOUN).

1. NOUN

/ hɪt / (AmE), / hɪt / (BrE)

Examples
