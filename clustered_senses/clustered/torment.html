1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔːrˌmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈtɔːmɛnt / (BrE)
Severe physical or mental suffering; a great affliction or distress.
severe suffering or affliction

Examples

The memory of his failure was a constant torment to him.
The prisoners endured unimaginable torment during their captivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agony - extreme physical or mental suffering
