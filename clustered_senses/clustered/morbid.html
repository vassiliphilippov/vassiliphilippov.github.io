morbid

Summary

  • Having an unhealthy or disturbing interest in unpleasant subjects, especially death, disease, or decay.
    • ADJ: having an unhealthy interest in disturbing subjects
    • ADJ: suggesting horror of death or decay
  • (of a part or process) caused by, relating to, or characteristic of disease; diseased or pathological.
    • ADJ: diseased or pathological
Having an unhealthy or disturbing interest in unpleasant subjects, especially death, disease, or decay.

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɔrbɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɔːbɪd / (BrE)
characterized by or suggesting an unhealthy interest in disturbing or unpleasant subjects, especially death or disease

Examples

The novel explores the protagonist's morbid thoughts and obsessions.
