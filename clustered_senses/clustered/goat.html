goat

Summary

  • A hardy domesticated animal related to the sheep, typically having a beard and straight horns.
    • NOUN: domesticated animal with beard and horns
  • a person who is the victim of ridicule, blame, or pranks, especially in a group setting
    • NOUN: person subject to ridicule or blame
  • (Astrology) A person born under the sign of Capricorn.
    • NOUN: person born under Capricorn (astrology)
  • informal slang. a person who is widely regarded as the greatest of all time in a particular field or activity.
    • NOUN: greatest of all time (slang)

1. NOUN

/ ɡoʊt / (AmE), / ɡəʊt / (BrE)
A hardy domesticated animal related to the sheep, typically having a beard and straight horns.
domesticated animal with beard and horns

Examples

A goat climbed effortlessly up the steep rocky hillside.
The farmer keeps a goat for its milk and meat.
They watched as the goat nibbled leaves from the bush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheep - a domesticated ruminant animal related to the goat, usually kept for its wool or meat

Etymology

From Old English *gāt*, from Proto-Germanic *gaits*.
