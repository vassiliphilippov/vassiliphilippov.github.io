nurture

Summary

  • the care and encouragement given to someone, especially a child, to help them grow and develop as an accepted member of society
    • NOUN: care and encouragement for growth and development
  • the influence of upbringing and environment on a person's development, especially as distinguished from inherited traits
    • NOUN: upbringing's influence on development
  • To care for and encourage the growth or development of someone or something.
    • VERB: to provide nourishment; to feed
    • VERB: to encourage growth or development
    • VERB: to bring up and care for a child

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɝː.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈnɜː.tʃə / (BrE)
the care and encouragement given to someone, especially a child, to help them grow and develop as an accepted member of society

Examples

The debate between nurture and nature continues in psychology.

Synset Examples
