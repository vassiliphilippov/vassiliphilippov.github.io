naughty

Summary

  • (especially of a child) badly behaved or disobedient.
    • ADJ: badly behaved; disobedient
  • (of words, jokes, or actions) suggestive of sexual impropriety; mildly indecent or risqué, especially in a playful or humorous way.
    • ADJ: mildly indecent; sexually suggestive

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɔti / (AmE), / ˈnɔːti / (BrE)
(especially of a child) badly behaved or disobedient.
badly behaved; disobedient

Examples

If you are naughty at school, you might get a warning from the teacher.
She scolded her naughty puppy for chewing the shoes.
The naughty boy was sent to his room for misbehaving.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: well-behaved - behaving in a polite and proper manner

Etymology

From Middle English naughtie, originally meaning 'having nothing'.
