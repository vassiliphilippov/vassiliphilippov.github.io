1. NOUN

/ ˈpælət / (AmE), / ˈpælət / (BrE)
the upper surface of the mouth that separates the oral and nasal cavities; commonly referred to as the roof of the mouth
roof of the mouth

Examples

A burn to the palate can be very painful and take time to heal.
The roof of your mouth is called the palate.
The surgeon repaired the child's cleft palate successfully.

Etymology

From Latin *palatum* (roof of the mouth).
