profound

Summary

  • having a very great and far-reaching effect; thoroughgoing or significant in influence or change
    • ADJ: of deep or far-reaching effect
  • (of a place or thing) situated or extending to a great depth; very deep or unfathomable.
    • ADJ: very deep; extending far down
  • Showing great knowledge, insight, emotional depth, or intensity; very great or complete in feeling, quality, or impression.
    • ADJ: showing great intellectual or emotional depth
    • ADJ: deeply felt; coming from within
    • ADJ: great intensity; complete or absolute
  • (of sleep) very deep and undisturbed; complete and intense.
    • ADJ: very deep and undisturbed (of sleep)
