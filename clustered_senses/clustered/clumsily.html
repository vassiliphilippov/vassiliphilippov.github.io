In a manner lacking skill, grace, or coordination; awkwardly.

1. ADV

/ ˈklʌm.zə.li / (AmE), / ˈklʌm.zɪ.li / (BrE)
In a manner lacking skill, grace, or coordination; awkwardly.
in an awkward or unskilled way

Examples

He clumsily knocked over the glass while reaching for the bread.
She clumsily dropped her phone while trying to answer a call.
The child clumsily tied his shoelaces, making a loose knot.

Synset Examples

he snatched the bills clumsily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awkwardly - in a manner that is not graceful or is lacking skill

Etymology

From 'clumsy' + '-ly', forming the adverb from the adjective 'clumsy'.
