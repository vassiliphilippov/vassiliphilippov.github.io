album

Summary

  • a book with blank pages, often with pockets or mounts, used for organizing and storing photographs, stamps, or similar items
    • NOUN: book for photos or collections
  • a collection of audio recordings issued together as a single item, originally on record, cassette, or compact disc
    • NOUN: a collection of audio recordings

1. NOUN

/ ˈæl.bəm / (AmE), / ˈæl.bəm / (BrE)
a book with blank pages, often with pockets or mounts, used for organizing and storing photographs, stamps, or similar items
book for photos or collections

Examples

He collects rare stamps and displays them in a large album.
She kept her childhood photographs safe in a special album on the bookshelf.
The wedding album was filled with beautiful pictures and precious memories.

Etymology

From Latin 'album' (white tablet), originally referring to a blank tablet for public notices.
