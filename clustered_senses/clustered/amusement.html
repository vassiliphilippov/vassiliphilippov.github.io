1. NOUN

/ əˈmjuzmənt / (AmE), / əˈmjuːzmənt / (BrE)
an activity or entertainment that is enjoyable and holds one's attention
an enjoyable activity or entertainment

Examples

Reading mystery novels is his main amusement during long train rides.
The park offers a variety of amusement for children and adults alike.
We went to the fair seeking some summer amusement on a hot afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entertainment - something that entertains or amuses
  • Antonym: boredom - the state of being bored

Etymology

From French 'amusement', from 'amuser' (to divert, entertain).

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enjoyment - pleasure or satisfaction gained from something
  • Antonym: boredom - the feeling of being bored or uninterested
