janitor

Summary

  • a person whose job is to clean and take care of a building, such as a school or office
    • NOUN: person who cleans and maintains a building
a person whose job is to clean and take care of a building, such as a school or office

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒænɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒænɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to clean and take care of a building, such as a school or office
person who cleans and maintains a building

Examples

After everyone left, the janitor locked up the building for the night.
The janitor swept the hallways every morning before the students arrived.
The school hired an extra janitor to help maintain the new gymnasium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caretaker - person responsible for taking care of a building
  • Synonym: custodian - person employed to clean and maintain a building

Etymology

From Latin 'ianitor' (doorkeeper), via Old French and Middle English.
