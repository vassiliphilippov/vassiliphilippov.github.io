1. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈzuːbərənt / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzjuːbərənt / (BrE)
(of feelings or behavior) uninhibited, enthusiastic, or overflowing; unrestrained and lively, especially in expression or mood.
uninhibited; extremely enthusiastic

Examples

He expressed his appreciation in the most exuberant terms, overflowing with gratitude.
Her exuberant personality makes her the life of every party.
The children greeted the news with exuberant cheers and laughter.

Synset Examples

extravagant praise
exuberant compliments
overweening ambition
overweening greed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiastic - showing intense and eager enjoyment or interest
  • Antonym: restrained - controlled, held back, or limited in expression or emotion

Etymology

From Latin exuberans, from exuberare (to be abundant), from ex- (out of) + uber (fertile, fruitful).

2. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈzuːbərənt / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzjuːbərənt / (BrE)
full of energy, excitement, and cheerfulness; joyously unrestrained.
full of joyful energy; unrestrained

Examples

Her exuberant laughter filled the room and made everyone smile.
The children were exuberant after their team won the championship game.
The garden was exuberant with colorful blooms in early summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiastic - having or showing intense and eager enjoyment or interest
  • Synonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic; in good spirits
  • Antonym: restrained - characterized by reserve or moderation; unemotional or dispassionate

Etymology

From Latin exuberans, exuberant-, present participle of exuberare (to be abundant), from uber (udder, fruitful).
