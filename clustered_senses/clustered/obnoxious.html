obnoxious

Summary

  • Extremely unpleasant, offensive, or likely to cause disapproval or protest.
    • ADJ: offensive or very unpleasant
Extremely unpleasant, offensive, or likely to cause disapproval or protest.

1. ADJ

/ əbˈnɑːkʃəs / (AmE), / əbˈnɒkʃəs / (BrE)
Extremely unpleasant, offensive, or likely to cause disapproval or protest.
offensive or very unpleasant

Examples

Many people complained about the obnoxious smell coming from the factory.
She found her roommate's obnoxious behavior intolerable after a while.
The man at the table next to us was being obnoxious and loud all evening.

Synset Examples

a vulgar and objectionable person

Related Terms

  • Synonym: offensive - causing anger, annoyance, or resentment; unpleasant, especially in a way that shocks or upsets people
  • Antonym: inoffensive - not likely to offend, cause harm, or provoke protest

Etymology

From Latin 'obnoxius' (subject, liable).
