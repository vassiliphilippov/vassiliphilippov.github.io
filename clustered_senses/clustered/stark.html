Complete, extreme, or blunt; used to emphasize the total or absolute nature of something, often unpleasant or clear. Also used as an adverb to intensify meaning.

1. ADJ

/ stɑrk / (AmE), / stɑːk / (BrE)
used to emphasize the complete or extreme quality of something, often in phrases such as 'stark raving mad' or 'stark naked'.
complete or extreme (emphasizing intensity)
informal

Examples

After hearing his stark warning, everyone fell silent.
She was stark naked when she ran out of the bathroom.
The early morning revealed the stark reality of the devastation.
