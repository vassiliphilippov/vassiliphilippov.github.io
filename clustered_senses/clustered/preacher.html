preacher

Summary

  • a person whose job is to deliver sermons, especially in a church or religious setting
    • NOUN: person who delivers religious sermons
a person whose job is to deliver sermons, especially in a church or religious setting

1. NOUN

/ ˈpriː.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpriː.tʃə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to deliver sermons, especially in a church or religious setting
person who delivers religious sermons

Examples

As a traveling preacher, she visited many rural communities to spread her message.
People gathered in the small chapel to listen to the preacher's sermon.
The preacher spoke passionately about forgiveness during the Sunday service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minister - a member of the clergy, especially in Protestant churches
  • Synonym: pastor - a clergyman responsible for a Christian church and its congregation
  • Antonym: layperson - a non-ordained member of a church

Etymology

From Middle English 'prechour', from Old French 'precheor', from Latin 'praedicator' (one who proclaims).
