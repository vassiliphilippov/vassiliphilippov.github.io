to smite

Summary

  • to strike someone or something with a heavy blow, especially with the hand, a tool, or a weapon
    • VERB: strike with a heavy blow
  • to inflict a heavy blow or to cause pain or suffering, especially as a punishment or by a supernatural force
    • VERB: to strike or inflict pain, often as punishment
  • to affect suddenly and powerfully with a strong emotion, especially love or desire
    • VERB: affect suddenly with strong emotion

to strike someone or something with a heavy blow, especially with the hand, a tool, or a weapon

Examples

Lightning seemed to smite the ancient oak during the fierce storm.

Related Terms

Etymology
