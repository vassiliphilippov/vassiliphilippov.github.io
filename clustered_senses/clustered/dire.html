Extremely serious, urgent, or causing great fear or worry; involving great danger or disaster.

1. ADJ

/ daɪɚ / (AmE), / daɪə / (BrE)
causing great fear, shock, or worry; extremely serious or urgent.
causing fear or extreme seriousness

Examples

Her face showed dire fear as she received the unexpected news.
The company is in dire financial trouble after several bad investments.
The villagers were warned of the dire consequences if the river flooded.

Examples
