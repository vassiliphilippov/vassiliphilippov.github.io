weave

Summary

  • the pattern or structure formed by interlacing threads in the production of a fabric
    • NOUN: pattern or structure of a fabric
  • To make fabric by interlacing threads, or to combine different things together to form a connected whole.
    • VERB: make cloth by interlacing threads
    • VERB: interlace threads or elements
  • To move or progress in a winding or side-to-side manner, especially to avoid obstacles.
    • VERB: move or sway from side to side
    • VERB: move in a winding or zigzag course
