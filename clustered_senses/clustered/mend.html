mend

Summary

  • A piece of sewing or the act of repairing something that is broken, torn, or worn, especially in clothing or objects.
    • NOUN: repair in fabric by sewing
    • NOUN: the act of repairing or restoring
  • to repair something that is broken or damaged by fixing what is torn or replacing a part
    • VERB: to repair something damaged or broken
  • (of an injury, illness, or person) to heal or recover; to become healthy again after damage or sickness.
    • VERB: heal or recover from injury or sickness
A piece of sewing or the act of repairing something that is broken, torn, or worn, especially in clothing or objects.

/ mɛnd / (AmE), / mɛnd / (BrE)
