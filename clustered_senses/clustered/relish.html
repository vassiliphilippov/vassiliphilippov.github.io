1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɛlɪʃ / (BrE)
A spicy or savory condiment made of chopped vegetables or fruits, typically used to enhance the flavor of food.
spicy or savory condiment

Examples

He added a spoonful of relish to his hot dog for extra flavor.
The picnic included hamburgers, chips, and homemade relish.
Would you like some relish with your sandwich?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chutney - a spicy condiment made of fruits or vegetables
  • Synonym: sauce - liquid or semi-liquid condiment for food

Etymology

From Old French *reles* (something left behind, remnant) and Middle English *reles*, later influenced by 'relish' meaning taste or flavor.

2. NOUN

/ ˈrɛlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɛlɪʃ / (BrE)
The characteristic, pleasing taste or flavour when a savoury condiment is eaten.
taste of a savoury condiment

Examples

With each bite, the sharp relish awakened his appetite.

Related Terms
