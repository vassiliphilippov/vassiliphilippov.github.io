aesthetic

Summary

  • relating to beauty, art, or the appreciation of beauty
    • ADJ: concerning beauty or good taste
    • ADJ: pleasing in appearance; artistic
    • ADJ: relating to beauty or aesthetics
  • a set of underlying principles concerned with the nature and appreciation of beauty, especially in art or philosophy.
    • NOUN: philosophy of beauty or art
relating to beauty, art, or the appreciation of beauty

1. ADJ

/ ɛsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / iːsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
concerned with beauty, artistic impact, or the appreciation of beauty and good taste
concerning beauty or good taste

Examples

He appreciates objects not just for their use, but for their aesthetic value.
Her choices in interior design reveal a highly aesthetic sense.
The building was aesthetic as well as functional, pleasing both to the eye and to the mind.

Synset Examples

the aesthetic faculties
an aesthetic person
aesthetic feeling
the illustrations made the book an aesthetic success

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artistic - relating to or characteristic of art or artists
  • Antonym: unattractive - not pleasing or appealing to the senses or aesthetic sense

Etymology

From Greek aisthētikos (perceptible by the senses), from aisthanesthai (to perceive, feel).
