1. ADJ

/ ˈtʌtʃ.i / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃ.i / (BrE)
easily offended or upset; sensitive to criticism or remarks.
easily offended; sensitive

Examples

After a stressful week, I feel unusually touchy about minor comments.
Be careful what you say around Mike; he's quite touchy about his work.
She became touchy whenever her cooking was criticized, even gently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensitive - easily affected emotionally; responsive to feelings or criticism
  • Antonym: thick-skinned - not easily offended or affected by criticism

Etymology

From 'touch' + -y, dating to the late 16th century; influenced by the sense of 'touch' relating to affect or feeling.
