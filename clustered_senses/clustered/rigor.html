rigor

Summary

  • the quality of being extremely thorough, accurate, and strict in approach or judgment
    • NOUN: thoroughness and strictness
  • the quality of being extremely strict, severe, or harsh, especially in the application of rules or discipline
    • NOUN: strictness; harshness; severity
  • a severe or harsh condition that is difficult to endure, especially relating to hardship or adversity
    • NOUN: severe hardship or adversity
