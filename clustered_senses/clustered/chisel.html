chisel

Summary

  • A tool with a flat steel blade and a sharp cutting edge, used for shaping wood, stone, or metal.
    • NOUN: tool with a flat cutting blade
  • to cut or shape something, especially stone or wood, with a chisel
    • VERB: carve or shape with a chisel
  • To cheat, trick, or deprive someone of something by using deceit or fraud.
    • VERB: to cheat by deception
    • VERB: deceive to obtain something, often money
