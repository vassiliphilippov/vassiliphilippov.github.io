1. NOUN

/ toʊt / (AmE), / təʊt / (BrE)
a large, sturdy bag or basket used for carrying things, especially for shopping or everyday use
large bag or basket for carrying things

Examples

A colorful canvas tote hung from the back of her chair.
I always carry my books in a spacious tote to the library.
She packed her groceries into a sturdy tote before leaving the store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bag - a flexible container used for carrying things
  • Synonym: basket - a container made of interwoven material, used for carrying items

Etymology

Probably from English dialect tote (to carry), of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Low German tote (bag).
