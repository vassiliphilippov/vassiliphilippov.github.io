reconciliation

Summary

  • The restoration of friendly or harmonious relations after a disagreement or conflict.

  • the process of making two sets of figures, records, or accounts consistent or bringing them into agreement

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛkənˌsɪliˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛkənˌsɪliˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
The restoration of friendly or harmonious relations after a disagreement or conflict.
restoring friendly relations

Examples

After years of hostility, the two countries finally achieved reconciliation.
The family reunion marked a long-awaited reconciliation between estranged siblings.
Their reconciliation came after many heartfelt conversations and apologies from both sides.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: conflict - a serious disagreement or argument
  • Degree Higher: harmony - a state of friendly and peaceful relations

Etymology

From Latin 'reconciliatio', from 'reconciliare' (to bring together again).

Bank reconciliation is a crucial step in ensuring accurate bookkeeping for any business.
