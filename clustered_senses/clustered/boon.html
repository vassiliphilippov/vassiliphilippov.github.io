boon

Summary

  • a thing that is helpful or beneficial; a blessing or advantage
    • NOUN: helpful benefit; blessing
  • (of a companion) close and convivial; given to cheerful or sociable company, especially in the phrase 'boon companion'.
    • ADJ: close and convivial (of a companion)
a thing that is helpful or beneficial; a blessing or advantage

1. NOUN

/ bun / (AmE), / buːn / (BrE)
a thing that is helpful or beneficial; a blessing or advantage
helpful benefit; blessing

Examples

Access to clean water is considered a great boon for the village.
The mild climate proved to be a boon for farmers throughout the region.
This new highway is a real boon to commuters in the area.

Synset Examples

enjoy the blessings of peace

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blessing - a thing conducive to happiness or welfare
  • Antonym: curse - a cause of harm or trouble

Etymology

From Middle English 'bone', from Old Norse 'bón' (prayer, request)
