haunt

Summary

  • a place often visited by a particular person or group
    • NOUN: frequently visited place
  • to visit a place frequently or regularly, often as part of a habit
    • VERB: visit a place frequently
  • to repeatedly appear in someone's mind or to persistently trouble or follow someone, especially in a way that causes anxiety or unease
    • VERB: recur constantly or trouble persistently
  • For a ghost or supernatural being to regularly appear or disturb people in a place.
    • VERB: to trouble persistently; to appear as a ghost
    • VERB: (of ghost) regularly visit a place
