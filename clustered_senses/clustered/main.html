main

Summary

  • a large principal pipe or conduit in a system that distributes water, gas, or electricity, or that collects sewage
    • NOUN: principal pipe for water, gas, or electricity
  • Most important or principal; chief in importance or rank.
    • ADJ: most important; chief
  • (of force or effort) of the greatest possible intensity; full, utmost, or with all one's strength, chiefly used in certain phrases.
    • ADJ: greatest possible intensity; full force (esp. in phrases)
  • the open sea; a very large body of salt water, especially the ocean
    • NOUN: the open sea or ocean
  • (in gaming) a player's primary in-game character or character class, the one they habitually use and identify with.
    • NOUN: player's primary game character

1. NOUN

/ meɪn / (AmE), / meɪn / (BrE)
a large principal pipe or conduit in a system that distributes water, gas, or electricity, or that collects sewage
principal pipe for water, gas, or electricity

Examples

A blockage in the sewage main can lead to serious health hazards.
The city's water main burst, causing flooding in several neighborhoods.
Workers are repairing the gas main along the highway this week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipeline - a long pipe for conveying oil, gas, etc. over long distances
  • Synonym: conduit - a channel or pipe for conveying water or other fluid
  • Antonym: branch - a secondary pipe leading from a main pipe

Etymology

From Old English mægen ('strength, power'), later referring to principal or major lines.
