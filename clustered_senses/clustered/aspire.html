to aspire

Summary

  • to have a strong desire or ambition to achieve something significant or important
    • VERB: to aim ambitiously for achievement
to have a strong desire or ambition to achieve something significant or important

1. VERB

/ əˈspaɪɚ / (AmE), / əˈspaɪə / (BrE)
to have a strong desire or ambition to achieve something significant or important
to aim ambitiously for achievement

Examples

As a child, he aspired to travel the world and learn about different cultures.
Many young athletes aspire to compete in the Olympics one day.
She has always aspired to become a successful novelist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strive - to make great efforts to achieve or obtain something
  • Antonym: despair - to lose or be without hope

Etymology

From Latin aspirare (to breathe toward, to strive for), from ad- (to) + spirare (to breathe).
