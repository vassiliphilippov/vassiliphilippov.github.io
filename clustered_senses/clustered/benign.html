benign

Summary

  • Having a gentle, kind, or beneficial nature; not harmful or severe.
    • ADJ: gentle and kind in manner
    • ADJ: pleasant or beneficial in effect
  • (of a disease or medical condition, especially a tumor) not harmful to health and not likely to recur or become worse.

Examples

Examples

Doctors confirmed the tumor was benign, providing great relief to the patient.
The test results showed that the growth was benign and not cancerous.

Related Terms
