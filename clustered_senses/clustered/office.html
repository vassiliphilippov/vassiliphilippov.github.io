office

Summary

  • A room or set of rooms where professional, administrative, or clerical work is conducted.

  • The group of professional or clerical workers employed in an office, regarded collectively.
    • NOUN: group of office workers
  • a religious rite or service prescribed by ecclesiastical authorities, especially as part of a set form of worship
    • NOUN: religious rite or service
  • A position of authority or responsibility, especially in a government, company, or organization.
    • NOUN: position of responsibility or authority
    • NOUN: position of power or authority (esp. in government)
