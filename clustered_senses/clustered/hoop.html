hoop

Summary

  • to bind or fasten (something) with a hoop, especially to strengthen or secure it
    • VERB: bind or fasten with a hoop
  • In basketball, a horizontal circular metal ring supporting a net, through which players attempt to score points by throwing the ball.
    • NOUN: basketball ring with net
  • A small metal arch placed in the ground through which balls are hit in the game of croquet.
    • NOUN: croquet: small metal arch
  • a rigid circular band, typically made of metal, wood, or other material, used for holding together or fastening objects, or for hanging or pulling things
    • NOUN: rigid circular band for fastening or holding
  • a lightweight, usually circular framework used to extend and support the shape of a skirt or dress
    • NOUN: framework for spreading out a skirt
