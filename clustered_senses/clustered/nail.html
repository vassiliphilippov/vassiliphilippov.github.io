nail

Summary

  • the hard, flat, horny plate covering and protecting the upper surface of the tip of a finger or toe in humans and other primates
    • NOUN: hard plate on a finger or toe
  • a thin, pointed piece of metal, usually with a flat head, that is hammered into wood or other materials to fasten them together
    • NOUN: thin metal fastener hammered into materials
  • a former unit of length for measuring cloth, equal to 1/16 of a yard (about 2 1/4 inches or 5.7 centimeters)
    • NOUN: old unit of length (1/16 yard)
  • to attach, fasten, or secure something using a nail or nails
    • VERB: attach with a nail
  • To achieve or succeed in something, especially through effort, skill, or by performing well (including getting a job, completing a task, or making a successful move in sports).
    • VERB: to secure a position or opportunity
    • VERB: succeed at something easily or impressively
    • VERB: to successfully complete or achieve, esp. in sports
  • to hit or strike with great force, especially in sports or physical activity
    • VERB: strike or hit with force
  • to determine or identify exactly; to pinpoint with precision
    • VERB: identify or locate exactly
  • (informal) To arrest or capture someone, especially in the act of wrongdoing.
    • VERB: to arrest or capture (someone, informal)
