compartment

Summary

  • A separate section or division within a larger enclosed space, often used for storing or organizing things.
    • NOUN: partitioned section within a larger space

A separate section or division within a larger enclosed space, often used for storing or organizing things.

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / kəmˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a separate section, chamber, or division within a larger enclosed space
partitioned section within a larger space

Examples

Please place your bag in the overhead compartment before takeoff.
She stored her jewelry in a small compartment inside the box.
The train's sleeper car had a private compartment for each passenger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: section - a distinct part or subdivision of something
  • Synonym: chamber - an enclosed space or room within a larger structure

Etymology

From French 'compartiment', from Italian 'compartimento', from 'compartire' (to divide).

2. NOUN

/ kəmˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / kəmˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a separate section or space into which an area is divided, used for storing or organizing things
a separate section or subdivision

Examples

Each suitcase has a separate compartment for shoes and toiletries.
He opened the glove compartment to retrieve his sunglasses.
The train carriage was divided into first-class and second-class compartments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: section - a distinct part separated from the whole
  • Synonym: chamber - an enclosed space in a structure

Etymology

From French 'compartiment', from Italian 'compartimento', from 'compartire' (to divide).
