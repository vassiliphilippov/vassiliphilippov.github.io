pelvis

Summary

  • the large bony structure near the base of the spine to which the lower limbs are attached in humans and corresponding structures in other vertebrates
    • NOUN: bony structure supporting lower limbs
  • the funnel-shaped structure at the outlet of the kidney that collects urine before it passes into the ureter
    • NOUN: funnel-shaped kidney structure for urine collection
