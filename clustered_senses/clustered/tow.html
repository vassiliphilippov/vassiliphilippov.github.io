tow

Summary

  • an act of pulling a vehicle or object behind another, typically with a rope, chain, or tow bar
    • NOUN: act of pulling something by a rope or hitch
  • to pull or drag something, typically a vehicle, behind another vehicle or animal using a rope, chain, or similar device.
    • VERB: pull or drag behind (often a vehicle)
