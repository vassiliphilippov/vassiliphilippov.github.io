profane

    • VERB: treat sacred things with disrespect
  • Showing a lack of respect for sacred things; irreverent, blasphemous, or using obscene language.
    • ADJ: irreverent toward sacred things
    • ADJ: irreverent or obscene language/behavior
  • Not related to religion; secular or not holy.
    • ADJ: not religious; secular

1. VERB

/ prəˈfeɪn / (AmE), / prəˈfeɪn / (BrE)
to corrupt morally, especially through indulgence in sensual pleasures or intemperance
corrupt morally by intemperance or sensuality

Examples
