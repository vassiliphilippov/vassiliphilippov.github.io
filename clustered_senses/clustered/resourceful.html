resourceful

Summary

  • Good at finding effective ways to solve problems, especially in difficult situations; able to use available resources creatively and efficiently.
    • ADJ: able to solve problems cleverly
Good at finding effective ways to solve problems, especially in difficult situations; able to use available resources creatively and efficiently.

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈsɔrsfəl / (AmE), / rɪˈsɔːsfʊl / (BrE)
Good at finding effective ways to solve problems, especially in difficult situations; able to use available resources creatively and efficiently.
able to solve problems cleverly

Examples

A resourceful teacher can turn everyday objects into valuable learning tools.
He is known for his resourceful approach to solving technical problems.
She proved to be remarkably resourceful when faced with unexpected challenges during the trip.

Synset Examples

someone who is resourceful is capable of dealing with difficult situations
an able and resourceful politician
the most resourceful cook in town

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ingenious - very clever and inventive, especially in problemsolving
  • Antonym: resource-less - lacking the ability or means to deal with situations

Etymology

From 'resource' + '-ful', formed in English in the 19th century.
