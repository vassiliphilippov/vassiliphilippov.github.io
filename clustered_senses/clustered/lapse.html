lapse

Summary

  • a temporary pause or interruption in the continuity of an activity or process

  • A temporary failure or small mistake, especially due to a lack of attention or concentration.
    • NOUN: a temporary failure or decline
    • NOUN: a minor mistake due to inattention
  • To fall back or slip into a previous bad habit, state, or lower standard.
    • VERB: to fall to a lower level (morals/standards)
    • VERB: go back to bad habits or behavior
    • VERB: pass into a state or condition
  • (of time) to pass or slip by.
    • VERB: (of time) to pass
  • For something to come to an end or expire, especially because it was not maintained.
    • VERB: to end, cease, or expire
    • VERB: to let something expire or cease
