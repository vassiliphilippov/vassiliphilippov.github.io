aura

Summary

  • a radiant light or luminous glow seen or depicted as surrounding the head or body of a holy figure, such as a saint, in religious art
    • NOUN: radiant light shown around a saint
  • A sensation, such as a cold breeze or bright light, experienced before the onset of certain disorders, especially migraines or epileptic seizures.
    • NOUN: preliminary sensation before migraine or seizure
  • A distinctive atmosphere or quality that seems to surround a person, place, or thing and is often intangible or subtle.
    • NOUN: distinctive atmosphere or quality
