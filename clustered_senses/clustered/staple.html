staple

Summary

  • A small U-shaped metal fastener used to attach papers or materials together, usually with a stapler.
    • NOUN: U-shaped wire for fastening paper
    • NOUN: short U-shaped wire fastener
  • to fasten or secure something with a staple or by using a stapler
    • VERB: fasten with a staple
  • A basic or principal item, especially a commodity that is in constant demand and forms an important part of something, such as a diet or economy.
    • NOUN: basic commodity in constant demand
  • A natural fiber, such as raw cotton, wool, hemp, or flax, that can be twisted together to form yarn.
    • NOUN: textile fiber used for yarn
  • (of food or commodities) regularly used, important, or forming a principal part of something.
    • ADJ: regularly used or essential, especially for food or goods

Examples
