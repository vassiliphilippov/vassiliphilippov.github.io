Summary

  • A sudden backward movement after force or impact, such as when a gun is fired or something hits another object. (noun)
    • NOUN: backward movement of a gun when fired
    • NOUN: sudden backward movement
  • To suddenly move or spring back, especially as a reaction to force or impact. (verb)
    • VERB: spring back from force or impact
    • VERB: to spring back after impact
  • to quickly move back or away as a reaction to fear, pain, or disgust
    • VERB: draw back quickly in fear or pain

    • VERB: to have a negative effect on the originator
A sudden backward movement after force or impact, such as when a gun is fired or something hits another object. (noun)

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːˌkɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈriː.kɔɪl / (BrE)
the sudden backward movement of a gun when it is fired
backward movement of a gun when fired

Examples

Proper stance can help manage the recoil of powerful rifles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kickback - the backward movement of a firearm when fired

Etymology
