1. ADJ

/ məˈdʒɛstɪk / (AmE), / məˈdʒɛstɪk / (BrE)
Having or showing impressive beauty, dignity, or grandeur; superior in manner or bearing.
having impressive beauty or dignity

Examples

Her majestic presence commanded attention in every room she entered.
The mountains looked especially majestic in the glow of the sunrise.
The orchestra performed a majestic symphony that filled the audience with awe.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grand - large and impressive in size, appearance, or style
  • Synonym: regal - suitable for a king or queen; royal
  • Antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance

Etymology

From Old French majesté and Latin maiestās (greatness, dignity).
