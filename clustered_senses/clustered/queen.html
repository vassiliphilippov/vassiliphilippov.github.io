A female ruler of a country, or the wife or widow of a king.

1. NOUN

/ kwin / (AmE), / kwiːn / (BrE)
A female sovereign ruler, especially one who inherits the position by right of birth.
female sovereign ruler

Examples

Queen Elizabeth II was a queen for most of her life.
She became queen after her father's death.
The queen addressed her subjects from the palace balcony.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: king - male sovereign ruler
