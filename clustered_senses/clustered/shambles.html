Examples

After the party, the house was in a complete shambles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chaos - complete confusion and disorder
  • Antonym: order - a state in which everything is arranged properly

Etymology

Originally from Middle English 'shameles' meaning a slaughterhouse, from Old English 'sceamol' (stool, stall).
