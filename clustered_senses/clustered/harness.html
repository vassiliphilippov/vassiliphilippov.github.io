  • a set of straps and fittings by which something is fastened to a person's body, especially for support or safety
    • NOUN: straps fastening something to the body
  • A set of straps and fittings by which a horse or other draft animal is fastened to a cart, plow, etc., and is controlled by its driver.
    • NOUN: straps attached to a draft animal

    • VERB: to control or keep in check
    • VERB: control and use (resources or forces)
