ubiquitous

Summary

  • present, appearing, or found everywhere; widespread.
    • ADJ: present or found everywhere
present, appearing, or found everywhere; widespread.

1. ADJ

/ juːˈbɪkwɪtəs / (AmE), / juːˈbɪkwɪtəs / (BrE)
present, appearing, or found everywhere; widespread.
present or found everywhere

Examples

Plastic pollution is ubiquitous in the world’s oceans, threatening marine life everywhere.
Smartphones have become so ubiquitous that it's hard to imagine life without them.
The company’s logo was ubiquitous, appearing on billboards, television, and even coffee cups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: omnipresent - present everywhere at the same time

Etymology

From Latin 'ubique' (everywhere) + '-ous'.
