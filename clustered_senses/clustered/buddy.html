1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌd.i / (AmE), / ˈbʌd.i / (BrE)

close friend; companion

Examples

After moving to a new city, she quickly found a buddy at work.
I went to the football game with my buddy Tom last night.
Kids and their buddies spent hours playing outside during the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pal - a close friend or companion
  • Antonym: enemy - a person who is actively opposed or hostile

Etymology

Perhaps an alteration of 'brother' or from 'buddy' in American English, first recorded in the mid-19th century.
