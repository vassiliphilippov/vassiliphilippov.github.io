flimsy

Summary

  • A thin, strong, lightweight, translucent paper used especially for making carbon copies.
    • NOUN: thin lightweight paper for carbon copies
  • lacking solidity, strength, or substance; easily broken or damaged
    • ADJ: lacking strength or solidity
  • Lacking strength, substance, or convincing quality; weak or unconvincing (especially of excuses, arguments, etc.).
    • ADJ: lacking strength or significance
    • ADJ: weak or unconvincing (argument, excuse)

2. ADJ

/ ˈflɪmzi / (AmE), / ˈflɪmzi / (BrE)
(of an excuse, argument, etc.) weak and unconvincing; not persuasive or credible.
weak or unconvincing (argument, excuse)

Examples

The committee rejected the proposal because it was based on flimsy evidence.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
